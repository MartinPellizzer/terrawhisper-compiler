post formulas:
    [power-word] [goal/problem] [time/effort]

ex:
    i want to lose weight + i'm struggling to stay motivated, i give up quickly 
    = top 5 tips to stay motivated when you want to lose weight
    
    i want to make money online + i don't have any skills
    = the skilkls you don't need to make money online

make the hook mysterious (don't reveal the "thing" in the hook)

    superlatives:
        top
        most
        best
        biggest
        double/triple
    negative:
        stop
        bad
        mistakes
        costly
        wont
        dont
        avoid
        wrong
    numbers:
        year (2023)
        list (5 tips)
        time (in 4 weeks)
        range (from 0 to 100k)
        hour (in 5 min)

        how to
        how I
        this is how
        what
