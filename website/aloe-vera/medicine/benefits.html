Anthraquinones, which are laxative compounds, help in improving bowel movements and ease constipation by regulating the functioning of intestines.. Polysaccharides, particularly acemannan, boosts immune system and promotes a healthy gut environment, thus helping to prevent digestive issues like bloating or diarrhea.. Aloin, an anthraquinone in aloe vera, has mild laxative properties that can help relieve constipation and promote regular bowel movements.. Bitter glycosides are responsible for aloe's bitter taste, but they also act as a natural antimicrobial agent, helping to eradicate harmful bacteria in the digestive system, preventing infections and aiding digestion.. Enzymes present in aloe vera break down food particles, making it easier for the body to absorb nutrients and promote proper digestion.. Vitamins like A, C, E, B1, B2, B3, and folic acid have important roles in maintaining a healthy digestive system and boosting immunity, which can help prevent digestive issues.. Minerals play critical roles in various metabolic processes, including the digestion of food particles into useable nutrients, thus contributing to overall digestive health.
