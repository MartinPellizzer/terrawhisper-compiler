The herb boosts immunity through flavonoids and alkaloids, defending against infections and autoimmune disorders. Yarrow improves skin health by combating inflammation and promoting healing, while its circulation-enhancing properties aid in conditions like peripheral vascular diseases.

Preparations include teas, tinctures, and infusions using various plant parts, providing convenient ways to integrate yarrow into daily wellness routines.
