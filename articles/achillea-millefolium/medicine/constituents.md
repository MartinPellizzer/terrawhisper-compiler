The concentration of phytosterols in Yarrow varies but generally ranges from 0.1% to 0.6% in the dried plant material. For instance, beta-sitosterol content in Yarrow can reach up to 0.3%, while campesterol and stigmasterol are found in smaller amounts, around 0.05% to 0.15%.
