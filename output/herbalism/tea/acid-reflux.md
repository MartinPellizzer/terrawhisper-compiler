# 13 Best Herbal Teas For Acid Reflux

![Herbal Tea For Acid Reflux](/images/herbal-tea-for-acid-reflux.jpg)

Acid reflux is a common digestive disorder characterized by the regurgitation of stomach acid into the esophagus.

According to the National Institutes of Health, more than 60 million Americans experience heartburn at least once a month. Acid reflux can negatively affect an individual's quality of life, causing discomfort and pain in the chest and throat. The main causes of acid reflux include eating spicy or fatty foods, consuming alcohol, and smoking.

This condition can also lead to other health problems such as asthma, chronic cough, and respiratory infections.

Herbal teas such as chamomile, ginger, and peppermint have long been known to provide relief from acid reflux symptoms.

These teas work by soothing the esophagus and reducing inflammation, which can help to alleviate pain, heartburn, and discomfort associated with acid reflux. They may also promote relaxation and improve digestion, making them an excellent choice for those looking for a natural remedy for this common condition.

Whether you prefer to sip on hot tea or iced tea throughout the day, incorporating herbal teas into your daily routine can be a simple yet effective way to manage acid reflux symptoms.

In this article, you'll learn which are the best herbal teas for acid reflux, why they works, and how to make them.
