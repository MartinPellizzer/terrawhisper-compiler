# 15 Best Herbal Teas For Overactive Bladder

![Herbal Tea For Overactive Bladder](/images/herbal-tea-for-overactive-bladder.jpg)

Overactive bladder is a medical condition where the bladder contracts too frequently or too forcefully, causing the person to feel an urgent need to urinate, even when they are not full.

Every year around 30 million people in the US experience this problem, which can negatively affect their quality of life. The main causes of overactive bladder include aging, certain medications, neurological conditions, and prostate problems. This condition can also increase the risk of developing urinary tract infections and kidney stones.

Additionally, people with overactive bladder may experience anxiety, depression, and social isolation due to the fear of not being able to control their bladder.

Herbal tea is a natural remedy that can help alleviate the symptoms of an overactive bladder.

It contains ingredients such as chamomile, hibiscus, and licorice root which have been shown to soothe the urinary tract and reduce inflammation. By reducing stress and promoting relaxation, herbal tea can also improve the overall quality of life for those with an overactive bladder. Drinking herbal tea regularly can provide long-term relief from this uncomfortable condition. However, it is important to note that not all herbal teas are created equal, and some may contain ingredients that can irritate the urinary tract.

It is always recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before drinking any herbal tea for overactive bladder symptoms.

In this article, you'll learn which are the best herbal teas for overactive bladder, why they works, and how to make them.
