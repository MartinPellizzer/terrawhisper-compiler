# 13 Best Herbal Teas For High Blood Pressure

![Herbal Tea For High Blood Pressure](/images/herbal-tea-for-high-blood-pressure.jpg)

High blood pressure, also known as hypertension, is a prevalent issue worldwide.

According to the World Health Organization, approximately 1.9 million deaths occur every year due to complications arising from high blood pressure. Hypertension can have detrimental effects on an individual's quality of life, including an increased risk of heart disease and stroke. The main causes of high blood pressure include a sedentary lifestyle, smoking, family history, obesity, and stress. High blood pressure can also lead to other health issues such as kidney damage and vision loss.

Therefore, it is crucial to maintain a healthy lifestyle and regularly monitor blood pressure levels to prevent potential complications.

Herbal tea is a natural and healthy alternative to other beverages, especially for those with high blood pressure.

It contains various antioxidants, vitamins and minerals that can help lower blood pressure levels and improve overall heart health. Hibiscus tea, for example, has been shown to have potent blood pressure-lowering effects, while chamomile tea can help reduce stress and anxiety, both of which can contribute to high blood pressure.

Drinking herbal tea regularly as part of a healthy diet and lifestyle can be an effective way to manage high blood pressure without the use of medication.

In this article, you'll learn which are the best herbal teas for high blood pressure, why they works, and how to make them.

## 1. Hibiscus Tea (Hibiscus Sabdariffa)

The hibiscus plant contains antioxidants that protect the heart and blood vessels from damage caused by free radicals, reducing the risk of developing hypertension.

Additionally, the plant has been shown to inhibit an enzyme called angiotensin-converting enzyme (ACE), which is responsible for constricting blood vessels and increasing blood pressure. This property of hibiscus tea makes it a natural alternative to medication used to treat high blood pressure.

Consuming hibiscus tea regularly can help lower blood pressure levels, reduce the risk of heart disease, and improve overall cardiovascular health.
