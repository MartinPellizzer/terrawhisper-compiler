# 15 Best Herbal Teas For Headaches

![Herbal Tea For Headaches](/images/herbal-tea-for-headaches.jpg)

Headaches are painful ailments that occur when blood vessels in the head constrict or dilate, causing pressure changes on nerve receptors in the scalp.

According to a World Health Organization (WHO) report from 2019, over 30% of adults worldwide suffer from headaches. In the United States alone, it is estimated that over 45 million people experience chronic migraines or other disabling headaches on a regular basis. These conditions can have a profound impact on an individual's quality of life, affecting work and daily activities. Some of the main causes of headaches include stress, lack of sleep, dehydration, changes in diet, hormonal fluctuations, and certain medications.

Headaches can also be a symptom of more serious underlying conditions such as migraines, cluster headaches, or even brain tumors.

Ginger tea is known to be effective in relieving headaches due to its anti-inflammatory properties.

Chamomile tea is another popular choice as it contains antispasmodic agents that can help to relax constricted blood vessels in the head. Peppermint tea can also provide relief from headaches by reducing inflammation and easing muscle tension. The best way to enjoy these teas for headache relief is to steep them in hot water for 5-10 minutes, add a slice of lemon or honey for flavor, and sip slowly.

If you want to enhance the effectiveness of these teas, consider adding turmeric or ginger to your cup as they both have powerful anti-inflammatory properties.

In this article, you'll learn which are the best herbal teas for headaches, why they works, and how to make them.
