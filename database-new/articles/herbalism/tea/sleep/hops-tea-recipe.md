
1. Boil water in a kettle. Fill it with enough water to cover the tea leaves by about an inch.
2. While the water is boiling, place 1-2 teaspoons of dried hops (depending on personal preference) into a teapot or infuser.
3. Once the water has finished boiling, pour it over the hops and let it steep for 5-10 minutes.
4. Remove the hops from the tea and add honey or lemon to taste.
5. Serve the tea warm and enjoy its calming effects before bedtime.
