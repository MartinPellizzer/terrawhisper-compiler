
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or pot.
Step 2: Grind Valerian root into a fine powder.
Step 3: Measure out 1 teaspoon of Valerian powder for every 8 oz of water.
Step 4: Steep the Valerian tea for 5-10 minutes to let the flavors infuse into the water.
Step 5: Strain the tea and drink it before bedtime to help with sleep.
