
Motherwort tea is commonly used as a natural remedy for insomnia because of its sedative properties. It contains compounds that help to calm the nervous system, relax muscles and promote sleep. Drinking motherwort tea before bedtime can help you fall asleep faster and enjoy deeper, more restful sleep. Additionally, motherwort tea may also help alleviate symptoms of anxiety and stress, which are often co-occur with insomnia. Overall, motherwort tea is a gentle and effective way to improve sleep quality and duration without the use of synthetic chemicals or prescription drugs.
