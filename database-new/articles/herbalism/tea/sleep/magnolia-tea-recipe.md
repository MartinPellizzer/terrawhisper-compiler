1. Boil water in a kettle or pot.
        2. Measure 1 teaspoon of dried magnolia flower into a teapot or cup.
        3. Pour hot water over the magnolia flower and let it steep for 5-7 minutes.
        4. Remove the tea leaves from the teapot or cup.
        5. Add honey or lemon to taste, if desired, and drink the tea warm or cold.
                             Dosage: Use 1 teaspoon of dried magnolia flower per 8 ounces of water. Time: Let it steep for 5-7 minutes before drinking.
