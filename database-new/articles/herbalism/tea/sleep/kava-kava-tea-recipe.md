
1. Boil water: Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a small saucepan over medium heat.

2. Add tea: Add 1 tablespoon of kava kava powder to the boiling water, stir well to combine.

3. Steep: Reduce heat to low and let the tea steep for 5-7 minutes.

4. Strain: Pour the tea through a fine mesh strainer into a mug or cup.

5. Serve: Add honey or lemon if desired, sip slowly and enjoy your Kava Kava Tea for sleep.
