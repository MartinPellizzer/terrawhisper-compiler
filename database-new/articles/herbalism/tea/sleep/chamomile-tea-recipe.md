
            Procedure:
            1. Boil water in a kettle or pot.
            2. While water is boiling, add 1-2 tablespoons of chamomile flowers (about 10-20 grams) to a teapot or infuser.
            3. Pour hot water over the chamomile flowers and let steep for 5-7 minutes.
            4. Add 1-2 teaspoons of honey to taste (optional).
            5. Remove tea leaves, sip warm and enjoy before bedtime.
