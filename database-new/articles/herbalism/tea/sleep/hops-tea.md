These compounds work by binding to certain receptors in the brain that help to reduce anxiety and stress levels, making it easier to fall asleep and stay asleep. Additionally, hops tea has been shown to improve the quality of sleep by reducing the amount of time it takes to fall asleep and decreasing the likelihood of waking up during the night. Overall, hops tea is a natural and effective way to improve sleep quality and duration.
