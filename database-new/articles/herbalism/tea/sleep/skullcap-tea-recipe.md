
1. Boil water in a kettle or pot, then remove from heat and allow it to cool down to about 200 degrees Fahrenheit (93 degrees Celsius).
2. Crush 1/2 cup of fresh skullcap herb leaves or use 1-2 teaspoons of dried skullcap leaves and add them to a tea infuser or reusable tea bag.
3. Place the infuser or tea bag in a mug or cup and pour the cooled water over it, making sure all the leaves are submerged.
4. Steep for 5-10 minutes to extract the flavor and potent compounds from the herb.
5. Remove the tea bag or infuser and enjoy your warm skullcap tea for sleep. For better results, add honey or lemon juice, which can help enhance the herb's effects and improve taste.
