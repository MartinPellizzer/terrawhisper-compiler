1. Boil water in a kettle or on stove top. 2 cups is recommended. 
       2. Steep 1-2 tablespoons of dried passionflower herb per cup of water for 5-7 minutes. 
       3. Remove from heat and let it cool down to room temperature. 
       4. Pour into a teapot or directly into a mug. 
       5. Add honey, lemon, or milk to taste, and enjoy before bedtime.
