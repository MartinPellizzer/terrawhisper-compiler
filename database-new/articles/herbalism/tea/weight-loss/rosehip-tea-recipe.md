1. Boil water in a kettle or pot.
        2. Crush 2 dried rosehips and place them in a teapot or cup.
        3. Pour hot water over the rosehips, making sure they are fully submerged. Steep for 5-7 minutes.
        4. Remove the rosehips from the tea using a strainer or spoon.
        5. Add honey or lemon to taste (optional). Enjoy your rosehip tea for weight loss.
        
Note: Dosage may vary depending on individual needs and preferences. It is recommended to consume 2-3 cups of rosehip tea per day for weight loss.
