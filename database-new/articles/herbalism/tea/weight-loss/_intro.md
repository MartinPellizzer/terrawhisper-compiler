Obesity is a global pandemic affecting over 300 million individuals every year. It is characterized by an excessive accumulation of body fat that negatively impacts overall health and quality of life. This condition can lead to numerous complications such as type 2 diabetes, heart disease, certain types of cancer, and arthritis. The main causes of obesity are unhealthy eating habits, lack of physical activity, genetics, stress, and sleep disorders. Obesity is also linked to other health issues like high blood pressure, cholesterol, and mental illnesses such as depression and anxiety.
