
1. Boil water: Fill a kettle or pot with cold water and bring it to a boil on high heat.
2. Steep yerba mate tea leaves: Place 2 tablespoons of yerba mate tea leaves in a teapot or a reusable mug. Pour the hot water over the tea leaves and let them steep for 5-10 minutes, depending on how strong you like your tea.
3. Strain tea: Remove the tea leaves from the pot or mug with a strainer or a spoon and pour the tea into a cup.
4. Add lemon juice and honey: Squeeze the juice of half a lemon into the cup and add 1 tablespoon of honey. Stir well to mix the ingredients.
5. Enjoy! Let the tea cool down for a few minutes before drinking it to help with weight loss. Drink 2-3 cups of this yerba mate tea per day for optimal results.
