
            The study investigates the effect of a Hibiscus sabdariffa calyces aqueous extract on body weight in obese mice induced by the administration of monosodium glutamate. The extract was administered orally for 60 days and measured various parameters such as food and liquid intake, AST and ALT levels, cholesterol, and triglycerides levels. The results showed that Hibiscus sabdariffa administration significantly reduced body weight gain in obese mice and increased liquid intake in healthy and obese mice. ALT levels were significantly increased on the 15th and 45th days in obese mice, but AST levels did not show significant changes. The study confirms the anti-obesity effect of Hibiscus sabdariffa.
