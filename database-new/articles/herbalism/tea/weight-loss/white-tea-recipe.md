
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or on stove.
            
Step 2: Add one teaspoon of loose leaf white tea to a cup or mug.
            
Step 3: Pour hot water over the tea leaves, making sure they are fully submerged. Steep for 3-5 minutes.
          
Step 4: Remove the tea leaves from the cup and discard them.
           
Step 5: Enjoy your white tea for weight loss!
