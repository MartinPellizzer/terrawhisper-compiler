
Step 1: Bring water to a boil in a kettle or pot.
Step 2: Add 1 tablespoon of loose-leaf hibiscus tea to the boiling water.
Step 3: Steep the tea for 5 minutes.
Step 4: Remove the tea bag or strain out the tea leaves.
Step 5: Pour the hibiscus tea into a cup and add honey or lemon to taste Serve warm or cold.
