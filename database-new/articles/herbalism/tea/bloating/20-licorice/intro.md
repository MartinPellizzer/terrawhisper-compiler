Licorice (Glycyrrhiza glabra) Tea contains bioactive compounds, such as glycyrrhizin and flavonoids, known for their anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties. These constituents alleviate bloating by reducing inflammation and relaxing the gastrointestinal muscles, thereby promoting smoother digestion. The tea's natural compounds also contribute to balancing electrolytes, which aids in minimizing fluid retention, a common factor in bloating. Regular consumption of licorice tea, within recommended limits, can be beneficial for individuals experiencing bloating.
