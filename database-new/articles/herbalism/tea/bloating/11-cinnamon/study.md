According to a study published by the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine, cinnamon has shown promise in alleviating bloating associated with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS). The study assessed the effects of a natural medicine formulation, the DA-IBS formula, which included cinnamon quills. Subjects in the DA-IBS group experienced significant reductions in bloating (p < 0.0001) during the treatment phase of the trial. This suggests that cinnamon, as part of a comprehensive formula, may contribute to improving abdominal symptoms, offering a potential natural remedy for bloating.
