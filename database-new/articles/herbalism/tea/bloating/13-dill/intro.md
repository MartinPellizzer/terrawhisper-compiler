Dill (Anethum graveolens) Tea contains carminative properties attributed to its essential oils, including carvone and limonene, which aid in alleviating bloating. These compounds facilitate the relaxation of the gastrointestinal tract muscles, promoting the expulsion of gas. Additionally, dill tea possesses anti-inflammatory characteristics, helping to soothe the digestive system and reduce bloating discomfort. The tea's efficacy in addressing bloating is underpinned by these bioactive constituents that contribute to improved digestive function.
