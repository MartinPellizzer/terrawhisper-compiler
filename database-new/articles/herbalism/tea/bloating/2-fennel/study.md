According to a study published by the J Gastrointestin Liver Dis, patients with irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) experienced significant relief from bloating symptoms after a 30-day course of a novel oral combination of two nutraceutical compounds: curcumin and fennel oil. The study utilized the IBS-SSS questionnaire, measuring abdominal pain and bloating, among other factors. The findings demonstrated early and consistent symptomatic relief without safety concerns, suggesting the potential effectiveness of fennel in alleviating bloating in IBS patients.
