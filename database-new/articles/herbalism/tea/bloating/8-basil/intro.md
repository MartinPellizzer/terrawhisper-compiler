Basil (Ocimum tenuiflorum) Tea contains compounds such as eugenol, linalool, and ursolic acid, known for their anti-inflammatory and carminative properties. These constituents aid in relieving bloating by reducing inflammation in the digestive tract and promoting smooth muscle contraction. Eugenol exhibits anti-spasmodic effects, while linalool contributes to the herb's calming influence on the digestive system. Ursolic acid further supports gastrointestinal health, collectively making basil tea an effective remedy for bloating.
