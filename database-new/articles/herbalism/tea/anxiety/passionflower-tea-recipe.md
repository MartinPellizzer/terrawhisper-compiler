
Passionflower Tea Recipe:
1. Boil water in a kettle or pot.
2. Steep 1 teaspoon of passionflower root powder or capsules per cup of water for 5-7 minutes.
3. Remove the tea from heat and add honey or lemon if desired.
4. Strain the tea into cups and serve immediately.
5. Repeat daily as needed to manage anxiety symptoms.
