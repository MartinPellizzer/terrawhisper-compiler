        Dosage: Use about 1-2 tablespoons of powder for every 8 oz (250 ml) of water.
        Time: Grind the kava until it's a fine, smooth powder.

Step 3: Add the ground kava to the boiling water and stir well.

        Dosage: Add the ground kava to the cooled water, then let it steep for about 10 minutes.
        Time: Let the tea steep for 5-10 minutes, depending on how strong you want it.

Step 4: Strain the tea through a cheesecloth or a fine mesh strainer into a cup or mug.

        Dosage: Discard the grounds or capsules and enjoy your kava kava tea for anxiety.
        Time: Pour the tea into a cup or mug and drink it hot.
