 Lemon balm tea, also known as Melissa officinalis, has been used for centuries to treat anxiety and other nervous disorders due to its calming properties. It contains a chemical compound called citronellol which helps reduce stress levels by increasing the production of gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA), a neurotransmitter that relaxes the brain. The tea is also rich in antioxidants, which can help improve mood and reduce inflammation in the body, further contributing to its anxiety-reducing effects. Drinking lemon balm tea may provide a natural alternative to traditional medication for managing anxiety, but it's important to consult with a healthcare professional before making any significant changes to your treatment plan.
