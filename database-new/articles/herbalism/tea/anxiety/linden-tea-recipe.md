1. Boil water in a kettle or pot. Heat the water to 200 degrees Fahrenheit (93 Celsius).
        2. Place 1 teaspoon of linden flowers into a teapot or directly into the cup.
        3. Pour the hot water over the linden flowers and let it steep for 5-7 minutes.
        4. Remove the linden flowers from the tea and add honey, if desired.
        5. Sip the tea slowly and enjoy its calming effects on anxiety.
