
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or pot.
      200 milliliters of filtered water for each serving.

Step 2: Chop fresh lemon balm leaves into small pieces.
      2 teaspoons of chopped lemon balm per serving.

Step 3: In a teapot, add the chopped lemon balm and boiled water.
      Steep for 5-10 minutes.

Step 4: Strain the tea through a fine mesh strainer into a cup or mug.
      Remove any remaining leaves or debris from the tea.

Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy your lemon balm tea for anxiety relief.
      Drink up to three servings per day, depending on personal needs and preferences.
