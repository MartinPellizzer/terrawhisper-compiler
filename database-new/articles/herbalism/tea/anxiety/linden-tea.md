
Linden tea is made from the leaves, flowers and bark of the linden tree, which has been used for medicinal purposes for centuries. It contains flavonoids, which are antioxidants that help to reduce inflammation and promote relaxation in the body. When consumed, linden tea can help to soothe nerves and reduce feelings of anxiety. Studies have shown that the natural compounds found in linden tea can act as neuroprotective agents, protecting brain cells from damage caused by stress and other environmental factors. Additionally, linden tea has been shown to improve sleep quality, which is a crucial component in managing anxiety. Overall, the anti-inflammatory, relaxing and neuroprotective properties of linden tea make it an excellent remedy for people suffering from anxiety.
