## What are the best herbal teas for stress?

Chamomile and lavender herbal teas are widely recognized for their calming properties, making them excellent choices for alleviating stress. Chamomile, with its mild floral flavor, contains compounds that interact with the brain receptors to promote relaxation and reduce anxiety. Lavender, known for its soothing aroma, can be brewed into a tea that helps ease nervous tension and induces a sense of calm. Both teas are caffeine-free and can be enjoyed throughout the day to support relaxation and stress relief. Incorporating these herbal teas into your routine may provide a comforting and natural way to manage stress.
