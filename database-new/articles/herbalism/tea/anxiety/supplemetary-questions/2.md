## What are the best herbal teas to help calm nerves?

Chamomile and lavender teas are renowned for their calming properties, making them excellent choices to help ease nerves and promote relaxation. Chamomile, derived from the chamomile flower, contains compounds that may have mild sedative effects, promoting a sense of calm. Lavender, known for its soothing aroma, can have a calming impact on the nervous system, reducing stress and anxiety. Enjoying a warm cup of chamomile or lavender tea before bedtime or during stressful moments can contribute to a more tranquil state of mind. However, individual responses to herbal teas may vary, and it's advisable to consult with a healthcare professional if you have specific health concerns or are taking medications.
