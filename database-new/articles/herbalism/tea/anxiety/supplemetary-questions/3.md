## What are the best herbal teas for depression?

Several herbal teas are believed to offer potential benefits for managing symptoms of depression. Chamomile tea, known for its calming properties, may help reduce stress and promote relaxation, which can alleviate depressive symptoms. Lavender tea is another option, appreciated for its soothing effects on the nervous system, potentially aiding in mood regulation. St. John's Wort tea, though controversial due to its interactions with certain medications, has been traditionally used to address mild to moderate depression. Lemon balm tea is also favored for its calming effects, potentially enhancing mood and reducing feelings of anxiety. However, it's important to consult with a healthcare professional before using herbal remedies, especially for managing mental health conditions like depression.
