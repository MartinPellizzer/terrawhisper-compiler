
1. Fill up a kettle with water and bring it to a boil.
2. Place one teaspoon of dried wood betony in a tea infuser or a reusable tea bag.
3. Pour the hot water over the herb and let it steep for 5-7 minutes.
4. Remove the tea bag or infuser from the cup and add honey or lemon to taste.
