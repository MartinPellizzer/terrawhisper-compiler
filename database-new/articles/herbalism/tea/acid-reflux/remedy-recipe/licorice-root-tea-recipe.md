
1. Boil water in a kettle or pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. 
2. Add 1 teaspoon of licorice root to the pot. Steep for 5-10 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and strain into a cup or mug.
4. Add honey or lemon to taste, if desired.
5. Drink the tea daily to help alleviate acid reflux symptoms.
