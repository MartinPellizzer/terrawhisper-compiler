
1. Fill a kettle with fresh water and bring it to a boil.
2. While waiting for the water, measure out 2 cups of calendula officinalis flowers and place them in a tea infuser or muslin bag.
3. Once the water has finished boiling, pour it over the calendula flowers and let it steep for at least 5 minutes, but no more than 10 minutes.
4. Strain the tea through a fine mesh strainer to remove any stray flower parts or pieces of the infuser/muslin bag.
5. Pour the tea into a cup and serve immediately, or store it in the refrigerator for later consumption. Enjoy your calendula tea for acid reflux relief!
