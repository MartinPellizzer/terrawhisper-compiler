
          1. Boil water: Boil 2 cups of water in a pot on the stove or in an electric kettle.
          
          2. Prepare aloe vera gel: Cut a 1/4-inch slice from an aloe vera leaf and extract its juice using a spoon or knife. Alternatively, you can buy aloe vera gel from a health food store. Measure out 1 tablespoon of the aloe vera gel.
          
          3. Steep the tea: Once the water has boiled, pour it into a mug or teapot. Add the aloe vera gel and let it steep for 5-10 minutes.
          
          4. Serve the tea: Pour the tea into a cup, and serve it hot. You can add honey or lemon to taste if desired.
          
          5. Repeat the process: Drink one cup of aloe vera tea per day for up to 2 weeks to help reduce acid reflux symptoms.
