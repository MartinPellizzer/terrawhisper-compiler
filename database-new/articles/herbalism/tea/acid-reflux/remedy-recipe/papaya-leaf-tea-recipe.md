                            1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot or kettle.
                            
                            2. Add 1-2 tablespoons of papaya leaves to the boiling water.
                            
                            3. Steep the tea for 5-10 minutes, depending on personal preference.
                            
                            4. Remove the papaya leaves from the pot and pour the tea into a cup or mug.
                            
                            5. Add honey or lemon to taste, if desired. Drink warm or cold.
