1) Chop up 1 teaspoon of marshmallow root and add it to a pot with 2 cups of water.
        2) Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
        3) Strain out the marshmallow root and discard it.
        4) Add 1 teaspoon of honey to taste (optional).
        5) Let cool down before drinking.
