1. Boil water in a kettle or pot, and pour it into a teapot.
        2. Add 1 teaspoon of lemon balm leaves to the teapot.
        3. Steep the tea for 5-7 minutes, depending on personal preference.
        4. Remove the lemon balm leaves from the tea before drinking.
        5. Drink the tea while it is still warm, and repeat as needed throughout the day.
