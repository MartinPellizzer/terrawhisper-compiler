
          1. Boil water in a kettle or pot.
          2. Grate 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger root and add it to the boiling water.
          3. Steep the ginger for 5-10 minutes, depending on personal preference.
          4. Remove from heat and strain out the ginger using a cheesecloth or tea infuser.
          5. Pour the ginger tea into a cup and enjoy.
