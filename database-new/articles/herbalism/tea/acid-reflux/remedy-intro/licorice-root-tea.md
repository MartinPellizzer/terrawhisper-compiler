
               Licorice root tea has been used for centuries to treat various gastrointestinal disorders, including acid reflux. The active ingredient in licorice root, glycyrrhizin, has natural antacid properties that can help neutralize stomach acid and reduce symptoms of acid reflux. Licorice root also contains mucilage, which can coat the esophagus and provide a barrier against irritating stomach acid. Additionally, licorice root may help increase the production of mucus in the stomach, which can act as a natural buffer against acid. Overall, licorice root tea can be an effective natural remedy for acid reflux symptoms.
