
Step 1: Boil water
                Bring 2 cups of fresh, cold water to a boil in a small saucepan over medium-high heat.

Step 2: Steep the tea
                Once the water has reached a rolling boil, remove it from heat and pour it over 1 teaspoon of loose-leaf raspberry tea. Let the tea steep for 5 minutes before removing the tea bag or straining out the leaves.

Step 3: Add honey or lemon to taste
                If desired, sweeten your tea with a spoonful of honey or a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to taste.

Step 4: Drink and relax
                Sip your warm raspberry tea slowly, allowing the flavors to fully develop. Take some time to enjoy your tea and unwind after a busy day.

Step 5: Repeat as needed
                If you find that you need more relief from constipation, repeat this process 1-2 times per day until symptoms improve.
