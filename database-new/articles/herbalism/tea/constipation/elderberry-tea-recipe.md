
1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot on the stove.
2. Add 1 tablespoon of dried elderberries to the boiling water.
3. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
4. Remove from heat and let steep for another 5-10 minutes.
5. Strain and pour into a cup. Enjoy your Elderberry Tea for constipation!
