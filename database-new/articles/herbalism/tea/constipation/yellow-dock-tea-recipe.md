
          1. Gather dried yellow dock seeds, boiling water, tea pot or mug, and tea spoon or strainer.
          2. Use one to two teaspoons of yellow dock seeds per cup of water.
          3. Boil the water in a kettle and pour it into the tea pot or mug.
          4. Steep the tea for 10 to 15 minutes.
          5. Remove the tea leaves with a tea spoon or strainer and enjoy the tea while warm.
