
Psyllium tea is a natural remedy for constipation that has been used for centuries. The primary ingredient in psyllium husk, which is the outer covering of the psyllium seed, absorbs water and expands into a gel-like substance when consumed. This gel formation helps to add bulk to stools, making them softer and easier to pass. Additionally, psyllium tea can help to stimulate bowel movements by increasing the frequency and force of contractions in the intestines. The high fiber content in psyllium husk also promotes regularity by preventing constipation-causing waste from accumulating in the colon. Overall, psyllium tea is an effective treatment for constipation because it increases bowel movement speed and frequency while also promoting stool softness and regularity.
