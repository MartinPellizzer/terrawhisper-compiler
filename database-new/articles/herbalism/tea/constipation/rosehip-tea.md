
Rosehip tea is a natural remedy for constipation due to its high fiber content. The tea is made from the dried fruit of the rosehip plant which is rich in soluble and insoluble fibers. These fibers help regulate bowel movements by adding bulk to stools, making them easier to pass through the digestive system. Additionally, the tea contains antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds that can help soothe irritated intestines and reduce inflammation in the gut, which can contribute to constipation. Overall, rosehip tea is a safe and effective way to alleviate symptoms of constipation and promote healthy digestion.
