
Step 1: Collect ingredients. Gather 2 cups of fresh dandelion leaves, 2 cups of water, and 1 tablespoon of honey or lemon juice to taste.
Step 2: Wash and dry the dandelion leaves. Remove any stems or flowers from the leaves.
Step 3: Boil the water in a pot on medium heat until it reaches boiling point. Add the washed dandelion leaves and remove the pot from heat.
Step 4: Let the tea steep for 5 to 10 minutes. Strain the tea into a cup and add honey or lemon juice to taste.
Step 5: Drink the tea warm and allow it to work its magic on constipation.
