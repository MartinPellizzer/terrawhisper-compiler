
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or pot.

Step 2: Add 1 teaspoon of dried hibiscus flowers to the boiling water.

Step 3: Steep the tea for 5-7 minutes.

Step 4: Remove from heat and strain into a cup.

Step 5: Serve hot and add sweetener if desired.

Note: If using fresh hibiscus flowers, use 2 tablespoons per cup of water instead of 1 teaspoon. Adjust steeping time to 7-10 minutes for fresh flowers.
