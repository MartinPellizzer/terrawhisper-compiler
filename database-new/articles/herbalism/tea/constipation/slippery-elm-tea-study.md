
               The Nutrition Care (NC) Gut Relief Formula is a combination of herbs and nutrients including curcumin, Aloe vera, slippery elm, guar gum, pectin, peppermint oil, and glutamine that benefits the gastrointestinal (GI) system. The study tested the hypothesis that the NC Gut Relief Formula would be effective in improving GI symptoms and gut health in adults with digestive disorders. The formula was taken by 43 participants for 16 weeks in two phases, with significant improvements in frequency and severity of upper and lower GI symptoms, including constipation or diarrhea, abdominal pain, and troublesome flatulence, as well as physical functioning, energy levels, mood, and sleep. Intestinal permeability improved, with a marked increase in Lactobacillus, Clostridium, and Faecalibacterium prausnitzii in the microbial profile. Most participants no longer required reflux medication or were able to reintroduce food triggers without symptom aggravation after 3 months. Overall, the NC Gut Relief Formula significantly improved GI symptoms and associated quality of life over 3 months while reducing intestinal permeability, improving the microbial profile, reducing the need for reflux medication, and enabling the consumption of previous food triggers in adults with digestive disorders.
