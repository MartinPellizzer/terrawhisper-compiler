
Slippery elm tea is a natural remedy for constipation. It is easy to make and can be enjoyed at any time of the day. Here's a simple 5-step recipe:

1. Boil water: Bring a pot of water to a boil on your stove.
2. Add slippery elm bark: Add 1-2 tablespoons of slippery elm bark to the pot of boiling water. Steep for 5-10 minutes.
3. Strain and serve: After steeping, strain the tea into a cup or mug. You can add honey or lemon to taste. Sip slowly and enjoy.
4. Repeat daily: Drink slippery elm tea every day for constipation relief.
5. Consult with a healthcare professional: If you experience severe constipation or any other health concerns, consult with a healthcare professional before using slippery elm tea.
