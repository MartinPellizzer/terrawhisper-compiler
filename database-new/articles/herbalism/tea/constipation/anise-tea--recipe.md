
Step 1: Boil water. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan over high heat.

Step 2: Steep tea. Reduce the heat to low, add 2 teaspoons of anise seeds and steep for 5 minutes.

Step 3: Strain the tea. Pour the tea through a fine-mesh strainer into two mugs or cups.

Step 4: Add sugar or honey. Stir in 1 teaspoon of sugar or honey to taste.

Step 5: Serve and enjoy. Enjoy your anise tea hot for constipation relief.
