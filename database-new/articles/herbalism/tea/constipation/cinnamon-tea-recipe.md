
1. Boil water: Heat a kettle or pot of cold water until it reaches boiling point.
2. Steep tea leaves and cinnamon stick: Add 1 teaspoon of loose leaf black tea (preferably chamomile) and 1/2 inch cinnamon stick to a tea infuser or reusable tea bag. Place the infuser or bag into a cup and pour in hot water, leaving it to steep for 5-7 minutes.
3. Remove tea leaves and cinnamon stick: Once the tea has finished steeping, remove the infuser or bag from the cup and discard the tea leaves and cinnamon stick.
4. Add honey or lemon (optional): Stir in a teaspoon of honey to sweeten up the drink, or squeeze in some fresh lemon juice for added flavor.
5. Drink the tea: Enjoy your warm and soothing Cinnamon Tea for constipation while you relax and wait for your digestive system to start working again.
