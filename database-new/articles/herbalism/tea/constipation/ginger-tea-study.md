 Ginger has been used for centuries in traditional Chinese and Indian medicine to treat constipation, flatulence, and other digestive problems. It stimulates blood circulation throughout the body, improving fluid flow in traditional Chinese medicine. A recent study found that an aqueous extract of ginger significantly reduced platelet thromboxane-B2 and prostaglandin-E2 (PGE2) production, indicating it may also be effective in reducing inflammation.
