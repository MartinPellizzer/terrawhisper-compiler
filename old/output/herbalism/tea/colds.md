# 11 Best Herbal Teas For Colds

![Herbal Tea For Colds](/images/herbal-tea-for-colds.jpg)

Colds are a common illness that affects millions of people worldwide every year.

The rhinovirus is the most common cause of colds, but there are over 200 different viruses that can cause this problem. According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), adults typically have two to three colds per year, while children can have even more. Colds can negatively affect people’s quality of life by causing symptoms such as fever, chills, coughing, sneezing, runny nose, sore throat, headache, body aches, and fatigue.

In fact, colds can result in missed work or school days, increased healthcare costs, and decreased productivity.

Herbal tea is a popular natural remedy for combating the common cold.

Made from a variety of herbs, spices and flowers, these teas have been used for centuries to alleviate symptoms such as congestion, sore throat, and cough. Some popular ingredients in herbal tea for colds include ginger, turmeric, elderberries, echinacea, and peppermint. Drinking warm herbal tea can help soothe an irritated throat, reduce inflammation, and boost the immune system.

Overall, herbal tea is a safe, effective, and delicious way to ease cold symptoms and promote overall wellness.

In this article, you'll learn which are the best herbal teas for colds, why they works, and how to make them.
