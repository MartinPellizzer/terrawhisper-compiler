Stomach cramps are painful, severe contraction of the abdominal muscles.

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), it is estimated that over 250 million people worldwide suffer from stomach cramps every year. These cramps can significantly affect an individual's quality of life by causing discomfort and pain. The main causes of stomach cramps include gastroenteritis, food allergies, and constipation. They are also linked to other health problems such as irritable bowel syndrome, gallbladder disease, and ovarian cysts. In addition, stomach cramps can lead to malnutrition and dehydration if left untreated.

It is important to seek medical attention for severe or persistent stomach cramps, especially in individuals with underlying health conditions.

Herbal tea is an effective remedy to calm and soothe the stomach, providing relief from cramping and discomfort.
