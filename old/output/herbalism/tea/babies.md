# 15 Best Herbal Teas For Babies

![Herbal Tea For Babies](/images/herbal-tea-for-babies.jpg)

Babies are one of the most innocent beings in the world.

But, unfortunately, many babies suffer from birth defects that can negatively affect their quality of life. According to the World Health Organization, every year around 3 million babies are born with some form of birth defect. This problem can cause a wide range of issues, including hearing loss, vision problems, physical deformities, and even intellectual disabilities. The main causes of birth defects include genetic factors, environmental factors, and lifestyle choices such as smoking and alcohol consumption during pregnancy.

These defects not only affect the baby's physical appearance and abilities but also have a significant impact on their overall health and well-being.

Herbal tea has been used for centuries to help with digestion and to calm the mind.

For babies, it can be a gentle alternative to sugary drinks like juice or formula. Chamomile tea is particularly popular among parents for its soothing properties, while peppermint tea can help with gas and bloating. Ginger tea can also aid in digestion and reduce inflammation. Rooibos tea is caffeine-free and rich in antioxidants, making it a great choice for a warm drink at bedtime.

No matter what herbal tea you choose, be sure to steep it gently and sweeten it with honey or another natural sweetener if desired.

In this article, you'll learn which are the best herbal teas for babies, why they works, and how to make them.

## 1. Chamomile Tea (Matricaria Recutita)

Chamomile tea has been used for centuries to help soothe and calm babies due to its mild sedative properties.

It can be given as a warm, sweet drink before bedtime or during the day when babies are fussy. The tea helps relax nerves, reducing the chances of overstimulation and anxiety in babies. Additionally, chamomile tea has anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties that can help alleviate teething pain and colic in babies.

Overall, chamomile tea is a gentle and effective way to soothe and calm babies, making it a popular choice among parents.
