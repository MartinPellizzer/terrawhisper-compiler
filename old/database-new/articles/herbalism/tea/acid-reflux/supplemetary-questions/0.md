## What are the worst herbal teas for acid reflux?

Herbal teas that are highly acidic, such as spearmint and citrus-based blends, can exacerbate acid reflux symptoms. These teas may relax the lower esophageal sphincter, allowing stomach acid to flow back into the esophagus. Additionally, spicy herbal teas like cinnamon may also trigger heartburn in some individuals. It's advisable for those with acid reflux issues to choose mild, non-acidic herbal teas like chamomile or licorice.

## What lifestyle changes help reduce acid reflux?

To alleviate acid reflux, adopting lifestyle changes is crucial. Incorporating a well-balanced diet with smaller, more frequent meals can prevent overeating and reduce pressure on the lower esophageal sphincter. Avoiding trigger foods, such as spicy and acidic items, and refraining from lying down immediately after eating can mitigate symptoms. Elevating the head of the bed during sleep and maintaining a healthy weight also contribute to managing acid reflux effectively. Regular exercise and stress management further promote digestive health.
