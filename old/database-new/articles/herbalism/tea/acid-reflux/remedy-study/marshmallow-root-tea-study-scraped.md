Marshmallow (Althea officinalis L.) contains a mucilage quality (may interfere with the absorption of other medications) which helps to coat the esophagus and stomach lining, creating a protective barrier against stomach acid. It’s an effective stimulator of cell physiology of epithelial cells which can prove the traditional use of Marshmallow preparations for treatment of irritated mucous membranes within tissue regeneration.
