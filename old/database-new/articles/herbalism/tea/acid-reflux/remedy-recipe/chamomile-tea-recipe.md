

        1. Boil water in a kettle or pot.
        2. Add one to two teaspoons of chamomile flowers, depending on personal taste, to the boiling water.
        3. Steep the tea for 5-7 minutes, allowing the chamomile to release its calming properties.
        4. Remove from heat and strain into a cup or mug.
        5. Add honey or lemon to taste.
