
Step 1: Boil water in a pot on stove.
Step 2: Cut off the top of an aloe vera leaf, leaving the gel inside. Scrape out the gel into a strainer or cheesecloth.
Step 3: Place aloe vera gel into the pot of boiling water and steep for 10-15 minutes.
Step 4: Strain aloe vera tea through a fine mesh strainer into a cup or glass.
Step 5: Drink the tea warm and continue drinking as needed to relieve constipation.
