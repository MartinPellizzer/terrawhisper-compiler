
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or on stove and let it cool down to boiling point.

Step 2: Finely chop 1 tablespoon of fennel seeds, 1 teaspoon of ground cumin, and 1 teaspoon of coriander seeds.

Step 3: Place the chopped spices in a teapot or cup.

Step 4: Pour the cooled water over the spices and let it steep for 5-7 minutes.

Step 5: Strain the tea into a cup and drink it warm. For constipation, take the tea twice a day until your stool is soft and easy to pass.
