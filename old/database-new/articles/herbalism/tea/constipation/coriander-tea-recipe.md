
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or pot.
            Heat water to boiling point.

Step 2: Mix equal parts of coriander seeds, cumin seeds, and fennel seeds in a small bowl.
            
Step 3: Add spice mix to hot water and steep for 5-10 minutes.
            
Step 4: Strain the tea into a cup and add honey or lemon to taste.
             
Step 5: Drink warm to relieve constipation.
