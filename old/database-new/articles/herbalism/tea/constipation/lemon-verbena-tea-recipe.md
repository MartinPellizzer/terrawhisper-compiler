1. Boil water in a kettle or pot.
        2. Fill a tea infuser with 1 tablespoon of dried lemon verbena leaves and 1 cinnamon stick.
        3. Pour the hot water over the herbs and let steep for 5-7 minutes.
        4. Remove the tea bag or infuser from the pot and add honey to taste.
        5. Drink the tea warm to help alleviate constipation symptoms.
        Note: This recipe makes one serving of tea. Adjust the amount of herbs and spices as desired.
