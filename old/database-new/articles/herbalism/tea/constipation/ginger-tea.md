
Ginger tea is a popular remedy for constipation due to its ability to stimulate digestion and increase gut mobility. The active compounds in ginger, such as gingerols and shogaols, have been shown to relax the muscles in the digestive tract, allowing food to pass through more easily. Additionally, ginger's anti-inflammatory properties can help reduce inflammation in the intestines, which can contribute to constipation. Drinking ginger tea can also increase the production of bile, which helps break down fats and improve digestion. Overall, the combination of these effects makes ginger tea an effective natural remedy for constipation.
