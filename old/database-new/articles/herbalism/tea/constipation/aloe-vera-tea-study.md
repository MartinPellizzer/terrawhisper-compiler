
            Aloe vera has been known for its medicinal properties for centuries. It is commonly used as a natural remedy to treat digestive problems such as constipation. In this study, the effects of aloe vera on chronic constipation were evaluated in a double-blind trial. The participants were randomized to receive capsules containing celandin, aloevera and psyllium, or placebo. After 28 days, the results showed that the preparation was effective in increasing bowel movements, softening stools, and reducing laxative dependence. Abdominal pain was not reduced in either group. Overall, this study provides evidence for the efficacy of aloe vera as a natural remedy for chronic constipation.
