 Step 1: Boil water in a pot, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to a simmer.
           Use about 2 cups of water per serving.
       
          Step 2: Add 1 teaspoon of marshmallow root powder to the water for each serving.
           Steep for 5-10 minutes.
        
         Step 3: Remove from heat and add honey or lemon to taste, if desired.
          Allow it to cool down before drinking.
      
      Step 4: Add black cohosh root powder and ground cinnamon to the tea.
      The dosage is 1 teaspoon of black cohosh per serving and 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon per serving.
       
     Step 5: Strain the tea through a fine-mesh strainer and discard the marshmallow root, black cohosh root, and cinnamon.
      Enjoy your Marshmallow root Tea for constipation!
