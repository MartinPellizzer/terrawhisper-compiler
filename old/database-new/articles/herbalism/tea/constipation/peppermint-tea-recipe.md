
Step 1: Boil water in a teapot or pot on the stove.
            
Step 2: Add 1 teaspoon of peppermint leaves to the boiling water.
             
Step 3: Steep the tea for 5 minutes to allow the mint to release its essential oils.
              
Step 4: Strain the tea into a mug or cup, and add honey if desired.
             
Step 5: Drink the tea daily or as needed to ease constipation symptoms.
