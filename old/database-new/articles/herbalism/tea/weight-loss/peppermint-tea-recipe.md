
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or pot on the stove.
Step 2: Add 1 teaspoon of loose leaf peppermint to a cup or mug.
Step 3: Pour hot water into the cup over the peppermint leaves, making sure they are fully submerged.
Step 4: Let the tea steep for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to fully develop.
Step 5: Remove the peppermint leaves and enjoy your weight loss peppermint tea!
