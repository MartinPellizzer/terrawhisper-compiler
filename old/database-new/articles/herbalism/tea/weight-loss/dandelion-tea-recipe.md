
Step 1: Boil water in a pot on the stove or use an electric kettle.

Step 2: Add 1 teaspoon of loose leaf dandelion greens to a cup or mug.

Step 3: Pour hot water over the dandelions, fill your cup to the top.

Step 4: Steep for 5-7 minutes, depending on how strong you like your tea.

Step 5: Remove the dandelions with a strainer or infuser and sip your tea for weight loss!
