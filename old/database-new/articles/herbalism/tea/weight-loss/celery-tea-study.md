
            Celery is a popular vegetable that has potential positive effects on weight loss, according to multiple studies. In treating or preventing the metabolic syndrome (MetS) and its components, lifestyle adjustment and weight loss are vital roles. Celery contains various phytochemicals such as phenolic acids, flavones, flavonols, antioxidants, vitamin C, beta-carotene, manganese, and celery phthalides. These compounds decrease the activity of proinflammatory cytokines, prevent inflammation, suppress cardiovascular inflammation, increase antioxidant activity, and have blood-pressure-lowering properties. All these mechanisms in which the active ingredients in celery (A. graveolens) have shown potential properties can make it a valuable addition to a weight loss diet or to an overall healthy lifestyle.
