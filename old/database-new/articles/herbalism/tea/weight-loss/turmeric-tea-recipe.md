
Step 1: Boil 8oz of filtered water in a pot or kettle. Remove from heat.

Step 2: Add 1 tsp of turmeric powder and 1 tsp of honey to the boiled water. Mix well.

Step 3: Steep for 5 minutes with 1 cinnamon stick and 1/2 inch ginger slice.

Step 4: Strain into a cup and drink hot.

Step 5: Repeat daily for weight loss.
