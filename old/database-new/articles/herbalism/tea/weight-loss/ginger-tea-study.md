 The study was conducted among overweight women to determine the effects of dietary supplements containing green tea, capsaicin and ginger extracts on weight loss and metabolic profiles. Participants were randomly divided into 2 groups, with group A receiving dietary supplements containing green tea, capsaicin and ginger extracts twice a day for 8 weeks. Compared to placebo, the study found that taking the green tea, capsaicin and ginger co-supplement resulted in significant decreases in weight and body mass index (BMI). Additionally, subjects who received these dietary supplements had significantly decreased serum insulin concentrations and increased quantitative insulin sensitivity check index and plasma glutathione levels. Overall, the study suggests that green tea, capsaicin and ginger co-supplements may have beneficial effects on weight loss and metabolic profiles in overweight women.
