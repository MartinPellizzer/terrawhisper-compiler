
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or on stove top. 2 cups of water per serving.

Step 2: Add 1 tablespoon of chopped celery to the boiling water. Let steep for 5-7 minutes.

Step 3: Remove from heat and strain out the celery pieces.

Step 4: Add 1 teaspoon of honey or lemon juice to taste, as desired.

Step 5: Serve hot and enjoy your delicious, natural celery tea for weight loss.
