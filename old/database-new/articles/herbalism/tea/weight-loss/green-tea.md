
Green tea is known to aid weight loss due to its high levels of antioxidants, specifically catechins. These antioxidants can increase the body's metabolism and boost the burning of fat cells, leading to weight loss. Green tea has been shown to have a thermogenic effect, which means it increases the body's heat production and burns more calories. Additionally, green tea has been linked to decreased appetite and cravings for unhealthy foods, which can contribute to weight loss. However, it is important to note that green tea should be used as part of a healthy and balanced diet and lifestyle for optimal results.
