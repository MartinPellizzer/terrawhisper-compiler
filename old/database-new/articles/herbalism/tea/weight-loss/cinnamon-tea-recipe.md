
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or pot.
            
Step 2: Add a pinch of cinnamon (about 0.5 teaspoon) to the boiling water.
            
Step 3: Steep the cinnamon in the water for about 5-7 minutes.
            
Step 4: Remove the cinnamon from the water and strain it into a cup or mug.
            
Step 5: Add honey or lemon to taste, and enjoy your cinnamon tea for weight loss!
