
1. Boil water: Fill a kettle with cold water and bring it to a boil.
2. Steep tea leaves: Add 1 teaspoon of loose-leaf oolong tea to a teapot or cup. Pour the hot water over the tea leaves and steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on personal preference.
3. Remove tea leaves: Once the tea has steeped to desired strength, remove the tea leaves from the teapot or cup using a strainer or infuser.
4. Add honey (optional): If desired, add 1 teaspoon of honey to the tea for added sweetness and flavor. Stir well to combine.
5. Serve hot: Enjoy your oolong tea for weight loss hot, either on its own or with a slice of lemon or orange for extra flavor.
