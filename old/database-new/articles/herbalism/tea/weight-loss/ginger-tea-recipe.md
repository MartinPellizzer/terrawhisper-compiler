1. Put together 1 teaspoon of freshly grated ginger, 1 teaspoon of honey, and 2 cups of boiling water. Mix well.
2. Pour the mixture into a teapot or heatproof mug. Steep for 5-7 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and strain out the ginger pieces.
4. Stir in lemon juice to taste. Add honey if desired.
5. Sip slowly and enjoy your weight loss ginger tea.
