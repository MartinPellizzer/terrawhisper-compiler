
Step 1: Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a kettle or pot on the stove.

Step 2: Once water is boiled, remove it from heat and add 1 teaspoon of black tea leaves per cup. Stir well to ensure all leaves are submerged. Cover the pot with a lid and let it steep for 3-5 minutes.

Step 3: After steeping, carefully pour the tea into a strainer or infuser to remove the leaves.

Step 4: If desired, add 1 teaspoon of honey per cup to sweeten the tea.

Step 5: Sip your black tea for weight loss and enjoy its delicious flavor.
