 Anxiety is a common mental health concern in which an individual feels worried or concerned about something with an uncertain outcome. Every year, millions of people across the world suffer from anxiety disorders. This problem can negatively impact a person's quality of life by affecting their ability to carry out daily activities, focus on work, and maintain relationships with friends and family. Some of the main causes of anxiety include stress, trauma, genetics, and substance abuse. In addition to the emotional toll it takes, anxiety can also have physical effects on an individual's overall health, such as increased heart rate, high blood pressure, and muscle tension.
