
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or pot.
    Fill the kettle or pot with fresh, cold water and place it on high heat to boil. Once the water reaches boiling point, remove it from heat.
    
Step 2: Steep valerian root and lemon peel in hot water for 5 minutes.
    Add 1 teaspoon of dried valerian root and 1/2 teaspoon of freshly grated lemon peel to a teapot or cup. Pour the boiling water over the ingredients and steep for 5 minutes.
    
Step 3: Strain the tea into a mug.
    After steeping, strain the tea through a fine-mesh strainer into a mug or cup. Discard the valerian root and lemon peel.
    
Step 4: Add honey or lemon to taste.
    If desired, add 1 teaspoon of honey or a squeeze of fresh lemon juice to the tea for added flavor. Stir well to combine.
    
Step 5: Enjoy your valerian tea for anxiety.
    Sip your warm valerian tea slowly and enjoy its calming effects. Drink one cup per day as needed to help manage anxiety symptoms.
