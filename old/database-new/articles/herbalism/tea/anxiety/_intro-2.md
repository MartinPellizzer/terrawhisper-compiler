 Herbal teas are made from natural plants that have been used for centuries to treat various health conditions, including anxiety. Chamomile tea is one of the most popular teas for relaxation and reducing anxiety symptoms. Lavender tea is another option that can help to calm the mind and promote sleep. Peppermint tea can also be helpful in reducing anxiety by soothing an upset stomach and promoting digestion. Valerian root tea is known for its calming effects and can be particularly beneficial for those experiencing severe anxiety or panic attacks.
