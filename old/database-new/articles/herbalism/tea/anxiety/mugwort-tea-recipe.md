
1. Boil water in a kettle or on stove top, just enough to fill your mug.
2. Add 1 tsp dried mugwort to the bottom of your mug.
3. Pour hot water over the mugwort and steep for 5-7 minutes.
4. Remove the mugwort from the tea and add honey or lemon to taste.
5. Drink the tea and allow it to settle in your system. This tea can help alleviate anxiety symptoms and promote relaxation.
