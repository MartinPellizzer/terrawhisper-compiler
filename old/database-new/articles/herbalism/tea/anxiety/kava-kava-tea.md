 Kava kava tea, made from the leaves and stems of the Piper methysticum plant, has been used for centuries in traditional Polynesian medicine to treat anxiety and other psychological disorders. The active compounds in kava kava, including kavalactones and kaavalin, work by binding to certain receptors in the brain that help regulate mood and reduce feelings of stress and anxiety. Additionally, kava kava tea may also have anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects that can further contribute to its calming properties. While more research is needed to fully understand how kava kava works, it has been shown to be safe and effective for many people seeking natural relief from anxiety.
