
Chamomile tea has been used for centuries to treat anxiety and promote relaxation because it contains antioxidants that reduce stress hormones such as cortisol. Drinking chamomile tea can help you feel calmer by interacting with the gamma-aminobutyric acid (GABA) receptors in your brain, which are responsible for reducing anxiety and promoting sleep. Additionally, chamomile tea may also contain apigenin, an antioxidant that binds to certain benzodiazepine receptors in the brain, further reducing anxiety and promoting relaxation. Overall, chamomile tea is a natural remedy that can help alleviate symptoms of anxiety without any harmful side effects.
