## What are the worst herbal teas for anxiety?

While herbal teas are generally considered a natural and calming remedy for anxiety, it's important to be cautious with certain varieties that may have stimulating effects. Teas containing high levels of caffeine, such as green tea or black tea, should be consumed in moderation by individuals prone to anxiety. Caffeine is a central nervous system stimulant that can exacerbate feelings of restlessness and contribute to increased heart rate. Additionally, some herbal teas, like yerba mate or ginseng tea, may have energizing properties that could potentially heighten anxiety symptoms. It's advisable for individuals seeking anxiety relief to opt for caffeine-free herbal teas, such as chamomile, lemon balm, or passionflower, which are known for their calming and soothing effects. Always consult with a healthcare professional if you have concerns about the impact of specific herbal teas on anxiety or if you are taking medications that may interact with herbal remedies.
