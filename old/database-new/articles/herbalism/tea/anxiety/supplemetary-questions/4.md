## What are the best herbal teas for panic attacks?

Chamomile and valerian root teas are often considered beneficial for managing panic attacks. Chamomile has mild sedative properties that can promote relaxation and reduce anxiety. Valerian root, known for its calming effects, can help alleviate nervous tension and promote a sense of tranquility. Both teas are caffeine-free, making them suitable for consumption throughout the day or before bedtime. It's important to note that individual responses to herbal teas may vary, and consulting with a healthcare professional is advisable for those seeking long-term solutions or experiencing severe symptoms.
