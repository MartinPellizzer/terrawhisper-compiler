## What are the best herbal teas for sleep disorders cause by anxiety?

Chamomile, valerian root, and lavender are among the best [herbal teas for sleep disorders](/herbalism/tea/sleep.html) caused by anxiety. Chamomile tea has calming properties that can help reduce anxiety and promote relaxation, making it a popular choice before bedtime. Valerian root tea is known for its sedative effects, helping to ease nervousness and improve sleep quality. Lavender tea not only has a soothing aroma but also contains compounds that can help reduce stress and promote restful sleep. Incorporating these herbal teas into a bedtime routine may help alleviate anxiety-related sleep disturbances and promote a sense of calmness before bedtime.
