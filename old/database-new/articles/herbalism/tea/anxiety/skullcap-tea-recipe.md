
Step 1: Boil water in a kettle or pot.

Step 2: Place 2-3 cups of skullcap herb into a tea infuser or reusable tea bag.

Step 3: Pour the boiled water over the skullcap, making sure all the herb is submerged.

Step 4: Steep for 5-10 minutes, depending on desired strength.

Step 5: Remove the infuser or bag from the tea and add honey or lemon to taste (optional). Sip slowly and enjoy your skullcap tea for anxiety relief!
