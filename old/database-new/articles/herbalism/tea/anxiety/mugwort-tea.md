 Mugwort tea is known for its calming properties due to its alkaloid content, particularly artemisinin. This compound has been shown to have anxiolytic effects in humans and animals, reducing feelings of stress and anxiety. Additionally, mugwort contains terpenoids and other compounds that may contribute to its relaxing properties. Drinking mugwort tea may help regulate the release of neurotransmitters like dopamine and serotonin, which can improve mood and reduce anxiety levels. Overall, mugwort tea is a natural remedy for anxiety that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine.
