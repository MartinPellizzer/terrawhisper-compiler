
Lavender tea has calming properties due to the high linalool content, which is a type of terpene found in lavender plants. Linalool helps reduce anxiety by promoting relaxation and lowering stress levels. When consumed as tea, lavender’s soothing effects can provide a sense of calmness and tranquility, making it an ideal beverage for those experiencing anxiety or stress. Furthermore, the act of sipping tea slows down breathing and heart rate, contributing to a feeling of relaxation. Overall, lavender tea offers a natural remedy for individuals looking to manage their anxiety levels.
