    
Wild lettuce tea contains natural compounds that have calming effects on the body and mind. These compounds, including lactucin and apigenin, interact with GABA receptors in the brain to promote relaxation and reduce anxiety. As a result, wild lettuce tea is often recommended as a natural sleep aid, helping individuals fall asleep faster and enjoy deeper, more restful sleep. Additionally, the tea's anti-inflammatory properties may help alleviate pain and discomfort associated with insomnia or other sleep disturbances. Overall, wild lettuce tea provides a gentle, natural way to improve sleep quality and promote relaxation throughout the day.
