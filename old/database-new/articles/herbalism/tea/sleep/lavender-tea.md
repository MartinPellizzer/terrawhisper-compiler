Lavender tea has calming properties that can help reduce stress and anxiety, making it easier to fall asleep. It also contains Linalool, a chemical compound that has been shown to improve the quality of sleep. Drinking lavender tea before bedtime can promote relaxation and help regulate the body's natural sleep-wake cycle. Additionally, lavender tea may help alleviate symptoms of insomnia and restless leg syndrome, further contributing to its sleep-promoting effects.
