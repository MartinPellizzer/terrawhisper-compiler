 The present study investigated the efficacy and safety of kava special extract WS® 1490 in patients with sleep disturbances associated with anxiety, tension, and restlessness states of non-psychotic origin. A multicenter, randomized, double-blind clinical trial was conducted in which 61 patients received daily doses of 200 mg WS® 1490 or placebo for a period of 4 weeks. The primary efficacy variables were measured by the sleep questionnaire SF-B and the Hamilton Anxiety Scale (HAMA). The results showed that kava extract demonstrated statistically significant superior therapeutic efficacy in improving sleep quality and recuperative effect after sleep, as well as reducing psychic anxiety. Additionally, improvements were noted in self-rating of well-being and global clinical evaluation. Overall, the study concluded that kava extract WS® 1490 is an effective and safe treatment option for sleep disturbances associated with non-psychotic anxiety disorders.
