Sleep disorder, commonly known as insomnia, plagues millions worldwide each year. Approximately 30% of the global population experiences some form of sleep disruption annually, turning what should be a restorative process into a widespread concern. 

The ramifications extend beyond restless nights, negatively impacting overall well-being and diminishing the quality of life for affected individuals. Causes range from stress and lifestyle factors to underlying health issues. This pervasive condition not only disrupts sleep patterns but also gives rise to an array of health concerns, contributing to a domino effect that compromises physical and mental health.

Herbal tea emerges as a promising remedy, harnessing natural properties to alleviate insomnia's grip. The constituents within herbal infusions engage with the body's mechanisms, promoting relaxation and restoring balance. These dynamic elements work synergistically to address the root causes of sleep disturbances, offering a holistic approach to combating insomnia without resorting to pharmaceutical interventions.
