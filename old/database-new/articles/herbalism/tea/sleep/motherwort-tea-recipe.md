
1. Boil water in a kettle or on stove, then remove from heat and pour into a mug.
2. Add 1 tablespoon of dried Motherwort herb to the mug.
3. Steep the herb in the hot water for 5-7 minutes.
4. Strain out the Motherwort herb using a tea strainer or cheesecloth.
5. Sip slowly and enjoy your Motherwort Tea for sleep. Optional sweeteners like honey or lemon can be added to taste.
