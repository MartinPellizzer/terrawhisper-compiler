
The study investigates the effects of hydro-alcoholic extract of Nepeta glomerulosa and its fractions on sleep-prolonging in mice. Results indicate that the extract and n-butanol fraction increased sleep duration, while other fractions had no effect. The LD50 value for Nepeta glomerulosa was found to be 2.4 g/Kg, indicating that it has no toxicity effects on neuronal cells. Therefore, the study suggests that Nepeta glomerulosa could potentiate sleeping behaviors without any cytotoxicity, and the components responsible for this effect are most likely non-polar agents found in n-butanol fraction.
