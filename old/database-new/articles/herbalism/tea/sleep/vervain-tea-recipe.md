
Step 1: Boil 4 cups water in a kettle or pot.
            
Step 2: Gather 1 teaspoon dried vervain flowers, 1 cinnamon stick, 5 whole cloves, and 3 cardamom pods.
            
Step 3: Add ingredients to a tea infuser, place in a teapot or directly into the pot, strain out after steeping for 5 minutes.
            
Step 4: Pour tea into mugs, add honey or lemon if desired.
            
Step 5: Sip slowly and allow tea to help induce relaxation and sleep.
