1) Boil water: fill pot with 8 oz of fresh, cold water and bring to a boil on stove.
        2) Steep catnip leaves: place 1 tablespoon of dried catnip leaves into a tea infuser or a reusable tea bag.
        3) Add honey: stir in 1 teaspoon of honey to sweeten the tea and enhance its calming effects.
        4) Steep for 5-7 minutes: submerge the tea infuser or tea bag into the boiling water, and let it steep for 5-7 minutes.
        5) Remove and serve: remove the tea leaves or tea bag from the water, pour the tea into a cup, add milk if desired, and enjoy before bedtime for sleep.
