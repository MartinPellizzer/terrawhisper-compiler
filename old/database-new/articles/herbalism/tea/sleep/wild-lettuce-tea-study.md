
            The study aimed to investigate the sleep-promoting effects and mechanisms of Heukharang lettuce leaf extract (HLE) in animal models. HLE was found to increase non-rapid eye movement (NREM) and decrease awake time, indicating its potential as a sleep aid. The EEG analysis showed that HLE increased delta waves and sleep duration by 59.5%. Additionally, HLE increased the gene and protein expression of GABAA, GABA type B, and serotonin receptor 1A. However, when the expression levels were checked using antagonists, the effects were similar to NOR, suggesting that the sleep-promoting effects of HLE may be mediated through GABAA receptors.
