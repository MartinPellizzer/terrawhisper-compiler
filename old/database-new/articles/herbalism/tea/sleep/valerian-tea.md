Valerian tea is a herbal drink made from the dried roots of the valerian plant. It has been used for centuries to help people fall asleep faster and stay asleep longer. The exact mechanism behind its sleep-promoting effects is not fully understood, but it is thought to work by increasing levels of a neurotransmitter called GABA in the brain. This can help to calm the nervous system and promote relaxation, making it easier to fall asleep and stay asleep. Additionally, valerian tea may also help to reduce anxiety and stress, which can further contribute to its sleep-promoting effects. Overall, valerian tea is a natural and safe way to improve sleep quality and duration.
