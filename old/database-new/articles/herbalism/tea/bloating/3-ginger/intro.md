Ginger (Zingiber officinale) Tea contains bioactive compounds such as gingerol and shogaol, known for their anti-inflammatory and carminative properties. These constituents aid in alleviating bloating by promoting smooth muscle relaxation in the gastrointestinal tract, reducing gas formation, and facilitating efficient digestion. Ginger Tea's anti-spasmodic effects contribute to relieving discomfort associated with bloating, making it a natural remedy for digestive issues.
