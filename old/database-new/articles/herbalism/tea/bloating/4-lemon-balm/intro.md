Lemon balm (Melissa officinalis) tea contains compounds such as polyphenols and terpenes that exhibit carminative properties, effectively alleviating bloating. The herb's essential oils, particularly citronellal and citral, contribute to smooth muscle relaxation in the gastrointestinal tract, reducing gas and bloating. Lemon balm's anti-spasmodic effects on the digestive system further enhance its efficacy in relieving abdominal discomfort associated with bloating.
