Peppermint (Mentha x piperita) Tea contains active compounds such as menthol, carvone, and limonene, which possess antispasmodic properties. These constituents relax the muscles of the gastrointestinal tract, alleviating bloating and gas. Additionally, peppermint tea helps stimulate the flow of bile, aiding in digestion. Its anti-inflammatory attributes contribute to the relief of bloating discomfort by reducing inflammation in the digestive tract.
