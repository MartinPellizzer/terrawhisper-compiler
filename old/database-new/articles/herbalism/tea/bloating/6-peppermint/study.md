According to a study published by the Phytomedicine Journal, peppermint oil (PO) has demonstrated effectiveness in alleviating symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), including bloating and abdominal distension. The antispasmodic action of PO, attributed to its main component menthol, acts as a calcium antagonist, providing relief from discomfort associated with IBS. Clinical trials revealed statistically significant effects favoring PO over placebo, with an average response rate of 58%, making it a potential first-choice drug for IBS patients with non-serious constipation or diarrhea.
