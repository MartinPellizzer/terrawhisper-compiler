Anise (Pimpinella anisum) Tea contains compounds such as anethole and carvone, which exhibit anti-spasmodic properties, aiding in the relief of bloating. These constituents contribute to the relaxation of the gastrointestinal tract muscles, minimizing gas and bloating. Anise Tea also possesses mild diuretic properties, promoting the elimination of excess fluids. Additionally, its carminative effects help alleviate digestive discomfort associated with bloating by facilitating the expulsion of gas from the digestive system.
