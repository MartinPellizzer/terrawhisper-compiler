According to a study published by the Asian Pacific Journal of Tropical Disease, licorice root, scientifically known for its rich composition including acid-2-beta-glycyrrhizic, glycyrrhetinic acid, and various flavonoids, demonstrates promising benefits for bloating. In Iran, licorice-based formulations like "Reglis" tablets are utilized to treat gastric and duodenal bloating, excess acid secretion, and gastric distention. The study highlights licorice's indigenous medicinal significance, supporting its potential as a therapeutic remedy for various health issues beyond its traditional uses.
