Fennel (Foeniculum vulgare) Tea contains volatile oils, such as anethole, which exhibit carminative properties. These oils facilitate the relaxation of gastrointestinal smooth muscles, reducing gas and bloating. Fennel's anti-spasmodic effects help alleviate digestive discomfort by preventing involuntary contractions. Additionally, it stimulates the production of gastric juices, aiding in digestion. The herb's ability to reduce inflammation and act as a mild diuretic further contributes to its efficacy in relieving bloating.
