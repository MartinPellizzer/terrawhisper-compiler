According to a study published by the Molecules Journal, dandelion (Taraxacum officinale) may be beneficial for bloating due to its potential to inhibit α-amylase and α-glucosidase, key enzymes in carbohydrate metabolism. The inhibition of these enzymes is considered a novel strategy for managing postprandial hyperglycemia associated with diabetes. Unlike some allopathic drugs used for this purpose, dandelion's natural properties may offer relief without the side effects commonly associated with them, including bloating.
