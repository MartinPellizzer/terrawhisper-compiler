Dandelion (Taraxacum officinale) Tea contains bioactive compounds, including flavonoids, terpenoids, and sesquiterpene lactones. These constituents exhibit diuretic properties, promoting increased urine production and reducing water retention. Additionally, dandelion tea stimulates bile production, aiding digestion and alleviating bloating. Its natural compounds contribute to the herb's ability to act as a mild laxative, facilitating bowel movements and further mitigating bloating issues.
