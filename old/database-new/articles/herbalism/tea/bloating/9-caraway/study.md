According to a study published by the Natural Products and Bioprospecting Publication, caraway (Carum carvi) has been identified as beneficial for alleviating bloating and gastro-intestinal complaints. The recommended dosage is 0.15–0.3 mL of caraway essential oil, corresponding to 273 mg of caraway oil. Traditionally used for digestive disorders, caraway's carminative properties help relieve symptoms like bloating and flatulence. Additionally, caraway has a long history in various cultures for treating ailments such as anemia, colic, and digestive issues, demonstrating its efficacy in promoting digestive well-being.
