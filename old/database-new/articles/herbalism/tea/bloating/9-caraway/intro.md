Caraway (Carum carvi) Tea contains carvol and limonene, which possess anti-spasmodic properties, aiding in the relaxation of gastrointestinal muscles. The tea's carminative effect facilitates the expulsion of gas, alleviating bloating. Caraway also harbors flavonoids and volatile oils, promoting digestive enzyme secretion and reducing inflammation. These constituents collectively contribute to the herb's efficacy in addressing bloating concerns.
