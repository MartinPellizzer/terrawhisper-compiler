According to a study published by the Iran Red Crescent Med Journal, dill (Anethum graveolens L.) emerges as a significant herbal remedy for bloating. Traditional Persian medicine highlights dill's efficacy in improving digestion, preventing stomach weakness, and eliminating flatulence. Modern research corroborates these claims, especially noting dill's carminative volatile oil, which aids digestion, relieves gas, and even soothes colic in babies. Dill water, recommended historically and in contemporary contexts, provides a soothing effect for gastrointestinal discomfort.
