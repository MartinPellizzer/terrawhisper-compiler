Coriander (Coriandrum sativum) Tea contains bioactive compounds, including linalool and borneol, known for their carminative properties. These compounds aid in relaxing gastrointestinal muscles, reducing gas, and alleviating bloating. Additionally, coriander tea possesses anti-spasmodic effects, preventing muscle contractions that contribute to bloating. Its digestive benefits are attributed to the herb's ability to stimulate enzyme secretion, promoting efficient digestion and relieving discomfort associated with bloating.
