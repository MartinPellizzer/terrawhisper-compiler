According to a study published by the Journal of Traditional Chinese Medical Sciences, fenugreek seeds have demonstrated potential benefits in reducing bloating. The research reveals that fenugreek's soluble fibers form a mucilage in the presence of gastric acids, covering the gastric mucosa and reducing direct contact of gastric contents with the stomach wall. Additionally, fenugreek seed proteins form a complex with stomach acids, effectively reducing bloating. While the study acknowledges the need for further investigation, it suggests that fenugreek seeds may offer a safe and promising approach to prevent and repair mucosal lesions associated with gastrointestinal issues.
