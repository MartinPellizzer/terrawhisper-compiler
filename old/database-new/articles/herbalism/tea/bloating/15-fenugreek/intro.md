Fenugreek (Trigonella foenum graecum) Tea contains soluble fiber and mucilage, which aid digestion by alleviating constipation and promoting bowel regularity. The tea's anti-inflammatory properties help reduce bloating, while its carminative effects assist in expelling gas from the digestive tract. Additionally, fenugreek's compounds stimulate digestive enzymes, enhancing the overall digestive process and minimizing abdominal discomfort associated with bloating.
