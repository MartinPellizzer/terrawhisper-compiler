
According to a study published by the Industrial Crops and Products Publication, basil (Ocimum bacilicum) has been identified as a valuable herb with diverse morphological varieties. The study, referencing Labra et al. (2004), Telci et al. (2006), and Khalid et al. (2006), highlights basil's historical use in treating various ailments, including digestive system disorders. The active ingredients in basil, consumed as appetizers, are reported to be effective in treating bloating and improving digestion. This makes basil a promising natural remedy for individuals seeking relief from gastrointestinal discomfort.
