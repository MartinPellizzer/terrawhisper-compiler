Cardamom (Elettaria cardamomum) Tea contains volatile oils, namely terpinene and cineol, which exhibit carminative properties, aiding in the relief of bloating. These compounds facilitate the expulsion of gas from the gastrointestinal tract, reducing abdominal discomfort. Additionally, cardamom possesses anti-inflammatory characteristics, further contributing to its efficacy in alleviating bloating. Its digestive benefits make it a valuable addition to alleviate gastrointestinal distress associated with excess gas accumulation.
