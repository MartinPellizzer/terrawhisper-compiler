According to a study published by the Scholars Research Library, chamomile extract has shown promise in alleviating symptoms associated with Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS). In a randomized trial involving 45 patients meeting ROOM III criteria, participants who took 20 drops of chamomile daily for four weeks experienced a significant reduction in IBS symptoms, including abdominal pain intensity, bloating, nausea, stool consistency, and altered bowel habits. The positive effects persisted for up to two weeks after the intervention, suggesting chamomile's potential in reducing bloating.
