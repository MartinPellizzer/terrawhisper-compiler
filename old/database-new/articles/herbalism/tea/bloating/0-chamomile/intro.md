Chamomile (Chamomilla recutita) Tea contains anti-inflammatory compounds such as chamazulene and bisabolol. These constituents inhibit the release of inflammatory mediators, alleviating bloating by reducing intestinal inflammation. Additionally, chamomile tea possesses carminative properties that relax the gastrointestinal muscles, easing gas expulsion. Its flavonoids contribute to smooth muscle relaxation, further aiding digestion. The combination of anti-inflammatory and carminative effects makes chamomile tea an effective remedy for bloating.
