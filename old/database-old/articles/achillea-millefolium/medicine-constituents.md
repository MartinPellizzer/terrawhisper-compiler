Yarrow has several medicinal constituents, such as flavonoids, volatile oils, iridoid glycosides. Flavonoids are plant pigments that give colour and protect plants against ultraviolet rays and also provide health benefits for humans including reduced inflammation. Volatile oils play a vital role in the aroma of yarrow, and their properties have antiseptic qualities, promoting wound healing. Iridoid glycosides are a group of bitter substances which possess anti-inflammatory as well as antibacterial capabilities. All these elements make yarrow an effective herbal remedy.
