Avocado has many medicinal preparations, such as tea, oil extracts, creams, juices, pastes and even more. Each type can help improve one's health in different ways. These are some of the most common forms of avocado-derived medical treatments.

 Made from dried or fresh avocado leaves, this tea is rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce inflammation, relieve pain, and improve overall health. Drinking it regularly may even boost the immune system and promote better digestion.

 Avocados offer significant amounts of healthy fats, such as oleic acid and linoleic acid, both vital for a healthy heart. The oil extracted from avocado fruit is ideal to enhance overall skin health, prevent hair loss, and even help with weight management. It can be used on the scalp or directly applied to the skin for better results.

 Avocado creams often include soothing and moisturizing properties that make them perfect for skincare. The nutrients in avocados promote healthier, younger-looking skin by reducing wrinkles, fading dark spots, and alleviating dryness or itchiness. They may even help treat eczema, psoriasis, and other inflammatory skin conditions.

 Making use of the fruit's nutrient-rich pulp and flesh, avocado juice provides essential vitamins and minerals like folic acid, vitamin B6, potassium, fiber, and more. Drinking this juice on a daily basis could offer various health benefits, including improved digestion, a strong immune system, and even better heart health.

 Similar to creams, avocado pastes offer beneficial properties for the skin. Applying them directly to the face can help reduce acne and other blemishes due to the high amounts of vitamins A, D, and E present in avocados. These nutrients work together to improve overall complexion while promoting healthier, smoother skin.

 Brewing an infusion using fresh or dried leaves provides another way to take advantage of avocado's medicinal properties. The infusion's flavonoids and antioxidants support heart health, lower blood pressure, and even help with weight loss by reducing one's appetite.

 Avocado salves are a popular choice for treating skin problems due to their moisturizing effects and healing capabilities. These topical applications can help reduce the appearance of scars and blemishes while improving the skin's overall texture and health.

 A serum made from avocados is often combined with other essential oils or ingredients, offering even more benefits to the skin. The combination of nutrients helps nourish the skin and protect it against damage caused by free radicals while encouraging new cell growth for a youthful appearance.

 An avocado poultice is created by applying mashed avocado directly to affected areas, such as sunburn or insect bites. The high fat content can provide relief from pain and discomfort while also promoting healing.

 Lastly, mixing an avocado with other ingredients like honey or yogurt creates a face mask that cleanses and moisturizes the skin. This combination leaves it feeling refreshed and nourished.
