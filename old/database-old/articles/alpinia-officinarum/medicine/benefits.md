Its antibacterial properties can also support joint health by eliminating bacteria or infection which might contribute to issues with these parts of the body.

Furthermore, this herb is believed to increase energy levels by stimulating blood circulation, increasing the delivery of oxygen and nutrients throughout the body. This can help promote an overall sense of well-being and vitality. 

         Another benefit associated with galangal is its potential role in aiding weight loss efforts. By boosting metabolism, it helps burn fat more efficiently. Additionally, this herb has been traditionally used to improve cognitive function due to its content of compounds that can enhance brain cell communication and memory retention. 

         As mentioned previously, galangal is also known for its effect in preventing nausea and vomiting. This can be attributed to the presence of various essential oils found within this herb, which may relax muscles involved in the process of vomiting and thus relieve symptoms associated with motion sickness or morning sickness experienced by pregnant women.
