galangal has many medicinal constituents, such as Volatile oils, Gingerols, Galangin, Shogaols, Chromones, Quercetin, Campesterol, Beta-sitosterol, Caffeic acid, and Kaempferol. These natural components contribute to its healing properties for many health issues. 

            The volatile oils help improve digestion and relieve nausea while gingerols are anti-inflammatory agents that can reduce pain and swelling. Galangin is a powerful antioxidant, protecting the body from free radicals which cause cell damage. Shogaols enhance circulation by promoting blood flow and prevent blood clots. Chromones have antibacterial properties, combating harmful bacteria in the body. Quercetin, on the other hand, is an essential phytochemical known to boost immunity and protect against diseases. 

            Campesterol and Beta-sitosterol are compounds that aid in maintaining healthy cholesterol levels, reducing risk of heart disease. Caffeic acid serves as a potent antioxidant and anti-carcinogen agent to combat cancerous cells, while Kaempferol provides anti-allergic benefits and acts as an antiviral agent. Overall, galangal proves to be an incredible herb for enhancing health due to these numerous beneficial compounds it contains.
