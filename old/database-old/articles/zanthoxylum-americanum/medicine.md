Additionally it contains Vitamin C which plays crucial roles as an antioxidant to scavenge free radicals protecting against oxidative damage of tissues; essential oils have anti-inflammatory qualities while its phenolic components possess significant antiseptic, analgesic and laxatives effects making Zanthoxylum americanum a multi functional medicinal plant.

 Commonly used parts for extraction include the bark (inner or outer) roots(fresh), seeds/seedpods containing its active ingredients like coumarins; hydrocarbons, volatile oils among others essential elements responsible to stimulating health improving action within human body systems. Medicinal preparations made from these components may be taken internally through tea infusions as traditional treatments for diarrhea and gastric ulcer diseases or applied topically using salves & liniments that possess insecticide properties useful against fleas, ticks on skin; easing itching sensitivities while offering relief to affected individuals suffering from minor dermatological conditions.

 Although Prickly Ash has many benefits there are possible side effects associated with the overuse or misapplications of this herb due mainly its potent chemical compounds present in different parts; including allergic reactions ranging between mild such as itchy skin, runny nose and water eyes through severe like anaphylaxis potentially life-threatening conditions which requires immediate medical assistance should any signs appear. Furthermore long term usage may lead to gastrointestinal issues or liver damage hence regular monitoring is necessary for safe consumption of this herb remedy.

 To ensure safety and efficacy, follow prescribed instructions from your healthcare professional when using Prickly Ash preparations as alternative medicines; be cautious around the contraindications such allergies or underlying health conditions that may react adversely with its properties either through interactions between medications being taken concurrently - so it's advisable to consult before use if pregnant, breastfeeding mothers and individuals taking certain prescription drugs should also seek medical advice prior incorporating Prickley Ash remedial treatments into their daily routines.
