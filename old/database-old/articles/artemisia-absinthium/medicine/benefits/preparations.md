
There are many preparations and uses of Wormwood, such as teas, tinctures, and capsules. Wormwood tea, made by steeping the dried leaves, is popular for digestive issues and as a tonic. Tinctures, created by macerating the herb in alcohol, are utilized to stimulate appetite and aid in digestion. Capsules containing powdered Wormwood are commonly used in alternative medicine to address parasites and support gut health due to its purported antimicrobial properties. Additionally, Wormwood extracts are sometimes employed in topical applications for skin conditions like eczema and insect bites.
