Sweet Annie has many medicinal constituents, such as Artemisinin, Dihydroartemisinic Acid, Artemisinine, Artemetin, Arteannuin A, Arteannuin B, Deoxyarthmetin, Deoxyartemetin, Artemiflavone, Cynaropicrin. These components help to improve health in several ways. They possess strong anti-malaria properties and can be used for the treatment of malaria. 

They play a vital role in combating bacteria, especially those that cause food poisoning or infect wounds. Sweet Annie has anti-bacterial actions which are effective against various pathogens like Staphylococcus aureus, Escherichia coli, and Salmonella enterica. 

The same plant also contains antifungal properties that help to fight off fungal infections. These include Candida albicans, which is responsible for yeast infections or other opportunistic infections that can affect the skin, mouth, gut and genitals. 

Sweet Annie aids in boosting immune system function by enhancing cellular health through its antioxidant activity. The rich collection of antioxidants found in this plant can neutralize free radicals which cause damage to cells and contribute to chronic illnesses or premature aging. 

Moreover, Sweet Annie is known for its ability to reduce inflammation throughout the body, particularly in cases of arthritis, rheumatism, and gout. Its anti-inflammatory constituents help calm joint pain and swelling associated with these conditions. 

Lastly, this plant has been used traditionally for its anticancer properties, though more research is needed to fully understand their mechanism of action. The presence of Artemisia herbs in Chinese medicine shows potential benefits against cancer and cellular damage.
