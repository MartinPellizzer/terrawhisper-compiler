Sweet Flag has several medicinal constituents, such as calamus oil, safrole, asarone, camphene, borneol. Calamus oil is renowned for its use in treating anxiety and stress, while safrole aids in digestion and reduces inflammation. Asarone can be beneficial in relieving pain and boosting cognitive function, and camphene may help with respiratory problems and coughs. Lastly, borneol is utilized to enhance blood circulation and ease muscle tension. These components work together to provide a comprehensive medicinal effect when using Sweet Flag.
