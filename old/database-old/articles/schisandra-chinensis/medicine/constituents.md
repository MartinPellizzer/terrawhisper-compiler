Schisandra has many medicinal constituents, such as Schisandrins, Gomisins, Schizanderols and others. The first group is known for its Schisandrin A and Schisandrin B constituents. These two are major flavonoid lignans with potential health benefits like antioxidant properties and immune system enhancement. 

Next comes the Gomisins family, including Gomisin A and Gomisin C. As their structures are similar to those of Schisandrins, they also possess these vital capabilities to a lesser extent. They are thought to have anti-inflammatory benefits alongside potential effects on blood sugar levels. 

The third significant family is the Schizanderols containing molecules such as Schisantherin A and Schisantherin B. These molecules display antioxidant activities and are also considered as having antidepressant effects. They have been shown to potentially support neurological functions. Furthermore, they might play a role in maintaining heart health by reducing blood fat concentrations. 

Schizandrin is another important constituent of Schisandra, which is a type of dibenzocyclo-octadiene lignan. It also demonstrates potent antioxidant properties and can protect the liver from damage caused by toxins. Additionally, it may be useful in treating certain types of inflammation. 

Deoxyschizandrin is a derivative of Schizandrin containing lesser double bonds compared to its parent compound. It possesses similar properties to Schizandrin but with slightly different effects. They are both considered effective antioxidants and might aid in liver protection. 

Lastly, Chlorogenic acid is a phenolic compound that exhibits strong antioxidant capacities too and serves the same purposes as other constituents - boosting immunity, protecting against oxidative stress, and potentially supporting heart health. 

In summary, Schisandra's medicinal value lies in its extensive list of important bioactive compounds such as Schisandrins, Gomisins, Schizanderols, Schizandrin/Deoxyschizandrin, and Chlorogenic acid. These constituents provide antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, immunity-enhancing, blood sugar controlling, heart health-promoting, liver protection, and antidepressant effects.
