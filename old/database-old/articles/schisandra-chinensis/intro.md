 "Schisandra (Scientifically known as Schisandra chinensis) is a unique medicinal flowering shrub with culinary uses that can be found in parts like China or Korea where it has been cultivated for centuries." It contains numerous vitamins, phytonutrients and antioxidants which contribute to its wide-ranging healing abilities. Apart from being used as an immunity booster due to the presence of Vitamin C & E in high quantity , these berries are also believed by many practitioners to be helpful for people suffering liver disease, diabetes or any respiratory issues such as Asthma and bronchitis making this remarkable plant highly significant with various medicinal applications.
