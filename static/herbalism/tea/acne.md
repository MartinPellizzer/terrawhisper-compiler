# 15 Best Herbal Teas For Acne

![Herbal Tea For Acne](/images/herbal-tea-for-acne.jpg)

Acne is a common skin condition that occurs when sebaceous glands produce too much oil, which blocks hair follicles and causes inflammation and breakouts.

It affects millions of people worldwide every year, with statistics estimating that around 65% of the population will experience acne at some point in their lives. Acne can negatively affect a person's quality of life, causing embarrassment and social anxiety, as well as potentially leading to other health issues such as depression and low self-esteem.

The main causes of acne include hormones, genetics, stress, diet, and certain medications..

Herbal teas have long been used as natural remedies to treat skin conditions such as acne.

They are packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals that can help reduce inflammation, promote healing, and improve overall skin health. Some popular herbs for acne-prone skin include chamomile, lavender, and peppermint. Drinking these teas can help soothe irritated skin, calm redness, and minimize the appearance of blemishes. Additionally, many herbal teas contain antibacterial properties that can help combat the bacteria that causes acne breakouts.

Overall, herbal teas are a gentle, effective way to treat acne from the inside out..

In this article, you'll learn which are the best herbal teas for acne, why they works, and how to make them.
