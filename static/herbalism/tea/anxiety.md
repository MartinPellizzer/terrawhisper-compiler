# 13 Best Herbal Teas For Anxiety

![Herbal Tea For Anxiety](/images/herbal-tea-for-anxiety.jpg)

 Anxiety is a common mental health concern in which an individual feels worried or concerned about something with an uncertain outcome. Every year, millions of people across the world suffer from anxiety disorders. This problem can negatively impact a person's quality of life by affecting their ability to carry out daily activities, focus on work, and maintain relationships with friends and family. Some of the main causes of anxiety include stress, trauma, genetics, and substance abuse. In addition to the emotional toll it takes, anxiety can also have physical effects on an individual's overall health, such as increased heart rate, high blood pressure, and muscle tension.

 Herbal teas are made from natural plants that have been used for centuries to treat various health conditions, including anxiety. Chamomile tea is one of the most popular teas for relaxation and reducing anxiety symptoms. Lavender tea is another option that can help to calm the mind and promote sleep. Peppermint tea can also be helpful in reducing anxiety by soothing an upset stomach and promoting digestion. Valerian root tea is known for its calming effects and can be particularly beneficial for those experiencing severe anxiety or panic attacks. Rooibos tea, which is made from the leaves of the rooibos plant, has been shown to have anti-anxiety properties and can help to reduce stress and promote relaxation.
