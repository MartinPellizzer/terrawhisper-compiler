# 11 Best Herbal Teas For Acid Reflux

![Herbal Tea For Acid Reflux](/images/herbal-tea-for-acid-reflux.jpg)

 Acid reflux is a medical condition in which stomach acid flows back into the esophagus. This happens to millions of people every year. The symptoms can be severe and can negatively affect their quality of life. The main causes of acid reflux include food choices, smoking, alcohol consumption, obesity, and stress. Acid reflux can also lead to other health problems such as asthma, heartburn, and lung cancer.

 Herbal tea is a popular natural remedy for acid reflux symptoms. Made from a blend of various herbs, spices and flowers, these teas have long been used to soothe the digestive tract, reduce inflammation, and promote relaxation. Chamomile tea, ginger tea, and licorice root tea are some of the most commonly recommended for acid reflux relief. Drinking herbal tea can provide a safe and effective way to manage acid reflux symptoms without the need for medication.

In this article, you'll learn which are the best herbal teas for acid reflux. You'll also find ascientific studies that prove the effectiveness of the herbs and easy to make recipes to make the medicinal herbal teas.

Additionally, marshmallow root is rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds that can help to reduce inflammation and irritation in the digestive system, further aiding in the prevention and relief of acid reflux symptoms.

It also promotes relaxation of smooth muscles in the esophagus and stomach which helps to reduce acid production.
