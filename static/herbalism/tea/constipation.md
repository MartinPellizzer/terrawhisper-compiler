# 11 Best Herbal Teas For Constipation

![Herbal Tea For Constipation](/images/herbal-tea-for-constipation.jpg)

 Constipation is a condition in which bowel movements occur less frequently than normal, or when stools are hard, dry, and difficult to pass. It is estimated that 20 million people worldwide suffer from constipation every year. This problem can negatively affect the quality of life of those affected, leading to discomfort, pain, and reduced productivity. The main causes of constipation include age-related changes in bowel habits, a diet low in fiber, lack of physical activity, stress, and certain medical conditions such as irritable bowel syndrome. Constipation can also lead to other health problems such as hemorrhoids, anal fissures, and colon cancer.

 Herbal teas can be an effective remedy for constipation, as many of them contain ingredients that promote digestion and encourage bowel movements. Peppermint tea, chamomile tea, ginger tea, and senna tea are all popular choices for their ability to relieve constipation. Drinking these teas can help to soothe the digestive system, reduce bloating and gas, and stimulate the appetite, which can all contribute to improved bowel function. Additionally, herbal teas are generally gentle on the body and can be enjoyed at any time of day, making them a convenient and natural choice for treating constipation.

In this article, you'll learn which are the best herbal teas for constipation. You'll also find ascientific studies that prove the effectiveness of the herbs and easy to make recipes to make the medicinal herbal teas.
