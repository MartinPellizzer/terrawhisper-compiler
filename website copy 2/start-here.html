How to get started with herbal healing and herbalism (the fast way)?

If you reading this, chances are you fall into one of the 2 following categories:

  1. You want to alleviate a health problem
  2. You want to learn herbalism

If you actually fall into one of the above categories, read the corresponding section down below to learn the fastest way to get to your goal.

If you don't fall into one of the above categories, maybe TerraWhisper is not for you. Still, feel free to browse around if you want.

How to get started if you want to alleviate a health problem?

This is the most common reason why people read articles here at TerraWhisper. You have a condition, ailment, or simply a symptom that's bothering you quite a lot, and you want to get rid of it for good.

In this case, visit the conditions page (currently under construction).

In this page, you'll find a list of health related problems that medicinal herbs help relieving.

These health problems are categorized by body systems for easier navigation. For example, if you have a gut related issue, go to the digestive system section. If you have a cardiovascular related issue, go to the cardiovascular system section. And so on.

Once you locate your problem, click the corresponding link (if available) to browse to that problem page, where you'll find a description about the problem, what are it's causes, and how to use herbal medicine to ease it.

More conditions and symptoms are added on a daily basis. If your issue is not present today, it may be present tomorrow, so visit this page regularly for updates.

How to get started if you want to know what learn herbalism?

If you don't have a specific condition that you want to heal, but you simply want to learn how to improve your general physical, mental, and spiritual health by practicing herbalism, visit the herbalism page.

In this page you'll discover what herbalism is and what are the primary conditions it helps relieving. Also it teaches you what are the most commonly used herbs, preparations, and tools.

Just to be clear, this section is meant to be a quick start guide on herbalism and it does not promise you that you'll become a herbalist or herbal practictioner. Learning the information provided in this section does NOT give you any legal rights to practice herbal medicine on other people.
