The small, red or orange berries of Lycium chinense are rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, making them a valuable ingredient in many dietary supplements and health products. Lycium chinense thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, and it can grow up to 12 feet tall in ideal conditions.
