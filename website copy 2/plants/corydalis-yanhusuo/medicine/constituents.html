10 Best Active Constituents of Corydalis yanhusuo (Corydalis)

Corydaline, on the other hand, has been shown to have a calming effect on the central nervous system and has been used to treat anxiety and insomnia. Additionally, both DL-THB and corydaline have been found to have a positive effect on the cardiovascular system, potentially lowering blood pressure and reducing the risk of heart disease.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the medicinal properties of Corydalis yanhusuo and its active constituents.
