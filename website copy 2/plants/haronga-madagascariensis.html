Haronga madagascariensis thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny or partially shaded location. It is a perennial plant that can grow up to 2 meters tall and has glossy, dark green leaves and funnel-shaped flowers that can be pink, red, or white.

The plant is drought-tolerant and can be propagated through cuttings or seeds.
