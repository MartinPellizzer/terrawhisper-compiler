The plant prefers moist, shady conditions and can often be found growing near rivers and streams. Scrophularia nodosa has a fibrous root system and produces small, reddish-purple flowers that bloom in the summer.

The plant contains various chemical compounds, such as iridoid glycosides and phenolic acids, which are believed to be responsible for its medicinal properties.
