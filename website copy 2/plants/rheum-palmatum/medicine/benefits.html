10 Best Health Benefits of Rheum palmatum (Chinese Rhubarb)

Rheum palmatum, also known as Turkish rhubarb, has a long history of use in traditional medicine.

One of the most well-known medicinal benefits of Rheum palmatum is its ability to act as a potent laxative. The roots of the plant contain anthraquinones, which stimulate the intestines and increase bowel movements.

Additionally, Rheum palmatum has been found to have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, making it useful in the treatment of conditions such as arthritis and muscle pain. The plant also contains antioxidants, which can help to protect the body against damage from free radicals. Furthermore, some studies have suggested that Rheum palmatum may have anti-cancer properties, although more research is needed in this area.

However, it is important to note that Rheum palmatum can have side effects, including stomach upset and electrolyte imbalances, and should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.

1. Alleviates constipation

Chinese Rhubarb alleviates constipation, meaning it can help promote and regularize bowel movements.

The main active constituents of Rheum palmatum that contribute to this benefit are anthraquinones, such as emodin and aloe-emodin, which have a laxative effect. The primary parts of the Rheum palmatum plant that provide this benefit are the roots and rhizomes, which contain the highest concentration of anthraquinones. The main medicinal preparations of Rheum palmatum that give this benefit include dried root slices, powder, decoctions, and extracts.

This benefit of Rheum palmatum helps relieve constipation, irregular bowel movements, and digestive disorders, making it a popular natural remedy for promoting gut health.
