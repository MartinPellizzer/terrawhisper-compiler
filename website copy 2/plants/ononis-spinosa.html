What to know before using Ononis spinosa (Spiny Restharrow)?

Ononis spinosa, also known as spiny restharrow, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It has several medicinal properties, including potential anti-inflammatory and diuretic effects, and has been used in traditional medicine to treat conditions such as arthritis and kidney ailments. Ononis spinosa prefers well-drained soil and full sun, and can be found growing in grasslands, scrubland, and rocky areas. The plant features pink or white flowers and small, spiny leaves, and can grow up to 30 inches tall.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which also includes beans and peas.
