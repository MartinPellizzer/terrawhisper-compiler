10 Best Health Benefits of Paeonia lactiflora (White Peony)

Paeonia lactiflora, also known as the Chinese peony, is a plant that has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries due to its medicinal benefits.

It contains compounds such as paeoniflorin and paederagenin, which have been shown to have anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and antioxidant properties. Research has suggested that Paeonia lactiflora may be effective in treating various health conditions, including menstrual disorders, muscle spasms, and even certain types of cancer. Additionally, it has been shown to have a calming effect on the nervous system, making it a potential natural remedy for anxiety and insomnia.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen.

1. Reduces inflammation

White Peony, or Paeonia lactiflora, reduces inflammation, meaning it decreases redness, swelling, and pain caused by tissue damage or infection.

The main active constituents of White Peony responsible for this benefit are paeoniflorin, paebootrin, and tetragalloyl glucose. The primary parts of the Paeonia lactiflora plant that contain these anti-inflammatory compounds are the root and rhizome. Medicinal preparations of White Peony that have been shown to reduce inflammation include decoctions, powdered extracts, and tinctures.

This benefit of Paeonia lactiflora helps relieve various health conditions, such as rheumatoid arthritis, systemic lupus erythematosus, and skin inflammation.
