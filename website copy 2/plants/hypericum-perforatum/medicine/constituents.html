10 Best Active Constituents of Hypericum perforatum (St. John’S Wort)

Hypericum perforatum, commonly known as St.

John's Wort, contains several active constituents that have been studied for their medicinal properties. The most notable of these compounds include hypericin and hyperforin, which are believed to be responsible for the herb's antidepressant and antiviral effects. Additionally, flavonoids, tannins, and essential oils found in St.

John's Wort also contribute to its medicinal value. Clinical studies have shown that St. John's Wort extracts can be effective in treating mild to moderate depression, and it has also been used traditionally to treat wounds, burns, and various skin conditions. However, it is important to note that St.

John's Wort can interact with a number of medications, including antidepressants, birth control pills, and HIV medications, and it should only be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
