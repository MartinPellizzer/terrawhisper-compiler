It is an annual herb with a prostrate growth habit, and it has opposite, sessile, oblong-spathulate leaves that are green in color and have a rough texture. This plant prefers moist, well-drained soil and can be found growing in disturbed areas, roadsides, and fields.

The medicinal properties of Eclipta prostrata include hepatoprotective, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant activities. It is also used to treat a variety of ailments such as liver disorders, skin diseases, and hair loss.
