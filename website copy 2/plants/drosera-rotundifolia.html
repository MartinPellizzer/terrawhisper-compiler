What to know before using Drosera rotundifolia (Sundew)?

Drosera rotundifolia, also known as round-leaved sundew, is a carnivorous plant species well-known for its medicinal properties.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis, asthma, and whooping cough, due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. Drosera rotundifolia thrives in wet, nutrient-poor soil, and requires high levels of sunlight, making it an excellent candidate for bog gardens or greenhouse cultivation.

Its botanical characteristics include a rosette of round, reddish leaves adorned with sticky, glandular hairs that trap and digest insects for additional nutrients.
