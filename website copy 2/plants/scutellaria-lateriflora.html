What to know before using Scutellaria lateriflora (Skullcap)?

Scutellaria lateriflora, also known as skullcap, is a hardy perennial herb native to North America.

It is primarily cultivated in well-drained soil and partial shade, and can grow up to three feet tall. The plant is known for its medicinal properties, including its ability to alleviate anxiety, promote relaxation, and improve sleep quality. Scutellaria lateriflora has a distinctive appearance, with pairs of opposite, lance-shaped leaves and small blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer.

The plant's above-ground parts are used in herbal medicine, and it is often prepared as a tea or tincture.
