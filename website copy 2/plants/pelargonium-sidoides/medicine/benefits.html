10 Best Health Benefits of Pelargonium sidoides (Pelargonium)

Pelargonium sidoides, also known as South African geranium, has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to treat a variety of ailments.

One of its most well-known medicinal benefits is its ability to alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections such as bronchitis, due to its anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties. Additionally, it has been shown to boost the immune system, helping the body to fight off infections more effectively.

Pelargonium sidoides has also been found to be effective in treating digestive issues such as irritable bowel syndrome and gastritis, as it has been shown to reduce inflammation in the digestive tract. Furthermore, it has been used to alleviate symptoms of pain and inflammation in conditions such as osteoarthritis and rheumatoid arthritis.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new supplement regimen.
