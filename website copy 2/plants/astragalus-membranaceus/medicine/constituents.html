10 Best Active Constituents of Astragalus membranaceus (Astragalus)

Astragalus membranaceus, also known as milkvetch, is a plant that has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.

The active constituents of Astragalus membranaceus include saponins, flavonoids, and polysaccharides, which are believed to have medicinal properties. One of the key saponins, astragaloside IV, has been studied for its potential to stimulate the immune system and protect against cell damage.

Flavonoids in Astragalus membranaceus, such as formononetin and calycosin, have been researched for their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects. The polysaccharides in the plant have also been of interest to researchers, who have found that they may have immunomodulatory and anti-tumor properties.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the medicinal benefits and mechanisms of these active constituents.
