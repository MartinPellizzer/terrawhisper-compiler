These compounds have been found to have a range of medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and diuretic effects. Research has also suggested that Barosma betulina may have antibacterial and antifungal properties, and may be effective in treating urinary tract infections and other conditions related to the genitourinary system.

Additionally, some studies have indicated that the plant may have potential in the treatment of prostate-related conditions and cancer, although more research is needed in this area.
