What to know before using Pinus pinaster (Cluster Pine)?

Pinus pinaster, also known as cluster pine or Mediterranean pine, is a species of pine tree that is native to the Mediterranean region.

Medicinally, it has been used in the treatment of ailments such as cardiovascular diseases, thanks to its rich supply of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties. It is a hardy tree that can thrive in a variety of horticultural conditions, including dry, sandy soil and coastal areas with high salinity. Botanically, Pinus pinaster is characterized by its long, slender needles that are grouped in bundles of two, and its thick, scaly bark.

The tree can grow to be quite large, with some specimens reaching heights of over 150 feet.
