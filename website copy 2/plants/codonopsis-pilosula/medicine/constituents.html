10 Best Active Constituents of Codonopsis pilosula (Codonopsis)

Codonopsis pilosula, also known as Dang Shen, is a traditional Chinese medicinal plant that contains various active constituents with therapeutic potential.

The main active constituents include saponins, alkaloids, sterols, and polysaccharides. Among these, saponins such as tangshenoside R1 and R2 have been found to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory effects.

Alkaloids like codonoposide C have been shown to exhibit anti-tumor activities. Sterols such as β-sitosterol have been found to have anti-inflammatory and anti-tumor properties. Polysaccharides, on the other hand, have been shown to have immunomodulatory, antioxidant, and anti-tumor activities.

These active constituents make Codonopsis pilosula a valuable medicinal plant with a wide range of therapeutic applications.
