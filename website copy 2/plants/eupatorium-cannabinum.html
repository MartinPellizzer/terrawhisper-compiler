What to know before using Eupatorium cannabinum (Hemp Agrimony)?

Eupatorium cannabinum, also known as hemp-agrimony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It thrives in moist to wet soil and partial shade to full sun, making it a popular choice for wetland gardens and naturalized areas. The plant can grow up to 1.5 meters tall and has distinctive, toothed, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in pairs along the stem. Hemp-agrimony is valued in herbal medicine for its diuretic, expectorant, and mild laxative properties.

Its leaves and flowers are used to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as kidney and bladder ailments.
