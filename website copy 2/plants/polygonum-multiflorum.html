What to know before using Polygonum multiflorum (Fo Ti)?

Polygonum multiflorum, also known as He Shou Wu, is a traditional Chinese medicinal plant that has been used for centuries to treat various health conditions.

It is known for its anti-aging, anti-cancer, and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a popular choice for those seeking to boost their overall health and well-being. In terms of horticultural conditions, Polygonum multiflorum thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, and can grow up to 10 feet tall. It is a perennial vine that produces small, purple flowers in the summer, which then develop into clusters of red berries.

Botanically, Polygonum multiflorum is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves, which are arranged in an alternate pattern along the stem.
