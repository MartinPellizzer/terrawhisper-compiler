10 Best Active Constituents of Withania somnifera (Ashwagandha)

Withania somnifera, also known as Ashwagandha, is a popular medicinal herb that contains several active constituents with therapeutic benefits.

The two main active compounds in Ashwagandha are withanolides and withaferins. Withanolides are a group of steroidal lactones that have been shown to have anti-inflammatory, antitumor, and antistress properties.

Withaferins, on the other hand, have been found to have anticancer, neuroprotective, and cardioprotective effects. Other constituents in Ashwagandha include alkaloids, saponins, and sitoindosides, which have been shown to have various medicinal properties, such as adaptogenic, sedative, and immunomodulatory effects.

Overall, the active constituents of Withania somnifera make it a valuable herb for managing various health conditions, including stress, anxiety, inflammation, and cancer.
