What to know before using Myosotis scorpioides (Forget-Me-Not)?

Myosotis scorpioides, also known as forget-me-not, is a perennial plant with delicate blue flowers and hairy, branched stems that can grow up to 60 cm tall.

It thrives in moist soil and partial shade, making it ideal for wetland gardens or near ponds. The plant contains saponins and alkaloids, which have been used in herbal medicine to treat a variety of ailments including respiratory conditions, skin irritations, and wounds. Myosotis scorpioides prefers consistently moist soil and does not do well in dry conditions.

It is a hardy plant that can spread easily in the right conditions, making it a beautiful and low-maintenance addition to any garden.
