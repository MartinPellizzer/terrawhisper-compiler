What to know before using Bupleurum chinense (Bupleurum)?

Bupleurum chinense, also known as Chinese thoroughwax, is a plant with significant medicinal properties.

It has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries to treat various ailments, including liver diseases, mood disorders, and skin problems. This herb contains saikosaponins, which are believed to have anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and antioxidant effects. In terms of horticultural conditions, B.

chinense prefers well-drained soil and partial shade. It is a hardy perennial that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall and has clusters of small, yellow flowers. Botanically, B.

chinense is a member of the carrot family and has alternate, lobed leaves and a thick, fleshy root.
