10 Best Health Benefits of Azadirachta indica (Neem)

Azadirachta indica, commonly known as neem, is a tree that has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

One of the most well-known medicinal benefits of neem is its antimicrobial properties, which are attributed to the presence of compounds such as nimbidol and nimbin. These compounds have been shown to be effective against a wide range of bacteria, fungi, and viruses, making neem a valuable tool in the treatment of various infectious diseases.

Additionally, neem extracts have been found to have anti-inflammatory effects, which can help alleviate symptoms of conditions such as arthritis and skin irritations. Neem is also a rich source of antioxidants, which can help protect the body against damage from free radicals and support overall health. Furthermore, neem has been shown to have immunomodulatory effects, which means it can help regulate and strengthen the immune system.

This makes neem a valuable tool in preventing and treating a wide range of health conditions.
