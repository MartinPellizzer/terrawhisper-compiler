In terms of horticultural conditions, Pinus sylvestris prefers full sun and well-drained soil. As for its botanical characteristics, Pinus sylvestris is an evergreen conifer that can grow up to 130 feet tall.

It has a distinctive, irregular shape, with a reddish-brown bark and blue-green needles.

Pinus sylvestris needs well-drained soil and partial shade to full sun for optimal growth.

Harvesting scots pine for timber or other uses should be done with care, ensuring new growth is not damaged and avoiding harvest during the growing season.
