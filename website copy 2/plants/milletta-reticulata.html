What to know before using Milletta reticulata (Ji Xue Teng)?

Milletta reticulata, also known as the Chinese Prickly Ash, is a small to medium-sized tree that is native to China and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.

This tree is known for its medicinal properties, including its ability to alleviate pain, reduce inflammation, and improve circulation. Milletta reticulata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, and it can grow up to 20 feet tall. The tree has compound, pinnate leaves with serrated leaflets, and it produces small, yellow flowers in the spring followed by red, fleshy fruit.

The bark of Milletta reticulata is grayish-brown and has a distinctive, reticulated pattern, which gives the tree its name.
