The plant has bright yellow, flowering spikes and serrated, green leaves, growing to a height of around 3 feet. Agrimonia eupatoria thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and along the edges of woods.

The plant's leaves and flowers are used in teas, tinctures, and ointments for their medicinal benefits.
