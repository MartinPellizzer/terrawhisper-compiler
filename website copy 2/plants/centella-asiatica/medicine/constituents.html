10 Best Active Constituents of Centella asiatica (Gotu Kola)

Centella asiatica, also known as gotu kola, is a medicinal plant that contains several active constituents with potential health benefits.

The main active constituents include triterpenoid saponins, particularly asiaticoside, madecassoside, and centelloside. These compounds have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and wound-healing properties, making them useful in the treatment of various skin conditions such as eczema, psoriasis, and wounds. Additionally, Centella asiatica contains triterpene acids, such as asiatic and madecassic acids, which have been found to have anti-cancer and neuroprotective effects.

Furthermore, the plant contains essential oils and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties and overall therapeutic potential.
