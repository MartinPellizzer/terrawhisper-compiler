Its roots contain cardiotonic glycosides, which have been used to treat heart ailments such as irregular heartbeat and heart failure. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, and it is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Adonis vernalis is a herbaceous perennial that grows to be around 10-30 cm tall, and it is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and large, showy, cup-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink with a prominent yellow center.

Additionally, they can slow the heart rate, which can be beneficial in the treatment of certain cardiac conditions such as atrial fibrillation.
