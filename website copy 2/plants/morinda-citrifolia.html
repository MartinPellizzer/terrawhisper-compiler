What to know before using Morinda citrifolia (Noni)?

Morinda citrifolia, commonly known as noni, is a small evergreen tree that thrives in tropical climates with well-drained soil and moderate rainfall.

Its thick, glossy leaves and white, fragrant flowers are characteristic of this species. The fruit of Morinda citrifolia is well-known for its medicinal properties; it contains a variety of beneficial compounds, including antioxidants, anthraquinones, and alkaloids, which have been shown to have anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and immune-boosting effects.

This versatile plant can also be used horticulturally as a hedge or screen, and its fruit can be eaten raw, cooked, or made into juice, providing a range of health benefits.
