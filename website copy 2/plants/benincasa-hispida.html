The fruit of Benincasa hispida is known for its cooling and moisturizing properties, and has been used in traditional medicine to treat a variety of ailments, including fever, inflammation, and digestive disorders. In addition to its medicinal uses, Benincasa hispida is an important food crop in many parts of the world, and its young fruit, seeds, and shoots are used in a variety of dishes.
