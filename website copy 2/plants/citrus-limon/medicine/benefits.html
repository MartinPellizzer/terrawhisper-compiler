10 Best Health Benefits of Citrus limon (Lemon)

Citrus limon, also known as the lemon, is a popular citrus fruit with a wide range of medicinal benefits.

Firstly, lemons are a rich source of vitamin C, which is a powerful antioxidant that helps to protect cells from damage and boost the immune system. Additionally, lemons have antibacterial and antiviral properties, making them useful in treating throat infections, fever, and other cold-related symptoms.

Furthermore, lemon juice can aid in digestion by alkalizing the gut and helping to flush out toxins. The peel of the lemon also contains essential oils with therapeutic properties, including limonene, which has been shown to have anti-cancer effects.

Finally, lemons have been used in traditional medicine to treat skin conditions, such as acne and eczema, due to their anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

1. Boosts Immunity

Lemon boosts immunity, meaning it helps to strengthen the body's natural defense system against harmful pathogens.

The main active constituents in lemons that provide this benefit are vitamin C, citric acid, and various bioflavonoids. The primary parts of the lemon plant that contribute to this benefit are the fruit and the peel. The most common medicinal preparations of lemons for boosting immunity include drinking lemon juice or tea, using lemon essential oil, and consuming lemon supplements.

This benefit of lemons is particularly helpful in relieving conditions such as the common cold, flu, and other respiratory infections.
