What to know before using Solidago virgaurea (Goldenrod)?

Solidago virgaurea, also known as European goldenrod, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and some parts of Asia.

It has yellow, narrow and spiky flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall, and can grow up to 1-2 feet tall. This plant is known for its medicinal properties, including its ability to treat wounds, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of colds and flu. Solidago virgaurea thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, and can be grown in USDA hardiness zones 4-8.

It is also an attractive addition to gardens and meadows due to its vibrant yellow flowers and low maintenance requirements.
