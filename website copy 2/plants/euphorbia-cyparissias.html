What to know before using Euphorbia cyparissias (Cypress Spurge)?

Euphorbia cyparissias, also known as cypress-leaf spurge, is a herbaceous perennial plant that boasts beautiful and unique botanical characteristics.

It has narrow, cypress-like leaves that are deep green and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant produces small, yellow-green flowers in clusters at the top of the stems.

Medicinally, Euphorbia cyparissias is known for its sap, which contains a milky latex that has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and wound-healing properties. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

However, it can be invasive in some areas, so it's important to plant it in controlled conditions and monitor its spread.
