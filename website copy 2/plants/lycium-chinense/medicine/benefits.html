10 Best Health Benefits of Lycium chinense (Goji Berry)

Lycium chinense, also known as Chinese boxthorn or wolfberry, has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries due to its various medicinal benefits.

The berries of this plant are rich in antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals, making them a potent supplement for overall health. They are believed to boost the immune system, improve eye health, and even help prevent certain diseases such as diabetes and cancer. Additionally, Lycium chinense has anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties, making it useful in treating conditions like hepatitis and various skin disorders.

Furthermore, it may also have a positive effect on cardiovascular health by reducing blood pressure and cholesterol levels.

1. Boosts Immune System

Goji Berry boosts immune system, meaning it helps strengthen the body's natural defense against harmful invaders, such as viruses and bacteria.

The main active constituents of Lycium chinense that contribute to this benefit are polysaccharides, carotenoids (such as beta-carotene and zeaxanthin), and antioxidants. The primary parts of the Lycium chinense plant that provide this benefit are the berries themselves. The main medicinal preparations of Lycium chinense that give this benefit include dried goji berries, goji berry juice, and goji berry extract in the form of capsules or tablets.

This benefit of Lycium chinense helps relieve various health conditions, such as weakened immune system, frequent colds or flu, and chronic fatigue syndrome.

Lycium chinense boosts immune system thanks to the active constituents listed below.

  • Flavonoids
  • Tannins

  • Phenolic acids
