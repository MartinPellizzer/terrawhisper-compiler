What to know before using Eleutherococcus senticosus (Siberian Ginseng)?

Eleutherococcus senticosus, also known as Siberian ginseng, is a woodland shrub native to Northeastern Asia.

It has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to boost energy, reduce stress, and enhance mental clarity. This adaptogenic herb thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, and can grow up to 8 feet tall with small white flowers and blue-black berries.

Eleutherococcus senticosus contains a variety of beneficial compounds, including eleutherosides, polysaccharides, and sterols, which have been shown to support the immune system, improve physical performance, and promote overall health and well-being.
