It contains compounds that may have anti-inflammatory and antibacterial effects, and has been used in herbal remedies to treat wounds, skin irritations, and respiratory ailments. This hardy fern is well-suited for horticultural cultivation, thriving in a variety of conditions including rocky crevices, shaded areas, and moist soil.

Asplenium ruta muraria is characterized by its bright green fronds, which grow in a rosette pattern and can reach up to 12 inches in length. The fronds are delicate and feathery, with small segments that give the plant a lacy appearance.

Overall, Asplenium ruta muraria is a fascinating and useful plant that can add both beauty and function to a garden or indoor space.
