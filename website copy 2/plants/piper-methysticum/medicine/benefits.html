10 Best Health Benefits of Piper methysticum (Kava)

Piper methysticum, also known as kava, is a plant that has been used for medicinal purposes in the Pacific Islands for centuries.

It is most commonly consumed as a tea or supplement and is known for its calming and anxiolytic effects. Kava has been shown to help alleviate symptoms of anxiety and stress, improve sleep quality, and reduce muscle tension. Additionally, some studies suggest that kava may have analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a potential alternative treatment for chronic pain conditions.

However, it is important to note that kava should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional, as it can cause liver toxicity in some individuals.

1. Reduces anxiety symptoms

Kava, referred to by its scientific name Piper methysticum, reduces anxiety symptoms, meaning it can help individuals feel more calm and relaxed while alleviating feelings of worry and fear.

The main active constituents in Piper methysticum that provide this benefit are kavalactones, which have been shown to have anxiolytic and sedative effects. The primary parts of the Piper methysticum plant that give this benefit are the roots, which contain the highest concentration of kavalactones. The main medicinal preparations of Piper methysticum that give this benefit are extracts, tinctures, and capsules, which are commonly used to treat generalized anxiety disorder, stress, and insomnia.

Additionally, Piper methysticum has been shown to help relieve symptoms of other health conditions such as depression and menopause.
