10 Best Active Constituents of Rehmannia glutinosa (Rehmannia)

Rehmannia glutinosa, a plant commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine, contains several active constituents with potential medicinal benefits.

These constituents include iridoid glycosides, such as catalpol and acteoside, which have been shown to have anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory effects. The plant also contains phenylethanoid glycosides, such as rehmannioside D and leucosceptoside A, which have been found to have hepatoprotective, neuroprotective, and antitumor properties.

Additionally, Rehmannia glutinosa contains steroidal glycosides, such as rehmaglutins A and B, which have been shown to have hypoglycemic and hypolipidemic effects. These active constituents contribute to the plant's wide range of medicinal uses, including treatment for anemia, hyperglycemia, and inflammatory conditions.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the mechanisms of action and potential therapeutic applications of these compounds.
