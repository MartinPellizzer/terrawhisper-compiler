What to know before using Sanicula europaea (Sanicle)?

Sanicula europaea, also known as European sanicle, is a perennial herb native to Europe and western Asia.

It has a variety of medicinal properties, including being an astringent, diuretic, and expectorant. The plant prefers partial shade and moist, well-draining soil, and it can grow up to 2 feet tall. Sanicula europaea has round, toothed leaves and small, white flowers arranged in umbels.

The plant's root is thick and fibrous, and it has a long history of use in traditional medicine for treating wounds, skin conditions, and respiratory ailments.
