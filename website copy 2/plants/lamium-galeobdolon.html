What to know before using Lamium galeobdolon (Yellow Archangel)?

Lamium galeobdolon, also known as yellow archangel, is a perennial plant species native to Europe.

Medicinally, it has been used to treat a variety of ailments, including digestive issues and skin conditions, due to its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties. It is a hardy plant that thrives in a variety of horticultural conditions, preferring partial shade and well-drained soil. Lamium galeobdolon is characterized by its bright yellow, snapdragon-like flowers and its creeping, mat-forming growth habit.

The plant's leaves are opposite and slightly toothed, and its stems are square.
