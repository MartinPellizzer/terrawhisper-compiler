What is hyperacidity and how can it affect your life?

Hyperacidity is a condition characterized by excessive production of stomach acid, leading to stomach discomfort, burning sensations, and even pain.

This condition significantly impacts people's lives by causing distress, discomfort, and disrupting daily activities.

Prolonged hyperacidity can lead to more severe health issues, such as stomach ulcers and digestive problems, thereby reducing the quality of life.

What other natural remedies help with hyperacidity?

Hyperacidity, also known as acid reflux, is a common condition characterized by the backflow of stomach acid into the esophagus, causing symptoms such as heartburn, chest pain, and difficulty swallowing.

Several natural remedies have proven effective in managing hyperacidity. One of the most common and effective remedies is consuming probiotics, which are beneficial bacteria that help maintain a healthy balance of gut flora.

Probiotics are found in fermented foods such as yogurt, sauerkraut, and kefir. Another remedy is drinking diluted apple cider vinegar, which helps balance stomach acid levels and aids digestion. Additionally, chewing gum after meals can stimulate saliva production, which acts as a natural acid buffer, relieving symptoms of hyperacidity.

Finally, eating smaller and more frequent meals throughout the day can help prevent overfilling of the stomach, reducing the risk of acid reflux.
