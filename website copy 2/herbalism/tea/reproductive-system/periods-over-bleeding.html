The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic yarrow tea for over bleeding during periods.

  1. Gather 2-3 teaspoons of dried yarrow flowers and leaves.
  2. Place the herbs in a teapot or infuser and pour 8 ounces of boiling water over them.
  3. Let the mixture steep for 5-10 minutes to allow the active compounds to extract.
  4. Strain the liquid into a cup, discarding the spent herbs.
  5. Drink the prepared yarrow tea up to three times a day to help alleviate heavy menstrual bleeding.
