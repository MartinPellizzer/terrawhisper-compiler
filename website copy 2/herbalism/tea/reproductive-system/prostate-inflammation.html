10 best herbal teas for prostate inflammation

1. Saw Palmetto

Saw palmetto tea addresses prostate inflammation by containing fatty acids and sterols that inhibit the conversion of testosterone into dihydrotestosterone, a hormone associated with prostate enlargement.

This tea also provides antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, such as flavonoids and plant sterols, that help reduce swelling and irritation in the prostate gland. Saw palmetto's therapeutic benefits for prostate health have been validated through extensive scientific research and clinical trials.

Right below you will find a list of the most important parts in saw palmetto tea that help with prostate inflammation.

  • Berries: Contain fatty acids and phytosterols that studies show can reduce symptoms of an enlarged prostate.
  • Leaves: Rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds that can help alleviate prostate inflammation.
  • Rhizomes: Contain high levels of plant sterols and fatty acids that can inhibit the production of DHT, a hormone associated with prostate enlargement.

The list below shows the primary active constituents in saw palmetto tea that aid with prostate inflammation.

  • Fatty acids: These provide anti-inflammatory effects, helping to reduce inflammation in the prostate.
  • Phytosterols: They inhibit the conversion of testosterone to DHT, reducing prostate inflammation.
  • Flavonoids: These compounds have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, aiding in decreasing prostate inflammation.
  • Volatile oils: They possess antiseptic and anti-inflammatory qualities, helpful in alleviating prostate inflammation.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic saw palmetto tea for prostate inflammation.

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a pot, then add 2 teaspoons of dried saw palmetto berries.
  2. Reduce heat to low, simmer the mixture covered for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Strain the tea to remove saw palmetto berries, discard the solid residue.
  4. Add honey or lemon to taste, if desired.
  5. Serve the tea warm, drink 2-3 times a day for prostate inflammation relief.
