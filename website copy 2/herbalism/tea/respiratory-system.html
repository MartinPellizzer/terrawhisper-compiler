Herbal tea for the respiratory system

Several herbal teas benefit the respiratory system due to their anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and expectorant properties.

These teas include licorice root, eucalyptus, ginger, and thyme. Licorice root soothes inflammation in the respiratory tract, while eucalyptus and ginger provide relief from congestion by acting as natural decongestants. Thyme, on the other hand, is an effective expectorant, helping to loosen and expel mucus from the lungs.

Regularly consuming these teas can bolster respiratory health, improve breathing, and support overall well-being.
