What is chronic hepatitis and how can it affect your life?

Chronic hepatitis is a long-term liver inflammation of at least six months, caused by hepatitis B, C, or D viruses.

This condition steadily deteriorates liver function, potentially leading to severe complications such as cirrhosis, liver failure, and liver cancer. The impact on people's lives is substantial, as it often requires lifelong monitoring, treatments, and adjustments to daily activities.

Chronic hepatitis affects not only physical health but also mental well-being, causing stress, anxiety, and decreased quality of life due to its unpredictable nature and potential long-term consequences.
