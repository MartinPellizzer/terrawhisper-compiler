The list below shows the primary active constituents in rooibos tea that aid with dark lips.

  • Aspalathin: Possesses antioxidant properties that help protect skin from damage and promote skin cell regeneration, which can reduce the appearance of dark lips.
  • Nothofagin: Acts as a potent antioxidant, helping to protect lips from environmental stressors that can cause discoloration and darkening of the lips.
  • Notofagin: Aids in reducing inflammation and irritation, contributing to a more even skin tone and reducing the appearance of dark lips.
  • Quercetin: Known for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, helping to reduce inflammation, combat free radicals, and brighten the skin tone of lips.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic rooibos tea for dark lips.

  1. Boil 1 cup of water, then steep 1 tablespoon of loose rooibos tea for 10 minutes to extract its color and antioxidants.
  2. Strain the tea to remove the tea leaves, leaving only the liquid.
  3. Let the tea cool down to room temperature or cooler to avoid irritating your lips.
  4. Apply the cooled tea to your lips using a cotton swab or your fingers, covering them entirely.
  5. Leave the tea on your lips for 10-15 minutes to stain them, then wipe off the excess with a damp cloth.
