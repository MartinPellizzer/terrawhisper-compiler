    10 best herbal teas for food intolerance

    Herbal teas, such as chamomile, ginger, and peppermint, offer soothing relief for individuals with food intolerance.

    Chamomile's anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate digestive discomfort, while ginger is known to reduce inflammation and nausea. Peppermint tea boasts antispasmodic qualities, providing relief from abdominal cramps and spasms.

    Regularly consuming these caffeine-free teas after meals supports healthy digestion and can lessen the symptoms associated with food intolerance.
