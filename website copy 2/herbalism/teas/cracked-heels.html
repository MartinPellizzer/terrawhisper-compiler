    The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic chamomile tea for cracked heels.

    1. Boil 4 cups of water and add 3 heaping teaspoons of dried chamomile flowers, letting it steep for 20 minutes.
    2. Strain the liquid, discarding the spent flowers, and add 1/4 cup of brewed chamomile tea to a basin or foot bath.
    3. Soak your feet in the chamomile tea for 15-20 minutes, allowing the tea to soften and gently exfoliate your cracked heels.
    4. Remove your feet from the basin, gently patting them dry with a clean towel.
    5. Apply a moisturizer or heavy cream to your feet, focusing on the cracked heels, to lock in the chamomile's soothing and healing properties.
