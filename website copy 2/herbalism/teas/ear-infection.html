    What is ear infection and how can it affect your life?

    An ear infection, medically known as otitis media or otitis externa, is a bacterial or viral inflammation of the inner or outer ear.

    This condition often causes symptoms such as ear pain, hearing loss, and difficulty balancing, leading to discomfort and impaired communication.

    Prolonged or frequent ear infections can result in temporary or permanent hearing damage, negatively affecting individuals' cognitive development, learning, and overall quality of life.
