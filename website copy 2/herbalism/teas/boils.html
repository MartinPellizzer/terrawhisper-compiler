    What is boils and how can it affect your life?

    Boils are painful, pus-filled bumps that form under the skin due to bacterial infections.

    They typically start as red, tender spots that eventually fill with pus, causing discomfort and limiting mobility in the affected area. Boils impact people's lives by causing distress, discomfort, and potential embarrassment. In severe cases, boils can lead to complications such as scarring or bloodstream infections, which may result in hospitalization.

    Proper hygiene and medical treatment are crucial to prevent and manage boils, ensuring a better quality of life.
