    • Rhizomes: Also not used, as eyebright is not a rhizomatous plant and does not possess rhizomes.
    • Bark: Not applicable, as eyebright is not a tree or a shrub, and does not have bark.
    • Buds: Not utilized in medicinal tea for eyesight, as the flowers and leaves are preferred for their higher concentration of beneficial compounds.

    The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic eyebright tea for eyesight.

    1. In a saucepan, combine 1 teaspoon of crushed Eyebright herb with 1 cup of water.
    2. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
    3. Strain the tea to remove the herb particles, then let it cool down to a warm temperature.
    4. Add honey or lemon juice to taste, if desired.
    5. Drink 2-3 cups of Eyebright tea daily for best results in supporting eye health.

    What is eyesight and how can it affect your life?

    Eyesight, also known as vision, is the ability to receive and interpret visual information from the surroundings through the eyes.

    This sensory function impacts people's lives significantly by enabling them to perform daily tasks with ease, such as reading, writing, recognizing faces, and navigating through their environment safely. Impaired eyesight can hinder one's ability to complete simple tasks, diminish their quality of life, and potentially lead to accidents or injuries.

    Therefore, maintaining good eyesight is crucial for overall well-being and independence.

    What other natural remedies help with eyesight?

    Among the most effective and widely used natural remedies for improving eyesight are regular eye exercises, a balanced diet, and protective eyewear.

    Eye exercises, such as focusing on a distant object and then a nearby one, help strengthen the eye muscles and improve accommodation. Consuming a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and omega-3 fatty acids provides essential nutrients like vitamins A, C, and E, lutein, and zeaxanthin, which nourish the eyes and reduce the risk of age-related macular degeneration. Wearing sunglasses and safety goggles shields the eyes from harmful ultraviolet rays and debris, preventing damage and strain.

    Implementing these natural remedies consistently enhances overall eye health and vision.
