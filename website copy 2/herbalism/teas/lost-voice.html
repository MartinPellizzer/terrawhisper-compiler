    What is lost voice and how can it affect your life?

    Lost voice, also known as aphonia, refers to the inability to speak due to damage or injury to the vocal cords.

    This condition severely impacts people's lives, as communication is a fundamental aspect of human interaction. Individuals with lost voice face challenges in expressing their thoughts, feelings, and needs, which often leads to isolation, frustration, and difficulties in personal and professional relationships.

    Furthermore, lost voice can result in a loss of independence, as individuals may require assistance in performing daily tasks, contributing to a decline in overall quality of life.
