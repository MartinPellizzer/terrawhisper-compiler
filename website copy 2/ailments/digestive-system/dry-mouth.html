What to know about dry mouth before using medicinal herbs

Several herbal teas effectively alleviate dry mouth symptoms.

These teas include chamomile, marshmallow root, and slippery elm. Chamomile tea, popular for its calming effects, also stimulates saliva production. Marshmallow root and slippery elm teas create a protective coating on mouth tissues, retaining moisture.

Regular consumption of these herbal teas brings significant relief to those suffering from dry mouth.

What is dry mouth and and how it affects your life?

Dry mouth, also known as xerostomia, is a condition characterized by a significant reduction in saliva production.

This decrease in saliva leads to a dry and sticky feeling in the mouth, making it difficult to speak, swallow, taste, and digest food. Saliva plays a crucial role in maintaining oral health by washing away food particles and neutralizing acids produced by bacteria, reducing the risk of tooth decay, gum disease, and bad breath.

Therefore, experiencing dry mouth poses a serious threat to your overall well-being, as it can lead to significant oral health issues if left untreated.
