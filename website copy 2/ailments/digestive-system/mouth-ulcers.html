What is mouth ulcers and and how it affects your life?

Mouth ulcers are small, shallow lesions that develop on the soft tissues inside the mouth, often on the inside of the cheeks, lips, or gums.

They result from a variety of causes, such as injury, vitamin deficiencies, and autoimmune disorders. Proper diagnosis and treatment of mouth ulcers are crucial because they can lead to painful eating, speaking, and swallowing, diminishing the overall quality of life.

Furthermore, persistent or recurring mouth ulcers can signal underlying medical conditions, such as gastrointestinal disorders or immune system dysfunctions, requiring prompt medical attention.
