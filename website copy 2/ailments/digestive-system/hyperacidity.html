What is hyperacidity and and how it affects your life?

Hyperacidity is a condition where there is an excessive amount of acid produced in the stomach.

This acid is crucial for breaking down food during digestion. However, when there is too much acid, it can lead to uncomfortable symptoms such as heartburn, stomach pain, and nausea. Maintaining a balanced level of stomach acid is essential for optimal digestion and overall well-being.

By ensuring proper acid levels, we can prevent digestive issues, promote nutrient absorption, and maintain a healthy gut.
