What to know about fever before using medicinal herbs

Ideal herbal teas for fever include chamomile, ginger, and yarrow.

Chamomile is a soothing option, promoting relaxation and aiding sleep. Its anti-inflammatory properties help reduce feverish symptoms.

Ginger, renowned for its warming effects, stimulates circulation and perspiration, assisting the body in eliminating toxins. Yarrow acts as a diaphoretic, encouraging sweat production and cooling the body.

These three teas, with their natural healing properties, bring comfort and relief during a fever.

What is fever and and how it affects your life?

A fever is an elevation in the body's normal temperature, typically above 38°C (100.4°F), indicating an ongoing immune response to an infection or illness.

Its significance extends beyond mere discomfort, as it plays a vital role in combating harmful pathogens. The heightened temperature creates an unfavorable environment for bacteria and viruses, thereby accelerating recovery and promoting overall well-being.

Hence, a fever should not be suppressed indiscriminately, but rather viewed as a crucial ally in the body's defense mechanisms.
