What to know about chapped lips before using medicinal herbs

Several herbal teas soothe chapped lips effectively due to their hydrating and healing properties.

Chamomile tea, rich in antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, reduces redness and irritation. Its gentle emollient effect softens lips.

Licorice root tea contains glycyrrhizin, a compound that naturally moisturizes and relieves discomfort. Lastly, calendula tea boasts anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, promoting tissue repair and protecting lips from environmental stressors.

Regularly sipping these herbal teas or using them as a compress helps restore smooth, healthy lips.

What is chapped lips and and how it affects your life?

Chapped lips refer to dry, cracked, and often painful lips resulting from environmental factors such as cold, dry weather or excessive sun exposure.

This condition is important as it not only causes discomfort but also leaves the lips more susceptible to infection.

Maintaining healthy, moisturized lips is crucial for overall lip health and can prevent potential complications.
