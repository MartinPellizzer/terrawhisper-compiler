What is stiff neck and and how it affects your life?

A stiff neck refers to a condition where there is limited mobility and discomfort in the neck region, often accompanied by pain and muscle tightness.

This issue is important for your life because it affects your ability to perform daily activities, such as driving, working, and even sleeping. A stiff neck can lead to reduced productivity, increased stress levels, and a decreased quality of life.

Furthermore, it may indicate underlying health concerns, such as poor posture, muscle strain, or spinal issues, which should be addressed promptly to prevent further complications.
