What to know about breastfeeding before using medicinal herbs

Herbal teas, such as fenugreek, fennel, and blessed thistle, are excellent choices for breastfeeding mothers.

Fenugreek contains phytoestrogens that stimulate milk production, while fennel's galactagogue properties help increase milk flow. Blessed thistle is another herb known to support breast milk supply.

These herbal teas are caffeine-free and can provide breastfeeding mothers with a soothing and comforting drink, promoting relaxation and hydration necessary for successful breastfeeding.

What is breastfeeding and and how it affects your life?

Breastfeeding is the act of feeding a baby with milk produced by the mother's body.

It is a natural and essential process that provides numerous benefits for both the infant and the mother. For the infant, breast milk is the perfect source of nutrition, containing all the necessary nutrients for growth and development.

It also helps to strengthen the baby's immune system, protecting them from illness and disease. For the mother, breastfeeding releases hormones that help the body recover from childbirth and reduces the risk of certain cancers and other health problems.

Therefore, breastfeeding is not only important for the life of the baby, but also for the mother's well-being.
