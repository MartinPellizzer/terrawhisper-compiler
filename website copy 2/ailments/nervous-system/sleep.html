What to know about sleep before using medicinal herbs

The top herbal teas for sleep include chamomile, lavender, and valerian root.

Chamomile tea, with its daisy-like flowers, brings a calming effect that aids in falling asleep. Lavender tea, known for its soothing scent, contains relaxing properties that promote sound sleep. Valerian root tea, extracted from the valerian plant's roots, has been used for centuries as a natural sleep aid due to its ability to reduce anxiety and improve sleep quality.

Enjoying a cup of any of these herbal teas before bed helps establish a reliable nightly ritual conducive to high-quality sleep.

What is sleep and and how it affects your life?

Sleep is a vital physiological process during which your body rests and restores itself.

It is a daily requirement, as essential as food and water, that allows your body to perform critical restoration and regeneration tasks. While you sleep, your brain forms new pathways that help you learn and remember information, your body repairs muscle tissue, and your immune system strengthens.

Adequate sleep is crucial for maintaining optimal physical, mental, and emotional well-being, ensuring you are energetic, focused, and resilient throughout your waking hours.
