What to know about hydration before using medicinal herbs

The top herbal teas for hydration are chamomile, peppermint, and lemon balm.

Chamomile tea, renowned for its soothing properties, effectively contributes to daily fluid intake. Packed with antioxidants, peppermint tea boasts excellent hydrating abilities while aiding digestion.

Lastly, lemon balm tea, a stress-reliever, offers superb hydration benefits, making it an ideal choice for staying refreshed and quenched.

What is hydration and and how it affects your life?

Hydration refers to the process of providing your body with sufficient water or fluids, which is essential for nearly every bodily function.

Proper hydration maintains the balance of bodily fluids, allowing for optimal digestion, nutrient absorption, detoxification, and maintenance of body temperature. Staying hydrated supports healthy skin, enhances physical performance, and promotes mental clarity.

Given its critical role in our overall well-being, hydration is not a choice but a fundamental necessity for a high-quality life.
