What to know about angina before using medicinal herbs

Herbal teas offer a natural approach to managing angina symptoms.

Two exceptional options include hawthorn and ginkgo biloba teas. Hawthorn tea, derived from the leaves and flowers of the hawthorn tree, strengthens the heart's muscles and dilates blood vessels.

This results in improved blood flow and reduced blood pressure. Ginkgo biloba tea, extracted from the ginkgo tree's leaves, has antioxidant properties and boosts circulation. By increasing blood flow in the body, it alleviates angina symptoms.

Both teas are caffeine-free, making them suitable for regular consumption and a valuable addition to an angina treatment plan.

What is angina and and how it affects your life?

Angina is a severe chest pain or discomfort that occurs when the heart muscle receives an insufficient supply of oxygen-rich blood.

This condition arises due to the narrowing or blockage of the coronary arteries, which transport blood to the heart. Angina is crucial to acknowledge because it serves as a warning signal for underlying heart disease, potentially leading to heart attacks or even death if left untreated.

Early detection and management of angina are vital to prevent further complications and ensure a healthy life.
