What to know about cholesterol before using medicinal herbs

Certain herbal teas are renowned for their cholesterol-lowering properties.

These teas include holy basil, hibiscus, and green tea. Holy basil, also known as tulsi, contains ursolic acid and eugenol, compounds that lower LDL cholesterol levels. Hibiscus tea is rich in antioxidants that prevent the oxidation of LDL cholesterol, while green tea boosts HDL cholesterol levels, which helps remove LDL cholesterol from the bloodstream.

Regular consumption of these herbal teas can significantly improve cholesterol levels, promoting better heart health.

What is cholesterol and and how it affects your life?

Cholesterol is a type of fat that is essential for human life.

It is a vital component of the outer layer of every cell in our body and is crucial for the production of certain hormones and vitamin D. Moreover, cholesterol plays a critical role in the digestion of food by aiding in the creation of bile acids, which help break down dietary fats in the intestines.

Therefore, maintaining appropriate levels of cholesterol in the blood is crucial for overall health.
