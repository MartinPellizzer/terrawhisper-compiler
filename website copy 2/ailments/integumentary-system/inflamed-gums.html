What to know about inflamed gums before using medicinal herbs

The top herbal teas for inflamed gums include chamomile, clove, and calendula.

Chamomile tea, known for its anti-inflammatory and soothing properties, helps reduce gum inflammation. Clove tea, containing eugenol, a natural anesthetic and antiseptic, alleviates pain and combats oral bacteria. Calendula tea, recognized for its healing and anti-inflammatory effects, promotes tissue regeneration and eases discomfort.

Regularly drinking these herbal teas supports gum health and fights inflammation.

What is inflamed gums and and how it affects your life?

Inflamed gums, also known as gingivitis, refer to a condition where the gums become red, swollen, and easily bleed due to bacteria buildup in the mouth.

This issue is important for your life because it can lead to more severe dental problems such as periodontitis, which can result in tooth loss. Ignored inflamed gums can also negatively impact your overall health, as the bacteria can enter the bloodstream and cause infections in other parts of the body, including the heart and lungs.

Early detection and treatment of inflamed gums are crucial to maintaining good oral and overall health.
