What to know about swollen gums before using medicinal herbs

Several herbal teas, including chamomile, clove, and calendula, bring effective relief to swollen gums.

Chamomile, known for its anti-inflammatory properties, soothes irritation and reduces swelling. Clove, with its potent germicidal qualities, combats oral bacteria, alleviating pain and promoting gum health. Calendula, an excellent tissue healer, stimulates gum tissue regeneration, diminishing inflammation and fostering a rapid recovery.

Regularly consuming these herbal teas brings substantial relief and supports oral well-being.

What is swollen gums and and how it affects your life?

Swollen gums refer to a condition where the gum tissue around the teeth becomes inflamed and painful, often due to bacterial infections or poor oral hygiene.

This issue is crucial because neglected swollen gums can evolve into severe dental problems, such as gum disease, periodontitis, and tooth loss. Additionally, various research studies indicate a correlation between gum disease and serious systemic health conditions, like diabetes, heart disease, and stroke.

Consequently, maintaining healthy gums is essential for overall well-being and should not be overlooked.
