What is reddened corners and and how it affects your life?

Reddened corners refer to the inflammation or redness that can occur in the eyes, specifically at the outer corners.

This condition is important for your life because it can be a sign of various health issues, such as dry eye, allergies, or infections. Neglecting reddened corners can lead to further complications, potentially affecting your vision and overall well-being.

Recognizing and addressing this symptom promptly is crucial to maintain optimal eye health and prevent potential long-term damage.
