  • Stimulant properties: Fennel can stimulate the production of saliva, which can help wash away bacteria and food particles that cause bad breath. Right below you will find a list of the most important parts in parsley that help with bad breath.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic parsley for bad breath.

  1. Chop 1 cup of fresh parsley, discarding stems, and place in a pot.

  5. Sip the parsley tea slowly, allowing it to coat your mouth and neutralize bad breath.
