This term has evolved through the centuries, but its cultural relevance never diminished significantly among native speakers of Sanskrit languages as well Hindi or even Indian English speaking communities where it is still used today for various purposes like calling one's beloved and referring to their beauty in a poetic fashion within texts including poems & stories.
