There may be some side effects associated with Dong Quai if this medicinal herb is misused. Common adverse effects include gastrointestinal disturbances such as stomach upset, diarrhea, or abdominal bloating. Additionally, allergic reactions like skin irritation, rash, or itching have been reported in some individuals. 

Prolonged use or excessive doses of Dong Quai may potentially increase sensitivity to sunlight, resulting in heightened risk of sunburn. Furthermore, for individuals with certain medical conditions or those taking specific medications, Dong Quai might interfere with blood clotting mechanisms, potentially leading to increased bleeding or bruising. Pregnant women are advised to avoid Dong Quai due to its potential effects on uterine contractions, which may induce miscarriage.
