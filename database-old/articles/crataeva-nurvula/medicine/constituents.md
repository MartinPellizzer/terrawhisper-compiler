Varuna has many medicinal constituents, such as alkaloids, flavonoids, tannins, glycosides and saponins. The alkaloids found 

 in the plant are known for their antioxidant properties which neutralise 

 the formation of free radicals that can cause cell damage. This leads to a 

 reduced risk of various ailments such as heart disease, cancer, and aging 

 symptoms, ensuring better overall health of an individual. The flavonoids 

 present in Varuna have anti-inflammatory properties which help alleviate pain 

 due to arthritis or gout, as well as protect against cardiovascular diseases by 

 reducing the risk factors associated with them. Flavonoids also possess 

 antimicrobial properties that aid in preventing and treating infections. Tannins 

 are astringent agents found in Varuna that can soothe irritation in the stomach 

 and help improve digestion by reducing inflammation. These tannins may also 

 be beneficial for treating diarrhea. The glycosides found in this plant possess 

 anti-inflammatory properties similar to those of flavonoids, thus providing an 

 additional layer of protection against inflammation and the risk factors that 

 accompany it. Furthermore, these compounds are also known for their antimicrobial 

 and antibacterial activities which can help in treating various bacterial and viral 

 infections. Lastly, saponins found in Varuna have been shown to exhibit 

 anti-cancer effects by inhibiting the growth of cancer cells, especially in 

 cases of breast and colon cancer. They also possess antioxidant properties similar 

 to alkaloids which help protect against various ailments caused by free radicals 

 such as heart disease, cancer, aging-related disorders, and neurological diseases. 

 In summary, Varuna is packed with numerous medicinal constituents that provide an 

 array of health benefits, ranging from antioxidant activities to anti-inflammatory, 

 antibacterial, antimicrobial, anticancer, and digestive aiding effects. These compounds 

 contribute to the overall wellbeing of individuals by combating various threats and 

 maintaining the health of different systems within our bodies.
