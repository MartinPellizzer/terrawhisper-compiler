However, it can be highly toxic when taken in large amounts, resulting in serious side effects like nausea, dizziness, hallucinations, delusions, seizures, and even death. Therefore, one must know the exact amount suitable for their body before incorporating Nutmeg into any medicinal regimen.

It has been traditionally utilized for treating respiratory issues like asthma, sinusitis, and cough. Though there are no reported major side effects or contraindications associated with Mace use, it's always advised to take it under professional guidance for its optimum benefits.
