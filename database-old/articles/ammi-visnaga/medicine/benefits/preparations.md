There are many preparations and uses of Visnaga, such as extracting its essential oil. The essential oil derived from Visnaga seeds is often utilized in traditional medicine to alleviate respiratory issues like asthma and bronchitis due to its bronchodilator properties. Additionally, dried Visnaga fruits and seeds are frequently ground into powder or brewed into teas and infusions. These preparations are employed to aid in urinary disorders and kidney stones, owing to Visnaga's diuretic effects. Moreover, Visnaga extracts are sometimes incorporated into topical creams or ointments for their potential to alleviate skin conditions such as vitiligo or psoriasis, offering localized therapeutic benefits.
