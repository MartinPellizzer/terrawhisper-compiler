 One major issue related to goldenseal is the possibility of developing an allergic reaction. Many individuals might not know that they have any sensitivity towards this herb until they experience an adverse reaction after using it. This can range from mild discomfort to severe symptoms, including skin irritations, hives, swelling, or difficulty in breathing. In some instances, a person may develop a rash or suffer from a more serious allergic response known as anaphylaxis. An individual's sensitivity to goldenseal is not always predictable, making the usage of this herb risky for those with unknown sensitivities.

Overdosing on goldenseal might result in adverse reactions and complications, including drowsiness, stomach discomfort, diarrhea, and vomiting. Overdoses generally happen either when someone takes too much at once or over an extended period, leading to a build-up in the body and potential health risks.

Additionally, goldenseal is an ingredient in many over-the-counter (OTC) remedies, including cold and flu treatments, cough syrups, and various skin care products. It's essential to check the label of any medication or supplement to ensure that there aren't any unintentional side effects caused by goldenseal.
