Gel - Aloe gel is the thick and clear substance found inside the leaves of an aloe vera plant. This natural product can be used to soothe burns, sunburns, and minor skin irritations like cuts or insect bites. It also has moisturizing properties that help keep your skin soft and hydrated.

Juice - Aloe juice is made by blending the inner gel with water and sometimes other ingredients. This drink can be consumed to promote digestive health, detoxification of the body, and as an immunity booster. Some people also use it for treating constipation and heartburn.

Cream - Aloe vera cream is a topical application that helps soothe skin conditions like eczema, psoriasis, and dermatitis. It can be used to relieve itching, redness, and inflammation caused by these conditions. In some cases, aloe vera cream might help reduce the appearance of scars, fine lines, or other signs of aging.

Powder - Aloe vera powder is made from drying the inner gel of the plant and grinding it into a fine powder. This form can be easily added to various recipes, such as smoothies or food items, helping maintain good health and internal system balance. It may also be used as an ingredient in face masks for skin care.

Ointment - Aloe vera ointments are thicker than gel formulas and provide a protective layer on the skin to heal wounds faster by boosting their healing process. They can be applied topically to treat minor cuts, scrapes, or abrasions.

Supplements - Aloe vera supplements come in various forms like pills or capsules and are designed to support overall health by increasing digestion, enhancing immunity, and promoting general well-being. These supplements can be used to help prevent illnesses and maintain good health.

Tea bag, Soap bar, Facial cleanser, and Shampoo - Aloe vera is also incorporated into these products as a natural ingredient. Using soap bars or facial cleansers containing aloe vera can provide extra hydration for the skin while cleansing it. Similarly, shampoos containing aloe vera are milder on hair and scalp, promoting hair health and growth. Lastly, tea bags infused with aloe vera offer a refreshing drink that may help improve digestion and overall well-being.
