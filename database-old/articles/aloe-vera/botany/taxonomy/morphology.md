Knowing the [morphology of Aloe vera](/aloe-vera/botany/morphology.html) is useful if you want to be able to classify this plant. 

Aloe vera features a rosette arrangement of lanceolate, succulent leaves with smooth, hairless surfaces, upright orientation, and parallel venation. Its succulent stem is cylindrical, green, and covered in a waxy layer. 

The plant produces tubular, bilaterally symmetric flowers in raceme inflorescences, typically orange, yellow, or red, with six sepals and petals. Aloe vera's fruit is a capsule that hangs vertically in clusters, while its seeds are hard, dark brown, ovate, and found in multiple seed clusters within capsules.
