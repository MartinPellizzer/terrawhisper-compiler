Yarrow has many medicinal preparations, such as Yarrow Infusion, Yarrow Decoction, Yarrow Tincture. Yarrow infusion involves soaking the dried yarrow leaves and flowers in hot water, whereas a decoction requires boiling them in water to extract their healing properties. Yarrow tincture is made by steeping the herb in alcohol for several weeks, resulting in a potent liquid that can be used as a remedy. These methods are widely employed for treating various ailments including minor wounds and inflammation.
