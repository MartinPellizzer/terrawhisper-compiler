
Yarrow's constituents bring many health benefits, but the most important ones are its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. This herb aids in digestion, supports wound healing, and helps alleviate menstrual discomfort. Yarrow also exhibits antimicrobial effects, potentially boosting the immune system and reducing the severity of cold and flu symptoms. Moreover, it can regulate blood pressure and improve overall cardiovascular health. Its diuretic properties may assist in detoxification and reducing water retention. Additionally, yarrow has been used to relieve anxiety and stress, promoting relaxation and mental well-being.
