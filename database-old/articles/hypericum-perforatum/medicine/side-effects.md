St. Johns Wort can have some side effects if used improperly, such as exposure to sunlight or medication. Understand the various consequences it may cause on physical, psychological and emotional levels in people using this herb wrongly. 

 One of the major issues associated with St. John's Wort is that it increases the skin's photosensitivity, which leads to burning and rashes when exposed directly under the sun or under other strong sources of light. This can be uncomfortable, potentially damaging to the skin and sometimes cause a significant amount of pain.

 St. John’s Wort has been known to interact negatively with different prescribed medications, causing severe reactions like lowering the effectiveness of contraceptive pills or increasing the risk of seizures in people taking anti-epileptic drugs. Other side effects from interaction with drugs may be fatigue, dizziness, nausea, diarrhea, and even irregular heartbeat.

 Although there are studies that show St. John’s Wort can help alleviate symptoms of depression during pregnancy, misusing it could lead to negative consequences such as premature contractions, a drop in breast milk production or complications during labor. Hence, the use of this herb during pregnancy is generally not advised and requires expert guidance from medical professionals.

 Some people have reported that using large quantities or incorrectly prepared doses of St. John’s Wort may result in a lack of focus, decreased alertness and difficulty concentrating on tasks at hand.

 The use of higher levels of St. John’s Wort than recommended could sometimes have adverse effects on the mind by causing increased anxiety or even triggering panic attacks in some individuals. These side effects can be particularly problematic, considering that people usually consume it with a view to alleviate these same conditions.

 While St. John’s Wort is often used as a natural remedy for insomnia and anxiety-related sleep disorders, it may have the opposite effect in some cases if taken incorrectly or in excess amounts. This could cause sleeping troubles such as difficulty falling asleep or staying asleep at night, thus worsening an already existing condition.

In summary, while St. John's Wort is often used to alleviate various health issues, misuse and excessive usage of it can lead to several negative effects on the individual ranging from physical discomfort to emotional instability. It is important to consult a healthcare professional before using any herbal remedy, especially if you are taking other medications or have specific medical conditions.
