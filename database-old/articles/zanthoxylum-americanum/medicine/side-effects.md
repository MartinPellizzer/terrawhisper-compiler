Prickly Ash can have some side effects if used improperly, such as affecting the skin to produce rashes, or even causing an irritation in the gastrointestinal tract resulting in problems like nausea and vomiting. Additionally, people can face various complications like increased heart rate, diarrhea, headaches, and excessive sweating due to its usage. In extreme cases, it may lead to a dangerous reaction called anaphylactic shock. These potential hazards underline the importance of carefully considering the appropriate dosage before taking Prickly Ash, or using other remedies when available, particularly in dealing with sensitive individuals who might be more susceptible to these negative effects.
