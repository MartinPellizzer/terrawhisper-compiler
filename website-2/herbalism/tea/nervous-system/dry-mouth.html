1. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra teas contains glycyrrhizin, a triterpenoid saponin, and flavonoids, like kaempferol and quercetin.

The flavonoids in Glycyrrhiza glabra teas also have antioxidant properties, which can help protect the mucous membranes in the mouth from damage.

By stimulating saliva production and reducing inflammation, Glycyrrhiza glabra teas may provide relief for people with dry mouth.
