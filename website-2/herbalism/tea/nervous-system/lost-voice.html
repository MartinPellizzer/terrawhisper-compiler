1. Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus teas contains several biologically active constituents, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties, which help to reduce swelling in the throat and vocal cords, making it easier to speak and regain a lost voice. The terpenes, specifically eucalyptol, have a soothing effect on the mucous membranes, calming irritation and discomfort. Additionally, flavonoids and phenolic acids have antioxidant properties, which protect the throat and vocal cords from damage and promote healing.

By reducing inflammation and promoting healing, Eucalyptus globulus teas helps to alleviate symptoms of a lost voice and support recovery.

  1. Gather 1 cup of water and 1 tablespoon of dried Eucalyptus globulus leaves.
  2. Heat the water in a pot until it boils.
  3. Add the dried Eucalyptus globulus leaves to the boiling water.
  4. Reduce heat to low and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea and drink it while it's warm to help soothe a lost voice.
