9 best mortars for apothecary

Mortars, a staple tool in apothecary shops, are shallow, bowl-like containers typically made of ceramic or marble, used for grinding and mixing various substances, including herbs, spices, and minerals.

This essential equipment enables apothecaries to prepare complex remedies and concoctions with precision and control. The flat surface and rounded edges of a mortar allow for efficient grinding and mixing of ingredients, while the pestle, a handheld tool used in conjunction with the mortar, facilitates the process. By using mortars, apothecaries can create a wide range of medicinal preparations, from salves and ointments to powders and tinctures.

Effective use of mortars requires a great deal of skill and technique, reflecting the high level of expertise required to work in an apothecary.
