13 best strainers for herbalists

A strainer, also known as a sieve or bolus, is a crucial tool for apothecaries in the preparation and dispensing of medicinal concoctions.

This handheld device features a perforated or mesh-like design, allowing for the separation of solid particles from liquids. Apothecaries utilize strainers to filter out impurities, such as sediment or unwanted debris, from potions, elixirs, and other medicinal preparations. By using a strainer, apothecaries can ensure the quality and purity of their products, thereby maintaining the health and safety of their patients.

Effective straining also helps to improve the texture and appearance of medicinal preparations.
