13 best double boilers for herbalists

A double boiler, also known as a bain-marie, is a heat-retaining cooking vessel used by apothecaries to gently warm and mix ingredients.

This product consists of a heat-resistant bowl or container placed over a pot of simmering water, allowing for precise temperature control. Apothecaries rely on double boilers to avoid scorching or overheating sensitive herbal extracts, essential oils, and other temperature-sensitive materials. The double boiler's controlled heat ensures the stability of ingredients, preserving their potency and properties.

By using a double boiler, apothecaries can create high-quality, effective remedies with precise and consistent results.
