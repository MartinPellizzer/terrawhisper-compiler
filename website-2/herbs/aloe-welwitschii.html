Aloe welwitschii

The bioactive constituents of Aloe welwitschii include aloin, aloe-emodin, and other anthraquinones, as well as glycoproteins and polysaccharides, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

In traditional medicine, Aloe welwitschii is often used to prepare topical creams, gels, and ointments, as well as oral supplements and teas, to promote wound healing and skin health.
