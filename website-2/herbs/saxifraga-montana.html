Saxifraga montana

Saxifraga montana, also known as mountain saxifrage, is a perennial plant species belonging to the saxifrage family (Saxifragaceae), native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of various conditions such as arthritis and skin irritations. The bioactive constituents of Saxifraga montana include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Saxifraga montana is often used to make teas, tinctures, and infusions, as well as being incorporated into topical creams and salves to support skin health and wound healing.
