Lavandula bipinnata

Lavandula bipinnata, also known as the pink lavender or Spanish lavender, is a species of flowering plant in the lavender genus Lavandula.

The bioactive constituents of Lavandula bipinnata include essential oils such as linalool, linalyl acetate, and camphor, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
