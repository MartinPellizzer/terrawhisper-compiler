Isolepis hystrix

Isolepis hystrix, commonly known as the spiny club-rush, is a species of sedge that belongs to the family Cyperaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Isolepis hystrix include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
