Triticum pinnatum

Triticum pinnatum, also known as Tibetan wheat grass, is a type of grass belonging to the wheat tribe and is native to the Himalayan region of Tibet and Nepal.

Triticum pinnatum is further classified within the tribe Triticeae, which includes other grasses like wheat and rye. Within this tribe, Triticum pinnatum is part of the genus Triticum, which includes several species of wild and domesticated wheat.
