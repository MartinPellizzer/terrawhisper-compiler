Limonium frutescens

Limonium frutescens, also known as sea lavender or sea pinks, is a perennial coastal plant in the family Plumbaginaceae.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including reducing inflammation and fighting off bacterial infections, which may be attributed to its antioxidant and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Limonium frutescens include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
