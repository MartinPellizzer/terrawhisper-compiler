What is Euphorbia clementei var. puberula?

Euphorbia clementei var. puberula is a succulent plant that belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae.

The leaves are opposite, sessile, and lanceolate, measuring 3-5 cm in length, with a membranous margin and a brownish-red tint on the lower side. The inflorescence is a cyathium, bearing 3-5 male flowers and a single female flower, with a greenish-yellow color.

The plant's latex is white and milky.
