Haplophyllum obtusifolium

Haplophyllum obtusifolium, also known as the Persian spurge or obtuse-leaved Haplophyllum, is a perennial shrub native to the Middle East and parts of Europe.

The bioactive constituents of Haplophyllum obtusifolium include alkaloids, flavonoids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
