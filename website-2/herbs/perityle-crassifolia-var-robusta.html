What is Perityle crassifolia var. robusta?

Perityle crassifolia var. robusta is a plant taxon in the Asteraceae family.

It is a shrub-like perennial herb with a woody base, typically 30-60 cm in height. The leaves are elliptical to lanceolate, 1-4 cm long, 0.2-1.5 cm wide, with a crenulate margin, glabrous to pubescent, and often with a reddish tinge. The inflorescence is a solitary capitate head, 1-2 cm in diameter, with 5-15 yellow flowers, each 0.5-1 cm in diameter, with 5-7 corolla lobes.

The plant is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
