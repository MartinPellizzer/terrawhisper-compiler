Valeriana mutisiana

Valeriana mutisiana, also known as the Colombian valerian, is a plant species within the Valeriana genus, commonly recognized for its medicinal properties.

The health benefits of Valeriana mutisiana include its sedative, anxiolytic, and antispasmodic effects, which can help alleviate anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness. The bioactive constituents of this plant include valepotriates, valerenic acids, and isovaleranone, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

Valeriana mutisiana is often used to make herbal preparations such as tinctures, teas, and capsules, which can be used to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and improve sleep quality.
