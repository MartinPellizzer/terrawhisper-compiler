Chrysanthemum poteriifolium

Chrysanthemum poteriifolium, also known as Yellow Chrysanthemum, is a type of flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae) native to Asia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects, making it a potential remedy for various health issues such as fever, headache, and digestive problems. The bioactive constituents of Chrysanthemum poteriifolium include flavonoids, triterpenoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

In traditional Chinese medicine, the plant is commonly prepared as a tea or infused in hot water to make a decoction, which is also known as Jing Jue Teng, to treat a range of health conditions.
