Delphinium baluchistanicum

Delphinium baluchistanicum, commonly known as Balochistan larkspur, is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Ranunculaceae.

It has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various health conditions, including inflammation, pain, and fever, due to its potential anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. The bioactive constituents of Delphinium baluchistanicum include delphinine, a diterpenoid alkaloid with reported anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial activities, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids.

Herbal preparations made from this plant include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to manage pain and reduce inflammation in traditional medicine.
