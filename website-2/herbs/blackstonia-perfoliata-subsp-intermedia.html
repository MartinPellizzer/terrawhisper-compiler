What is Blackstonia perfoliata subsp. intermedia?

Blackstonia perfoliata subsp.

intermedia is a plant taxon belonging to the family Gentianaceae. It is a perennial herb with a woody base and an almost leafless stem, typically 2-10 cm tall.

The plant has solitary, nodding flowers, 10-15 mm in diameter, with five petals and a yellow corolla with dark red or purple spots. The flowers are arranged in a single whorl in the axil of a scale-like leaf that completely surrounds the stem, creating a cup-like structure.

The plant's leaves are scale-like, 1-5 mm long, and are found at the base of the stem.
