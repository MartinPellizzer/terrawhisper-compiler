Clinopodium × thymifolium

Clinopodium × thymifolium, also known as wild bergamot mint or bergamot mint, is a hybrid plant belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae), characterized by its distinctive minty aroma and compact growth habit.

Clinopodium × thymifolium contains bioactive constituents such as linalool, limonene, and thymol, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
