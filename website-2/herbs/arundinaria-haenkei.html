Arundinaria haenkei

Arundinaria haenkei, also known as Haenke's bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to the mountains of East Asia.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine, where it is used to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and respiratory issues.
