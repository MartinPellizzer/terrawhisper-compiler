Crepis diffusa

Crepis diffusa, also known as Hawksbeard, is a perennial weed species belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The bioactive constituents of Crepis diffusa include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
