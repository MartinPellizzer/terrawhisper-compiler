Stillingia agallocha

Stillingia sanguinea, also known as Stillingia agallocha, is a plant species native to North America.

It is a perennial herb that contains compounds with anti-inflammatory and anti-arthritic properties, traditionally used to treat various health conditions.

The medicinal properties of Stillingia agallocha include its ability to reduce inflammation, relieve pain, and combat conditions such as rheumatism and arthritis.

The plant is often prepared as a tincture, salve, or infusion, and has been used in herbal medicine to promote overall health and well-being.
