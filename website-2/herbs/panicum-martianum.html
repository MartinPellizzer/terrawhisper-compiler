Panicum martianum

Panicum martianum, also known as Martian Panic Grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to alleviate conditions such as arthritis and improve overall well-being. The bioactive constituents of Panicum martianum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Martian Panic Grass is often used to make herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into topical creams and ointments for its potential skin-soothing effects.
