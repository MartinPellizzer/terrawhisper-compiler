classification

Carthamus strictus is classified as a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family (Asteraceae). It is a type of annual plant native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa. The exact classification of Carthamus strictus can be broken down further into the kingdom Plantae, followed by the division Magnoliophyta, class Magnoliopsida, order Asterales, family Asteraceae, genus Carthamus, and then the species C. strictus. This classification is based on the plant's physical characteristics and evolutionary relationships with other organisms. Carthamus strictus is also known as strict santonica or strict safflower.
