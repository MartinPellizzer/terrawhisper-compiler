Hieracium microstictum

Hieracium microstictum, also known as small-scaled hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the dandelion-like hawkweed family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been traditionally used for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of conditions such as arthritis, digestive issues, and oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Hieracium microstictum include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Hieracium microstictum is often made into tinctures, infusions, or decoctions, and is sometimes used in combination with other herbs to promote overall well-being and support the immune system.
