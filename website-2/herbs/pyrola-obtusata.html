Pyrola obtusata

Pyrola obtusata, also known as wintergreen or bearberry wintergreen, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the heath family (Ericaceae).

The plant has been traditionally used to aid in digestion and to relieve menstrual cramps, with its potential health benefits attributed to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Pyrola obtusata include salicylic acid, methyl salicylate, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Pyrola obtusata is often prepared as a tea, tincture, or infused oil, and is commonly used to treat various ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and gastrointestinal issues.
