Cnidium pyrenaeum

Cnidium pyrenaeum is a plant species commonly known as the Chinese burns sponge or cnidium, belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-arthritic properties, as well as its ability to improve cardiovascular health and reduce pain. The bioactive constituents of Cnidium pyrenaeum include coumarins, flavonoids, and volatile oils, with the primary active compound being O-hydroxyferulic acid.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Cnidium pyrenaeum is commonly prepared as a decoction or tincture, often in combination with other herbs, to treat various health conditions, including arthritis, joint pain, and cardiovascular disease.
