classification

Chroniochilus virescens is a species of flowering plant in the borage family (Boraginaceae). It belongs to the genus Chroniochilus and is classified as a member of the family Boraginaceae. The plant is native to India and is part of the order Lamiales. Within the Boraginaceae family, Chroniochilus virescens is a member of the tribe Cynoglosseae. It is further classified into the subfamily Cynoglossoideae.
