Swida amomum

Swida amomum, also known as Sweet Gale or Chinese Sweet Gale, is a perennial shrub native to East Asia, characterized by its fragrant, aromatic leaves and stems.

The health benefits of Swida amomum include its antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and expectorant properties, making it useful in treating respiratory issues such as bronchitis and coughs. The bioactive constituents of this plant include essential oils, glycosides, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Swida amomum is commonly prepared into teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being used in the production of essential oils, which are used to relieve respiratory and digestive issues.
