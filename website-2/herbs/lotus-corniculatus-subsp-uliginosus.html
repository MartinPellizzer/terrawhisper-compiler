What is Lotus corniculatus subsp. uliginosus?

The flowers are pink to purple, 5-7 mm long, and are in axillary racemes, producing 2-4 seeds per pod, which are brown, ovate, and 3-4 mm long. This subspecies grows in wet meadows and marshes, typically in areas with full sun to partial shade, and is native to Europe, North Africa, and Western Asia.
