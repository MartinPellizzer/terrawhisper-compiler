Seseli bunius

Seseli bunius, also known as Bunius or Sichuan Lovage, is a type of perennial plant in the carrot family (Apiaceae) native to the mountainous regions of China.

Bioactive constituents present in Seseli bunius include sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and essential oils, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.

The plant is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine and can be found in various herbal preparations, including teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to treat a range of health issues, including indigestion, diarrhea, and menstrual cramps.
