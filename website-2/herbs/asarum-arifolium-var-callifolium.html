What is Asarum arifolium var. callifolium?

Asarum arifolium var. callifolium is a botanical variety of the Asarum arifolium species within the Aristolochiaceae family.

This variety is characterized by heart-shaped leaves with cordate bases, measuring up to 7-9 cm in length and 6-8 cm in width, and having a glossy green color. The leaves are borne on a short rhizome with fleshy, scale-like leaves, and they are arranged in a basal rosette pattern. Flowers are absent in this variety, but the plant produces red-brown, elliptical fruits, 1-1.5 cm in length, containing numerous seeds.

Asarum arifolium var. callifolium is native to East Asia, specifically in China, Japan, and Korea.
