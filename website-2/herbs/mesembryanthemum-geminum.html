Mesembryanthemum geminum

Mesembryanthemum geminum, also known as the twin succulent or twin-leaf ice plant, is a succulent plant species native to Australia and New Zealand.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may help to soothe skin irritations and promote wound healing. The bioactive constituents of Mesembryanthemum geminum include saponins, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the plant is often used to make infusions, tinctures, and salves, which are used topically to treat skin conditions, such as eczema and acne.
