Proboscidea althaeifolia

Proboscidea althaeifolia, also known as Velvet-leaf or Devil's Claw, is a perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The bioactive constituents of Proboscidea althaeifolia include harpagoside, harpagide, and procumbide, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
