Ziziphora rigida

Ziziphora rigida, also known as Russian tarragon or Persian tarragon, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and the Caucasus.

The bioactive constituents of Ziziphora rigida include essential oils, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
