Senegalia angustifolia

Senegalia angustifolia, also known as Senegal thorn or Cassia tora, is a plant species belonging to the Fabaceae family of legumes.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, contributing to its potential in treating various health conditions such as fever, rheumatism, and skin infections. The bioactive constituents of Senegalia angustifolia include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
