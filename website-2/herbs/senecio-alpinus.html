Senecio alpinus

Senecio alpinus, also known as Alpine Ragwort, is a flowering plant species belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Senecio alpinus include sesquiterpene lactones, such as alpinum and jacolin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditional herbal preparations made from Senecio alpinus include infusions, tinctures, and decoctions, often used to treat digestive problems, skin conditions, and respiratory issues.
