What is Linaria alpina subsp. filicaulis?

Linaria alpina subsp.

filicaulis is a plant taxon that belongs to the family Plantaginaceae. It is a perennial herb with linear-lanceolate leaves that are 5-20 mm long and 0.5-2 mm wide, and are arranged in a rosette.

The leaves are glabrous or sparsely hairy, and are often purple-tinged. The plant produces upright, hairy spikes of tubular flowers that are 15-30 mm long and are pale blue or white in color, typically with purple or yellow markings.

The flowers have a four-lobed corolla with a tube that is slightly longer than the lobes.
