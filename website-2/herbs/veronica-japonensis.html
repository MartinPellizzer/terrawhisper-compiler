Veronica japonensis

Veronica japonensis, also known as Japanese speedwell, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Veronicaceae family, native to East Asia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, including its ability to treat fever, rheumatism, and respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. The bioactive constituents of Veronica japonensis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
