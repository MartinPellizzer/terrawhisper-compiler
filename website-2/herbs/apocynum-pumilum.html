Apocynum pumilum

Apocynum pumilum, also known as dwarf dogbane, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Apocynaceae family, characterized by its small size and milky sap.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making it a possible remedy for treating conditions such as arthritis and skin irritations. The bioactive constituents of Apocynum pumilum include apocynin, a lignan with potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, as well as other compounds like flavonoids and phenolic acids.

Traditionally, dwarf dogbane is used to make various herbal preparations, including teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of ailments, including fever, digestive issues, and respiratory problems.
