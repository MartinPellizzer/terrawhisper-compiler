Erigeron klamathensis

Erigeron klamathensis, also known as Klamath fleabane, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States and Canada, belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

Bioactive constituents of Erigeron klamathensis include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
