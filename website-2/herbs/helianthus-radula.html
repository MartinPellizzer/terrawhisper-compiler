classification

Helianthus radula is classified in the kingdom Plantae, and within this kingdom, it belongs to the phylum Magnoliophyta. Further classification places it in the class Magnoliopsida. The order Asterales is the next classification level, followed by the family Asteraceae, commonly known as the sunflower family. This family is then divided into the subfamily Helianthoideae, and finally, the genus Helianthus. Within this genus, Helianthus radula is a specific species.
