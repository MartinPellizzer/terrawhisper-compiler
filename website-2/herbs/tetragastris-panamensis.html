Tetragastris panamensis

Tetragastris panamensis, also known as Palo Santo, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America, belonging to the Burseraceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Tetragastris panamensis include sesquiterpenes, diterpenes, and essential oils, particularly limonene, pinene, and sabinene, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
