What is Hieracium doronicifolium subsp. seneciiflorum?

Hieracium doronicifolium subsp.

seneciiflorum is a taxonomic subspecies of the Hieracium genus, belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its leaves, which are pinnate and have 5-7 pairs of lobes, and its stems, which are erect, 5-15 cm tall, and sparsely hairy.

The plant produces yellow flowers in a capitulum, 2-3 cm in diameter, with 8-12 ray florets and 50-60 disc florets. The flowers are hermaphroditic, with the corolla being 5-6 mm long, and the anthers 1.5-2 mm long.

This subspecies is found in mountainous areas, typically at high elevations, in the Iberian Peninsula.
