Dalea leucantha

Dalea leucantha, also known as White Prairie Clover, is a perennial legume native to North America, valued for its medicinal properties and potential as a forage crop.

The bioactive constituents of Dalea leucantha include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal activities.
