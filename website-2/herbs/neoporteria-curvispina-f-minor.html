What is Neoporteria curvispina f. minor?

Neoporteria curvispina f. minor is a variant of the species Neoporteria curvispina, belonging to the family Cactaceae.

The stems are green, with a diameter of 2-3 cm and a height of 2-5 cm. The flowers are funnel-shaped, pale yellow in color, and approximately 3-4 cm in length.

This variant of Neoporteria curvispina typically produces yellow flowers in the spring and summer months.
