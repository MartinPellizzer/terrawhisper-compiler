Ponera dubia

Ponera dubia, also known as Antrodia camphorata or simply Taiwan honeysuckle, is a type of medicinal plant belonging to the Polyporaceae family.

The health benefits of Ponera dubia include its potential anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory effects, which may help alleviate symptoms of various diseases such as fever, cough, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include triterpenoids, polysaccharides, and phenolic compounds, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Ponera dubia is commonly prepared in the form of teas, infusions, or dried extracts, as well as being used in combination with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects.

side effects

Ponera dubia is known to cause irritation and allergic reactions in some individuals, particularly when the plant's sap comes into contact with the skin. This irritation can manifest as redness, itching, and swelling. In severe cases, an allergic reaction may lead to more serious symptoms, such as difficulty breathing or rapid heartbeat. Ponera dubia sap can also cause eye irritation and should be avoided if you have sensitive eyes. It is essential to handle the plant with caution and wear protective gloves if necessary.
