Senna velutina

Senna velutina is a plant species belonging to the Legume family, commonly known for its use in traditional medicine.

The health benefits of Senna velutina include its ability to act as a natural laxative and to treat digestive issues such as constipation, due to its high concentration of anthraquinone glycosides. The bioactive constituents of Senna velutina include sennosides A and B, which are responsible for its purgative properties, as well as other compounds like flavonoids and phenolic acids.

In traditional medicine, Senna velutina is often used to make herbal preparations such as tea, capsules, and tinctures, which are commonly used to treat digestive disorders and promote bowel movements.
