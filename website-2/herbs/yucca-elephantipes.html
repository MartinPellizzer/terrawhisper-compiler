classification

Yucca elephantipes is classified as a species of flowering plant in the family Asparagaceae, specifically within the subfamily Agavoideae. It is native to Mexico and Central America. The plant's botanical name Yucca elephantipes translates to "elephant foot" due to its large, bulbous trunk. This classification places it among the many succulent plants that have adapted to survive in dry, arid environments. As a member of the Asparagaceae family, Yucca elephantipes is closely related to other plants such as agaves and hostas.
