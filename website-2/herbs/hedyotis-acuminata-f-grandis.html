What is Hedyotis acuminata f. grandis?

Hedyotis acuminata f. grandis is a variety of Hedyotis acuminata, a species of flowering plant in the family Rubiaceae.

The leaves are glabrous or have a few scattered hairs, and are often provided with a pair of caducous stipules. The inflorescence is a terminal, 1-2-flowered cyme, with flowers having a greenish-yellow corolla, 5-6 mm in diameter, and a calyx with 5 lobes.
