The bioactive constituents of Blumea viscosa include flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, the plant is commonly prepared as a decoction, powder, or infusion, and is often used in herbal remedies such as cough syrups, expectorants, and anti-inflammatory ointments.
