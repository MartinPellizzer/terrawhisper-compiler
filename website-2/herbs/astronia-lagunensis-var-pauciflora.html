What is Astronia lagunensis var. pauciflora?

Astronia lagunensis var. pauciflora is a plant species within the family Melastomataceae.

It is a shrub with simple, alternate, and coriaceous leaves, measuring 6-15 cm in length and 2-5 cm in width, with a cuneate base and an acute apex. The leaves are glabrous on the upper surface and tomentose on the lower surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with 4-6 petals, arranged in axillary cymules, and has an inflorescence with 1-5 flowers.

The flowers are dioecious, with male flowers having 4-6 stamens and female flowers having 2-4 staminodes.
