What is Alnus rugosa var. americana?

Alnus rugosa var. americana is a deciduous shrub or small tree belonging to the family Betulaceae, native to eastern North America.

It has a smooth, grayish-brown to reddish-brown bark, with a conical, broad, or rounded crown and a straight or slightly curved trunk. The flowers are monoecious, with catkins 2-4 cm long, produced in the spring, and the cones are pendulous, 4-6 cm long, and 1.5-2 cm wide, produced in the fall.

The wood of Alnus rugosa var. americana is hard and reddish-brown, used for furniture, flooring, and other wood products.
