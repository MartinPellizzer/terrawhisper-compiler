Cryptantha texana

Cryptantha texana, also known as Texas cryptantha, is a perennial plant species in the borage family (Boraginaceae) characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers and distinctive leaf shapes.

Bioactive constituents of Cryptantha texana include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which have been shown to possess antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activities.
