Potentilla rubens

Potentilla rubens, also known as Cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the rose family (Rosaceae).

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects, making it useful in treating wounds and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Potentilla rubens include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Potentilla rubens include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to support the immune system and promote overall well-being.
