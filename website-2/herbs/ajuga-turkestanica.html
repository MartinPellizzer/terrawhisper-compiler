Ajuga turkestanica

Ajuga turkestanica is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae) that is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, making it useful in treating various health conditions such as wounds, respiratory issues, and gastrointestinal problems. The bioactive constituents of Ajuga turkestanica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoid glycosides, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Ajuga turkestanica include infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of health issues and promote overall well-being.
