Tephrosia uliginosa

Tephrosia uliginosa, also known as Marsh Tephrosia, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The bioactive constituents of Marsh Tephrosia include isoflavones, alkaloids, and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
