medicinal uses

Ruellia scarlatina, also known as the Mexican petunia, has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various ailments. The plant's leaves and stems are used in teas, infusions, and decoctions to relieve fever, inflammation, and respiratory issues. In some parts of Mexico, Ruellia scarlatina is used to treat digestive problems and skin conditions. Its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties make it a popular remedy for wounds and minor injuries. Further research is needed to confirm the efficacy and safety of using Ruellia scarlatina for medicinal purposes.
