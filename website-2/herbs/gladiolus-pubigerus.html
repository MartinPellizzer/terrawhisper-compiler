Gladiolus pubigerus

Gladiolus pubigerus, commonly known as the pubescent gladiolus, is a species of flowering plant in the iris family.

The bioactive constituents of Gladiolus pubigerus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are believed to contribute to its therapeutic effects.
