Melinis maroccana

Melinis maroccana, also known as Moroccan grass or Moroccan feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to Morocco and parts of North Africa.

The bioactive constituents of Melinis maroccana include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, the plant is often prepared as a tea or infusion, and in some cases, it is used as a tincture or in capsule form, to leverage its health benefits.
