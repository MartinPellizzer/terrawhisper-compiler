Ilex minutifolia

Ilex minutifolia, also known as the Mexican Holly or Small-leafed Holly, is a shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

The bioactive constituents of Ilex minutifolia include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
