Rubus schorleri

Rubus schorleri, also known as the Schorler's bramble or Schorler's blackberry, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Rubus schorleri include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and ellagitannins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
