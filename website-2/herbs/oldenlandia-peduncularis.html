Oldenlandia peduncularis

Oldenlandia peduncularis, also known as Lian Qiao or Oldenlandia, is a plant species belonging to the family Rubiaceae, commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of fever, cough, and respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of Oldenlandia peduncularis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Oldenlandia is used to make herbal teas, decoctions, and infusions, as well as being incorporated into various Chinese medicinal formulas, such as Lian Qiao San, to treat various health conditions.
