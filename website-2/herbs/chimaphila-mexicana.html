Chimaphila mexicana

Chimaphila mexicana, commonly known as Mexican wintergreen, is a perennial plant species belonging to the heath family (Ericaceae).

Bioactive constituents of Chimaphila mexicana include phenolic acids, flavonoids, and iridoid glycosides, which are thought to contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, the plant is often prepared as a tea or tincture, as well as being incorporated into ointments and salves for topical use.
