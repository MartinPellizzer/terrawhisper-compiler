Senegalia merrillii

Senegalia merrillii, also known as Acacia merrillii, is a species of tree in the Fabaceae family, native to the tropical forests of the Philippines.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it useful for treating various health conditions such as wounds, fever, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Senegalia merrillii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Senegalia merrillii is commonly prepared as a decoction, tea, or tincture, and is also used in the production of herbal capsules and powders.
