What is Hieracium schmidtii subsp. floccosum?

Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

floccosum is a subspecies of Hieracium schmidtii, a plant in the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette of leaves that are lanceolate, 1-2.5 cm long, and 2-4 mm wide, and its stemless habit.

The plant produces yellow dandelion-like flowers in a corymbiform corymb, 2-5 cm in diameter, in July and August. The flowers have 10-15 ray florets and 20-30 disc florets, and are surrounded by a covering of white, cotton-like hairs. Hieracium schmidtii subsp.

floccosum is found in high mountain meadows of the Alps.
