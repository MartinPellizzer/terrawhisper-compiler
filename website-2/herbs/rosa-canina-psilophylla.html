Rosa canina psilophylla

Rosa canina psilophylla, also known as dog rose or rose hip, is a deciduous shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its fragrant flowers and edible, vitamin-rich fruit.

The health benefits of Rosa canina psilophylla include its potential to boost the immune system, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antioxidant properties, making it a popular ingredient in natural remedies for colds and flu. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and carotenoids, as well as vitamins A and C, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Rosa canina psilophylla is commonly used to make herbal tea, infusions, and extracts, as well as being incorporated into supplements and jams, to harness its health benefits.
