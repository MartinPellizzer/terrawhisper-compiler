Matricaria multiflora

Matricaria multiflora, commonly known as multiflowered chamomile or wild chamomile, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Matricaria multiflora include essential oils such as chamazulene, sesquiterpene lactones, and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
