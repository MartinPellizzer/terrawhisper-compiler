classification

Aloe rigida is classified as a species of succulent plant in the family Asphodelaceae and is further grouped into the genus Aloe. It belongs to the kingdom Plantae, phylum Angiospermophyta, class Liliopsida, and order Asparagales. Aloe rigida is native to South Africa, where it grows in dry, rocky areas and is often associated with scrubby vegetation. This plant is known for its sharp, rigid leaves and tall stem that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. Aloe rigida is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Internal use of Aloe rigida is not recommended, especially for pregnant or breastfeeding women.
