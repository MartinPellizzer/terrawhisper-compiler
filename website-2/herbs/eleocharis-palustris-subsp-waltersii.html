What is Eleocharis palustris subsp. waltersii?

waltersii is a plant species belonging to the family Cyperaceae. It is a perennial aquatic rhizomatous herb with a short stem and a few scales at the base of the leaves.

The plant's leaves are reduced to scales, which is a characteristic of the Eleocharis genus.
