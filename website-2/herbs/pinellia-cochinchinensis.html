Pinellia cochinchinensis

Pinellia cochinchinensis, also known as Ban Xia in traditional Chinese medicine, is a type of plant belonging to the Arum family.

It is valued for its potential health benefits, including the alleviation of respiratory issues such as asthma and bronchitis, as well as its ability to aid digestion and relieve symptoms of nausea and vomiting. The bioactive constituents of Pinellia cochinchinensis include alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Pinellia cochinchinensis is often used to make herbal preparations such as Ban Xia Huo Xue Tang, a decoction that is used to treat respiratory issues, and Ban Xia Ba He Tang, a decoction that is used to treat digestive problems.
