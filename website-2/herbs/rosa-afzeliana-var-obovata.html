What is Rosa afzeliana var. obovata?

Rosa afzeliana var. obovata is a botanical variety of the species Rosa afzeliana, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by obovate leaves, typically 2.5-4 cm long and 1-2.5 cm wide, with 5-7 leaflets, and a leaflet tip that is cuneate. The flowers are pink to reddish-purple, 3-5 cm in diameter, with 5 petals, and are produced in clusters at the end of branches. The stems are thorny, 2-3 mm in diameter, and covered with pubescence.

Rosa afzeliana var. obovata is native to the Himalayas and southwestern China.
