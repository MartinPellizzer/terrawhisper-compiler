Tillandsia cerosa

Tillandsia cerosa, also known as the "Air Plant", is a type of epiphytic bromeliad that obtains moisture and nutrients from the air, rather than through soil.

The bioactive constituents of Tillandsia cerosa include flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
