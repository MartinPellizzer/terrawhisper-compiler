Castilleja racemosa

Castilleja racemosa, also known as Indian paintbrush, is a perennial plant that belongs to the family Orobanchaceae and is native to the western coast of North America.

The health benefits of Castilleja racemosa include its potential to reduce inflammation, improve respiratory health, and act as an antispasmodic agent. The bioactive constituents of this plant include alkaloids, such as rosmarinic acid, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Castilleja racemosa is commonly used in herbal preparations, including teas, tinctures, and capsules, to alleviate symptoms of colds, coughs, and bronchitis, as well as to calm digestive issues and promote relaxation.
