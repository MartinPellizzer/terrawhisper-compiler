The bioactive constituents of Aristea dichotoma include iridoid glycosides, such as aristol and aristenin, as well as other iridoid compounds like 6-hydroxy-7,8-dihydroaristol.

Aristea dichotoma is often used to make herbal teas, infusions, and extracts, which are commonly used to treat coughs, colds, and digestive problems.
