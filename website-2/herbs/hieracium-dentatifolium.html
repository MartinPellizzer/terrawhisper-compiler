Hieracium dentatifolium

Hieracium dentatifolium, also known as hawkweed, is a flowering plant species in the dandelion family (Asteraceae) characterized by its serrated leaves and bright yellow flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Hieracium dentatifolium include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with hawkweed include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to promote immune function, reduce inflammation, and alleviate respiratory issues.
