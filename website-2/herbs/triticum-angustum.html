classification

Triticum angustum is a species within the genus Triticum, which includes various types of wheat. The genus Triticum is part of the tribe Hordeae and the subfamily Pooideae, both within the family Poaceae. Poaceae is a large family of flowering plants commonly known as grasses. The family Poaceae is divided into several subfamilies, including Pooideae and Panicoideae. The classification of Triticum angustum reflects its evolutionary relationships to other grasses.
