Vitellaria marginata

Vitellaria marginata, also known as the shea tree or karite tree, is a tropical tree species native to Africa, valued for its oil-rich seeds and medicinal properties.

The health benefits of Vitellaria marginata include its ability to moisturize and protect the skin, reduce inflammation, and improve wound healing, making it a popular ingredient in skincare products and traditional medicine. The bioactive constituents of this plant include fatty acids, such as oleic and stearic acid, as well as triterpenes and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal and cosmetic properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Vitellaria marginata is often used to make creams, lotions, and ointments, as well as teas and infusions, to treat skin conditions, wounds, and other health issues.
