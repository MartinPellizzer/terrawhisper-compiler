Thymus commutatus

Thymus commutatus, also known as creeping thyme, is a low-growing, spreading perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

The bioactive constituents of Thymus commutatus include thymol, carvacrol, and borneol, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
