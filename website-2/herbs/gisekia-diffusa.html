Gisekia diffusa

Gisekia diffusa, also known as the Drunkard's Plum or False Mallow, is a perennial plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its showy white flowers and heart-shaped leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Gisekia diffusa include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
