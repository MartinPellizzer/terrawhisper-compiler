Astragalus kirilovii

Astragalus kirilovii, also known as Huangqi, is a perennial plant species belonging to the legume family (Fabaceae), traditionally used in Chinese medicine.

The health benefits of Astragalus kirilovii include its immunomodulatory and anti-inflammatory properties, making it effective in boosting the immune system, reducing fatigue, and alleviating symptoms of various diseases. The bioactive constituents of Astragalus kirilovii include saponins, flavonoids, and polysaccharides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Astragalus kirilovii is commonly prepared as a decoction or used in combination with other herbs in formulas such as Huangqi decoction, which is often used to treat respiratory infections and promote overall well-being.
