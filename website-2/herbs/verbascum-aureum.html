Verbascum aureum

Verbascum aureum, also known as Yellow Mullein, is a perennial plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family, characterized by its yellow flowers and velvety leaves.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antiviral properties, making it effective in treating respiratory issues, skin infections, and wounds. The bioactive constituents of Verbascum aureum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Verbascum aureum is commonly prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture to treat various health conditions, particularly respiratory issues such as bronchitis, coughs, and colds.
