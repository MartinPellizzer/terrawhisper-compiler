Chrysanthemum seticuspe

The Chrysanthemum seticuspe, also known as the Setitip Chrysanthemum, is a type of flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine for its health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and antioxidant properties, which can help to relieve fever, headache, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Chrysanthemum seticuspe include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and volatile oils, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made from this plant include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat colds, flu, and other respiratory infections.
