Rosa × centifolia bullata

Rosa × centifolia bullata, also known as Bullata Rose, is a fragrant, climbing rose species native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its unique, compact growth habit and abundant, fragrant flowers.

The health benefits of Rosa × centifolia bullata include its potential to reduce inflammation, improve mood, and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and insomnia, due to its calming and sedative properties. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, particularly geraniol and linalool, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Rosa × centifolia bullata include rose tea, rose oil, and rose petal extracts, which are used in traditional medicine for their therapeutic and cosmetic applications.
