Cordyline magnifica

Cordyline magnifica, commonly known as the cabbage palm or ti plant, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand and Australia.

The bioactive constituents of Cordyline magnifica include cordycepin, a compound with immunomodulatory and antiviral properties, as well as other polysaccharides and alkaloids that may contribute to its medicinal effects.
