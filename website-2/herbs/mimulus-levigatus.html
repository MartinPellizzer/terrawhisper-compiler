Mimulus levigatus

Mimulus levigatus, also known as smooth monkey flower, is a perennial plant species native to the western United States.

The bioactive constituents of Mimulus levigatus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.
