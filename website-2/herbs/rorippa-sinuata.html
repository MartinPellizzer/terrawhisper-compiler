Rorippa sinuata

Rorippa sinuata, also known as western watercress or sharppeaked pepperwort, is a perennial aquatic plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Rorippa sinuata include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and glucosinolates, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
