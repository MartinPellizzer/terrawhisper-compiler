Trisetaria dufourei

Trisetaria dufourei, also known as Dufour's hair-grass, is a perennial grass species belonging to the family Poaceae.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Bioactive constituents of Trisetaria dufourei include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
