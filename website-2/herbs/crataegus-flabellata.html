Crataegus flabellata

Crataegus flabellata, also known as the Fan-leaved Hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China, belonging to the Rosaceae family.

The Fan-leaved Hawthorn is valued for its medicinal properties, which include lowering blood pressure, improving cardiovascular health, and providing antioxidant effects, making it a popular natural remedy for heart conditions. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Crataegus flabellata include tea, capsules, and extracts, often used to support cardiovascular health and reduce the risk of cardiovascular disease.
