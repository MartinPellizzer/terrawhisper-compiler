What is Anthriscus sylvestris subsp. alpestris?

Anthriscus sylvestris subsp.

alpestris is a perennial plant belonging to the family Apiaceae. It has a slender stem, typically 30-90 cm tall, with a glabrous to pubescent base and pubescent upper part.

The plant's leaves are pinnatisect or bipinnatisect, with 4-6 pairs of pinnae, and have a linear-lanceolate shape, 10-30 cm long and 2-6 cm wide. The inflorescence is a compound umbel, bearing 10-30 radiate umbellules, each with 10-30 small white flowers, and a diameter of 5-10 cm.

The plant's fruit is a schizocarp, consisting of 5-8 mericarps, 5-7 mm long, with a length/width ratio of 1.4-2.5.
