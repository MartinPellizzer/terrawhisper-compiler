side effects

Palladia can cause side effects such as nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, fatigue, and anemia in some patients. The medication may also lead to low blood cell counts, including neutropenia and thrombocytopenia. Additionally, patients taking Palladia may experience mouth sores, hair loss, and skin rash. In rare cases, Palladia can cause more serious side effects, such as bleeding and infections. It is essential for patients to discuss the potential side effects with their healthcare provider before starting treatment.
