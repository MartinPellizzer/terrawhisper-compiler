What is Scirpus maritimus var. fernaldii?

The leaves are typically flat at the base and keeled at the tip, with a distinct midrib. The plant produces a dense, brownish-gray spike, 5-15 cm long, containing numerous, small, greenish-yellow flowers that are arranged in a single whorl, with the lower flowers being sterile and the upper ones fertile.
