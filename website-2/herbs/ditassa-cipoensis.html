Ditassa cipoensis

Ditassa cipoensis is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae, native to Brazil.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine, and its extracts have been shown to possess various health benefits, including antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Ditassa cipoensis include alkaloids, such as ditassinine and nor-ditassinine, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Ditassa cipoensis include infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs.
