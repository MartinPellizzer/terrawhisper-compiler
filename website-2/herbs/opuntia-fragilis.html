Opuntia fragilis

Opuntia fragilis, also known as brittle prickly pear, is a type of cactus plant that belongs to the Opuntia genus.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antiviral properties, which can aid in wound healing, reducing fever, and relieving digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Opuntia fragilis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Opuntia fragilis include teas, tinctures, and topical creams, which are often used to treat skin conditions, digestive issues, and respiratory problems.
