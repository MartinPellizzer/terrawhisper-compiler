Lobelia natalensis

Lobelia natalensis, also known as Lobelia, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa, belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Lobelia natalensis include alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
