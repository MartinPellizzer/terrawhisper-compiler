Solanum villosum

Solanum villosum, also known as the hairy nightshade or villosum nightshade, is a perennial plant species native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various health issues, including fever, rheumatism, and respiratory problems, due to its potential anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties. The bioactive constituents of Solanum villosum include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Solanum villosum include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat colds, coughs, and skin conditions.
