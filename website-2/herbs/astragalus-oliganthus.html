Astragalus oliganthus

Astragalus oliganthus, also known as Mongolian milkvetch, is a perennial plant species native to the mountainous regions of China and Mongolia, belonging to the legume family (Fabaceae).

The root of Astragalus oliganthus has been used in traditional medicine for its adaptogenic and immunomodulatory properties, offering health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which can enhance cardiovascular health and boost the immune system. The bioactive constituents of Astragalus oliganthus root include flavonoids, saponins, and polysaccharides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Astragalus oliganthus is commonly prepared as a decoction, capsule, or tablet in traditional Chinese medicine and modern herbal supplements, often combined with other herbs to enhance its adaptogenic and immune-boosting effects.
