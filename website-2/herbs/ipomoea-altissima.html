Ipomoea altissima

Ipomoea altissima, also known as Tall Morning Glory, is a perennial climbing plant native to the Americas, characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant purple flowers.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, which include reducing inflammation, relieving anxiety and stress, and improving sleep quality. The bioactive constituents of Ipomoea altissima include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

In herbal medicine, Ipomoea altissima is commonly prepared as a tea or tincture, and is also used in infusions and supplements to support overall well-being and relaxation.
