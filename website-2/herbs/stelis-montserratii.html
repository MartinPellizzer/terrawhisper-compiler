Stelis montserratii

Stelis montserratii, commonly known as the "Mountain Orchid", is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which have been shown to aid in the treatment of various ailments such as fever, coughs, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Stelis montserratii include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Stelis montserratii is commonly prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is often used to treat respiratory conditions, fever, and as an antiseptic.
