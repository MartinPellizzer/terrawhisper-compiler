classification

Populus × brabantica is a hybrid cultivar of the black poplar (Populus nigra) and the white poplar (Populus alba). It is primarily used as an ornamental tree due to its attractive foliage and robust growth. The classification of Populus × brabantica is a man-made cultivar, making it a non-scientific classification. However, it is often classified within the species Populus section Aigeiros. As a cultivar, its classification is based on its specific traits and characteristics rather than a strict botanical classification.
