Rhodiola rotundifolia

Rhodiola rotundifolia, also known as golden root or Arctic root, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Rhodiola rotundifolia include rosavin, rosarin, and salidroside, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Rhodiola rotundifolia is commonly used in herbal preparations such as capsules, tablets, and tea, and is often combined with other adaptogenic herbs to create formulas that support mental clarity and resilience.
