Bulbophyllum capillipes

Bulbophyllum capillipes, also known as the hanging bulb orchid, is a type of epiphytic orchid that grows in the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including fever, inflammation, and respiratory problems, due to its potential antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Bulbophyllum capillipes include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Bulbophyllum capillipes is often prepared as a decoction or a tincture, and is commonly used to treat respiratory infections, coughs, and sore throats.
