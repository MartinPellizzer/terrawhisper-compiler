What is Medium grandiflorum?

Medium grandiflorum is a species of flowering plant in the family Araliaceae.

It is a perennial herb with an erect stem, reaching heights of 2-3 meters. The leaves are pinnate, with 3-7 pairs of leaflets, measuring 10-20 cm in length. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in a compound umbel, which are followed by red or purple berries.

Medium grandiflorum is native to China, specifically in the provinces of Sichuan and Guizhou.
