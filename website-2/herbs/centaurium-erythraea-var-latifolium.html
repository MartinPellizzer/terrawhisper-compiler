What is Centaurium erythraea var. latifolium?

Centaurium erythraea var. latifolium is a plant species belonging to the family Gentianaceae.

It is a perennial herb with a taproot system, characterized by a stem that grows up to 10-30 cm in height and 1-3 cm in diameter. The leaves are alternate, elliptic in shape, and measure 5-10 cm in length and 2-4 cm in width. The flowers are purple, radially symmetrical, and have 4 petals, occurring in axillary clusters.

This variety of Centaurium erythraea is distinguished from the species by its larger leaves and narrower inflorescences.
