Lithocarpus elaeagnifolius

Lithocarpus elaeagnifolius, also known as the Pagoda tree or Lithocarpus, is a species of evergreen tree in the beech family, Fagaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Lithocarpus elaeagnifolius include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Lithocarpus elaeagnifolius is commonly prepared as a tea or decoction to relieve coughs, colds, and flu symptoms, or as a tincture to treat fever and inflammation.
