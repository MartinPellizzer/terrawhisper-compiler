Hesperis fenestralis

Hesperis fenestralis, also known as the fenestrate evening primrose, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Hesperis fenestralis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic potential.
