Euphrasia mollis

Euphrasia mollis, commonly known as the smooth eyebright, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Orobanchaceae, traditionally used to treat eye disorders and respiratory issues.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include iridoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

In herbal medicine, Euphrasia mollis is often prepared as a tincture, eye drops, or infusion, and is commonly used to treat eye infections, colds, and flu, as well as to promote eye health and relieve respiratory issues.
