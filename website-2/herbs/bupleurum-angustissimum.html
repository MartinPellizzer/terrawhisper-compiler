Bupleurum angustissimum

Bupleurum angustissimum, also known as narrow-leaved hare's ear, is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Apiaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to exhibit anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antiviral properties, which can help to reduce fever, alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression, and boost the immune system. The bioactive constituents of Bupleurum angustissimum include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Bupleurum angustissimum is commonly prepared as a decoction or tea, or made into capsules or tablets, and is often combined with other herbs such as licorice root and ginseng to enhance its therapeutic effects.
