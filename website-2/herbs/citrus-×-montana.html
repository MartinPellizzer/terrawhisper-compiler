classification

Citrus × montana is a hybrid of the pummelo (Citrus maxima) and the orange (Citrus sinensis). It is classified as a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae. The genus Citrus is a group of flowering plants that includes various citrus fruits such as oranges, lemons, and limes. Citrus × montana is one of the many hybrids of the Citrus genus, resulting from cross-breeding different species to produce unique characteristics. This hybrid is native to Southeast Asia and is also known as the pomelo-orange or sweet orange-pomelo.

Citrus × montana is a hybrid of orange and lemon, and its side effects are not well-documented.
