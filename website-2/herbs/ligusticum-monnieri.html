Ligusticum monnieri

Ligusticum mongolicum, also known as Mongolian angelica or Wang bu liu xing, is a plant species native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Ligusticum mongolicum include sesquiterpenes, phenolic compounds, and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
