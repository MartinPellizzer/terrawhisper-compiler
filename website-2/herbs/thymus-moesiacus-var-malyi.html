What is Thymus moesiacus var. malyi?

Thymus moesiacus var. malyi is a taxonomic variety within the species Thymus moesiacus, belonging to the family Lamiaceae.

It is a perennial herb with an upright growth habit, typically reaching heights of 10-30 cm, with a dense, woody base. The leaves are ovate, 2-6 mm long, and have a crenate margin, being pubescent and glandular-dotted. The inflorescence is a terminal spike, composed of small, tubular flowers with a pinkish-purple color, which are also glandular-dotted and densely pubescent.

The plant is native to the Balkan Peninsula and parts of Turkey.
