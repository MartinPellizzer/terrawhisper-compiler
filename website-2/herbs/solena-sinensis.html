Solena sinensis

Solena sinensis, also known as the Chinese bur cucumber or Chinese gourd, is a perennial vine native to East Asia.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antiviral properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of fever, sore throats, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Solena sinensis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Solena sinensis is commonly prepared into teas, infusions, or tinctures, which are often used to treat fever, cough, and sore throats, as well as to promote digestion and alleviate nausea.
