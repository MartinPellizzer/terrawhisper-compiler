Psychotria ipecacuanha

Psychotria ipecacuanha, also known as ipecacuanha or ipecac, is a type of plant in the Rubiaceae family that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine.

The health benefits of this plant include its ability to induce vomiting and relieve symptoms of nausea and indigestion, as well as its potential anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties. The bioactive constituents of ipecacuanha include alkaloids such as emetine and cephaeline, which are responsible for its emetic properties.

Ipecacuanha is commonly used in herbal preparations, including tinctures, capsules, and syrups, to treat a variety of ailments, including nausea and vomiting associated with gastrointestinal issues and motion sickness.
