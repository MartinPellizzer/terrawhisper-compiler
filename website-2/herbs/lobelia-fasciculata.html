Lobelia fasciculata

The bioactive constituents of Lobelia fasciculata include lobeline, lobelanine, and other alkaloids, which are believed to contribute to the plant's medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Lobelia fasciculata is often prepared as a tincture, infusion, or capsule, and is used to treat respiratory issues, as well as to support the body's natural detoxification processes.
