Convolvulus × dubius

Convolvulus × dubius, also known as climbing bindweed, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Convolvulus × dubius include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
