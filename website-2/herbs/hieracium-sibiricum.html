Hieracium sibiricum

Hieracium sibiricum, also known as Siberian hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

Traditionally, Hieracium sibiricum has been used in folk medicine to treat various ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and gastrointestinal disorders, due to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant activities.

The most common herbal preparations made from Hieracium sibiricum include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to support immune function, reduce inflammation, and promote overall well-being.
