Lygos raetam

Lygos raetam, also known as African pepper tree or Raetam, is a small, deciduous shrub native to the savannas and grasslands of sub-Saharan Africa.

The bioactive constituents of Lygos raetam include flavonoids, tannins, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional African medicine, Lygos raetam is often prepared as a tea or infused in water to treat various health conditions, and it is also used in the production of traditional remedies such as tonics, ointments, and salves.
