Hypericum × erosum

Hypericum × erosum, also known as Tutsan, is a compact, evergreen shrub that belongs to the St. John's Wort family.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, offering potential health benefits in wound healing, skin conditions, and mental health. The bioactive constituents of Hypericum × erosum include hypericin, hyperforin, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Tutsan include infusions, tinctures, and topical creams, which are often used to treat skin conditions such as eczema and acne.
