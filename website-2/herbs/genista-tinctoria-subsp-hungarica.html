What is Genista tinctoria subsp. hungarica?

Genista tinctoria subsp.

hungarica is a plant species belonging to the family Fabaceae. It is an annual or biennial herb, growing up to 30-100 cm tall, with a woody base and an unbranched stem.

hungarica is native to Central and Eastern Europe, and is known to be a source of natural dye.
