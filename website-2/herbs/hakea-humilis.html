Hakea humilis

Hakea humilis, commonly known as the low Hakea or low white needlewood, is a shrub native to the southwestern regions of Western Australia.

The bioactive constituents of Hakea humilis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
