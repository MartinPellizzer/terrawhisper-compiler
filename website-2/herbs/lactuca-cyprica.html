Lactuca cyprica

Lactuca cyprica, also known as Cypriot lettuce or Cyprus milk thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Lactuca cyprica include flavonoids, saponins, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
