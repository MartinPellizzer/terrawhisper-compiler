Festuca tenuicula

Festuca tenuicula, also known as Chinese grass or Chinese feather grass, is a type of grass that belongs to the Poaceae family.

The plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Festuca tenuicula include sesquiterpenoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Festuca tenuicula include teas, infusions, and decoctions, which are often used to promote relaxation, reduce inflammation, and improve respiratory health.
