Rhynchospora megapotamica

Rhynchospora megapotamica, also known as Puyán or Puyán grass, is a type of sedge plant that belongs to the family Cyperaceae.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can help to alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma. The bioactive constituents of Rhynchospora megapotamica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Rhynchospora megapotamica is often made into teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being used in cough syrups and expectorants to help relieve respiratory congestion.
