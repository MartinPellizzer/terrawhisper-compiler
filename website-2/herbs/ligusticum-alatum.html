Ligusticum alatum

Ligusticum alatum is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Apiaceae, commonly known as Chinese angelica or Liangfu.

It has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Ligusticum alatum include volatile oils, alkaloids, and coumarins, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

In traditional herbal preparations, Ligusticum alatum is often combined with other medicinal plants to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are commonly used to treat respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs.
