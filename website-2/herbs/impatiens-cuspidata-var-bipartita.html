What is Impatiens cuspidata var. bipartita?

Impatiens cuspidata var. bipartita is a taxonomic variety of plant that belongs to the family Balsaminaceae.

It is characterized by its bipartite leaves, which are divided into two distinct parts, and its stems that are covered with hairs. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are borne on axillary peduncles. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a pointed apex and a cuneate base, and measure up to 10 cm in length.

Impatiens cuspidata var. bipartita is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.
