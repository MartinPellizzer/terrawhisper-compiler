Convolvulus auricomus

Convolvulus auricomus, also known as bindweed or golden bindweed, is a perennial climbing plant in the Convolvulaceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its numerous health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and wound-healing properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of various conditions such as arthritis, gastrointestinal issues, and skin irritations. The bioactive constituents of Convolvulus auricomus include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Convolvulus auricomus is often used to make herbal teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are commonly used to treat digestive issues, skin problems, and as a natural anti-inflammatory agent.
