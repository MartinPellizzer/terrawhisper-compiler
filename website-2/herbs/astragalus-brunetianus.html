Astragalus brunetianus

Astragalus brunetianus is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, commonly found in mountainous regions of China.

The plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to promote overall health and well-being, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory, immunomodulatory, and antioxidant effects, which may help to boost the immune system and reduce the severity of various diseases. The bioactive constituents of Astragalus brunetianus include saponins, flavonoids, phenolic acids, and polysaccharides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Astragalus brunetianus is often prepared as a decoction or a tincture, and is commonly used in combination with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects.
