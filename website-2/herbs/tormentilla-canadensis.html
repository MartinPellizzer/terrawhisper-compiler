Tormentilla canadensis

Tormentilla canadensis, also known as Yellow Root, is a perennial plant species native to North America, belonging to the Saxifragaceae family.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, making it beneficial for treating digestive issues, skin conditions, and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of Tormentilla canadensis include iridoid glycosides, phenolic acids, and flavonoids, such as 6-hydroxy-7,8-dihydroxy-3-(3-methyl-but-2-enyl)-2(3H)-benzothiophenone and tormentic acid.

Traditional herbal preparations made with Tormentilla canadensis include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to treat various health issues, including digestive discomfort and skin conditions.
