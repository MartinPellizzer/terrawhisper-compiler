Sequoiadendron giganteum

Sequoiadendron giganteum, commonly known as the giant sequoia, is a coniferous tree species native to the Sierra Nevada mountain range in California, USA.

The extracts of this plant have been reported to possess various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce inflammation, fight infections, and protect against cell damage. The bioactive constituents of Sequoiadendron giganteum include triterpenoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with giant sequoia include teas, infusions, and capsules, which are often used to support immune function, reduce stress, and promote overall well-being.
