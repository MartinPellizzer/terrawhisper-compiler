The bioactive constituents of Nepenthes mirabilis include iridoids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Nepenthes mirabilis is commonly prepared as a decoction or infusion, and its extracts are also used in modern pharmaceuticals to create topical creams, ointments, and capsules for the treatment of skin conditions and other health issues.
