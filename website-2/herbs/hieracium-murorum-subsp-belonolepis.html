What is Hieracium murorum subsp. belonolepis?

Hieracium murorum subsp.

belonolepis is a botanical subspecies within the Hieracium murorum species, belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is a perennial herb with a rosette of leaves, typically 5-15 cm in diameter, consisting of linear to lanceolate, glabrous to ciliate leaflets.

The inflorescence is a corymbiform array of capitula, each 2-4 cm in diameter, composed of 10-20 ligulate florets and a few tubular florets. The plant grows in moist to wet, sandy to stony soils, often in limestone outcrops, and is typically found in mountainous regions. Hieracium murorum subsp.

belonolepis is endemic to the Iberian Peninsula and southwestern France.
