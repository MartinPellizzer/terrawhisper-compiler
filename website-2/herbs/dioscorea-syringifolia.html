Dioscorea syringifolia

Dioscorea syringifolia is a plant species belonging to the Dioscoreaceae family, commonly known as a type of yam or wild yam.

The health benefits of Dioscorea syringifolia include its potential anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and estrogenic properties, making it a sought-after ingredient in herbal remedies for menopause symptoms and hormone balance. The bioactive constituents of this plant include diosgenin, a steroidal saponin with reported medicinal properties, as well as other saponins and alkaloids.

Dioscorea syringifolia is often used to make herbal preparations such as teas, capsules, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into creams and ointments for topical use.
