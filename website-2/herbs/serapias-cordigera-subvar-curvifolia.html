What is Serapias cordigera subvar. curvifolia?

Serapias cordigera subvar. curvifolia is a subvariety of the orchid species Serapias cordigera, belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a caespitose habit, characterized by a short stem and 2-4 linear to lanceolate leaves, 6-15 cm in length. The inflorescence is a raceme of 1-3 flowers, each with a greenish-brown lip and a white to yellowish labellum. The flowers are 1.5-2.5 cm in length and have a yellowish-green sepals and petals.

The subvariety is found in the Mediterranean region, primarily in Spain and France.
