What is Cyclamen purpurascens subsp. immaculatum?

Cyclamen purpurascens subsp.

It is a perennial plant characterized by heart-shaped, usually dark green to variegated leaves with silver or white markings, and by funnel-shaped, nodding flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple, typically arranged in a terminal raceme.

The flowers have five petals, are 2-4 cm long, and are often fragrant. This taxon has a creeping habit, with long, thin stems and roots, and grows in woodland environments.

It is a diploid species with a chromosome number of 2n = 30.
