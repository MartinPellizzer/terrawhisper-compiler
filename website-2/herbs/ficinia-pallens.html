Ficinia pallens

Ficinia pallens, also known as the Cape rush or rush, is a type of rhizomatous perennial plant belonging to the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Ficinia pallens include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
