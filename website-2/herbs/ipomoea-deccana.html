Ipomoea deccana

Ipomoea deccana, also known as Indian bindweed or Deccan morning glory, is a perennial climber native to the Western Ghats region of India.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of anxiety, insomnia, and pain. The bioactive constituents of Ipomoea deccana include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
