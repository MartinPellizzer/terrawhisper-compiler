The bioactive constituents of Prestonia riedelii include alkaloids, such as voacangine, voacristine, and voacamine, which have been shown to exhibit psychoactive and medicinal effects.

Prestonia riedelii is often combined with other plants to create Ayahuasca, a traditional herbal preparation that is traditionally brewed and consumed in shamanic rituals, as well as being used in modern medicine to treat mental health conditions and addiction.
