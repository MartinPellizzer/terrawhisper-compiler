Vriesea imbricata

Vriesea imbricata, also known as the "Shingling Air Plant," is a type of epiphytic bromeliad native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The bioactive constituents of Vriesea imbricata include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
