Anthemis aucheri

Anthemis aucheri, also known as Arabian chamomile or Roman chamomile, is a perennial herb belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The plant has been used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as soothing digestive issues, calming anxiety, and promoting relaxation due to its anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic effects. The bioactive constituents of Anthemis aucheri include flavonoids, essential oils (including alpha-pinene, beta-pinene, and sesquiterpenes), and apigenin, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Anthemis aucheri is commonly used in herbal preparations, including teas, tinctures, and infusions, as well as in topical applications such as creams and ointments for skin irritations and wounds.
