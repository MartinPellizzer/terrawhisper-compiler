Carduus kumaunensis

Carduus kumaunensis, also known as Kumaun thistle, is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat various health conditions, including inflammation, fever, and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory, antipyretic, and carminative properties. The bioactive constituents of Carduus kumaunensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Carduus kumaunensis is commonly prepared as a decoction, infusion, or powder, and is used to treat conditions such as rheumatism, arthritis, and indigestion.
