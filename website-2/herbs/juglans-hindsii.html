Juglans hindsii

Juglans hindsii, also known as Hinds' walnut or Chinese walnut, is a deciduous tree species native to China and Southeast Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Juglans hindsii include juglone, a phenolic compound with known anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial activities, as well as flavonoids, saponins, and tannins.

Juglans hindsii is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine and can be prepared into various herbal products, such as decoctions, infusions, and extracts, which are often used to treat fever, skin conditions, and digestive issues.
