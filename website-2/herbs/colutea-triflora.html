Colutea triflora

Colutea triflora is a plant species commonly known as the 'bladder clover' or 'sensitive plant', belonging to the Fabaceae family.

The plant has been traditionally used in various cultures for its medicinal properties, offering potential health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities, which may aid in treating conditions like wounds, digestive issues, and respiratory problems.
