Stelis ligulata

Stelis ligulata, also known as Ligulate Stelis, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central America.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including digestive problems and fever, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Stelis ligulata include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

In herbal medicine, Stelis ligulata is commonly prepared as a tea or tincture to treat digestive issues, and is also used in traditional remedies for colds and flu.
