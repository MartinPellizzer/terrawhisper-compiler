Mimosa mollissima

Mimosa mollissima, also known as the velvet bean, is a tropical legume native to Asia, characterized by its climbing habit and velvety leaves.

The plant is valued for its health benefits, which include the treatment of anxiety, insomnia, and menopausal symptoms, attributed to its ability to modulate serotonin levels. The bioactive constituents of Mimosa mollissima include L-DOPA, a precursor to dopamine, as well as other alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins.

In herbal medicine, Mimosa mollissima is commonly prepared as a tea, capsule, or extract, often used to support mood regulation and alleviate symptoms of depression and anxiety.
