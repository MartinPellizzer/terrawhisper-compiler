Hieracium robertsii

Hieracium robertsii, also known as Robert's Hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the dandelion-like hawkweed family (Asteraceae).

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including inflammation, digestive problems, and respiratory issues, due to its potential anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal properties. The bioactive constituents of Hieracium robertsii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which have been shown to possess antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial activities.

In traditional herbal medicine, Hieracium robertsii is often made into teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat colds, fever, and skin conditions, although its efficacy and safety as a medicinal herb have not been extensively scientifically studied.
