Althaea hispida

The bioactive constituents of Althaea hispida include mucilages, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Hispid marshmallow is often used to make herbal teas, syrups, and ointments, which are commonly used to soothe sore throats, calm irritated skin, and provide relief from coughs and colds.
