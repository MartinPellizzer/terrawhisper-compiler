Andropogon sibiricus

Andropogon sibiricus, also known as Siberian bluestem, is a perennial grass species native to the steppes of Siberia and Mongolia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects that can help to alleviate symptoms of fever, cough, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Andropogon sibiricus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

Traditional herbal preparations made from this plant include decoctions, infusions, and tinctures, often used to treat respiratory infections and to reduce fever and inflammation.
