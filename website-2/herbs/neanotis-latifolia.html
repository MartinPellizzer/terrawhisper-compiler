Neanotis latifolia

Neanotis latifolia, also known as Asystasia latifolia, is a plant species belonging to the Acanthaceae family, commonly found in tropical regions.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, due to its potential health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. Neanotis latifolia contains bioactive constituents like flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, the plant is often prepared as a decoction, infusion, or tincture, and is commonly used to treat fever, cough, and respiratory issues, as well as to promote wound healing.
