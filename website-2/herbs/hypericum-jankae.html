Hypericum jankae

Hypericum jankae, also known as St. John's Wort, is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Hypericaceae.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antidepressant effects, making it a popular natural remedy for anxiety, insomnia, and other mood disorders. The bioactive constituents of Hypericum jankae include hyperforin, hypericin, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Hypericum jankae is commonly prepared as a tea, tincture, or oil infusion, and is often used to treat a range of health conditions, including wounds, skin irritations, and menstrual cramps.
