Uniola capensis

Uniola capensis, commonly known as the Cape spear grass, is a perennial grass native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

The bioactive constituents of Uniola capensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
