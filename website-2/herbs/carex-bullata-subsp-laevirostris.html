What is Carex bullata subsp. laevirostris?

Carex bullata subsp.

laevirostris is a plant species within the family Cyperaceae. It is a perennial sedge that grows up to 40 cm tall, with a rhizome and linear-lanceolate leaves that are 5-10 mm wide and 10-30 cm long.

The leaves have a keeled upper surface and a flat lower surface, and are often glaucous and waxy. The inflorescence is a solitary spike that is 3-10 cm long, with 3-10 spikelets that are 1-2 cm long.

The spikelets are 6-10 mm long, with 2-3 fertile florets and 2-3 sterile florets.
