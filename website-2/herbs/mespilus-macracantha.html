Mespilus macracantha

Mespilus macracantha, also known as Chinese hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree belonging to the Rosaceae family.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering various health benefits such as lowering cholesterol levels, improving digestion, and exhibiting anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. The bioactive constituents of Chinese hawthorn include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Chinese hawthorn is commonly used to make teas, tinctures, and decoctions, as well as being incorporated into various herbal formulas to support cardiovascular and digestive health.
