Ranunculus montellii

Ranunculus montellii is a plant species commonly known as Montell's crowfoot, which belongs to the family Ranunculaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Ranunculus montellii include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Ranunculus montellii include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome.
