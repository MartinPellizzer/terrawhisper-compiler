Sorbus lanata

Sorbus lanata, also known as the Woolly Whitebeam, is a small, deciduous shrub or tree native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

The bioactive constituents of Sorbus lanata include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
