It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are 5-10 mm long, with a leathery texture and a glabrous surface. The leaves are alternate, simple, and have a crenate margin. The inflorescences are axillary, bearing small, yellowish flowers with 5 petals, which are followed by small, drupe-like fruits.
