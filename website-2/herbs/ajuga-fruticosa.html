Ajuga fruticosa

Ajuga fruticosa, also known as bugleweed, is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

Traditionally used in herbal medicine, bugleweed is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to calm the nervous system, reduce anxiety and stress, and exhibit anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Ajuga fruticosa include glycosides, phenolic acids, flavonoids, and iridoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Bugleweed is commonly prepared as a tea, tincture, or infusion, and is used in traditional herbal remedies to support the treatment of anxiety, insomnia, and menopausal symptoms.
