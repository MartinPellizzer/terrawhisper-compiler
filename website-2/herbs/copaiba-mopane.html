Copaiba mopane

The Copaiba mopane, also known as Colophospermum mopane, is a tree species native to southern Africa, characterized by its broad, flat leaves and fragrant resin.

The health benefits of Copaiba mopane include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been used to treat various conditions, including wounds, skin infections, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Copaiba mopane include diterpenoid resin acids, specifically copalic acid, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Copaiba mopane include essential oils, extracts, and resin tinctures, which are often used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy to promote relaxation and reduce inflammation.
