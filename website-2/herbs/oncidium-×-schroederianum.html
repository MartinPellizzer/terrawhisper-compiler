Oncidium × schroederianum

Oncidium × schroederianum, commonly known as the Oncidium orchid, is a hybrid orchid species belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

The plant is rich in bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the Oncidium orchid is often prepared as a tea, tincture, or inflorescence, and is used to treat various health conditions, although its use should be approached with caution and under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
