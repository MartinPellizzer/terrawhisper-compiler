Huperzia mildbraedii

Huperzia mildbraedii, also known as Chinese club moss, is a type of lycopodiophyte, a small to medium-sized evergreen plant native to the tropical forests of Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Huperzia mildbraedii include huperzine A, a potent alkaloid that inhibits acetylcholinesterase and has been shown to improve memory and cognitive function.

Huperzine A is often extracted and concentrated for use in herbal supplements, where it is typically sold as an extract or capsule, and is also sometimes incorporated into traditional Chinese herbal remedies such as formulas and teas.
