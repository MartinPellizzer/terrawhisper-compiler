What is Equisetum elongatum var. dregeanum?

Equisetum elongatum var. dregeanum is a variety of the vascular plant Equisetum elongatum, belonging to the family Equisetaceae.

It is a perennial, herbaceous plant with rhizomes and stems that grow up to 2 meters tall, having a quadrangular, ridged, and hollow stem with 2-4 whorls of branches and spines. The plant's leaves are needle-like, linear, and alternate, ranging from 2-8 centimeters in length. The plant produces cone-like structures called strobili, which contain spores that are released for reproduction.

Equisetum elongatum var. dregeanum is native to southern Africa, where it grows in moist, shaded areas.
