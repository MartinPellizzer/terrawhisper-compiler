Ranunculus euryanthes

Ranunculus euryanthes, commonly known as the buttercup, is a perennial plant that belongs to the Ranunculaceae family.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can help in treating wounds, skin conditions, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Ranunculus euryanthes include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
