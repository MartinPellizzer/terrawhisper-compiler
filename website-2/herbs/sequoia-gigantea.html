classification

Sequoia gigantea, also known as the giant sequoia, is classified as a species within the coniferous tree family Cupressaceae. However, some classifications place it in the family Taxaceae. It belongs to the genus Sequoia and is further classified as a member of the order Coniferales, which comprises coniferous trees. At the kingdom level, it falls under Plantae, and at the phylum level, it is classified as Pinophyta. Sequoia gigantea is also known by its binomial nomenclature, Sequoiadendron giganteum, which is widely accepted in botanical classifications.
