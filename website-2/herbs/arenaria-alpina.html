Arenaria alpina

Arenaria alpina, also known as Alpine Sandwort, is a low-growing, perennial plant species belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Arenaria alpina include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
