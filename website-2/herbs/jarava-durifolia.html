Jarava durifolia

Jarava durifolia, also known as Gentianella durifolia, is a species of flowering plant in the gentian family (Gentianaceae) native to the Andean regions of South America.

The bioactive constituents of Jarava durifolia include gentiopicrin, gentiogenin, and gentianine, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Jarava durifolia is often used to prepare teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are commonly used to treat digestive problems and other health conditions.
