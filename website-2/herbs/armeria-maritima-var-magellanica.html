What is Armeria maritima var. magellanica?

Armeria maritima var, magellanica is a perennial plant with dense, globose, pink to purple flowers, 1-2 cm in diameter, borne in clusters on a tall, slender stem; its leaves are linear, 1-4 cm long, and are arranged in a basal rosette; it belongs to the family Plumbaginaceae, and is one of the several varieties of Armeria maritima; this variety is native to subalpine and alpine regions of South America; it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade.
