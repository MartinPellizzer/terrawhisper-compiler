Frangula revoluta

The bioactive constituents of Frangula revoluta also include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Frangula revoluta is commonly used in herbal preparations, including extracts, teas, and capsules, as well as in traditional medicine to treat various gastrointestinal issues and other health conditions.
