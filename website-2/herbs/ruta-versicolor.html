Ruta versicolor

Ruta versicolor, also known as garden rue or joint pine, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant has been utilized for centuries in traditional medicine due to its numerous health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been shown to aid in the treatment of various health conditions such as arthritis, digestive issues, and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of Ruta versicolor include alkaloids, flavonoids, and volatile oils, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Ruta versicolor is commonly prepared as an infusion, decoction, or tincture, with the latter being one of the most popular forms used to treat joint pain and inflammation.
