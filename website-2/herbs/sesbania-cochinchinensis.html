Sesbania cochinchinensis

Sesbania cochinchinensis, also known as Agati or Horsefly Tree, is a leguminous plant species native to Southeast Asia, valued for its medicinal and nutritional properties.

The health benefits of Sesbania cochinchinensis include its ability to treat various conditions such as fever, rheumatism, and skin diseases, as well as its potential to act as an anti-inflammatory and antioxidant. The plant's bioactive constituents include sesbanian, agatin, and sesbanianins, which have been found to possess anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant activities.

Sesbania cochinchinensis is commonly used to prepare herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being formulated into capsules and tinctures, making it a popular ingredient in traditional medicine and wellness products.
