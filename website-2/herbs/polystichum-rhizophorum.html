Polystichum rhizophorum

Polystichum rhizophorum, also known as the rhizome-bearing lady fern, is a species of fern that is native to East Asia and is widely distributed across various regions.

The health benefits of Polystichum rhizophorum include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve cardiovascular health, and possess antioxidant properties, making it a potential treatment for various diseases. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Polystichum rhizophorum is used in herbal medicine to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of health issues, including digestive problems and respiratory infections.
