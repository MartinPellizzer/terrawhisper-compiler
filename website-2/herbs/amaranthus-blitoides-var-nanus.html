What is Amaranthus blitoides var. nanus?

Amaranthus blitoides var. nanus is a small annual or biennial plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It has leaves that are linear to lanceolate, 1-10 cm long, and 0.2-1.5 cm wide, with a glabrous or pubescent surface and a cuneate to attenuate base. The plant produces greenish or reddish flowers in axillary spikes, 1-5 cm long, with sepals 0.8-1.5 mm long and petals absent. The flowers are unisexual, with male flowers having 5 stamens and female flowers having 1 style.

The plant typically grows to a height of 1-20 cm and has a taproot.
