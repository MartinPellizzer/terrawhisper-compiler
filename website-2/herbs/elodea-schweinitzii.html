Elodea schweinitzii

Elodea schweinitzii, also known as Waterweed or Anacharis, is a submerged aquatic plant belonging to the family Hydrocharitaceae.

It is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can help to reduce swelling and promote wound healing. The bioactive constituents of Elodea schweinitzii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
