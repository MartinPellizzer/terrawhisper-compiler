Liparis merrillii

Liparis merrillii, also known as the Merrill's Liparis, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in treating various ailments such as fever, cough, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Liparis merrillii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Liparis merrillii is commonly prepared into teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being used in traditional Filipino remedies such as tonics and poultices.
