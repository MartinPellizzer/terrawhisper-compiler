classification

Ilex alternifolia is classified as a species of holly native to eastern North America. It belongs to the family Aquifoliaceae, which is a family of flowering plants commonly known as the holly family. This species is further classified as a member of the genus Ilex, which comprises approximately 400 to 600 species of holly plants. Ilex alternifolia is often referred to as the alternate-leaf holly due to its distinctive leaf arrangement. The classification of Ilex alternifolia is widely accepted within the botanical community.
