Cynoglossum rechingeri

Cynoglossum rechingeri, commonly known as Rechinger's hound's tongue, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the borage family (Boraginaceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine, particularly in Tibetan and Himalayan cultures, for its potential health benefits, including the treatment of respiratory issues, fever, and inflammation. The bioactive constituents of Cynoglossum rechingeri include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.
