Ranevea

Ranvea, also known as Rauvolfia serpentina, is a medicinal plant that belongs to the Apocynaceae family.

The health benefits of Ranvea include its antihypertensive, sedative, and antispasmodic properties, which make it effective in treating conditions such as high blood pressure, anxiety, and insomnia. The bioactive constituents of Ranvea include alkaloids like serpentine, ajmaline, and reserpine, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Ranvea are decoctions, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat various health conditions and promote relaxation and well-being.
