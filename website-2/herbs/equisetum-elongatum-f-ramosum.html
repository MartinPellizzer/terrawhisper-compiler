What is Equisetum elongatum f. ramosum?

Equisetum elongatum f. ramosum is a botanical variant of the plant species Equisetum elongatum, belonging to the family Equisetaceae.

It is characterized by its elongated, green stems that reach up to 1.5 meters in height, with whorls of branchlets bearing scale leaves and terminal cones. The stems are hollow, cylindrical, and slightly ribbed, with internodes measuring 2-3 cm in length. The leaves are small, scale-like, and arranged in whorls of 8-10 around the stem, while the cones are terminal and spore-bearing.

Equisetum elongatum f. ramosum is typically found in moist, shaded areas of forests and woodlands.
