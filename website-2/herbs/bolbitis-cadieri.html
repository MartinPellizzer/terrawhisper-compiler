Bolbitis cadieri

Bolbitis cadieri, also known as Chinese tuckahoe, is a type of aquatic fern used in traditional medicine.

It has been used to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Bolbitis cadieri include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

Bolbitis cadieri is often used to make herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and decoctions, which are commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine and other Eastern herbal traditions.
