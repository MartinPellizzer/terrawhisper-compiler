Crataegus oxyacantha splendens

Crataegus oxyacantha splendens, commonly known as the midland hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree that belongs to the Rosaceae family.

The midland hawthorn has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to support cardiovascular health, with potential benefits including lowered blood pressure, improved heart function, and antioxidant effects. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, such as quercetin and kaempferol, as well as oligomeric proanthocyanidins (OPCs) and triterpenoid saponins.

The midland hawthorn is often used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and capsules, as well as in combination with other herbs in heart health supplements.
