classification

Bolbitis feeiana is a species of fern in the family Lomariopsidaceae. It is native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa. The classification of Bolbitis feeiana is within the genus Bolbitis, which is further classified in the family Lomariopsidaceae, order Polypodiales, class Polypodiopsida, and division Pteridophyta. The family Lomariopsidaceae is a part of the larger group of ferns known as the Polypodiidae. This fern is also sometimes placed in the family Davalliaceae or Nephrolepidaceae in some classifications.
