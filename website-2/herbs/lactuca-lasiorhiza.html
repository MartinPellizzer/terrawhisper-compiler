Lactuca lasiorhiza

Lactuca lasiorhiza, also known as wild lettuce, is a perennial plant species in the sunflower family (Asteraceae) that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to promote relaxation, reduce anxiety, and improve sleep quality, making it a natural remedy for insomnia and stress relief. The bioactive constituents of wild lettuce include lactucarium, lactucopicrin, and sesquiterpene lactones, which are responsible for its sedative and anti-inflammatory effects.

Wild lettuce is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, often in combination with other herbs, to promote relaxation and alleviate symptoms of insomnia and anxiety.
