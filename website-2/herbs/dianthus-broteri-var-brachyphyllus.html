What is Dianthus broteri var. brachyphyllus?

Dianthus broteri var. brachyphyllus is a variety of the plant Dianthus broteri, belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

This perennial herb has a short stem, usually less than 10 cm tall, with a simple and glabrous leaves arrangement. The leaves are linear, pointed, and 1-2 cm long, with a glabrous upper surface and ciliate lower surface. The inflorescence is a corymb, bearing 5-petaled flowers with a corolla diameter of about 2-4 cm and a pink to purple color.

The flowers have a prominent spur and are pollinated by bees and other insects.
