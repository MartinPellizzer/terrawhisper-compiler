Mariscus viridis

Mariscus viridis, also known as the green club moss, is a type of plant belonging to the lycopod family.

This plant is valued for its numerous health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which can help to soothe digestive issues, reduce fever, and protect against infections. The bioactive constituents of Mariscus viridis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Mariscus viridis is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures to support respiratory health, boost the immune system, and alleviate symptoms of various diseases.
