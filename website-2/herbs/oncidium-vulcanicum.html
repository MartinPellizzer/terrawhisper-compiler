Oncidium vulcanicum

Oncidium vulcanicum, also known as the Vulcan's orchid or Vulcan's Oncidium, is a species of orchid that belongs to the Oncidiinae subtribe.

The bioactive constituents of Oncidium vulcanicum include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The plant is typically used to make herbal preparations such as infusions, tinctures, and capsules, which can be found in traditional medicine and health food stores, particularly in Latin America where it is native.
