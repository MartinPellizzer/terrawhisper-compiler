Carum flabellifolium

Carum flabellifolium, also known as Ajwain or Trachyspermum ammi, is a plant species belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae) that is commonly used in traditional medicine and culinary practices.

The health benefits of Carum flabellifolium include its antispasmodic, carminative, and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to alleviate digestive issues, relieve pain, and reduce inflammation in the body. The bioactive constituents of this plant include volatile oils, particularly thymol, carvone, and limonene, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

In traditional herbal medicine, Carum flabellifolium is often used to prepare teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being incorporated into supplements and herbal extracts to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of digestive disorders.
