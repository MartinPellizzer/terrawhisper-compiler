Pinalia graminifolia

Pinalia graminifolia, also known as Bamboo Orchid, is a type of terrestrial orchid native to tropical forests, characterized by its grass-like leaves and white, star-shaped flowers.

Bioactive constituents of Pinalia graminifolia include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are thought to contribute to its medicinal properties.
