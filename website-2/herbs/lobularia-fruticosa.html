Lobularia fruticosa

Lobularia fruticosa, commonly known as Sweet Alyssum, is a low-growing, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its delicate white blooms and spreading habit.

The plant has been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including anxiety, insomnia, and respiratory problems, due to its calming and sedative properties. The bioactive constituents of Sweet Alyssum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal and therapeutic effects.
