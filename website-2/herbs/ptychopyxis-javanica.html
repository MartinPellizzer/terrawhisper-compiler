Ptychopyxis javanica

Ptychopyxis javanica, also known as Javanese leaf aris, is a plant species native to Southeast Asia, belonging to the family Phyllanthaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Ptychopyxis javanica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The plant is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and extracts, which are used to treat fever, cough, and sore throat.
