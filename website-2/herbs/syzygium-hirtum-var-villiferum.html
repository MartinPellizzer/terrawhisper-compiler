What is Syzygium hirtum var. villiferum?

Syzygium hirtum var. villiferum is an endemic plant species of New Caledonia, belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree that grows up to 10 meters in height, characterized by a smooth, grey bark and a dense, greyish-green foliage. The leaves are elliptical to lanceolate in shape, measuring 2-6 cm in length, with a petiole of up to 1 mm in length, and are covered with a dense, soft pubescence. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in clusters, and the fruit is a drupe, 4-6 mm in diameter, with a single seed.

Syzygium hirtum var. villiferum is often found in humid, shaded environments, and is typically associated with the ultramafic soils of New Caledonia's forests.
