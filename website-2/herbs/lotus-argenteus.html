Lotus argenteus

The Lotus argenteus, also known as the Silver Lotus or Pale Lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to China and Southeast Asia, characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers and silver-green leaves.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which are believed to aid in reducing stress and promoting overall well-being. The bioactive constituents of Lotus argenteus include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
