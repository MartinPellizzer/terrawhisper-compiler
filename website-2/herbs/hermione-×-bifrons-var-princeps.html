What is Hermione × bifrons var. princeps?

Hermione × bifrons var. princeps is a horticultural variety of the Amaryllidaceae family, resulting from the cross between Hippeastrum hondurense and Hippeastrum x johnsonii.

It is a bulbous perennial with strap-shaped leaves, typically 10-20 cm long, and funnel-shaped flowers that are red with a yellow throat, measuring up to 15 cm in diameter. The inflorescence is a single stem with 2-4 flowers, and the plant grows up to 30-40 cm in height. Hermione × bifrons var. princeps has a growth habit similar to Hippeastrum hondurense, but with more vibrant flower colors and increased flower size compared to the parent species.

The variety is known to thrive in well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade conditions.
