classification

Pouteria rigida is classified within the family Sapotaceae, commonly known as the sapodilla family. This family comprises around 200 species of trees and shrubs, primarily native to tropical regions of the Americas and Africa. The genus Pouteria itself contains around 30 species of trees, many of which are native to the tropical forests of Central and South America. Pouteria rigida is native to the tropical forests of Central America, specifically in Mexico and Central America. Within the Sapotaceae family, Pouteria rigida is part of the order Ebenales and the class Magnoliopsida.
