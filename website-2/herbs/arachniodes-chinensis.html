Arachniodes chinensis

Arachniodes chinensis, also known as Chinese maidenhair fern, is a type of fern species native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its health benefits, including reducing inflammation, improving respiratory function, and acting as an antioxidant. The bioactive constituents of Arachniodes chinensis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant include teas, infusions, and dried extracts, often used to treat coughs, bronchitis, and other respiratory conditions.
