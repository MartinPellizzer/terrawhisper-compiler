What is Equisetum multiforme var. ramosum?

Equisetum multiforme var. ramosum is a variant of the plant species Equisetum multiforme, belonging to the family Equisetaceae.

It is a vascular plant characterized by its green, branching stems, 1-3 mm in diameter, with whorled, 4-6 whorled, needle-like leaves, 1-2 mm long, and 0.2-0.5 mm wide, arranged in a spiral pattern. The plant produces small, brown, cone-like sporangia, 1-2 cm long, on the nodes of the stems, which give rise to the gametophyte generation. Equisetum multiforme var. ramosum grows in moist environments, often in shaded areas, and is typically found in Europe, Asia, and North America.

This variant of Equisetum multiforme is often found in riparian zones, particularly in areas with rich soil and adequate water supply.
