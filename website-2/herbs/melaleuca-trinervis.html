Melaleuca trinervis

Melaleuca trinervis, also known as the Three-Nerved Paperbark, is a species of plant in the family Myrtaceae, native to Australia.

The bioactive constituents of Melaleuca trinervis include terpenoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
