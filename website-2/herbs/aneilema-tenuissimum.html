Aneilema tenuissimum

Aneilema tenuissimum, also known as thin Aneilema or slender Aneilema, is a plant species in the family Commelinaceae.

It has been used in traditional medicine, particularly in Asia, for its purported health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it useful in treating various ailments such as wounds, fever, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Aneilema tenuissimum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, the plant is often prepared as a decoction or infusion, or used in combination with other herbs to create teas, tinctures, and ointments for treating various health conditions.
