Azalea albrechtii

Azalea albrechtii is a species of flowering plant in the heath family (Ericaceae), native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

The plant has been traditionally used in medicine for its various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can aid in reducing stress, improving respiratory health, and boosting the immune system. Bioactive constituents of Azalea albrechtii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Azalea albrechtii is commonly used to make herbal preparations such as tea, infusions, and extracts, which are often used to treat respiratory issues, coughs, and skin conditions.
