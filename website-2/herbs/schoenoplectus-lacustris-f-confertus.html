What is Schoenoplectus lacustris f. confertus?

Schoenoplectus lacustris f. confertus is a variant of the aquatic plant Schoenoplectus lacustris, belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

This plant is characterised by its rhizomatous habit, forming dense stands in freshwater environments. The culms are erect, up to 3 metres tall, with a diameter of 1-2 mm and a smooth, greenish-brown colour. Leaves are distichous, linear, and flat, up to 3 mm wide, and 1-2 cm long.

The inflorescence is a spicate panicle, up to 10 cm long, with spikelets bearing 2-3 flowers and a yellowish-brown colour.
