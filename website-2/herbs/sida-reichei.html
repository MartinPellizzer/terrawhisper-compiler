Sida reichei

Sida reichei, also known as African Jointvetch, is a legume plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its small, yellow flowers and brown seed pods.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include the treatment of various health conditions such as fever, rheumatism, and skin infections, thanks to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antifungal properties. The bioactive constituents of Sida reichei include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

In traditional medicine, Sida reichei is often used to prepare infusions, decoctions, and ointments, which are used to treat various ailments, including wounds, ulcers, and skin conditions.
