Thymus przewalskii

Thymus przewalskii, also known as Przewalsk's thyme, is a perennial plant species belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant activities, making it useful in the treatment of respiratory infections, digestive issues, and as an antiseptic. The bioactive constituents of Thymus przewalskii include thymol, carvacrol, and ursolic acid, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

Thymus przewalskii is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine to prepare teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into topical creams and ointments for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory effects.
