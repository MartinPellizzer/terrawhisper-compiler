Althaea cretica

Althaea cretica, also known as Cretan marshmallow, is a perennial plant belonging to the Malvaceae family, with thick, fleshy roots and leaves that are traditionally used for medicinal purposes.

The health benefits of Althaea cretica include its anti-inflammatory, soothing, and protective properties, making it a popular remedy for gastrointestinal issues, skin irritations, and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of this plant include mucilages, flavonoids, phenolic acids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Althaea cretica is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and poultices, as well as in the form of dried root powder or capsules, to support digestive health and provide relief from various inflammatory conditions.
