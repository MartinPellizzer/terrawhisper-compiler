Coelogyne linearifolia

Coelogyne linearifolia, also known as the linear-leaved Coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to the tropical forests of Southeast Asia.

This plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine, and its extracts have been found to possess antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, which contribute to its health benefits. The bioactive constituents of Coelogyne linearifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Coelogyne linearifolia is commonly prepared as a decoction or infusions to treat various ailments, and its extracts are also used in modern herbal preparations, such as capsules and tinctures, to promote overall health and well-being.
