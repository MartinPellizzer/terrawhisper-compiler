Orbea orientalis

Orbea orientalis, also known as the starfish cactus, is a species of succulent plant in the Apocynaceae family, native to the deserts of North Africa and the Middle East.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may help to treat wounds, reduce fever, and alleviate digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Orbea orientalis include alkaloids, such as orbeamine and orbeanine, as well as other secondary metabolites with potential pharmacological activity.

The most common herbal preparations made with Orbea orientalis include infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat various health conditions, including fever, digestive issues, and skin problems.
