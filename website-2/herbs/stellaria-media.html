Stellaria media

Stellaria media, commonly known as chickweed, is a small, annual plant with oval-shaped leaves and white, star-shaped flowers that grow in clusters.

The health benefits of chickweed include its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help soothe skin irritations, reduce pain and inflammation, and support the immune system. The bioactive constituents of chickweed include flavonoids, such as quercetin and kaempferol, as well as phenolic acids and terpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Chickweed is commonly used in herbal preparations, including teas, tinctures, and salves, which are often used to treat skin conditions, such as eczema and acne, as well as to reduce inflammation and promote wound healing.
