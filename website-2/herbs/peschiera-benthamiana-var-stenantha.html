What is Peschiera benthamiana var. stenantha?

Peschiera benthamiana var. stenantha is a plant variety belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its erect shrub habit, reaching up to 2 meters in height, with simple, alternate, and petiolate leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape and measure up to 10 cm in length. The leaves are dark green, glabrous, and have a leathery texture. The inflorescence is a cymose panicle, bearing funnel-shaped flowers that are pale yellow to cream-colored, with a five-lobed corolla and a tubular calyx.

The flowers are followed by oblong, green, and glabrous fruit capsules that contain numerous seeds.
