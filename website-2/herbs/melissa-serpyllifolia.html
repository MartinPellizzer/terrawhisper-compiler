Melissa serpyllifolia

Melissa serpyllifolia, also known as Lemon Balm or Heart of the Earth, is a perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

The plant is valued for its calming and anti-inflammatory properties, which make it beneficial for relieving stress, anxiety, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Melissa serpyllifolia include rosmarinic acid, luteolin, and beta-caryophyllene, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to treat insomnia, restlessness, and irritable bowel syndrome.
