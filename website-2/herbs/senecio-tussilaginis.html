Senecio tussilaginis

Senecio tussilaginis, also known as butterbur, is a perennial plant native to Europe and Asia, characterized by its large, dark green leaves and small yellow flowers.

The bioactive constituents of butterbur include sesquiterpenes and pyrrolizidine alkaloids (PVAs), although the latter are often removed in commercial preparations due to their potential toxicity.
