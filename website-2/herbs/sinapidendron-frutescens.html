Sinapidendron frutescens

Sinapidendron frutescens, also known as prickly poppy, is a spiny annual plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal activities, contributing to its potential in treating various health conditions such as wounds, skin infections, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Sinapidendron frutescens include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
