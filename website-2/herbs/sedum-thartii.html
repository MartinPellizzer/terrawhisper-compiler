Sedum thartii

Sedum tataricum, also known as Sedum thartii, is a succulent plant species that belongs to the Crassulaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Sedum tataricum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, such as ursolic acid and oleanolic acid, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
