What is Campanula sibirica var. pannonica?

Campanula sibirica var. pannonica is a plant taxon within the Campanulaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows up to 30-40 cm in height, with a single stem and basal leaves. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, 10-20 cm long, and have a crenate margin. The flowers are blue, tubular, and 2-3 cm long, arranged in a raceme at the top of the stem.

Campanula sibirica var. pannonica is native to the Pannonian region of Europe.
