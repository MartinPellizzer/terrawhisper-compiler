Odontites litoralis

Odontites litoralis, also known as coastal toothwort or sea toothwort, is a perennial plant species belonging to the balsam family (Orobanchaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Odontites litoralis include iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
