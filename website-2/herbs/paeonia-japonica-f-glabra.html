What is Paeonia japonica f. glabra?

Paeonia japonica f. glabra is a botanical variant of the species Paeonia japonica, belonging to the family Paeoniaceae.

It is characterized by its glabrous stems and leaves, lacking the typical pubescence of other Paeonia species. The plant features ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated margins, measuring 5-10 cm in length. Paeonia japonica f. glabra produces showy, pink to purple flowers with five sepals and numerous stamens, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.

The plant grows to a height of 50-80 cm, with a spread of 30-50 cm, and thrives in moist, well-drained soil in partial shade.
