Origanum aestivum

Origanum aestivum, also known as summer marjoram, is a perennial herb belonging to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its sweet, piney aroma and delicate flavor.

The health benefits of Origanum aestivum include its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, which have been shown to aid in digestion, relieve stress, and boost the immune system. The bioactive constituents of this plant include carvacrol, thymol, and rosmarinic acid, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Summer marjoram is commonly used to make herbal teas, infusions, and essential oils, as well as being a popular ingredient in Mediterranean cuisine, particularly in soups, sauces, and salads.
