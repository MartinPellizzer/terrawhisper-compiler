Primula curviflora

Primula curviflora, also known as the curved-leaved primrose, is a perennial plant belonging to the Primulaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Primula curviflora include iridoids, phenolic compounds, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
