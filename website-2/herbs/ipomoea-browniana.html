Ipomoea browniana

Ipomoea browniana, also known as Mexican morning glory, is a perennial vine native to the Americas, belonging to the Convolvulaceae family of flowering plants.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include the potential to help with anxiety, insomnia, and inflammation, due to its adaptogenic and anti-inflammatory effects on the body. The bioactive constituents of Ipomoea browniana include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
