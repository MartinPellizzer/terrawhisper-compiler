Hieracium carnosum

Hieracium carnosum, also known as musk hawkweed or meadow hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

Traditionally used in herbal medicine, Hieracium carnosum is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in the treatment of various conditions such as wounds, skin irritations, and gastrointestinal issues. The bioactive constituents of Hieracium carnosum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenes, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal preparations, Hieracium carnosum is often combined with other plants to create teas, tinctures, and infusions, such as topical creams and ointments for skin conditions, or internal supplements for digestive health.
