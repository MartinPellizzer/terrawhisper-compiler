Passiflora parvifolia

Passiflora parvifolia is a species of passionflower, a type of flowering vine native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Bioactive constituents of Passiflora parvifolia include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
