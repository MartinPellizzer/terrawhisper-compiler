Scirpus valdivianus

Scirpus valdivianus, also known as Valdivian bulrush, is a type of rhizomatous aquatic plant that belongs to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Scirpus valdivianus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
