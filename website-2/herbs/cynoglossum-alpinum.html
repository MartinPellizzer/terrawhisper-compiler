Cynoglossum alpinum

Cynoglossum alpinum, also known as Alpine Hound's Tongue, is a perennial plant species belonging to the borage family (Boraginaceae).

Cynoglossum alpinum contains bioactive constituents like flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions to treat various health conditions, and it is also used in the production of topical creams and salves for skin issues and wound care.
