What is Coix lacryma-jobi var. tubulosa?

Coix lacryma-jobi var. tubulosa is a perennial plant that belongs to the family Poaceae.

It has a height of up to 2 meters, with a leafy stem and narrow, linear leaves that are 10-30 centimeters long and 0.5-2 centimeters wide. The leaves have a glabrous or hairy surface and are arranged in a distichous pattern. The plant produces a spike-like inflorescence that is 10-30 centimeters long, bearing spikelets that are 1-2 centimeters long and have 2-4 florets.

Each spikelet contains 2-4 fertile florets and 1-2 sterile florets.
