Saponaria pumila

Saponaria pumila, also known as creeping soapwort, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial plant that belongs to the carnation family (Caryophyllaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Saponaria pumila include saponins, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
