Amaranthoides globosa

Amaranthoides globosa, also known as globe amaranth, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family, native to tropical Africa.

The health benefits of Amaranthoides globosa include its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in reducing inflammation, protecting against oxidative stress, and preventing infections. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Amaranthoides globosa is used to make herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are used to treat various health conditions, including fever, cough, and respiratory infections.
