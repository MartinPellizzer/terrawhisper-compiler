What is Rosa canina f. villosiuscula?

Rosa canina f. villosiuscula is a botanical variety of the dog rose species, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its deciduous habit, with thorny stems and compound leaves composed of 5-7 leaflets, 2-5 cm long. The flowers are pink, 4-6 cm in diameter, with 5-7 petals and a prominent cluster of stamens. The fruit is a red, oval-shaped hip, 1-2 cm long, containing 2-3 seeds.

This variety is distinguished from other forms of Rosa canina by its densely hairy, glandular leaves and stems.
