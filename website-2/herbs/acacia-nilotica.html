Acacia nilotica belongs to the family Fabaceae, which is commonly known as the legume family. This classification is due to the plant's characteristic fruit, which is a legume. The plant is also known as the black thorn or the Egyptian thorn. The classification of Acacia nilotica reflects its relationships with other flowering plants and its unique characteristics.
