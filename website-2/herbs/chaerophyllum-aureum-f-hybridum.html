What is Chaerophyllum aureum f. hybridum?

Chaerophyllum aureum f. hybridum is a botanical variant of the species Chaerophyllum aureum, belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is a perennial herb with a height range of 30-60 cm and a basal rosette of pinnate leaves, composed of 3-5 pairs of leaflets. The inflorescence is a compound umbel, bearing small, yellowish flowers with 5 petals and 5 stamens. The leaves and stems of Chaerophyllum aureum f. hybridum are covered in a fine, yellowish pubescence, while the roots are taprooted.

The fruits are schizocarps, consisting of 2-4 mericarps, each containing a single seed.
