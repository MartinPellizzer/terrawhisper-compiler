What is Vachellia leucophloea var. microcephala?

Vachellia leucophloea var. microcephala is a botanical variant of the tree species Vachellia leucophloea, belonging to the family Fabaceae.

This tree is characterized by its slender branches, greenish-grey bark, and a broad, flat crown. The leaves are bipinnate, with 2-3 pairs of pinnae, each 1-2 cm long, and 5-7 pairs of leaflets, each 0.5-2 cm long. The inflorescence is a globose spike, 2-5 cm long, bearing small, white flowers.

The fruit is a pod, 4-6 cm long, containing 4-6 seeds, 0.5-1 cm long.
