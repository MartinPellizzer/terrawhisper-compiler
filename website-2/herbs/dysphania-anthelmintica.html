Dysphania anthelmintica

Dysphania anthelmintica, also known as Blite or Chenopodium anthelminticum, is a plant species in the goosefoot family (Chenopodiaceae) that is native to various regions around the world.

This plant has been traditionally used for its health benefits, including its anthelmintic properties to expel intestinal parasites and its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects to alleviate symptoms of digestive issues and infections. The bioactive constituents of Dysphania anthelmintica include alkaloids, flavonoids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Dysphania anthelmintica is often used to make herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and capsules, which are used to treat a range of health conditions, including gastrointestinal issues and infections.
