Solanum parviflorum

The bioactive constituents of Solanum parviflorum include solanine, flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Solanum parviflorum is often used in herbal preparations, including infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of health conditions, although it is essential to use caution due to the potential toxicity of some of its constituents.
