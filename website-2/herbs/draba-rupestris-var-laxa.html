Draba rupestris var. laxa

Draba rupestris var. laxa, also known as the lax alpine draba, is a plant species that belongs to the mustard family (Brassicaceae) and is native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The plant is believed to have health benefits such as reducing inflammation and improving wound healing, making it a potential treatment for various skin conditions and injuries.

The herbal preparations of Draba rupestris var. laxa include infusions, tinctures, and salves, which can be used topically or taken internally under the guidance of a qualified healthcare professional.
