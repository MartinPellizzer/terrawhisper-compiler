What is Crepis albida subsp. asturica?

Crepis albida subsp.

asturica is a plant taxon that belongs to the family Asteraceae. It is a perennial herb with a rosette of leaves, 10-20 cm in diameter, with linear-lanceolate to lanceolate leaflets, 5-15 cm long, and 0.5-2 cm wide.

The inflorescence is a dense, flat-topped corymb, 10-30 cm in diameter, with numerous capitula, each containing 10-15 florets. The involucre is 5-7 mm high, with 8-12 phyllaries, 5-7 mm long.

The plant grows in dry to mesic places, 100-2000 m above sea level, in mountainous regions of northern Spain.
