Avenula pungens

Avenula pungens, also known as pungent oat-grass or pungent oat-grass, is a type of grass in the genus Avenula within the family Poaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various health issues, with its health benefits including the reduction of inflammation, improvement of respiratory problems, and regulation of gastrointestinal issues, due to its ability to soothe and calm the digestive system. The bioactive constituents of Avenula pungens include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

In herbal medicine, Avenula pungens is commonly prepared as an infusion or a tincture, and is often used to treat conditions such as bronchitis, coughs, and digestive issues.
