Phoradendron villosum

Phoradendron villosum, also known as the hairy mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that obtains its nutrients by tapping into the vascular system of its host tree.

The bioactive constituents of P. villosum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
