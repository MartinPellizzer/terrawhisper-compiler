The bioactive constituents of Albuca prasina include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

In traditional medicine, Albuca prasina is often prepared as a decoction, tincture, or powder, and is commonly used to treat coughs, colds, and skin infections.
