Spiraea × macrothyrsa

Spiraea × macrothyrsa, also known as Maybush, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the rose family (Rosaceae).

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues, fever, and rheumatism, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Spiraea × macrothyrsa include flavonoids, such as spiraeoside, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Spiraea × macrothyrsa include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat colds, flu, and other respiratory issues.
