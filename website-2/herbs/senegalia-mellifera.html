Senegalia mellifera

Senegalia mellifera, also known as Umzimbe tree, is a species of tree belonging to the Fabaceae family, native to southern Africa.

The bark and leaves of Senegalia mellifera have been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

Senegalia mellifera is often prepared as a decoction or tea, as well as being used in the form of infusions and tinctures, to take advantage of its health benefits.
