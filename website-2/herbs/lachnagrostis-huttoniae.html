Lachnagrostis huttoniae

Lachnagrostis huttoniae, also known as Hutton's wallaby grass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

The bioactive constituents of Lachnagrostis huttoniae include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
