Helichrysum rigidum

Helichrysum rigidum, also known as Bush Everlasting, is a shrub-like perennial plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) that is native to the southwestern regions of Western Australia.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in wound healing and reducing inflammation. The bioactive constituents of Helichrysum rigidum include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
