Linum collinum

Linum collinum, also known as Common Blue Flax or Russian Flax, is a perennial plant species in the flax genus (Linum) that is native to Eastern Europe and Western Asia.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help to reduce inflammation, improve cardiovascular health, and protect against oxidative stress. Bioactive constituents of Linum collinum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Common herbal preparations made from Linum collinum include tinctures, teas, and infusions, which are used to treat a range of health conditions, including anxiety, insomnia, and gastrointestinal issues.
