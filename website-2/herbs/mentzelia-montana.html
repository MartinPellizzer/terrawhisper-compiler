Mentzelia montana

Mentzelia montana, also known as mountain blazing star, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

Traditionally, this plant has been used in herbal medicine to treat various health conditions, including inflammation and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties. The bioactive constituents of Mentzelia montana include alkaloids, glycosides, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant are teas, tinctures, and infusions, often used to relieve symptoms of indigestion and irritable bowel syndrome.
