Sedum soboliferum

Sedum soboliferum, also known as Live Forever or Burro's Tail, is a succulent plant species native to Mexico, characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and vibrant green leaves.

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antimicrobial properties, making it beneficial for skin and digestive health. The bioactive constituents of Sedum soboliferum include triterpenoid saponins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Sedum soboliferum is often used in herbal preparations, such as tinctures, teas, and ointments, to support skin health and wound healing.
