What is Hieracium racemosum subsp. crinitum?

Hieracium racemosum subsp.

crinitum is a plant species in the family Asteraceae. It has leaves that are lance-shaped, 3-10 cm long, and 2-10 mm wide, with a ciliate margin.

The plant produces a raceme with 10-40 yellow flowers, 10-15 mm in diameter, that have 5-6 mm long corolla lobes. The achenes are 3-4 mm long and have a pappus of 10-15 mm long, white bristles.

This species is found in the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.
