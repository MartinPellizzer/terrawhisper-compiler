Lapsana takasei

The bioactive constituents of Lapsana takasei include flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

In traditional medicine, Lapsana takasei is often used to make herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, which are used to treat a range of health issues, including coughs, colds, and respiratory problems.
