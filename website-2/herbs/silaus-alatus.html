Silaus alatus, also known as winged spurge, is a plant species within the family Euphorbiaceae. It belongs to the genus Silaus. Silaus alatus is native to East Asia, specifically in China and Japan. This classification provides a clear understanding of the plant's evolutionary relationships and its position within the plant kingdom.
