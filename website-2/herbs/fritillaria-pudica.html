Fritillaria pudica

Fritillaria pudica is a species of flowering plant in the lily family, native to China, characterized by its unique, bell-shaped flowers and slender, linear leaves.

Bioactive constituents of Fritillaria pudica include alkaloids, such as galantamine, which have been shown to exhibit neuroprotective and cholinergic activity.
