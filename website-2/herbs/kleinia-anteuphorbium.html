Kleinia anteuphorbium

Kleinia anteuphorbium, also known as Chrysanthemoides monocephala or Kleinia, is a succulent plant species that belongs to the Asteraceae family.

The bioactive constituents of the Kleinia plant include flavonoids, triterpenoids, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
