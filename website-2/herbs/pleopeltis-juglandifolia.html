Pleopeltis juglandifolia

Pleopeltis juglandifolia, also known as Oak Fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The bioactive constituents of Pleopeltis juglandifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
