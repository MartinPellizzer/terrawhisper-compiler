What is Consolida pubescens var. dissitiflora?

Consolida pubescens var. dissitiflora is a perennial plant that belongs to the family Ranunculaceae.

It has a taprooted stem, 10-30 cm tall, with leaves divided into 3-5 lobes, 2-5 cm long, and a raceme of 10-30 flowers, 1.5-2.5 cm in diameter, with 5 petals and 5 sepals. The flowers are purple, with a yellow center, and are arranged in a terminal, axillary, or lateral position. The plant is found in mountainous areas of Europe and Western Asia, growing in rocky, stony, or scree habitats.

It blooms from June to August, and its seeds mature in September.
