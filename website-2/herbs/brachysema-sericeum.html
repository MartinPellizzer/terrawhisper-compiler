Brachysema sericeum

Brachysema sericeum is a flowering plant native to Western Australia, belonging to the family Fabaceae.

Bioactive constituents found in Brachysema sericeum include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Brachysema sericeum is often used to make herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are commonly used to treat colds, coughs, and skin irritations.
