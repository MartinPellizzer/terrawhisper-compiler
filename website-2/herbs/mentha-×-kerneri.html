classification

Mentha × kerneri is a hybrid species of mint, classified as a nothospecies. It is a cross between Mentha aquatica and Mentha requienii, resulting in a unique combination of characteristics. The hybrid name indicates that it is not a naturally occurring species but a cultivated one. This classification helps in understanding the genetic makeup and behavior of the plant.
