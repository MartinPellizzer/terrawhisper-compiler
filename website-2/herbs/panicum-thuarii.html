Panicum thuarii

Panicum thuarii, also known as Thai panic, is a type of grass used as a medicinal plant.

The plant has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including fever, inflammation, and gastrointestinal issues, due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Panicum thuarii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

In traditional medicine, Thai panic is commonly used to make herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being incorporated into remedies for fever, digestive problems, and skin conditions.
