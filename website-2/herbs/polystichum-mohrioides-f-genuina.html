What is Polystichum mohrioides f. genuina?

Polystichum mohrioides f. genuina is a fern species within the family Polypodiaceae.

It is a terrestrial plant with a rhizome and stoloniferous stems, growing up to 30-60 cm tall. The fronds are bipinnate, with a petiole up to 10-20 cm long, bearing pinnae that are 10-30 cm long and 2-4 cm wide. Each pinna has 4-10 pairs of pinnules that are lanceolate to ovate in shape, measuring up to 1-2 cm long and 0.5-1 cm wide.

The fertile fronds are similar in shape but smaller in size, with sporangia borne on the underside of the pinnules.
