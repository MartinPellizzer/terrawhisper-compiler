Polygala heterorhyncha

Polygala heterorhyncha is a plant species commonly known as Polygala root, also referred to as Yuan Zhi or Xiang Fu in traditional Chinese medicine.

This plant has been used for centuries to treat various health conditions, including anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues, due to its adaptogenic and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Polygala heterorhyncha include iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

Polygala root is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and is commonly formulated into herbal teas, capsules, and extracts, as well as being incorporated into soups and tonics.
