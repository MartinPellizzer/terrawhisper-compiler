Jacobaea × mirabilis

Jacobaea × mirabilis, also known as the Mirabilis Hawkweed, is a hybrid plant species within the dandelion family (Asteraceae) that exhibits remarkable medicinal properties.

The health benefits of Jacobaea × mirabilis include its potential to stimulate liver function and increase bile production, as well as its capacity to act as an anti-inflammatory and antioxidant agent, making it beneficial for digestive and liver issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include sesquiterpene lactones, particularly jacaranone and 8-deoxycrotilin, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Jacobaea × mirabilis is commonly used in herbal preparations such as tinctures and infusions, often in combination with other herbs to support liver and digestive health, although its use should be approached with caution due to potential toxicity.
