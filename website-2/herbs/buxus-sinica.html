Buxus sinica

Buxus sinica, also known as Chinese boxwood or Jin Bu Huan, is a traditional medicinal plant used in Chinese herbal medicine, belonging to the family Buxaceae.

The health benefits of Buxus sinica include its ability to improve cognitive function, relieve anxiety and insomnia, and alleviate symptoms of Parkinson's disease due to its neuroprotective properties. The bioactive constituents of this plant include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which have been found to have antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and neuroprotective effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Buxus sinica include tea, capsules, and tablets, often in combination with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects.
