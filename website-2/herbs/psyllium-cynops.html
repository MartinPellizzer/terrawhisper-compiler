Psyllium cynops

Psyllium cynops, also known as French tamarisk or Psyllium tamarisk, is a perennial herb belonging to the plantain family (Plantaginaceae).

The plant is prized for its health benefits, which include promoting digestive regularity, lowering cholesterol levels, and regulating blood sugar. The bioactive constituents of Psyllium cynops include mucilages, which are complex carbohydrates that form a protective gel-like substance in the gut, and flavonoids, which have antioxidant properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and capsules, as well as being added to foods and beverages to support gut health and overall well-being.

Psyllium cynops has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with applications in treating digestive issues and maintaining gut health.
