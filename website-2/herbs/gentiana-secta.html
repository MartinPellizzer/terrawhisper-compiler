Gentiana secta

The Gentiana secta, also known as the yellow gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the Gentianaceae family, characterized by its yellow flowers and medicinal properties.

The bioactive constituents of Gentiana secta include iridoid glycosides, such as gentiopicroside and swertiamarin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make herbal preparations, including tea, tinctures, and capsules, which are used to treat digestive issues and as a natural remedy to stimulate appetite and improve digestion.
