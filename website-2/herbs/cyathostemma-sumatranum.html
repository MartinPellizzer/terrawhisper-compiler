Cyathostemma sumatranum

Cyathostemma sumatranum, also known as Sumatran Cyathostemma or Sumatran Cyathostemma Tree, is a plant species in the family Annonaceae, native to the tropical forests of Sumatra in Indonesia.

The health benefits of Cyathostemma sumatranum include its potential anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may contribute to its use in traditional medicine for the treatment of various health conditions. Bioactive constituents of this plant include alkaloids, such as isobolidine and isobolidine-N-oxide, which have been reported to exhibit notable pharmacological activities.

Traditional herbal preparations made from Cyathostemma sumatranum include infusions and decoctions, often used to treat fever, cough, and respiratory issues, as well as to boost immune function and alleviate pain.
