What is Arabis alpina var. meruensis?

Arabis alpina var. meruensis is a plant variety within the species Arabis alpina, belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is an annual or biennial herbaceous plant, typically growing 10-30 cm tall, with a glabrous stem and ovate-lanceolate leaves that are 1-5 cm long and 0.5-2 cm wide. The leaves are alternate, simple, and glabrous, with a serrate margin and an obtuse base. Arabis alpina var. meruensis produces white or pinkish flowers in a raceme, with four petals that are 3-4 mm long and four sepals that are 2-3 mm long.

The plant typically flowers from April to June and is found in high-altitude areas of East Africa.
