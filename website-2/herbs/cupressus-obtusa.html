Cupressus obtusa

Cupressus obtusa, also known as Japanese cypress or hinoki, is a slow-growing evergreen tree native to Japan, valued for its aromatic wood and medicinal properties.

The health benefits of Cupressus obtusa include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been shown to alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues and improve overall well-being. The bioactive constituents of this plant include hinokitiol, a compound with potential antiviral and antibacterial properties, as well as other terpenoids and flavonoids.

The most common herbal preparations made with Cupressus obtusa include essential oils, teas, and infusions, often used to promote relaxation and alleviate respiratory problems.
