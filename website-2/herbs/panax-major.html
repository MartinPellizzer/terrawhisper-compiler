Panax major

Panax major, also known as Asian ginseng or San Qi, is a perennial plant native to East Asia, belonging to the genus Panax in the family Araliaceae.

It has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to promote vitality, improve cognitive function, and enhance physical performance, due to its potential to reduce fatigue, boost immune system, and exhibit anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Panax major include ginsenosides, specifically Rb1, Rg1, and Re, as well as other compounds such as polyphenols, flavonoids, and terpenoids.

Panax major is commonly prepared as a decoction, tincture, or capsule, often combined with other herbs, to produce a variety of herbal supplements and traditional medicines.
