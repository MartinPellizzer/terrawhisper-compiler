classification

Opuntia curassavica is a species of cactus in the family Cactaceae. It belongs to the tribe Opuntieae and the genus Opuntia. Within the genus Opuntia, Opuntia curassavica is classified as a type of prickly pear cactus. The species is native to the Americas and is widely distributed throughout Central and South America. Its classification is further supported by its distinctive physical characteristics, including its flat stems and spines.
