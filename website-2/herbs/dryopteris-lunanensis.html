Dryopteris lunanensis

Dryopteris lunanensis, also known as Lunan's shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the Dryopteris genus.

It has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, helping to alleviate conditions such as fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Lunan's shield fern is often prepared as a tea or decoction, and is also used in combination with other herbs to create tonics and infusions for overall health and well-being.
