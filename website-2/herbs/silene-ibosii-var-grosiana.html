What is Silene ibosii var. grosiana?

Silene ibosii var. grosiana is a botanical variety within the species Silene ibosii, belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows up to 30-50 cm in height, with glabrous stems and leaves that are lanceolate to elliptic in shape, measuring 1-4 cm in length. The leaves are alternate and have entire margins, while the inflorescence is a corymb with 5-10 flowers, each measuring 1-2 cm in diameter. The flowers have five petals, which are pink to purple in color, and are radially symmetrical with a five-lobed calyx.

The plant is endemic to the Philippines and is characterized by its glabrous habit and distinctive floral morphology.
