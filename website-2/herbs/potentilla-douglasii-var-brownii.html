What is Potentilla douglasii var. brownii?

Potentilla douglasii var. brownii is a perennial shrub that belongs to the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its decumbent stems, which are 10-40 cm long, and pinnate leaves with 3-9 leaflets that are 1-4 cm long. The leaves are dark green on the upper surface and pale green on the lower surface, with serrated margins. The plant produces small, 5-petaled flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, which are 6-10 mm in diameter and clustered in axillary corymbs.

The flowers are hermaphroditic and have a sweet fragrance, blooming from May to August.
