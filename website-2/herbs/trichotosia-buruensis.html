classification

Trichotosia buruensis is a species of orchid in the genus Trichotosia. It belongs to the family Orchidaceae. This classification places it within the order Asparagales and the subfamily Epidendroideae. Furthermore, the kingdom it is classified under is Plantae, and the phylum is Magnoliophyta. At the rank of superorder, it falls under the superorder Lilianae.
