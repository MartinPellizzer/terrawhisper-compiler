Festuca syrtica

Festuca syrtica, also known as Festuca grass, is a perennial grass species native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Western Asia.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory effects, which can help to alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma. The bioactive constituents of Festuca syrtica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenes, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Festuca syrtica is often prepared as a decoction or tea, as well as being used in combination with other herbs in various tinctures and infusions to support respiratory health and boost the immune system.
