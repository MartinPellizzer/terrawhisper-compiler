Tragacantha eriocarpa

Tragacantha eriocarpa, also known as Astragalus eriocarpus, is a perennial plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Tragacantha eriocarpa include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Tragacantha eriocarpa is often prepared as a decoction or tincture, and is commonly used to treat respiratory infections, reduce fever, and alleviate symptoms of cough and sore throat.
