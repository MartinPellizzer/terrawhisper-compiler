Scrophularia suffruticans

Scrophularia suffruticosa, also known as the sub-shrub figwort, is a perennial plant species within the figwort family (Scrophulariaceae) characterized by its small, tubular flowers and oval-shaped leaves.

Bioactive constituents of Scrophularia suffruticosa include iridoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
