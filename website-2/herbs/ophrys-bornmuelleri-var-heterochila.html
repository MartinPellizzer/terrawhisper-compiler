What is Ophrys bornmuelleri var. heterochila?

Ophrys bornmuelleri var. heterochila is a variety of the orchid Ophrys bornmuelleri, belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its solitary flowers, which are pale yellow or greenish-yellow in color and have a distinctive shape, resembling a female insect. The flowers are approximately 2-3 cm in length and have a 3-lobed labellum, with the lateral lobes being much smaller than the central lobe. The leaves are linear-lanceolate in shape and are arranged in a rosette at the base of the stem.

The plant typically grows in rocky, mountainous areas, and is found in specific regions of the Mediterranean.
