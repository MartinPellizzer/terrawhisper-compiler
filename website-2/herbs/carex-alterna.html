Carex alterna

Carex alterna, also known as alternate-leaved sedge or mountain sedge, is a species of sedge that belongs to the family Cyperaceae.

In traditional herbal medicine, Carex alterna is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a variety of health issues, including digestive problems, respiratory infections, and skin conditions.
