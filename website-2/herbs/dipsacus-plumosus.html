Dipsacus plumosus

Dipsacus plumosus, also known as Echites plumosus or Chinese Echium, is a perennial plant species in the teasel family (Dipsacaceae) that is native to Asia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antipyretic effects, making it a traditional remedy for treating fever, rheumatism, and other inflammatory conditions. The bioactive constituents of Dipsacus plumosus include flavonoids, triterpenoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
