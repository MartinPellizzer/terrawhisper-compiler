classification

Lathyrus hispanicus is classified in the kingdom Plantae, specifically within the family Fabaceae. This classification places it within the order Fabales and the genus Lathyrus. It is further classified into the species Lathyrus hispanicus and the variety Lathyrus hispanicus var. hispanicus. The species is native to the Iberian Peninsula. The classification is based on its botanical characteristics and genetic makeup.
