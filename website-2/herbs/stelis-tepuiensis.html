Stelis tepuiensis

Stelis tepuiensis is a species of orchid found in the tepuis of Venezuela, characterized by its unique, epiphytic growth habit and distinctive flowers.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in the treatment of various conditions such as wounds, infections, and respiratory issues. Research has identified bioactive constituents in Stelis tepuiensis, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are thought to be responsible for its medicinal properties.
