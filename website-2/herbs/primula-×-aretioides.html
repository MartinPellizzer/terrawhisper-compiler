Primula × aretioides

Primula × aretioides, also known as the drumstick primrose, is a hybrid perennial plant species belonging to the Primulaceae family, characterized by its unique, drumstick-shaped seed capsules.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, contributing to its potential health benefits in treating respiratory issues, wounds, and gastrointestinal problems. The bioactive constituents of Primula × aretioides include primulin, isoprimulin, and primveroside, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

Herbal preparations made from the plant include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat various ailments, including bronchitis, coughs, and digestive issues.
