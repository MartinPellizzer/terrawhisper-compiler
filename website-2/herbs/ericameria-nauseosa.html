Ericameria nauseosa

Ericameria nauseosa, also known as Rubber Rabbitbrush or Grey Rabbitbrush, is a shrubby perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

The bioactive constituents of Ericameria nauseosa include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
