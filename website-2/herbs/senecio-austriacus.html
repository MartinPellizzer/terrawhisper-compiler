Senecio austriacus

Senecio austriacus, also known as Austrian ragwort or Mountain ragwort, is a perennial plant in the sunflower family that is native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

The bioactive constituents of Senecio austriacus include sesquiterpene lactones, such as alantolactone and dihydroalantolactone, which are responsible for the plant's medicinal properties.
