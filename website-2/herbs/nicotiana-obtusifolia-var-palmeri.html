What is Nicotiana obtusifolia var. palmeri?

Nicotiana obtusifolia var. palmeri is a variety of the plant species Nicotiana obtusifolia, belonging to the family Solanaceae.

It is an annual or perennial herb, typically growing to a height of 0.5-1.5 meters, with a woody base and a branching habit. The leaves are lanceolate or ovate in shape, measuring 5-20 centimeters in length and 2-5 centimeters in width, with a pointed or obtuse apex and a glabrous or sparsely hairy surface. The inflorescence is a terminal spike or raceme, bearing tubular or funnel-shaped flowers with a five-lobed corolla, measuring 4-8 centimeters in length and 3-5 centimeters in width, with a white or cream-colored coloration.

The flowers are hermaphroditic, with five stamens and a superior ovary containing many seeds.
