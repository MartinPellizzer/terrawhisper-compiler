Parasenecio tenianus

Parasenecio tenianus, also known as Chinese silk aconite or silk aconitum, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Aconitum genus.

This plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving effects, which contribute to its potential health benefits in treating conditions such as arthritis and nerve pain. The bioactive constituents of Parasenecio tenianus include alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins, particularly aconitine, which is responsible for its analgesic and anti-inflammatory activities.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Parasenecio tenianus is often used to make herbal preparations such as decoctions, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat various health conditions.
