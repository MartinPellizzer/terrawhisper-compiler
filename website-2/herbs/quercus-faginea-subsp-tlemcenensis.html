What is Quercus faginea subsp. tlemcenensis?

Quercus faginea subsp.

tlemcenensis is a plant endemic to Algeria. It belongs to the family Fagaceae and is a subspecies of Quercus faginea.

Botanically, it is characterized by its deciduous habit, elliptical to lanceolate leaves with 7-11 lobes, and acorns that are 2-3 cm long. The leaves have a glabrous upper surface and a pubescent lower surface, while the acorns have a short, straight peduncle. Quercus faginea subsp.

tlemcenensis is a diploid species with a chromosome number of 2n = 2x = 24.
