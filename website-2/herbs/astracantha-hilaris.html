classification

Astracantha hilaris is classified within the family Fabaceae, commonly known as legumes or pea family. It belongs to the subfamily Faboideae and the genus Astracantha, which is a section of the genus Caragana. It is often found in dry regions and is a member of the vast and diverse legume family.
