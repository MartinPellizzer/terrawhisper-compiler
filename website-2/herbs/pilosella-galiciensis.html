Pilosella galiciensis

Pilosella galiciensis, also known as Galician hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the dandelion-like Asteraceae family.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate conditions such as arthritis and skin irritations. The bioactive constituents responsible for these benefits include sesquiterpene lactones, particularly galiliol and pilosilin, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids.

Traditionally, Pilosella galiciensis is used in herbal medicine and is often prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, although further research is needed to confirm its efficacy and safety for human consumption.
