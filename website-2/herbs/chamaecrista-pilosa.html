Chamaecrista pilosa

Chamaecrista pilosa, commonly known as hairy buttonweed or hairy partridge pea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

Bioactive constituents of Chamaecrista pilosa include flavonoids, saponins, and terpenoids, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.
