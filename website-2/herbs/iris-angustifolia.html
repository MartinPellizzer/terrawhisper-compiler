Iris angustifolia

Iris angustifolia is a perennial herbaceous plant, also known as the Narrowleaf Iris, belonging to the Iridaceae family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic activities, making it beneficial for treating wounds, reducing pain and swelling, and preventing infections. The bioactive constituents of Iris angustifolia include iridoids, glycosides, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

In traditional herbal medicine, Iris angustifolia is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures to treat various health issues, including skin conditions and digestive problems.
