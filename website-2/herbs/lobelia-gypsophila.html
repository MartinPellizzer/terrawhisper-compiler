Lobelia gypsophila

The bioactive constituents of Lobelia gypsophila include alkaloids, glycosides, and phenolic compounds, including lobeline, lobelanine, and isopelletierine, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Lobelia is commonly used to make herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and capsules, as well as being incorporated into smoking cessation products due to its potential to alleviate nicotine withdrawal symptoms.
