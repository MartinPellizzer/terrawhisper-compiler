Dioscorea toldosensis

Dioscorea toldosensis, also known as Toldos yam, is a species of yam native to the Andean region of South America.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, providing health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, improve blood sugar regulation, and boost the immune system. The bioactive constituents of Dioscorea toldosensis include diosgenin, a steroidal saponin with potential therapeutic applications, as well as other compounds like flavonoids and phenolic acids.

Dioscorea toldosensis is commonly used in traditional herbal preparations, such as teas and infusions, as well as in the form of dried root extracts or capsules, which are often used to support joint health and well-being.
