Ruscus terminalis

Ruscus terminalis, also known as Butcher's Broom or Ruscus, is a type of flowering plant in the Asparagaceae family, native to Europe and Western Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Ruscus terminalis include saponins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
