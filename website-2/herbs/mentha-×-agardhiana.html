Mentha × agardhiana

Mentha × agardhiana, commonly known as West Indian pennyroyal, is a hybrid of the mint genus that is native to Asia and Australia.

The bioactive constituents of Mentha × agardhiana include menthone, limonene, and beta-caryophyllene, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
