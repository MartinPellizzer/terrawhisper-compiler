Kentia sapida

The Kentia palm, also known as Kentia sapida, is a species of flowering plant in the palm family native to Lord Howe Island in Australia.

Kentia palm is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of anxiety, insomnia, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of the Kentia palm include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Kentia palm include teas, tinctures, and infusions, often used to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality.
