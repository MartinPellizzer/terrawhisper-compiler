Spartium baeticum

Spartium baeticum, also known as French broom or Spanish broom, is a flowering plant in the legume family that is native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce pain, improve wound healing, and protect against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Spartium baeticum include isoflavones, phenolic acids, and flavonoids, such as genistein and kaempferol, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Spartium baeticum is often made into infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, as well as being used in topical preparations to support skin health and wound healing.
