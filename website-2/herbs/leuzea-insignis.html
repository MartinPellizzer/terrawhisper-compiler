Leuzea insignis

Leuzea insignis, also known as Rhaponticum carthamoides or Khasyrkhan, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and Eastern Europe.

The plant is valued for its health benefits, which include stimulating the immune system, enhancing physical endurance, and improving mental clarity. The bioactive constituents of Leuzea insignis include sesquiterpene lactones, particularly leuzeanin and its derivatives, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used in herbal preparations such as tinctures, extracts, and infusions, which are often used to improve vitality, reduce fatigue, and enhance overall well-being.
