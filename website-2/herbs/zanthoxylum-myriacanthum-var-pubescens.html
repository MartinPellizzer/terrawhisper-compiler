What is Zanthoxylum myriacanthum var. pubescens?

Zanthoxylum myriacanthum var. pubescens is a variety of Zanthoxylum myriacanthum, a plant species within the family Rutaceae.

It is characterized by its shrub-like habit, 2-5 meters tall, with a broad, rounded crown, and a densely hairy stem and branches. The leaves are compound, 2-3-pinnate, with 10-20 pairs of leaflets, 3-12 mm long, elliptic to ovate in shape, and dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in axillary and terminal panicles, 5-10 mm in diameter, and is dioecious, with male and female flowers on separate plants.

The fruits are small, green to yellowish drupes, 6-10 mm long, with a single seed in each fruit.
