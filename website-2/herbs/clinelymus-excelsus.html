I can find information on Clinelymus excelsus. Clinelymus excelsus is classified under the family Poaceae and the genus Clinelymus. Clinelymus excelsus is also known as the Stipa excelsa and is further classified as a species of the Stipa genus. Further classifications place it under the family Poaceae and the subfamily Pooideae. Clinelymus excelsus is native to the mountainous regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
