Terpsichore cretata

Terpsichore cretata, also known as Copaiba or Copaiba Balsam, is a plant species native to the Amazon rainforest, characterized by its resinous sap, which is highly valued for its medicinal properties.

Copaiba is often used in herbal preparations such as tinctures, essential oils, and topical creams, which are commonly used to treat pain, skin conditions, and respiratory issues.
