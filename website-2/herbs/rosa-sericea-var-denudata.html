Rosa sericea var. denudata

Rosa sericea var. denudata, also known as the Naked Rose, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

This plant is prized for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, making it useful for treating wounds and skin conditions.

The health benefits of Rosa sericea var. denudata include its ability to reduce fever, alleviate digestive issues, and soothe respiratory problems, such as bronchitis and asthma.

Rosa sericea var. denudata can be prepared as a herbal tea, infusion, or tincture, and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine to promote overall well-being and vitality.
