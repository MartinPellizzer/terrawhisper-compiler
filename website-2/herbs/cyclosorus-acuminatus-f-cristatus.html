The leaflets of this variant are typically 5-15 pairs, with a linear to lanceolate shape and a spiny to wavy margin. The stipe of Cyclosorus acuminatus f. cristatus is 1-5 cm in length, bearing a lanceolate to linear lamina with a spiny to wavy margin.
