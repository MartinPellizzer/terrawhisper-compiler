Trifolium altissimum

Trifolium altissimum, commonly known as Big Trefoil or Tall Clover, is a perennial legume species native to Europe and Western Asia, characterized by its tall stem and clusters of pink or white flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Trifolium altissimum include flavonoids, isoflavones, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Big Trefoil is often used in traditional herbal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to promote overall well-being and alleviate symptoms of digestive disorders and menopausal symptoms.
