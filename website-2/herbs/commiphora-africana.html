Commiphora africana

Commiphora africana, also known as African myrrh, is a shrub or small tree species native to Africa, belonging to the Burseraceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Commiphora africana include sesquiterpenes, diterpenes, and triterpenes, particularly 15-oxopregna-5,16-dien-3beta-ol, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
