Pleopeltis sordida

Pleopeltis sordida, also known as the spotted polypody or sordid polypody, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The bioactive constituents of Pleopeltis sordida include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
