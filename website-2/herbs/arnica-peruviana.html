Arnica peruviana is classified within the family Asteraceae and the genus Arnica. The species is native to South America, specifically in the Andean region. It belongs to the class Magnoliopsida and the order Asterales. The family Asteraceae is a large and diverse group of flowering plants that includes daisies, sunflowers, and many others.
