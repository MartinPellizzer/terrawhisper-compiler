Urochloa mauritiana

The bioactive constituents of Urochloa mauritiana include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Urochloa mauritiana is often prepared as a decoction, infusion, or tincture, and is commonly used in herbal remedies for fever, cough, and respiratory issues.
