Huperzia mortonii

Huperzia mortonii, also known as Queensland tree fern or Huperzia, is a species of liverwort in the family Huperziaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Huperzia mortonii include huperzine A, a potent inhibitor of acetylcholinesterase, as well as other alkaloids and flavonoids.

Huperzia mortonii is commonly prepared as an extract or capsule for oral consumption, and is also used in some traditional Chinese medicine formulas.
