Piper miquelianum

Piper miquelianum, also known as Sichuan pepper plant, is a type of flowering plant in the pepper family (Piperaceae) native to the mountainous regions of China.

The plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues, fever, and respiratory problems, due to its anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antiviral properties. The bioactive constituents of Piper miquelianum include piperine, pipernonaline, and sesquiterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, the plant is often made into herbal preparations such as tea, capsules, and powder, and is also used to flavor dishes, particularly in Sichuan cuisine.
