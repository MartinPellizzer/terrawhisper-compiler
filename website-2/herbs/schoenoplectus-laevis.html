Schoenoplectus laevis

Schoenoplectus laevis, also known as smooth cordgrass, is a perennial wetland grass native to North America.

The bioactive constituents of Schoenoplectus laevis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and polysaccharides, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
