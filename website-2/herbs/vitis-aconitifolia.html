Vitis aconitifolia

Vitis aconitifolia, also known as Wu Zhu Yu, is a species of vine in the grape family (Vitaceae) native to China, characterized by its dark purple berries and aconitine alkaloid content.

The health benefits of Vitis aconitifolia include its ability to stimulate circulation, relieve pain, and exhibit anti-inflammatory properties, making it a valuable component in traditional Chinese medicine. The bioactive constituents of this plant include aconitine, mesaconitine, and hypaconitine alkaloids, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditional herbal preparations made with Vitis aconitifolia include dried berries, decoctions, and infusions, often combined with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects, and are commonly used to treat conditions such as arthritis and numbness.
