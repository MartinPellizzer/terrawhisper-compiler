Pilosocereus rupicola, also known as the rock pilosocereus or rock column cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Americas, characterized by its columnar stems and short, fine spines.

The bioactive constituents of Pilosocereus rupicola include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
