classification

Schlumbergera obtusangula is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae. However, it is more commonly associated with the family of cacti and is a member of the subfamily Rhipsalidoideae, within the family Cactaceae. Some sources also classify it as a member of the family Rhipsalaceae. It is native to Brazil and is often confused with its relative, the Christmas cactus, Schlumbergera bridgesii. This confusion may arise due to the similarities in their appearance and growth habits.
