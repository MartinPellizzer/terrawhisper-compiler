Cardamine tenuifolia

Cardamine tenuifolia, also known as bitter cress or slender bittercress, is a perennial plant in the family Brassicaceae, characterized by its delicate, fern-like foliage and small white flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Cardamine tenuifolia include isothiocyanates, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
