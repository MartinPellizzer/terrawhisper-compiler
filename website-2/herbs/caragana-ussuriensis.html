Caragana ussuriensis

Caragana ussuriensis, also known as Siberian pea tree, is a deciduous shrub native to northeastern Asia, characterized by its thorny stems and yellow flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Caragana ussuriensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
