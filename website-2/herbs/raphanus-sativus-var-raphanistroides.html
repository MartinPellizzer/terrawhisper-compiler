What is Raphanus sativus var. raphanistroides?

Raphanus sativus var. raphanistroides is an annual or biennial plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It has simple, alternate leaves that are pinnately lobed or dissected, with a glaucous or subglaucous pubescent surface. The plant produces a raceme of white or sometimes yellow flowers, with four petals and six stamens. The fruit is a silique, 1.5-2.5 cm long, with a glabrous or pubescent surface, and contains numerous seeds.

The plant is native to Asia and has been introduced to other parts of the world as a weed.
