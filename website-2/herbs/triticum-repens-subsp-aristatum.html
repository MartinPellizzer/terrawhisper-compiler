What is Triticum repens subsp. aristatum?

Triticum repens subsp.

aristatum is a botanical taxon within the species Triticum repens, a perennial grass belonging to the family Poaceae. It exhibits a distinctive growth habit, characterized by a tufted appearance and a rhizomatous stem, typically growing 10-50 cm in height.

The leaves are linear to lanceolate in shape, 2-10 cm in length, and feature a glabrous, smooth surface with a folded ligule. The inflorescence is a panicle, 5-20 cm in length, bearing spikelets with a glume and lemma, and a staminate flower.

The stamens are 3, with a filiform, glabrous, and unequal style.
