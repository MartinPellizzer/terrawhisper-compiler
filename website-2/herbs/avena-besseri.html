Avena besseri

Avena besseri, also known as Oat Avena, is a perennial grass species belonging to the Poaceae family.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to soothe digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and promote relaxation, which may be attributed to its anxiolytic and anti-stress properties. The bioactive constituents of Avena besseri include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and avenanthramides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Avena besseri is often used to make herbal preparations such as tea, infusions, and tinctures, which are commonly used to promote relaxation, improve sleep quality, and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and stress.
