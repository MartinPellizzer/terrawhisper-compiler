classification

Meum piperitum is a species of flowering plant in the carrot family, Apiaceae. It is native to Europe and Western Asia. Meum piperitum is a biennial herb with fern-like leaves and small, white or greenish flowers. The plant is often found in moist to dry woodland and hedgerow habitats. Meum piperitum is classified as Meum piperitum in the binomial nomenclature system.
