Urtica debilis

Urtica debilis, also known as Slender Nettle or Weak Nettle, is a perennial plant species in the nettle family (Urticaceae) that is native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It has been traditionally used for its health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, skin conditions, and allergies. The bioactive constituents of Urtica debilis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and other compounds with potential medicinal value.

In herbal medicine, Urtica debilis is often prepared as a tea, tincture, or capsule, and is used to treat various conditions, including digestive issues, fever, and respiratory problems.
