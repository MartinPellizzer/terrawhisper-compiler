What is Ophrys fuciflora subsp. heterochila?

heterochila is an orchid subspecies belonging to the family Orchidaceae. The sepals are elliptical, lanceolate or ovate, and the petals are similar to the sepals but smaller. The plant typically blooms in the spring and has a Mediterranean distribution, occurring in Greece and Turkey.
