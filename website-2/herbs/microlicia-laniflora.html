Microlicia laniflora

Microlicia laniflora is a plant species native to the Amazon rainforest, belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

The bioactive constituents of M. laniflora include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, M. laniflora is often prepared as an infusion or decoction, as well as being used to make tinctures and ointments, which are used to treat various health conditions.
