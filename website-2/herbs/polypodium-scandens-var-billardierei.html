What is Polypodium scandens var. billardierei?

The fronds are typically 10-30 cm in length and have a dark green color. Polypodium scandens var. billardierei is native to Australia, specifically in the eastern and southern regions.

It is found growing on trees and rocks in humid environments, often in association with other epiphytic species.
