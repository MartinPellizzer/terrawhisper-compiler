What is Scutellaria albida subsp. colchica?

Scutellaria albida subsp.

colchica is a subspecies of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae. It is an annual or biennial herbaceous plant with a height of 10-30 cm, characterized by a hairy stem, ovate-lanceolate leaves with a length of 3-10 cm and a width of 1-4 cm, and a white or pale blue flower that blooms in July-September. The inflorescence is a corymbose cyme with a diameter of 2-5 cm, consisting of 2-5 flowers.

The plant is native to the Caucasus region and is found in rocky, mountainous areas at elevations of 1000-2500 m. Its leaves and flowers are used in traditional medicine in Georgia.
