Senna confinis belongs to the family Fabaceae, commonly known as the legume or pea family, and is further classified as a genus within the subfamily Caesalpinioideae. The family Fabaceae is a large and diverse group of flowering plants that includes over 19,000 species. The classification of Senna confinis highlights its evolutionary relationships with other plants within the legume family.
