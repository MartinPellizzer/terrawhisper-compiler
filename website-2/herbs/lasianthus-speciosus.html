Lasianthus speciosus

Lasianthus speciosus, also known as the Showy Lasianthus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Theaceae, native to tropical Asia.

Bioactive constituents of Lasianthus speciosus include alkaloids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are believed to be responsible for its medicinal properties.
