Matricaria aurea

Matricaria aurea, also known as bitter chamomile, is a flowering plant in the daisy family that is native to Europe and Asia.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory and soothing effects, which can help to alleviate anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Matricaria aurea include flavonoids, terpenoids, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
