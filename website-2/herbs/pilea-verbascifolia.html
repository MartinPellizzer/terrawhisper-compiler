Pilea verbascifolia

Pilea verbascifolia, also known as the Chinese mayapple or Chinese mayapple vine, is a type of flowering plant in the family Urticaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and skin disorders, due to its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antipyretic properties. The bioactive constituents of Pilea verbascifolia include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Pilea verbascifolia include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat fever and skin conditions.
