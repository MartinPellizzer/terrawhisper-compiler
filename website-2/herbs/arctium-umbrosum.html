Arctium umbrosum

Arctium umbrosum, also known as lesser burdock or European burdock, is a biennial herbaceous plant belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including the treatment of fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, as well as its potential anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. The bioactive constituents of Arctium umbrosum include inulin, mucilage, and sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the roots and leaves of Arctium umbrosum are commonly used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being added to salves and creams for topical application.
