Microlepia chinensis

Microlepia chinensis, also known as the Chinese ladder fern, is a type of plant that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat a variety of health conditions, including inflammation, fever, and respiratory issues, due to its antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antiviral properties. The bioactive constituents of Microlepia chinensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and lignans, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

Microlepia chinensis is commonly prepared as a decoction, capsule, or tea, and is often used in combination with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic benefits.
