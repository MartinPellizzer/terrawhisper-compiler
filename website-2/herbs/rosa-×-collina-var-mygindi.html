What is Rosa × collina var. mygindi?

Rosa × collina var. mygindi is a rose variety that belongs to the family Rosaceae.

It is an evergreen or deciduous shrub with a height of up to 1.5 meters, and a spread of up to 1 meter. The leaves are pinnate, with 7-9 leaflets, and are 5-10 centimeters long. The flowers are pink to red, with 5 petals, and are 3-5 centimeters in diameter.

The fruit is a red or purple pome, 1-2 centimeters in diameter, containing 3-5 seeds.
