Wedelia jugata

Wedelia jugata, also known as Wedelia or Mexican mint, is a perennial herb belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

This plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various health issues, including fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions, due to its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antiviral properties. The bioactive constituents of Wedelia jugata include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Wedelia is commonly prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is also used in traditional herbal medicine to treat digestive issues, wounds, and respiratory problems.
