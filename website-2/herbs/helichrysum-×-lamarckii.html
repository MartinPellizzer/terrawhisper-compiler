Helichrysum × lamarckii

Helichrysum × lamarckii, also known as Lamark's Everlasting or Helichrysum, is a perennial shrub belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae) and is native to South Africa.

The bioactive constituents of Helichrysum × lamarckii include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
