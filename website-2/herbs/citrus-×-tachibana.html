classification

Citrus × tachibana is a hybrid of the sour orange (Citrus × aurantium) and the mandarin (Citrus reticulata). It is classified as a species of the genus Citrus. This plant is part of the Rutaceae family, which is a group of flowering plants commonly known as the citrus family. As a result of its hybrid origin, Citrus × tachibana exhibits characteristics from both the sour orange and the mandarin. The exact classification and taxonomy of Citrus × tachibana can vary depending on the source.
