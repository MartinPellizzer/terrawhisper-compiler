What is Opuntia lindheimeri var. lucens?

Opuntia lindheimeri var. lucens is a plant species that belongs to the family Cactaceae.

It is a spineless or nearly spineless variety of Opuntia lindheimeri, characterized by its flat, round to oval stems (cladodes) with 2-3 areoles per cladode, and yellow to orange flowers that are 2-3.5 cm in diameter. The cladodes are 4-7.5 cm in diameter and 2-5 cm long, with a dense covering of fine, short hairs (trichomes). Opuntia lindheimeri var. lucens is native to Texas, USA, and is found in dry, open areas.

It is a succulent plant that stores water in its stems.
