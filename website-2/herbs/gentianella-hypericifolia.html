Gentianella hypericifolia

Gentianella hypericifolia, also known as autumn gentian, is a perennial plant species belonging to the gentian family (Gentianaceae).

Traditionally, Gentianella hypericifolia has been used in folk medicine to treat various ailments, including digestive issues and fever, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can help to soothe and calm the body. The bioactive constituents of this plant include gentiopicroside, gentiogenin, and amarogentin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Gentianella hypericifolia is often used in herbal preparations, such as tinctures and decoctions, which are commonly used to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of gastrointestinal disorders.
