classification

Eulophia seleensis is classified as a species of orchid in the genus Eulophia, which belongs to the family Orchidaceae. The genus Eulophia is further classified under the subfamily Orchidoideae. The Orchidaceae family is one of the largest and most diverse plant families, with over 30,000 known species. The subfamily Orchidoideae is characterized by its monopodial growth habit and mostly leafless pseudobulbs. Eulophia seleensis is a terrestrial orchid native to Africa.
