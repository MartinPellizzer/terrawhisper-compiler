Mentha viridifolia

Mentha viridifolia, also known as green peppermint, is a species of mint plant that belongs to the family Lamiaceae.

The health benefits of Mentha viridifolia include its ability to alleviate digestive issues, such as nausea and indigestion, as well as its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to soothe sore throats and calm irritated skin. The bioactive constituents of Mentha viridifolia include menthone, menthyl acetate, and limonene, which contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Mentha viridifolia include teas, tinctures, and essential oils, which can be used to treat a range of health issues, from digestive problems to respiratory infections.
