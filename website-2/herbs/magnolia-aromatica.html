Magnolia aromatica

Magnolia aromatica, also known as Magnolia biondii, is a plant species belonging to the Magnoliaceae family, characterized by its fragrant flowers and aromatic bark.

The health benefits of Magnolia aromatica include its anti-inflammatory, anti-oxidant, and anti-stress properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various conditions such as anxiety, insomnia, and gastrointestinal issues. The bioactive constituents of Magnolia aromatica include magnolol, honokiol, and 4-O-methylhonokiol, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Magnolia aromatica include teas, tinctures, and capsules, often used in traditional Chinese medicine to promote relaxation and reduce stress.
