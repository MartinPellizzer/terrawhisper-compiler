Withania echinata

Withania echinata, also known as Earth's shield or withania, is a shrub-like plant native to the tropical regions of Asia, belonging to the Solanaceae family.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and adaptogenic properties, which may help to reduce stress, improve sleep, and boost the immune system. Withania echinata contains a range of bioactive constituents, including withanolides, alkaloids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Ayurvedic medicine, the plant is commonly used to make herbal preparations such as powder, decoctions, and oils, which are often used to treat various health conditions, including fever, digestive issues, and respiratory problems.
