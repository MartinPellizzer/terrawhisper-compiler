What is Crypsinus hastatus var. longisquamatus?

Crypsinus hastatus var. longisquamatus is a fern variety in the family Polypodiaceae.

It has fronds that are 30-60 cm long and 5-10 cm wide, with a lorate to ovate-lanceolate shape and an entire margin. The pinnae are alternate, with an acute apex and a cuneate base. The sori are linear, and the spores are monolete, measuring 70-80 μm in diameter.

The rhizome is short, with scales that are 2-3 mm long and 0.5-1 mm wide.
