What is Lindera megaphylla f. trichoclada?

Lindera megaphylla f. trichoclada is a botanical variety within the species Lindera megaphylla, a flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is characterized by elliptical leaves, 10-20 cm long, with entire margins, and a glossy upper surface, while the lower surface is glabrous. The leaves are opposite, and the plant has a woody stem. Lindera megaphylla f. trichoclada is a deciduous shrub, typically 1-3 m tall, with small, yellowish flowers in axillary cymes.

The flowers are 3-5 mm in diameter, with 3-4 petals, and are pollinated by bees and other insects.
