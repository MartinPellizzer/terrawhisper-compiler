Pseuderanthemum tetrasepalum

Pseuderanthemum tetrasepalum, also known as the four-veined pseuderanthemum, is a plant species in the Acanthaceae family, commonly found in tropical regions.

Bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.
