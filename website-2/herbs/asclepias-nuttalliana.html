Asclepias nuttalliana

Asclepias nuttalliana, also known as the Nootka milkweed or Nootka milkweed plant, is a perennial plant species native to the western regions of North America.

The bioactive constituents of Asclepias nuttalliana include cardenolides, which are a group of compounds that have been shown to have antitumor and cardioprotective effects, as well as other alkaloids and glycosides.
