Piper sumatranum

Piper sumatranum, also known as Sumatran long pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Piperaceae, native to the island of Sumatra in Indonesia.

The health benefits of Piper sumatranum include its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which have been traditionally used to alleviate digestive issues and reduce pain. The bioactive constituents of this plant include piperine, a piperidine alkaloid with potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activities, as well as other piperamides and sesquiterpenes.

In traditional medicine, Piper sumatranum is often prepared in herbal remedies such as decoctions, infusions, and tinctures, and is also used in combination with other herbs to create formulations for digestive and respiratory issues.
