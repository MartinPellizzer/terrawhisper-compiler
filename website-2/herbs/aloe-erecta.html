Aloe erecta

Aloe erecta, also known as the Aloidendron, is a succulent plant species native to southern Africa, characterized by its tall, branching stems and yellow or orange flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Aloe erecta include aloin, aloe-emodin, and anthraquinones, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Aloe erecta is commonly used to make herbal preparations such as capsules, juices, and gels, which are used to treat a range of health issues, including digestive problems, skin conditions, and minor injuries.
