classification

Festuca mediterranea belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family. It is classified as a species within the genus Festuca, which consists of various species of fescue grasses. This species is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its distinctive growth habit and leaf morphology. Festuca mediterranea is further classified as a perennial grass, meaning it grows and dies back annually but persists as a living plant over multiple years.
