Mimosa montevidensis

Mimosa montevidensis, also known as sensitive plant or sensitive mimosa, is a perennial legume native to South America, characterized by its distinctive compound leaves that fold up when touched or disturbed.

Bioactive constituents of Mimosa montevidensis include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which have been shown to exhibit immunomodulatory and antimicrobial activities.
