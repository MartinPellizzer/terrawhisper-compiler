Hystrix bigeloviana

Hystrix bigeloviana, also known as California thistle, is a perennial thistle plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects, which may help alleviate symptoms of colds, fever, and skin irritations. The bioactive constituents of California thistle include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

California thistle is commonly prepared as an infusion or tincture, and is often used in herbal medicine to treat respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs, as well as to support immune function.
