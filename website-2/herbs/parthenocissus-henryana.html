Parthenocissus henryana

Parthenocissus henryana, also known as the Henry's spirea or Chinese Virginia creeper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Vitaceae.

The plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its various health benefits, including reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and exhibiting antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Parthenocissus henryana include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and lignans, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
