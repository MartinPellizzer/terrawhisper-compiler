Ajania khartensis

Ajania khartensis, also known as Khartang or Russian tarragon, is a perennial plant belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

The plant is valued for its numerous health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, wounds, and infections. The bioactive constituents of Ajania khartensis include sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and essential oils, such as borneol and bornyl acetate, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Ajania khartensis is commonly prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture to utilize its health-promoting effects.
