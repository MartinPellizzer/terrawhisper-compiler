Poa rariflora

Poa rariflora, also known as meadow grass or slender poa, is a type of perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce inflammation and protect against cell damage. The bioactive constituents of Poa rariflora include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, the plant is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of health conditions, including digestive issues and respiratory problems.
