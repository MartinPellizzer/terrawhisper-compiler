What is Agave filifera var. filamentosa?

Agave filifera var. filamentosa is a plant that belongs to the family Asparagaceae.

It is a succulent with a stout, unbranched stem and a rosette of sharp, linear leaves that are 50-60 cm long and have a sharp, terminal spine. The leaves are green with yellow or brown margins and are covered with fine, white hairs. The inflorescence is a tall spike with yellow flowers that are 4-5 cm long and have six tepals.

The plant typically grows to 2-3 m in height and is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
