Eriocereus pomanensis

Eriocereus pomanensis, also known as the Poman's pitaya or Poman's hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus in the family Cactaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Eriocereus pomanensis include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
