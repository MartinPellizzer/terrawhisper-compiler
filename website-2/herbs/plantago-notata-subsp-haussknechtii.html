The plant is annual or biennial, with a taproot and a stem 10-40 cm tall, bearing a few leaves and a terminal spike of flowers. The leaves are glabrous or puberulent, and the plant is found in dry, stony areas and roadsides in the Mediterranean region. Plantago notata subsp.
