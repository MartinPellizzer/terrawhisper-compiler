What is Hirtella thouarsiana subsp. abrupte-acuminata?

abrupte-acuminata is an endemic species native to Madagascar, belonging to the family Chrysobalanaceae. It is a tree with a straight bole, up to 10 cm in diameter, and a rounded crown, typically growing to a height of 3-10 meters.

The bark is smooth, greyish-brown, and exfoliates in thin, papery scales.
