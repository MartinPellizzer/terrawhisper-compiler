Alliaria balansana

Alliaria balansana, also known as Balkan garlic mustard, is a perennial plant species in the Brassicaceae family, closely related to the common garlic mustard (Alliaria petiolata).

The plant has been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including respiratory problems, digestive issues, and skin conditions, due to its anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antiviral properties. The bioactive constituents of Alliaria balansana include isothiocyanates, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of health conditions and promote overall well-being.
