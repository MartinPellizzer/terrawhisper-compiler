What is Porophyllum coloratum var. obtusifolium?

Porophyllum coloratum var. obtusifolium is a botanical variety within the species Porophyllum coloratum, belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is a shrub or subshrub, typically growing to a height of 0.5-2 meters, with a spread of 0.5-1.5 meters. The leaves are alternate, elliptical to lanceolate, 2-7 centimeters long, and 0.5-3 centimeters wide, with a flat or slightly wavy margin. The inflorescence is a capitulum, composed of numerous small florets, typically yellow in color, and 1-2 centimeters in diameter.

The plant is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, thriving in dry to moist areas with well-drained soil.
