Hylotelephium purpureum

Hylotelephium purpureum, also known as purple leafed stonecrop, is a perennial plant species in the succulent family Crassulaceae, characterized by its striking deep purple foliage and vibrant pink flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Hylotelephium purpureum include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.
