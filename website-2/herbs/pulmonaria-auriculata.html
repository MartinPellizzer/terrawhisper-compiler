Pulmonaria auriculata

Pulmonaria auriculata, also known as lungwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the borage family (Boraginaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Pulmonaria auriculata include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
