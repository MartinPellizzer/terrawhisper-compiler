Medicago officinalis

Medicago officinalis, also known as the barrel medic, is a perennial legume native to Europe and Western Asia, characterized by its distinctive barrel-shaped pods and trifoliate leaves.

The plant is valued for its various health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which have been shown to help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, improve digestion, and support immune function. The bioactive constituents of Medicago officinalis include isoflavones, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant has been used to make herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are commonly used to support cardiovascular health and relieve menstrual disorders.
