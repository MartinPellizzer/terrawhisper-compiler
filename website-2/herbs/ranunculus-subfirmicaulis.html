Ranunculus subfirmicaulis

Ranunculus subfirmicaulis, also known as the Alpine Buttercup, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its yellow or white flowers and hairy leaves.

Bioactive constituents of Ranunculus subfirmicaulis include alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
