What is Tamarix hampeana var. philistaea?

Tamarix hampeana var. philistaea is a plant variety in the family Tamaricaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree, typically 2-5 meters tall, with a woody stem, green or yellowish-green branchlets, and scale-like leaves that are 1-2 millimeters long. The leaves are usually arranged in fascicles of 4-8. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in axillary clusters, which are yellowish-green or brownish-green in color.

The flowers are followed by small, brownish-red capsules, containing many small seeds.
