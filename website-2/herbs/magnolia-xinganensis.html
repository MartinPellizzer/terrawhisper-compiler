Magnolia xinganensis

Magnolia xinganensis is a plant species belonging to the Magnoliaceae family, characterized by its unique, aromatic flowers and broad, elliptical leaves.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities, making it beneficial for treating various health conditions such as fever, cough, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Magnolia xinganensis include magnolol, honokiol, and other lignans, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and capsules, which are used to treat colds, flu, and other respiratory infections.
