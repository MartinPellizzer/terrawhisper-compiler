Satureja serpyllifolia

Satureja serpyllifolia, also known as Creeping savory, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Europe.

The bioactive constituents of Satureja serpyllifolia include carvacrol, thymol, and borneol, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
