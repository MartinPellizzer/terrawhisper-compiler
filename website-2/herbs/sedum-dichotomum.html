Sedum dichotomum

Sedum dichotomum, also known as Burdock or Gypsum Burdock, is a succulent plant species native to China and parts of Asia, traditionally used in traditional medicine for its therapeutic properties.

The plant has been found to possess several health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may aid in the treatment of various ailments such as fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Sedum dichotomum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Sedum dichotomum is often used to make infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, as well as being used in topical applications such as ointments and creams to harness its therapeutic benefits.
