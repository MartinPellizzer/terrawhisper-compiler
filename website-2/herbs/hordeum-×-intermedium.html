Hordeum × intermedium

Hordeum × intermedium, also known as intermediate barley, is a hybrid plant derived from the cross-breeding of Hordeum vulgare (common barley) and Hordeum murinum (wall barley).

The bioactive constituents of Hordeum × intermedium include sesquiterpenes, phenolic compounds, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
