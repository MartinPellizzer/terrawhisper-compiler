classification

Halophila ovalis is classified as a species of flowering plant in the family Hydrocharitaceae. This classification places it within the order Hydrocharitales and the class Liliopsida. As a member of the family Hydrocharitaceae, it is closely related to other aquatic plants such as Vallisneria and Elodea. The family Hydrocharitaceae is a group of monocotyledonous flowering plants commonly known as the tape grasses or water weeds. Halophila ovalis is native to tropical and subtropical waters around the world.
