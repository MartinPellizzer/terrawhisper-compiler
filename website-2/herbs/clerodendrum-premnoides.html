Clerodendrum premnoides

Clerodendrum premnoides, also known as the Premna or Thai cigar plant, is a tropical plant species native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its unique, cigar-shaped inflorescences.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include potential health benefits such as antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant effects, making it useful in treating various ailments. The bioactive constituents of C. premnoides include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, C. premnoides is often used to make herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and extracts, which are used to treat conditions like fever, cough, and skin infections.
