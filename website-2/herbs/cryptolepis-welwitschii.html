Cryptolepis welwitschii

Cryptolepis welwitschii, also known as the Cryptolepis plant, is a perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Africa, particularly in the Democratic Republic of Congo.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include cryptolepine, a bisbenzylisoquinoline alkaloid with potent antimalarial activity, as well as other compounds like 7-hydroxycryptolepine and cryptoside.
