Astragalus orthanthoides

Astragalus orthanthoides, also known as Huang Qi, is a perennial plant species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Astragalus orthanthoides include flavonoids, saponins, polysaccharides, and alkaloids, which are thought to contribute to its medicinal effects.

Astragalus orthanthoides is commonly used in herbal preparations, such as capsules, teas, and tinctures, as well as being added to soups and other dishes in traditional Chinese cuisine.
