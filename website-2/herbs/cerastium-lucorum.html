Cerastium lucorum

Cerastium lucorum, commonly known as mountain chickweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant species belonging to the carnation family (Caryophyllaceae).

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
