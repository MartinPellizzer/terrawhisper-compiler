classification

Vriesea castaneobulbosa belongs to the family Bromeliaceae. This family consists of a wide variety of plants, commonly known as bromeliads. Bromeliaceae is a monocot family, which means they have one cotyledon in their seeds. They are native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, often found growing in shaded, humid areas. As a member of this family, Vriesea castaneobulbosa shares characteristics such as its epiphytic growth habit and modified leaves that form a water-holding rosette.
