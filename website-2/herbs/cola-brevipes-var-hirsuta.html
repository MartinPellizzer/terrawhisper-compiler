What is Cola brevipes var. hirsuta?

Cola brevipes var. hirsuta is a botanical variant of the species Cola brevipes, belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree with a height range of 3-12 meters, characterized by a glabrous to hirsute stem and branchlets. The leaves are elliptic to ovate in shape, measuring 7-20 cm in length and 3-9 cm in width, with a cuneate base and a glabrous to hirsute underside. The inflorescence is a corymb, bearing numerous small flowers with a calyx of 5-6 mm in length and a corolla of 4-5 mm in diameter.

The fruit is a capsule, measuring 3-5 cm in length and 2-3 cm in width, with a glabrous to hirsute surface and 5-6 seeds.
