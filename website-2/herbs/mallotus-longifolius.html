Mallotus longifolius

The bioactive constituents of Mallotus longifolius include flavonoids, saponins, and triterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The plant is commonly used to prepare herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to alleviate symptoms of fever, arthritis, and skin irritations.
