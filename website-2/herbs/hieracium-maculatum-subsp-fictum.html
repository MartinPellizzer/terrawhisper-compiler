What is Hieracium maculatum subsp. fictum?

The plant produces yellow dandelion-like flowers, 3-4 cm in diameter, with an outer floret of 20-30 florets. Each floret is 8-10 mm long, with a corolla that is 3-4 mm long and has 3-4 mm long, yellow, strap-shaped lobes.

The species is endemic to the Iberian Peninsula, specifically in the northern and central regions of Spain.
