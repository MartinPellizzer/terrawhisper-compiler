Gentiana carinata

Gentiana carinata is a species of flowering plant in the gentian family, known for its vibrant blue flowers and potential medicinal properties.

The plant is valued for its ability to stimulate digestion and alleviate symptoms of indigestion and nausea, making it a popular remedy for gastrointestinal issues. Gentiana carinata contains bioactive constituents such as gentiopicrin, gentianine, and amarogentin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Gentiana carinata is commonly prepared as a tincture, decoction, or dry extract, and is often used to support digestive health and relieve symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome.
