Calliandra cubensis

The Calliandra cubensis, also known as the Pink Powder Puff tree, is a flowering tree native to the tropical regions of Cuba, the Dominican Republic, and parts of Central and South America.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses including the treatment of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which may help to reduce the risk of chronic diseases. The bioactive constituents of Calliandra cubensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which have been shown to possess antimicrobial and antioxidant activities.

In traditional herbal medicine, Calliandra cubensis is commonly prepared as a tea or infusion, as well as being used in tincture and capsule forms, to take advantage of its potential health benefits.
