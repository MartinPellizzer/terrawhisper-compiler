Gerbera kunzeana

Gerbera kunzeana, also known as Gerbera, is a flowering plant species within the daisy family (Asteraceae) that is native to South Africa.

The bioactive constituents of Gerbera kunzeana include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
