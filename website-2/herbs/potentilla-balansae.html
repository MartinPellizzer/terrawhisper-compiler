Potentilla balansae

Potentilla balansae, also known as Balansa cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

Traditionally, this plant has been used in herbal medicine to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues, fever, and respiratory problems, due to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Potentilla balansae include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

In herbal medicine, Potentilla balansae is often prepared as a tea, tincture, or infusion to alleviate symptoms associated with colds, sore throats, and coughs.
