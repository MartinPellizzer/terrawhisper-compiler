Batatas quinquefolia

Batatas quinquefolia, also known as Purple Passionflower or Purple Passion Vine, is a perennial plant native to South America.

The plant has been traditionally used for its calming and sedative properties, providing relief from anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness. Its bioactive constituents include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, including batatin, batatine, and linarin, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Batatas quinquefolia include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality.
