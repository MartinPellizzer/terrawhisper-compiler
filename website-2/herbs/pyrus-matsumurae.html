Pyrus matsumurae

Pyrus matsumurae, also known as Japanese Mountain Apple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and coumarins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
