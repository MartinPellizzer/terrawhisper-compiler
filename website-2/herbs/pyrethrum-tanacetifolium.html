Pyrethrum tanacetifolium

Pyrethrum tanacetifolium, also known as pyrethrum, is a perennial daisy-like plant native to Asia and Europe, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The health benefits of pyrethrum include its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antiviral properties, making it effective in treating various conditions such as skin irritations, wounds, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of pyrethrum include pyrethrin I and II, which are responsible for its insecticidal properties, as well as flavonoids and sesquiterpenes that contribute to its medicinal effects.

Pyrethrum is commonly used in herbal preparations, including tinctures, infusions, and ointments, which are often used to treat skin conditions, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing.

side effects

Pyrethrum tanacetifolium, also known as Pyrethrum or Dalmatian pyrethrum, is a plant-based insecticide. While generally considered safe, it can cause skin irritation, allergic reactions, and respiratory problems in some individuals. Prolonged exposure may also lead to neurological effects like headaches and dizziness.
