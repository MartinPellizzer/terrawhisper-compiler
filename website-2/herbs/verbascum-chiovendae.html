Verbascum chiovendae

Verbascum chiovendae, also known as Chiovenda's mullein, is a plant species within the mullein genus, characterized by its tall stalks and large, velvety leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Verbascum chiovendae include flavonoids, saponins, and mucilage, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Verbascum chiovendae is often prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is sometimes combined with other herbs to create syrups and cough medicines for the treatment of respiratory issues.
