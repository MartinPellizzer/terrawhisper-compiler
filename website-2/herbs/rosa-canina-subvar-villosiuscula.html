What is Rosa canina subvar. villosiuscula?

Rosa canina subvar. villosiuscula is a subspecies of dog rose, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems, typically 1-3 meters tall, and has dark green, pinnate leaves with 5-7 leaflets, 3-5 cm long. The flowers are pink to purple, 4-6 cm in diameter, with five petals and a central cluster of stamens. The fruit is a red, 1-2 cm long, hairy hip, containing 5-6 seeds, which are dispersed by birds and other animals.

The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and has been naturalized in other parts of Europe.
