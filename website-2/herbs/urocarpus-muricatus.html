Urocarpus muricatus

Urocarpus muricatus, also known as the prickly juniper or prickly cedar, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its potential to aid in digestion and relieve symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome due to its antispasmodic and anti-inflammatory effects. The bioactive constituents of Urocarpus muricatus include essential oils, flavonoids, and terpenes, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
