Phragmites flavescens

Phragmites flavescens, also known as yellow loosestrife or yellow spike-grass, is a perennial herb belonging to the grass family.

The bioactive constituents of Phragmites flavescens include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
