The bioactive constituents of Sabadilla intermedia include alkaloids like calycanthine and sabadillin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Sabadilla intermedia is commonly prepared as a tincture, powder, or infusion, and is used to treat a range of health issues, including parasitic infections, skin conditions, and respiratory problems.
