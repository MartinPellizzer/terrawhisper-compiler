Ligularia ramosissima

Ligularia ramosissima, also known as Himalayan Ligularia, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its bright yellow flowers and edible leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Ligularia ramosissima include sesquiterpenoids, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
