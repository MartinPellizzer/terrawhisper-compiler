Hypericum monogynum

Hypericum monogynum, also known as Saint John's Wort, is a flowering plant in the St. John's Wort family (Hypericaceae) native to Africa, Europe, and Asia.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as reducing anxiety and depression, improving sleep quality, and exhibiting antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects. The bioactive constituents of Hypericum monogynum include hyperforin, hypericin, and flavonoids, which are believed to contribute to its therapeutic properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with St. John's Wort include teas, capsules, and tinctures, which are often used to treat mild to moderate depression and anxiety disorders.
