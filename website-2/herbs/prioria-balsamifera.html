Prioria balsamifera

The bioactive constituents of Prioria balsamifera resin include benzoic and cinnamic acids, as well as phenolic compounds like gallic acid and ellagic acid, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Prioria balsamifera include tinctures, creams, and oils, which are often used topically to soothe skin conditions, promote wound healing, and reduce inflammation.
