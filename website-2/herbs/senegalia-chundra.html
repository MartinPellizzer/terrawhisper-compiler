Senegalia chundra

Senegalia chundra, also known as Chundra's tree or Acacia chundra, is a species of tree in the pea family, Fabaceae, native to India.

The bioactive constituents of Senegalia chundra include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Ayurvedic medicine, the plant is commonly prepared as a decoction, powder, or infusion, often used to treat fever and respiratory issues, and is also used in the form of a paste or powder applied topically to treat skin conditions.
