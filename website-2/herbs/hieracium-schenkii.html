Hieracium schenkii

Hieracium schenkii, also known as Hawkweed or Schenk's Hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in wound healing, reducing fever, and treating respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Hieracium schenkii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Hieracium schenkii is often prepared as a tincture, infusion, or tea, and sometimes used in combination with other herbs to create topical creams or ointments for skin conditions and wound care.
