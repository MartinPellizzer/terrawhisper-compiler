What is Sabulina juniperina subsp. glandulifera?

Sabulina juniperina subsp.

glandulifera is a taxonomic subdivision of the plant species Sabulina juniperina, belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae. It is a succulent perennial herb, with a short stem and leaves that are densely pubescent and covered with glandular hairs.

The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in axillary clusters, which are pedunculate and have a five-lobed calyx. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, have a crenulate margin, and are 1-4 mm in length.

The species is found in the Mediterranean region.
