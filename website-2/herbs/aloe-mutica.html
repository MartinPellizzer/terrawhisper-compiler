Aloe mutica

Aloe mutica, also known as the desert aloe, is a succulent plant species native to the deserts of Africa and the Middle East.

The health benefits of Aloe mutica include its ability to soothe and treat various skin conditions, such as burns, wounds, and eczema, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Aloe mutica include aloe-emodin, aloin, and other anthraquinone compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Aloe mutica include gel, ointments, and creams for topical application, as well as oral supplements in the form of capsules or tablets.
