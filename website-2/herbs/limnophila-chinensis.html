Limnophila chinensis, also known as Swamp Violet or Swamp Loosestrife, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Limnophila chinensis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal activities.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Limnophila chinensis is often prepared as a decoction or tincture, and is also used in combination with other herbs to treat conditions such as bronchitis, coughs, and sore throats.
