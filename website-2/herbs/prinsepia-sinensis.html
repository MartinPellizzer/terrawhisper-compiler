Prinsepia sinensis

Prinsepia sinensis, also known as Chinese Prinsepia or Prinsepia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China, belonging to the family Myricaceae.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which have been shown to possess significant pharmacological activities.

Prinsepia sinensis is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine and is prepared into various herbal products, including tea, capsules, and tinctures, which are often used to relieve respiratory symptoms and boost the immune system.
