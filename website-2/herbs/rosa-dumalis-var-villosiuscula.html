What is Rosa dumalis var. villosiuscula?

Rosa dumalis var. villosiuscula is a variety of shrub in the family Rosaceae, native to the Himalayas.

It has a woody base and thorny stems, typically growing to 3-4 meters in height, with a spread of about 2 meters. The leaves are dark green, elliptical, and serrated, measuring 4-10 cm in length and 1-3 cm in width. The flowers are pink to rose-purple, fragrant, and solitary, with 5-7 petals, measuring 4-6 cm in diameter, and appearing from June to August.

The plant produces red, oval-shaped hips, 1-2 cm in length, containing numerous seeds.
