Pavonia latifolia

Pavonia latifolia, also known as the rose mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Central and South America.

The bioactive constituents of Pavonia latifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
