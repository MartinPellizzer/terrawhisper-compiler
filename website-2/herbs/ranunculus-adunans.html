Ranunculus adunans

Ranunculus adunans, also known as Adonis or Spring Adonis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and Western Asia, characterized by its bright yellow flowers and long, narrow leaves.

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in the treatment of wounds, skin conditions, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Ranunculus adunans include alkaloids, flavonoids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

In herbal medicine, Ranunculus adunans is often prepared as a tincture, decoction, or infusement, and is used to treat conditions such as eczema, acne, and bronchitis.
