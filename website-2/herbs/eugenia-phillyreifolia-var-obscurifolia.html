The leaf margins are entire, and the leaves have a petiole of 0.3-0.5 cm in length. The plant produces small, white flowers in axillary corymbose panicles, while the fruits are red or purple, globose, and 5-7 mm in diameter.

The variety is native to Brazil, where it grows in the Atlantic Forest biome.
