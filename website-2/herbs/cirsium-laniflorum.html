Cirsium laniflorum

Cirsium laniflorum, also known as Woolly Thistle or Woolly Cirsium, is a perennial plant species belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

This plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. The bioactive constituents of Cirsium laniflorum include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to support immune function and alleviate symptoms of respiratory infections.
