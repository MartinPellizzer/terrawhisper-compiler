Dioscorea esculenta var. fasciculata

Dioscorea esculenta var. fasciculata, also known as the fascicled yam, is a variety of yam plant that belongs to the Dioscoreaceae family, characterized by its tuberous roots.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-diabetic effects, which are attributed to the presence of bioactive compounds such as diosgenin and other steroidal saponins.

The health benefits of Dioscorea esculenta var. fasciculata include its potential to reduce blood sugar levels, improve insulin sensitivity, and provide relief from menstrual discomfort, among others.

In herbal preparations, the tuberous roots of Dioscorea esculenta var. fasciculata are often dried, powdered, and used to make teas, capsules, or tablets that can be consumed to harness its medicinal properties.
