What is Seseli rigidum var. intermedium?

Seseli rigidum var. intermedium is a plant that belongs to the family Apiaceae.

It is a perennial herb with a stout, woody base and a leafy stem that can grow up to 30-60 cm in height. The leaves are pinnate, composed of 3-5 pairs of leaflets, with a leaflet length of 1-3 cm. The inflorescence is a compound umbel, containing 10-30 small, white flowers, with a calyx of 3-4 green bracts and a corolla of 5 petals.

The fruit is a schizocarp, consisting of 2-3 mericarps, each mericarp measuring 3-5 mm in length.
