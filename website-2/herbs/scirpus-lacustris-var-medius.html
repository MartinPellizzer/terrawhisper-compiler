What is Scirpus lacustris var. medius?

Scirpus lacustris var. medius is a perennial aquatic plant that belongs to the family Cyperaceae.

It has a stout stem, typically 0.5-2 meters tall, with a woody base and a few nodes. The leaves are flat, linear, 5-20 mm wide, and have a glabrous or slightly hairy surface. The inflorescence is a spike-like raceme, 5-20 cm long, with 5-20 spikelets, each containing 1-3 flowers.

The flowers are unisexual, with the male flowers having 3 stamens and the female flowers having 3 stigmas.
