Abelmoschus officinalis

Abelmoschus officinalis, also known as Kuan Zhao, is a plant species belonging to the mallow family (Malvaceae) and is native to Asia.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antidiabetic properties, making it useful in traditional medicine for treating various ailments such as fever, cough, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Abelmoschus officinalis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

In traditional Chinese medicine, the plant is often prepared as a decoction or tea, or used in the form of capsules or tablets, and is also used to make a medicinal wine.
