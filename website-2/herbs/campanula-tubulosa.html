classification

Campanula tubulosa belongs to the family Campanulaceae, commonly known as the bellflower family. This classification is based on the plant's morphological characteristics, such as its bell-shaped flowers and hairy leaves. Campanula tubulosa is a genus of flowering plants that includes over 300 species, many of which are native to the Mediterranean region. The plant's classification is further supported by its genetic relationships with other members of the Campanulaceae family. As a result, Campanula tubulosa is classified within the Campanulaceae family and the Campanula genus.
