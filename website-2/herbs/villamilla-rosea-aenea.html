Villamilla rosea-aenea

The Villamilla rosea-aenea is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae, commonly known as the Villamilla or Mexican Villamilla.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which may help to alleviate symptoms of conditions such as arthritis and respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of Villamilla rosea-aenea include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The plant is often used to make herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into traditional remedies and folk medicines in some parts of Mexico.
