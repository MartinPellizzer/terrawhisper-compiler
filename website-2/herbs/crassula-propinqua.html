Crassula propinqua

Crassula propinqua, also known as String of Bananas or Necklace Plant, is a succulent plant species native to South Africa, characterized by its long, trailing stems and small, banana-shaped leaves.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include the treatment of digestive issues, such as constipation and irritable bowel syndrome, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. Crassula propinqua contains bioactive constituents like flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
