Meibomia grahamii

Meibomia grahamii, also known as Graham's meadow beauty, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America, belonging to the legume family (Fabaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Meibomia grahamii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
