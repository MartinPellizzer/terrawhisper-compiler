Halimium × cheiranthoides

Halimium × cheiranthoides, commonly known as Cistus x purpureus or Purple Rockrose, is a hybrid perennial plant species in the family Cistaceae.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in wound healing and skin protection. The bioactive constituents of Halimium × cheiranthoides include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, such as ursolic acid, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
