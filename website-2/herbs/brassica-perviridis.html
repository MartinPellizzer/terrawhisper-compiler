Brassica perviridis

Brassica perviridis, also known as Chinese flowering mustard, is a species of plant in the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its bright yellow flowers and edible greens.

The bioactive constituents of Brassica perviridis include glucosinolates, isothiocyanates, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditional Chinese medicine often uses the plant to make herbal preparations, such as teas and infusions, as well as soups and stir-fries, to treat a range of health conditions, including fever, cough, and digestive issues.
