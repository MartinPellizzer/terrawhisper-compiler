Oberonia ensifolia

Oberonia ensifolia is a species of orchid native to tropical Asia, known for its unique, sword-shaped leaves and delicate white flowers.

The plant has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine, offering potential health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may aid in the treatment of various ailments, including fever and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Oberonia ensifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Oberonia ensifolia is often prepared as a decoction, tea, or powder, and is commonly used to treat fever, cough, and other respiratory problems.
