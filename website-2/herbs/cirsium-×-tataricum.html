Cirsium × tataricum

Cirsium × tataricum, also known as Tatar flower thistle or Tatar weed, is a hybrid plant species within the thistle family (Asteraceae) that is native to Eurasia.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory properties, which can aid in alleviating symptoms of colds, fever, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Cirsium × tataricum include flavonoids, saponins, and polysaccharides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the plant is commonly prepared as a tea, infusion, or decoction, as well as being used in tinctures and salves, to harness its potential health benefits.
