What is Gossypium barbadense f. suffruticosum?

Gossypium barbadense f. suffruticosum is a plant taxon that belongs to the family Malvaceae, characterized by its shrub-like habit and glabrous stem.

It is a variety of Gossypium barbadense, a species of cotton native to South America. The plant's leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, measuring 5-10 cm in length, with a cuneate base and an acute apex. The inflorescence is a terminal panicle, bearing 5-merous flowers with a greenish-yellow corolla and a staminal column with 5 anthers.

The plant's flowers are followed by a capsule of 3-5 locules, each containing 1-2 seeds.
