Leucanthemum tenuifolium

Leucanthemum tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved daisy or silvery daisy, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including the treatment of digestive issues, fever reduction, and relief from inflammation due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. Bioactive constituents of Leucanthemum tenuifolium include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The plant is commonly used to make herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into traditional remedies for its purported health benefits.
