Bletia martiana

Bletia martiana, also known as Martiana orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, characterized by its unique and fragrant flowers.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine, particularly in the Philippines, for its various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which are thought to aid in the treatment of fever, respiratory infections, and other health conditions. The bioactive constituents of Bletia martiana include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Bletia martiana is commonly prepared as a tea or decoction, or used in the form of a tincture or infusion, to harness its health benefits.
