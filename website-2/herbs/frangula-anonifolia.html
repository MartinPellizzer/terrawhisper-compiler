Frangula anonifolia

Frangula anonifolia, also known as Buckthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Europe and Western Asia, characterized by its dark green leaves and red berries.

The health benefits of Frangula anonifolia include its potential use as a laxative and treatment for various gastrointestinal issues due to its anthraquinone content. The bioactive constituents of this plant include anthraquinones, such as frangulin, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Frangula anonifolia is commonly used to make herbal preparations like tinctures, teas, and capsules, which are used to treat constipation and other digestive issues.
