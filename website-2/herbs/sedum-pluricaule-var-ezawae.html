What is Sedum pluricaule var. ezawae?

Sedum pluricaule var. ezawae is a plant variety within the species Sedum pluricaule, belonging to the family Crassulaceae.

It is a succulent perennial with a woody base and several upright stems, 10-50 cm tall, bearing fleshy, lanceolate leaves that are 2-6 cm long and 0.5-2 cm wide. The leaves are green, sometimes with a reddish tint, and have a ciliate margin. Sedum pluricaule var. ezawae produces white or pale yellow flowers in late summer, in a corymbous inflorescence.

The flowers are 3-5 cm in diameter, with five petals that are 5-7 mm long.
