Picris tuberosa

The bioactive constituents of Picris tuberosa include sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made from this plant include teas, infusions, and decoctions, which are often used to treat liver and gallbladder problems, as well as to aid in digestion and reduce inflammation.
