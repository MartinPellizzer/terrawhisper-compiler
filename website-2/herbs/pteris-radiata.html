classification

Pteris radiata is a species of fern in the family Pteridaceae. It belongs to the genus Pteris, which comprises approximately 300 species of bracken ferns. The Pteridaceae family is a part of the division Pteridophyta, which includes all ferns. The division Pteridophyta is further classified under the kingdom Plantae. This classification system reflects the plant's evolutionary relationships and characteristics.
