classification

Juncus sylvaticus is a species of rush that belongs to the family Juncaceae. It is a perennial plant native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa. The scientific classification of Juncus sylvaticus is as follows: Kingdom: Plantae, Clade: Angiosperms, Clade: Monocots, Order: Poales, Family: Juncaceae, Genus: Juncus, Species: J. sylvaticus. This classification places Juncus sylvaticus within the monocot group of flowering plants. The family Juncaceae is a small group of flowering plants commonly known as rushes or rushes family.
