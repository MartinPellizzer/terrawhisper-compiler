What is Triticum ciliatum var. monostachyon?

Triticum ciliatum var. monostachyon is a taxonomic variety within the species Triticum ciliatum, belonging to the family Poaceae.

It possesses a diploid chromosome number of 14, with two sets of seven chromosomes. The plant exhibits a monocot habit, with alternate and distichous phyllotaxis, and consists of linear, flat, and narrow leaves. Triticum ciliatum var. monostachyon is characterized by a single stem (monostachy), with spikelets that are paired and peduncled, and a 5-nerved glume.

The plant produces spikelets that are 8-12 mm long, with a rachilla that is 1.5-3 mm long and a lemma that is 6-8 mm long.
