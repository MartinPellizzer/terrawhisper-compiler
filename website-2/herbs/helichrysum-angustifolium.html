Helichrysum angustifolium

Helichrysum angustifolium, also known as Narrow-leaved Everlasting, is a perennial herb belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it useful for wound healing, skin conditions, and reducing inflammation. The bioactive constituents of Helichrysum angustifolium include sesquiterpene lactones, such as dihydroartemisinin and dihydrocostuslactone, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids.

The plant is often used in herbal medicine and is commonly prepared as a tea, tincture, or cream, which can be used topically to soothe skin irritations and promote wound healing.
