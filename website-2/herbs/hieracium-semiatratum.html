Hieracium semiatratum

Hieracium semiatratum, commonly known as yellow hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, making it a promising natural remedy for various ailments. The bioactive constituents of Hieracium semiatratum include sesquiterpene lactones, such as lactucopicrin and helenalin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Hieracium semiatratum is often used to prepare teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are traditionally used to treat digestive issues, fever, and skin conditions.
