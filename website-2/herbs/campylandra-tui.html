Campylandra tui

Campylandra tui, also known as Tu'i or Tu'i root, is a type of rhizome-bearing plant native to the Pacific Islands.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which may aid in wound healing and reducing pain. Bioactive constituents of Campylandra tui include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, often used to treat various health conditions, including fever, sore throats, and skin irritations.
