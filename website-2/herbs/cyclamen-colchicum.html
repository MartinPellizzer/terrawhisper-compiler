Cyclamen colchicum

Cyclamen colchicum, also known as autumn-flowering cyclamen, is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the primrose family (Primulaceae).

The health benefits of Cyclamen colchicum include its potential to act as a diuretic, helping to reduce fluid retention, and its anti-inflammatory properties, which may alleviate symptoms of arthritis and other inflammatory conditions. Bioactive constituents of this plant include colchicine, a glycoside that has been shown to have anti-inflammatory and immunosuppressive effects, as well as flavonoids and alkaloids, which may contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Cyclamen colchicum is often prepared as a tincture or dried root powder, which can be used to treat conditions such as gout, arthritis, and joint pain.
