Capparis trifoliata

Capparis trifoliata, also known as three-leaf caper or caper bush, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Capparaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Capparis trifoliata include alkaloids, flavonoids, and polyphenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
