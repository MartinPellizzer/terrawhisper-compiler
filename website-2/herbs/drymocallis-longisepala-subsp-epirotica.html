What is Drymocallis longisepala subsp. epirotica?

Drymocallis longisepala subsp.

epirotica is a plant species within the family Rosaceae. It is characterized by its glabrous stems, 10-40 cm in length, with leaves that are pinnately compound, having 3-9 leaflets.

Each leaflet is 2-7 cm in length, lanceolate to elliptic in shape, with serrate margins. The flowers are small, 5-6 mm in diameter, with 5 white petals and a yellow center, arranged in corymbose inflorescences.
