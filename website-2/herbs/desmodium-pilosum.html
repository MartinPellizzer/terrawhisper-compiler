Desmodium pilosum

Desmodium pilosum, also known as tick clover or hairy vetch, is a perennial legume native to Africa and Asia, characterized by its hairy stems and white or purple flowers.

Bioactive constituents of Desmodium pilosum include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
