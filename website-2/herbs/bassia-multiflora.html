Bassia multiflora

Bassia multiflora, also known as Mongolian thistle or wormwood, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Bassia multiflora include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
