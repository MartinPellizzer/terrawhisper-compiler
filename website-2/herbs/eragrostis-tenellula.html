Eragrostis tenellula

Eragrostis tenellula, also known as Chinese love grass, is a type of perennial grass native to Asia and parts of Africa, characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, greenish-yellow flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Eragrostis tenellula include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
