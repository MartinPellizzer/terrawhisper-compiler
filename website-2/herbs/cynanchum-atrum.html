Cynanchum atrum

Cynanchum atrum, also known as the black swallowwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, and it is believed to have health benefits such as reducing fever, relieving pain, and improving cardiovascular health. The bioactive constituents of Cynanchum atrum include alkaloids, such as atracotoxin and chasmanthin, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Cynanchum atrum is commonly prepared as a decoction or infusion, and it is often combined with other herbs to create herbal preparations such as the "Chenpi San" and "Sang Huang Tang" formulas.
