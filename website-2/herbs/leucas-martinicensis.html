Leucas martinicensis

Leucas martinicensis is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family, native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

The bioactive constituents of Leucas martinicensis include sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
