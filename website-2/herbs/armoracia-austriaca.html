Armoracia austriaca

Armoracia austriaca, also known as Austrian horseradish, is a perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its pungent roots and leaves.

The health benefits of Austrian horseradish include its ability to act as a natural digestive aid, anti-inflammatory agent, and antimicrobial compound, making it useful in alleviating symptoms of indigestion, arthritis, and infections. The bioactive constituents of this plant include isothiocyanates, such as allyl isothiocyanate and phenylethyl isothiocyanate, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Austrian horseradish is commonly prepared as a root extract, tincture, or powdered supplement, and is often used in herbal remedies to support digestive health and relieve respiratory issues.
