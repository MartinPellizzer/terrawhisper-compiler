Nephrolepis rufescens

Nephrolepis rufescens, also known as the Boston fern or Rufescens fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions.

The bioactive constituents of Nephrolepis rufescens include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
