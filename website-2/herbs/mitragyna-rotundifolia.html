Mitragyna rotundifolia

Mitragyna rotundifolia, also known as the African kratom or Thai mitragyna, is a plant species belonging to the Rubiaceae family, native to Southeast Asia and Africa.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate pain, reduce fever, and protect against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Mitragyna rotundifolia include a variety of alkaloids, such as mitragynine, paynanthine, and speciogynine, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

The most common herbal preparations made from this plant include dried leaves, tea infusions, and capsules, which are used to support overall well-being and provide relief from various health issues.
