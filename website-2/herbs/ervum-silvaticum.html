Ervum silvaticum

Ervum silvaticum, also known as Silver Medick, is a perennial legume native to Europe and Asia, characterized by its small, yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including its potential to treat digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of menopause due to its estrogenic activity. The bioactive constituents of Ervum silvaticum include isoflavones, saponins, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

The plant is commonly used to make herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, and is often used in traditional medicine to support women's health and alleviate menopausal symptoms.
