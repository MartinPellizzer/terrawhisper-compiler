Stellaria laevis

Stellaria laevis, also known as pale chickweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its delicate white flowers and creeping stems.

The health benefits of Stellaria laevis include its potential to reduce inflammation and alleviate symptoms associated with eczema, acne, and other skin conditions due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Stellaria laevis is commonly prepared as a tea, tincture, or topical cream, and is often used to soothe skin irritations, wounds, and insect bites.
