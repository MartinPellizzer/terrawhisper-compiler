Pyrus sachalinensis

Pyrus sachalinensis, also known as the Sakhalin pear, is a deciduous tree species native to the Russian Far East and northeastern China.

The bioactive constituents of Pyrus sachalinensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
