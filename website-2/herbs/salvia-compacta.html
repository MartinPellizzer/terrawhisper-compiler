classification

Salvia compacta is a member of the Lamiaceae family. This family includes a wide range of herbs and flowering plants commonly known as mint or sage. The Lamiaceae family is part of the larger clade known as asterids. Asterids are a group of flowering plants that also include the Ericales and Gentianales orders. These classifications help scientists understand the evolutionary relationships between different plant species.
