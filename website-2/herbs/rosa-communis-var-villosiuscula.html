What is Rosa communis var. villosiuscula?

Rosa communis var. villosiuscula is a variety of shrub in the family Rosaceae, native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Western Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub, typically growing 1-3 meters tall, with a woody base and a branching system of thorny stems. The leaves are pinnate, with 3-5 pairs of leaflets, and are covered in fine, soft hairs. The flowers are pink, with 5 petals, and are borne in clusters, typically in the summer months.

The fruits are aggregate, consisting of numerous achenes, each containing a single seed.
