What is Urochloa fasciculata var. reticulata?

Urochloa fasciculata var. reticulata is a variety of the grass species Urochloa fasciculata, belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is an annual or perennial grass with a decumbent to erect growth habit, typically reaching 20-120 cm in height. The leaves are linear, flat, and usually 2-10 mm wide, with a distinct midrib and a ciliate ligule. The inflorescence is a dense panicle, typically 2-10 cm long, with spikelets that are 3-4 mm long and contain 2-3 florets.

The spikelets are characterized by their reticulated venation and the presence of a well-developed lemma.
