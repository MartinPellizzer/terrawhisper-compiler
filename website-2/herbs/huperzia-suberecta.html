Huperzia suberecta

Huperzia suberecta, also known as a firm-leaved fir club moss, is a small, evergreen plant belonging to the Huperziaceae family.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include cognitive enhancement, improved memory, and neuroprotective properties.

The bioactive constituents of Huperzia suberecta include huperzine A, a potent inhibitor of acetylcholinesterase, an enzyme that breaks down the neurotransmitter acetylcholine, as well as other alkaloids such as huperzine B and huperzine C. Huperzia suberecta is commonly used in herbal preparations, including capsules, tablets, and extracts, often marketed as a dietary supplement to support brain health and cognitive function.
