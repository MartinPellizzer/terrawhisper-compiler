Clinopodium griseum

Clinopodium griseum, also known as Grayleaf Clinopodium, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which can help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, respiratory problems, and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Clinopodium griseum include essential oils, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant include teas, tinctures, and topical creams, which are often used to soothe digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing.
