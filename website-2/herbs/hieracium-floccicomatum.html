Hieracium floccicomatum

Hieracium floccicomatum, also known as fleecy hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae) and is native to Europe and Western Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Hieracium floccicomatum include flavonoids, sesquiterpenes, and phenolic acids, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.
