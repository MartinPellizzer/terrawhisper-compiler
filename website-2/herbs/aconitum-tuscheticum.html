Aconitum tuscheticum

Aconitum tuscheticum, also known as the Chinese aconite or Tushet aconitum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Ranunculaceae.

The plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries, offering health benefits such as reducing pain and inflammation, as well as exhibiting antimicrobial and anti-fungal properties. The bioactive constituents of Aconitum tuscheticum include aconitine, mesaconitine, and hypaconitine, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Aconitum tuscheticum is often prepared into herbal teas, tinctures, or decoctions, and is commonly used to treat conditions such as arthritis, rheumatism, and sore throats.
