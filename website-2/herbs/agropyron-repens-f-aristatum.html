What is Agropyron repens f. aristatum?

Agropyron repens f. aristatum is a taxon of the species Agropyron repens, a perennial grass, belonging to the family Poaceae.

It exhibits a clonal growth habit, with a rhizomatous stem system, and produces erect, unbranched culms, typically 50-100 cm tall, with a leafy, flat, and keeled ligule. The culm nodes are glabrous, and the leaf blades are flat, linear, and 5-10 mm wide, with a prominent midrib and a glabrous margin. Awns are present on the spikelets, which are arranged in a two-ranked spike, and the glumes are 4-8 mm long, with a keeled lower glume.

The chromosome number is 2n = 28.
