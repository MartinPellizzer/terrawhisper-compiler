Ferula meifolia

Ferula meifolia, commonly known as Ferula, is a perennial plant belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Ferula meifolia include sesquiterpenes, alkaloids, and coumarins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Ferula is often used to make herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and decoctions, which are used to treat a variety of health conditions, including respiratory issues and skin infections.
