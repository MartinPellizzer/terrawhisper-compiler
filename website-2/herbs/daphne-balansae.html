Daphne balansae

Daphne balansae, also known as Tibetan spurge or Himalayan spurge, is a perennial herb belonging to the family Thymelaeaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in Tibetan and Chinese medicine to treat various ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and inflammation, due to its anti-inflammatory, antipyretic, and analgesic properties. The bioactive constituents of Daphne balansae include daphnin, daphnane triterpenoids, and sesquiterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal activities.

Daphne balansae is commonly used in traditional herbal preparations, including teas, infusions, and extracts, which are also used as topical applications for skin conditions and wounds.
