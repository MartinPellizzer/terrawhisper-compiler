Astragalus araucanus

The bioactive constituents of Astragalus araucanus include astragaloside, astragalin, and flavonoids, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.

Astragalus araucanus is often used to make herbal teas, infusions, and extracts, as well as capsules and tablets, which are commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine and other folk remedies.
