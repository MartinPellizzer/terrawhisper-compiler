classification

Dactylorhiza × sooi is a nothospecies of orchid in the family Orchidaceae. It is a hybrid between Dactylorhiza fuchsii and Dactylorhiza maculata. The name 'sooi' comes from the Dutch orchid enthusiast Sooi. As a nothospecies, it is not considered a natural species but rather a man-made hybrid. Dactylorhiza × sooi is native to Europe.
