Mesembryanthemum muticum

Mesembryanthemum muticum, commonly known as the round-leaf iceplant, is a succulent plant species native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

The bioactive constituents of M. muticum include flavonoids, such as kaempferol and quercetin, as well as other compounds like glycosides and saponins.
