Aeonium glandulosum

Aeonium glandulosum, also known as the 'Glandular Aeonium', is a succulent plant species within the Aeonium genus, characterized by its unique, leafy rosettes and small white flowers.

The plant has been found to possess various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may aid in reducing inflammation and protecting against cell damage. Bioactive constituents of Aeonium glandulosum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditional herbal preparations made from Aeonium glandulosum include infusions and decoctions, often used to treat digestive issues, fever, and skin conditions.
