Chrysanthemum bulbosum

Chrysanthemum bulbosum, also known as Yuchi chrysanthemum, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family, commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The plant has been found to possess various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which can help to alleviate symptoms of fever, headache, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Chrysanthemum bulbosum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made from this plant are teas, infusions, and dried flower powders, which are often used to treat fever, cough, and respiratory infections.
