Alchemilla cavillieri

Alchemilla cavillieri is a perennial herbaceous plant, commonly known as Lady's Mantle, belonging to the Rosaceae family.

Traditionally, the plant has been used to aid in wound healing, reduce menstrual cramps and heavy bleeding, and soothe digestive issues due to its anti-inflammatory and astringent properties. The bioactive constituents of Alchemilla cavillieri include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

Alchemilla cavillieri is commonly prepared as a tea, tincture, or dried extract, and can also be used in topical creams and salves to harness its wound-healing and anti-inflammatory properties.
