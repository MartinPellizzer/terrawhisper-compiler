Senecio crispus

Senecio crispus, also known as Ragwort, is a perennial plant native to Europe and Asia, characterized by its yellow flowers and serrated leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Senecio crispus include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its pharmacological activities.
