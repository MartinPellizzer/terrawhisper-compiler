Gilibertia elongata

Gilibertia elongata is a plant species native to New Zealand, belonging to the family Asparagaceae.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant activities, which may help to alleviate symptoms of colds, flu, and other respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of Gilibertia elongata include iridoids, glycosides, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
