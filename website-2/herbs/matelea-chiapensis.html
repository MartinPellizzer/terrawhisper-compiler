Matelea chiapensis

The bioactive constituents of Matelea chiapensis include alkaloids, specifically 8-oxo-3,4-dihydro-4-β-carbomethoxy-3-methoxyphenanthrene, as well as other compounds that have been shown to possess antimicrobial and antioxidant activities.

In traditional medicine, Matelea chiapensis is often prepared as a decoction, tea, or infusion, as well as being used in topical applications, such as ointments and salves.
