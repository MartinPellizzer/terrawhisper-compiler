Papaver racemosum

Papaver racemosum, also known as the racemed poppy or California poppy, is a perennial plant in the poppy family (Papaveraceae) that is native to the western coast of North America.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including relieving anxiety, insomnia, and inflammation, due to its sedative and analgesic properties. The bioactive constituents of Papaver racemosum include alkaloids such as meconic acid and protopine, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
