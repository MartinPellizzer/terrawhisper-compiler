Cynanchica × jordanii

Cynanchica × jordanii, commonly known as Jordan's Cynanchum, is a hybrid plant species within the Apocynaceae family.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of digestive issues and promote overall well-being. Bioactive constituents of Jordan's Cynanchum include diterpenoid alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The plant is commonly used to make herbal teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are used to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and boost the immune system.
