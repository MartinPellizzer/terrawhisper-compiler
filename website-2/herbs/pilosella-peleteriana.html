Pilosella peleteriana

Pilosella peleteriana, commonly known as Alpine Hawkweed or Peleter's Hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

The bioactive constituents of Pilosella peleteriana include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Alpine Hawkweed is often used in herbal medicine to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as in topical preparations, such as creams and ointments, to support skin health and reduce inflammation.
