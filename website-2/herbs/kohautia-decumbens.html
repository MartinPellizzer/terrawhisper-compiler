Kohautia decumbens

Kohautia decumbens, also known as Bush Violet or Kohautia, is a low-growing, spreading shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

Kohautia decumbens contains bioactive constituents like flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
