Helianthus amplexicaulis

Helianthus amplexicaulis, also known as greenthread, is a perennial plant native to North America, belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including the treatment of fever, rheumatism, and gastrointestinal issues, as well as its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Helianthus amplexicaulis include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the plant is often prepared as a decoction or tincture, and is sometimes used in combination with other herbs to treat a range of health conditions.
