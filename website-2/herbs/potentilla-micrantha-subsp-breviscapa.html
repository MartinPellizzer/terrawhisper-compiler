What is Potentilla micrantha subsp. breviscapa?

Potentilla micrantha subsp.

breviscapa is a subspecies of flowering plant that belongs to the family Rosaceae. It is a perennial herb with a short rhizome and hairy stems, 5-20 cm tall.

Leaves are compound, trifoliate, with leaflets 1.5-5 cm long, having 5-7 pairs of lateral veins and a short petiole. Flowers are small, 5-6 mm in diameter, yellow with 5 petals, borne in clusters in the axils of the leaves.

The fruit is an aggregate of achenes, 2-3 mm long, with a short persistent calyx.
