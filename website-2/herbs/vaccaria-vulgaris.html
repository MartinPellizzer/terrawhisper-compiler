Vaccaria vulgaris

Vaccaria vulgaris, also known as cow cockle or vaccaria, is an annual or biennial herbaceous plant in the balsam family (Balsamaceae) that is native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
