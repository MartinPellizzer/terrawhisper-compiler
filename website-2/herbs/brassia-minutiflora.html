Brassia minutiflora

Brassia minutiflora, also known as Small-flowered Brassia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Brassia minutiflora include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
