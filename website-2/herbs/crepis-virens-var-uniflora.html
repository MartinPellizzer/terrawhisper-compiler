What is Crepis virens var. uniflora?

Crepis virens var. uniflora is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its stemless rosette of leaves, which are lanceolate in shape, 10-30 cm long, and 1-4 cm wide, with 3-5 mm long, pointed tips. The leaves are glabrous and have a crenate margin, while the base of the leaf is wedge-shaped. The plant produces a solitary, terminal flower head, 5-10 cm in diameter, with 10-20 yellow ligules and 20-40 yellow tubular florets.

The achenes are 4-5 mm long, lanceolate in shape, and have a glabrous surface with a distinctive beak-shaped apex.
