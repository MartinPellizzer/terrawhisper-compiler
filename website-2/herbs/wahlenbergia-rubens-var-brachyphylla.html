What is Wahlenbergia rubens var. brachyphylla?

Wahlenbergia rubens var. brachyphylla is an infraspecific taxon within the species Wahlenbergia rubens, belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

The calyx is 8-12 mm long and 4-6 mm wide, with 5 lobes. The plant grows up to 30 cm in height and 30 cm in width, and is found in New South Wales, Australia.

The inflorescence is a terminal, solitary corymb.
