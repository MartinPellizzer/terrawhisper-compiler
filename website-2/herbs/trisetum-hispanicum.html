Trisetum hispanicum

Trisetum hispanicum is a perennial grass species, commonly known as Spanish needlegrass or hispanic wheat grass, native to the Iberian Peninsula.

The bioactive constituents of Trisetum hispanicum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
