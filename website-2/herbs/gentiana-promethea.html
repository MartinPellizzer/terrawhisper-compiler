Gentiana promethea

Gentiana promethea, also known as Promethea's gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the gentian family (Gentianaceae) that is native to North America.

The roots of Gentiana promethea have been used in traditional medicine for their potential health benefits, including the stimulation of digestion, relief of cough and sore throat, and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of this plant include gentianine, gentiopicroside, and swertiamarine, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Gentiana promethea is commonly used to make herbal preparations such as tinctures, teas, and capsules, which are marketed for their digestive and anti-inflammatory effects, particularly in the treatment of gastrointestinal issues and sore throats.
