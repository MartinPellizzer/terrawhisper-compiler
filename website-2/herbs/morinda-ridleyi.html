Morinda ridleyi

Morinda ridleyi, also known as the Asian noni or morinda, is a tropical plant species native to the forests of Southeast Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Morinda ridleyi include iridoid glycosides, such as asperuloside and morindin, as well as other compounds like flavonoids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
