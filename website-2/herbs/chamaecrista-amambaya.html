Chamaecrista amambaya

Chamaecrista amambaya, also known as Amambay bean or partridge pea, is a plant species belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Chamaecrista amambaya include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
