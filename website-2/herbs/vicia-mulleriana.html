Vicia mulleriana

Vicia mulleriana, also known as Müller's vetch or Müller's pea, is a perennial legume plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Vicia mulleriana include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
