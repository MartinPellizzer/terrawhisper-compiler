Pastinaca nudicaulis

Pastinaca nudicaulis, also known as wild carrot or bird's nest carrot, is a perennial plant in the carrot family (Apiaceae) native to Europe and Western Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Pastinaca nudicaulis include flavonoids, terpenoids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
