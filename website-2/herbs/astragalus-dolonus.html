Astragalus dolonus

Astragalus membranaceus, also known as Astragalus dolonus, is a perennial plant species belonging to the legume family (Fabaceae).

The health benefits of Astragalus dolonus include its ability to boost the immune system, reduce inflammation, and improve cardiovascular health, making it a popular herbal remedy in traditional Chinese medicine. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, saponins, and polysaccharides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Astragalus dolonus is commonly used to make teas, capsules, and tinctures, with the most common herbal preparations including Astragalus extract or Astragalus tea, which is often used to support the body's natural defenses against illness and disease.
