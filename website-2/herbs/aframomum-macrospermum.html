Aframomum macrospermum

Aframomum macrospermum, also known as African Ginger, is a species of flowering plant in the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) native to tropical Africa.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects that can aid in the treatment of various health issues such as fever, pain, and respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of African Ginger include gingerols, shogaols, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
