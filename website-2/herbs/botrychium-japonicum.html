classification

Botrychium japonicum is classified as a species of fern within the family Ophioglossaceae. This plant is a member of the genus Botrychium, commonly known as the moonworts. The genus Botrychium is part of the larger group of vascular plants known as the euphyllophytes. Botrychium japonicum is native to East Asia, specifically in countries such as China and Japan. It belongs to the phylum Pteridophyta, which includes all vascular plants that reproduce by spores.
