classification

Prosthechea maculosa is classified as a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae. It is a type of epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America. The genus Prosthechea is a subgenus of the larger genus Maxillaria, which is itself a part of the tribe Maxillarieae. This species is further classified as a member of the subtribe Maxillariinae. Prosthechea maculosa is typically found in humid, tropical environments.
