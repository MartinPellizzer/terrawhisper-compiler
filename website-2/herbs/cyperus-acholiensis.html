Cyperus acholiensis

Cyperus acholiensis, also known as Acholi sedge, is a medicinal plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

Traditionally, this plant is used to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and wounds, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Cyperus acholiensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which have been found to contribute to its medicinal properties.
