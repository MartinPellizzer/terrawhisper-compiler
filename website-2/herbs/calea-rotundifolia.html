Calea rotundifolia

Calea rotundifolia, also known as Mexican Dream Herb, is a perennial plant native to Mexico and Central America, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used for its sedative, anxiolytic, and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a valuable remedy for stress relief, insomnia, and anxiety disorders. The bioactive constituents of Calea rotundifolia include flavonoids, sesquiterpenes, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Calea rotundifolia include teas, tinctures, and capsules, as well as traditional remedies such as infusions and decoctions.
