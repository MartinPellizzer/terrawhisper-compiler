Lyonia maritima

Lyonia maritima, also known as Beachdog or Coastal Lyonia, is a shrub-like plant native to the coastal regions of eastern North America.

The bioactive constituents of Lyonia maritima include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and phenolic glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
