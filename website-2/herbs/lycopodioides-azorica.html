classification

Lycopodioides azorica is a species of club moss in the family Lycopodiaceae. It belongs to the order Lycopodiales and the class Lycopodiopsida. The family Lycopodiaceae is part of the division Lycopodiophyta, a group of vascular plants that reproduce via spores. The division Lycopodiophyta is within the kingdom Plantae. Lycopodioides azorica is a unique and endemic species found in the Azores Islands, a UNESCO Biosphere Reserve in the North Atlantic Ocean.
