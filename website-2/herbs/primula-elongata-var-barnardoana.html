What is Primula elongata var. barnardoana?

Primula elongata var. barnardoana is a botanical variety within the species Primula elongata, belonging to the family Primulaceae.

It is characterized by its leaves, which are ovate to lanceolate in shape, measuring 2-5 cm in length and 1-2.5 cm in width, with a crenate margin and a petiole of up to 1.5 cm. The leaves are densely covered with yellowish-brown hairs, particularly on the lower surface. The inflorescence is a short, dense raceme, bearing 2-5 flowers that are pink to purple in color, with a tube of 1-2 cm and a corolla of 1.5-2.5 cm in diameter.

The flowers have 5 lobes and are borne on a pedicel of up to 2.5 cm.
