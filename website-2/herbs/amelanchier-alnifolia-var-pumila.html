What is Amelanchier alnifolia var. pumila?

Amelanchier alnifolia var. pumila is a dwarf shrub that belongs to the family Rosaceae.

It has a compact, rounded growth habit, typically reaching heights of 0.5 to 2 meters, with a spread of 1 to 2 meters. The leaves are lanceolate, 2 to 5 centimeters long, and 1 to 3 centimeters wide, with a serrated margin and a petiole 1 to 2 centimeters long. The flowers are white, 5-petaled, and 1 to 2 centimeters in diameter, produced in late spring in clusters of 2 to 5.

The fruit is a blue, globose pome, 1 to 2 centimeters in diameter, ripening in late summer.
