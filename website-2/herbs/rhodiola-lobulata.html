classification

Rhodiola lobulata is classified as a perennial plant, specifically belonging to the family Crassulaceae. It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in China, Russia, and Mongolia. Rhodiola lobulata is also known as golden root or Arctic rhodiola, and it is often cultivated for its medicinal properties. The plant is a succulent, characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water and help it survive in harsh conditions. Its classification also includes the genus Rhodiola, which consists of several species of flowering plants.
