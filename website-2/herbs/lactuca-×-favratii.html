Lactuca × favratii

Lactuca × favratii, also known as Favrat's lettuce or prickly lettuce, is a hybrid species of the Lactuca genus, characterized by its edible leaves and taproot.

The plant has been utilized for its potential health benefits, including reducing inflammation and promoting relaxation, due to its sedative and antispasmodic properties. The bioactive constituents of Lactuca × favratii include lactucarium, a sesquiterpene lactone responsible for its medicinal properties, as well as flavonoids and alkaloids.

Traditionally, the plant has been used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to treat insomnia, anxiety, and other nervous system disorders.
