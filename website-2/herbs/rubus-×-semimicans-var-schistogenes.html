What is Rubus × semimicans var. schistogenes?

Rubus × semimicans var. schistogenes is a variety of hybrid blackberry that belongs to the family Rosaceae.

It is a thorny shrub with a height range of 2-3 meters, featuring pinnate leaves with 5-7 leaflets, 1-2 cm long and 1-2 cm wide, and white flowers arranged in corymbose inflorescences. The flowers are 2-3 cm in diameter and have 5 petals, while the fruit is an aggregate of several drupes, each with a single seed, and is red or black in color. The stems are brown, woody, and have a diameter of 1-2 cm, while the leaves are green, simple, and alternate on the stem.

This variety of blackberry is native to North America and has been naturalized in other regions.
