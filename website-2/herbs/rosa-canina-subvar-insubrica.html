What is Rosa canina subvar. insubrica?

Rosa canina subvar. insubrica is a subspecies of Rosa canina, a deciduous shrub that belongs to the family Rosaceae.

It is characterized by its prickly stems, elliptical leaves with 3-5 serrated lobes, and pink to red flowers with 5 petals, arranged in clusters. The flowers are followed by red hips, which are edible and rich in vitamins A and C. Rosa canina subvar. insubrica is native to the western Alps, specifically in the region of Insubria.

Its botanical characteristics and distribution indicate that it is a variant of the common rose species Rosa canina.
