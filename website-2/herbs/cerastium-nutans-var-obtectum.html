What is Cerastium nutans var. obtectum?

Cerastium nutans var. obtectum is a plant variety that belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae, a group of flowering plants commonly known as the pink or carnation family.

This variety is a type of chickweed, a low-growing, spreading herb with short stems that are often prostrate or ascending. Leaves of Cerastium nutans var. obtectum are narrow, linear to lance-shaped, and have entire margins; they are typically 1-2 cm long and arranged oppositely on the stem. The plant produces white or pink flowers with five petals, which are clustered in a corymboid inflorescence; each flower has a peduncle that is 1-2 cm long.

The flowers are followed by small capsules that contain numerous seeds, each about 0.5-1 mm in diameter.
