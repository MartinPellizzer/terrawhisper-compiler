Astragalus pycnorrhizus

Astragalus pycnorrhizus, also known as Huangqi, is a type of perennial herb belonging to the legume family (Fabaceae).

It has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine for its adaptogenic and immunomodulatory properties, providing health benefits such as enhancing immune function, reducing inflammation, and improving cardiovascular health. The bioactive constituents of Astragalus pycnorrhizus include flavonoids, polysaccharides, and saponins, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal effects.

Astragalus pycnorrhizus is commonly used in herbal preparations such as capsules, tablets, and teas, as well as in combination with other herbs in traditional Chinese medicine formulas.
