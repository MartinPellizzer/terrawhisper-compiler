Indocalamus sinicus

Indocalamus sinicus, also known as Chinese ladder fern or Chinese ladder bamboo, is a species of bamboo-like plant native to China and Southeast Asia, characterized by its unique climbing habit and edible rhizomes.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Indocalamus sinicus is often used to make herbal teas, infusions, and dried rhizome preparations, which are used to treat a range of health issues, including fever, sore throats, and respiratory problems.
