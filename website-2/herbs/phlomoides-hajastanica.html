Phlomoides hajastanica

Phlomoides hajastanica, also known as Armenian phlomoides, is a type of flowering plant in the mint family that is native to the mountainous regions of Armenia.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, including its ability to reduce inflammation and alleviate respiratory issues due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties.

The health benefits of Phlomoides hajastanica include relief from coughs, colds, and bronchitis, as well as its potential to lower blood pressure and improve cardiovascular health.

Phlomoides hajastanica can be prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is often used in traditional medicine to treat various health conditions.
