Capsicum tomentosum

Capsicum tomentosum, also known as Tomentose Pepper or Hungarian Sweet Paprika, is a perennial plant belonging to the Capsicum genus, native to the Mediterranean region.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may aid in reducing pain and improving cardiovascular health. The bioactive constituents of Capsicum tomentosum include capsaicin, flavonoids, and carotenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
