Berberis duclouxiana

Berberis duclouxiana, also known as Chinese barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

The plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits, including its ability to treat digestive issues, fever, and infections, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Berberis duclouxiana include berberine, a compound with potent antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory activities, as well as other alkaloids such as palmitine and berbamine.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Berberis duclouxiana is commonly prepared as a decoction or used in combination with other herbs to treat a range of health conditions, although it is often used as an ingredient in supplements and herbal teas due to its potential health benefits.
