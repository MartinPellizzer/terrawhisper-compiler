side effects

Philodendron lingulatum, like other Philodendron species, can be toxic to humans and pets if ingested. The plant contains calcium oxalate crystals, which can cause oral and gastrointestinal irritation, and potentially lead to more severe symptoms if a large amount is consumed. If ingested, individuals may experience symptoms such as drooling, vomiting, and difficulty swallowing. In severe cases, the plant's sap can also cause skin irritation. It is essential to handle the plant with care and keep it out of reach of children and pets.
