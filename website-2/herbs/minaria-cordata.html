The bioactive constituents of Minaria cordata include alkaloids such as bufotenin, cordatine, and cordatoxin, as well as other compounds like flavonoids and phenolic acids.

The most common herbal preparations made with Minaria cordata include decoctions, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of health issues in traditional medicine.
