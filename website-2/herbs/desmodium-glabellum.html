Desmodium glabellum

Desmodium glabellum, commonly known as smooth tick clover, is a perennial legume native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

Bioactive constituents present in Desmodium glabellum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
