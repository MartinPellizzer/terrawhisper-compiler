What is Scaevola linearis subsp. confertifolia?

Scaevola linearis subsp.

confertifolia is a subspecies of flowering plant in the family Goodeniaceae. It is a shrub to 1 meter tall with linear, glabrous leaves to 10 cm long and 1-2 mm wide, and axillary inflorescences with 1-3 blue to purple flowers.

The flowers are 1-1.5 cm long, with a calyx of 5-7 mm long, and a corolla of 10-12 mm long. The fruit is a capsule 6-8 mm long and 4-5 mm wide, with 4-6 seeds per locule. Scaevola linearis subsp.

confertifolia is endemic to Western Australia.
