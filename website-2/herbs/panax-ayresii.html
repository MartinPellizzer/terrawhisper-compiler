Panax ayresii

Panax ayresii, also known as Insam or Korean ginseng, is a perennial plant species belonging to the genus Panax and is native to Korea and China.

The health benefits of Panax ayresii include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and adaptogenic properties, which can help to reduce stress and improve overall well-being. The bioactive constituents of Panax ayresii include ginsenosides, particularly compound K, as well as other triterpenoid saponins and phenolic compounds.

The most common herbal preparations made with Panax ayresii include capsules, tablets, and teas, which are often used in traditional Korean medicine to promote vitality, reduce fatigue, and enhance cognitive function.
