classification

Sorbus edulis is a species of rowan tree, classified within the family Rosaceae. This family includes other popular fruit-bearing trees such as apples and pears. The genus Sorbus comprises several species of rowan trees, which are native to the Northern Hemisphere. Sorbus edulis is a deciduous shrub or small tree that is native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas. It is closely related to other species of Sorbus, including Sorbus aucuparia and Sorbus tianschanica.
