Genista densa

Genista densa, also known as Dyer's broom or Purple broom, is a shrub-like perennial plant in the pea family (Fabaceae).

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, as well as its ability to support the immune system and protect against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Genista densa include flavonoids, such as genistein and kaempferol, as well as other polyphenolic compounds like alkaloids and terpenoids.
