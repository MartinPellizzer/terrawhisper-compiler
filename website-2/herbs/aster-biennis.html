Aster biennis

Aster biennis, also known as tall white aster or wood's aster, is a biennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its tall stem and white or pinkish flowers.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and inflammation, due to its anti-inflammatory and antipyretic properties. The bioactive constituents of Aster biennis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
