Croton haitiensis

Croton haitiensis, also known as Haitian croton or Croton lechleri, is a plant species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, and the Caribbean.

The health benefits of Croton haitiensis include its anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and antioxidant properties, which can be used to treat various conditions such as digestive issues, skin irritations, and respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of this plant include anthraquinones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made from Croton haitiensis include tinctures, capsules, and topical creams, which are used to treat a range of health issues, including wounds, digestive problems, and skin conditions.
