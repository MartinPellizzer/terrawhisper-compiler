Lonicera denudata

Lonicera denudata, also known as the Naked Honeysuckle or Chinese Honeysuckle, is a perennial climbing vine native to China and Japan, known for its fragrant white flowers and edible red berries.

The bioactive constituents of Lonicera denudata include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
