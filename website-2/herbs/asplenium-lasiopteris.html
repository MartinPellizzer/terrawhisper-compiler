classification

Asplenium lasiopteris is classified within the plant family Aspleniaceae. It belongs to the genus Asplenium, which includes various species of ferns. Specifically, A. lasiopteris is a species of deciduous fern native to Europe and Western Asia. The classification of A. lasiopteris is further divided into the kingdom Plantae and the phylum Pteridophyta. This classification reflects its unique characteristics and evolutionary relationships within the plant kingdom.
