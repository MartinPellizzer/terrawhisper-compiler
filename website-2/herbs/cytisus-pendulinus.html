Cytisus pendulinus

Cytisus pendulinus, also known as pendulous broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula and other parts of southern Europe.

Bioactive constituents of Cytisus pendulinus include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
