What is Poa macrocalyx var. scabriflora?

Poa macrocalyx var. scabriflora is a taxon of the Poaceae family, characterized by its annual habit, 5-15 cm tall culms, and linear-lanceolate leaves 2-6 cm long.

It has a dense panicle, 3-6 cm long, with spikelets bearing 2-3 florets. The glumes are equal, 4-7 mm long, and the lemmas are 3-5 mm long, with a scabrous surface. The anthers are 1-2 mm long, and the caryopsis is 2-3 mm long.

This taxon is a variant of Poa macrocalyx, a species native to the high-altitude areas of South America.
