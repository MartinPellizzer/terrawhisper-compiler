Matelea bicolor

Matelea bicolor, also known as the "Two-colored Swallow Wort," is a perennial plant species in the Apocynaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The bioactive constituents of Matelea bicolor include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
