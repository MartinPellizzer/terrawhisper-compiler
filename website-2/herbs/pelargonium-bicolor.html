classification

Pelargonium bicolor belongs to the family Geraniaceae, commonly known as the geranium family. This classification is due to the plant's shared characteristics with other members of the family, such as its five-petaled flowers and palmate leaves. The genus Pelargonium is further divided into several subgroups, with Pelargonium bicolor being one of the many species within this group. The species is native to South Africa, where it can be found growing in a variety of habitats. As a member of the Geraniaceae family, Pelargonium bicolor shares its classification with other ornamental plants popular for their flowers and foliage.
