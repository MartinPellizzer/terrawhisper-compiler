Aloe atrovirens

Aloe atrovirens, also known as the green aloe, is a species of succulent plant belonging to the Aloe genus, characterized by its thick, green leaves with reddish or yellowish hues.

The health benefits of Aloe atrovirens include its potential to treat skin irritations, wounds, and burns, as well as its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Aloe atrovirens include aloin, aloe-emodin, and aloin-A, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Aloe atrovirens is often used to make herbal preparations such as gel and juice, which are commonly used to soothe skin irritations and promote wound healing.
