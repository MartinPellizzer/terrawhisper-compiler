Sauropus albiflorus subsp. microcladus

Sauropus albiflorus subsp.

microcladus, also known as the "Five-Pointed Star Cactus," is a plant species that belongs to the family Phyllanthaceae.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, particularly for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The health benefits of this plant include the potential treatment of various conditions such as fever, rheumatism, and respiratory issues.

The plant can be prepared into herbal remedies like infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are often used in folk medicine to promote overall well-being.
