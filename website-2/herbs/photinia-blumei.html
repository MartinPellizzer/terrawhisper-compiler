Photinia blumei

Photinia blumei, also known as Red Robin or Christmas Berry, is a flowering evergreen shrub native to Australia and New Zealand.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses including the treatment of fever, rheumatism, and respiratory issues, with benefits attributed to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Photinia blumei include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal effects.
