Cynanchum guianense

Cynanchum guianense, also known as Guianan swallowwort, is a perennial plant species in the Apocynaceae family, characterized by its woody stem and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Cynanchum guianense include glycosides, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, such as guanensine and guanidine, which are believed to contribute to the plant's medicinal effects.
