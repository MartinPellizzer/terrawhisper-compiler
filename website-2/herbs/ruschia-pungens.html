Ruschia pungens

Ruschia pungens, also known as the prickly pear ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its small, pink to white flowers and sharp, thorny leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Ruschia pungens include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
