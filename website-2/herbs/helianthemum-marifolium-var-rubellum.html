What is Helianthemum marifolium var. rubellum?

The plant produces solitary, terminal flowers, 1-2 cm in diameter, with five petals that are usually pink to reddish-purple in color and have a distinctive five-lobed shape.

The flowers are borne in the late spring to early summer, and are often associated with dry, rocky habitats.
