classification

Cynodendron ferrugineum is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae. It is native to the island of Réunion in the Indian Ocean and is a member of the genus Cynodendron. The plant is a shrub or small tree that is typically found in the mountainous regions of Réunion. It belongs to the order Myrtales and the clade Angiosperms. Cynodendron ferrugineum is a part of the larger family of plants known as dicotyledons.
