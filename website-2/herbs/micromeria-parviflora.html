Micromeria parviflora

Micromeria parviflora, commonly known as small-flowered heath, is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Micromeria parviflora include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, particularly borneol and bornyl acetate, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
