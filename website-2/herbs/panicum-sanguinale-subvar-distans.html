What is Panicum sanguinale subvar. distans?

Panicum sanguinale subvar. distans is a variant of Panicum sanguinale, a perennial grass of the family Poaceae.

It has erect, smooth stems that are 30-100 cm tall and 1-3 mm in diameter, with a slightly purple tint. The leaves are linear, 5-15 cm long, 1-5 mm wide, and have a keel or a narrow groove along the midrib. The inflorescence is a panicle that is 5-20 cm long, with spikelets that are 1-2 cm long, containing 2-3 florets.

The spikelets are reddish-purple and have a midrib that is dark purple in color.
