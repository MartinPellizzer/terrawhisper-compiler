Aspalathus linearis

Aspalathus linearis, commonly known as Rooibos, is a shrub-like plant that belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to South Africa.

The health benefits of Rooibos include reducing inflammation, improving cardiovascular health, and providing antioxidant properties, making it a popular herbal remedy for various ailments. The bioactive constituents of Rooibos include aspalathin, a unique flavonoid, as well as other flavonoids, phenolic acids, and ascorbic acid, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Rooibos is typically used to make tea, and it is also available in herbal supplements, capsules, and infusions, often combined with other herbs to enhance its health benefits.
