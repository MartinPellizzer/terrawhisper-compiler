What is Chamaecrista conferta var. machrisiana?

Chamaecrista conferta var. machrisiana is a taxonomic variety of the plant species Chamaecrista conferta within the Fabaceae family.

It is a perennial herb native to South America, characterized by its ovate leaves, 1-2 cm long, with 3-5 pairs of pinnae and 10-20 pairs of leaflets, and its yellow flowers, arranged in axillary racemes, 10-30 cm long. The flowers are zygomorphic, with 5 petals, and contain 10-20 stamens. Chamaecrista conferta var. machrisiana produces 1-2 seed pods, 4-6 cm long, with 1-2 seeds per pod.

This variety is often found in grasslands and open areas, typically growing up to 1 meter in height.
