What is Viscum cuneifolium var. cryptophlebium?

Viscum cuneifolium var. cryptophlebium is a plant variety in the family Santalaceae, native to China.

It is a hemiparasitic shrub with cuneate leaves, 2-4 cm long and 1-2 cm wide, and axillary white flowers that are 2-3 mm in diameter. The leaves are glabrous, lanceolate to elliptic, and with a cuneate base, and are arranged oppositely on the stem. The plant grows up to 1-2 meters in height, and its stems are woody and slightly woody at the base, with a brownish-gray color.

The stems are also branched and twigs have a smooth surface.
