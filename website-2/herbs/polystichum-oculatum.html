Polystichum oculatum

Polystichum oculatum, also known as the spiny wood fern, is a type of perennial fern native to Japan and parts of East Asia.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, making it useful in traditional medicine for treating various ailments. The bioactive constituents of Polystichum oculatum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
