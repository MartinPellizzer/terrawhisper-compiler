classification

Uva-ursi polifolia is a species of flowering plant in the heath family, Ericaceae. It is native to North America and is commonly known as kinnikinnick or bearberry. The plant is an evergreen shrub that typically grows up to 2 feet in height and produces small, white or pink flowers in the spring. Uva-ursi polifolia is also known for its red, waxy berries that are eaten by birds and small mammals. The plant's berries and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their antimicrobial properties.
