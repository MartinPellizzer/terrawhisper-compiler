Aronia arbutifolia

Aronia arbutifolia, also known as the red chokeberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

The plant is rich in health benefits, particularly due to its high antioxidant content, which has been shown to help reduce inflammation and protect against oxidative stress, contributing to improved cardiovascular health and potentially even reducing the risk of certain cancers. The bioactive constituents of Aronia arbutifolia include anthocyanins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Aronia arbutifolia is commonly used in herbal preparations, including juices, supplements, and teas, which are often used to support cardiovascular health and immune function, as well as to reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
