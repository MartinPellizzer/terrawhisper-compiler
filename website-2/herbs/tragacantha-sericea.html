Tragacantha sericea

Tragacantha sericea, also known as Psoralea sericea, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Fabaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Tragacantha sericea include psoralen, isopsoralen, and bergapten, which are responsible for its phototoxic and anticancer properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Tragacantha sericea is commonly prepared as a decoction, tea, or extract, and is used to treat a range of conditions, including skin disorders, arthritis, and respiratory issues.
