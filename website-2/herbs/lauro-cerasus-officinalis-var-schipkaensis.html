What is Lauro-cerasus officinalis var. schipkaensis?

Lauro-cerasus officinalis var. schipkaensis is a deciduous shrub or small tree that belongs to the family Rosaceae.

It has simple, alternate, and elliptical leaves, typically 5-9 cm long and 2-4 cm wide, with entire margins and a pointed apex. The leaves are dark green on the upper surface and pale green on the lower surface, with a glabrous or slightly pubescent surface. The flowers are small, white, and fragrant, arranged in corymbose cymes, and have 5 petals and 5 stamens.

The plant grows 3-6 meters tall and 2-4 meters wide, with a broad, rounded crown and a straight or slightly curved trunk.
