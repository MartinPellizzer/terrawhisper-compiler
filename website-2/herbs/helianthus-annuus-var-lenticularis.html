What is Helianthus annuus var. lenticularis?

Helianthus annuus var. lenticularis is a variant of the sunflower (Helianthus annuus) that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is an annual herb with a height range of 100-150 cm, producing large, daisy-like flower heads with 10-20 ray florets and 100-200 disc florets. The flower heads are 8-12 cm in diameter, with a dark brown or black centre and yellow or brown florets. The leaves are alternate, simple, and lance-shaped, measuring 10-30 cm in length and 2-5 cm in width.

The plant is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
