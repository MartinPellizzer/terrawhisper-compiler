Symphyotrichum pilosum

Symphyotrichum pilosum, also known as the hairy white aster or frost aster, is a perennial plant species belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

The health benefits of Symphyotrichum pilosum include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various ailments such as wounds, fever, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Symphyotrichum pilosum include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and support immune function.
