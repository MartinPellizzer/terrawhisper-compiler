Lobelia luzoniensis

The bioactive constituents of Lobelia luzoniensis include alkaloids, glycosides, and phenolic compounds, including lobeline, lobelanine, and other isquinoline alkaloids.

The most common herbal preparations made from Lobelia luzoniensis include teas, tinctures, and capsules, which are often used to treat respiratory problems and promote relaxation.
