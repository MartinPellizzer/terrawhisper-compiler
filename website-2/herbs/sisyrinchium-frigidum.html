Sisyrinchium frigidum

Sisyrinchium frigidum, commonly known as Blue-eyed Grass, is a perennial flowering plant species in the Iridaceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including inflammation and respiratory issues, due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. The bioactive constituents of Sisyrinchium frigidum include iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Blue-eyed Grass is often prepared as a tincture, infusion, or syrup, and is used to treat coughs, colds, and bronchitis.
