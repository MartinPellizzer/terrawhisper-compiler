Chamaecrista kleinii

Chamaecrista kleinii, also known as Klein's sensitive plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family (Fabaceae) that is native to tropical regions.

The bioactive constituents of Chamaecrista kleinii include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
