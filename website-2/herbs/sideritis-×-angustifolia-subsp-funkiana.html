What is Sideritis × angustifolia subsp. funkiana?

funkiana is a plant species belonging to the family Lamiaceae. It is a perennial herb with a woody base, growing up to 30-50 cm in height, and has linear-lanceolate leaves that are 2-4 cm long and 0.5-1.5 mm wide.

The inflorescence is a terminal spike of small, tubular flowers that are 2-3 mm long and bear a 5-lobed calyx. The flowers are purple and are densely packed on the spike.

This plant is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is characterized by its narrow, pointed leaves and small, purple flowers.
