Phrynium tuberosum

Phrynium tuberosum, also known as the purple gingers or tuberous Phrynium, is a perennial plant species native to Southeast Asia, belonging to the family Marantaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Phrynium tuberosum include alkaloids, phenolic compounds, and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
