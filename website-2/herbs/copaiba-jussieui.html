Copaiba jussieui

Copaiba Jussieui, also known as Copaiba Balsam, is a type of resinous tree native to the Amazon rainforest, belonging to the family Burseraceae.

The health benefits of Copaiba Jussieui include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various health conditions such as skin irritations, wounds, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Copaiba Jussieui include sesquiterpenes, particularly beta-caryophyllene and alpha-copaene, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Copaiba Jussieui include essential oils, tinctures, and topical creams, which are often used to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and alleviate pain.
