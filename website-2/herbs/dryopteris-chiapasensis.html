Dryopteris chiapasensis

Dryopteris chiapasensis, also known as Chiapas shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the Dryopteris genus.

This plant has been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including inflammation, pain, and fever, due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. The bioactive constituents of Dryopteris chiapasensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

The plant is commonly used to make herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat respiratory issues, wounds, and skin conditions.
