Searsia pondoensis

Searsia pondoensis, also known as the Pondo currant or Pondo currant bush, is a shrub-like plant species belonging to the Anacardiaceae family.

Searsia pondoensis is rich in bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
