What is Begonia sutherlandii subsp. latior?

Begonia sutherlandii subsp.

latior is a subspecies of plant in the family Begoniaceae. It has glabrous to sparsely hairy stems, 2-6 cm in diameter, and nodes that are slightly swollen.

Leaves are elliptical to obovate, 10-22 cm long, and 5-10 cm wide, with a chartaceous texture and an acute to obtuse apex. Inflorescences are axillary, with 2-3 bracts, and bear white flowers with a greenish-yellow center.

The plant is endemic to the southwestern coast of Australia, specifically in the southwestern corner of Western Australia.
