What is Eriophyllum lanatum var. arachnoideum?

Eriophyllum lanatum var. arachnoideum is a variety of shrub in the family Asteraceae, native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

This variety exhibits a branched, glandular, and grayish-green shrub form, typically reaching a height of 1-3 meters. The leaves are oblanceolate, 2-5 cm long, and covered with a dense, white, felted indumentum. The flowers are yellow, daisy-like, and arranged in small clusters, with a diameter of 1-2 cm.

The inflorescence is a capitulum, a composite flower head characteristic of the Asteraceae family.
