classification

Lindsaea coriacea is a species of fern in the family Lindsaeaceae. It belongs to the genus Lindsaea, which consists of a group of ferns commonly known as climbing lindsaeas. The family Lindsaeaceae is a part of the division Pteridophyta, which comprises vascular plants that reproduce via spores. Pteridophyta is further classified under the kingdom Plantae.
