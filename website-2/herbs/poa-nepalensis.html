Poa nepalensis

Poa nepalensis, also known as Nepal blue grass or Himalayan blue grass, is a species of grass in the family Poaceae.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression, improve respiratory health, and boost the immune system. The bioactive constituents of Poa nepalensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Poa nepalensis is often prepared as a tea or infused in water to make a decoction, or dried and made into capsules or tablets to support overall health and well-being.
