Aconitum × stoerkianum

The bioactive constituents of Aconitum include alkaloids, such as aconitine, mesaconitine, and hypaconitine, as well as other compounds like sesquiterpenes and phenolic acids.

In herbal medicine, Aconitum is commonly prepared as a tincture, decoction, or powder, often used to treat acute respiratory infections, rheumatic pain, and as a sedative, although its use should only be under the guidance of a qualified healthcare professional.
