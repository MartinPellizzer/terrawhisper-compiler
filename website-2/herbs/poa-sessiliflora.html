Poa sessiliflora

Poa sessiliflora, also known as Blue Fescue Grass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in the treatment of various health conditions such as wounds, skin infections, and respiratory issues. Bioactive constituents present in Poa sessiliflora include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Herbal preparations made from Poa sessiliflora include teas, infusions, and tinctures, often used to promote wound healing, reduce inflammation, and support overall well-being.
