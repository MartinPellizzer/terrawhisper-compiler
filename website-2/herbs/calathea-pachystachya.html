Calathea pachystachya

The Calathea pachystachya, also known as the 'Purpurascens' or 'Purple Calathea', is a species of flowering plant in the family Marantaceae, commonly used for its ornamental value due to its striking purple leaves.

Bioactive constituents of Calathea pachystachya include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
