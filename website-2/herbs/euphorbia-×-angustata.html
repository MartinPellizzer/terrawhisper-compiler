Euphorbia × angustata

Euphorbia × angustata, also known as the Narrow-leaved Spurge or Euphorbia, is a succulent plant species belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its narrow, pointed leaves and bright yellow flowers.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may help to soothe skin irritations, reduce inflammation, and protect against oxidative stress. Bioactive constituents of Euphorbia × angustata include euphorbin, diterpenes, and alkaloids, which have been shown to exhibit medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made from Euphorbia × angustata include creams, ointments, and tinctures used topically to treat skin conditions, wounds, and inflammatory disorders.
