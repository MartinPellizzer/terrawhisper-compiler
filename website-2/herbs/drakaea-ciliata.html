Drakaea ciliata

Drakaea ciliata, commonly known as the hairy Drakaea or the hairy spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Western Australia.

The bioactive constituents of Drakaea ciliata include alkaloids, phenolic compounds, and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
