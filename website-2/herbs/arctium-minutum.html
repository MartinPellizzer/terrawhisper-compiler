Arctium minutum

The bioactive constituents of this plant include polyphenols, flavonoids, and sesquiterpene lactones, such as arctiin and inulin, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Arctium minutum is often prepared as a tincture, tea, or infusion, and is commonly used to treat skin conditions, digestive problems, and as a natural detoxifier.
