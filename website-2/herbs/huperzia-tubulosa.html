Huperzia tubulosa

Huperzia tubulosa is a species of liverwort, a type of non-vascular plant belonging to the moss family.

The bioactive constituents of Huperzia tubulosa include huperzine A and huperzine B, which are known for their ability to inhibit acetylcholinesterase, an enzyme involved in the breakdown of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter in the brain.

Huperzia tubulosa is typically used in herbal preparations such as capsules, tablets, and teas, which are often standardized to contain a specific concentration of huperzine A.
