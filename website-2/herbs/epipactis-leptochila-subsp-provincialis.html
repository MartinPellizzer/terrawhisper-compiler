What is Epipactis leptochila subsp. provincialis?

Epipactis leptochila subsp.

provincialis is a subspecies of orchid in the family Orchidaceae. It is native to Europe, specifically in the provinces of the Italian Alps and the French Alps.

This terrestrial orchid has a short, erect stem, 6-12 cm long, with two to three leaf-like bracts and a raceme of 5-10 flowers. The leaves are ovate and pointed, 3-5 cm long, with a light green color.

The flowers are greenish-brown, 5-7 mm long, with a white lip and a prominent labellum.
