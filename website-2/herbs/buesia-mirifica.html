Buesia mirifica

Buesia mirifica, also known as Thai milk vine or Thai asafoetida, is a plant species native to Southeast Asia, particularly in Thailand.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to prevent age-related diseases such as osteoporosis and menopausal symptoms. Buesia mirifica contains bioactive constituents like daucosterol, bauerenol, and mirificin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Thai medicine, the plant is commonly prepared as a capsule, powder, or extract, often combined with other herbs to create formulations for menopausal support and bone health.
