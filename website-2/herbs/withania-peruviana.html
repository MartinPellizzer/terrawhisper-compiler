Withania peruviana

Withania peruviana, also known as Winter Cherry or Indian Ground Berry, is a shrub-like plant belonging to the Solanaceae family, native to the dry regions of India and Peru.

The plant is valued for its health benefits, including reducing stress and anxiety, improving sleep quality, and exhibiting anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Withania peruviana include withanolides, alkaloids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Withania peruviana include capsules, tablets, and teas, which are often used in Ayurvedic and Unani medicine to promote overall well-being and longevity.
