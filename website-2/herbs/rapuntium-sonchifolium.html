Rapuntium sonchifolium

Rapuntium sonchifolium, also known as Sonchus oleraceus or sow thistle, is a type of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The bioactive constituents of Rapuntium sonchifolium include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
