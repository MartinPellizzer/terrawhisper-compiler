What is Rosa canina f. hirtescens?

Rosa canina f. hirtescens is a variety of dog rose that belongs to the family Rosaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub with thorny branches, growing up to 3 meters in height and 2 meters in width. The leaves are pinnate, consisting of 5-7 leaflets that are 2-5 cm long and have a serrated margin. The flowers are pink to red, 4-6 cm in diameter, and have five petals, while the fruit is a red, hairy hip that contains 5-6 seeds.

The variety hirtescens is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which distinguish it from the typical Rosa canina.
