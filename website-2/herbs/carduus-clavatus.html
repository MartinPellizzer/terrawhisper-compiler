Carduus clavatus

Carduus clavatus, also known as the Narrow-leaved Thistle, is a perennial plant species native to Europe and parts of Asia, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Carduus clavatus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
