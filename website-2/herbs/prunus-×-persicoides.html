Prunus × persicoides

Prunus × persicoides, also known as apricot plum, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Prunus armeniaca (apricot) and Prunus domestica (plum).

The health benefits of Prunus × persicoides include its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, which may help protect against various diseases and promote overall well-being. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Prunus × persicoides is often used to make herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are traditionally used to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of colds and fever.
