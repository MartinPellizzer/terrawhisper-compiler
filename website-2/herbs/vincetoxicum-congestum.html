Vincetoxicum congestum

Vincetoxicum congestum, also known as Chinese toad-flowered milkweed or Chinese swallowwort, is a plant species within the Apocynaceae family, native to China and other parts of Asia.

Vincetoxicum congestum is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, anti-cancer, and cardiovascular-protective properties, which may be attributed to its ability to modulate immune responses and inhibit cell proliferation. The bioactive constituents of Vincetoxicum congestum include vincetoxicines, vincetoxins, and other glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Vincetoxicum congestum is used in herbal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and capsules, often in combination with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects.
