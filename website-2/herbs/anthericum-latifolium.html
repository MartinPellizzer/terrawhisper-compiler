Anthericum latifolium

Anthericum latifolium, commonly known as White Snakeroot or Anthericum, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region, belonging to the family Asparagaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, as well as its ability to treat various conditions such as fever, digestive issues, and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of Anthericum latifolium include alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Anthericum is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into topical creams and ointments to treat skin conditions and wounds.
