Aphragmus involucratus

Aphragmus involucratus, also known as white mustard or charlock, is a flowering plant that belongs to the Brassicaceae family.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and diuretic properties.

The health benefits of Aphragmus involucratus include treating respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, as well as skin conditions like eczema and acne.

In herbal preparations, Aphragmus involucratus is often used as a powder or tincture, and can be consumed as a tea or added to topical creams and ointments.
