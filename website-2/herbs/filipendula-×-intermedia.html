classification

Within the Rosaceae family, Filipendula × intermedia is classified as a member of the subfamily Rosoideae and tribe Rosae. The specific epithet 'intermedia' suggests its intermediate characteristics between its parent species. Filipendula × intermedia is often cultivated in gardens for its showy, pinkish-white flowers.
