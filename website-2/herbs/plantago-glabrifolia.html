Plantago glabrifolia

Plantago glabrifolia, also known as smooth plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant in the plantain family (Plantaginaceae) native to the Himalayan region.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, providing health benefits such as anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects, which can aid in wound healing, reducing fever, and soothing digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Plantago glabrifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant are infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which can be used to treat a range of health conditions, including respiratory issues, skin problems, and gastrointestinal disorders.
