Vicia saxatilis

Vicia saxatilis, also known as Rock Vetch, is a perennial legume species that belongs to the pea family (Fabaceae).

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs, due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to ease congestion and soothe the throat. The bioactive constituents of Rock Vetch include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its health benefits.

Rock Vetch is commonly prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is also used in traditional herbal medicine as a cough syrup and expectorant to help relieve respiratory symptoms.
