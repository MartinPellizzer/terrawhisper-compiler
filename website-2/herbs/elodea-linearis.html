Elodea linearis

Elodea linearis, also known as Canadian waterweed, is a species of aquatic plant that belongs to the family Hydrocharitaceae.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating respiratory issues such as bronchitis, asthma, and coughs, due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory effects. The bioactive constituents of Elodea linearis include flavonoids, terpenoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Elodea linearis include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to aid in the relief of respiratory symptoms and to support immune function.
