Cassia herpetica

Cassia herpetica, also known as Kutaj, is a plant species belonging to the legume family, traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine for its medicinal properties.

The plant is valued for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties, which contribute to its health benefits, including wound healing, digestive issues, and fever reduction. The bioactive constituents of Cassia herpetica include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, such as kutajone, which have been found to exhibit significant pharmacological activities.

Cassia herpetica is commonly used in herbal preparations, including decoctions, infusions, and powders, which are used to treat various health conditions, including fever, digestive issues, and skin problems.
