What is Prunus avium var. sylvestris?

Prunus avium var. sylvestris is a deciduous shrub or small tree, belonging to the family Rosaceae, native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa.

It has a broad, spreading crown, with a height range of 3-15 meters, and a diameter of 10-30 cm. The leaves are alternate, simple, and serrated, measuring 5-12 cm in length, with a petiole of 1-2 cm, and a stipule of 2-3 mm. The flowers are white or pink, fragrant, and 1-2 cm in diameter, with 5 petals and 20-30 stamens.

The fruit is a drupe, 1-2 cm in diameter, with a single seed and a red or purple skin.
