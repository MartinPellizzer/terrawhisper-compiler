Aspalathus grandiflorus

Aspalathus grandiflorus, commonly known as Rooibos, is a unique plant species native to South Africa, belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

The health benefits of Rooibos include its ability to reduce inflammation, improve cardiovascular health, and act as an antioxidant, making it a popular choice for teas and herbal remedies. The bioactive constituents of Rooibos include aspalathin, nothofagin, and quercetin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Rooibos is commonly prepared as a tea, and is often combined with other herbs to create herbal supplements and infusions, including Rooibos-based chais and caffeine-free tea blends.
