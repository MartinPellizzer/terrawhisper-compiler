Dryopteris × tauschii

Dryopteris × tauschii, also known as Tausch's wood fern, is a hybrid species of fern that belongs to the Dryopteris genus.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which may help to alleviate symptoms of conditions such as arthritis and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Dryopteris × tauschii include flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the plant is commonly prepared as a tea or tincture, with some herbalists also using it in salves and creams to support skin health and wound healing.
