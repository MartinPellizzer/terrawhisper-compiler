classification

Scirpus robustus is classified under the family Cyperaceae. It is a member of the genus Scirpus. The species is commonly referred to as the robust bulrush. Its roots and rhizomes are rich in starch and have been used to create a paste that has been applied topically to treat wounds and skin irritations.
