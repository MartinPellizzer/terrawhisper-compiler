classification

Selliguea soridens, also known as the lesser umbrella fern, is a species of fern in the family Polypodiaceae. The family Polypodiaceae is a group of vascular plants that are classified under the division Pteridophyta. Pteridophyta is a division of plants that are non-flowering, vascular plants, and are commonly known as ferns and horsetails. The kingdom to which Pteridophyta belongs is Plantae, which encompasses all vascular plants. The classification of Selliguea soridens can be summarized as: kingdom Plantae, division Pteridophyta, class Polypodiopsida, family Polypodiaceae, genus Selliguea.
