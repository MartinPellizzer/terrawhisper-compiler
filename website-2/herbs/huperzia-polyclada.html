Huperzia polyclada

Huperzia polyclada, also known as Chinese club moss, is a type of lycopod, a group of primitive, non-vascular plants that belong to the Huperziaceae family.

This plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits, including the enhancement of cognitive function, improvement of memory, and neuroprotective effects. The bioactive constituents of Huperzia polyclada include huperzine A, a potent inhibitor of acetylcholinesterase, an enzyme that breaks down the neurotransmitter acetylcholine, which plays a crucial role in memory and learning.

Huperzia polyclada is commonly used in herbal preparations such as capsules, tablets, and extracts, often in combination with other herbs, to support cognitive function and memory.
