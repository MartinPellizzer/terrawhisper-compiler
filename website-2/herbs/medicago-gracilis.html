classification

Medicago gracilis is classified as a species within the genus Medicago, which belongs to the family Fabaceae, commonly known as legumes or pea family. This classification reflects the plant's characteristics, such as its bean-like pods and ability to fix nitrogen from the air. The genus Medicago contains around 80 species, all of which are nitrogen-fixing legumes. As a member of the Fabaceae family, Medicago gracilis is closely related to other legumes, including beans, peas, and lentils. The classification of Medicago gracilis is based on its morphology, anatomy, and genetic characteristics.
