Vetiveria nemoralis

Vetiveria nemoralis, commonly known as Khus or Cuscuta, is a type of grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The bioactive constituents of Vetiveria nemoralis include vetiverol, vetiverone, and other sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Vetiveria nemoralis include essential oils, extracts, and infusions, which are used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and improve overall well-being.
