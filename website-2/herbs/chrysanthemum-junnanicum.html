Chrysanthemum junnanicum

Chrysanthemum junnanicum, also known as Yin Chen Hao, is a type of flowering plant in the daisy family that is native to Asia.

The plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits, including reducing fever, relieving headaches and menstrual cramps, and aiding in the treatment of gastrointestinal disorders. Chrysanthemum junnanicum is rich in bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and polysaccharides, which are thought to contribute to its medicinal properties.
