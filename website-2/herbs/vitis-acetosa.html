Vitis acetosa

Vitis acetosa, also known as the woodbine or wild vine, is a species of grapevine native to Europe and Western Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Vitis acetosa include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and anthocyanins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
