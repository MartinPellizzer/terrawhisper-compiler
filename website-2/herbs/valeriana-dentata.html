Valeriana dentata

Valeriana dentata, also known as sharp-toothed valerian, is a perennial plant species that belongs to the Valerianaceae family.

The plant is valued for its sedative and anxiolytic properties, offering relief from insomnia, anxiety, and restlessness, making it a natural remedy for promoting relaxation and improving sleep quality. The bioactive constituents of Valeriana dentata include valerenic acid, valeranone, and valepotriates, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

Valeriana dentata is commonly used to prepare herbal teas, tinctures, and capsules, with the dried roots being the most commonly used part for medicinal purposes.
