Chrysanthemum modestum

Chrysanthemum modestum, also known as Dried Mugwort or Chrysanthemum, is a perennial herb belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

The plant is rich in various bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which are responsible for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antiviral properties, which may help in reducing fever, alleviating headaches, and soothing respiratory issues. Traditionally, Chrysanthemum modestum has been used to make herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat a range of ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues.

In herbal medicine, Chrysanthemum modestum is commonly used to make Yunnan Paiyao, a traditional Chinese herbal formula, as well as other herbal preparations that aim to promote blood circulation and alleviate symptoms of fever and inflammation.
