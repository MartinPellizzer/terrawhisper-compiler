Picris alpina

Picris alpina, also known as Alpine Picris, is a perennial plant species in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and digestive properties, which may aid in relieving symptoms of digestive disorders and inflammation. The bioactive constituents of Picris alpina include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Picris alpina is often prepared as a tincture, infusion, or decoction, and is used to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues, respiratory problems, and skin conditions.
