What is Indigofera dissitiflora var. major?

Indigofera dissitiflora var. major is a taxonomic variety within the Indigofera dissitiflora species, belonging to the family Fabaceae.

This variety is characterized by its shrub-like habit, reaching heights of 2-3 meters, with a spread of 3-4 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, comprising three leaflets, each 5-10 cm in length, with an elliptical shape and a pointed tip. The inflorescence is a spike-like raceme, bearing 5-10 purple flowers, each 1-2 cm in length, which are hermaphroditic and arranged in a spiral pattern.

The flowers are followed by flat, linear pods, 5-10 cm in length, containing 2-3 seeds.
