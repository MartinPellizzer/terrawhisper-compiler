Astragalus maowensis

Astragalus maowensis is a type of flowering plant in the legume family, commonly referred to as a medicinal herb.

It is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may aid in reducing stress and improving immune function. The bioactive constituents of Astragalus maowensis include saponins, flavonoids, and polysaccharides, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.

Astragalus maowensis is often used to make herbal preparations such as capsules, tablets, and teas, and is also used in combination with other herbs in traditional Chinese medicine to promote overall well-being.
