What is Choisya dumosa var. mollis?

Choisya dumosa var. mollis is an evergreen shrub native to Mexico, belonging to the family Rutaceae.

It has a compact, rounded growth habit, typically reaching 1-2 meters in height and 1-1.5 meters in width. The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and entire, measuring 5-10 cm in length and 2-4 cm in width, with a leathery texture and a pungent aroma. The flowers are white, fragrant, and arranged in terminal panicles, consisting of 4-5 petals and numerous stamens.

The plant produces small, yellow or orange fruit in late summer, which are berry-like and contain 2-3 seeds.
