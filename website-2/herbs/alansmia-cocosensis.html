Alansmia cocosensis

Alansmia cocosensis, also known as Cocos Fern, is a type of epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The bioactive constituents of Alansmia cocosensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
