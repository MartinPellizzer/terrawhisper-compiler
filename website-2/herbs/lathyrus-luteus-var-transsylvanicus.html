What is Lathyrus luteus var. transsylvanicus?

Lathyrus luteus var. transsylvanicus is a legume plant that belongs to the family Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb with erect stems, 50-150 cm in height, and pinnate leaves composed of 5-11 leaflets, 4-12 cm in length. The plant produces yellow, fragrant flowers in axillary racemes, 5-15 cm in length, and yellow, 1.5-2.5 cm long pods, 2-4 seeded. Lathyrus luteus var. transsylvanicus grows in woodland edges and rocky slopes in Eastern Europe.

The plant is native to Romania, specifically Transylvania, from which it gets its varietal name.
