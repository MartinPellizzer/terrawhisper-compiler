Cornicina loeflingii

Cornicina loeflingii, also known as Lofling's corn, is a plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is used for its medicinal properties, which include reducing inflammation and alleviating conditions such as fever, rheumatism, and respiratory issues due to its antioxidant, antimicrobial, and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Cornicina loeflingii include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

Traditionally, the plant is used to prepare herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being incorporated into topical creams and ointments to exploit its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.
