classification

Coelogyne latifolia is classified as a species of orchid in the family Orchidaceae. The genus Coelogyne is a group of terrestrial orchids, and Coelogyne latifolia is one of its species. It belongs to the tribe Coelogyninae and is native to the Himalayas and parts of Asia. The species was first described by the botanist Lindley in 1833. Coelogyne latifolia is further divided into several subspecies, each with distinct characteristics.
