Astragalus solandri

Astragalus solandri, also known as Astragalus, is a type of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine for its adaptogenic and immunomodulatory properties, providing relief from fatigue, improving overall health, and enhancing the body's ability to resist illness. The bioactive constituents of Astragalus solandri include flavonoids, saponins, polysaccharides, and isoflavones, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Astragalus is often prepared as a tea, capsule, or tincture, and is commonly used in herbal preparations such as Astragalus membranaceus extracts, and other formulas to boost energy and immune function.
