Tragacantha chinensis

Tragacantha chinensis, also known as Astragalus membranaceus, is a perennial plant belonging to the Fabaceae family, commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The roots of Tragacantha chinensis are valued for their adaptogenic properties, which are believed to promote immune function, reduce inflammation, and improve cardiovascular health. The bioactive constituents of this plant include saponins, flavonoids, and polysaccharides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Tragacantha chinensis is often used to make herbal decoctions, capsules, and teas, as well as being incorporated into traditional formulas to support overall well-being and prevent disease.
