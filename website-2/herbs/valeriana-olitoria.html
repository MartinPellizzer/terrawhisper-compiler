Valeriana olitoria

Valeriana olitoria, also known as wild valerian, is a perennial herb native to Europe and Asia, belonging to the Valerianaceae family.

The plant is prized for its sedative and calming properties, which have been traditionally used to treat anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness, making it a natural remedy for promoting relaxation and improving sleep quality. The bioactive constituents of Valeriana olitoria include valepotriates, isovaleric acid, and valerenic acid, which are responsible for its anxiolytic and sedative effects.

Valerian root is commonly prepared as a tea, tincture, or capsule, and is often combined with other herbs in sleep-promoting and stress-relieving supplements.
