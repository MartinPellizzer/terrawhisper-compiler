Desmodium × humifusum

Desmodium × humifusum, also known as Humble Indigo or Humble Pea, is a perennial legume native to North America, characterized by its small, pinkish-purple flowers and trifoliate leaves.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been shown to aid in the treatment of wounds, skin conditions, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Desmodium × humifusum include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The plant is often used to make herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are used to support immune function, reduce inflammation, and promote overall well-being.
