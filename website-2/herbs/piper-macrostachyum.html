Piper macrostachyum

Piper macrostachyum, commonly known as Indian long pepper or long pepper, is a perennial climber of the Piperaceae family, native to the tropical and subtropical regions of India.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include piperine, piperylene, and beta-sitosterol, which have been found to possess potent medicinal properties.

Traditional herbal preparations made with Piper macrostachyum include decoctions, infusions, and powder, which are commonly used in Ayurvedic and Unani medicine to treat a range of health conditions.
