Ixora indica

Ixora indica, also known as the Jungle Geranium or Sinhala, is a flowering plant species native to tropical Asia, belonging to the family Rubiaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Ixora indica include flavonoids, glycosides, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Ayurvedic and Unani medicine, Ixora indica is often prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as made into powders and ointments, to harness its medicinal benefits.
