Lobelia digitalifolia

Lobelia digitalifolia, also known as Lobelia, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa, belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma. The bioactive constituents of Lobelia include lobeline, lobelanine, and other alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Lobelia is commonly used in herbal preparations, including teas, tinctures, and extracts, often used to treat respiratory and circulatory issues, and as a natural expectorant to facilitate cough relief.
