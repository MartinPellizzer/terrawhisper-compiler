What is Arabis perstellata var. phalacrocarpa?

Arabis perstellata var. phalacrocarpa is a botanical variety of the species Arabis perstellata, belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

The leaves are glabrous or ciliate, and the inflorescence is a raceme of white or pinkish flowers with 4 petals, 4-6 mm in diameter, and 6 stamens. The flowers are arranged in a spiral pattern on the raceme, which is 2-10 cm long.
