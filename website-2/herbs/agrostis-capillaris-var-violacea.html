What is Agrostis capillaris var. violacea?

Agrostis capillaris var. violacea is a perennial grass that belongs to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender stems, typically 10-60 cm tall, and narrow leaves with a blade length of 1-10 cm and a ligule length of 0.5-1 mm. The inflorescence is a panicle, usually 5-20 cm long, with spikelets bearing two florets, the lower one sterile and the upper one fertile. The plant is native to the British Isles and is found in areas with acidic soils.

Its flowering period typically occurs from June to August, with the plant producing purplish flowers.
