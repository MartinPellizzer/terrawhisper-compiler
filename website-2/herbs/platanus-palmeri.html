Platanus palmeri

Platanus palmeri, also known as Palmer's sycamore or Palmer's plane tree, is a deciduous tree species native to the Baja California Peninsula in Mexico.

The tree's bark, leaves, and roots have been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive problems, due to its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antiseptic properties. The bioactive constituents of Platanus palmeri include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
