Chionochloa crassiuscula

Chionochloa crassiuscula, also known as Karamu, is a native New Zealand grass species that has been used in traditional Maori medicine for centuries.

The health benefits of Chionochloa crassiuscula include its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can aid in wound healing, reducing inflammation, and fighting off infections. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
