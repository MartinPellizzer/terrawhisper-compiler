Carex sciocapensis

Carex sciocapensis, also known as the Japanese sedge, is a type of flowering plant in the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

Traditionally, the plant has been used in folk medicine to treat various health conditions, including respiratory issues and inflammation, due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Carex sciocapensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
