Elaeocarpus griffithii

Elaeocarpus griffithii, also known as the Griffith's Blueberry Ash, is a species of flowering plant in the family Elaeocarpaceae, native to the tropical regions of Asia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include its ability to treat fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial activities. The bioactive constituents of Elaeocarpus griffithii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make various herbal preparations, including teas, infusions, and ointments, which are used to treat a range of health conditions.
