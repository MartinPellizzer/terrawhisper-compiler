Polygonum pangianum

Polygonum pangianum, also known as Chinese knotweed or Hu Zhang, is a type of plant native to China that belongs to the buckwheat family (Polygonaceae).

This plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, sore throats, and rheumatism, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Polygonum pangianum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
