Cynanchica rigidula

The bioactive constituents of Cynanchum rigidum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

Cynanchum rigidum is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into traditional herbal remedies, such as cough syrups and expectorants, to promote respiratory health and alleviate congestion.
