What is Rubus glaucellus var. microtrichus?

Rubus glaucellus var. microtrichus is a taxonomic variety of the Rubus glaucellus species in the genus Rubus, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

This perennial shrub is characterized by its dark green, pinnate leaves with 3-5 leaflets, which are 5-10 cm long and have a glaucous appearance. The plant produces white to pinkish flowers in a corymb, with 5 petals and a prominent cluster of stamens. The fruit is an aggregate of drupelets, similar to other Rubus species, but with a more glaucous appearance due to the presence of microtrichs on the surface.

The leaves and stems of Rubus glaucellus var. microtrichus are covered with short, fine hairs, giving them a characteristic microtrichous appearance.
