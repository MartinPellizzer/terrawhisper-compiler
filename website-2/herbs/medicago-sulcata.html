Medicago sulcata

Medicago sulcata, also known as black medick or hop clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its small yellow flowers and edible seeds.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antioxidant properties, which may help to alleviate digestive issues and boost the immune system. The bioactive constituents of Medicago sulcata include isoflavones, saponins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Medicago sulcata is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, which can be consumed to support overall health and well-being.
