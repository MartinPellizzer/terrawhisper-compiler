Astragalus piutensis

Astragalus piutensis, also known as Piute milkvetch, is a perennial plant species in the legume family (Fabaceae) native to the western United States.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immune-boosting properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of chronic diseases such as arthritis, diabetes, and respiratory infections. Bioactive constituents of Astragalus piutensis include saponins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make herbal preparations such as teas, capsules, and tinctures, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine to promote overall health and well-being.
