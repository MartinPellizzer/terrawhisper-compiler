Agati tomentosa

Agati or Agati tomantosa, also known as the Nile flower or pigeon wings, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Agati tomentosa include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
