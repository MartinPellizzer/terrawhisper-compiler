classification

Astragalus parryanus is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae. It is a member of the genus Astragalus, which comprises over 2,000 species of milkvetch plants. The plant is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Astragalus parryanus is a perennial herb with small, yellow or purple flowers. The classification of Astragalus parryanus is further categorized under the family Fabaceae and the order Fabales.
