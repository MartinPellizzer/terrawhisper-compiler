Huperzia phylicifolia

Huperzia phylicifolia, also known as Chinese club moss, is a species of evergreen shrub in the family Huperziaceae.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including its ability to improve cognitive function and memory due to its purported nootropic and neuroprotective effects. The bioactive constituents of Huperzia phylicifolia include huperzine A, a potent inhibitor of acetylcholinesterase, as well as other alkaloids and flavonoids.

Huperzia phylicifolia is commonly used in herbal preparations, such as capsules, tablets, and extracts, often marketed as a cognitive enhancer and memory aid, particularly for the treatment of conditions like Alzheimer's disease and age-related cognitive decline.
