What is Elatostema monandrum f. subincisum?

Elatostema monandrum f. subincisum is a taxonomic variant of Elatostema monandrum, a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It has heart-shaped to triangular leaves, 2-5 cm long, with 6-10 pairs of lateral veins and a prominent midrib, and is characterized by a sub-incised leaf margin. The leaves are dark green on the upper surface and pale green on the lower surface, with a glabrous upper surface and a pubescent lower surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in axillary cymes, 4-6 mm in diameter, with 4-6 stamens and a 4-parted calyx.

Elatostema monandrum f. subincisum is native to the subtropical regions of Asia.
