Sclerocarpus uniserialis

Sclerocarpus uniserialis is a plant species commonly known as the Cape daisy, a flowering plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

Traditionally, the leaves and flowers of Sclerocarpus uniserialis have been used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including inflammation, fever, and digestive problems, due to its potential anti-inflammatory, antipyretic, and antiseptic properties. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal activities.
