Clinopodium atlanticum

Clinopodium atlanticum, also known as Atlantic clinopodium, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate digestive issues, reduce fever, and protect against infections. The bioactive constituents of Clinopodium atlanticum include essential oils, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds, such as borneol, borneol acetate, and ursolic acid, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Clinopodium atlanticum is commonly used in herbal preparations, including teas, tinctures, and infusions, often in combination with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects.
