Olearia tenuifolia

Olearia tenuifolia, also known as New Zealand flax-leaved daisy, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand and is a member of the Asteraceae family.

Bioactive constituents of Olearia tenuifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
