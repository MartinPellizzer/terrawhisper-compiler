Artemisia sawanensis

Artemisia sawanensis, also known as Japanese wormwood or sawan wormwood, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to exhibit anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and anti-cancer properties, offering potential health benefits in relieving fever, digestive issues, and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of Artemisia sawanensis include sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
