Plantago americana

Plantago americana, also known as American Plantain, is a perennial herb native to North America, belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties, which can help soothe skin irritations, wounds, and respiratory issues such as coughs and colds. The bioactive constituents of Plantago americana include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and mucilages, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

American Plantain is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and salves, which are often utilized to treat skin conditions, respiratory issues, and digestive problems.
