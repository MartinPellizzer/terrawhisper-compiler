What is Rosa glauca var. obovata?

Rosa glauca var. obovata is a botanical variety of the species Rosa glauca, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

This evergreen shrub is characterized by its ovate to obovate leaves, typically 10-20 cm long and 5-10 cm wide, with a glaucous indumentum on the lower surface. The leaves are pinnate with 3-5 leaflets, and the flowers are pink to red, fragrant, and borne in terminal corymbose inflorescences. Each flower has 5-7 petals and a calyx with 5 sepals.

The variety Rosa glauca var. obovata is endemic to the Mediterranean region.
