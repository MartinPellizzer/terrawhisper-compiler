What is Jurinea mollis subsp. moschata?

Jurinea mollis subsp.

moschata is a subspecies of plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its leaves, which are elliptical in shape, 5-10 cm long, and covered with soft, fine hairs.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and have entire margins. The plant produces yellow flowers in a capitulum, which is 2-3 cm in diameter and has 8-12 ray florets and 20-30 disc florets.

The capitulum is surrounded by a ring of phyllaries that are linear, lance-shaped, and 5-10 mm long.
