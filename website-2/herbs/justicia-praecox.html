Justicia praecox

Justicia praecox, also known as 'Praecox Justicia' or 'Tulsi of the south', is a perennial plant species belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine for its numerous health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which help to alleviate respiratory issues, boost the immune system, and protect against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Justicia praecox include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Justicia praecox is commonly prepared as a decoction, tea, or tincture, and is often used to treat coughs, colds, and fever, making it a valuable herb in the treatment of various respiratory disorders.
