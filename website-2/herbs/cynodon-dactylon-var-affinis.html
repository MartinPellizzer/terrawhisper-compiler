What is Cynodon dactylon var. affinis?

Cynodon dactylon var. affinis is a grassy plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial or annual herb with a short stem, typically less than 1 meter in height, and produces narrow, pointed leaves that are 2-10 cm long. The inflorescence is a dense panicle, usually yellow or reddish-brown in color, and produces spikelets that are 2-4 mm long, with two or more florets. Each floret has a single lemma and palea, and the plant reproduces through seeds.

This variety of Cynodon dactylon is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions.

What are the main herbal preparations this herb?

The main herbal preparations of Cynodon dactylon var. affinis are derived from its unique properties, particularly in traditional Ayurvedic and Unani medicine.

Cordyceps tea is a preparation made by infusing the plant in hot water, valued for its adaptogenic and antioxidant properties.
