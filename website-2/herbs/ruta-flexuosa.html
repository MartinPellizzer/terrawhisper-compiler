Ruta flexuosa

Ruta flexuosa, also known as flexible rue or common rue, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its fragrant leaves and yellow flowers.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antispasmodic effects, making it beneficial for treating digestive issues, reducing pain and inflammation, and alleviating respiratory problems. Ruta flexuosa contains a range of bioactive constituents, including volatile oils, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

The plant is often used in herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into topical creams and ointments for skin and wound care.
