What is Dactylorhiza majalis var. purpurella?

Dactylorhiza majalis var. purpurella is a subspecies of orchid that belongs to the family Orchidaceae.

It has a perennial habit, with pseudobulbs and 2-5 narrow, linear to lanceolate leaves, 10-20 cm long. The flowers are typically purple, with a lip that is 3-4 mm long and 5-7 mm wide, and a spur that is 3-4 mm long. The inflorescence is a raceme, 5-15 cm long, bearing 5-15 flowers.

This subspecies is endemic to the British Isles, where it grows in wet, acidic heathland and grassland.
