What is Hypericum aegypticum subsp. webbii?

Hypericum aegypticum subsp.

webbii is a plant species belonging to the family Hypericaceae. It is a perennial herb with woody base, 10-40 cm tall, and a spread of 20-30 cm, bearing sessile leaves that are oval, 2-4 cm long, and 1-2 cm wide.

The leaves are dark green, with a glabrous surface, and have 3-5 pairs of lateral veins. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers with 5 petals, 1-1.5 cm in diameter, in the axils of the leaves, from June to September.

The flowers are followed by 3-4 mm long, dark brown capsules containing 2-3 seeds.
