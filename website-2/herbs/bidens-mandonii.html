Bidens mandonii

Bidens mandonii is a plant species commonly known as Mandon's beggar-ticks or bidens, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, contributing to its potential in treating various health conditions such as wounds, fever, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Bidens mandonii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

In traditional medicine, Bidens mandonii is often used to make herbal preparations such as infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of health conditions, including digestive issues and fever.
