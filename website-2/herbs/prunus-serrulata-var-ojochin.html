What is Prunus serrulata var. ojochin?

Prunus serrulata var. ojochin is a cultivar of Prunus serrulata, a deciduous cherry tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae, a large and diverse group of flowering plants. This plant is characterized by its pink to red flowers, 4-5 petals, and a diameter of approximately 3-4 cm. Prunus serrulata var. ojochin typically grows to a height of 15-20 meters, with a spread of around 10-15 meters, and has a broad, rounded crown with a straight or slightly curved trunk.

The leaves of Prunus serrulata var. ojochin are elliptical in shape, 4-9 cm long, and have a serrated margin.
