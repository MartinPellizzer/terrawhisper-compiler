Panax hainla

Panax hainla, also known as Himalayan ginseng or Chinese ginseng, is a type of plant in the Araliaceae family native to the Himalayan Mountains.

This plant is valued for its adaptogenic properties, which enable it to help the body adapt to physical and mental stress, promoting overall well-being and vitality. The bioactive constituents of Panax hainla include ginsenosides, which have been shown to have anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-cancer properties, as well as other compounds such as polyphenols and saponins.

Panax hainla is commonly prepared as a herbal tea, capsule, or extract, and is often used to enhance physical performance, improve cognitive function, and boost the immune system.
