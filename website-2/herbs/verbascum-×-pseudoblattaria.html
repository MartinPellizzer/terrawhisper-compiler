Verbascum × pseudoblattaria

Verbascum × pseudoblattaria, also known as Blattaria, is a hybrid species of the Verbascum genus, a type of flowering plant in the mullein family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and expectorant effects, making it beneficial for treating respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma. The bioactive constituents of Verbascum × pseudoblattaria include iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, with the dried leaves and flowers being commonly used in herbal preparations to alleviate respiratory issues and promote relaxation.
