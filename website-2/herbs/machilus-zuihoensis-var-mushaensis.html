What is Machilus zuihoensis var. mushaensis?

Machilus zuihoensis var. mushaensis is an endemic plant species in Japan, belonging to the family Lauraceae.

It is a small, evergreen tree or shrub with a height range of 2-5 meters, characterized by dark green, elliptical to ovate leaves, 6-12 centimeters in length, with entire margins and cuneate bases. The leaves are opposite, with a petiole up to 1 centimeter long, and a dark green, glabrous upper surface, and a pale green, pubescent lower surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in axillary racemes, 2-5 centimeters in length, with a 4-6 millimeter long peduncle.

The flowers are bisexual, with 6-9 stamens and 6-8 staminodes, and are pollinated by insects.
