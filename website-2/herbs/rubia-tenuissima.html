Rubia tenuissima

Rubia tenuissima, also known as Himalayan madder or Chinese madder, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include anthraquinones, such as rubiadin and rubic acid, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids.
