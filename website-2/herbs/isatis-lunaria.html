Isatis lunaria

Isatis lunaria, also known as Lunaria or Silverweed, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

The plant is traditionally used for its anti-inflammatory and immune-stimulating properties, making it a valuable remedy for various health conditions, including respiratory infections and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Isatis lunaria include isatin, lunasin, and flavonoids, which have been shown to exhibit potent antimicrobial and antioxidant activities.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Isatis lunaria is often used to prepare herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being used in the production of traditional patent medicines such as Qingfei Baidu and LianQin.
