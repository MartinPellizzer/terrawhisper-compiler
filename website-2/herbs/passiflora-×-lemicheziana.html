Passiflora × lemicheziana

Passiflora × lemicheziana, also known as the passionflower vine, is a hybrid species of flowering plant in the Passifloraceae family.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including its anxiolytic and sedative effects, making it a popular remedy for anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness. The bioactive constituents of Passiflora × lemicheziana include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Herbal preparations made from the plant include teas, tinctures, and capsules, which are often used to promote relaxation and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and insomnia.
