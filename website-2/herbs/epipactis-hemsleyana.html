Epipactis hemsleyana

Epipactis hemsleyana, also known as Hemsley's helleborine, is a species of orchid native to China.

The bioactive constituents of Epipactis hemsleyana include iridoids, phenylethanoid glycosides, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

In traditional Chinese medicine, the plant is often prepared as a tea or infused in water to make a decoction, which is used to treat respiratory and immune system-related disorders.
