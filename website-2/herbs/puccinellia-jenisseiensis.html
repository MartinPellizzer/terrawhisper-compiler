Puccinellia jenisseiensis

Puccinellia jenisseiensis, also known as Jenissei grass or Siberian saltwort, is a perennial plant species belonging to the grass family (Poaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Puccinellia jenisseiensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
