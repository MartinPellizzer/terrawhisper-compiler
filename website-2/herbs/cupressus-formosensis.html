Cupressus formosensis

Cupressus formosensis, also known as Taiwan cypress, is a species of evergreen coniferous tree native to Taiwan.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities, making it beneficial for treating various health issues such as skin problems, respiratory diseases, and infections. The bioactive constituents of Cupressus formosensis include flavonoids, terpenes, and essential oils, particularly cedrol, which is responsible for its therapeutic effects.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make herbal teas, infusions, and essential oil preparations, which are commonly used to relieve respiratory issues, promote wound healing, and reduce stress.
