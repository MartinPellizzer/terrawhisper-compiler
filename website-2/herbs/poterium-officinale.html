Poterium officinale

Poterium officinale, also known as Agrimony, is a perennial herb belonging to the rose family (Rosaceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits, including its antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which are said to aid in wound healing, reduce pain and inflammation, and protect against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Poterium officinale include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Poterium officinale is often used to make teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are used to treat digestive issues, anxiety, and skin problems, among other ailments.
