Carum dichotomum

Carum dichotomum, also known as Bupleurum, is a plant species belonging to the family Apiaceae, commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The health benefits of Carum dichotomum include its ability to enhance liver function, reduce inflammation, and exhibit anti-viral properties, making it a potential treatment for various liver and immune-related disorders. The bioactive constituents of this plant include alkaloids, glycosides, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Carum dichotomum is often prepared as a decoction or made into a tablet, capsule, or powder, and is commonly used in herbal preparations such as Chai Hu Shu Gan San and Jia Wei Bupleurum.
