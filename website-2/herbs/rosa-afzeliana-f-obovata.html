What is Rosa afzeliana f. obovata?

Rosa afzeliana f. obovata is a botanical variant of the species Rosa afzeliana, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a shrub with a woody stem, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, and is characterized by its pinnate leaves, with 3-5 leaflets that are ovate to elliptic in shape and have serrated margins. The leaves are dark green on the upper side and lighter green on the lower side, and have a length of 5-12 centimeters. The flowers of Rosa afzeliana f. obovata are pink to red in color, with 5 petals and a diameter of 5-7 centimeters, and are fragrant, producing nectar and attracting pollinators.

The plant produces red to purple hips, which are edible and rich in vitamins and antioxidants.
