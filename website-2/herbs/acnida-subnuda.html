side effects

Acnida subnuda is known to cause skin irritation, allergic reactions, and contact dermatitis in some individuals due to the presence of urushiol, a potent allergen found in the sap of the plant. This can lead to redness, itching, swelling, and blisters on the affected skin area. In severe cases, exposure to Acnida subnuda can cause anaphylaxis, a life-threatening allergic reaction that requires immediate medical attention. Prolonged exposure to the plant's sap can also cause skin lesions and rashes. It is essential to exercise extreme caution when handling Acnida subnuda or any other plant containing urushiol to avoid these side effects.
