Pelargonium × quinquevulnerum

Pelargonium × quinquevulnerum, commonly known as Five-Leaf Geranium or Scented Geranium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Geraniaceae.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to reduce stress, anxiety, and inflammation, as well as its antimicrobial and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Pelargonium × quinquevulnerum include geraniol, linalool, and geranial, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
