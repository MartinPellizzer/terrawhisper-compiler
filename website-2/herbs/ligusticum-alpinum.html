Ligusticum alpinum

Ligusticum alpinum, also known as Lovage or Mountain Lovage, is a perennial plant species belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae) native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, including its ability to aid in digestion, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antimicrobial and antioxidant activities, making it a popular remedy for various health issues. The bioactive constituents of Ligusticum alpinum include volatile oils, flavonoids, and coumarins, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Lovage include teas, infusions, and dried root extracts, which are often used to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of colds and flu.
