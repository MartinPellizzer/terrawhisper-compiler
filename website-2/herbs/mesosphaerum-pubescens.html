Mesosphaerum pubescens, also known as American hyssop or hairy false pennyroyal, is a perennial herb belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

Traditionally, this plant has been used to treat various health issues, including digestive problems, respiratory issues, and skin conditions, due to its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Mesosphaerum pubescens include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
