Cyperus cayennensis

Cyperus cayennensis, also known as the Cayenne sedge, is a type of sedge plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, coughs, and respiratory issues, due to its expectorant properties and ability to reduce inflammation. The bioactive constituents of Cyperus cayennensis include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
