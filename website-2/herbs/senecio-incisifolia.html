Senecio incisifolia

Senecio incisifolia, also known as California ragwort, is a perennial herb native to the western United States, belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

The bioactive constituents of Senecio incisifolia include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
