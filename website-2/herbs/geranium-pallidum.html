Geranium pallidum

Geranium pallidum is a perennial plant species belonging to the Geraniaceae family, native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to support the body's natural hormonal balance, reduce stress and anxiety, and promote skin health. The bioactive constituents of Geranium pallidum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
