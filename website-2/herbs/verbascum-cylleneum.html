Verbascum cylleneum

Verbascum cylleneum, also known as Cretan Mullein, is a biennial herbaceous plant in the Scrophulariaceae family, characterized by its tall stature and white, yellow, or purple flowers.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may aid in treating respiratory issues, wounds, and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Verbascum cylleneum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Verbascum cylleneum is often prepared as a tincture, infusion, or topical cream, and is used to treat coughs, sore throats, and skin irritations.
