Clerodendrum andamanense

Clerodendrum andamanense is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae, commonly known as the Andaman glory bower, native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

The bioactive constituents of Clerodendrum andamanense include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
