Leptocarpus membranaceus

Leptocarpus membranaceus, also known as Swamp Spike or Spike Rush, is a perennial sedge that belongs to the family Restionaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in various cultures for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, which can help alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma. The bioactive constituents of Leptocarpus membranaceus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
