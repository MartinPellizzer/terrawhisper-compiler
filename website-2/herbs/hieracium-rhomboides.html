Hieracium rhomboides

Hieracium rhomboides, also known as rhomboid hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including fever reduction, digestive problems, and skin irritations, due to its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. The bioactive constituents of Hieracium rhomboides include flavonoids, such as kaempferol and quercetin, as well as sesquiterpenes and triterpenes.
