Cathartolinum scabrellum

Cathartolinum scabrellum, also known as scabrous cathartic, is a plant species with medicinal properties.

It is characterized by its bitter and astringent properties, which make it effective in treating digestive issues such as diarrhea and nausea.

The plant's medicinal properties include its ability to stimulate bowel movements, reduce inflammation, and ease digestive discomfort, resulting in health benefits such as improved digestion, reduced risk of infections, and alleviation of symptoms associated with gastrointestinal disorders.

Cathartolinum scabrellum can be prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which can be consumed to leverage its medicinal properties and promote overall well-being.
