classification

Magnolia lucida is classified as a species of flowering plant in the family Magnoliaceae, native to China. It belongs to the genus Magnolia, which includes over 210 species of evergreen and deciduous trees and shrubs. The Magnolia lucida is further classified as a part of the subgenus Magnolia, section Magnolia, and subsection Magnolia. This classification is based on the plant's characteristics, such as its leaf shape, flower structure, and growth habits. As a member of the Magnoliaceae family, Magnolia lucida shares similarities with other magnolia species in terms of its unique flower morphology and seed dispersal mechanisms.
