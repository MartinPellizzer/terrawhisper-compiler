Ranunculus spectabilis

Ranunculus spectabilis, also known as the crested buttercup, is a perennial plant species in the buttercup family (Ranunculaceae) characterized by its distinctive, crested sepals and bright yellow flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Ranunculus spectabilis include alkaloids, saponins, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Ranunculus spectabilis is often prepared as a tincture, decoction, or ointment to harness its therapeutic benefits, and is commonly used to treat skin conditions and minor injuries.
