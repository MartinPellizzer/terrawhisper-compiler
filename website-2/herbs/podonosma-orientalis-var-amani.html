What is Podonosma orientalis var. amani?

Podonosma orientalis var. amani is a botanical variety within the species Podonosma orientalis, belonging to the family Boraginaceae.

This variety is characterized by its shrub-like habit, growing up to 30-40 cm in height, with a woody base and herbaceous branches. The leaves are small, elliptical in shape, and measure 1-2 cm in length, with a green color on the upper surface and a lighter green or whitish color on the lower surface. The inflorescence is a corymb, composed of small, tubular flowers with a blue or purple color, arranged in clusters.

The flowers measure 2-3 mm in length and are surrounded by a calyx with five lobes.
