What is Linaria saxatilis var. rubella?

Linaria saxatilis var. rubella is a plant variety within the genus Linaria, belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is a perennial herb, typically growing up to 20-30 cm in height, with a woody base and linear, lance-shaped leaves that are 2-6 cm long. The plant produces small, tubular flowers with a reddish-brown color, typically in shades of reddish-pink to reddish-brown, often with yellow markings, and are densely packed in a spike-like inflorescence. The flowers are 10-15 mm long and have a short, 5-7 mm long calyx.

The plant typically blooms from June to September in the Mediterranean region.
