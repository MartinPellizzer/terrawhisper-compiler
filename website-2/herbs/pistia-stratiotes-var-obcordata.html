What is Pistia stratiotes var. obcordata?

Pistia stratiotes var. obcordata is an aquatic plant that belongs to the family Araceae.

It is characterized by its cordate-obovate leaves, which are typically 10-15 cm in length and 5-8 cm in width, with a membranous petiole and a sheathing base. The plant has a submersed or emergent growth habit, with the leaves often floating on the surface of the water. Pistia stratiotes var. obcordata exhibits a sympodial growth pattern, with each node producing two or more new stems.

The plant's flowers are borne on a spadix, which is surrounded by a white or yellowish spathe.
