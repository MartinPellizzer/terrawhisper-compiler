Koeleria macrantha

Koeleria macrantha, also known as June grass or prairie junegrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and Asia, characterized by its tall stems and panicles of purple flowers.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, which may help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of Koeleria macrantha include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

The plant is commonly used in herbal preparations, including infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat respiratory issues and digestive problems, as well as to boost the immune system.
