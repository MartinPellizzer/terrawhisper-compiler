Gentiana pedicellata

Gentiana pedicellata, also known as Himalayan gentian or small gentian, is a perennial plant species in the Gentianaceae family, native to the Himalayas and mountainous regions of Asia.

The health benefits of Gentiana pedicellata include its use as a traditional remedy for digestive issues, inflammation, and as an antioxidant to boost immune function. The bioactive constituents of this plant include gentiopicroside, loganic acid, and swertiamarin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Gentiana pedicellata is commonly prepared as a decoction or tincture, and is used to treat various health conditions, including indigestion, fever, and respiratory issues.
