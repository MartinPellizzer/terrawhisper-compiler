What is Scutellaria laeteviolacea var. maekawae?

Scutellaria laeteviolacea var. maekawae is a variety of Scutellaria laeteviolacea, a plant species within the family Lamiaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a taproot, and has a caespitose habit, meaning it grows in clumps. The leaves are opposite, decussate, and subovate to elliptic in shape, measuring 2-3 cm in length, with an acute to subacute apex and a cuneate base. The inflorescence is a verticillaster, bearing whorled flowers that are blue or white in color and tubular in shape, with a 2-lipped corolla.

The plant is endemic to Japan, specifically on the Ryukyu Islands and Shikoku.
