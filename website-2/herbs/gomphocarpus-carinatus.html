Gomphocarpus carinatus

Gomphocarpus carinatus, also known as the hairy balon or balloon cottonbush, is a plant species in the milkweed family (Asclepiadaceae) that is native to the tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

The bioactive constituents of Gomphocarpus carinatus include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are thought to contribute to its medicinal properties.
