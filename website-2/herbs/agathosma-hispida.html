The bioactive constituents of Agathosma hispida include essential oils, particularly borneol and borneol acetate, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make tea, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat digestive issues and promote overall well-being.
