Grammitis linearifolia

Grammitis linearifolia, also known as the linear-leaved goldback fern, is a species of epiphytic fern that belongs to the family Polypodiaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Grammitis linearifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and polysaccharides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
