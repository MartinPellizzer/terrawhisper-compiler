classification

Cyperus cyperinus is classified as a species of sedge, belonging to the family Cyperaceae. This family is commonly known as the sedge family, which consists of about 5,000 species. Cyperaceae is a part of the monocot order Poales. The Poales order is a group of flowering plants that also includes grasses, rushes, and other sedges. Cyperus cyperinus is further classified as a member of the genus Cyperus, which comprises around 700 species.
