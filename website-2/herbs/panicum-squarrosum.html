classification

Panicum squarrosum is classified in the kingdom Plantae, further categorized within the family Poaceae. It belongs to the genus Panicum and the species P. squarrosum. This classification is based on its botanical characteristics, including its morphology and anatomy. Panicum squarrosum is also known as the rough panicgrass or square-stemmed panicgrass, further emphasizing its unique features. As a member of the Poaceae family, it shares characteristics with other grasses in the same family.
