Vernonia lewisii

Vernonia lewisii, also known as Lewis's ironweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

The bioactive constituents of Vernonia lewisii include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
