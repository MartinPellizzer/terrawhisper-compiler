Rhodiola tibetica

The bioactive constituents of Rhodiola tibetica include rosavin, rosmarinic acid, and salidroside, which have been shown to possess antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and neuroprotective properties.

Rhodiola tibetica is commonly prepared as a dietary supplement in the form of capsules, tablets, or extracts, as well as in traditional Tibetan medicine, where it is used to make teas and infusions.
