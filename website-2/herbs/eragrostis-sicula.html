Eragrostis sicula

Eragrostis sicula, also known as Sicilian love grass or sweet grass, is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Poaceae.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, such as diarrhea and gastritis. The bioactive constituents of Eragrostis sicula include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat various ailments, including gastrointestinal problems and stress-related disorders.
