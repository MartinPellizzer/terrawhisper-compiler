Bidens asperata

Bidens asperata, also known as rough biddens or rough beggarticks, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Bidens genus.

Bidens asperata is rich in bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
