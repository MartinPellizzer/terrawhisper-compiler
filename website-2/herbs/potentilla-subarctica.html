Potentilla subarctica

Potentilla subarctica, also known as Arctic Cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its yellow flowers and a sprawling habit.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce inflammation, boost the immune system, and protect against oxidative stress. Bioactive constituents of Potentilla subarctica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the plant is often used in teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues, respiratory problems, and skin conditions.
