Ficus catesbaei

Ficus catesbaei, also known as the small-leafed fig, is a deciduous tree native to the southeastern United States.

The bioactive constituents of Ficus catesbaei include triterpenoid saponins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
