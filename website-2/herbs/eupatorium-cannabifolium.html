Eupatorium cannabifolium

Eupatorium cannabifolium, also known as Hempweed or Joe-Pye weed, is a perennial plant in the Asteraceae family that grows in damp environments and is native to Eastern North America.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antimicrobial properties that may aid in the treatment of various conditions such as wounds, fever, and gastrointestinal issues. The bioactive constituents of Eupatorium cannabifolium include terpenoids, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Eupatorium cannabifolium include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to support immune function, reduce inflammation, and promote overall well-being.
