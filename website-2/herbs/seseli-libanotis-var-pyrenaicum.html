What is Seseli libanotis var. pyrenaicum?

Seseli libanotis var. pyrenaicum is a botanical variety within the species Seseli libanotis, belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is an herbaceous perennial plant with a height of 10-60 cm and a spread of 10-30 cm. The leaves are pinnate, with 5-9 leaflets, 1-2 cm long, and have a distinctive bitter and aromatic taste. The inflorescence is a compound umbel, bearing small white flowers with 5 petals, and is 2-3 cm in diameter.

The fruit is a schizocarp, consisting of 2 mericarps, 3-4 mm long, and containing 2-3 seeds each.
