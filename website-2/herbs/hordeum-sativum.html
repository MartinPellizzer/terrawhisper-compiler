classification

Hordeum sativum is a type of grass that belongs to the family Poaceae. It is classified as a monocotyledonous plant, which means it has one seed leaf during germination. Hordeum sativum further falls under the genus Hordeum and the tribe Hordeae within the family Poaceae. The species name Hordeum sativum is often used to refer to domesticated barley. It is closely related to other species of Hordeum, which are also known as barley.
