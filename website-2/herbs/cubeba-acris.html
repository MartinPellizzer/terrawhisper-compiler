Cubeba acris

Cubeba acris, also known as Chinese cubeb, is a species of Piper plant, a type of flowering vine native to Asia.

The plant is valued for its numerous health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can help to alleviate symptoms of colds, flu, and other respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of Cubeba acris include piperine, cubebin, and β-sitosterol, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Cubeba acris is often used to make herbal preparations such as tea, tinctures, and capsules, which are commonly used to relieve coughs, congestion, and other respiratory issues.
