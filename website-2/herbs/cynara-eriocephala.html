Cynara eriocephala

Cynara eriocephala, also known as prickly artichoke, is a perennial thistle-like plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The health benefits of Cynara eriocephala include its ability to stimulate digestion, protect against oxidative stress, and support liver health, making it a popular ingredient in natural remedies. The bioactive constituents of this plant include cynarin, a sesquiterpene lactone responsible for its medicinal properties, as well as other compounds such as flavonoids and saponins.

Cynara eriocephala is commonly used in herbal preparations, including capsules, tea, and tinctures, often marketed as a dietary supplement to support digestive health and liver function.
