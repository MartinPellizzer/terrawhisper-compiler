Diplazium vastum

Diplazium vastum, also known as Indian fern or vast Indian rock fern, is a species of fern in the family Athyriaceae, native to the Himalayas and parts of Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Diplazium vastum include flavonoids, triterpenoids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Diplazium vastum is commonly prepared as an infusion or decoction, and is also used in the form of syrup, tincture, and powder for the treatment of various ailments.
