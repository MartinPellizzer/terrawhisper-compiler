What is Scorzonera villosa subsp. columnae?

Scorzonera villosa subsp.

columnae is a subspecies of plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its perennial growth habit, glabrous stems, and leaves that are pinnatipartite with 3-9 linear lobes, measuring 10-20 cm in length.

The inflorescence is a capitulum with 30-60 florets, yellow in color, and surrounded by a involucre of 10-12 linear bracts. The plant grows up to 50-60 cm in height and produces a single umbel of 20-30 flowers at the apex of the stem.

The florets are hermaphroditic and contain both male and female reproductive organs.
