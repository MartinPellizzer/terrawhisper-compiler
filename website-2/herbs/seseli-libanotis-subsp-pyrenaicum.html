What is Seseli libanotis subsp. pyrenaicum?

Seseli libanotis subsp.

pyrenaicum is a plant species that belongs to the family Apiaceae. It is a perennial herb with a height of 40-100 cm, bearing pinnate leaves with 3-5 pairs of leaflets and a terminal leaflet.

The inflorescence is a compound umbel with 10-20 rays, containing small white flowers with 5-6 mm corolla. The fruits are schizocarps, 4-5 mm long, with 5-7 ridges. Seseli libanotis subsp.

pyrenaicum is found in the Iberian Peninsula and the Pyrenees, typically growing in dry rocky areas and scrublands.
