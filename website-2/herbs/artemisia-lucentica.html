Artemisia lucentica

Artemisia lucentica, also known as Silver Mugwort, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and white or yellow flowers.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, as well as its potential to aid in digestion and relieve symptoms of menopause. The bioactive constituents of Artemisia lucentica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

In herbal medicine, Artemisia lucentica is commonly prepared in teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being used in topical applications to soothe skin irritations and promote wound healing.
