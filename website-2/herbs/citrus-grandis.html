Citrus grandis

Citrus grandis, also known as the pomelo or Chinese grapefruit, is a species of citrus fruit native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

classification

Citrus grandis is a species of citrus commonly known as the pomelo or Chinese grapefruit. It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is native to Southeast Asia. The classification of Citrus grandis is as follows: Kingdom: Plantae, Clade: Angiosperms, Clade: Eudicots, Clade: Rosids, Order: Sapindales, Family: Rutaceae, Genus: Citrus, Species: C. grandis. This species is closely related to other citrus fruits, such as the grapefruit and the orange. Citrus grandis is often considered a primitive citrus species due to its unique characteristics and simple fruit structure.
