Tylophora wallichii

Tylophora wallichii, also known as Indian ipecac or tylophorine, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antitumor properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various ailments such as fever, cough, and skin infections. The bioactive constituents of Tylophora wallichii include alkaloids like tylophorine, tylophorinone, and tylophorinine, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Ayurvedic medicine, Tylophora wallichii is commonly used to prepare herbal decoctions, infusions, and extracts, as well as being incorporated into various formulations such as tablets and capsules.
