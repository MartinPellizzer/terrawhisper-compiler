Huperzia mooreana

Huperzia mooreana, also known as Huperzia serrata or Qiang Huo in traditional Chinese medicine, is a type of club moss, a small, evergreen, perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

The bioactive constituents of Huperzia mooreana include huperzine A, a potent inhibitor of acetylcholinesterase, an enzyme involved in the breakdown of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter essential for memory and cognitive function.
