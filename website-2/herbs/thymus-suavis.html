classification

Thymus suavis is a species of flowering plant that belongs to the family Lamiaceae. It is a member of the genus Thymus, which comprises many other species of thyme. The Lamiaceae family is a large and diverse group of plants commonly known as mint or labiate plants. These plants are characterized by their distinctive flowers, often with a bilateral or radial symmetry. Thymus suavis is a perennial herb with fragrant leaves and flowers, commonly found in dry environments.
