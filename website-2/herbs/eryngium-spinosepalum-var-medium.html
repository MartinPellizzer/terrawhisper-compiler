What is Eryngium spinosepalum var. medium?

Eryngium spinosepalum var. medium is an annual or biennial plant within the Apiaceae family.

It has a taproot and a branched stem, typically growing 30-60 cm tall. Leaves are pinnate, consisting of 1-2 pairs of leaflets, with a linear or lanceolate shape and a spiny margin. Inflorescences are compound umbels, bearing 5-10 radially arranged flowers with 5-merous corollas.

Flowers are blue, with a diameter of 1-2 mm.
