Fritillaria micrantha

Fritillaria micrantha, commonly known as small-flowered fritillaria, is a perennial plant species belonging to the lily family (Liliaceae).

The plant has been traditionally used in medicine for its health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties, helping to relieve coughs and respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma. The bioactive constituents of Fritillaria micrantha include alkaloids, such as veratridine and veratrine, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Fritillaria micrantha is often used to prepare herbal teas, tinctures, and pills, and is also used in combination with other herbs to make expectorant syrups and cough medicines.
