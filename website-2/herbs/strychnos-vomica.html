side effects

Strychnos vomica seeds contain a highly toxic compound called strychnine, which can cause severe side effects. The toxicity of the seeds makes them extremely hazardous to humans and animals if ingested. If mishandled, even small amounts of the seeds can lead to fatal consequences.
