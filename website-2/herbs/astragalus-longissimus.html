Astragalus longissimus

Astragalus longissimus is a plant species within the legume family, commonly known as a type of Astragalus or Milk Vetch, which is traditionally used in Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties.

The bioactive constituents of Astragalus longissimus include flavonoids, saponins, and polysaccharides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
