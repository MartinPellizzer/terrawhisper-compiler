classification

Micranthes virginiensis is classified within the family Saxifragaceae. This family of flowering plants is commonly known as the saxifrage or stonecrop family. The genus Micranthes is a group of small, herbaceous plants native to the Northern Hemisphere. Micranthes virginiensis, specifically, is native to eastern North America. It is a perennial plant that grows in a variety of habitats, including rocky outcroppings and moist woodlands.
