What is Heliosperma veselskyi subsp. glutinosa?

Heliosperma veselskyi subsp.

glutinosa is a plant taxon belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae. It is characterized by its succulent, glabrous or sparsely tomentose leaves, and glabrous or sparsely hairy stems, which are green to reddish-green in color.

The leaves are lanceolate to elliptic, measuring 1-3.5 cm in length, and have entire margins. The inflorescence is a corymb, bearing 4-8 flowers, each with 5 pinkish-red petals, 5 sepals, and 10 stamens.

The fruits are 5-6 mm long, elliptic to ovate, and glabrous or sparsely hairy.
