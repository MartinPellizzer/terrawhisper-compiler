Pelargonium oxaloides

Pelargonium oxaloides, also known as the bitter geranium or red-tinged pelargonium, is a succulent perennial plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects that can help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, menstrual cramps, and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Pelargonium oxaloides include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its health benefits.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant are teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to support digestive health and relieve menstrual discomfort.
