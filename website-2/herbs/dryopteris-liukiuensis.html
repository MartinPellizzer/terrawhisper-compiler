classification

Dryopteris liukiuensis is a species of fern in the family Dryopteridaceae. It is native to the Ryukyu Islands, Japan. This species of fern is classified in the genus Dryopteris, which includes approximately 250 species of wood ferns. Dryopteris liukiuensis is further classified into the subgenus Dryopteris and the section Dryopteris. The species is characterized by its lacy fronds and delicate growth habit.
