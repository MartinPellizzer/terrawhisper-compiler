Pulmonaria × ovalifolia

Pulmonaria × ovalifolia, also known as Lungwort, is a hybrid perennial plant species in the borage family, characterized by its distinctive spotted foliage.

The plant has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, helping to alleviate respiratory issues such as bronchitis and coughs. The bioactive constituents of Pulmonaria × ovalifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Lungwort include teas, tinctures, and capsules, often used to support respiratory health and immune function.
