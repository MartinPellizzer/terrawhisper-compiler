Pecteilis euryloba

Pecteilis euryloba, also known as the "Chinese orchid", is a species of orchid native to China and Southeast Asia, known for its medicinal properties and unique appearance.

The plant has been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including fever, rheumatism, and respiratory problems, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. Bioactive constituents of Pecteilis euryloba include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Pecteilis euryloba is commonly prepared as a decoction, or in capsule form, to take advantage of its health benefits.
