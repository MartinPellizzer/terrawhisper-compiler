Sinapis eruca

The plant Sinapis eruca, also known as Wild Rocket or Rocket Cress, is a fast-growing annual plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its pungent, peppery leaves and stems.

The health benefits of Sinapis eruca include its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of colds, flu, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include isothiocyanates, glucosinolates, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Sinapis eruca is commonly prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, as well as being used in salad dressings and sauces, to take advantage of its flavor and medicinal benefits.
