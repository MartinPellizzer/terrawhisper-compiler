Brunellia pinnata

Brunellia pinnata is a medicinal plant species native to tropical regions of Central and South America, belonging to the family Brunelliaceae.

Brunellia pinnata contains bioactive constituents like alkaloids, flavonoids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
