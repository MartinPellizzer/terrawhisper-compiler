Valeriana hidalgoana

Valeriana hidalgoana is a species of flowering plant in the family Valerianaceae, commonly known as the valerian or valerian root.

The bioactive constituents of Valeriana hidalgoana include valerenic acid, valeranone, and valepotriates, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Valeriana hidalgoana include teas, capsules, and tinctures, often used in traditional medicine to promote a good night's sleep and alleviate symptoms of restlessness and irritability.
