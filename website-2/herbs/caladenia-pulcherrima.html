Caladenia pulcherrima

Caladenia pulcherrima, commonly known as the "pink fairy orchid," is a species of orchid native to Western Australia.

The bioactive constituents of Caladenia pulcherrima include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
