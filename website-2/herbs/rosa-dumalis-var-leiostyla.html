What is Rosa dumalis var. leiostyla?

Rosa dumalis var. leiostyla is a botanical variety of the Rosa dumalis species, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub, typically growing up to 2 meters in height, with a spread of approximately 1.5 meters. The leaves of Rosa dumalis var. leiostyla are pinnate, consisting of 7-13 leaflets that are elliptical in shape, measuring 2-4 centimeters in length, and have a serrated margin. The flowers are small, pink to reddish-purple in color, and have five petals, measuring 3-4 centimeters in diameter, with 20-30 stamens and a globose, greenish-yellow ovary.

The fruit is a small, red or purple, hip-like structure, approximately 1-2 centimeters in diameter, containing 2-5 seeds.
