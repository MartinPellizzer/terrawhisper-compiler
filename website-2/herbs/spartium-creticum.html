Spartium creticum

Spartium creticum, also known as Cretan broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region, belonging to the legume family (Fabaceae).

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial effects, contributing to its potential health benefits in treating various conditions, such as wounds, skin infections, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Spartium creticum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into topical creams and ointments for its therapeutic applications.
