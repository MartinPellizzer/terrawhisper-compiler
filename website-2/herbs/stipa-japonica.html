Stipa japonica

Stipa japonica, also known as Japanese feather grass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia, characterized by its slender stems and delicate, feathery foliage.

The bioactive constituents of Stipa japonica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and polysaccharides, which are thought to be responsible for its medicinal properties.
