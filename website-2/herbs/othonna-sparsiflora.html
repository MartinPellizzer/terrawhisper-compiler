Othonna sparsiflora

Othonna sparsiflora, also known as the Cape daisy or Othonna, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa, belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, and its extracts have been found to have anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which may help in soothing skin irritations, wounds, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Othonna sparsiflora include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic potential.

In traditional medicine, Othonna sparsiflora is often prepared as a tea, infusion, or topical cream, and it is also used in capsule or tablet form for its health benefits.
