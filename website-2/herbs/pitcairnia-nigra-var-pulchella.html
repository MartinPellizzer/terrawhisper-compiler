What is Pitcairnia nigra var. pulchella?

Pitcairnia nigra var. pulchella is a plant species belonging to the family Bromeliaceae.

It is characterized by its rosette of dark green leaves with reddish-brown margins and tips, measuring 60-80 cm in height. The leaves are rigid, strap-shaped, and have a sharp apex. The inflorescence is a panicle of dark purple flowers, 50-60 cm in length, that emerges from the center of the leaf rosette.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments in Mexico and Central America.
