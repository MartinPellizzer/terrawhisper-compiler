What is Hieracium racemosum subsp. athanasii?

Hieracium racemosum subsp.

athanasii is a plant taxon within the Hieracium genus, belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is a perennial herb with a rosette of leaves, 10-30 cm in diameter, bearing 15-50 linear to lanceolate leaves, 1-3 cm long, 1-2 mm wide, and 5-10-nerved.

The inflorescence is a corymb of 10-50 flowers, 1.5-2.5 cm in diameter, with 5-merous yellow corolla, 1.5-2.5 cm in diameter, and 50-100 anther-fragments. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions at high altitudes, preferring well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. Hieracium racemosum subsp.

athanasii is characterized by a dense, greyish-villous pubescence on the leaves and stems.
