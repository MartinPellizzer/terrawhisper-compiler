Melaleuca flammea

Melaleuca flammea, commonly known as Paperbark Melaleuca, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family (Myrtaceae) that is native to Australia.

The bioactive constituents of Melaleuca flammea include compounds such as cymene, limonene, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
