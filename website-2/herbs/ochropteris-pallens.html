Ochropteris pallens

Ochropteris pallens, also known as the Ochropteris fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of the world.

The bioactive constituents of Ochropteris pallens include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made from Ochropteris pallens include teas, infusions, and extracts, which are often used in traditional medicine to promote overall well-being and alleviate symptoms of various health conditions.
