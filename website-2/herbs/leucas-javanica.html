Leucas javanica

Leucas javanica, also known as Java leucas or Java tetra, is a type of flowering plant belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

Traditionally, the plant has been used in folk medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, headache, and respiratory issues, due to its potential anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Leucas javanica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Leucas javanica include infusions, decoctions, and extracts, which are often used in traditional medicine to treat a range of health complaints.
