Oryza palustris

Oryza palustris, commonly known as the marsh wild rice or black rice, is a type of wetland grass that belongs to the family Poaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Oryza palustris include phenolic acids, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
