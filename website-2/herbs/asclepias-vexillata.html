Asclepias vexillata

Asclepias vexillata, also known as the silvery-leafed milkweed or white-antennae milkweed, is a perennial plant species within the Apocynaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Asclepias vexillata include cardenolides, glycosides, and other alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
