Zinnia schiedeana

Zinnia schiedeana, also known as the Mexican zinnia or golden zinnia, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae) native to Mexico.

The plant has been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including fever, cough, and skin conditions, due to its anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal properties. The bioactive constituents of Zinnia schiedeana include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Zinnia schiedeana include teas, infusions, and tinctures, often used to soothe respiratory issues and promote wound healing.
