Chlorocharis balansana

Chlorocharis balansana, also known as Balansa clover, is a perennial legume native to tropical Asia, characterized by its small white or pink flowers and trifoliate leaves.

The plant is rich in bioactive constituents, including isoflavones, flavonoids, and saponins, which have been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including menopausal symptoms, cardiovascular diseases, and digestive issues. The health benefits of Balansa clover include its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and estrogenic properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of menopause, such as hot flashes and night sweats.

Balansa clover is commonly used in herbal preparations, including teas, capsules, and extracts, often in combination with other herbs, to support women's health and well-being.
