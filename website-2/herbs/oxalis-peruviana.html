Oxalis peruviana

Oxalis peruviana, also known as wood sorrel or purple wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Oxalidaceae family, characterized by its distinctive purple flowers and sour-tasting leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Oxalis peruviana include oxalic acid, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
