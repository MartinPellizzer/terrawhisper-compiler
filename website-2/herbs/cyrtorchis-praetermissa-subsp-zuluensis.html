What is Cyrtorchis praetermissa subsp. zuluensis?

Cyrtorchis praetermissa subsp.

zuluensis is a subspecies of orchid belonging to the family Orchidaceae. It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulbous habit, having 2-3 narrow, leathery leaves that are often keeled and elliptical in shape.

The inflorescence is a raceme, bearing 2-8 flowers that are white with a yellow lip and are often spotted with purple. The flowers have a cylindrical, greenish-yellow column and are 1-2 cm in diameter.

This subspecies is endemic to the mountainous regions of KwaZulu-Natal in South Africa.
