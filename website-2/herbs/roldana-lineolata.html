Roldana lineolata

Roldana lineolata, also known as Mexican Gold or Golden Roldana, is a flowering plant native to Mexico, characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers and silvery-green foliage.

Roldana lineolata is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as in traditional medicine to promote wound healing and reduce inflammation.
