What is Asplenium affine var. mettenii?

Asplenium affine var. mettenii is a fern variety belonging to the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a terrestrial plant with a rhizomatous habit, featuring dark brown to black stipe bases and black rachises. The fronds are linear-lanceolate to ovate-lanceolate, measuring 10-40 cm long by 5-15 cm wide, with a simple or bipinnate pinnae arrangement. The plant's fertile fronds are smaller, measuring 5-15 cm long, with smaller pinnae and a more densely crowded arrangement.

The species is endemic to Southeast Asia, specifically in Myanmar and Thailand.
