Helichrysum candidissimum

Helichrysum candidissimum, also known as Yellow Everlasting, is a perennial herb belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which can help alleviate various health issues such as wounds, digestive problems, and respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of Helichrysum candidissimum include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Helichrysum candidissimum include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to soothe and calm the body, reducing inflammation and promoting overall well-being.
