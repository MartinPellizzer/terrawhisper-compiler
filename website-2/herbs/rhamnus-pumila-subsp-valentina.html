What is Rhamnus pumila subsp. valentina?

Rhamnus pumila subsp.

valentina is a shrub-like plant that belongs to the family Rhamnaceae. It has alternate, simple leaves that are dark green on the upper surface and light green on the lower surface, measuring 2-5 cm in length and 1-2.5 cm in width.

The leaves are elliptical to ovate in shape, with a pointed tip and an entire margin. The plant produces greenish-yellow flowers in axillary cymes, with four to six sepals and four petals, in May and June.

The fruits are red or purple, globular berries, 6-10 mm in diameter, that ripen in July and August.
