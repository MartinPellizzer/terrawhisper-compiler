Verbascum creticum

Verbascum creticum, also known as Cretan mullein, is a flowering plant species belonging to the mullein genus, native to the Mediterranean region.

It has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and expectorant effects, making it useful in treating respiratory issues such as bronchitis and coughs. The bioactive constituents of Verbascum creticum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Verbascum creticum include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to treat respiratory problems and skin conditions.
