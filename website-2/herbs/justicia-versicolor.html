Justicia versicolor

Justicia versicolor, commonly known as the Jacob's Ladder or Wild Potato, is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Justicia versicolor include alkaloids, flavonoids, and glycosides, which have been shown to possess antioxidant and immunomodulatory activities.
