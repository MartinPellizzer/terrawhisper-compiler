Lyonia racemosa

Lyonia racemosa, also known as the fetterbush or mountain holly, is a shrub native to eastern North America.

The bioactive constituents of Lyonia racemosa include phenolic acids, flavonoids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
