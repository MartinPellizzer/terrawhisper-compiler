What is Volkameria ligustrina var. nicaraguensis?

Volkameria ligustrina var. nicaraguensis is an endemic variety of plant that belongs to the family Lamiaceae.

It has a shrub-like habit with a height range of 0.5 to 1.5 meters, and its branches are quadrangular with a pubescent texture. The leaves are oppositely arranged, elliptical to ovate in shape, 5-10 cm long, and have a pointed apex with a cuneate base. The inflorescence is a verticillate cyme, bearing tubular flowers with a 5-lobed corolla, 6-8 mm long, and a yellow color.

The flowers are borne on a peduncle 2-5 mm long, and the calyx is 5-6 mm long, with a lobed shape.
