Cymbopogon lividus

Cymbopogon lividus, also known as East Indian lemon grass, is a species of grass that belongs to the Poaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Cymbopogon lividus include citral, geraniol, and limonene, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
