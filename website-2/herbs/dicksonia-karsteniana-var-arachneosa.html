What is Dicksonia karsteniana var. arachneosa?

Dicksonia karsteniana var. arachneosa is a fern variety within the species Dicksonia karsteniana, which belongs to the family Cyatheaceae.

It is a species of tree fern characterized by its upright stem, known as a stipe, which is covered in scales, and its frond, a compound leaf that is made up of pinnae and pinnules. The frond of D. karsteniana var. arachneosa is typically 1-2 meters in length, with pinnae that are 20-30 cm long and pinnules that are 2-5 cm long. The species exhibits a unique pattern of venation, with a midrib that is present on the underside of the frond and smaller veins that are present on both the upper and lower surfaces.

The plant is native to tropical regions of Central and South America.
