Psychotria leonardii

Psychotria leonardii, also known as Indian ayurvedic herb or Picrorhiza, is a plant species native to the Himalayas that has been used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for centuries.

The bioactive constituents of Psychotria leonardii include alkaloids, glycosides, and iridoid glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Psychotria leonardii include capsules, tablets, and powder, often used to support liver and gallbladder health, as well as to treat conditions such as jaundice and hepatitis.
