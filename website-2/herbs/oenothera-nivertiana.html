Oenothera nivertiana

Oenothera nivertiana, also known as evening primrose, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America, characterized by its delicate white or pink flowers and heart-shaped leaves.

The bioactive constituents of evening primrose include gamma-linolenic acid (GLA), a polyunsaturated fatty acid with anti-inflammatory properties, as well as other fatty acids, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds.

Evening primrose is commonly prepared as a supplement in oil or capsule form, as well as in herbal teas, tinctures, and salves, which are used to support skin health and alleviate various health conditions.
