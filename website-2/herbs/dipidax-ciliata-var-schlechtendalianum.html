What is Dipidax ciliata var. schlechtendalianum?

The inflorescence is a raceme with 3-8 flowers, each 3-4 cm in diameter, featuring 6 tepals, 3 stamens, and 3 styles. The flowers are white with yellow centers and purple or pink markings, and are protogynous, meaning the female reproductive parts mature before the male reproductive parts.

The plant grows in moist, shaded areas, typically in grasslands or rocky outcrops, in South Africa.
