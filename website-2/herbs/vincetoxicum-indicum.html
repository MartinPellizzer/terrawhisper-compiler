Vincetoxicum indicum

Vincetoxicum indicum, also known as the Indian Vincetoxicum or Roxburgh's Vincetoxicum, is a perennial plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family, traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine.

The health benefits of Vincetoxicum indicum include its potential to lower cholesterol levels, reduce blood sugar, and exhibit anti-inflammatory properties, making it a valuable remedy for managing cardiovascular and metabolic disorders.
