What is Arabidopsis lyrata subsp. kamchatica?

Arabidopsis lyrata subsp.

The leaves are 5-15 cm long and 2-6 cm wide, with a petiole that is 1-3 cm long. Arabidopsis lyrata subsp. kamchatica is native to Kamchatka, Russia, and typically grows in moist, shaded areas.
