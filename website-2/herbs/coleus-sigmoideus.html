Coleus sigmoideus

Coleus sigmoideus, also known as Coleus aromaticus, is a tropical plant species belonging to the Lamiaceae family, commonly used in traditional medicine for its aromatic and medicinal properties.

The bioactive constituents of Coleus sigmoideus include flavonoids, terpenoids, and essential oils, such as borneol and camphor, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Coleus sigmoideus is commonly prepared as a decoction, infusion, or essential oil, and is often used to treat various ailments, including fever, cough, and digestive issues.
