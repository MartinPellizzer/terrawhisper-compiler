classification

Chaetaria mauritiana is classified as a genus within the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family. It is further categorized into the subfamily Chloridoideae, which is a group of grasses that are typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The tribe in which Chaetaria mauritiana is placed is Chlorideae. At the family level, it is closely related to other grasses such as Ctenium and Trichachne. The classification of Chaetaria mauritiana is based on its anatomical characteristics and phylogenetic relationships with other plant species.
