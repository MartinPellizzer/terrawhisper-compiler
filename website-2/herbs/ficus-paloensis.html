Ficus paloensis

Ficus paloensis, also known as Palo Santo, is a tropical plant native to the Americas, characterized by its unique, fragrant heartwood.

The bioactive constituents of Ficus paloensis include terpenoids, flavonoids, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
