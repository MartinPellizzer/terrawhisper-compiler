What is Musa × sapientum var. pruinosa?

Musa × sapientum var. pruinosa is a plant variety within the species Musa × sapientum, which belongs to the family Musaceae.

It is a type of banana plant with a pruinose (waxy or powdery) appearance, characterized by a white or bluish-gray coating on its leaves and stems. Botanically, this variety exhibits large, paddle-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length, and a short, stout stem with a terminal inflorescence bearing edible fruit. The plant thrives in tropical climates, requiring high temperatures and humidity levels to grow.

Musa × sapientum var. pruinosa is a sterile variety, meaning it is unable to produce viable seeds and relies on vegetative propagation for reproduction.
