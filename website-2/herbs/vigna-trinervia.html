Vigna trinervia

Vigna trinervia, also known as the Indian laburnum, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa and Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Vigna trinervia include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which have been found to exhibit antioxidant, anti-proliferative, and anti-inflammatory activities.
