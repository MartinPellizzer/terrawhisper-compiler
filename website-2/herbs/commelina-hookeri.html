Commelina hookeri

Commelina hookeri, commonly known as Hooker's dayflower or small blue dayflower, is a species of flowering plant in the family Commelinaceae.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
