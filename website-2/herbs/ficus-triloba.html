classification

Ficus triloba is a species of fig tree native to tropical Asia, belonging to the family Moraceae. It is a member of the genus Ficus, which comprises over 800 species of fig trees and other related plants. The Moraceae family is a group of flowering plants commonly known as mulberry or fig families. Ficus triloba is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in various conditions. The classification of Ficus triloba reflects its unique characteristics and evolutionary relationships within the plant kingdom.
