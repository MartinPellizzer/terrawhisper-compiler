Bolboschoenus fluviatilis

Bolboschoenus fluviatilis, also known as river clubrush, is a perennial aquatic plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including the treatment of inflammation, fever, and respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma, due to its anti-inflammatory and expectorant effects. The bioactive constituents of Bolboschoenus fluviatilis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Bolboschoenus fluviatilis is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being dried and used in smoking blends to aid in respiratory relief.
