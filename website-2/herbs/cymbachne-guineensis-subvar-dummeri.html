What is Cymbachne guineensis subvar. dummeri?

Cymbachne guineensis subvar. dummeri is a plant that belongs to the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial grass species characterized by its culms, which are up to 1.5 meters tall, and its leaves, which are 30-50 cm long and 2-3 mm wide. The plant's panicle is a dense, contracted spike with a length of up to 20 cm, containing 2-4 spikelets, each with 2-4 florets. The glumes are 4-5 mm long, while the lemma and palea are 6-8 mm long, and the stamen is 10-12 mm long.

The plant is native to tropical Africa.
