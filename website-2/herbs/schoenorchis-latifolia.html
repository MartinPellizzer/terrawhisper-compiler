Schoenorchis latifolia

Schoenorchis latifolia, also known as the broad-leaved Schoenorchis, is a species of orchid native to tropical Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Schoenorchis latifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to prepare herbal teas, infusions, and tonics, which are also available in capsule and extract form in modern herbal preparations.
