Gentianella detonsa

Gentianella detonsa, also known as Deton's gentian or lesser gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the gentian family (Gentianaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Gentianella detonsa include iridoid glycosides, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
