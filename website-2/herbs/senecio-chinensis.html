Senecio chinensis

Senecio chinensis, also known as Qianhu, is a plant species in the dandelion family that is native to China and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

The plant has been shown to have various health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and antioxidant properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of fever, cough, and respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of Senecio chinensis include sesquiterpene lactones, triterpenoids, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Senecio chinensis is commonly prepared as a decoction, capsule, or tablet, and is often used to treat respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and asthma.
