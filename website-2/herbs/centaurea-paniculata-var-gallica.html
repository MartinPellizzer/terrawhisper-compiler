What is Centaurea paniculata var. gallica?

Centaurea paniculata var. gallica is an annual or biennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It has a height of 20-50 cm, with a taproot and a branching stem. The leaves are lanceolate to ovate, 5-15 cm long, and 1-3 cm wide, with a crenate margin and a pubescent surface. The inflorescence is a dense, elongated panicle with numerous small, blue to purple flowers, each 10-15 mm in diameter, with 5 petals and an involucre of 10-20 bracts.

The plant flowers from June to September and is native to the Iberian Peninsula and southwestern France.
