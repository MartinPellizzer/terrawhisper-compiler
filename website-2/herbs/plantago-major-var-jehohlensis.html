What is Plantago major var. jehohlensis?

Plantago major var. jehohlensis is a botanical variety of the species Plantago major, belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of ovate to lanceolate leaves, typically 3-10 cm long and 1-3 cm wide, with a cordate base and an entire margin. The plant produces a slender spike of small, greenish-white flowers, 3-5 mm long, in the summer months. The inflorescence is terminal and axillary, with a peduncle 1-5 cm long.

The seeds are small, triangular, and 1-2 mm long, with a characteristic reticulate pattern on the surface.
