What is Tragopogon porrifolius subsp. eriospermus?

Tragopogon porrifolius subsp.

eriospermus is a subspecies of perennial plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It has a taprooted habit, with a height of up to 1.5 meters, and a basal rosette of linear, elliptic to lanceolate leaves that are 10-50 cm long and 0.5-2 cm wide.

The inflorescence is a corymbiform panicle of white to lilac flowers, each 3-5 cm long, with a pappus of 2-3 cm long, setose bristles. The achenes are 3-4 cm long, with a pappus of 2-3 cm long, setose bristles, and contain 10-12 seeds.

The subspecies is native to the Balkan Peninsula and the Mediterranean region.
