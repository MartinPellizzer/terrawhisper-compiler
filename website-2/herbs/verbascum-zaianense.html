Verbascum zaianense

Verbascum zaianense, also known as Zaian mullein, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Scrophulariaceae family, characterized by its tall stature and woolly leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Verbascum zaianense include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
