Villarsia indica

Villarsia indica, also known as the Indian villarsia, is a type of aquatic plant belonging to the family Menyanthaceae.

The plant has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, inflammation, and respiratory issues, due to its potential health benefits such as antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Villarsia indica include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
