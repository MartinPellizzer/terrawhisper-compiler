What is Helianthus annuus f. lenticularis?

Helianthus annuus f. lenticularis is a variety of sunflower that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its unique, flat-topped inflorescence, typically consisting of hundreds of small, tubular flowers. The plant has an annual growth habit, with a height range of 10-30 cm, and produces yellow, rayless florets. Helianthus annuus f. lenticularis has a taproot and a stem covered in coarse, retrorse hairs, and its leaves are lanceolate to ovate in shape, measuring 5-20 cm in length.

This variety is native to North America and is often found in dry, sandy areas.
