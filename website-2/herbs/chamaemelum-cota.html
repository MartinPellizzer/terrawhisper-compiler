Chamaemelum cota

Chamaemelum nobile, commonly known as Chamomile or Chamaemelum cota, is a low-growing, perennial herb belonging to the daisy family (Asteraceae).

The health benefits of Chamaemelum nobile include its calming and soothing properties, which can help alleviate anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, terpenoids, and phenolic acids, such as apigenin, luteolin, and bisabolol, which are responsible for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Chamaemelum nobile include teas, tinctures, and essential oils, which are used to treat a range of health issues, from stress and sleep disorders to skin irritations and wound healing.
