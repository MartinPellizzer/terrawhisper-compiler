classification

Dryopteris bermudiana is a species of fern in the family Dryopteridaceae. It belongs to the genus Dryopteris and the class Polypodiopsida. The kingdom it is classified under is Plantae and the phylum is Pteridophyta. The order of this fern is Polypodiales and the suborder is Dryopteridoidae. At the level of family, it is part of the monotypic family Dryopteridaceae.
