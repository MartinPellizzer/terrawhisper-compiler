Cirsium makinoi

Cirsium makinoi, also known as Makino's thistle, is a perennial plant species belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

Traditionally used in Japanese medicine, Makino's thistle is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may aid in the treatment of various conditions such as arthritis, gout, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Cirsium makinoi include sesquiterpene lactones, such as makinothrin and cirsitidin, as well as other compounds like flavonoids and phenolic acids.

In traditional herbal medicine, Makino's thistle is often prepared as an infusion, decoction, or tincture, and is also used in combination with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic effects.
