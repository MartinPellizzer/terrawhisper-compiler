What is Monardella undulata subsp. crispa?

Monardella undulata subsp.

The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, measuring 2-5 centimeters in length, with an undulate margin and a pointed apex. The flowers are purple, tubular, and 2-3 centimeters in length, produced in a terminal inflorescence. Monardella undulata subsp.

crispa is endemic to the coastal mountains of southern California, USA.
