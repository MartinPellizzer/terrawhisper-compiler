classification

Tomex myrrha, also known as Balsamodendron myrrha, is a species of plant in the family Burseraceae. It is a resinous tree native to the Middle East and North Africa. The classification of Tomex myrrha is as follows: Kingdom: Plantae, Clade: Angiosperms, Clade: Eudicots, Clade: Rosids, Order: Sapindales, Family: Burseraceae, Genus: Balsamodendron, Species: B. myrrha. Tomex myrrha is a member of the Balsamodendron genus, which consists of several species of resinous trees.
