Arundinaria shibuyana

Arundinaria shibuyana, also known as Shibuya bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to Japan, characterized by its slender culms and delicate foliage.

The bioactive constituents of Shibuya bamboo include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which have been shown to possess antimicrobial, antiviral, and anticancer activities.
