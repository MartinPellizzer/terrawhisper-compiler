Schizostachyum distans

Schizostachyum distans, commonly known as the bamboo grass or Thai bamboo, is a type of bamboo plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

Traditionally, Schizostachyum distans has been used in Southeast Asian medicine to treat various health issues, including fever, respiratory problems, and skin infections, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The plant contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Schizostachyum distans is often prepared as a decoction or tincture, and is used to treat conditions such as bronchitis, coughs, and skin irritations.
