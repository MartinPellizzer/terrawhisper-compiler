What is Abies concolor f. brevifolia?

Abies concolor f. brevifolia is a coniferous plant endemic to the southwestern United States and Mexico.

It belongs to the family Pinaceae and is a subspecies of Abies concolor. Botanically, it has needle-like leaves that are 1-3 cm long, dark green on top, and lighter green on the bottom, and are arranged in a two-ranked pattern. The plant grows up to 20 meters tall, with a trunk diameter of up to 60 cm, and has a conical shape with a narrow crown.

The bark is reddish-brown and smooth, becoming fibrous and scaly with age.
