Jacea nigrescens

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits, including its ability to act as an anti-inflammatory and antioxidant, and to support digestion and immune function. The bioactive constituents of Jacea nigrescens include glucosinolates, which have been shown to have anti-cancer and anti-inflammatory properties, as well as other compounds such as isothiocyanates and phenolic acids.

Jacea nigrescens is commonly used to make herbal preparations such as tinctures, capsules, and teas, which are often marketed as supplements for immune system support and overall health.
