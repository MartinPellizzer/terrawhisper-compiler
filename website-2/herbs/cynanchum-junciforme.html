Cynanchum junciforme

Cynanchum junciforme, also known as Juncus vine or Small-flowered Trumpet Creeper, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Cynanchum junciforme include alkaloids, glycosides, and phenolic compounds, such as ajmalicine, norajmalicine, and scopoletin, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
