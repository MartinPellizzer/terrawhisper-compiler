classification

Acacia fasciculata is classified within the family Fabaceae, commonly known as legume or pea family. This plant belongs to the subfamily Mimosoideae, which is a large and diverse group of flowering plants. The genus Acacia is native to Australia, but species like Acacia fasciculata have been introduced to other parts of the world. Acacia fasciculata is further classified into the tribe Acacieae, which comprises about 1,300 species of Acacia. The classification of Acacia fasciculata is also sometimes revised as Vachellia fasciculata, reflecting changes in the classification of the Acacia genus.
