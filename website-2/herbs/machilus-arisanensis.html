Machilus arisanensis

Machilus arisanensis, also known as the Taiwanese machilus or arisan machilus, is a species of plant in the laurel family (Lauraceae) native to Taiwan.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may contribute to its use in traditional medicine for treating respiratory issues and fever. Machilus arisanensis contains bioactive constituents such as lignans, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
