classification

Arundo vulgaris, also known as common reed, is a species of flowering plant that belongs to the family Poaceae. It is a perennial grass that grows in wetlands and can form dense stands along rivers and lakes. Within the family Poaceae, Arundo vulgaris is classified in the subfamily Arundoideae. The exact classification of Arundo vulgaris can also be broken down further into the genus Arundo and the species A. vulgaris. This classification helps to identify the unique characteristics and growth habits of the common reed plant.
