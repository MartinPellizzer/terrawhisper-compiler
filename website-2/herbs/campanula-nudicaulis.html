Campanula nudicaulis

Campanula nudicaulis, commonly known as lady's mantle or bellflower, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may aid in wound healing, reduce fever, and alleviate gastrointestinal issues. The bioactive constituents of Campanula nudicaulis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenes, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Campanula nudicaulis is used to make herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of health conditions, including digestive problems and skin irritations.
