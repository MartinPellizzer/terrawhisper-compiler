Stipa laevissima

Stipa laevissima, also known as the smooth feather-grass, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

Traditionally, the plant has been used for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which may aid in the treatment of various gastrointestinal issues and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of Stipa laevissima include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Herbal preparations made from Stipa laevissima include teas and tinctures, as well as dried infusions, which are often used to alleviate digestive issues and promote overall well-being.
