Zieria laxiflora

Zieria laxiflora is a shrub native to eastern Australia, belonging to the Rutaceae family and commonly known as the 'White Musk Lily Bush'.

The bioactive constituents of Zieria laxiflora include flavonoids, terpenoids, and limonoids, which are responsible for its pharmacological activities.
