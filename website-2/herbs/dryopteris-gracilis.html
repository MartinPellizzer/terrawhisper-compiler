Dryopteris gracilis

Dryopteris gracilis, also known as Slender Wood Fern or Autumn Fern, is a species of fern native to North America, characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds.

The bioactive constituents of Dryopteris gracilis include flavonoids, sesquiterpenes, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
