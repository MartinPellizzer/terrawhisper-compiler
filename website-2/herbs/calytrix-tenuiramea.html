Calytrix tenuiramea

Calytrix tenuiramea, also known as the delicate fringe-lily or white fringe-lily, is a flowering plant species native to Western Australia.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering potential health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, as well as antioxidant and wound-healing properties. The bioactive constituents of Calytrix tenuiramea include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Western Australian medicine, the plant is often prepared as an infusion or tincture, and is commonly used to treat a range of health issues, including wounds, skin conditions, and respiratory infections.
