Perilla urticifolia

Perilla urticifolia, also known as Korean perilla or wild perilla, is a perennial plant native to East Asia, characterized by its fragrant leaves and vibrant purple flowers.

The health benefits of Perilla urticifolia include its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-diabetic properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various ailments, such as fever, sore throat, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include perilla aldehyde, perillaldehyde, and rosmarinic acid, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Perilla urticifolia is commonly used in traditional herbal preparations, including tea, extract, and tincture, as well as in modern supplements to promote overall health and well-being.
