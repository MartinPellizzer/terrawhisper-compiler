    The main herbal preparations of Dianthus requienii var. cognobilis are dianthus tincture, dianthus salve, infused oil, dianthus tea, and dianthus infusion.

Dianthus salve is a topical preparation made by infusing the flowers in a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil, then mixing it with beeswax to create a balm that can be used to soothe skin irritations. Dianthus tea and infusion are both made by steeping the dried flowers in hot water, with tea being a more diluted preparation and infusion being a stronger, more concentrated liquid that can be used as a remedy for respiratory issues.

    These preparations are valued for their potential to promote relaxation, ease digestive discomfort, and soothe skin irritations due to the presence of flavonoids and phenolic acids in the plant.
