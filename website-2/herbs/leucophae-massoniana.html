Leucophae massoniana

Leucophae massoniana, also known as Silvergrass, is a perennial plant species native to the mountainous regions of Asia, characterized by its attractive silver-gray foliage and delicate white flowers.

Leucophae massoniana is rich in bioactive constituents, including flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
