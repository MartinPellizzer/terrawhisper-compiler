What is Acacia ramulosa var. linophylla?

Acacia ramulosa var. linophylla is a botanical variety within the species Acacia ramulosa, belonging to the family Fabaceae, commonly known as legumes or pea family.

This variety is characterized by its small, linear, or filiform phyllodes (modified leaves) that resemble those of Acacia linophylla, hence the varietal name linophylla. The plant grows as a shrub or small tree, typically reaching a height of up to 3 meters. Its bark is grey or brown, with a smooth or slightly fissured texture.

The phyllodes are 10-30 millimeters long, with a linear or filiform shape and a pointed tip.
