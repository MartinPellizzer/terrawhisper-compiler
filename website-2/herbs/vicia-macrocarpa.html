Vicia macrocarpa

Vicia macrocarpa, also known as the large pod vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that may aid in the management of conditions such as arthritis, diabetes, and cardiovascular disease. The bioactive constituents of Vicia macrocarpa include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Vicia macrocarpa is commonly prepared as a decoction or infusion, often used to treat digestive issues and as a natural remedy for menstrual problems.
