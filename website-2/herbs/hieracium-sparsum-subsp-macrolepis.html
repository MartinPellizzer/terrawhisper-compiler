What is Hieracium sparsum subsp. macrolepis?

Hieracium sparsum subsp.

macrolepis is a botanical taxon belonging to the family Asteraceae, native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb with a rosette of leaves, consisting of 1-4 cm long, lance-shaped, and ciliate blades, often bearing a few scattered, linear lobes.

The leaves are green, glabrous or pubescent, and sometimes possess a reddish tinge at the base. The flowering stem, usually 10-30 cm tall, bears a terminal, yellow to orange-yellow capitulum, comprising 5-12 female florets surrounded by 15-30 ray florets.

The inflorescence is often solitary and appears from May to September.
