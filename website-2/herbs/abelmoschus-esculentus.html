Abelmoschus esculentus

Abelmoschus esculentus, commonly known as okra, is a tropical plant belonging to the mallow family (Malvaceae).

The bioactive constituents of okra include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
