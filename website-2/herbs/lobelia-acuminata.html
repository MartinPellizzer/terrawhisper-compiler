Lobelia acuminata

Lobelia acuminata, also known as Lobelia or Indian Tobacco, is a perennial plant native to North America, belonging to the family Campanulaceae.

Lobelia acuminata contains bioactive constituents like lobeline, lobelanine, and lobelamine, which have been studied for their potential in treating addiction, anxiety, and other conditions.
