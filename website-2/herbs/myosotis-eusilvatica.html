Myosotis eusilvatica

Myosotis eusilvatica, also known as blue forget-me-not, is a flowering plant species in the borage family (Boraginaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Myosotis eusilvatica include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
