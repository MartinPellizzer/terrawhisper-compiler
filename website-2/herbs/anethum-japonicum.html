Anethum japonicum

Anethum japonicum, also known as Japanese dill or Japanese anise, is a perennial herb belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae) native to East Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Anethum japonicum include volatile oils, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
