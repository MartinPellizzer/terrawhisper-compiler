What is Hypoestes floribunda var. distans?

Hypoestes floribunda var. distans is a plant variety that belongs to the family Acanthaceae, a family of flowering plants commonly known as the acanthus or thyme family.

It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are white or pale purple in color, and its calyx with four or five lobes, where the upper lobe is larger than the others. The leaves of Hypoestes floribunda var. distans are ovate or lanceolate in shape, with serrated margins and a short petiole, and are typically 5-10 cm in length. This plant variety is native to tropical Africa, where it grows in woodland and forest environments.

The specific epithet 'distans' refers to the relatively distant flowers, which are borne on long stalks from the leaf axils.
