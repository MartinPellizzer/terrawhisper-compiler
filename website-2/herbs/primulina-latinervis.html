Primulina latinervis

Primulina latinervis is a species of flowering plant in the family Gesneriaceae, native to China.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in treating various conditions such as fever, cough, and respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of Primulina latinervis include iridoid glycosides, caffeic acid, and sesquiterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Primulina latinervis is used to make tea, infusions, and decoctions, which are often used in Chinese medicine to treat respiratory and gastrointestinal issues.
