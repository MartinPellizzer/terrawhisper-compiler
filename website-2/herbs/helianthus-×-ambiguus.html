Helianthus × ambiguus

Helianthus × ambiguus, also known as ambiguous sunflower, is a hybrid species of the sunflower genus, characterized by its unique flower characteristics and limited geographical distribution.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can aid in reducing inflammation, improving wound healing, and protecting against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Helianthus × ambiguus include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Helianthus × ambiguus is commonly prepared as a tincture, tea, or decoction, often used to treat various health conditions, including skin issues, digestive problems, and respiratory infections.
