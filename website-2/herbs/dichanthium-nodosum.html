Dichanthium nodosum

Dichanthium nodosum, also known as Bambatsi grass or Buffalo grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical Africa.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as reducing inflammation and fever, as well as exhibiting antimicrobial and antifungal activities. The bioactive constituents of Dichanthium nodosum include flavonoids, glycosides, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Dichanthium nodosum is often used to prepare infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are commonly used to treat various ailments, including respiratory issues and gastrointestinal problems.
