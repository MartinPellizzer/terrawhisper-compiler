Astragalus donianus

Astragalus donianus, also known as Donian's milk-vetch, is a perennial plant species belonging to the legume family (Fabaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Astragalus donianus include saponins, flavonoids, and polysaccharides, which are thought to be responsible for its medicinal effects.
