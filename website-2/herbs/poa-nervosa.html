Poa nervosa

Poa nervosa, also known as Nerve Grass or Nervous Grass, is a perennial grass species belonging to the family Poaceae.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and sedative properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of anxiety, insomnia, and stress. The bioactive constituents of Poa nervosa include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are believed to contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Traditionally, Poa nervosa is used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to promote relaxation, reduce muscle tension, and improve sleep quality.
