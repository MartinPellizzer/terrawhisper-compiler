Oberonia brevifolia

Oberonia brevifolia, also known as the short-leaved Oberonia orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical Asia, characterized by its small, white flowers and short, narrow leaves.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with potential health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which may help alleviate respiratory issues, boost immune function, and protect against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Oberonia brevifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Oberonia brevifolia is commonly used in traditional herbal preparations, including teas, infusions, and extracts, which are often employed to treat respiratory conditions such as bronchitis and asthma.
