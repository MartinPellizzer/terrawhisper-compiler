What is Camellia petelotii var. microcarpa?

Camellia petelotii var. microcarpa is an evergreen shrub or small tree endemic to Vietnam, belonging to the family Theaceae.

It is a woody plant with a height of 3-5 meters, having elliptic to lanceolate leaves that are 6-13 cm long and 2-4 cm wide, with a glossy, dark green upper surface and a paler underside. The inflorescence is a solitary, showy flower with 5 petals, measuring 3-4 cm in diameter, having a pink to red color, and is borne on a short peduncle.
