Sabina racemosa

Sabina racemosa, also known as Juniperus sabina, is a small shrub-like evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The health benefits of Sabina racemosa include its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and diuretic properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of urinary tract infections, kidney stones, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include terpenes, flavonoids, and alkaloids, including sabinafrine and sabinaline, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Sabina racemosa include infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, often used to treat urinary issues, skin conditions, and respiratory problems.
