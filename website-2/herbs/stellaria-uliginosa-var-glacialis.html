What is Stellaria uliginosa var. glacialis?

Stellaria uliginosa var. glacialis is a plant variety in the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is an alpine, perennial herb with a rosette of leaves, typically 1-2 cm long and 0.5-1.5 cm wide, linear to lanceolate in shape, and glabrous on both surfaces. The inflorescence is a corymb, bearing white or pink flowers, 1-2 cm in diameter, with five petals and a prominent calyx. Each flower has five stamens, with the filaments united below the anthers, and the style is trifid.

The plant grows in alpine and subalpine meadows, often in moist, sandy or peaty areas, at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters above sea level.
