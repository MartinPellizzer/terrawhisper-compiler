Psyllium erectum

Psyllium erectum, also known as Indian plantago or seed plantain, is a type of flowering plant in the plantain family that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine.

The bioactive constituents of Psyllium erectum include mucilages, polysaccharides, and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
