Polygonum calophyllum

Polygonum calophyllum, also known as Polygonum multiflorum, is a traditional Chinese medicinal plant belonging to the buckwheat family (Polygonaceae).

It is valued for its potential health benefits, including its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and immunomodulatory properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of arthritis, improve liver function, and boost the immune system. The bioactive constituents of Polygonum multiflorum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and polysaccharides, among others, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Polygonum multiflorum is commonly prepared as a decoction or extract, and is often used in combination with other herbs to make tonifying and anti-inflammatory remedies, such as Hong Shen or Shenqi Fuzheng.
