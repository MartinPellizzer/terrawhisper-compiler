Dalea gracilis

Dalea gracilis, also known as the slender prairie clover or slender false clover, is a perennial legume native to North America.

The bioactive constituents of Dalea gracilis include isoflavones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
