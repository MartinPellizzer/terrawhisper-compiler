What is Pachycereus marginatus f. gibbosus?

Pachycereus marginatus f. gibbosus is a cactus taxon belonging to the family Cactaceae.

It is a variant of Pachycereus marginatus, characterized by its distinctive gibbous stems with prominent tubercles and short, fine spines. The stems are columnar, green, and up to 10 meters tall, with a diameter of up to 30 centimeters. The areoles are tubular and bear short, yellow flowers that open at night, followed by red fruits.

The plant is native to Mexico and is often found in mountainous regions.
