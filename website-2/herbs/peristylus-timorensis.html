classification

Peristylus timorensis belongs to the family Orchidaceae and the genus Peristylus. It is a species of orchid native to Timor, an island in Southeast Asia. The classification of Peristylus timorensis places it within the subfamily Orchidoideae and tribe Orchideae. Further classification includes its order Asparagales and division Magnoliophyta. Peristylus timorensis is also known by its synonym as Platanthera timorensis.
