Ruehssia serpentina

Ruehssia serpentina, also known as Indian snakeroot, is a plant species native to the Himalayan region and parts of India, characterized by its unique, serpentine root system.

The bioactive constituents of Ruehssia serpentina include alkaloids, particularly serpentinine and serpentinine N-oxide, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
