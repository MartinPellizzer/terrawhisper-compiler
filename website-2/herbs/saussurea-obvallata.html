Saussurea obvallata

Saussurea obvallata, commonly known as Tibetan Club Moss or Saussurea, is a type of plant that belongs to the Asteraceae family and is native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas and Tibetan Plateau.

Saussurea obvallata contains bioactive constituents such as saussurin, saussurellin, and sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Tibetan medicine, Saussurea is often prepared as a tea or infusion, or in the form of a decoction, to take advantage of its health benefits.
