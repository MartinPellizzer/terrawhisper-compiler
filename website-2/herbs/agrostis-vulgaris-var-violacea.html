What is Agrostis vulgaris var. violacea?

Agrostis vulgaris var. violacea is a botanical variety belonging to the genus Agrostis and family Poaceae.

It is a perennial grass characterized by its leafy stems, typically growing 30-80 cm tall, and narrow, flat, or folded leaves that are 1-2.5 cm wide. The leaves are often purplish-green in color and have a distinct ligule that is 0.5-1.5 mm long. This variety produces a panicle inflorescence with 2-6 spikelets, each containing 2-4 flowers with 2-3 glumes and 1-3 lemmas.

The flowers are wind-pollinated and typically produce achenes, the fruit of the grass, which are 1-1.5 mm long and have a distinctive awn.
