Ampelocissus arachnoidea

Ampelocissus arachnoidea, also known as the spider vine, is a deciduous climber belonging to the family Vitaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Ampelocissus arachnoidea include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the plant is often prepared as a decoction or infusion, and it is also used in the form of a paste or a tincture to treat various health conditions.
