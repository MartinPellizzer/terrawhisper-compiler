Hydriastele wendlandiana

Hydriastele wendlandiana, also known as Kava, is a plant species native to the Pacific Island region, belonging to the family Arecaceae.

Kava has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including pain relief, anxiety reduction, and sleep improvement. The bioactive constituents of Hydriastele wendlandiana include kavalactones, specifically kawain, desmethoxyyangonin, and dihydrokawain, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
