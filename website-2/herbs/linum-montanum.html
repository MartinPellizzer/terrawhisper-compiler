Linum montanum

Linum montanum, also known as Alpine flax, is a perennial plant species within the genus Linum, characterized by its delicate blue flowers and slender stems.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering relief from anxiety and insomnia due to its sedative effects. Bioactive constituents of Alpine flax include linoleic acid and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

Alpine flax is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often employed to alleviate stress and promote relaxation.
