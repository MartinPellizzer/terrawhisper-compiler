What is Seseli libanotis f. glabrum?

Seseli libanotis f. glabrum is a botanical variant of Seseli libanotis, a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It has a height of 10-60 cm, with a glabrous stem and leaves that are 1-2-pinnate and 5-10 cm long. The plant's umbels are 3-6 cm in diameter and contain 5-15 flowers, which are white or yellowish in color and have 5 petals. The flowers are arranged in a 3-4 whorled pattern and have a characteristic 5-merous structure.

The plant's root is taprooted and fibrous, with a distinct scent and taste.
