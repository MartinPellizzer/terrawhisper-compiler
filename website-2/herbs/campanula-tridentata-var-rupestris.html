What is Campanula tridentata var. rupestris?

Campanula tridentata var. rupestris is a variety of the species Campanula tridentata within the family Campanulaceae.

It is an alpine perennial plant, with leaves that are oval-shaped and serrated, measuring 2-7 cm in length. The plant bears white or pale blue, bell-shaped flowers with five lobes, measuring 6-8 mm in length and having a tubular calyx. The flowers are clustered together on a spike, emerging from a leafy stem that can grow up to 30-60 cm in height.

The plant thrives in rocky and mountainous areas, typically found between 1,000-2,500 meters in elevation.
