Launaea aspleniifolia

Launaea aspleniifolia, also known as the Chabahar lettuce or desert lettuce, is a succulent perennial plant belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The bioactive constituents of Launaea aspleniifolia include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Launaea aspleniifolia is often prepared as a tea or infusion, as well as being used in the form of capsules or tinctures to leverage its health benefits.
