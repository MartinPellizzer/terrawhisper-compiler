Medicago hamosa

Medicago hamosa, also known as annual medick or bur-clover, is a perennial legume plant native to Europe and Asia, characterized by its small, pinkish-purple flowers and ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen.

The bioactive constituents of Medicago hamosa include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its health-promoting effects.
