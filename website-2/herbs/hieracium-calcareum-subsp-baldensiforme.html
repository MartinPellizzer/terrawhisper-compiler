What is Hieracium calcareum subsp. baldensiforme?

Hieracium calcareum subsp.

baldensiforme is a plant taxon belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its rosette of leaves, typically 10-30 cm in diameter, with lanceolate to ovate leaflets, 5-20 cm long, and 0.5-2.5 cm wide, with entire margins.

The inflorescence is a corymbiform capitulum, 5-10 cm in diameter, bearing 10-20 ligulate florets, 1-2.5 cm long, and 0.5-1 cm wide, with yellow or orange-yellow corolla. The plant grows in rocky, mountainous areas at elevations between 2,000 and 3,200 meters, and is endemic to the Italian Alps.

The taxon was first described by the Italian botanist Giovanni Giacomo Mariotti in 1885.
