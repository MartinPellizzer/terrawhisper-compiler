Nicotiana excelsior

The bioactive constituents of Nicotiana excelsior include nicotine, anabasine, and other alkaloids, as well as flavonoids and phenolic compounds.

In herbal medicine, Nicotiana excelsior is commonly prepared as a tincture, salve, or infusion to be used topically for skin conditions and internally for respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and coughs.
