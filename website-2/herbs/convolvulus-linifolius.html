Convolvulus linifolius

Convolvulus linifolius, also known as the bindweed or slender bindweed, is a perennial plant species within the Convolvulaceae family, characterized by its slender stems and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including inflammation, skin conditions, and gastrointestinal problems, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Convolvulus linifolius include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
