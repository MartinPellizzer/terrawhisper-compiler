Phaius blumei

Phaius blumei, also known as the Chinese orchid or Tong Su, is a type of flowering plant belonging to the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

Bioactive constituents of Phaius blumei include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
