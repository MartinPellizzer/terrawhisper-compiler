Calamagrostis mauritanica

Calamagrostis mauritanica, also known as Moroccan feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

The bioactive constituents of Calamagrostis mauritanica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
