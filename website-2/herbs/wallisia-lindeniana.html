Wallisia lindeniana

Wallisia lindeniana, also known as Tilia lindeniana, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, belonging to the Tiliaceae family of plants.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its calming and anti-inflammatory properties, and it is said to have various health benefits, including reducing anxiety and stress, improving sleep quality, and soothing respiratory issues such as coughs and colds. The bioactive constituents of Wallisia lindeniana include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Herbal preparations made from the plant include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to promote relaxation, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of insomnia and respiratory conditions.
