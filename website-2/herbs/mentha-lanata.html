Mentha lanata

Mentha lanata, commonly known as Woolly Mint, is a perennial herb belonging to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its grayish, woolly leaves and purple flowers.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include menthone, limonene, and carvone, which contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.
