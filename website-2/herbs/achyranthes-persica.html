Achyranthes persica

Achyranthes persica, also known as the Persian bugloss, is a perennial herb belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

Traditionally, the plant has been used in medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and inflammation, due to its anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and antipyretic properties. The bioactive constituents of Achyranthes persica include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the plant is commonly prepared into decoctions, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat a range of health issues, including arthritis and joint pain.
