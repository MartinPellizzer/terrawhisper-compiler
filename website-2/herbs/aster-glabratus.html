Aster glabratus

Aster glabratus, also known as smooth aster, is a perennial plant species belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, as well as its ability to support the immune system and alleviate digestive issues. Bioactive constituents found in Aster glabratus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
