Crassula capensis

Crassula capensis, also known as the Cape jade or waterwise flower, is a succulent plant species native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

The bioactive constituents of Crassula capensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
