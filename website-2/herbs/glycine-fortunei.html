Glycine fortunei

Glycine fortunei, also known as fortune's glycine, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family, native to China and Japan.

The plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and respiratory issues, due to its anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Glycine fortunei include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which have been shown to have immunomodulatory, antimicrobial, and anti-cancer activities.
