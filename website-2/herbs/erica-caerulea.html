classification

Erica caerulea is a species of flowering plant that belongs to the family Ericaceae. This family is commonly known as the heath family, and it comprises a wide range of plants found in heath and moorland habitats. The Ericaceae family is further divided into 30 to 40 genera, including Erica, which is one of the largest genera within the family.
