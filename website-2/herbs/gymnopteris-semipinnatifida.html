Gymnopteris semipinnatifida

Gymnopteris semipinnatifida is a species of fern, specifically a type of polypod fern, commonly found in tropical regions.

The bioactive constituents of Gymnopteris semipinnatifida include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
