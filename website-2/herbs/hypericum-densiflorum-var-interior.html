What is Hypericum densiflorum var. interior?

Hypericum densiflorum var. interior is a variety of Hypericum densiflorum, a species of flowering plant in the family Hypericaceae.

This variety is a perennial shrub with an erect stem, reaching heights of 0.5-1.5 meters, and has a woody base, with a density of small, white flowers in axillary clusters, blooming in summer. The leaves are opposite, simple, elliptical, and 1.5-3.5 cm long, with a glabrous adaxial surface and a tomentose abaxial surface. The fruit is a capsule, 5-7 mm long, containing 3-5 seeds.

The variety typically thrives in woodland environments and is native to the southeastern United States.
