Urochloa turbinata

Urochloa turbinata, also known as buffalo grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The bioactive constituents of Urochloa turbinata include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
