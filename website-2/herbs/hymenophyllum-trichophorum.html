Hymenophyllum trichophorum, also known as the net veined filmy fern, is a species of fern in the family Hymenophyllaceae, characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds and net-like venation.

The bioactive constituents of Hymenophyllum trichophorum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
