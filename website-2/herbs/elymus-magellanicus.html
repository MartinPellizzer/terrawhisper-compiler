Elymus magellanicus

Elymus magellanicus, also known as Magellanic wildrye, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues, fever, and inflammation, due to its purported health benefits. The bioactive constituents of Elymus magellanicus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.
