Pinus fabri, also known as Chinese pine or Formosa pine, is a species of pine tree native to China and Taiwan.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include terpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Pinus fabri is commonly used to make herbal preparations such as tea, oil, and resin, which are used to treat coughs, sore throats, and bronchitis, as well as to promote wound healing and reduce inflammation.
