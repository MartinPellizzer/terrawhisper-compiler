Satureja myrtifolia

Satureja myrtifolia, also known as Crete savory or Greek savory, is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

This plant has been traditionally used in medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, respiratory problems, and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Satureja myrtifolia include carvacrol, thymol, and rosmarinic acid, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Satureja myrtifolia is commonly used to make herbal teas, infusions, and essential oils, which are used to treat various health issues, including indigestion, colds, and sore throats.
