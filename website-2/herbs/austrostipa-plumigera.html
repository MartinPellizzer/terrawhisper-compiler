classification

Austrostipa plumigera is classified as a species of grass in the family Poaceae. It belongs to the genus Austrostipa, which is a group of grasses native to Australia. The species name plumigera is derived from the Latin words 'pluma,' meaning feather, and 'gera,' meaning bearing, referring to the feathery plumes of the plant. This classification places Austrostipa plumigera within the larger kingdom of Plantae, specifically within the division Magnoliophyta and class Liliopsida. This classification helps to identify and distinguish Austrostipa plumigera from other species of grass.
