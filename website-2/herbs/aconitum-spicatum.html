Aconitum spicatum

The bioactive constituents of Aconitum spicatum include alkaloids such as aconitine, mesaconitine, and hypaconitine, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to prepare herbal remedies such as tinctures, infusions, and ointments, although its use requires caution due to its potential toxicity and should be administered under the guidance of a qualified healthcare professional.
