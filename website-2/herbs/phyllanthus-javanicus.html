Phyllanthus javanicus

The bioactive constituents of P. javanicus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Phyllanthus javanicus is commonly prepared as a decoction or infusion, often in the form of tea, or as a capsule or tablet, to take advantage of its health benefits.
