Althaea rosea

Althaea rosea, also known as the pink marshmallow, is a perennial herb belonging to the mallow family (Malvaceae) and is native to Europe and Western Asia.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with health benefits including soothing digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and acting as a natural demulcent to protect and heal mucous membranes. The bioactive constituents of Althaea rosea include mucilages, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

Althaea rosea is commonly prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and extracts, as well as being used in topical applications such as creams and ointments to alleviate skin irritations and wounds.
