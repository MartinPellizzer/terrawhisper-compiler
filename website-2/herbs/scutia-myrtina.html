Scutia myrtina

Scutia myrtina, also known as the devil tree, is a medicinal plant native to the tropical forests of Asia.

The plant has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and skin infections, due to its antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, and antiseptic properties, which promote wound healing and reduce pain. Scutia myrtina contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
