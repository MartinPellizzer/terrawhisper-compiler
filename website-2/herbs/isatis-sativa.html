classification

Isatis sativa belongs to the Brassicaceae family of plants. This family is commonly known as the mustard or cabbage family. Isatis sativa is a perennial herb native to Europe and Asia. The plant is also known as dyer's rocket or woad. It is closely related to other plants in the same family, such as cabbage and broccoli.
