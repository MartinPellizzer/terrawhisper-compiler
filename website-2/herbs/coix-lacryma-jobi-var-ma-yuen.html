What is Coix lacryma-jobi var. ma-yuen?

Coix lacryma-jobi var. ma-yuen is a variety of Coix lacryma-jobi, a species of grass belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is an annual or perennial herb with a height of up to 2.5 meters, featuring a robust stem and large, ovate leaves that are 10-30 cm long and 5-15 cm wide. The leaves are dark green, glaucous and have a wavy margin. The plant produces spathes that enclose a cluster of flowers, which are arranged in a terminal inflorescence.

The flowers are yellowish-brown and 2-3 cm long, with a distinctive, tear-shaped (lacrimal) seed called a caryopsis that is used in traditional medicine.
