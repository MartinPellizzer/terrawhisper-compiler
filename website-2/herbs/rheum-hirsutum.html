Rheum hirsutum

Rheum hirsutum, also known as Chinese rhubarb or hairy rhubarb, is a type of perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of China and Tibet.

The bioactive constituents of Rheum hirsutum include anthraquinones, flavonoids, and tannins, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
