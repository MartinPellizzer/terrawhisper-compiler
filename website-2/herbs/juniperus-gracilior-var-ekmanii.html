What is Juniperus gracilior var. ekmanii?

Juniperus gracilior var. ekmanii is a taxonomic variety of the species Juniperus gracilior, belonging to the family Cupressaceae.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically growing 5-10 meters tall, with a conical to pyramidal shape and a straight to slightly curved trunk. The leaves are scale-like, dark green to glaucous, and 0.5-1 cm long, arranged in whorls of 3. The bark is reddish-brown, becoming grayish-brown with age, and is fibrous and peeling in thin layers.

The cones are small, 0.5-1.5 cm long, and reddish-brown to brown, containing 2-3 seeds each.

What are the therapeutic properties this herb?

The main therapeutic properties of Juniperus gracilior var. ekmanii are rooted in its antioxidant capacity, which helps mitigate oxidative stress and cell damage, while also exhibiting antimicrobial activity, inhibiting the growth of various pathogens.

Furthermore, Juniperus gracilior var. ekmanii has expectorant properties, helping to relieve respiratory issues by promoting the clearance of mucus and other secretions from the lungs and airways.
