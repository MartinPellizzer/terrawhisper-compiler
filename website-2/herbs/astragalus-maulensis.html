Astragalus maulensis

Astragalus maulensis, also known as Mongolian astragalus or Huangqi, is a medicinal plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

This plant has been widely used in traditional Chinese medicine for its various health benefits, including its immunomodulatory and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to boost the immune system and alleviate symptoms of fatigue, stress, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Astragalus maulensis include polysaccharides, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Astragalus maulensis is commonly used in herbal preparations such as capsules, tablets, and teas, and is often combined with other herbs to enhance its effectiveness.
