Oncidium gloriosum

The Oncidium gloriosum, commonly known as the 'Glorious Oncidium', is a type of orchid native to the tropical forests of Central and South America.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, including the potential health benefits of reducing inflammation, relieving respiratory issues such as bronchitis and asthma, and even exhibiting antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Oncidium gloriosum include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are used to harness its therapeutic properties for various health conditions.
