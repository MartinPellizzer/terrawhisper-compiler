What is Eriodictyon trichocalyx var. lanatum?

Eriodictyon trichocalyx var. lanatum is a taxonomic variety within the Eriodictyon trichocalyx species, classified under the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its glabrous to puberulent stems, 1-4 dm in height, bearing opposite, lanceolate to ovate leaves, 2-10 cm long, with entire margins and 1-3 pairs of lobes. Leaves are glabrous or puberulent, with the lower surface having a prominent midrib and lateral veins. The inflorescence is a corymb of 2-6 flowers, each with a white to pinkish corolla, 1-2 cm in diameter, and five lobes.

Flowers are borne on a pedicel 1-2 mm long, with a calyx of 5 ovate to lanceolate lobes.
