Senna × floribunda

Senna × floribunda, also known as Alexandrian senna, is a hybrid plant belonging to the legume family (Fabaceae) and is traditionally used as a laxative due to its cathartic properties.

The health benefits of Senna × floribunda include its ability to stimulate bowel movements, relieve constipation, and aid in detoxification. The bioactive constituents of this plant include anthraquinone glycosides, such as aloe-emodin, rhein, and sennosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Senna × floribunda is commonly used to make herbal preparations like tea, capsules, and tablets, often in combination with other herbs like licorice root and peppermint, to promote digestive health and relieve symptoms of constipation.
