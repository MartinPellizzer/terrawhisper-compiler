What is Osteomeles anthyllidifolia f. subrotunda?

Osteomeles anthyllidifolia f. subrotunda is a plant taxon belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It has elliptic to ovate leaves that are 1.5-5 cm long, 1-3 cm wide, and have an acute tip, a cuneate base, and a serrate margin. The plant is a shrub that is 1-2 m tall, with a brownish-gray bark, and produces small white flowers in clusters from March to May. The flowers have 5 petals, 5 sepals, and are 1-2 mm in diameter.

The fruit is a pome, 5-7 mm in diameter, with 5 fleshy carpels and 5 seeds.
