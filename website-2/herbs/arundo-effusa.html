Arundo effusa

Arundo effusa, also known as cattail rush or giant reed, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical Africa and Asia, characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery plumes.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, making it useful in treating various conditions such as fever, cough, and wounds. The bioactive constituents of Arundo effusa include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Arundo effusa is commonly prepared as teas, infusions, or decoctions, and is also used in the production of herbal supplements and topical creams.
