Cnidium silaifolium

Cnidium silaifolium, also known as Cnidium fruit or Cnidium officinalis, is a plant species belonging to the Apiaceae family, traditionally used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The health benefits of Cnidium silaifolium include anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and analgesic properties, which have been shown to alleviate pain, improve cardiovascular health, and support immune function. In traditional Chinese medicine, Cnidium silaifolium is often prepared into herbal formulas, teas, and tinctures, and is also used in combination with other herbs, such as ginseng and astragalus, to enhance its therapeutic effects.
