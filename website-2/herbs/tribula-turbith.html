Tribula turbith

Tribula turbith, also known as Chinese caviar flower or cowhage, is a plant species native to Asia, belonging to the legume family.

Tribula turbith is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to alleviate digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing due to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Tribula turbith include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Tribula turbith is often used in traditional Chinese medicine and is available in various herbal preparations, such as capsules, teas, and infusions, as well as in topical creams and ointments for skin and wound care.
