Dryopteris himalayensis

This family is part of the larger group of plants known as the ferns, which are classified under the division Pteridophyta. The division Pteridophyta is further classified under the kingdom Plantae. The kingdom Plantae is a part of the domain Eukaryota, which includes all organisms with complex cells. This classification is based on the plant's characteristics and evolutionary history.
