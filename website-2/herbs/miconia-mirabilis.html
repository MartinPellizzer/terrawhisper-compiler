Miconia mirabilis

Miconia mirabilis is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae, commonly known as the "mirabilis" or "cloud forest" tree.

The bioactive constituents of Miconia mirabilis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Miconia mirabilis is often prepared as a tea or infusion, and in some cases, it is used in combination with other herbs to make tinctures, extracts, and capsules for various health applications.
