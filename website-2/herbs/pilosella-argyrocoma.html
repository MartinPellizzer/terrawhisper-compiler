Pilosella argyrocoma

Pilosella argyrocoma, also known as Oxeye Daisy or False Oxeye, is a perennial plant species in the sunflower family (Asteraceae) characterized by its yellow-centered, white-petaled flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Pilosella argyrocoma include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
