Ajuga pallescens

Ajuga pallescens, also known as pale bugleweed, is a perennial plant species in the mint family (Lamiaceae) native to the mountainous regions of China.

The bioactive constituents of Ajuga pallescens include phenolic compounds, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Ajuga pallescens is commonly prepared as a decoction or tincture, and is also used in the production of herbal teas, capsules, and infusions.
