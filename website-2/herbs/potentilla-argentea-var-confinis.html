What is Potentilla argentea var. confinis?

Potentilla argentea var. confinis is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It has a short, hairy stem that grows to 5-15 cm in height, with alternate, pinnate leaves divided into 5-7 pairs of leaflets. The leaves are 1-3 cm long, with a grey-green color, and are covered with dense, soft hairs. The flowers are small, 1-2 mm in diameter, with 5 petals of a yellow color, arranged in a corymb, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant produces small, red or brown fruit, aachenia, that are 3-4 mm long and contain 1-2 seeds.
