What is Rosa medioxima f. villosiuscula?

Rosa medioxima f. villosiuscula is a subspecies of Rosa medioxima, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems, 2-4 m in height, and a dense, spreading habit. The leaves are pinnately compound, with 7-9 leaflets, each 2-4 cm long, with a pointed tip and serrated margins. The flowers are pink to purple, 3-4 cm in diameter, with five petals and numerous stamens, produced in clusters.

The fruits are red to purple, 1-2 cm in diameter, with a fleshy pericarp and a single seed.
