The bioactive constituents of Dortmanna digitalifolia include cardiac glycosides, specifically digoxin and digitoxin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the plant is commonly prepared as a tincture, capsule, or extract, and is also used in teas and infusions, although its use should be closely monitored due to its potential toxicity and interactions with other medications.
