Draco cylindrica

Draco cylindrica, also known as African Bloodroot or Red Aloe, is a plant species belonging to the Asphodelaceae family, characterized by its unique red or reddish-brown sap and cylindrical stems.

Draco cylindrica contains bioactive constituents such as anthraquinones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
