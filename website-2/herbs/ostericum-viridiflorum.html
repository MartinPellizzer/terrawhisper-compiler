Ostericum viridiflorum

Ostericum viridiflorum, also known as Dongquai or Angelica, is a perennial plant belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae) that is native to East Asia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and antioxidant effects, making it a popular remedy for relieving menstrual cramps, nausea, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Ostericum viridiflorum include volatile oils, flavonoids, and coumarins, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, the plant is commonly used to make herbal teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to treat a variety of health conditions, including menstrual disorders, respiratory issues, and fever.
