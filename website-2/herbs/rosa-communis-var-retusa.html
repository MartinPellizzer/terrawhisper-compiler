What is Rosa communis var. retusa?

Rosa communis var. retusa is a botanical variety of wild rose in the family Rosaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub with a height range of 50-150 cm and a spread of 100-200 cm. The leaves are pinnate with 5-7 leaflets, 1-3 cm long, and have a leathery texture with a glabrous surface. The flowers are pink to purple, 3-5 cm in diameter, and are arranged in corymbose inflorescences.

The fruit is a red, 1-2 cm long, aggregate berry comprising 5-7 carpels.
