Sorghum avenaceum

Sorghum avenaceum, also known as broom sorghum, is a type of perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions, belonging to the family Poaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Sorghum avenaceum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
