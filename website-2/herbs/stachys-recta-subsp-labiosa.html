What is Stachys recta subsp. labiosa?

Stachys recta subsp.

labiosa is a plant species belonging to the family Lamiaceae. It is a perennial herb with a height of 30-100 cm, having erect stems and ovate, cordate leaves that are 4-12 cm long, with a cuneate base and a serrate margin.

The leaves are dark green on the upper surface and pale green on the lower surface, with a short petiole. The inflorescence is a terminal spike with purple flowers, which are bilabiate and have a zygomorphic corolla, with the upper lip being 12-15 mm long and the lower lip being 10-12 mm long.

The flowers are produced from June to September.
