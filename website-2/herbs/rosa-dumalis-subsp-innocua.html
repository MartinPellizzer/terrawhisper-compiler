What is Rosa dumalis subsp. innocua?

Rosa dumalis subsp.

innocua is a subspecies of flowering plant belonging to the family Rosaceae. It is a deciduous shrub with erect, thorny stems, and pinnate, dark green leaves, consisting of 3-5 leaflets, 1-3 cm long.

The leaves have serrated margins and are glandular-dotted. The plant produces fragrant, pink to purple flowers, 4-6 cm in diameter, with 5 petals, in clusters at the ends of the branches.

The flowers are followed by red, globose hips, 1-2 cm in diameter, containing numerous achenes.
