classification

Melaleuca cajuputi is classified as a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae. It belongs to the genus Melaleuca, which comprises over 300 species of paperbarks. Melaleuca cajuputi is native to Southeast Asia, particularly in Indonesia and Malaysia. This plant is also known as the cajeput tree or white cajeput tree. It is a medium-sized tree that grows up to 25 meters in height, with a straight trunk and a conical crown.
