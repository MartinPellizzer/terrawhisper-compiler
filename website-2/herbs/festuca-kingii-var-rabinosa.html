What is Festuca kingii var. rabinosa?

Festuca kingii var. rabinosa is a perennial grass that belongs to the family Poaceae.

It has a tufted habit, with a height range of 30-80 cm, and a leaf length of 2-5 cm, typically with a glabrous surface and a folded or involute margin. The inflorescence is a dense panicle, 5-15 cm long, composed of spikelets with 3-5 florets, each with a lemma and palea, and a 2-flowered caryopsis. The plant's distribution is limited to high-altitude alpine and sub-alpine meadows in the mountainous regions of western North America.

The specific characteristics and distribution of Festuca kingii var. rabinosa distinguish it from other varieties within the Festuca kingii complex.
