Cyclosorus kusaianus

Cyclosorus kusaianus, also known as Japanese leather fern or Kusa-iwa, is a species of fern native to Japan, characterized by its distinctive fronds with a leathery texture.

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine, offering potential health benefits such as antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antiviral properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of various ailments, including fever and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Cyclosorus kusaianus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and polysaccharides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Japanese medicine, the plant is often used to make herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and decoctions, which are used to treat a range of health conditions.
