What is Sonchus arvensis subsp. aquatilis?

Sonchus arvensis subsp.

aquatilis is a plant species that belongs to the family Asteraceae. It is a perennial herb with erect to ascending stems, 30-120 cm tall, and a rosette of leaves at the base, with leaf blades 10-30 cm long and 2-10 cm wide, having a lanceolate shape and a wavy margin.

The leaves are glabrous or nearly so, with a petiole 1-5 cm long. The plant produces yellow flower heads, 10-20 mm in diameter, with 10-20 florets, arranged in a capitulum, and an involucre of 3-5 bracts. Sonchus arvensis subsp.

aquatilis typically grows in wetlands and aquatic environments.
