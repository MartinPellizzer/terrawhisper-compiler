What is Dioscorea amazonum var. klugii?

Dioscorea amazonum var. klugii is a variety of Dioscorea amazonum, a species of yam that belongs to the family Dioscoreaceae.

Dioscorea amazonum var. klugii is a perennial climber with twining stems, dark green or reddish-brown stems and petioles, and trifoliate leaves with elliptical to ovate leaflets. Its leaves are 6-9 cm long, with 7-11 mm petioles, and its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and unisexual, produced on a spadix. The plant has tuberous roots with edible tubers, 5-8 cm long and 2-4 cm wide, and produces small, yellowish-brown berries.

Dioscorea amazonum var. klugii is native to the tropical regions of South America.
