What is Tephrosia hockii subsp. hirsutostylosa?

Tephrosia hockii subsp.

hirsutostylosa is a plant species belonging to the family Fabaceae, commonly known as the legume family. It is a shrub with a height range of 0.6-1.5 meters, featuring pubescent stems and leaves that are also pubescent, elliptical to ovate in shape, and 5-13 cm in length.

The leaves have a 2-3 cm petiole, and the margins are entire or slightly wavy. The flowers are axillary, in racemes of 4-8, and have a yellow color with a red or purple spot; they are 3-5 cm in diameter and have 5 petals.

The plant produces flat, linear seeds 4-5 mm in length, which are arranged in a 3-6 cm long pod.
