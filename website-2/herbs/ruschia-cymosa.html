Ruschia cymosa

Ruschia cymosa, also known as the Silver Carpet, is a low-growing, succulent plant species native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

The bioactive constituents of Ruschia cymosa include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
