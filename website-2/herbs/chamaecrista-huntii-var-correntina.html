What is Chamaecrista huntii var. correntina?

Chamaecrista huntii var. correntina is a plant that belongs to the family Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb with a woody base, stem length up to 3 meters and a 2-5 cm diameter. Leaves are alternate, trifoliate with leaflets 2-6 cm long, elliptic to ovate in shape, with a 1-2 cm long petiole and a 1-2 mm long stipule. Flowers are yellow, axillary, and in racemes, with a 2-3 mm long calyx and 6-8 mm long corolla.

Fruits are dehiscent, 2-3 cm long pods, containing 2-4 seeds.
