Brickellia rosmarinifolia

Brickellia rosmarinifolia, also known as Rosin Weed or Mexican Brickellia, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Bioactive constituents of Brickellia rosmarinifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
