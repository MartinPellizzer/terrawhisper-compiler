Solidago vulgaris

Solidago vulgaris, also known as common solidago or goldenrod, is a perennial herb belonging to the aster family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including digestive problems, respiratory infections, and skin conditions, due to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Solidago vulgaris include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
