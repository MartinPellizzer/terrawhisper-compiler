Senna perrieri

Senna perrieri, also known as the Madagascar senna, is a plant species native to Madagascar, belonging to the family Fabaceae.

The health benefits of Senna perrieri include its use as a natural laxative due to its high anthraquinone content, which helps to stimulate bowel movements and relieve constipation. The bioactive constituents of Senna perrieri include sennosides, rhein, and aloe-emodin, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Senna perrieri is often used to prepare teas, capsules, and tinctures, which are commonly used to treat digestive issues and promote gut health.
