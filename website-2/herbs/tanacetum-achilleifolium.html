Tanacetum achilleifolium

Tanacetum achilleifolium, also known as Pyrethrum or Achillea millefolium's relative, is a flowering plant species in the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

This plant is valued for its medicinal and insecticidal properties, with potential health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which can aid in the treatment of various conditions such as fever, headaches, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Tanacetum achilleifolium include pyrethrins, flavonoids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which are responsible for its therapeutic and insecticidal properties.
