Rhododendron menziesii

Rhododendron menziesii, also known as Menzies' rhododendron, is a species of flowering plant in the heath family (Ericaceae).

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine due to its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in reducing inflammation, soothing respiratory issues, and supporting the immune system. Rhododendron menziesii is commonly used to make herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and extracts, often employed in traditional medicine to treat colds, coughs, and other respiratory ailments.
