Nananthus loganii

Nananthus loganii, also known as the "logan's nipple plant", is a small succulent plant belonging to the Aizoaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Nananthus loganii include flavonoids, such as kaempferol and quercetin, which are responsible for its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activities.

Traditionally, the plant is used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions to treat various health conditions, and it is also used in traditional medicine in some parts of Africa, where it is known as "Afrikaanse melkbos".
