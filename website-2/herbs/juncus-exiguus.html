Juncus exiguus

Juncus exiguus, commonly known as Dwarf Rush or Small Rush, is a perennial aquatic plant species that belongs to the Juncaceae family.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to soothe irritated skin, reduce joint pain, and protect against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Juncus exiguus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
