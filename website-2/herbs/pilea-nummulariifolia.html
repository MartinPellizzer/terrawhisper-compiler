Pilea nummulariifolia

Pilea nummulariifolia, also known as Money Plant or Coin Leaf, is a perennial plant species native to the tropical regions of Asia, belonging to the Urticaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Pilea nummulariifolia include flavonoids, triterpenoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
