What is Scutellaria albida subsp. subsimilis?

Scutellaria albida subsp.

subsimilis is a subspecies of flowering plant that belongs to the family Lamiaceae. It is a perennial herb with a woody base, greenish-yellow flowers in a whorled arrangement, and a calyx with a 2-lipped upper lip and a 3-lobed lower lip.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and serrated, with 5-7 pairs of veins and a hairy underside. This subspecies is characterized by its densely hairy stem, and its leaves are smaller than those of the typical subspecies Scutellaria albida.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia, where it grows in dry, rocky areas and woodlands.
