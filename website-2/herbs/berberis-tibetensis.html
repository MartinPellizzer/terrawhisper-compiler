Berberis tibetensis

Berberis tibetensis is a species of flowering plant in the barberry family (Berberidaceae), native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

The bioactive constituents of Berberis tibetensis include alkaloids, such as berberine, which is responsible for its medicinal properties, as well as other compounds like saponins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.
