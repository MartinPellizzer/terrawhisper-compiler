Lactuca winkleri

Lactuca winkleri, also known as Winkler's milk thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion tribe within the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

The bioactive constituents of Lactuca winkleri include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
