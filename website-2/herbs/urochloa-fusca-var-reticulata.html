What is Urochloa fusca var. reticulata?

The inflorescence is a panicle, typically 5-20 cm in length, with spikelets that are 1-2 cm long and contain 2-4 florets, each with a single staminate flower and a 3-awned glume.

The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, and is often found in a variety of habitats, including grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands.
