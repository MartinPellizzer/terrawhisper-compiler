Bryonia foetidissima

The bioactive constituents of Bryonia foetidissima include alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Bryonia foetidissima is commonly used in herbal preparations such as tinctures, infusions, and ointments, which can be used to treat a range of conditions, including arthritis, menstrual cramps, and skin irritations.
