What is Helichrysum nitens subsp. robynsii?

Helichrysum nitens subsp.

robynsii is a subspecies of flowering plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae. It is a perennial herb with a woody base, erect stems, and narrow, linear leaves that are 2-3 mm long and 0.5-1 mm wide.

The leaves are arranged in a distichous pattern and are covered with a dense indumentum of small, glandular trichomes. The plant produces yellow or orange daisy-like flowers with 10-20 florets, each with 5-6 mm long corolla and a yellow or orange disc, in a terminal inflorescence.

The flowers are borne on a 10-15 cm long peduncle and are visited by various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
