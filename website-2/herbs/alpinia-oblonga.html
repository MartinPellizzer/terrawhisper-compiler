Alpinia oblonga

Alpinia oblonga, also known as Galangal, is a type of rhizomatous perennial plant in the ginger family (Zingiberaceae) native to Asia.

The health benefits of Alpinia oblonga include anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which can aid in reducing fever, relieving nausea, and alleviating respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include gingerols, shogaols, and sesquiterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Alpinia oblonga is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as in traditional Asian medicines like Thai and Indonesian galangal.
