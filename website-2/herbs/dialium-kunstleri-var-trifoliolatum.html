What is Dialium kunstleri var. trifoliolatum?

Dialium kunstleri var. trifoliolatum is a plant variety within the species Dialium kunstleri, belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by trifoliate leaves, each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape, 1-3 cm long, and 0.5-1.5 cm wide, with a rounded base and a pointed apex. The plant produces yellow flowers, 1-2 cm in diameter, in axillary panicles. The flowers are pentamerous, with five sepals, five petals, and ten stamens.
