Psyllium squalidum

Psyllium squalidum, also known as Kneerant or Indian hawthorn, is a shrub-like plant species native to tropical regions of Asia, belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

The plant is valued for its numerous health benefits, including the ability to support digestive health, lower cholesterol levels, and regulate blood sugar levels, making it a potential natural remedy for various gastrointestinal issues and metabolic disorders. The bioactive constituents of Psyllium squalidum include mucilages, flavonoids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
