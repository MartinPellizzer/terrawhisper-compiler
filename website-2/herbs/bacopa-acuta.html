Bacopa acuta

Bacopa acuta, also known as Bacopa monnieri var. acuta, is a perennial aquatic plant that belongs to the family Scrophulariaceae.

The plant has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to enhance memory, cognitive function, and overall brain health, with reported benefits including improved learning, memory retention, and reduced stress levels. The bioactive constituents of Bacopa acuta include alkaloids, saponins, and bacosides, which are believed to be responsible for its neuroprotective and memory-enhancing properties.

In traditional medicine, Bacopa acuta is often prepared as a powder, capsule, or liquid extract, and is commonly used to make herbal supplements that support brain health and cognitive function.
