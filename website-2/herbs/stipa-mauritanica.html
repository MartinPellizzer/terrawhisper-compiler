Stipa mauritanica

Stipa mauritanica, also known as Mauretanian fescue or Moroccan fescue, is a species of grass that belongs to the family Poaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Stipa mauritanica include flavonoids, terpenes, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
