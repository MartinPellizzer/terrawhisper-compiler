classification

Isodon lasiocarpus is a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae. It is a perennial herb native to East Asia. The classification of Isodon lasiocarpus includes the kingdom Plantae, phylum Angiospermophyta, class Magnoliopsida, order Lamiales, family Lamiaceae, genus Isodon, and species I. lasiocarpus. This species is further divided into two varieties: I. lasiocarpus var. lasiocarpus and I. lasiocarpus var. yunnanensis. Isodon lasiocarpus is often found in mountainous regions, where it grows in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.
