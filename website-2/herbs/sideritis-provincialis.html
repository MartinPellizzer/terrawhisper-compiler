Sideritis provincialis

Sideritis provincialis, also known as Provence tea or white sage, is a perennial plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family, native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in reducing stress and promoting relaxation. The bioactive constituents of Sideritis provincialis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, such as borneol and camphor, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The plant is commonly used to make herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often consumed to support immune function, improve sleep quality, and alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression.
