Bromus reptans

Bromus reptans, also known as Slender Brome or Slender Cheatgrass, is a type of perennial grass that belongs to the Poaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Bromus reptans include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
