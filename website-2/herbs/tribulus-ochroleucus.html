Tribulus ochroleucus

Tribulus ochroleucus, also known as yellow spotted caltrop or desert horse radish, is a small annual plant belonging to the Caltrop family (Zygophyllaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Tribulus ochroleucus include alkaloids, flavonoids, saponins, and glycosides, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
