What is Saxifraga globulifera f. divaricata?

Saxifraga globulifera f. divaricata is a botanical variant of the species Saxifraga globulifera, belonging to the family Saxifragaceae.

The inflorescence is a terminal raceme bearing white flowers with 5-7 petals and 10-20 stamens. The plant grows to a height of 5-20 cm and is native to alpine regions in Asia.

The specific epithet 'divaricata' likely refers to the plant's characteristic divergent or spreading growth habit.
