What is Bernardia tamanduana var. major?

Bernardia tamanduana var. major is a variety of the plant species Bernardia tamanduana, belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is a shrub or small tree with a height of up to 8 meters, characterized by a brown and grayish-brown bark. The leaves are opposite, elliptical in shape, 4-12 cm long, and 2-5 cm wide, with a 1-2 cm long petiole, having a brown and hairy margin, and a brown and hairy underside. The plant is dioecious, with male flowers in a yellowish-green corymb, and female flowers in a yellowish-green corymb.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green capsules, 1.5-2.5 cm in diameter, containing three seeds, 1-2 cm long, and 0.5-1 cm wide.
