classification

Persicaria × fennica is a hybrid species of flowering plant, resulting from a cross between Persicaria amphibia and Persicaria lapponica. It is classified as a perennial herb, native to Northern Europe. The plant belongs to the family Polygonaceae. Persicaria × fennica is often found growing in damp, woodland environments. This hybrid species is known for its distinctive white, pink, or purple flowers.

Persicaria × fennica is primarily used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
