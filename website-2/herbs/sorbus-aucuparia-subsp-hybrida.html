What is Sorbus aucuparia subsp. hybrida?

Sorbus aucuparia subsp.

hybrida is a subspecies of the mountain ash plant, belonging to the family Rosaceae. It is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are 2.5-6 cm long, with 7-15 lobes and a finely serrated margin.

The leaves are dark green on the upper surface and pale green on the lower surface, and they are arranged oppositely on the branches. The flowers are white, 5-petaled, and 2-3 mm in diameter, and they are arranged in corymbose inflorescences.

The fruit is a red, 6-10 mm long pome that ripens in late summer.
