Stillingia macrantha

Stillingia macrantha, also known as Yellowroot, is a perennial plant native to North America, belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Stillingia macrantha include alkaloids, glycosides, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
