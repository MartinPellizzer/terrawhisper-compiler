classification

Eulophia zanzibarica is classified under the kingdom Plantae, further divided into the phylum Angiospermophyta, class Liliopsida, order Asparagales, family Orchidaceae, and genus Eulophia. It belongs to the tribe Cranichideae, subtribe Eulophiinae. The species Eulophia zanzibarica is a member of the subgenus Eulophia. The classification of Eulophia zanzibarica reflects its evolutionary relationships with other orchids. The classification system used is based on modern botanical taxonomy.
