Glyceria angustata

Glyceria angustata, also known as Slender Mannagrass or Slender Sweet-grass, is a perennial grass native to North America, belonging to the Poaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Glyceria angustata include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
