Within the Geraniaceae family, Pelargonium cradockense is classified under the genus Pelargonium, which comprises approximately 280 species of succulent plants. The genus Pelargonium is further divided into several sections, and Pelargonium cradockense is part of the section Hoarea. The species is native to South Africa and is often cultivated for its unique, decorative flowers.
