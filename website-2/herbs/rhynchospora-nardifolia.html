classification

Rhynchospora nardifolia is a species of sedge in the family Cyperaceae. It belongs to the genus Rhynchospora, which comprises around 170 species of sedges. Rhynchospora nardifolia is native to tropical Africa, specifically in countries such as Kenya, Tanzania, and the Democratic Republic of Congo. The plant is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along riverbanks, where it thrives in environments with high humidity and partial shade. As a member of the Cyperaceae family, Rhynchospora nardifolia is closely related to other species of sedges and rushes.
