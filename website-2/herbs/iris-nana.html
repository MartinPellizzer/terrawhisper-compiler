Iris nana

Iris nana, also known as Dwarf Iris or Miniature Iris, is a small, delicate flowering plant belonging to the Iridaceae family.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can aid in wound healing, reduce fever, and protect against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Iris nana include iridoid glycosides, such as iridin and irigloxin, which have been shown to possess therapeutic properties.

Traditional herbal preparations made from Iris nana include infusions, decoctions, and tinctures, which are often used to treat various ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues.
