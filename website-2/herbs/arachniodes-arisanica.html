Arachniodes arisanica

Arachniodes arisanica, also known as Taiwan fern, is a species of fern in the genus Arachniodes.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory and antiviral activities, as well as potential treatments for respiratory issues and gastrointestinal problems. The bioactive constituents of Arachniodes arisanica include flavonoids, triterpenoids, and polysaccharides, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used to make herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, which are often consumed to alleviate symptoms of colds, fever, and digestive issues.
