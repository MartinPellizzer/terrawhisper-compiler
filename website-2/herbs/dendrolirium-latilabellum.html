Dendrolirium latilabellum

Dendrolirium latilabellum, also known as Dendrobium latilabellum, is a species of orchid native to China, characterized by its unique, cylindrical pseudobulbs and fragrant white flowers.

Bioactive constituents of Dendrobium latilabellum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Dendrobium latilabellum is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine and is often prepared as a decoction or tea, as well as being formulated into capsules or tablets for convenience.
