Grammitis gracilis

Grammitis gracilis, also known as the Slender Grammitis or Lady's Hair Fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific Islands.

This plant is rich in health benefits, particularly in its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various ailments, including fever, cough, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Grammitis gracilis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Grammitis gracilis is often used to prepare teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being used in decoctions and poultices to treat various health conditions.
