Straussia mariniana

Straussia mariniana, also known as Sea Rose, is a perennial flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering various health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which can help alleviate conditions like arthritis and skin irritations. The bioactive constituents of Straussia mariniana include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Straussia mariniana is commonly prepared as a tea, capsule, or tincture, and is often combined with other herbs to create remedies for pain relief, wound healing, and digestive issues.
