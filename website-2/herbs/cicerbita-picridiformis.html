Cicerbita picridiformis

Cicerbita picridiformis, also known as Sow Thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its health benefits, which include treating digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and alleviating symptoms of fever. The bioactive constituents of Cicerbita picridiformis include triterpenoid saponins, flavonoids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Cicerbita picridiformis is often prepared as a tea or infused in oil to create a topical treatment for skin conditions and wounds, although it can also be found in some commercial herbal supplements.
