classification

Orobus hispanicus is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae. It belongs to the genus Orobus, which is a small genus of annual or biennial legumes. The species is further classified under the subfamily Faboideae, which is one of the largest subfamilies within the Fabaceae family. The classification of Orobus hispanicus reflects its botanical relationship to other legumes, such as clover and peas. Orobus hispanicus is native to the Iberian Peninsula in southwestern Europe.
