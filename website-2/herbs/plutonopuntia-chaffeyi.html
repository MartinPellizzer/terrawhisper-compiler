Plutonopuntia chaffeyi

Plutonopuntia chaffeyi, also known as the Chaffey's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including the treatment of wounds, inflammation, and skin conditions, due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. Bioactive constituents of Plutonopuntia chaffeyi include flavonoids, glycosides, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with this plant are topical creams and gels, as well as teas and tinctures, which are used to support skin health and wound healing.
