Thymus origanum

Thymus origanum, also known as Sweet Thyme, is a perennial herb belonging to the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and pink or purple flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Sweet Thyme include thymol, carvacrol, and rosmarinic acid, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
