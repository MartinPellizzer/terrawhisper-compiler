Dalea fuscescens

Dalea fuscescens, also known as Scarlet Milkpea or Purple Prairie Clover, is a perennial legume native to North America, characterized by its clusters of pink to purple flowers and nitrogen-fixing properties.

The bioactive constituents of Dalea fuscescens include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal activities.
