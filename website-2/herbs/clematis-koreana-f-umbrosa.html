What is Clematis koreana f. umbrosa?

Clematis koreana f. umbrosa is a cultivar of flowering plant belonging to the family Ranunculaceae.

It is a woody, deciduous climber with large, dark green leaves that are pinnately compound, having 5-7 leaflets. The leaves are elliptical in shape and have a cleft or notched apex. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically around 15-20 cm in diameter, with 4-6 petals and 4-6 stamens.

The flowers are borne on long, nodding panicles in late summer to early autumn.
