Silaum foliosum

Silaum foliosum, also known as Indian pennywort or longstalk pennywort, is a perennial herb native to tropical Asia and Australia, characterized by its edible and medicinal properties.

The plant has been used for centuries in traditional medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Silaum foliosum contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

In herbal medicine, Silaum foliosum is often used to make teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being incorporated into topical creams and ointments to promote wound healing and reduce inflammation.
