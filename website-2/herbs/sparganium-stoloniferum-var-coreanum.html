What is Sparganium stoloniferum var. coreanum?

Sparganium stoloniferum var. coreanum is a plant taxon classified in the genus Sparganium, family Typhaceae.

The inflorescence is a panicle of spike-like structures, with male and female flowers arranged alternately along the spikes, and the fruits are three-angled nutlets, 4-6 millimeters long.

This plant taxon is found in East Asia, typically in shallow water environments.
