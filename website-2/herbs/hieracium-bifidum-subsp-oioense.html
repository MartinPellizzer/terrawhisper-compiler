What is Hieracium bifidum subsp. oioense?

Hieracium bifidum subsp.

oioense is a taxonomic subspecies of the Hieracium bifidum species, belonging to the Asteraceae family. It has basal rosettes of linear to lanceolate leaves, 1-2 mm wide and 1-4 cm long, with entire or sparsely serrate margins, and a leafy flowering stem 3-10 cm tall, bearing 1-2 flower heads.

Each flower head has 10-20 yellow ray florets and 20-50 yellow disc florets, with a height of 2-3 mm. The plant produces achenes 2-3 mm long with a pappus of 10-15 white or pinkish bristles, 1-2 mm long. Hieracium bifidum subsp.

oioense is endemic to Hawaii.
