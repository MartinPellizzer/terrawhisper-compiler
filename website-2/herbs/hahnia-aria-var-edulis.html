What is Hahnia aria var. edulis?

Hahnia aria var. edulis is a taxonomic variety of the species Hahnia aria, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a shrub with a woody base and a height range of 0.5-2 meters, characterized by simple, alternate, and stipulate leaves with 5-7 lobes, measuring 3-6 cm in length and 2-4 cm in width. The leaves are glabrous, with serrate margins and a rounded base, bearing 5-7 pairs of primary veins. The inflorescence is a corymb, bearing 5-7 white, fragrant flowers with a diameter of 2-3 cm, each containing 5-7 stamens and 1 pistil.

The fruit is an aggregate accessory fruit, consisting of 5-7 follicles, each containing 2-3 seeds.
