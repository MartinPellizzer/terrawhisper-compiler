What is Arabis shortii var. phalacrocarpa?

Arabis shortii var. phalacrocarpa is a variety of the species Arabis shortii within the genus Arabis, which is part of the family Brassicaceae.

This plant is an annual herb with a simple stem, typically 5-10 cm in height, and a basal rosette of leaves, which are lanceolate, 2-3 cm long, and have a rounded tip and a cuneate base. The leaves are also glabrous, with a glaucous sheen, and have 2-4 pairs of lateral veins. The inflorescence is a raceme, bearing white flowers with 4 petals, 5-7 mm in diameter, and 6 stamens, 3 of which are longer than the others.

The seeds are flat, 0.5-1.5 mm long, and have a distinctive beaked shape.
