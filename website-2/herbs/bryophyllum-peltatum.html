Bryophyllum peltatum

Bryophyllum peltatum, also known as the life plant or alligator plant, is a succulent species of flowering plant in the family Crassulaceae, native to South Africa and Madagascar.

The bioactive constituents of Bryophyllum peltatum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and anthraquinones, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
