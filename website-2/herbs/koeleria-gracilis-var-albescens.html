What is Koeleria gracilis var. albescens?

Koeleria gracilis var. albescens is a plant variety that belongs to the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial grass with slender culms typically up to 30 cm tall and 1-2 mm in diameter. Leaves are linear to lanceolate, flat to folded, and 2-8 cm long, with a ligule 0.5-2.5 mm long. Inflorescence is a dense, narrow panicle, usually 2-6 cm long, with spikelets 1.5-2.5 cm long, bearing 2-5 florets.

Flowers are arranged in a regular, two-ranked pattern, with the upper floret sterile and the lower floret fertile, producing a 1-seeded caryopsis.
