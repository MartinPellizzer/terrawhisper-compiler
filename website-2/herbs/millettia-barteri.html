Millettia barteri

Millettia barteri, also known as the African millettia, is a plant species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

The plant has been traditionally used in African medicine for its potential health benefits, including the treatment of fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Millettia barteri include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional African herbal medicine, the plant is often prepared as a tea or infusion, or used in the form of a paste or salve, to harness its potential health benefits.
