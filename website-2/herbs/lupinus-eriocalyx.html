Lupinus eriocalyx

Lupinus eriocalyx, also known as Bush Lupine or Spiny Lupine, is a perennial plant species native to the southwestern United States and Mexico, characterized by its spiny stems and vibrant purple flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Lupinus eriocalyx include alkaloids, particularly lupanine and sparteine, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
