Lomatium purpureum

Lomatium purpureum, also known as Lomatium, is a perennial plant species native to North America, belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae).

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, making it useful in treating respiratory issues such as colds and flu. Bioactive constituents of Lomatium include sesquiterpenes, alkaloids, and phenylpropanoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Lomatium is often used in herbal preparations such as tinctures, infusions, and capsules, with the most common preparations being Lomatium root tincture and Lomatium root capsules, which are used to support immune function and alleviate respiratory symptoms.
