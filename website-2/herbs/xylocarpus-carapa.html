Xylocarpus carapa

Xylocarpus carapa, commonly known as the Indian cork tree or mangrove tree, is a tropical evergreen plant native to the coastal regions of the Indian and Pacific Oceans.

The bioactive constituents of Xylocarpus carapa include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
