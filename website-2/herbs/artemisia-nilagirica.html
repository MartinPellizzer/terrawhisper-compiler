Artemisia nilagirica

Artemisia nilagirica, also known as Nilagiri wormwood, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
