Cacalia mexicana

Cacalia mexicana, also known as Mexican wild lettuce or Mexican sunchokes, is a perennial plant native to Mexico and Central America, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

Its bioactive constituents, including sesquiterpene lactones and flavonoids, have been found to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial activities.
