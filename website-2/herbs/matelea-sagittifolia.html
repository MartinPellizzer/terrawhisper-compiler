Matelea sagittifolia

Matelea sagittifolia, also known as the arrowleaf milkvine or arrowhead milkvine, is a plant species belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce inflammation, improve cardiovascular health, and protect against cell damage. The bioactive constituents of Matelea sagittifolia include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Matelea sagittifolia is often used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are traditionally used to support immune function, reduce stress, and promote overall well-being.
