What is Mimosa bifurca var. desmanthoides?

Mimosa bifurca var. desmanthoides is a taxonomic variety of the species Mimosa bifurca within the genus Mimosa, belonging to the family Fabaceae.

This variety is characterized by its shrub-like habit, reaching heights of approximately 1-2 meters, with pinnate leaves consisting of 8-12 pairs of leaflets. The leaves are elliptical in shape, measuring 4-8 cm in length, and have a pale green color with a pubescent texture. Mimosa bifurca var. desmanthoides produces small, pink to red flowers in axillary racemes, with a calyx of five petals and a corolla of five petals, which are a similar size.

The plant's fruit is a pod, containing 2-4 seeds, approximately 2-3 cm in length, and is a brownish color.
