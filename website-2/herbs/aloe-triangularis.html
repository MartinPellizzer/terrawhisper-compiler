Aloe triangularis

Aloe triangularis, also known as the triangular aloe, is a succulent plant species belonging to the Aloe genus, characterized by its triangular-shaped leaves.

The health benefits of Aloe triangularis include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and wound-healing properties, making it useful in treating skin irritations, burns, and minor cuts. The bioactive constituents of Aloe triangularis include aloin, aloe-emodin, and other anthraquinones, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Aloe triangularis include topical creams and gels for skin conditions, as well as oral supplements and teas for promoting digestive health and immune system function.
