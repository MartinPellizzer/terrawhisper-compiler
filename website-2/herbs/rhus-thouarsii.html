Rhus thouarsii

Rhus thouarsii, also known as the Sooty Rhus or Madagascar Rhus, is a plant species in the Anacardiaceae family, commonly used in traditional medicine.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Rhus thouarsii is often prepared as a decoction, infusion, or tincture, and is used to treat a range of ailments, including fever, digestive issues, and respiratory problems.
