Ipomoea × multifida

Ipomoea × multifida, also known as Man of the Sea or Sea Vine, is a tropical flowering plant belonging to the morning glory family (Convolvulaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Ipomoea × multifida include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
