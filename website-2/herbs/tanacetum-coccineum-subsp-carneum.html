What is Tanacetum coccineum subsp. carneum?

Tanacetum coccineum subsp.

carneum is a plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is a perennial herb with a height range of 30-60 cm, featuring a taproot and a branched stem with linear, lanceolate to oval-shaped leaves that are 2-5 cm long and 0.5-2 cm wide.

The plant produces solitary, terminal flower heads with 10-20 florets, each measuring 1-2 cm in diameter, and having pink to red coloration. The flowers are hermaphroditic, with five-merous corollas and a white to yellowish center. Tanacetum coccineum subsp.

carneum is native to Central Asia.
