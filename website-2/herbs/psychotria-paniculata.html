Psychotria paniculata

Psychotria paniculata, also known as Kava-Kava or Kava, is a tropical plant species native to the Pacific Island region, belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

The health benefits of Psychotria paniculata include its anxiolytic and anti-inflammatory properties, which have been traditionally used to treat anxiety, insomnia, and muscle relaxation. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavokavains, kawain, and yangonin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Psychotria paniculata is often used to make Kava tea, tinctures, and capsules, which are commonly used to promote relaxation, reduce anxiety, and alleviate muscle tension.
