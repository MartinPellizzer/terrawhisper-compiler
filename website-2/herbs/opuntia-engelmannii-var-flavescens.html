What is Opuntia engelmannii var. flavescens?

Opuntia engelmannii var. flavescens is a variety of the Opuntia engelmannii species, belonging to the family Cactaceae.

It is a succulent plant characterized by its flat, pad-like stems (cladodes) that are 30-60 cm long and 10-20 cm wide, with 3-6 areoles per centimeter. The cladodes are covered with dense, yellowish-brown spines (glochids) that can be easily detached, and more prominent, sharp, yellow or brown spines up to 4 cm long. The plant produces yellow flowers with 20-25 petals, 5-7 cm in diameter, that bloom in the spring and early summer.

The fruits are yellow or orange, 2-3 cm in diameter, and contain 1-5 seeds.
