Minuartia biebersteinii

Minuartia biebersteinii, also known as Bieber's winter-cress or Bieber's rock-cress, is a perennial herb belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

In herbal medicine, Minuartia biebersteinii is often made into teas, infusions, or tinctures, and is also used in the production of herbal remedies for respiratory issues and other health conditions.
