Cyperus capitatus

Cyperus capitatus, also known as umbrella sedge, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions, belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, which can help alleviate symptoms of fever, cough, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Cyperus capitatus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
