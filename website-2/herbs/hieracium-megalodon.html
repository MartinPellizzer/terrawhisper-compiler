Hieracium megalodon

Hieracium megalodon, also known as hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae) characterized by its yellow flowers and a rosette of leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Hieracium megalodon include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
