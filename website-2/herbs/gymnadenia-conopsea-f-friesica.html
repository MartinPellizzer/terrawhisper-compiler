What is Gymnadenia conopsea f. friesica?

Botanically, Gymnadenia conopsea f. friesica has green, linear, and keeled leaves, as well as a racemose inflorescence bearing white or yellowish flowers with a yellow labellum and a distinctive, three-lobed lip. The inflorescence typically grows to a height of 30-50 cm and bears 5-15 flowers.
