Dulcamara flexuosa

Dulcamara flexuosa, also known as climbing nightshade or bittersweet nightshade, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Solanaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Dulcamara flexuosa include alkaloids, glycosides, and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Dulcamara flexuosa is commonly used to make teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to treat colds, coughs, and other respiratory complaints.
