What is Kalanchoe rosei var. variifolia?

Kalanchoe rosei var. variifolia is a plant variant of Kalanchoe rosei, belonging to the Crassulaceae family.

It is a succulent plant with stemless rosettes of leaves, 2-5 cm long, and 1-2 cm wide, with crenate margins and a dense, woolly indumentum. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a glabrous midrib and a green color turning yellowish-red during winter. Kalanchoe rosei var. variifolia produces white to pinkish flowers with 5-7 lobes, in a terminal inflorescence, from July to September.

This variant typically grows to 10-20 cm in height and 20-30 cm in width.
