Peucedanum elatior

Peucedanum elatior, also known as tall hog's fennel, is a perennial plant species in the carrot family (Apiaceae), characterized by its tall, slender stems and umbrella-shaped clusters of small white flowers.

The bioactive constituents of P. elatior include volatile oils, flavonoids, and coumarins, such as bergamotene, fenchone, and scopoletin, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
