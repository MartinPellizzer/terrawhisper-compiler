Triticum aethiopicum

Triticum aethiopicum, also known as tef, is a small-grained annual grass that belongs to the wheat family (Poaceae) and is native to Ethiopia.

The bioactive constituents of Triticum aethiopicum include phenolic acids, flavonoids, and other compounds that have been shown to exhibit antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.
