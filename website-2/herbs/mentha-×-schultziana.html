Mentha × schultziana

Mentha × schultziana, commonly known as Corn Mint, is a hybrid mint plant that belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is native to Europe.

The health benefits of Corn Mint include its ability to alleviate digestive issues, such as nausea and indigestion, due to its carminative and anti-inflammatory properties, and its potential to reduce stress and anxiety with its calming effects. The bioactive constituents of Corn Mint include menthone, menthyl acetate, and linalool, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Corn Mint is often used to make herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, and is commonly used in traditional medicine to soothe digestive issues and promote relaxation.
