Ipomoea diversifolia

The bioactive constituents of Ipomoea diversifolia include flavonoids, saponins, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made from this plant include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are used to treat various ailments, including fever, cough, and gastrointestinal disorders.
