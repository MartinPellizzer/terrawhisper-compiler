Asclepias grantii

The bioactive constituents of Asclepias grantii include alkaloids, glycosides, and cardenolides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Herbal preparations made from Asclepias grantii include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are often used to treat a range of health conditions, although further research is needed to confirm its efficacy and safety for human use.
