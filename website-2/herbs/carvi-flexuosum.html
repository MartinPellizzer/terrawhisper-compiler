Carvi flexuosum

Carvi flexuosum, also known as wild caraway or caraway, is a perennial herb belonging to the parsley family (Apiaceae).

It is valued for its potential health benefits, which include relieving digestive issues, reducing inflammation, and acting as a natural diuretic. The bioactive constituents of Carvi flexuosum include volatile oils, particularly carvone, limonene, and borneol, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids.

Wild caraway is commonly used in herbal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and tinctures, often in combination with other herbs like peppermint or lemon balm, to promote digestion and alleviate symptoms of indigestion and bloating.
