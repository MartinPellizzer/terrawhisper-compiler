Hieracium leucurolepium

Hieracium leucurolepium, also known as mountain hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae) that grows in mountainous regions.

The bioactive constituents of Hieracium leucurolepium include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
