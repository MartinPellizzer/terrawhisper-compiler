Launaea taraxacifolia

Launaea taraxacifolia, also known as the taraxacum leaf dandelion, is a type of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The bioactive constituents of Launaea taraxacifolia include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
