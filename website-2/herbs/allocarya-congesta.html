Allocarya congesta

Allocarya congesta, also known as Mexican valerian or Mexican valerian root, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico and other parts of North America.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to promote relaxation, reduce anxiety, and improve sleep quality, making it a natural remedy for stress relief and insomnia. The bioactive constituents of Allocarya congesta include valerenic acid, isovalerenic acid, and valepotriates, which are responsible for its sedative and calming effects.

Allocarya congesta is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, capsules, and tinctures, as well as in traditional medicine, where it is often used to treat restlessness, nervousness, and sleep disorders.
