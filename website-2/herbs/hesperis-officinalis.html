Hesperis officinalis

Hesperis officinalis, commonly known as Dame's Rocket, is a flowering plant in the mustard family (Brassicaceae).

This plant has been used in traditional medicine for its health benefits, which include treating respiratory issues such as bronchitis and coughs, as well as its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. The bioactive constituents of Hesperis officinalis include isothiocyanates, flavonoids, and terpenes, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
