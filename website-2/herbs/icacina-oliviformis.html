Icacina oliviformis

Icacina oliviformis, also known as the Icacina or African caterpillar plant, is a plant species native to tropical Africa, belonging to the family Icacinaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Icacina oliviformis include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
