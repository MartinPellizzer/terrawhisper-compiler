What is Persoonia myrtilloides subsp. cunninghamii?

Persoonia myrtilloides subsp.

The leaves have a leathery texture and are oppositely arranged on the stem. The white, star-shaped flowers are solitary and axillary, with a diameter of 3-6 centimeters, and are pollinated by bees and other insects. Persoonia myrtilloides subsp.

cunninghamii is a rare and endangered subspecies due to habitat loss and fragmentation.
