Ranunculus pekinensis

Ranunculus pekinensis, also known as Peony root or She gan cao, is a perennial plant native to East Asia, with its roots being used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may aid in the treatment of various conditions such as fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Ranunculus pekinensis include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, the plant is used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and powders, which are used to treat a range of health issues, including menstrual disorders and respiratory problems.
