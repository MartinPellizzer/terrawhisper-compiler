What is Carex limosa var. rariflora?

Carex limosa var. rariflora is a rare variant of the sedge species Carex limosa, belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It has a perennial, rhizomatous habit with culms that typically reach 40-120 cm in height. The leaves are flat, linear, and typically 3-8 mm wide, with a glabrous upper surface and a hairy lower surface. The inflorescence is a terminal, cylindrical spike with 2-7 racemes, bearing 3-6 spikelets per raceme.

The spikelets are 1-2 cm long, with 2-5 sterile bracts and 1-2 fertile flowers per spikelet.
