Opuntia ganderi

Opuntia ganderi, also known as the Gander's prickly pear cactus, is a species of cactus belonging to the Opuntia genus, characterized by its flat, rounded stems (pads) and sharp spines.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including reducing inflammation, improving wound healing, and providing antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Opuntia ganderi include flavonoids, such as kaempferol and quercetin, as well as vitamins and minerals like vitamin C and beta-carotene, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Opuntia ganderi is often used to make herbal preparations, such as teas, tinctures, and infusions, as well as ointments and creams for topical application.
