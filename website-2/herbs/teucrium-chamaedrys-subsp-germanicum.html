What is Teucrium chamaedrys subsp. germanicum?

Teucrium chamaedrys subsp.

germanicum is a subspecies of perennial herb belonging to the family Lamiaceae. It has a woody base, with erect stems up to 50 cm tall, bearing ovate to lanceolate leaves that are 2-5 cm long and have a pointed tip.

The leaves are dark green, glabrous, and have a crenate margin. The inflorescence is a terminal spike, bearing small, tubular flowers that are 5-7 mm long and have a greenish-yellow color.

The flowers are clustered in whorls of 3-4, giving the plant a distinctive, dense appearance.
