Seymeria coahuilana

Seymeria coahuilana is a perennial herbaceous plant in the borage family (Boraginaceae), native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The bioactive constituents of Seymeria coahuilana include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
