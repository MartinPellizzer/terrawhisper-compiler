What is Astragalus lentiginosus var. ineptus?

Astragalus lentiginosus var. ineptus is a plant species that belongs to the family Fabaceae.

It is a perennial legume with a taproot, growing up to 30 cm tall and 20 cm wide. The leaves are alternate, pinnate, and 2-5 cm long, composed of 10-20 pairs of leaflets that are 2-5 mm long. The flowers are pink or purple, 8-10 mm long, and borne in a raceme of 10-20 flowers.

The pods are 2-3 cm long, flat, and dehiscent, containing 2-4 seeds.
