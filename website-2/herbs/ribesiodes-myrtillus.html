Ribesiodes myrtillus

Ribesiodes myrtillus, also known as the bilberry, is a type of small, rounded fruit-bearing shrub belonging to the heath family (Ericaceae).

The bilberry is rich in health benefits, including its potential to improve cardiovascular health, reduce inflammation, and protect against oxidative stress due to its high antioxidant content. The bioactive constituents of the bilberry include anthocyanins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Bilberries are commonly used to make jams, preserves, and herbal teas, as well as being incorporated into capsules and supplements, which are often used to support eye health and alleviate symptoms of arthritis.
