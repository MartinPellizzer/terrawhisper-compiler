Helianthus annuus lenticularis

Helianthus annuus lenticularis, commonly known as sunflower seed, is a plant species that belongs to the Asteraceae family and is widely cultivated for its edible seeds.

The health benefits of Helianthus annuus lenticularis include its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease and cancer. The most common herbal preparations made with Helianthus annuus lenticularis include sunflower seed oil, which is used as a dietary supplement to support cardiovascular health, and sunflower seed extracts, which are used in traditional medicine to treat a range of ailments.
