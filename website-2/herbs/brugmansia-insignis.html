Brugmansia insignis

Brugmansia insignis, also known as Angel's Trumpet, is a flowering plant species that is native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, and its health benefits include anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and sedative effects, which can help alleviate symptoms of anxiety, insomnia, and muscle spasms. The bioactive constituents of Brugmansia insignis include tropane alkaloids, such as scopolamine and hyoscyamine, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its pharmacological properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Brugmansia insignis include teas, tinctures, and infusions, often used to treat anxiety, insomnia, and pain, although its use should be cautioned due to potential side effects and interactions.
