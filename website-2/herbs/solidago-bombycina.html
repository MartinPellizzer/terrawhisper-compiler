Solidago bombycina

Solidago bombycina, also known as silky goldenrod, is a perennial herb belonging to the Asteraceae family, native to North America.

The bioactive constituents of Solidago bombycina include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
