What is Agrostis tenuis var. setulosa?

Agrostis tenuis var. setulosa is a botanical variety of the grass species Agrostis tenuis, belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial grass with erect, smooth leaves, 2-15 mm long, and a glabrous sheath. The inflorescence is a dense panicle, 2-10 cm long, with spikelets of 3-5 florets. Each spikelet has one fertile floret, with a lemma and palea, and two sterile florets.

It is found in temperate and subalpine zones, typically in moist, shaded areas.
