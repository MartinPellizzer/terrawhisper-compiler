Telinaria lobelii

Telinaria lobelii, also known as Lobelia, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions, valued for its medicinal properties.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include alkaloids, flavonoids, and glycosides, particularly lobeline, lobelanine, and lobelidine, which have been shown to exhibit pharmacological activities.

The most common herbal preparations made from Telinaria lobelii include teas, tinctures, and capsules, often used to treat respiratory conditions, such as asthma and chronic bronchitis, as well as to aid in smoking cessation.
