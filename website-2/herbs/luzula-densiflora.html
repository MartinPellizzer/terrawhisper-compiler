Luzula densiflora

Luzula densiflora, commonly known as dense-flowered woodrush, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan and other parts of East Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Luzula densiflora include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
