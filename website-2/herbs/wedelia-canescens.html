Wedelia canescens

Wedelia canescens, also known as bitter wedelia or white butterfly weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

The bioactive constituents of Wedelia canescens include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
