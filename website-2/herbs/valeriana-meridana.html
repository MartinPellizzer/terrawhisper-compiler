Valeriana meridana

Valeriana meridana, also known as the Southern valerian, is a perennial plant belonging to the Valerianaceae family, characterized by its fragrant, white, and pink flowers.

The health benefits of Valeriana meridana include its anxiolytic and sedative properties, making it a natural remedy for stress relief, insomnia, and anxiety disorders. The bioactive constituents of this plant include valerenic acid, valepotriates, and isovalerenic acid, which are responsible for its sedative and antispasmodic effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Valeriana meridana include teas, tinctures, and capsules, often used to promote relaxation and improve sleep quality.
