Lycopus maackianus

Lycopus maackianus, also known as Chinese water-horehound or Chinese bugleweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Lycopus maackianus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.
