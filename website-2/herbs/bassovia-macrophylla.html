Bassovia macrophylla

Bassovia macrophylla, also known as the "cat's claw vine," is a tropical plant species native to the Americas, characterized by its climbing habit and large, dark green leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Bassovia macrophylla include alkaloids, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
