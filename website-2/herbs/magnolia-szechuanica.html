Magnolia szechuanica

Magnolia szechuanica, also known as Szechuan magnolia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Magnoliaceae, native to China.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential in reducing inflammation and alleviating symptoms of fever, cough, and respiratory issues, such as bronchitis and pneumonia. The bioactive constituents of Magnolia szechuanica include magnolol, honokiol, and other lignans, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

In traditional Chinese medicine, the plant is often used to make tea, tinctures, and decoctions, which are commonly used to treat respiratory and digestive issues, as well as to promote relaxation and reduce stress.
