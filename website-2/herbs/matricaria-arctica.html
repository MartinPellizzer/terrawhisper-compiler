The bioactive constituents of Matricaria arctica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, including matricine, which is responsible for its medicinal properties.

Matricaria arctica is commonly used to make teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are used to treat a variety of health issues, as well as being added to skincare products for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.
