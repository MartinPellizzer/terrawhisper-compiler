Dioscorea nepalensis

Dioscorea nepalensis, also known as the Nepal yam, is a species of yam native to the Himalayan region.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, as well as the potential to lower blood pressure and cholesterol levels. The bioactive constituents of Dioscorea nepalensis include saponins, phenolic compounds, and flavonoids, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made from this plant include dried root extracts and capsules, as well as traditional Ayurvedic and Unani medicines that utilize the yam's roots and tubers.
