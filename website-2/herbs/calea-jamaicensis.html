Calea jamaicensis

Calea jamaicensis, also known as Mexican Dream Herb, is a plant species native to Mexico and Central America, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal and psychoactive properties, with reported health benefits including relief from anxiety, insomnia, and stress, as well as its potential in treating gastrointestinal disorders. The bioactive constituents of Calea jamaicensis include flavonoids, sesquiterpenes, and alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Calea jamaicensis is often prepared as a tea or tincture, and is sometimes used in combination with other herbs to enhance its effects and promote relaxation.
