What is Hypericum nitidum subsp. exile?

Hypericum nitidum subsp.

exile is a plant taxon that belongs to the family Hypericaceae. It is characterized by its shrub-like habit, typically growing up to 1 meter in height, with a spreading or erect stem, and glabrous to subglabrous leaves that are narrowly ovate to ovate-lanceolate in shape, measuring 1.5-4.5 cm in length, and have 5-9 pairs of lateral veins.

The leaves are oppositely arranged on the stem, and are often glaucous on the lower surface. The inflorescences are cymose or paniculate, bearing 3-5 yellow flowers, each approximately 1-1.5 cm in diameter, with 5 petals and 5 stamens.

The flowers are dioecious, with male and female flowers produced on separate plants.
