Lactuca biennis

Lactuca biennis, commonly known as tall wild lettuce or biennial wild lettuce, is a perennial plant native to North America, characterized by its tall, branching stem and large, coarse leaves.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, which include reducing inflammation, alleviating anxiety and insomnia, and providing relief from digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Lactuca biennis include lactucarium, a glycoside responsible for its sedative and anti-inflammatory effects, as well as flavonoids and alkaloids, which contribute to its overall medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Lactuca biennis is often prepared as a tincture, tea, or capsule, and is used to promote relaxation, soothe digestive issues, and support overall well-being.
