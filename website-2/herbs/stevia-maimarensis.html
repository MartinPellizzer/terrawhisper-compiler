Stevia maimarensis

Stevia maimarensis, also known as the Bolivian stevia, is a perennial plant native to South America, belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Stevia maimarensis include steviol glycosides, such as stevioside and rebaudioside, which are responsible for its unique sweetness and medicinal properties.
