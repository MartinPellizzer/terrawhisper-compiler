Tillandsia paraibica

Tillandsia paraibica is a type of epiphytic bromeliad, a plant that grows on other surfaces without deriving nutrients from them.

The bioactive constituents of Tillandsia paraibica include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
