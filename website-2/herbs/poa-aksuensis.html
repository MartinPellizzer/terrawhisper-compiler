Poa aksuensis

Poa aksuensis, also known as Aksu meadow grass, is a type of perennial grass that belongs to the family Poaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Poa aksuensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
