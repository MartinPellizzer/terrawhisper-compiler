Pandanus diodon

Pandanus diodon, also known as the spotted pandanus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Pandanaceae, native to tropical Australia.

The bioactive constituents of Pandanus diodon include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
