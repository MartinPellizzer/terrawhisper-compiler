Scutellaria cordifolia

Scutellaria cordifolia, also known as Heartleaf Skullcap, is a perennial plant native to East Asia, characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and blue to purple flowers.

The plant has been traditionally used to treat various health conditions, including anxiety, insomnia, and inflammation, due to its potential anti-anxiety, sedative, and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Scutellaria cordifolia include flavonoids, such as baicalein and baicalin, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

Heartleaf Skullcap is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine and is often prepared as a tea, tincture, or capsule, although its use should be consulted with a healthcare professional before consumption.
