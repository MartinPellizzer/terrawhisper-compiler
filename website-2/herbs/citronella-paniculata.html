Citronella paniculata

The bioactive constituents of Citronella paniculata include citronellal, citronellol, and limonene, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Citronella paniculata is often used to make teas, infusions, and essential oil preparations, which are commonly used to treat respiratory issues, skin conditions, and as a natural insect repellent.
