What is Tripogon trifidus var. sivarajanii?

Tripogon trifidus var. sivarajanii is a taxon of grass, classified within the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tufted habit, slender culms up to 50 cm tall, and linear-lanceolate leaves, 10-20 cm long, and 1-2 mm wide. The inflorescence is a panicle, 5-10 cm long, with a central rachis and two lateral rachillae. Spikelets are 5-8 mm long, with two fertile florets, and a single sterile floret.

The plant is endemic to the Western Ghats, India, and is a rare variant of the species Tripogon trifidus.
