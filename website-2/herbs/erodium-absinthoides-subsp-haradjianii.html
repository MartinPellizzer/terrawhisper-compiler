What is Erodium absinthoides subsp. haradjianii?

Erodium absinthoides subsp.

haradjianii is a subspecies of the species Erodium absinthoides, belonging to the family Geraniaceae. It is an annual herbaceous plant, 10-30 cm tall, with erect stems, simple, alternate leaves and deeply divided leaflets.

The plant produces pink to purple flowers, 6-8 mm in diameter, with 5 petals and 10 stamens, in axillary clusters. The flowers are bilaterally symmetrical and have a characteristic spur-like structure.

The fruit is a schizocarp, a type of dry dehiscent fruit, comprising 5 mericarps that split apart at maturity.
