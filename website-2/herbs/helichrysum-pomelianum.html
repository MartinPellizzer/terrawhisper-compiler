Helichrysum pomelianum

Helichrysum pomelianum is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae), commonly known for its fragrant, yellow flowers and its traditional use in herbal medicine.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of anxiety, stress, and wounds. Bioactive constituents of Helichrysum pomelianum include sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The plant is commonly used to make teas, tinctures, and essential oils, which are used to promote relaxation, reduce pain and inflammation, and support wound healing.
