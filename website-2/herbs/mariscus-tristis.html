Mariscus tristis

Mariscus tristis, also known as Marsh Cattail or Yellow Flag, is a perennial aquatic plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Mariscus tristis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
