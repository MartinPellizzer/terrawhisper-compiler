Eucalyptus × tetragona

Eucalyptus × tetragona, commonly known as the silver dollar gum or Wollemi pine, is a rare and unique plant species that is a natural hybrid of Eucalyptus melliodora and Eucalyptus piperita.

This plant has been used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as relieving respiratory issues, reducing inflammation, and soothing coughs due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Eucalyptus × tetragona include eucalyptol, a key compound responsible for its decongestant and antimicrobial effects, as well as other volatile oils and terpenoids.

Eucalyptus × tetragona is commonly used in herbal preparations such as cough drops, inhalers, and teas, as well as in aromatherapy to promote relaxation and alleviate respiratory issues.
