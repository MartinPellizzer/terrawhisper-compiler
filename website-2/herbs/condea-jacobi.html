Condea jacobi

Condea jacobi, also known as Jacob's bush, is a shrub native to Australia that belongs to the Lamiaceae family.

The plant has been traditionally used in medicine for its various health benefits, including antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, which can aid in treating wounds, skin conditions, and respiratory issues. Condea jacobi contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, phenolic acids, and diterpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
