Sesleria autumnalis

Sesleria autumnalis, also known as autumn moor-grass, is a perennial grass species that belongs to the family Poaceae.

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may help alleviate respiratory issues and wounds. Sesleria autumnalis contains bioactive constituents such as sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The plant is commonly used in traditional herbal preparations, including teas and infusions, to treat respiratory issues, and is sometimes combined with other herbs to enhance its effects.

classification

Within the genus Sesleria, it is classified as Sesleria autumnalis, and is also known as autumnal sesleria.
