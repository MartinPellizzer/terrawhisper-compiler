Huperzia polytrichoides

Huperzia polytrichoides, also known as Huperzia serrata or Chinese club moss, is a perennial plant belonging to the Lycopodiaceae family.

The plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to treat various health conditions, including Alzheimer's disease, anxiety, and depression, due to its potential neuroprotective and anxiolytic properties. The bioactive constituents of Huperzia polytrichoides include huperzine A, a potent inhibitor of acetylcholinesterase, as well as other alkaloids and flavonoids.

In herbal medicine, Huperzia polytrichoides is often prepared as a standardized extract or a tablet, and is commonly used to enhance cognitive function, reduce symptoms of anxiety and depression, and support the treatment of neurodegenerative diseases.
