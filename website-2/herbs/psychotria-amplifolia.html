Psychotria amplifolia

Psychotria amplifolia, also known as Irama, is a plant species native to the Pacific Islands, belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Psychotria amplifolia include alkaloids, particularly iramidine and psychoal, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids.
