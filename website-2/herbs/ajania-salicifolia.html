Ajania salicifolia

Ajania salicifolia, also known as Tanacetum salicifolium or Russian tansy, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its silver-gray foliage and yellow flowers.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, including its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic effects, which are believed to contribute to its potential health benefits, such as relieving digestive issues and reducing pain. The bioactive constituents of Ajania salicifolia include sesquiterpenes, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which have been identified as the primary compounds responsible for its pharmacological activities.

In traditional medicine, the plant is commonly used to prepare herbal teas, infusions, and extracts, which are used to treat a range of health issues, including digestive problems, fever, and respiratory infections.
