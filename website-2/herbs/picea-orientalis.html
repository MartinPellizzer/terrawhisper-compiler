Picea orientalis

Picea orientalis, also known as the Oriental spruce, is a species of evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of southeastern Europe and western Asia.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, terpenes, and phenolic acids, such as isoprenoids and sesquiterpenes, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
