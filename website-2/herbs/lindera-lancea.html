Lindera lancea

Lindera lancea, also known as Chinese Spicebush, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia, belonging to the family Lauraceae.

The bioactive constituents of Lindera lancea include lignans, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
