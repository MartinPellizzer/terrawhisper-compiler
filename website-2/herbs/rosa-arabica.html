Rosa arabica

Rosa arabica, also known as the Arabian rose, is a medicinal plant species in the Rosaceae family, known for its distinctive fragrance and various health benefits.

The health benefits of Rosa arabica include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, such as geraniol and linalool, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Rosa arabica include rosewater, rose oil, and dried rose petals, which are used in traditional medicine, perfumery, and culinary applications.
