Blumea spinidens

Blumea spinidens, also known as Spiny Thistle or Indian Thistle, is a perennial plant species native to India and Southeast Asia, belonging to the family Asteraceae.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which have been shown to exhibit antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and anticancer activities.

Blumea spinidens is commonly used in herbal preparations such as decoctions, infusions, and tinctures, as well as in traditional Indian medicine, Ayurveda, to support overall health and well-being.
