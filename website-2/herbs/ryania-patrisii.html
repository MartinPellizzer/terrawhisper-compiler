Ryania patrisii

Ryania patrisii, also known as Ryania, is a shrub-like plant native to South America, specifically in the tropical regions of Brazil and Peru.

The health benefits of Ryania include its analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and antispasmodic properties, which have been traditionally used to relieve pain, reduce fever, and alleviate digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Ryania include sesquiterpene glycosides, triterpenoids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Ryania is commonly prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, as well as being used in topical applications, such as liniments and ointments, to treat various health conditions.
