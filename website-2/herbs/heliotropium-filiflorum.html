Heliotropium filiflorum

Heliotropium filiflorum, also known as Mexican heliotrope, is a small, annual plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

The bioactive constituents of Heliotropium filiflorum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.
