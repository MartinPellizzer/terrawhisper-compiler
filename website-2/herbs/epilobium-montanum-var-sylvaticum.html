What is Epilobium montanum var. sylvaticum?

Epilobium montanum var. sylvaticum is a perennial plant that belongs to the family Onagraceae, commonly known as the willowherb family.

It is a variant of Epilobium montanum, a species native to Europe and Western Asia. Botanically, Epilobium montanum var. sylvaticum is characterized by its hairy, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are 2-10 cm long and 1-3 cm wide, arranged oppositely on the stem. The inflorescence is a terminal, dense, and pyramidal spike, bearing white to pinkish flowers with four petals, 1-2 cm long, and four stamens.

The plant grows up to 30-90 cm tall, with a taproot and fibrous roots.
