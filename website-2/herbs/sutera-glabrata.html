Sutera glabrata

Sutera glabrata, also known as Sutera, is a flowering plant in the phlox family that is native to Southeast Asia.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties, and it has been traditionally used to treat various health issues, including fever, cough, and respiratory problems, due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Sutera glabrata include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

In traditional medicine, Sutera is often prepared as a decoction, infusion, or tincture, and it is also used in combination with other herbs to treat a range of health conditions.
