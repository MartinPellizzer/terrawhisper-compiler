Lantana strigosa

Lantana strigosa, also known as yellow button bush, is a shrub-like plant species belonging to the Lantana genus, native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its health benefits, including reducing inflammation and alleviating symptoms of fever, cough, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Lantana strigosa include lantanin, flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal preparations, Lantana strigosa is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are commonly used to treat respiratory and skin conditions, as well as to support immune function.
