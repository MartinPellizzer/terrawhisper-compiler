Tragopogon lanatus

Tragopogon lanatus, commonly known as the woolly meadow salsify or yellow salsify, is a biennial plant belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae) native to Europe and Western Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Tragopogon lanatus include inulin, sesquiterpenoids, and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
