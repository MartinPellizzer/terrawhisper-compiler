Feuilleea fendleriana

Feuilleea fendleriana, also known as Devil's Claw, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa, belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including pain relief and anti-inflammatory properties, which can alleviate conditions such as arthritis and other musculoskeletal disorders. The bioactive constituents of Devil's Claw include harpagoside, a iridoid glycoside responsible for its medicinal properties, as well as other compounds like harpagidin and proanthocyanidins.

Traditionally, the plant is prepared as a decoction or a tincture, and is also used in capsule or tablet form, making it a popular ingredient in herbal supplements for pain management and inflammation relief.
