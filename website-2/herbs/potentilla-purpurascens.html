Potentilla purpurascens

Potentilla purpurascens, also known as Roseroot or Mountain Cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, traditionally used in herbal medicine for its various health benefits.

The plant is valued for its adaptogenic and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help alleviate stress, improve sleep quality, and reduce inflammation in the body. The bioactive constituents of Roseroot include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and coumarins, such as rosarin and rosavin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Roseroot is commonly prepared as a tincture, tea, or syrup, and is often used to treat anxiety, fatigue, and digestive issues.
