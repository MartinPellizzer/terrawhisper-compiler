Phyllolobium lasaense

Phyllolobium lasaense, also known as Lasagao or Chinese knotweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the family Fabaceae.

The plant is valued for its various health benefits, including the treatment of fever, rheumatism, and respiratory issues, due to its anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and expectorant properties. The bioactive constituents of Phyllolobium lasaense include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
