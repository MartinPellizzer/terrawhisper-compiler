What is Cardamine flagellifera var. hugeri?

Cardamine flagellifera var. hugeri is a taxonomic variant of the species Cardamine flagellifera within the genus Cardamine, belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is an annual or perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its stemless rosettes of leaves and a branched stem that can grow up to 10 cm tall, bearing white or purple flowers in a raceme. The leaves are pinnately divided into 3-7 leaflets, each 1-3 cm long, with a base that clasps the stem. The plant produces a raceme of white or purple flowers, 2-4 mm long, in an umbrella-like arrangement, and contains seeds 0.8-1.2 mm long.

The species has been found in China and has not been widely cultivated or studied.
