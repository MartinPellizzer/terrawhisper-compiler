Gentiana carinthiaca

Gentiana carinthiaca, also known as the alpine gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the gentian family (Gentianaceae) that is native to the European Alps.

The health benefits of Gentiana carinthiaca include its use as a digestive aid, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial agent, making it a popular remedy for various gastrointestinal issues and infections. The bioactive constituents of this plant include gentiopicrin, gentiogenin, and amarogentin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Gentiana carinthiaca is commonly used in herbal preparations such as tinctures, infusions, and capsules, often in combination with other herbs like peppermint and licorice root, to support digestive health and alleviate symptoms of indigestion and irritable bowel syndrome.
