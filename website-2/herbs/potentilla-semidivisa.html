Potentilla semidivisa

Potentilla semidivisa, also known as Fiveleaf potentilla, is a flowering plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Bioactive constituents of Potentilla semidivisa include triterpenoids, phenolic acids, and flavonoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
