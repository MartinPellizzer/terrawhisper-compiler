Polyscias alternifolia

Polyscias alternifolia, also known as Kanuka, is a small tree native to Australia and New Zealand, belonging to the family Araliaceae.

The leaves and essential oil of Kanuka have been used in traditional medicine for their antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties, providing health benefits such as wound healing, skin protection, and immune system support. The bioactive constituents of Kanuka include sesquiterpene compounds like alpha-bisabolol and guaianolide, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.

Kanuka is commonly used in herbal preparations such as essential oil, tea, and tincture, often utilized in aromatherapy and natural remedies for skin and respiratory issues.
