Epilobium cleistogamum

Epilobium cleistogamum, also known as the small-fruited willowherb, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the willowherb family (Onagraceae).

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Epilobium cleistogamum is often used to prepare teas, infusions, and capsules, which are commonly used to support urinary health, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of prostatitis and benign prostatic hyperplasia.
