Helenium viscosum

Helenium viscosum, commonly known as sticky greenthread, is a perennial plant native to North America, belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antimicrobial properties, which have been attributed to its potential health benefits, including wound healing and protection against infections. Bioactive constituents of Helenium viscosum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpene lactones, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, sticky greenthread is often made into teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to support immune function, reduce inflammation, and promote wound healing.
