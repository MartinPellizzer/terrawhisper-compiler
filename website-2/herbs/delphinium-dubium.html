Delphinium dubium

Delphinium dubium, also known as giant larkspur, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and Western Asia, characterized by its tall spikes of blue, pink, or white flowers.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help to reduce symptoms of arthritis, improve cardiovascular health, and protect against oxidative stress. The bioactive constituents of Delphinium dubium include delphinidin, a powerful anthocyanidin flavonoid, as well as other polyphenolic compounds such as flavonols and phenolic acids.

In traditional herbal medicine, Delphinium dubium is often used to make teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are said to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and improve sleep quality.
