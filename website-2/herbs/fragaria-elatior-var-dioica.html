What is Fragaria elatior var. dioica?

Fragaria elatior var. dioica is a plant species belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a perennial herb with a rhizome-based growth habit, producing stolons and leaves that are trifoliate with toothed margins. The leaves are 3-5 cm long, with petioles that are 1-3 cm long. The flowers are small, white, and have 5-10 petals, produced in corymbose inflorescences.

The fruits are aggregate berries, consisting of 3-5 carpels and containing 3-5 seeds each.
