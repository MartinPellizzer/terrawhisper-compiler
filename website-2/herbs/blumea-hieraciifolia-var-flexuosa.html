What is Blumea hieraciifolia var. flexuosa?

Blumea hieraciifolia var. flexuosa is a variety of plant that belongs to the family Asteraceae.

The plant produces yellow flowers in a capitulum, with an outer ring of 8-12 florets and an inner ring of 5-10 florets, which are all borne on a peduncle that is 2-5 cm long.

The plant grows up to 1.5 m tall and is found in India, Nepal, and Bhutan.
