Cardamomum cereum

Cardamomum cereum, also known as Pink Cardamom or Pink Ginger, is a species of flowering plant in the ginger family, characterized by its unique pink or reddish-colored berries.

The plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its ability to aid digestion, reduce nausea and vomiting, and exhibit anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Cardamomum cereum include gingerols and shogaols, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
