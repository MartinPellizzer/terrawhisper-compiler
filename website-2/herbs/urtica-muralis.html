classification

Urtica muralis is a species of stinging nettle. It belongs to the family Urticaceae, commonly known as the nettle family. Urtica muralis is a type of flowering plant, specifically a perennial herb. The scientific classification for Urtica muralis includes being in the genus Urtica, which comprises several species of nettle. The classification is further divided into the kingdom Plantae, the clade Angiosperms, and the order Urticales.
