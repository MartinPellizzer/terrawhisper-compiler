Galium hispidum

Galium hispidum, also known as Rough Bedstraw, is a perennial herb belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

Traditionally, it has been used to treat various health issues due to its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and diuretic properties, making it beneficial for treating urinary tract infections, wounds, and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Galium hispidum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoid glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Galium hispidum is often prepared as a tincture or a decoction, and is commonly used to treat urinary problems, skin conditions, and as a wound-healing agent.
