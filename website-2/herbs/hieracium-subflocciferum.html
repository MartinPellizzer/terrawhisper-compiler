Hieracium subflocciferum

Hieracium subflocciferum is a perennial plant species, commonly known as hawkweed, belonging to the dandelion-like plants within the Asteraceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Hieracium subflocciferum include flavonoids, sesquiterpenes, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
