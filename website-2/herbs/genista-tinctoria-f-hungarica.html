What is Genista tinctoria f. hungarica?

Genista tinctoria f. hungarica is a subspecies of the dyer's greenweed (Genista tinctoria) plant, which belongs to the family Fabaceae.

This perennial shrub has a woody base, slender stems, and pinnate leaves with 3-5 pairs of leaflets, each leaflet 5-12 mm long and 2-4 mm wide. The plant produces yellow flowers, 5-7 mm in length, in axillary racemes, typically in June and July. The flowers are followed by 2.5-4 cm long pods containing 4-6 seeds, 5-6 mm in length.

Genista tinctoria f. hungarica is native to Hungary and parts of Eastern Europe.
