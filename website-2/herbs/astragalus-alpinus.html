Astragalus alpinus

Astragalus alpinus, also known as Mongolian milkvetch, is a perennial plant species belonging to the legume family (Fabaceae).

This plant has been traditionally used in Chinese medicine to boost immune function, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of fatigue and respiratory diseases. The bioactive constituents of Astragalus alpinus include flavonoids, saponins, and polysaccharides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Astragalus alpinus is commonly prepared as a decoction or extract, and is often combined with other herbs in traditional Chinese medicine to make tonifying and adaptogenic formulas.
