Hieracium tuscheticum

Hieracium tuscheticum, also known as Tuschek's hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

It is valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses including the treatment of respiratory issues and as an anti-inflammatory agent, which may help to alleviate symptoms associated with conditions such as arthritis and bronchitis. The bioactive constituents of Hieracium tuscheticum include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to the plant's therapeutic effects.

Infusions and tinctures are the most common herbal preparations made with Hieracium tuscheticum, often used to create teas and remedies for respiratory and inflammatory conditions.
