Ligusticum tenuifolium

Ligusticum tenuifolium, also known as Western Ligusticum or Oregon Ligusticum, is a perennial plant species native to the western coastal regions of North America.

The bioactive constituents of Ligusticum tenuifolium include sesquiterpenes, coumarins, and lignans, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
