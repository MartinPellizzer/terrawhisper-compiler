Hieracium pseudohalleri

Hieracium pseudohalleri, also known as False Hawkweed, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae) that is native to mountainous regions in Europe.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including inflammation, digestive problems, and skin conditions, due to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Hieracium pseudohalleri include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
