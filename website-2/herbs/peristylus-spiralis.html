Peristylus spiralis

Peristylus spiralis, also known as the spiral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its delicate, spiraling flowers and slender stems.

The bioactive constituents of Peristylus spiralis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal effects.
