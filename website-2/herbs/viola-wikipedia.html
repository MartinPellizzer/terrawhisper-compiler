Viola wikipedia

The Viola plant, also known as the Violet, is a low-growing, flowering plant that belongs to the Violaceae family.

The health benefits of Viola include its potential to reduce inflammation and improve sleep quality due to its sedative properties, as well as its ability to exhibit antioxidant and antimicrobial activities. The bioactive constituents of Viola include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Viola include teas, tinctures, and infusions, which are used to treat various health conditions, such as anxiety, digestive issues, and skin problems.

classification

Viola wikipedia states that the genus Viola is classified within the family Violaceae. This family is part of the order Malpighiales in the clade Eurosidae I. The order Malpighiales is within the clade rosids. The rosids are part of the superclade eurosids. The superclade eurosids is part of the clade eudicots.
