Ficus pseudobenjamina

Ficus pseudobenjamina, commonly known as the Chinese banyan fig or Chinese weeping fig, is a species of fig tree native to southern China.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of fever, cough, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Ficus pseudobenjamina include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Ficus pseudobenjamina is used in herbal preparations, such as teas, infusions, and decoctions, which are often used to treat respiratory infections and fever in traditional Chinese medicine.
