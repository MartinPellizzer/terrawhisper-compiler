Bromus confertus

Bromus confertus, also known as soft brome, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and Asia, characterized by its delicate panicles and fine leaf blades.

The bioactive constituents of Bromus confertus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Soft brome is commonly used to make herbal teas and infusions, as well as being incorporated into traditional folk remedies and tonics.
