Dennstaedtia obtusifolia

Dennstaedtia obtusifolia, also known as the obtuse leather fern, is a species of fern that belongs to the Dennstaedtiaceae family.

The health benefits of Dennstaedtia obtusifolia include its potential anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may aid in the treatment of various health conditions such as fever, respiratory issues, and gastrointestinal problems. The bioactive constituents of this plant include triterpenoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Dennstaedtia obtusifolia is commonly used to prepare teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being used in the production of fermented liquor, known as "yin chen" in traditional Chinese medicine.
