What is Abutilon pannosum var. figarianum?

This variety is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, as well as its ovate to elliptic leaf shape with a rounded base and a pointed tip, measuring up to 15 cm in length. The plant's flowers are yellow with five petals, arranged in a corymbiform inflorescence, and have a diameter of up to 10 cm. Abutilon pannosum var. figarianum is native to the Mediterranean region and has a perennial growth habit.

The plant's roots are taprooted, and its stems are woody and branched.
