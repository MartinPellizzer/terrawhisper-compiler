Ferula cretica

Ferula cretica, also known as Cretan asafoetida, is a perennial plant belonging to the carrot family (Apiaceae) native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

The health benefits of Ferula cretica include its anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antifungal properties, which have been traditionally used to treat digestive issues, respiratory problems, and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of this plant include ferulic acid, asafoetida resin, and volatile oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Ferula cretica is commonly used to make herbal preparations, such as capsules, teas, and infusions, as well as being used as a natural remedy in traditional medicine to treat various health conditions.
