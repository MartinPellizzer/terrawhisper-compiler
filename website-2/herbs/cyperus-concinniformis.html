Cyperus concinniformis

Cyperus concinniformis, also known as umbrella sedge, is a perennial aquatic plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

Traditionally, the plant has been used in folk medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, inflammation, and respiratory issues, due to its potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Cyperus concinniformis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, the plant is commonly prepared as a decoction or infusion, and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine to make teas, tinctures, and infusions for treating various health conditions.
