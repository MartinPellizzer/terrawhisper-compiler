classification

Gaultheria amboinensis is a species of flowering plant in the heath family, Ericaceae. It is native to the island of Ambon in the Maluku Islands of Indonesia. As a member of the Ericaceae family, Gaultheria amboinensis is commonly known as a heath or heather.
