Cinna setifolia

Cinna setifolia, also known as sweet grass, is a perennial grass native to North America, prized for its sweet fragrance and medicinal properties.

The health benefits of Cinna setifolia include its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which can help soothe digestive issues, reduce stress, and promote relaxation. The bioactive constituents of Cinna setifolia include essential oils, particularly limonene, beta-pinene, and caryophyllene, which contribute to its medicinal and aromatic properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Cinna setifolia is commonly prepared as a tea, infusion, or smudge stick to promote relaxation, reduce anxiety, and purify the air.
