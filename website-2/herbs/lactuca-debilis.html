Lactuca debilis, also known as Asian milkweed or Japanese lettuce, is a perennial plant species belonging to the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

In traditional herbal medicine, Lactuca debilis is often prepared as a tea or infusion, or used in combination with other herbs to make tinctures and poultices, which are used to treat a range of health conditions.
