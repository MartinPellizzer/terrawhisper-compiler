Andropogon latifolius

Andropogon latifolius, also known as East Indian vetiver or Vetivera zizanoides, is a perennial grass native to tropical Asia, known for its extensive root system and aromatic leaves.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include vetiverol, vetiverone, and zizanene, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
