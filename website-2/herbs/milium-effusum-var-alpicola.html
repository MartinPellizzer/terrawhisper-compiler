What is Milium effusum var. alpicola?

Milium effusum var. alpicola is a perennial grass that belongs to the family Poaceae.

It has a tufted habit, 10-30 cm tall, with flat, linear leaves up to 10 mm long, and a flat panicle up to 10 cm long. The panicle is lax, with a narrow, spike-like axis, bearing numerous spikelets, each 1-2 cm long, with 2-3 florets. The glumes are 1-2 mm long, lanceolate, and acute, while the lemmas are 1-2 mm long, lanceolate, and acute, with a ciliate lower margin.

The plant is native to high-altitude mountainous regions of Europe.
