Portulaca formosana

Portulaca formosana, also known as Taiwanese purslane, is a species of flowering plant in the family Portulacaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Portulaca formosana include flavonoids, saponins, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
