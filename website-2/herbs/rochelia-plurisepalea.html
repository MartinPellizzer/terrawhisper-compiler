Rochelia plurisepalea

Rochelia plurisepalea, also known as the Rock rose or Plurisepaline Rockrose, is a plant species belonging to the borage family (Boraginaceae).

The bioactive constituents of Rochelia plurisepalea include flavonoids, saponins, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
