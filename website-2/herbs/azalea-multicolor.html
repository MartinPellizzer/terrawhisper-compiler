Azalea multicolor

The Azalea multicolor, also known as the Azalea or Rhododendron, is a flowering plant belonging to the heath family (Ericaceae) that is native to Asia and North America.

The health benefits of Azalea multicolor include its potential to lower blood pressure, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable herb in traditional medicine. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
