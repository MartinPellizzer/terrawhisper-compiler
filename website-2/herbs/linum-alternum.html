medicinal parts

Linum alternum, also known as yellow flax or golden flax, contains various medicinal parts, primarily the seeds and leaves. The seeds are a rich source of mucilage, a thick, gel-like substance that can be used to soothe and protect mucous membranes. In traditional medicine, the seeds are often used to treat digestive issues, such as constipation and diarrhea. The leaves of Linum alternum have also been used in herbal remedies to treat skin conditions and reduce inflammation.
