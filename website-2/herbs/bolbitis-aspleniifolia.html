Bolbitis aspleniifolia

Bolbitis aspleniifolia is a fern species native to tropical regions, commonly found in shaded areas near waterfalls and streams.

It has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can aid in reducing fever and alleviating respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Bolbitis aspleniifolia include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Bolbitis aspleniifolia is often prepared as a tea, decoction, or tincture, and is commonly used to treat coughs, sore throats, and digestive issues.
