Wedelia leucoglossa

Wedelia leucoglossa, also known as Mexican daisy, is a flowering plant species in the sunflower family (Asteraceae) that is native to Mexico.

The bioactive constituents of Wedelia leucoglossa include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
