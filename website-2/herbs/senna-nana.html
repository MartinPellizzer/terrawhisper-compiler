Senna nana

Senna nana, also known as dwarf cassia or small cassia, is a shrubby plant belonging to the Fabaceae family, commonly used as a medicinal plant due to its laxative properties.

The health benefits of Senna nana include its ability to treat constipation and other digestive issues, as well as its potential to lower blood sugar levels and reduce inflammation. The bioactive constituents of Senna nana include glycosides (such as sennosides and anthraquinone glycosides), which are responsible for its purgative effects, as well as other compounds like flavonoids and phenolic acids.

Senna nana is commonly used in herbal preparations such as tea, capsules, and tinctures, often in combination with other herbs to enhance its medicinal properties and reduce potential side effects.
