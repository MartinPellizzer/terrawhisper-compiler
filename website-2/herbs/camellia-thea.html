Camellia thea is a member of the genus Camellia, which consists of over 200 species of flowering plants. The species Camellia thea is native to China and is often considered to be a variety of Camellia sinensis, the plant from which tea is harvested. However, the exact classification of Camellia thea is still a subject of debate among botanists.
