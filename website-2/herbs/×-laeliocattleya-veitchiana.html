classification

× Laeliocattleya veitchiana is a hybrid orchid, specifically a cross between Laelia and Cattleya orchids. It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is a part of the subtribe Laeliinae. This orchid is further classified as a natural hybrid, resulting from the natural cross-pollination of its parent species. The classification of × Laeliocattleya veitchiana is a combination of its parent genera, which is a common practice in horticulture for naming hybrid orchids.
