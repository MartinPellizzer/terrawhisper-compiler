Rubus dumosus

Rubus dumosus, also known as the Himalayan raspberry or Himalayan bramble, is a species of thorny, deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

The bioactive constituents of Rubus dumosus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and tannins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
