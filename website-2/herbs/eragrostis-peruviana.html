Eragrostis peruviana

Eragrostis peruviana, also known as Peruvian love grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America, traditionally used in folk medicine for its medicinal properties.

The bioactive constituents of Eragrostis peruviana include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Eragrostis peruviana is often prepared as an infusion or decoction, and is commonly used to make teas, tinctures, and infusions to treat various health conditions.
