Passiflora umbilicata

Passiflora umbilicata is a species of passionflower, a type of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant has been traditionally used for its calming and sedative properties, and its health benefits include relieving anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness, as well as reducing inflammation and pain. The bioactive constituents of Passiflora umbilicata include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
