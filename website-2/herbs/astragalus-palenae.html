Astragalus palenae

Astragalus palenae, also known as Mongolian milkvetch, is a type of perennial legume native to Mongolia and other parts of Asia.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, which include enhancing immune function, reducing inflammation, and improving cardiovascular health. The bioactive constituents of Astragalus palenae include flavonoids, saponins, and polysaccharides, which are believed to contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Astragalus palenae is often prepared into teas, infusions, or capsules, and is also used in combination with other herbs to create formulas such as tonifying and adaptogenic blends.
