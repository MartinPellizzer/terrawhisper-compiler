What is Aria nivea var. edulis?

Aria nivea var. edulis is a fruit-producing plant within the family Rosaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 10 meters in height. The plant's leaves are pinnate, with 3-9 leaflets, and are 2-4 centimeters long, having a dark green color above and a light green color below. The leaves are alternate, with a petiole length of 1-2 centimeters.

The plant produces small, edible fruits, 1-2 centimeters in diameter, which are red or yellow in color and have a sweet, edible pulp.
