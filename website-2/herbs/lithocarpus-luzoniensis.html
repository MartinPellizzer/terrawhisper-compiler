Lithocarpus luzoniensis

Lithocarpus luzoniensis, also known as Bagtikan or Bagtikan Tree, is a species of evergreen tree in the beech family (Fagaceae) native to the Philippines.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant activities, which may aid in the treatment of various conditions such as fever, cough, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Lithocarpus luzoniensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Filipino medicine, the plant is commonly prepared into herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being used in the production of topical creams and ointments.
