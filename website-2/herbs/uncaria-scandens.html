Uncaria scandens

Uncaria scandens, also known as Cat's Claw Vine, is a type of tropical vine native to South America and Central America.

The bioactive constituents of Uncaria scandens include alkaloids, flavonoids, and glycosides, particularly oxindole alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Uncaria scandens is prepared as a tea or tincture, and it is also used in capsule or tablet form, allowing for its bioactive compounds to be easily absorbed and utilized by the body.
