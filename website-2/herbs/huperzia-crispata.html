Huperzia crispata

Huperzia crispata, also known as Chinese club moss or huperzia, is a species of club moss in the family Huperziaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits, including the potential to improve memory and cognitive function, as well as its neuroprotective and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Huperzia crispata include huperzine A, a natural inhibitor of acetylcholinesterase, as well as other alkaloids and flavonoids.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Huperzia crispata is often prepared as a crude extract or tea, while in modern herbalism, it is often used in capsule or tincture form to support brain health and function.
