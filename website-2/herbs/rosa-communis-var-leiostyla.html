What is Rosa communis var. leiostyla?

Rosa communis var. leiostyla is a botanical variety of the wild rose species, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a perennial shrub with a height of up to 2 meters, characterized by pinnate leaves with 7-11 leaflets, each 2-3 cm long and 1-2 cm wide, with serrated margins and a pointed tip. The leaves are dark green, with a petiole up to 2 cm long. The flowers are solitary, 4-6 cm in diameter, with 5-7 petals, and a yellow center surrounded by pink or reddish-purple sepals.

The flowers are followed by red or purple hips, 1-2 cm in diameter, containing 10-20 seeds.
