What is Prunus × lannesiana f. ojochin?

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, with 5 white petals that are 2-3 cm long and a central cluster of yellow stamens. The flowers are followed by edible, red to purple fruits that are 1-2 cm in diameter and contain 1-2 seeds.
