Fumaria glaucescens

Fumaria glaucescens, commonly known as small fumitory, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and anti-cancer properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of digestive issues, improve wound healing, and reduce the risk of certain diseases. The bioactive constituents of Fumaria glaucescens include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

In herbal medicine, Fumaria glaucescens is often prepared as a tea, infusion, or tincture, and is used to treat a range of health conditions, including digestive issues, skin problems, and respiratory complaints.
