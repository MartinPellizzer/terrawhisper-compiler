Scirpus purshianus

Scirpus purshianus, also known as Cottontop Rush or Purple Bulrush, is a perennial aquatic plant that belongs to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

This plant has been used in traditional medicine to treat various health conditions, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Scirpus purshianus include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Scirpus purshianus is often prepared as a decoction or infusion, or used in tinctures and salves, to treat conditions such as wound healing, fever reduction, and digestive issues.
