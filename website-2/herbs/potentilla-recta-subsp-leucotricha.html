What is Potentilla recta subsp. leucotricha?

Potentilla recta subsp.

leucotricha is a plant taxon belonging to the family Rosaceae. It is a perennial herb with erect stems, 10-30 cm tall, and leaves consisting of 3-5 leaflets, each 1-3 cm long, and having a lanceolate to elliptic shape.

The leaflets are covered with white tomentose hairs on both sides. Flowers are yellow, 1-2 cm in diameter, and have 5 petals with an ovate shape, occurring in axillary clusters.

The fruit is an aggregate of achenes, 4-6 mm in diameter, with a crown of 5 sepals.
