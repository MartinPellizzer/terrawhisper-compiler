Melilotus lupulinus

Melilotus lupulinus, also known as sweet clover, is a perennial plant in the legume family that is native to Europe and Asia.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may help to alleviate symptoms of menopausal hot flashes, reduce anxiety, and support cardiovascular health. The bioactive constituents of Melilotus lupulinus include coumarins, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, sweet clover is commonly prepared as a tea, tincture, or infusion, and is also used in combination with other herbs to create remedies for anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues.
