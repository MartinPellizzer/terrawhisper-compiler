Rubia palustris

Rubia palustris, also known as purple dragon or swamp dock, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health conditions, including inflammation, fever, and digestive issues, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Rubia palustris include anthraquinones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

In herbal preparations, Rubia palustris is commonly used to make tinctures, decoctions, and infusions, often combined with other herbs to enhance its therapeutic properties and create remedies for conditions such as arthritis and skin issues.
