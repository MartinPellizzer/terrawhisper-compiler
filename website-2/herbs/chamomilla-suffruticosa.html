Chamomilla suffruticosa

Chamomilla suffruticosa, also known as low-growing chamomile, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

The health benefits of Chamomilla suffruticosa include its calming and soothing properties, which can help alleviate anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, such as apigenin and luteolin, as well as sesquiterpenes, which contribute to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Chamomilla suffruticosa include teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are often used to promote relaxation and alleviate symptoms of stress and anxiety.
