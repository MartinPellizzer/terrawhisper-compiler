What is Plantago notata var. haussknechtii?

Plantago notata var. haussknechtii is a variety of the species Plantago notata, belonging to the family Plantaginaceae.

It is characterized by linear-lanceolate leaves that are 5-15 cm long and 1-4 mm wide, with entire margins and a glaucous-green color. The leaves are sessile, arranged in a rosette pattern, and have a prominent midrib. The inflorescence is a spike-like raceme, bearing brownish-green flowers that are 3-4 mm long, with a 5-lobed calyx and 5 stamens.

The fruits are nutlets, 3-4 mm long, with a glaucous-green color and a reticulate pattern.
