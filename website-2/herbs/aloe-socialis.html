Aloe socialis

Aloe socialis, also known as the Cape Aloe or Red Aloe, is a succulent plant species native to the southern parts of Africa.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering health benefits such as soothing skin irritations, reducing inflammation, and promoting wound healing due to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties.

The bioactive constituents of Aloe socialis include aloin, aloe-emodin, and other anthraquinones, as well as vitamins A, C, and E. The most common herbal preparations made with Aloe socialis include gel and juice extracts, which are often used topically in creams, ointments, and gels to treat various skin conditions, as well as orally in capsule or liquid form to support digestive health.
