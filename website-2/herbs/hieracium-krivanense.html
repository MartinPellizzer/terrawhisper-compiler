classification

Hieracium krivanense belongs to the family Asteraceae, commonly known as the daisy or sunflower family. It is a species of flowering plant in the genus Hieracium, which includes hawkweeds. The genus Hieracium is a part of the tribe Senecioneae, a group of plants characterized by their daisy-like flowers. The species is native to the Czech Republic, specifically in the Krkonoše Mountains. As a member of the Asteraceae family, Hieracium krivanense exhibits composite flowers with both ray and disc florets.
