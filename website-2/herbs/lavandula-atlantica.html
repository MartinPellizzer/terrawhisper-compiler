Lavandula atlantica

Lavandula atlantica, commonly known as English lavender or Portuguese lavender, is a fragrant, flowering plant native to the western Mediterranean region.

The health benefits of Lavandula atlantica include its ability to promote relaxation, reduce anxiety and stress, and improve sleep quality, due to its calming and sedative properties. The bioactive constituents of Lavandula atlantica include linalool, linalyl acetate, and camphor, which are responsible for its medicinal and aromatic properties.

Lavandula atlantica is often used in herbal preparations such as essential oils, teas, and infusions, as well as in topical applications like skincare products and perfumes.
