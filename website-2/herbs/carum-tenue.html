Carum tenue

Carum tenue, also known as Chervil or Garden Chervil, is a low-growing, annual or biennial herbaceous plant belonging to the parsley family (Apiaceae).

The health benefits of Carum tenue include its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate digestive issues and reduce the risk of certain diseases. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and volatile oils, particularly carvone and limonene, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Carum tenue is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being used as a fresh herb in salads and as a garnish.
