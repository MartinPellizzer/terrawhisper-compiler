classification

Capsella ceratocarpa is a species of flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae. It belongs to the genus Capsella. The plant's classification is as follows: Kingdom: Plantae, Clade: Angiosperms, Clade: Eudicots, Clade: Rosids, Order: Brassicales, Family: Brassicaceae, Genus: Capsella, Species: C. ceratocarpa. This classification places it within the mustard family of plants. The species is closely related to the common shepherd's purse (Capsella bursa-pastoris).
