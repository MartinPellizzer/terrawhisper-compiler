Corallorhiza odontorhiza

Corallorhiza odontorhiza, also known as the summer coralroot, is a unique, leafless orchid species native to North America that obtains its nutrients by parasitizing fungi associated with the roots of surrounding trees.

The summer coralroot has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which may aid in wound healing and reducing fever. Bioactive constituents of Corallorhiza odontorhiza include alkaloids, glycosides, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

The plant is commonly used in herbal preparations such as teas, tinctures, and capsules, often employed to treat various ailments, including respiratory infections and skin conditions.
