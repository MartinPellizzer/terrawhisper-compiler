Chrysanthemum hookeri, also known as Hooker's Chrysanthemum or Korean Chrysanthemum, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, commonly used in traditional medicine.

The bioactive constituents of Chrysanthemum hookeri include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
