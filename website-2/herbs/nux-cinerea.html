Nux cinerea

Nux cinerea, also known as black walnut, is a deciduous tree native to eastern North America.

The health benefits of Nux cinerea include its potential to support digestive health, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antimicrobial properties, making it a popular natural remedy for various ailments. The bioactive constituents of Nux cinerea include juglone, a lignan with antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, as well as other tannins and phenolic compounds.

The most common herbal preparations made with Nux cinerea include capsules, tinctures, and topical creams, which are often used to treat fungal infections, skin conditions, and digestive issues.
