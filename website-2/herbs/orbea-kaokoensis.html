Orbea kaokoensis

Orbea kaokoensis is a species of succulent plant in the Apocynaceae family, native to the Kaokoland region of Namibia.

The plant has been traditionally used in folk medicine, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiproliferative activities. Bioactive constituents of Orbea kaokoensis include pregnane glycosides, pregnane saponins, and other bioactive steroidal compounds, which have been shown to exhibit potential therapeutic properties.

Orbea kaokoensis is often used in traditional herbal preparations, including infusions and decoctions, as well as in the production of tinctures and extracts, which are valued for their potential to support immune function and overall well-being.
