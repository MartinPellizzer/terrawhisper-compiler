classification

Trichotosia rubiginosa is a species of orchid. It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. Within this family, Trichotosia rubiginosa is classified under the subfamily Epidendroideae and tribe Vandeae. The genus Trichotosia is part of the larger orchid family and is native to Southeast Asia. This classification helps in understanding the evolutionary relationships and characteristics of the plant.
