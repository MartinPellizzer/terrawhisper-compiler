classification

Arabis humifusa is a species of flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae. It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial native to eastern North America. The classification of Arabis humifusa is as follows: Kingdom - Plantae, Clade - Angiosperms, Clade - Eudicots, Clade - Rosids, Order - Brassicales, Family - Brassicaceae, Genus - Arabis, Species - A. humifusa. Arabis humifusa is also known as sandwort. This species is often found in sandy areas and rocky outcrops.
