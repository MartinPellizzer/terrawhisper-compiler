What is Chuquiraga spinosa subsp. rotundifolia?

Chuquiraga spinosa subsp.

rotundifolia is a plant species belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is a shrub with a woody base, 0.5-1.5 meters in height, with a rounded crown and a branching system that becomes more ramified upwards.

The leaves are elliptical to lanceolate, 5-20 cm long, and 2-7 cm wide, with a wavy margin and a glandular pubescence on the lower surface. The inflorescence is a capitulum, 2-5 cm in diameter, with 40-60 florets, and a purple corolla, 6-8 mm long.

The capitulum is surrounded by a persistent involucre with phyllaries, which are glandular pubescent and have a purple tinge.
