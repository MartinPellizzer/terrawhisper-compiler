Cerasus sylvestris

Cerasus sylvestris, also known as the wild cherry or morello cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree belonging to the rose family (Rosaceae).

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and salicylic acid, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
