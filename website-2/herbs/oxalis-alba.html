Oxalis alba

Oxalis alba, commonly known as the white wood sorrel, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Oxalidaceae family, characterized by its white flowers and edible, acidic leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Oxalis alba include oxalic acid, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
