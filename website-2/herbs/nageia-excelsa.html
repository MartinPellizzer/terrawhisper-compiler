Nageia excelsa

Nageia excelsa, also known as Kevazingo, is a species of coniferous tree native to Borneo and the Philippines.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its potential to reduce inflammation and improve respiratory health, making it a popular remedy for conditions such as bronchitis and asthma. The bioactive constituents of Nageia excelsa include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and terpenoids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
