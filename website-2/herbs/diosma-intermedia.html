classification

Diosma intermedia is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae, commonly known as the citrus or rue family. It is native to the southwestern and southern parts of Africa. This plant is a member of the genus Diosma, which includes around 60 species of shrubs and small trees. The classification of Diosma intermedia places it within the kingdom Plantae, phylum Angiospermophyta, and class Magnoliopsida. Further classification includes the order Sapindales, and family Rutaceae.
