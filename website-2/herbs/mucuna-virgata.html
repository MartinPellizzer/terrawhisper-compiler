Mucuna virgata

Mucuna virgata is a perennial plant species belonging to the Fabaceae family, commonly found in tropical regions of Africa.

The plant is known for its potential health benefits, which include improving mental clarity, reducing stress and anxiety, and enhancing mood due to its high levels of L-DOPA, a precursor to neurotransmitters such as dopamine, serotonin, and norepinephrine.
