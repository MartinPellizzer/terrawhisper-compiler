What is Herniaria scabrida var. unamunoana?

Herniaria scabrida var. unamunoana is a taxonomic variety of the plant species Herniaria scabrida, belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is an annual or biennial herbaceous plant, characterized by its scabrid leaves and stems with hooked hairs. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, 1-3 cm long, and have a cuneate base. The flowers are small, pink to red, and borne in axillary clusters.

The fruits are capsules, 3-4 mm long, dehiscing by 3 teeth.
