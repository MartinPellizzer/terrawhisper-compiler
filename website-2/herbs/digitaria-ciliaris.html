classification

Digitaria ciliaris is a type of grass that belongs to the family Poaceae. This species is further classified within the tribe Paniceae and the genus Digitaria. It is a cosmopolitan grass, meaning it is found on every continent except Antarctica. Within the genus Digitaria, D. ciliaris is classified as a species of grass that is commonly known as southern crabgrass. It is closely related to other Digitaria species, such as D. sanguinea.
