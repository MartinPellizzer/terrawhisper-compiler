What is Leucas decemdentata var. sebastiana?

Leucas decemdentata var. sebastiana is a taxonomic variety of Leucas decemdentata, a species of flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae, native to Sri Lanka.

The leaves are glabrous or sparsely hairy on the upper surface and densely hairy on the lower surface, with a 3-4-nerved lamina.
