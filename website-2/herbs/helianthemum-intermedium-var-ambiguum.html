What is Helianthemum intermedium var. ambiguum?

Helianthemum intermedium var. ambiguum is a variety of the intermediate rockrose, a flowering plant in the family Cistaceae.

It is a low-growing, woody shrub with a sprawling habit, typically growing 10-30 cm in height and 30-50 cm in width. The flowers are pink to purple in color, 1-2 cm in diameter, and are borne in clusters at the ends of the branches.

The plant produces small, brown seeds in a dry, dehiscent capsule.
