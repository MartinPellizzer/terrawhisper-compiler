Cnicus × tartaricus

Cnicus × tartaricus, also known as Tartar Echinops, is a hybrid plant species within the thistle family, characterized by its purple flowers and spiny foliage.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties, which may aid in treating various conditions such as wounds, digestive issues, and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of Cnicus × tartaricus include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Cnicus × tartaricus is often prepared as a tincture, tea, or infusion, and is sometimes used in combination with other herbs to create tonics and salves.
