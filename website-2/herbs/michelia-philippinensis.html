Michelia philippinensis, also known as Philippine magnolia or magnolia champaca, is a plant species native to the Philippines and other parts of Southeast Asia, belonging to the family Magnoliaceae.

The bioactive constituents of Michelia philippinensis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its therapeutic properties.
