Ficus maximiliana

The Ficus maximiliana, also known as the Moreton Bay fig or Queensland fig, is a large and ancient evergreen tree native to Australia.

This plant is valued for its medicinal and therapeutic properties, with potential health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant activities, which can help to reduce stress and promote overall well-being. The bioactive constituents of Ficus maximiliana include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, the plant is often used to make teas, tinctures, and capsules, which are used to treat a range of health conditions, including anxiety, insomnia, and digestive issues.
