Clinopodium dalmaticum

Clinopodium dalmaticum, also known as Dalmatian pennyroyal, is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It has been traditionally used for its various health benefits, including its antispasmodic, carminative, and antiseptic properties, which can help alleviate digestive issues, menstrual cramps, and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of Dalmatian pennyroyal include essential oils, such as borneol, camphor, and pinene, as well as flavonoids and phenolic acids.

In herbal medicine, Dalmatian pennyroyal is commonly used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into topical creams and salves for its antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties.
