What is Geum rossii var. humile?

The flowers are 5-merous, with 5 sepals, 5 petals, and numerous stamens. The plant grows to a height of 10-30 cm and has a hairy, glandular stem.

Geum rossii var. humile is native to the mountainous regions of Japan and is often found in moist, rocky areas.
