What is Rubus hirtus var. longipes?

Rubus hirtus var. longipes is a plant variety within the species Rubus hirtus, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub with a woody stem, typically growing 1-2 meters tall, with a dense mass of thorns. The leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets, ovate to lanceolate in shape, and 3-7 cm long, with a serrated margin and a hairy underside. The flowers are small, white, and arranged in a corymb, appearing from May to July, followed by red or purple berries.

The variety is native to the mountainous regions of southwestern Europe and western Asia.
