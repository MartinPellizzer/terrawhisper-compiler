What is Lobelia trullifolia subsp. delicatula?

Lobelia trullifolia subsp.

delicatula is a taxonomic rank within the species Lobelia trullifolia, belonging to the family Campanulaceae. It is characterized by its annual or biennial habit, glabrous to villous stems, and ovate to elliptical leaves 2-5 cm long with obtuse to acute apices.

The inflorescence consists of a terminal spike of blue, bell-shaped flowers 4-7 mm long, with a subequal calyx and a narrow, 4-lobed corolla. The fruit is a globose capsule 3-4 mm long, containing 4-6 seeds. Lobelia trullifolia subsp.

delicatula is endemic to the mountains of southeastern Spain.
