Gentiana mathewsii

Gentiana mathewsii, also known as Mathew's gentian, is a species of flowering plant in the gentian family (Gentianaceae).

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to treat various health conditions, including digestive issues, fever, and respiratory problems, due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. The bioactive constituents of Gentiana mathewsii include iridoids, triterpenoids, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

Mathew's gentian is commonly prepared as a tincture, tea, or dried root, and is used in traditional medicine to aid digestion and relieve symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome.
