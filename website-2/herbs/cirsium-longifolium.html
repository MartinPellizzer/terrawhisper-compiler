Cirsium longifolium

Cirsium longifolium, also known as Prairie Thistle, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its tall stature and showy purple flowers.

The health benefits of Cirsium longifolium include its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various health issues such as fever, skin conditions, and respiratory problems. The bioactive constituents of this plant include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Cirsium longifolium is commonly used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into salves and ointments for topical application.
