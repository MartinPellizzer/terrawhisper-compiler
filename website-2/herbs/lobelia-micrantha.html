Lobelia micrantha

Lobelia micrantha, also known as Lobelia or Indian tobacco, is a flowering plant species that belongs to the Campanulaceae family.

Traditionally, Lobelia micrantha has been used in herbal medicine to treat various health issues, including bronchitis, coughs, and respiratory problems, due to its expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of this plant include lobeline, lobelanine, and other alkaloids, which are responsible for its medicinal effects.

Lobelia micrantha is commonly used in herbal preparations such as tinctures, infusions, and smoking mixtures, although its use should be done under the guidance of a healthcare professional due to potential toxicity.
