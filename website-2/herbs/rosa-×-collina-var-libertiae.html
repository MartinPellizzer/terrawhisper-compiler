What is Rosa × collina var. libertiae?

Rosa × collina var. libertiae is a variety of the wild rose, belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is a deciduous shrub with thorny stems, typically growing up to 1-2 meters in height. The leaves are pinnate, with 3-5 leaflets, and are dark green in color. The flowers are pink to reddish-pink, solitary and axillary, with 5 petals and a diameter of up to 5 cm.

The fruit is a red or orange pome, typically 2-4 cm in diameter, containing 5-7 seeds.
