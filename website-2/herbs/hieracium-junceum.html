Hieracium junceum

Hieracium junceum, commonly known as hawkweed or woolly hawkweed, is a type of flowering plant in the dandelion family (Asteraceae).

The bioactive constituents of Hieracium junceum include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
