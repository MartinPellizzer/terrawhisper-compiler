Patrinia sericea

Patrinia sericea, also known as downy patrinia or downy false ginseng, is a perennial plant native to East Asia, belonging to the family Valerianaceae.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, which include anti-inflammatory, antibacterial, and antiviral activities, making it useful for treating various health conditions such as fever, sore throat, and respiratory infections. The bioactive constituents of Patrinia sericea include sesquiterpenoid compounds, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional Chinese medicine, Patrinia sericea is commonly used to prepare herbal teas, infusions, and decoctions, as well as being incorporated into patent medicines and topical creams.
