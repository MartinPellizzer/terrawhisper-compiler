Acrostichum tahitense

Acrostichum tahitense, also known as Tahitian leather fern, is a species of fern that is native to the tropical Pacific, particularly in the islands of French Polynesia.

The bioactive constituents of Acrostichum tahitense include flavonoids, saponins, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
