Lantana odorata

Lantana odorata, commonly known as Fragrant Lantana or Yellow Lantana, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America, characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Lantana odorata include flavonoids, alkaloids, and terpenoids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
