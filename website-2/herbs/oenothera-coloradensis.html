Oenothera coloradensis

Oenothera coloradensis, also known as Hooker's evening primrose, is a perennial herb native to North America, characterized by its delicate white flowers and yellow centers.

The bioactive constituents of Oenothera coloradensis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and carotenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
