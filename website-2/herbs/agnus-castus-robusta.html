Agnus-castus robusta

Agnus-castus robusta, also known as chaste tree or vitex, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its distinctive purple flowers and edible fruits.

The health benefits of Agnus-castus robusta include its ability to regulate menstrual cycles, alleviate symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS), and support menopausal health due to its antioxidant and estrogen-balancing properties. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, diterpenoids, and alkaloids, particularly aucubin, rosmarinic acid, and agnuside, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Agnus-castus robusta include tinctures, capsules, and teas, which are often used to support reproductive health and alleviate symptoms of hormonal imbalance.
