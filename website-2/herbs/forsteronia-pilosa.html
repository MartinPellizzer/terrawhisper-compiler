classification

Forsteronia pilosa belongs to the family Annonaceae. This plant is a species of evergreen vine that is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The classification of Forsteronia pilosa at the kingdom level is Plantae, and at the division level it is Magnoliophyta. At the class level, it is Magnoliopsida, and at the order level it is Magnoliales. Its classification at the family level, as mentioned earlier, is Annonaceae.
