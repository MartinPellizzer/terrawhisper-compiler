Justicia flexuosa

Justicia flexuosa, also known as the flexuosa acacia or simply Justicia, is a tropical plant species belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

This plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, with reported health benefits including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiviral activities, which can aid in treating various conditions such as fever, cough, and respiratory issues. The bioactive constituents of Justicia flexuosa include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Justicia flexuosa is commonly prepared in infusions, decoctions, and tinctures to treat various health conditions, with the most common preparations being tea, powder, and liquid extracts.
