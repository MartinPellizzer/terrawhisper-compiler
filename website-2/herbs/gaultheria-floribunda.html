Gaultheria floribunda

Gaultheria floribunda, also known as mountain tea or Gaultheria, is a species of flowering plant in the heath family that is native to the Himalayas and other parts of Asia.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties, with health benefits including its ability to act as an antimicrobial agent, reduce inflammation, and alleviate symptoms of colds and flu. The bioactive constituents of Gaultheria floribunda include triterpenoid saponins, phenolic acids, and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
