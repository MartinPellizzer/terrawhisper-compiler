Gymnopogon wightii

Gymnopogon wightii, also known as Wight's feather grass, is a species of plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

The bioactive constituents of G. wightii include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
