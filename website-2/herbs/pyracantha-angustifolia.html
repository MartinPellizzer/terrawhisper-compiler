Pyracantha angustifolia

Pyracantha angustifolia, also known as the narrow-leaved firethorn, is a flowering evergreen shrub native to China, belonging to the Rosaceae family.

The plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine to promote digestive health, reduce inflammation, and exhibit antimicrobial properties, making it a potential remedy for various health issues. Pyracantha angustifolia contains bioactive constituents such as pyracantha, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

The most common herbal preparations made from Pyracantha angustifolia include teas, tinctures, and infusions, often used to support immune function, relieve gastrointestinal issues, and combat infections.

classification
