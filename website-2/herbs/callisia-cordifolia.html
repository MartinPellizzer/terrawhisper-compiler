Callisia cordifolia

Callisia cordifolia, also known as Rosary Vine or Heartleaf, is a perennial plant species belonging to the Commelinaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Callisia cordifolia include flavonoids, alkaloids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
