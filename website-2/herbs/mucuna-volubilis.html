Mucuna volubilis

Mucuna volubilis is a tropical vine plant native to tropical Asia and Africa, commonly known as the Velvet Bean or Cowhage.

It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly its rich source of L-DOPA, a precursor to neurotransmitters such as dopamine, serotonin, and norepinephrine, which play a crucial role in mood regulation, motivation, and other cognitive functions.

The plant is used to alleviate symptoms of various health conditions, including anxiety, stress, insomnia, and fatigue, as well as to enhance cognitive function, memory, and mood.

Mucuna volubilis is typically prepared as a herbal tea, capsule, or powder, and can be consumed in various forms, including as a supplement or added to food and beverages.
