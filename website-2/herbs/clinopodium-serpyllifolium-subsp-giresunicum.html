What is Clinopodium serpyllifolium subsp. giresunicum?

Clinopodium serpyllifolium subsp.

giresunicum is a plant taxon within the family Lamiaceae. It possesses small, oval-shaped leaves that are 1-3 mm in length and arranged in a whorled pattern along the stem.

The plant produces small, blue-purple flowers in the axils of the leaves, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence, with corollas that are 3-4 mm in length and have five lobes.

This taxon is endemic to Turkey and was first described in 1979.
