classification

Selliguea nigrovenia is a species of plant within the family Polypodiaceae. It is a type of fern that belongs to the genus Selliguea. Selliguea nigrovenia is a tropical species native to Asia, specifically in countries such as the Philippines and India. The classification of Selliguea nigrovenia is further categorized under the order Polypodiales. The exact classification can be broken down into the kingdom Plantae, division Pteridophyta, and class Polypodiopsida.
