classification

It is a deciduous shrub known for its fragrant flowers, which are often used in perfumery and landscaping. As a hybrid, Syringa × varina is not found in the wild but is cultivated for its ornamental value. The specific classification of Syringa × varina is Syringa × varina, a cross between Syringa persica and Syringa vulgaris. This hybrid is commonly known as the 'Olympiad' lilac or the 'Olympiad' syringa.
