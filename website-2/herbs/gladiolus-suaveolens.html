Gladiolus suaveolens

Gladiolus suaveolens, also known as the Sweet Gladiolus or Fragrant Gladiolus, is a perennial plant species belonging to the iris family (Iridaceae).

The health benefits of Gladiolus suaveolens include its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which have been traditionally used to treat various ailments such as colds, fever, and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Gladiolus suaveolens has been used to make herbal teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being used in traditional medicine to treat respiratory and skin issues.
