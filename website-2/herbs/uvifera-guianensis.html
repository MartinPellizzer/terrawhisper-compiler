Uvifera guianensis

Uvifera guianensis, also known as the Guianan tree, is a species of plant in the family Fabaceae.

The Guianan tree is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional medicine, where it is used to treat various health issues such as fever, rheumatism, and respiratory problems, thanks to its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Uvifera guianensis include flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds, which contribute to its medicinal effects.

In herbal medicine, Uvifera guianensis is commonly prepared as a decoction or infusion, and is also used in the production of herbal teas and extracts.
