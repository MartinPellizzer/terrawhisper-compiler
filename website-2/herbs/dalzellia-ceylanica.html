Dalzellia ceylanica

Dalzellia ceylanica, also known as Ceylonese dalzellia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Menispermaceae.

The plant is used in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which are believed to aid in the treatment of various ailments such as fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues. The bioactive constituents of Dalzellia ceylanica include alkaloids, glycosides, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

In traditional medicine, Dalzellia ceylanica is commonly prepared as a decoction or a powder, and is often used to make herbal teas, capsules, and ointments.
