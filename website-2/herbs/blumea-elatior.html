Blumea elatior

Blumea elatior, also known as camphorweed, is a perennial herb belonging to the family Asteraceae.

The plant has been traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to treat various health conditions, including fever, inflammation, and skin infections, due to its anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antiseptic properties. The bioactive constituents of Blumea elatior include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
