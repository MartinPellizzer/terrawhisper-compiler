Arundinaria angustifolia

Arundinaria angustifolia, also known as bamboo cane or narrow-leaved bamboo, is a type of perennial bamboo plant native to Eastern Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Arundinaria angustifolia include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and polysaccharides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
