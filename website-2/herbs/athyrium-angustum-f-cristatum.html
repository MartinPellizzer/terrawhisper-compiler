What is Athyrium angustum f. cristatum?

Athyrium angustum f. cristatum is a fern species within the family Aspleniaceae.

It is a rare, sterile, and non-flowering variant of Athyrium angustum, characterized by the presence of sterile fronds and the absence of fertile fronds. Botanically, it features linear-lanceolate to lanceolate pinnae with serrate margins, and the leafy fronds typically grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant has a delicate appearance, with fronds usually growing in a compact, tufted manner.

The sterile fronds of A. angustum f. cristatum are morphologically distinct from the fertile fronds of its parent species, A. angustum.
