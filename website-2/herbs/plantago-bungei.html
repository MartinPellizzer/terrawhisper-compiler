Plantago bungei

Plantago bungei, also known as Russian plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family, commonly found in temperate regions of Asia and Europe.

The bioactive constituents of Plantago bungei include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and mucilages, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In herbal medicine, Plantago bungei is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, as well as being incorporated into ointments and salves to treat skin and wound conditions.
