Viburnum hayatae

Viburnum hayatae, also known as Taiwan snowball, is a deciduous shrub native to Taiwan, belonging to the Adoxaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of Viburnum hayatae include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and lignans, such as isoflavones, quercetin, and kaempferol, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
