Tragacantha rupicola

Tragacantha rupicola, also known as mountain goat's beard or Tragacanth, is a succulent plant species native to the mountainous regions of Europe, Western Asia, and North Africa, characterized by its unique ability to store water in its leaves and stems.

Tragacantha rupicola is commonly used in traditional herbal medicine to prepare topical creams, ointments, and suppositories, as well as oral suspensions and decoctions, which are used to treat a range of health conditions, including digestive issues and skin irritations.
