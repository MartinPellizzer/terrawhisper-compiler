Rotala indica

Rotala indica is a type of flowering plant that belongs to the Lythraceae family, commonly known as the loosestrife family.

Traditionally, Rotala indica has been used in Ayurvedic and Unani medicine to treat various health issues, including fever, cough, and respiratory problems, due to its antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Rotala indica include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional medicine, Rotala indica is often used to make herbal preparations such as decoctions, infusions, and syrups, as well as being used in the form of powder or capsules.
