classification

Ranunculus aequalis is a species of flowering plant in the family Ranunculaceae, commonly known as the buttercup family. This family is a group of flowering plants that are primarily native to the Northern Hemisphere. Ranunculaceae is part of the larger order Ranunculales, which includes other flowering plant families such as the Clematis family. The order Ranunculales is then part of the clade Magnoliidae, a large group of flowering plant families. Further classification places the order Ranunculales within the clade Spermatophyta, which includes all flowering plants.
