Hibiscus flaviflorus

Hibiscus flaviflorus, also known as yellow hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant in the mallow family, characterized by its bright yellow flowers and large, ovate leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Hibiscus flaviflorus include flavonoids, alkaloids, and anthocyanins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
