Millettia hookeriana

Millettia hookeriana is a perennial legume native to the Himalayas, belonging to the Fabaceae family.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antimicrobial properties, which may aid in reducing inflammation, protecting against cell damage, and preventing infections. The bioactive constituents of Millettia hookeriana include isoflavones, flavonoids, and alkaloids, such as millettin and homomillettin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

Traditionally, Millettia hookeriana has been used to make various herbal preparations, including teas, capsules, and extracts, which are often used to alleviate symptoms of menopause, improve cardiovascular health, and boost immune function.
