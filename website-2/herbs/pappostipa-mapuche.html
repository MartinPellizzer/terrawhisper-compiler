Pappostipa mapuche

Pappostipa mapuche, also known as Mapuche Grass, is a plant species native to Chile, belonging to the Poaceae family.

The bioactive constituents of this plant include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and sesquiterpenes, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
