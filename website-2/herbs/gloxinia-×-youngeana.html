Gloxinia × youngeana

Gloxinia × youngeana, commonly known as Younghusband's Gloxinia, is a rare and endangered hybrid plant species within the Gloxinia genus, characterized by its unique, bell-shaped flowers and dark green, heart-shaped leaves.

Traditionally used in herbal medicine, Gloxinia × youngeana is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may aid in reducing inflammation and protecting against cell damage. Bioactive constituents of this plant include iridoids, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Gloxinia × youngeana is often prepared as a tea or tincture, and is used to treat various ailments, including fever, colds, and respiratory issues.
