The species name 'integrifolium' comes from the Latin words 'integer' meaning whole and 'folium' meaning leaf. It is also known as whole-leaf hawkweed due to its distinctive leaf shape. This classification allows for a more detailed understanding of the plant's characteristics and its place within the larger plant kingdom.
