Oxalis langloisii

Oxalis langloisii, also known as the wood sorrel, is a perennial herb belonging to the Oxalidaceae family, characterized by its distinctive white flowers and heart-shaped leaves with pointed tips.

Oxalis langloisii contains bioactive constituents such as oxalic acid, flavonoids, and ascorbic acid, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
