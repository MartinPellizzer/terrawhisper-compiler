Saxifraga obtusa

Saxifraga obtusa, also known as the Spoon-leaved Saxifrage, is a perennial plant species native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The bioactive constituents of Saxifraga obtusa include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
