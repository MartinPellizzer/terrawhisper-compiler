Distichlis distichophylla

Distichlis distichophylla, also known as Saltwort or Glasswort, is a halophytic shrub that thrives in coastal salt marshes and salt lakes, characterized by its distinctive two-ranked leaves and ability to tolerate high salt concentrations.
