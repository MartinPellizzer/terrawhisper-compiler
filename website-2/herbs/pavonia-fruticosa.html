Pavonia fruticosa

Pavonia fruticosa, also known as Mexican rosemallow or Pavonia, is a flowering plant species in the mallow family (Malvaceae) native to Mexico and Central America.

The bioactive constituents of Pavonia fruticosa include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
