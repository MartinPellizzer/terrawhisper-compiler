What is Koeleria cristata var. albescens?

Koeleria cristata var. albescens is a taxon of the species Koeleria cristata, belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is an annual or biennial grass with erect stems, 5-20 cm tall, and a caespitose growth habit. The leaves are linear, 2-7 mm wide, glabrous, and have a prominently keeled ligule. The inflorescence is a panicle, 1-3 cm long, with spikelets that are paired and pedunculate.

The spikelets have 3-5 florets, each with 2-3 florettes.
