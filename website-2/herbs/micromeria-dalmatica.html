Micromeria dalmatica

Micromeria dalmatica, also known as Balkan pennyroyal or Dalmatian pennyroyal, is a perennial herb belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae) native to the Balkan Peninsula.

The bioactive constituents of Micromeria dalmatica include essential oils, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, such as borneol, bornyl acetate, and rosmarinic acid, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
