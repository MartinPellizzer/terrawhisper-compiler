Alternanthera micrantha

Alternanthera micrantha, commonly known as 'Parrot Leaf', is a tropical plant species belonging to the family Amaranthaceae, characterized by its vibrant foliage and sprawling habit.

The bioactive constituents of Alternanthera micrantha include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoid saponins, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Alternanthera micrantha is often prepared as a decoction or infusion, and can also be found in topical creams and ointments for skin issues.
