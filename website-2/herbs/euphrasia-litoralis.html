Euphrasia litoralis

Euphrasia litoralis, also known as seaside eyebright, is a perennial plant species that belongs to the genus Euphrasia, commonly used in traditional herbal medicine.

It is valued for its potential health benefits, including its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, which may help to soothe and calm the eyes, as well as alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues such as bronchitis and coughs. The bioactive constituents of Euphrasia litoralis include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and iridoid glycosides, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Euphrasia litoralis include eye drops, capsules, and tinctures, often used to treat eye infections, conjunctivitis, and other eye-related conditions.
