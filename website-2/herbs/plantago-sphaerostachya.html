Plantago sphaerostachya

Plantago sphaerostachya, also known as blunt psyllium or Indian plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family, commonly found in dry and temperate regions.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits, including treating digestive issues, lowering cholesterol levels, and soothing skin irritations due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. The bioactive constituents of Plantago sphaerostachya include mucilages, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
