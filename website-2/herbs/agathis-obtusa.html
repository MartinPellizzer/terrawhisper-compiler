Agathis obtusa

Agathis obtusa, also known as the Damar or Agathis tree, is a species of coniferous evergreen tree in the family Araucariaceae.

The health benefits of Agathis obtusa include its potential to reduce inflammation, lower blood pressure, and exhibit antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable resource in traditional medicine. The bioactive constituents of this plant include resin, terpenes, and flavonoids, which have been shown to have antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial effects.
