Senecio angustifolius

Senecio angustifolius, also known as Narrow-leaved Ragwort or Groundsel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia, characterized by its narrow leaves and yellow daisy-like flowers.

The bioactive constituents of Senecio angustifolius include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.
