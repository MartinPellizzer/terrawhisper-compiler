Chamomilla aurea

Chamomilla aurea, commonly known as Golden Chamomile, is a flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae) native to the grasslands and dry scrublands of South Africa.

The health benefits of Golden Chamomile include its anti-inflammatory, antiseptic, and anxiolytic properties, which can help soothe digestive issues, reduce anxiety and stress, and promote relaxation. The bioactive constituents of Golden Chamomile include flavonoids, terpenoids, and sesquiterpene lactones, such as apigenin and chamazulene, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
