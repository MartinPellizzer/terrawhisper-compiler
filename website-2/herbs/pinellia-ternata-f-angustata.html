What is Pinellia ternata f. angustata?

Pinellia ternata f. angustata is a botanical variety of Pinellia ternata, a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is characterized by narrow or lance-shaped leaves that are typically green in color and have a pointed tip, typically measuring between 8-20 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white or pale yellowish flowers in a spadix, which is surrounded by a spathe, and grows in a clump-forming habit. Pinellia ternata f. angustata is native to eastern Asia, specifically China, Japan, and Korea, where it grows in moist, shaded areas.

The plant's inflorescence and leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
