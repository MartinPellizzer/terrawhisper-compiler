Psyllium scabrum

Psyllium scabrum, also known as Indian plantain, is a perennial herbaceous plant that belongs to the Plantaginaceae family.

The health benefits of Psyllium scabrum include its ability to regulate bowel movements, lower cholesterol levels, and manage blood sugar levels due to its high fiber content. The bioactive constituents of this plant include mucilage, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Psyllium scabrum is commonly prepared as a powder or infusion, and is often used to treat digestive disorders, including constipation and irritable bowel syndrome.
