Hypericum lycium

Hypericum lycium, also known as St. John's Wort, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and Asia, characterized by its yellow flowers and red berries.

The bioactive constituents of Hypericum lycium include hyperforin, hypericin, and flavonoids, which contribute to its therapeutic effects.
