Erythroxylum indicum

The bioactive constituents of Erythroxylum indicum include alkaloids, flavonoids, and phenolic compounds, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Erythroxylum indicum is commonly used to prepare teas, decoctions, and infusions, as well as being incorporated into herbal tonics and extracts to promote overall health and well-being.
