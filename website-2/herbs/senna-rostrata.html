Senna rostrata

The bioactive constituents of Senna rostrata include rostratin, sennosides, and other anthraquinone derivatives, which contribute to its laxative and anti-inflammatory effects.

Senna rostrata is traditionally used in herbal medicine and is often prepared as a dried root or leaf tea, as well as in capsules or tinctures, to promote digestive health and alleviate symptoms of irritable bowel syndrome.
