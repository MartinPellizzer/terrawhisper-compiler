Bacopa domingensis

Bacopa domingensis is a species of aquatic plant in the family Bacopa, native to the Americas, and is also known as water hyssop.

The health benefits of Bacopa domingensis include improving memory and cognitive function, enhancing neural plasticity, and reducing stress and anxiety levels. The bioactive constituents of this plant include bacosides, alkaloids, and saponins, which are responsible for its neuroprotective and nootropic effects.

The most common herbal preparations made with Bacopa domingensis include extracts, capsules, and teas, which are often used in traditional medicine to support brain health and cognitive function.
