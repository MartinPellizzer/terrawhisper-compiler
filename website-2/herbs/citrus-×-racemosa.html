Citrus × racemosa

Citrus × racemosa, also known as the Sudachi or Yuzu, is a hybrid citrus plant, resulting from the cross between Citrus sudachi and Citrus junos.

The plant is valued for its numerous health benefits, including its antimicrobial and antioxidant properties, which help to boost the immune system and protect against cell damage. The bioactive constituents of Citrus × racemosa include limonoids, flavonoids, and coumarins, which contribute to its medicinal and therapeutic properties.

The most common herbal preparations made with Citrus × racemosa include teas, essential oils, and juices, which are often used to treat digestive issues, reduce inflammation, and promote overall well-being.
