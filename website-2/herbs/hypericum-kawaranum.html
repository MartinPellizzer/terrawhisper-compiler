Hypericum kawaranum

Hypericum kawaranum, also known as Kawaran St. John's Wort, is a species of flowering plant in the St. John's Wort family (Hypericaceae) native to Japan.

The plant has been traditionally used for its medicinal properties, offering potential health benefits such as anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects, as well as its ability to alleviate mild to moderate depression due to its adaptogenic properties. The bioactive constituents of Hypericum kawaranum include hypericin, hyperforin, and flavonoids, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

In traditional herbal medicine, the plant is commonly prepared as a tea or infused oil, and is also used in capsule or tablet form to support mental health and well-being.
