Antitoxicum officinale

Antitoxicum officinale, also known as Aconitum, is a perennial plant of the Ranunculaceae family, native to mountainous regions in Asia and Europe.

The bioactive constituents of Aconitum officinale include alkaloids, such as aconitine and mesaconitine, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
