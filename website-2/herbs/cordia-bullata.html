The bioactive constituents of Cordia bullata include cordioside, a flavonoid glycoside, as well as other compounds such as alkaloids and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Cordia bullata is often prepared as an herbal tea, capsule, or tincture, and is also used in traditional medicine to treat a variety of ailments, including fever, rheumatism, and digestive issues.
