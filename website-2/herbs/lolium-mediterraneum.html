Lolium mediterraneum

Lolium mediterraneum, also known as Mediterranean ryegrass, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Europe.

This plant is valued for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which may help alleviate symptoms of respiratory issues and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Lolium mediterraneum include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

In traditional herbal medicine, Lolium mediterraneum is often used to make teas, infusions, and tinctures, which are used to treat coughs, colds, and skin irritations.
