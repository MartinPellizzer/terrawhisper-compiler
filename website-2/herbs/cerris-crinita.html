Cerris crinita

Cerris crinita, also known as the Chinese hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China, characterized by its thorny branches and showy white flowers.

The plant has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits, including reducing cholesterol levels and improving cardiovascular health due to its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. The bioactive constituents of Cerris crinita include flavonoids, phenolic acids, and triterpenoids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
