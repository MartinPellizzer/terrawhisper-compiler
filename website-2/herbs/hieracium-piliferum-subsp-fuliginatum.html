What is Hieracium piliferum subsp. fuliginatum?

Hieracium piliferum subsp.

fuliginatum is a plant endemic to the Pyrenees. It belongs to the family Asteraceae.

The plant has a height of 10-20 cm, with a rosette of basal leaves and a flowering stem with white or pale yellow flowers, arranged in a capitate inflorescence. The leaves are lanceolate, with an acute apex and a ciliate margin, while the stems are herbaceous and hirsute.

The inflorescence is surrounded by a ring of white or yellowish bracts, which are often ciliate.
