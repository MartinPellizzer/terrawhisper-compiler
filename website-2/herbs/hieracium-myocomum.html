Hieracium myocomum

Hieracium myocomum, also known as Myocomma hawkweed, is a perennial plant species belonging to the dandelion family (Asteraceae) characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy leaves.

The bioactive constituents of Hieracium myocomum include sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
