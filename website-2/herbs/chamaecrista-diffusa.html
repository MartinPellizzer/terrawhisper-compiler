Chamaecrista diffusa

Chamaecrista diffusa, also known as partridge pea or sensitive plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits, including anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties, which may help to treat various ailments such as fever, rheumatism, and skin conditions. The bioactive constituents of Chamaecrista diffusa include flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.
