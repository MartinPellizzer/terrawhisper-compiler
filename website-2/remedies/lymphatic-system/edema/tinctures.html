11 Tinctures to Help Diabetics with Edema Naturally

When you have diabetes, your body can't use sugar (glucose) properly, and this can lead to high blood sugar levels.

If left uncontrolled, diabetes can damage the tiny blood vessels in your body, making it harder for your body to get rid of extra fluids. This can cause swelling in your feet, ankles, and legs, a condition known as edema. Edema can be painful and make it hard to move around, which can be a big problem for people with diabetes who already have trouble with their feet and legs due to nerve damage or poor circulation. Edema can also make it harder to fit into your shoes and can be a sign of a more serious problem, like kidney damage or heart disease.

If you have diabetes and edema, you may need to take medication to lower your blood pressure, or to reduce fluid buildup in your body. But there's another way to help manage edema - herbal tinctures. These are liquid extracts from plants that have been used for centuries to help reduce swelling and improve circulation. They work by relaxing the blood vessels and helping your body get rid of excess fluids.

They can be taken orally and are often used in combination with other treatments to help manage edema and improve overall health.
