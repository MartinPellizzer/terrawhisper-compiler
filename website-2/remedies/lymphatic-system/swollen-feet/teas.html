1. Achillea millefolium

Achillea millefolium teas contains bioactive constituents like sesquiterpene lactones, flavonoids, and phenolic acids.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce swelling in the feet. The sesquiterpene lactones, particularly acetorin and leucodine, have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes, thereby reducing inflammation. The flavonoids in Achillea millefolium teas, such as kaempferol and quercetin, also have potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects, which can help soothe and calm swollen feet.

By reducing inflammation and promoting healing, Achillea millefolium teas can help alleviate swollen feet.

  1. Gather 1 tablespoon of dried Achillea millefolium leaves.
  2. Measure 1 cup of boiling water and pour it over the leaves in a cup.
  3. Let the leaves steep in the hot water for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Strain the liquid and discard the leaves.
  5. Drink the tea warm, 2-3 times a day, to help reduce swelling in your feet.

4. Hamamelis virginiana

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in swollen feet?

What time of day is best to drink herbal tea for swollen feet?
