8 Herbal Teas For Swollen Feet

As runners, we put a lot of stress on our feet, which can lead to swelling.

This is because of the repeated impact and pressure on our feet, especially in our ankles and toes. When we run, our feet absorb the shock of each step, which can cause the blood vessels to swell and fill with fluid. This can lead to painful and tender feet, especially after a long run. Swollen feet can also affect our daily lives, making it hard to walk or even stand for long periods. It can be painful to put on shoes or socks, and even simple tasks like cooking or cleaning can become challenging.

To make matters worse, swollen feet can also lead to other issues like plantar fasciitis or Achilles tendonitis. So, what can we do to relieve our swollen feet?. One option is to try herbal teas that have been shown to help reduce swelling and promote relaxation. These teas work by helping to reduce inflammation and improve blood flow, which can help to reduce the swelling in our feet. They also promote relaxation, which can help to reduce stress and anxiety that can contribute to swelling.

When we drink these teas, they can help to calm our body and mind, making it easier to recover from our runs and get back to our normal activities.
