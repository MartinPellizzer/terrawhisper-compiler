1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis creams contains triterpene saponins, flavonoids, and carotenoids as active constituents.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties, which help to reduce swelling and promote healing in the skin. The flavonoids in Calendula officinalis creams also have a soothing effect on the skin, which can help to ease pain and discomfort associated with swollen feet. The antiseptic properties of the triterpene saponins in Calendula officinalis creams can help to prevent infection and promote a healthy environment for the skin to heal.

By reducing inflammation and promoting healing, Calendula officinalis creams can help to alleviate swollen feet and provide relief from discomfort.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Calendula officinalis flowers, 1 cup of coconut oil, and 1/2 cup of beeswax in a heat-proof bowl.
  2. Combine the Calendula flowers and coconut oil in the bowl. Heat the mixture in a double boiler or a microwave-safe bowl in 10-second increments, stirring between each interval, until the oil is warm.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl. Discard the Calendula flowers.
  4. Add 1/4 cup of beeswax to the oil mixture and heat it in the double boiler or microwave until the beeswax is melted.
  5. Remove the bowl from the heat and let it cool for 10 minutes. Whip the mixture until it thickens and becomes creamy. Pour the Calendula cream into a glass jar and store it in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.
