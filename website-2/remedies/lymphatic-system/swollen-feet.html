Herbal Remedies for Swollen Feet: Causes and Effective Treatments

What are the causes of swollen feet?

Swollen feet can be caused by several issues.

Varicose veins are a common cause, where the veins in your legs become enlarged and twisted. This makes it harder for blood to flow back up to the heart, causing fluid to build up in your feet. Edema, on the other hand, is a condition where fluid builds up in your body due to poor circulation or other medical conditions.

This excess fluid puts pressure on your feet, causing them to swell. Diabetes can also cause swollen feet because it can damage the nerves in your legs, leading to poor circulation and fluid buildup. Kidney failure is another cause, where the kidneys are not able to filter waste and excess fluids from the body, resulting in fluid buildup in the feet.

Similarly, heart failure can cause swollen feet because the heart is not able to pump enough blood throughout the body, leading to poor circulation and fluid buildup in the feet.
