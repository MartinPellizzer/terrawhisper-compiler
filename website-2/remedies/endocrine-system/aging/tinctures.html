1. Panax ginseng

Panax ginseng tinctures contains active constituents like ginsenosides, which have been found to help slow down the aging process.

These compounds have antioxidant properties, neutralizing free radicals that damage cells and contribute to aging. Ginsenosides also stimulate blood flow, improving circulation and delivery of oxygen and nutrients to cells, which is essential for maintaining healthy skin and organs. Additionally, Panax ginseng tinctures contain eleutherosides, which have anti-inflammatory properties, reducing inflammation and cell damage associated with aging.

By promoting healthy cell function and reducing oxidative stress, Panax ginseng tinctures may help support overall health and well-being as we age.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Panax ginseng root, a glass jar with a lid, and a cup.
  2. Combine the dried root and 2 cups of 80-proof vodka in the glass jar.
  3. Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar once a week.
  4. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth into another glass jar, discarding the solids.
  5. Store the tincture in the fridge, using 20-30 drops per day as needed.

2. Withania somnifera

Withania somnifera tinctures contains a high concentration of bioactive constituents such as withanolides, alkaloids, and saponins.

These compounds have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which help protect cells from damage caused by free radicals and oxidative stress. The tannins and glycosides in Withania somnifera tinctures also have a soothing effect on the body, reducing inflammation and promoting relaxation. Withanolides, in particular, have been shown to stimulate the production of antioxidants and protect against cell death, helping to mitigate the effects of aging on the body.

By reducing oxidative stress and inflammation, Withania somnifera tinctures may help support healthy aging and promote cellular well-being.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Withania somnifera roots, 2 cups of vodka (95% or higher), and a clean glass jar.
  2. Chop the Withania somnifera roots into small pieces and place them in the glass jar.
  3. Pour 2 cups of vodka over the chopped roots, making sure they are completely covered.

3. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba tinctures contains flavonoids, bilobalide, and ginkgolides as its active constituents.

These compounds help improve blood flow to the brain and other vital organs, which is essential for maintaining healthy cognitive function as we age. The flavonoids and ginkgolides also act as antioxidants, protecting the brain cells from oxidative stress and damage caused by free radicals. Regular use of ginkgo biloba tinctures may help slow down age-related cognitive decline and support overall brain health.

  3. Combine 1 cup of the crushed leaves with 2 cups of vodka in a clean glass jar. Stir well.
  4. Store the jar in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks. Shake the jar every day to help the mixture.
