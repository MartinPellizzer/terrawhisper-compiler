If you have hypothyroidism, there are some herbal creams you should avoid.

Another herb to avoid is licorice root (Glycyrrhiza uralensis is not mentioned, but its relative Glycyrrhiza glabra is), as it can raise blood pressure and worsen heart conditions associated with hypothyroidism. Additionally, you should be cautious with creams containing ashwagandha (Withania is not mentioned, but its relative 'Ashwagandha' is), as high doses can interfere with thyroid function. Furthermore, some herbal creams contain saw palmetto (Serenoa repens), which can interact with thyroid medications and worsen hypothyroidism.

Finally, those containing ginkgo biloba should be avoided, as it can lower blood pressure, which can be a problem for people with hypothyroidism who may already have low blood pressure.
