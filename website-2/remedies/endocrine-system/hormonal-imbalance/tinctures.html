9 Herbal Tinctures For Hormonal Imbalance

As people age, their hormone levels can change.

This is because the body's natural hormone production slows down, and sometimes, it can become imbalanced. Hormonal imbalance can cause many problems in older adults, such as hot flashes, mood swings, sleep disturbances, and changes in appetite and weight. It can also affect their bone density, leading to osteoporosis, and increase their risk of heart disease and diabetes. For some older adults, hormonal imbalance can even lead to cognitive decline and memory loss. These symptoms can be distressing and affect their daily lives, making it difficult for them to manage their daily activities, enjoy their hobbies, and maintain their relationships. Hormonal imbalance in older adults can also be caused by other factors, such as certain medical conditions, medications, and lifestyle changes.

For example, some older adults may be taking medications that can disrupt their hormone levels, or they may be experiencing stress, which can also affect their hormone balance. Additionally, some older adults may have underlying conditions, such as thyroid problems or polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS), which can also contribute to hormonal imbalance. To address hormonal imbalance, some older adults may turn to conventional treatments, such as hormone replacement therapy (HRT). However, HRT can have its own set of risks and side effects, and not all older adults may be suitable candidates for this treatment. This is where herbal tinctures come in. Herbal tinctures are liquid extracts made from plants that have been used for centuries to support hormone balance and overall health. They work by stimulating the body's natural hormone production, reducing inflammation, and promoting relaxation and stress relief.

By taking herbal tinctures, older adults may be able to alleviate their symptoms and improve their overall well-being without the risks associated with conventional treatments.
