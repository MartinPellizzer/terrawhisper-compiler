Withania somnifera, also known as ashwagandha, is an adaptogenic herb that helps the body adapt to stress and can increase energy levels without overstimulating the body. This is particularly helpful for people with adrenal fatigue, as they often have trouble regulating their energy levels.

Fixing adrenal fatigue is essential because it can affect every aspect of a person's life. When the body is not producing enough cortisol, it can lead to a range of symptoms, from fatigue and brain fog to mood swings and weight gain. Using herbal decoctions to manage adrenal fatigue can be a natural and effective way to improve energy levels and overall well-being.
