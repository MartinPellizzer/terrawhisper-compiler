Another one to avoid is 'Cuscuta epithymum'. This herb can increase thyroid-stimulating hormone (TSH) levels, which can worsen hyperthyroidism.

Also, 'Ginkgo biloba' should be avoided. It can increase the risk of bleeding and interact with blood thinners, which people with hyperthyroidism may be taking. Similarly, 'Panax ginseng' should be avoided, as it can increase cortisol levels and worsen hyperthyroidism symptoms. People with hyperthyroidism should also avoid 'Rauvolfia serpentina', as it can increase the risk of low blood pressure and interact with medications.

Lastly, 'Sutherlandia frutescens' and 'Terminalia arjuna' should be avoided due to the potential to increase thyroid hormone levels and interact with medications.
