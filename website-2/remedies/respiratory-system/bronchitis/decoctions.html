What herbal decoctions you should avoid if you have bronchitis?

If you have bronchitis, it's best to avoid certain herbal decoctions that can worsen symptoms or interact with medications.

You should also avoid Khat, or Catha edulis, as it can cause increased heart rate and blood pressure, which can be problematic for people with bronchitis. Additionally, Cichorium intybus, or Chicory, can cause stomach upset and diarrhea, which can further dehydrate a person with bronchitis.

Lastly, Digitalis purpurea, or Foxglove, should be avoided as it can be toxic and interact with medications.
