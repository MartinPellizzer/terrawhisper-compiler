By using Echinacea purpurea tinctures, you can help your body recover from laryngitis more efficiently.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Echinacea purpurea flowers and leaves, fresh or dried.
  2. Use 2 cups of 80-proof vodka or high-proof vodka (at least 40% ABV) in a clean glass jar.
