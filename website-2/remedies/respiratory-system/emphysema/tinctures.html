7 Herbal Tinctures For Emphysema

As people age, their lungs can lose their ability to function properly, making it harder to breathe.

This condition is called emphysema. Older adults with emphysema may experience shortness of breath, even when doing simple tasks like walking or talking. They may also cough and wheeze a lot, which can disrupt their sleep and daily activities. Emphysema can be caused by smoking, exposure to pollutants, or genetics. It's a progressive disease, meaning it gets worse over time.

Older adults with emphysema may need to use oxygen tanks or take medication to manage their symptoms. However, these treatments may have side effects and can be expensive. This is where herbal tinctures come in. They are liquid extracts made from plants that have been used for centuries to help with breathing and other health issues. Some herbal tinctures may help to open up airways, reduce inflammation, and even boost the immune system.

By taking these tinctures, older adults may be able to breathe easier and improve their quality of life.
