Wheezing Treatment with Medicinal Herbs and Herbal Preparations

What are the causes of wheezing?

Wheezing is a high-pitched sound that occurs when air passes through narrowed airways in the lungs.

There are several reasons why people may experience wheezing. Asthma is a common cause, where the airways become inflamed and narrowed, leading to wheezing and difficulty breathing. This is often due to an allergic reaction or sensitivity to certain substances, such as dust mites, pollen, or pet dander. Another cause of wheezing is bronchitis, an inflammation of the bronchial tubes that can be triggered by a viral or bacterial infection.

When the bronchial tubes become inflamed, they narrow, causing the characteristic wheezing sound. Pneumonia is a more severe lung infection that can also lead to wheezing, especially if the infection is caused by a type of bacteria called Streptococcus pneumoniae. Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) is a long-term lung condition that can cause wheezing, as well as shortness of breath and coughing. This is often due to smoking or exposure to pollutants and toxins over a long period.

Bronchiectasis is another lung condition that can cause wheezing, where the bronchial tubes become permanently damaged and widened, making it harder for air to pass through and leading to wheezing and recurring infections.
