'Foeniculum vulgare' (Fennel oil) can be too pungent for the throat, and its anise-like flavor may not be suitable for people with sensitive throats. 'Juniperus communis' (Juniper berry oil) can be too strong for the throat due to its astringent and diuretic properties, and may cause dryness and irritation.

These oils may not be safe for direct application to the throat area, so it's best to consult with a healthcare professional or an aromatherapist before using them.
