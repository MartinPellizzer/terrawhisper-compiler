1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale creams contains active constituents like gingerols and shogaols, which have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

These properties help reduce swelling in the nasal passages and throat, making it easier to breathe. The anti-inflammatory properties also help soothe the airways, reducing congestion and mucous production. The antioxidant properties help protect the airways from oxidative stress, which can contribute to snoring.

By reducing swelling, soothing the airways, and protecting against oxidative stress, Zingiber officinale creams may help alleviate snoring symptoms.

  1. Gather 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil, and 2 tablespoons of Zingiber officinale essential oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl in a pot of simmering water.
  3. Once melted, remove from heat and stir in the vitamin E oil and Zingiber officinale essential oil.
  4. Pour the mixture into a heat-proof container and let it cool until solidified.
  5. Apply a small amount of the Zingiber officinale cream to your nostrils or throat area before sleeping to help reduce snoring.

2. Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus creams contains eucalyptol, cineole, and globulol as its main active constituents.

These compounds have expectorant and anti-inflammatory properties, which can help relieve congestion and swelling in the nasal passages and throat. By reducing swelling and congestion, Eucalyptus globulus creams can help open up airways and improve breathing, making it easier to breathe while sleeping. This can lead to a reduction in snoring caused by restricted airflow.

The anti-inflammatory properties of Eucalyptus globulus creams may also help soothe and calm the mucous membranes, further reducing inflammation and congestion.

  1. Gather 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, and 2 tablespoons of dried Eucalyptus globulus leaves in a double boiler.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in the double boiler over low heat, stirring until smooth.
  3. Add 2 cups of distilled water to the melted mixture and stir well.
  4. Steep the Eucalyptus globulus leaves in the mixture for 2 hours, then strain the mixture and discard the leaves.
  5. Pour the mixture into a tin and let it cool and solidify before using it as a cream to help relieve snoring.
