Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus, also known as blue gum, is a plant that is commonly used to help with mucus in the throat. The leaves of this plant contain a type of oil called eucalyptol, which is known for its ability to thin and clear out mucus. When you breathe in the scent of eucalyptus globulus, the eucalyptol oil goes directly into your lungs and throat, where it helps to break down the thick, sticky mucus that can cause congestion and coughing. This makes it easier to cough up the mucus and feel more comfortable. Eucalyptus globulus is also an anti-inflammatory, which means it can help to reduce swelling and irritation in the throat, making it a popular ingredient in cough drops, inhalers, and other products designed to help with respiratory issues.
