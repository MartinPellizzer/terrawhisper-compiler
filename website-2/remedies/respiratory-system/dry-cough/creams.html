What herbal creams you should avoid if you have dry cough?

'Digitalis purpurea' (foxglove) is another herb to avoid, as it can cause heart problems if ingested in large amounts or used for extended periods. 'Hydrastis canadensis' (goldenseal) and 'Hyssopus officinalis' (hyssop) can interact with medications or worsen dry cough.
