Fixing the issue of altitude sickness is crucial because it allows you to fully enjoy your time at high elevations, whether you're hiking, traveling, or engaging in other outdoor activities.

By alleviating the symptoms of altitude sickness, you can improve your quality of life and enjoy the benefits of high-altitude environments without the discomfort and risk associated with altitude sickness.
