10 Herbal Creams to Help Hikers Overcome Altitude Sickness

When hiking at high elevations, the air is thinner and there's less oxygen.

This can cause problems for hikers, as their bodies aren't getting the oxygen they need. The brain, heart, and other organs can get damaged from a lack of oxygen, leading to altitude sickness. Symptoms can range from mild headaches and fatigue to more serious issues like nausea, vomiting, and even seizures. In severe cases, altitude sickness can be life-threatening. Hikers may need to stop their ascent, rest, and take medication to alleviate their symptoms. However, this can be a setback, and hikers may lose valuable time on their trip. As hikers ascend higher, their bodies take time to adapt to the lower oxygen levels.

This process can be slow, and some people may not be able to adapt at all. Factors like physical fitness, age, and pre-existing medical conditions can also play a role in how well someone adapts to high altitudes. Hikers who are not prepared for altitude sickness may experience severe symptoms, which can ruin their trip and even their health. Herbal creams have been used by hikers to help alleviate symptoms of altitude sickness. These creams typically contain plant extracts that have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. They can be applied to the skin, where they're absorbed into the body, helping to reduce swelling and inflammation. By using these creams, hikers may be able to reduce their symptoms and speed up their body's adaptation to high altitudes.

This can help them continue their ascent and enjoy their trip without being held back by altitude sickness.

When using Ginkgo biloba creams for altitude sickness, it's best to apply a small amount to the skin and gently massage it in.
