Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba is a plant that has been used for centuries to help with breathing problems. It is believed to work well for shortness of breath because it helps improve blood flow to the lungs. This is done by widening the blood vessels, allowing more oxygen to reach the body's cells. When the blood vessels are widened, the heart doesn't have to work as hard, which can help reduce fatigue and make breathing easier. Ginkgo biloba also has anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce swelling in the lungs and airways, further improving breathing. Some people also believe that Ginkgo biloba can help improve the efficiency of oxygen use by the body's cells, which can help make breathing feel less labored.
