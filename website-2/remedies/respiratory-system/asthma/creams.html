9 Herbal Creams For Asthma

Smoking is a bad habit that not only affects the smoker but also their family members.

When someone smokes, they release chemicals into the air that can irritate the lungs and cause problems with breathing. This is especially true for asthma, a condition where the airways in the lungs become narrow and swollen, making it hard to breathe. If one or both parents smoke, their children are more likely to develop asthma, and it's not just because they're exposed to second-hand smoke. The chemicals in cigarette smoke can also pass through the mother's bloodstream to the baby during pregnancy, increasing the risk of asthma in their child. For families where someone smokes, life can be challenging. Simple tasks like going for a walk or playing outside can become difficult and stressful.

Asthma attacks can be triggered by anything from pollen to dust, and for smokers' families, even the smallest things can set off an asthma attack. This can lead to constant worry and anxiety about when the next attack will happen, and it can be exhausting trying to avoid triggers all the time. But there is some hope. Herbal creams have been found to be effective in helping to reduce asthma symptoms and improve lung function. These creams work by soothing and calming the airways in the lungs, making it easier to breathe. They can be applied directly to the skin, usually on the chest or back, and can help to relax the muscles in the airways, reducing inflammation and congestion.

By using herbal creams, smokers' families may be able to find some relief from the symptoms of asthma and enjoy a better quality of life.
