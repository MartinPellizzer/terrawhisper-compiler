What herbal teas you should avoid if you have chest congestion?

If you have chest congestion, you may want to avoid some herbal teas that can make the condition worse.

For example, tea made from Yerba Mate (Ilex paraguariensis) is not a good choice because it's a stimulant that can increase heart rate and blood pressure, making congestion worse. Another tea to avoid is those made from Cinnamon (Cinnamomum verum), as it can be too spicy and heat up the body, which can irritate the lungs and make congestion more uncomfortable. Also, teas made from Ginger-like herbs like Galangal (Alpinia galanga) or Kava (Piper methysticum) may not be a good choice because they can cause stomach upset or allergic reactions, which can worsen congestion.

Additionally, teas made from Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) or other herbs that act as stimulants or sedatives may interact with medications or worsen underlying health conditions, so it's best to consult a doctor before trying them.
