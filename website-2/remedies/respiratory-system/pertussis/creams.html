Antibiotics are often prescribed to treat pertussis, so using valerian root cream could reduce the effectiveness of your medication.

You should also avoid creams containing Saw Palmetto, which can slow down the healing process of wounds. This is a concern, as pertussis can cause severe coughing, which can lead to open sores in the mouth and nose. Additionally, avoid creams with Licorice root, which can cause blood pressure to rise.
