Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba is a plant that has been used for a long time to help people with altitude sickness. This condition happens when people go to high places and their bodies can't get enough oxygen. Ginkgo biloba helps by improving blood flow to the brain and other important parts of the body. It also helps to dilate blood vessels, which means they get wider and can carry more oxygen. This makes it easier for the body to get the oxygen it needs, even at high altitudes. Some people who take Ginkgo biloba for altitude sickness report fewer headaches, nausea, and fatigue, which are common symptoms of the condition. Ginkgo biloba may also help to reduce inflammation in the body, which can make altitude sickness worse. It's thought that Ginkgo biloba works by improving the body's ability to adapt to high altitudes, rather than just treating the symptoms of the condition.
