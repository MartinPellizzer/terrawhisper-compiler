Asthma: Causes, Symptoms, and Herbal Treatments

Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus is a type of plant that has been used for a long time to help people with asthma. The leaves of this plant contain a medicine called eucalyptol, which makes breathing easier. When you inhale eucalyptol, it opens up the airways in your lungs, so you can breathe more easily. This helps to relieve the symptoms of asthma, such as wheezing and coughing. Eucalyptol also has anti-inflammatory properties, which means it can help to reduce swelling in the airways and make it easier to breathe. Many people use eucalyptus oil, which is extracted from the leaves, in a steam inhaler or a humidifier to help their asthma. It's also found in cough drops and inhalers that are used to treat asthma.

When thyme's anti-inflammatory compounds are inhaled, they can help reduce this inflammation and make breathing easier. By reducing inflammation, protecting the lungs, and preventing infections, thyme may be a useful addition to a treatment plan for people with asthma.
