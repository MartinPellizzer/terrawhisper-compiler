You should also avoid Tea Tree (Melaleuca alternifolia), which can be too harsh for your throat and cause more irritation. Additionally, Green Tea (Camellia sinensis) might not be the best choice if you're sensitive to caffeine, as it contains a small amount that can make your cough worse.

Last but not least, you should be cautious with Peppermint (Agastache rugosa) as it can cause your airways to relax, leading to more congestion and discomfort.
