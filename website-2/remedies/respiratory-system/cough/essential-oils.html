To make Zingiber officinale, also known as Ginger, herbal essential oil that helps with cough, you will need fresh ginger roots, a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil, a glass jar, and a clean environment.

First, peel the fresh ginger roots and chop them into small pieces. Next, combine the chopped ginger with a carrier oil in a glass jar, making sure the ginger pieces are fully covered. Store the jar in a cool, dark place and let it sit for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily.

This process is called maceration, which helps to release the active compounds from the ginger into the carrier oil. After the maceration period, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another container, discarding the ginger solids. The resulting liquid is the Ginger herbal essential oil, which can be used to help soothe a cough.

To use it, mix a few drops of the oil with a carrier oil or water and rub it on the chest or inhale it through steam inhalation.
