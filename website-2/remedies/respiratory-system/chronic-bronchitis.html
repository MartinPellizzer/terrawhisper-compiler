When you breathe in the vapors from eucalyptus globulus, it helps loosen and clear out the mucus that's causing the problem. This can help reduce the frequency and severity of coughing fits. Additionally, eucalyptus globulus has anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce swelling in the airways and make it easier to breathe. Some people also find that it helps reduce congestion and opens up their airways, making it easier to take deep breaths. This can be especially helpful for people with chronic bronchitis who often feel short of breath.
