Bronchitis: The Causes, Medicinal Herbs, and Homeopathic Preparations

What are the causes of bronchitis?

Bronchitis is a lung infection that causes inflammation in the airways.

There are several main causes of bronchitis. One of the most significant causes is tobacco smoke. When you smoke or inhale secondhand smoke, the chemicals in it can irritate your airways, making them inflamed and swollen. This can lead to a cough, mucus production, and difficulty breathing. Air pollution is another major cause of bronchitis. When you breathe in polluted air, it can irritate your airways and trigger an infection. This can be due to traffic fumes, dust, or other airborne pollutants.

People who live in areas with high levels of air pollution are more likely to develop bronchitis. Influenza, also known as the flu, can also cause bronchitis. When you have the flu, it can spread to your airways and cause an infection. This can be especially serious if you have a pre-existing lung condition. Pneumonia is another type of lung infection that can lead to bronchitis. When you have pneumonia, it can spread to your airways and cause inflammation. Lastly, viral infections can also cause bronchitis. These infections can be spread through contact with an infected person, and can cause inflammation in the airways.

Some common viral infections that can cause bronchitis include the common cold and respiratory syncytial virus (RSV).
