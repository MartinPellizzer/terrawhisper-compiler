Many people with hay fever also suffer from eczema or skin allergies, which can cause itchy, red, and dry skin. Herbs like Aloe Vera and Calendula have soothing and anti-inflammatory properties that can help calm irritated skin and reduce itching. Some people with hay fever may also experience digestive issues, such as bloating, gas, and stomach pain, due to food sensitivities or allergies. Herbs like Peppermint and Ginger can help ease digestive discomfort and reduce inflammation in the gut.

These medicinal herbs work by reducing inflammation, fighting off infections, and soothing irritated tissues, which can help alleviate symptoms of these related health issues.
