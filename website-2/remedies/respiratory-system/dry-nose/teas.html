10 best herbal teas for dry nose

1. Eucalyptus Globulus

Eucalyptus globulus tea is a natural remedy for a dry nose. This tea is made from the leaves of the eucalyptus tree, which are rich in a compound called eucalyptol. Eucalyptol has strong decongestant properties, which means it helps to clear mucus from the nasal passages. When you drink eucalyptus globulus tea, the eucalyptol is absorbed into your body and travels to your nose, where it breaks down the thick mucus that's causing your congestion. This helps to open up your airways and makes it easier to breathe. The tea also has anti-inflammatory properties, which helps to reduce swelling in the nasal passages and sinuses. This makes it easier for mucus to drain out, relieving congestion and dryness.
