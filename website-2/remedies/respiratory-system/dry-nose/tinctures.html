1. Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus tinctures contains the bioactive constituents cineole and borneol.

Cineole is known for its anti-inflammatory and decongestant properties, which help to reduce swelling and loosen mucus in the nasal passages. Borneol, on the other hand, has a soothing effect on the mucous membranes, making it easier to breathe. The expectorant properties of cineole help to bring up phlegm and mucus, relieving congestion and dryness in the nose.

Regular use of eucalyptus globulus tinctures may help to clear nasal passages and provide relief from dryness and congestion.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh Eucalyptus globulus leaves and 2 cups of vodka in a clean glass container.
  2. Add the fresh Eucalyptus leaves to the vodka and make sure they are fully covered.
  3. Steep the mixture in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the container every day.
  4. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass container, discarding the solids.
  5. Store the Eucalyptus tincture in a dark glass bottle and use 5-10 drops in a cup of water to relieve dry nose symptoms.
