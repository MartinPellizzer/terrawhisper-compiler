One common problem is fatigue. This is because the body is not getting enough oxygen, making it difficult to do daily tasks. Medicinal herbs like Ashwagandha can help reduce fatigue. It has anti-inflammatory properties that improve blood flow and increase oxygen supply to the body. Another issue people with shortness of breath may face is anxiety. This is because the body is under stress, making it hard to breathe. Herbs like Lavender and Chamomile have a calming effect, which can help reduce anxiety. They also promote relaxation, making it easier to breathe. People with shortness of breath may also experience insomnia. This is because they may have trouble falling asleep or staying asleep due to difficulty breathing. Herbs like Valerian root can help with sleep.

It has a sedative effect, making it easier to fall asleep and stay asleep. In addition, people with shortness of breath may experience muscle weakness. This is because the body is not getting enough oxygen, making it difficult to move. Herbs like Ginkgo Biloba can help improve circulation and increase oxygen supply to the muscles. This can help reduce muscle weakness. People with shortness of breath may also experience dizziness or lightheadedness. This is because the body is not getting enough oxygen, making it difficult to stay steady. Herbs like Peppermint can help improve circulation and reduce dizziness. It also has a cooling effect, which can help reduce inflammation in the body. Finally, people with shortness of breath may experience a dry cough. This is because the body is producing mucus to help clear the airways. Herbs like Thyme and Echinacea can help reduce inflammation in the airways and promote mucus production.

They also have antimicrobial properties, which can help fight off infections.
