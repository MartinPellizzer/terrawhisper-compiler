7 remedies for asthmatics with throat congestion using herbal creams

Asthmatics often experience throat congestion due to inflammation and irritation caused by the air they breathe.

This can be triggered by allergens, pollution, or even certain foods. Throat congestion can also lead to coughing, wheezing, and shortness of breath, making it hard for asthmatics to go about their daily lives. They may need to use their inhalers more frequently, or even be hospitalized in severe cases.

To manage throat congestion, many asthmatics turn to over-the-counter medications or prescription treatments. However, these can have side effects and may not be effective for everyone. This is where herbal creams come in - they provide an alternative way to soothe and calm the throat. These creams typically contain anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties that help reduce swelling and kill bacteria. They can be used in combination with other treatments, or on their own, to help manage symptoms.
