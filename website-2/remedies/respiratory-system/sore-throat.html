Sore Throat Causes, Herbal Cures, and Medicinal Plant Remedies

What are the causes of sore throat?

A sore throat can be caused by several things.

A viral infection is one of the most common causes. This type of infection occurs when a virus enters your body and attacks the tissues in your throat. It can make your throat feel sore, scratchy, and swollen. A bacterial infection is another possible cause. This happens when bacteria, like strep, multiply in your throat and cause an infection.

Tonsillitis is also a cause of sore throat. It occurs when your tonsils become inflamed and infected, often due to a bacterial infection. Mononucleosis, also known as mono, is a viral infection that affects your lymph nodes and can cause a sore throat. Laryngitis is a cause of sore throat as well. This occurs when your vocal cords become inflamed, often due to overuse or a viral infection. When you have laryngitis, it can be painful to talk or make sounds.

All these causes can lead to a sore throat, and it's essential to know the underlying cause to get the right treatment.
