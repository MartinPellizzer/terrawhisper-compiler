One of these changes is the increased pressure on the stomach, which can cause a hiatus hernia. This is when part of the stomach bulges up into the chest through an opening in the diaphragm. This can make everyday activities, like eating and sleeping, very challenging.

As the pregnancy progresses, the symptoms can worsen, affecting the woman's quality of life. She may become irritable, anxious, and feel like she's losing control. In some cases, the hernia can even cause breathing difficulties and chest pain. Herbal creams can be a helpful alternative for pregnant women who want to avoid medication or surgery. These creams contain natural ingredients that soothe and calm the stomach, reducing inflammation and discomfort. They can be applied directly to the affected area, providing quick relief from symptoms.

The creams also help to strengthen the muscles that support the stomach, making it less likely for the hernia to occur.
