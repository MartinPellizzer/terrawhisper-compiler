1. Curcuma longa

Curcuma longa teas contains compounds like curcumin, demethoxycurcumin, and bisdemethoxycurcumin, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

These bioactive constituents have potent anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce swelling and pain in the abdominal region. Curcumin also has antioxidant properties that prevent cell damage and promote healing, which can help alleviate abdominal pain caused by inflammation. Additionally, curcuma longa teas has natural anti-spasmodic properties that can help relax the muscles in the abdominal area, reducing cramping and discomfort.

By consuming curcuma longa teas, the bioactive compounds can enter the body and start to work on reducing abdominal pain and inflammation.

  1. Gather 1 tsp of dried Curcuma longa roots and 1 cup of water in a saucepan.
  2. Heat the water over medium heat until it starts boiling.
