People with a sour taste in their mouth may also be more susceptible to oral thrush, a fungal infection that causes white patches on the tongue and inside the cheeks. Herbs like tea tree oil and eucalyptus can help kill the fungus and reduce inflammation. Finally, some people with a sour taste in their mouth may experience sinusitis, an infection of the sinuses.

Herbs like eucalyptus and thyme can help clear mucus from the sinuses and reduce inflammation, which can help alleviate a sour taste in the mouth.
