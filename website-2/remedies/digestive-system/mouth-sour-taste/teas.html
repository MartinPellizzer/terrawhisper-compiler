1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale teas contains compounds like gingerols and shogaols, which have anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce swelling and irritation in the mouth.

These compounds also have antimicrobial properties, which can help combat the growth of bacteria that cause bad breath and a sour taste. The antioxidants present in ginger, such as gingerols and shogaols, can help neutralize free radicals and reduce inflammation in the mouth. By reducing inflammation and promoting saliva production, Zingiber officinale teas can help alleviate a sour taste in the mouth.
