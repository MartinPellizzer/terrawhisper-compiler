Ulcerative colitis is a painful and stressful condition that affects many Caucasians worldwide.

Simple tasks like traveling or even just eating a meal can become daunting experiences.

People with ulcerative colitis may also struggle with anxiety, depression, and fatigue due to the chronic nature of the condition and the impact it has on their physical and mental well-being. As a result, they may need to make significant lifestyle changes, such as following a special diet or taking medication to manage their symptoms. However, for those who are looking for alternative or complementary approaches to manage their condition, herbal essential oils have shown promise in providing relief.
