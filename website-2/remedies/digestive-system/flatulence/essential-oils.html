As people age, their digestion can slow down, making it harder for their body to break down food properly.

Fortunately, there are natural remedies that can help alleviate flatulence in older adults. Certain herbal essential oils have been shown to have anti-inflammatory and carminative properties, helping to reduce gas and bloating. These oils can be inhaled, applied topically, or added to baths to promote relaxation and ease digestive discomfort.
