As people age, their digestion can slow down, making it harder for their body to break down food properly.

This can lead to feelings of isolation and frustration, making it harder for older adults to enjoy their golden years. Flatulence can also be a sign of underlying health issues, such as irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), gastroparesis, or gastrointestinal infections.

These conditions can be serious and require medical attention. However, some older adults may be reluctant to seek medical help due to concerns about being treated like children or being stigmatized. Fortunately, there are natural remedies that can help alleviate flatulence in older adults.
