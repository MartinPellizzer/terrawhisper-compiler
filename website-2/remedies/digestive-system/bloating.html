Foeniculum vulgare, also known as fennel, is a plant-based solution for bloating. As a result, fennel may help alleviate symptoms of bloating, such as discomfort, swelling, and cramps. Fennel's natural compounds can also help regulate bowel movements and improve gut health, further reducing the likelihood of bloating.
