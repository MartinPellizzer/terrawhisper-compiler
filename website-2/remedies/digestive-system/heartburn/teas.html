10 best herbal teas for heartburn

1. Zingiber Officinale

Zingiber officinale tea, also known as ginger tea, is a natural remedy for heartburn. Ginger has anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce swelling in the esophagus and stomach, which can cause heartburn. When you drink ginger tea, the warmth and soothing properties of ginger calm the digestive system and ease digestion. This can help prevent stomach acid from flowing back up into the esophagus, which is a common cause of heartburn. Additionally, ginger tea can also help relax the muscles in the digestive tract, which can help food move through the digestive system more smoothly. This can also help reduce the amount of acid produced in the stomach, further alleviating heartburn symptoms.
