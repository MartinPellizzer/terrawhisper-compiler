Gas and the elderly: 9 herbal remedies for a happy tummy

As people age, their digestive systems can slow down, making it harder for them to break down food.

This can lead to gas building up in the stomach, which can be painful and uncomfortable. Elderly people may also have existing health conditions, such as diabetes, high blood pressure, or irritable bowel syndrome, that can make gas and bloating worse. The symptoms of gas in the stomach can be embarrassing and affect a person's quality of life, making it difficult to enjoy meals with family and friends, or even engage in everyday activities. Some elderly people may also experience feelings of anxiety or depression due to their discomfort.

Eating certain foods, such as beans, cabbage, or broccoli, can trigger gas in the stomach. Other factors, like swallowing too much air while eating or drinking, or having an imbalance of gut bacteria, can also contribute to the problem. Herbal tinctures are a natural way to help alleviate gas and bloating in the elderly. They work by soothing the digestive system, reducing inflammation, and improving gut health.

These tinctures can be taken in small amounts, usually under the tongue or mixed with water, and can be a gentle and non-invasive way to manage symptoms.

- To get the best results, make sure to drink plenty of water and eat smaller, more frequent meals. When you take fennel tincture, you may notice a reduction in gas and bloating within 30 minutes to an hour. You may also feel more comfortable and experience a decrease in stomach discomfort. However, if your symptoms persist or worsen, it's always best to consult with a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.
