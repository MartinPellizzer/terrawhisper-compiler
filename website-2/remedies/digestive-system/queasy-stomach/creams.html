  1. Gather ingredients: 2 cups of coconut oil, 1 cup of shea butter, 1/2 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of ginger essential oil, and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.

2. Foeniculum vulgare

  3. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler. Stir until the beeswax dissolves.

3. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra creams contains glycyrrhizin and saponins, which are its active constituents.

By reducing inflammation and calming down stomach muscles, Glycyrrhiza glabra creams can provide relief from queasy stomach.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Glycyrrhiza glabra root, 1 cup of coconut oil, 1 cup of shea butter, and 1 tablespoon of beeswax.
  2. Grind the dried Glycyrrhiza glabra root into a fine powder using a coffee grinder or mortar and pestle.
  3. Melt the coconut oil and shea butter in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  4. Add the ground Glycyrrhiza glabra root powder and beeswax to the melted coconut oil and shea butter mixture. Stir until well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into a heat-proof container and let it cool and solidify. Once solid, store the cream in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.
