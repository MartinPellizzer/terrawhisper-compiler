What Are The Main Causes Of Throat Pain?

Throat pain can be caused by several factors.

One common cause is tonsillitis, an inflammation of the tonsils, which are two small tissues at the back of the throat. This usually occurs when a virus or bacteria infects the tonsils, causing swelling and pain. Another cause is laryngitis, an inflammation of the vocal cords, which can become irritated and swollen due to overuse of the voice, such as screaming or talking loudly for a long time.

This can also be caused by a viral or bacterial infection. Tonsillectomy, or the surgical removal of the tonsils, can also cause throat pain. This is often done to treat recurring tonsillitis or to remove diseased tonsils.

After the surgery, patients may experience pain, discomfort, and swelling in the throat area.

Below are the main causes of throat pain.

  • Tonsillitis: Tonsillitis is a cause of throat pain because the infected tonsils become swollen and inflamed, causing them to rub against the surrounding tissues and muscles in the throat, leading to discomfort and pain.
  • Laryngitis: Laryngitis is a cause of throat pain because the inflamed tissues in the throat become sore and irritated, causing a sharp, stabbing pain when you try to speak or swallow.
  • Tonsillectomy: A tonsillectomy is a cause of throat pain because the removal of the tonsils can leave the throat feeling sore and irritated for several days after the surgery due to inflammation and healing of the wound.
  • Pharyngitis: Pharyngitis is a cause of throat pain because the inflammation and swelling of the pharynx (the back of the throat) make it sore and uncomfortable to swallow or talk.
  • Strep throat: Strep throat is a cause of throat pain because the strep bacteria make the throat's lining become red, swollen, and sore, causing pain and discomfort when swallowing.

Sage (Salvia officinalis) can be too drying for the throat, making it difficult to swallow and causing irritation. Also, avoid pennyroyal (Mentha pulegium) as it can cause inflammation and make throat pain worse.

Finally, avoid wormwood (Artemisia absinthium) because it can irritate the throat and make pain more uncomfortable.
