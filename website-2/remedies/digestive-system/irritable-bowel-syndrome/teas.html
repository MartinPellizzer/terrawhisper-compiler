This can provide relief from IBS symptoms such as bloating, gas, and abdominal pain. Additionally, fennel tea may help slow down digestion, giving the body more time to absorb nutrients and reducing the likelihood of diarrhea. Furthermore, fennel's anti-inflammatory properties may also help reduce the severity of IBS symptoms and promote healing in the digestive tract.
