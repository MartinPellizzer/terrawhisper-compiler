This can also impact their mental health, leading to feelings of anxiety and frustration. Furthermore, constipation can be a sign of an underlying issue, such as a low-fiber diet or inadequate hydration, which can affect the mother's overall health.

Constipation can also affect a mother's ability to breastfeed, as she may experience pain or discomfort while nursing. This can lead to a decrease in milk supply and a decrease in the baby's ability to nurse effectively. Moreover, constipation can be a sign of a larger issue, such as a thyroid problem or a hormonal imbalance, which can affect the mother's overall health. Herbal creams can be a helpful treatment option for postpartum mothers suffering from constipation. These creams typically contain natural ingredients that stimulate bowel movements, reduce inflammation, and promote relaxation.

They can be applied directly to the skin, where they are absorbed into the body, helping to relieve constipation and promote overall digestive health.
