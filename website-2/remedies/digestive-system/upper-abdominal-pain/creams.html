Can drinking herbal tea prevent upper abdominal pain from forming?

Is it safe to consume herbal teas for upper abdominal pain every day?

Moderation is key.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in upper abdominal pain?

What time of day is best to drink herbal tea for upper abdominal pain?

For upper abdominal pain, try drinking herbal tea in the evening. This allows your body to digest the tea and any other foods you've eaten throughout the day, helping to soothe your stomach.
