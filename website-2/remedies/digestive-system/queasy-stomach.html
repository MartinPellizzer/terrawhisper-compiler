Queasy Stomach Causes and Herbal Remedies: A Comprehensive Guide

What are the causes of queasy stomach?

Let's break down what causes a queasy stomach.

One common reason is food poisoning, which happens when you eat something contaminated with bacteria or other germs. This can cause your stomach to churn and feel sick. Hormonal changes, like those experienced during pregnancy or menstruation, can also affect your stomach.

This is because hormonal fluctuations can slow down digestion and make you feel queasy. Migraines are another culprit, and it's not just the headache itself but the associated nausea and stomach discomfort. Gastroenteritis, also known as stomach flu, is an inflammation of the stomach and intestines that can cause queasiness. Lastly, stress is a major contributor to a queasy stomach.

When you're under a lot of stress, your body releases more acid in your stomach, leading to discomfort and nausea.
