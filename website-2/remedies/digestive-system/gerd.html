Zingiber officinale

Ginger, also known as Zingiber officinale, is a natural remedy that can help with heartburn and acid reflux, common symptoms of GERD. When you eat, the food goes down your esophagus and into your stomach. The lower esophageal sphincter is a muscle that stops the food from coming back up. However, in people with GERD, this muscle doesn't work properly and stomach acid leaks back up into the esophagus, causing heartburn and discomfort. Ginger has anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce swelling and pain in the esophagus. It also has a compound called gingerol that can help relax the lower esophageal sphincter, allowing it to function properly again. Additionally, ginger can help reduce the production of stomach acid, which can contribute to heartburn and acid reflux. Some studies have shown that ginger can be as effective as antacids in relieving heartburn and other symptoms of GERD.
