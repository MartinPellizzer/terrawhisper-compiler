9 Herbal Tinctures For Acid Reflux

Smoking is a major contributor to acid reflux in many people.

When you smoke, the chemicals from tobacco damage your stomach's lining, allowing stomach acid to flow back up into the esophagus. This leads to a burning sensation in the chest and throat, making it hard to swallow and eat. For smokers, acid reflux can be a constant companion, disrupting their daily lives. They may avoid social gatherings, favorite foods, or even lying down after meals to prevent symptoms. This can be frustrating and affect their self-esteem. Smokers may also experience heartburn, bloating, and discomfort after eating, which can be painful and embarrassing.

They may even feel anxious or irritable due to their symptoms. Smokers can try various treatments, but some may not want to take medication or undergo surgery. This is where herbal tinctures come in. They can be made from plants that have natural soothing properties, helping to calm the stomach and reduce acid production. When taken in small amounts, these tinctures can provide relief from acid reflux symptoms without causing harm to the body. They can be added to water or taken under the tongue, making them a convenient and discreet option for smokers.

By reducing their reliance on tobacco and incorporating herbal tinctures into their routine, smokers may find some relief from their acid reflux symptoms.

1. Glycyrrhiza glabra

If you're considering trying Glycyrrhiza glabra tinctures for acid reflux, start by taking a small dose of 20-30 drops, 2-3 times a day, mixed with water.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before adding any new supplements to your regimen, especially if you're already taking medications or have underlying health conditions.
