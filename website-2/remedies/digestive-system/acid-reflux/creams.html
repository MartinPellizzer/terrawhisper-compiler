  2. Heat the coconut oil and shea butter in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water until melted.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the Glycyrrhiza glabra root powder and beeswax. Add the powdered mixture to the melted coconut oil and shea butter mixture.
  4. Stir until the mixture is smooth and well combined. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
