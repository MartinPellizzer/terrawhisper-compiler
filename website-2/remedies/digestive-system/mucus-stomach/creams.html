Mucus in stomach remedies for celiacs: 11 herbal creams

People with celiac disease often struggle with stomach issues, one of which is excess mucus.

This happens because when they eat gluten, it damages the small intestine, making it hard for nutrients to be absorbed. As a result, the body starts producing more mucus to try and protect itself. This excess mucus can cause discomfort, nausea, and vomiting, making everyday life a challenge. Simple tasks like eating, sleeping, and working become difficult, and some people even experience weight loss and fatigue. The excess mucus can also lead to digestive problems, such as bloating, gas, and stomach pain.

It can make people feel bloated and uncomfortable, and even affect their mental health. They may feel anxious or depressed due to their symptoms. To manage this issue, some people turn to herbal creams, which are applied topically to the abdomen. These creams contain certain plant extracts that have anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. They help to calm the digestive system, reducing the production of excess mucus.

The creams can be applied before meals to help ease digestion and reduce symptoms.
