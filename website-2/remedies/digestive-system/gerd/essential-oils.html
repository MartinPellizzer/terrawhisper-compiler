Fixing GERD is essential because it can lead to complications such as esophagitis, a condition where the esophagus becomes inflamed, and potentially even esophageal cancer. By incorporating herbal essential oils into your management plan, you can potentially alleviate symptoms, improve digestion, and reduce the frequency of acid reflux episodes. To use these essential oils, you can add a few drops to a diffuser, apply them topically (diluted with a carrier oil), or add them to a warm bath. It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using any new essential oils, especially if you're already taking medications or have any underlying health conditions.
