Stomach growling can be a sign that the digestive system is not working properly, and it can be a recurring problem for some children.

This can be frustrating for parents and caregivers who want to help their child feel better. In some cases, stomach growling can be a sign of an underlying issue, such as acid reflux or food allergies, which need to be addressed. Herbal creams can be a helpful way to soothe a growling stomach and ease discomfort in children.
