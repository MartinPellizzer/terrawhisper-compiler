Zingiber officinale tea, also known as ginger tea, is a popular remedy for vomiting. One reason it's helpful is that ginger has anti-inflammatory properties, which can soothe the stomach and reduce nausea. Additionally, ginger has natural chemicals that can block the body's "vomiting center," a part of the brain that sends signals to the stomach to expel food. By blocking these signals, ginger tea can help reduce vomiting and make you feel better. The anti-inflammatory properties of ginger tea also help to reduce inflammation in the digestive tract, which can contribute to nausea and vomiting. By reducing inflammation, ginger tea can help your body recover from vomiting and prevent future episodes.
