1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale creams contains active constituents like gingerols and shogaols.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, which help reduce nausea and vomiting. Gingerols and shogaols also have antioxidant properties, which help protect the stomach and reduce inflammation. The antispasmodic properties of Zingiber officinale creams help relax the stomach muscles, reducing the spasms that can cause vomiting.

By reducing inflammation and relaxing the stomach muscles, Zingiber officinale creams can help alleviate vomiting caused by stomach problems.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of honey, and 1 teaspoon of dried Zingiber officinale root powder.
  2. Melt coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Add shea butter and honey to the melted mixture. Stir until combined.
  4. Remove from heat and stir in Zingiber officinale root powder. Let cool until mixture thickens.
  5. Pour mixture into small containers and let cool completely. Store in a cool, dry place for up to 6 months.
