What herbal creams you should avoid if you have painful swallowing?

If you have painful swallowing, it's best to avoid herbal creams with certain herbs that can irritate your throat.

'Hydrastis canadensis', or goldenseal, is also a concern, as it can cause allergic reactions and increase the risk of bleeding. 'Graphites' or silver nitrate can also be problematic, as it can burn and irritate the skin and mucous membranes. Finally, 'Arctium lappa' or burdock root can cause allergic reactions and interact with medications, including blood thinners.

When choosing a cream, look for one that's specifically designed for soothing sore throats and is free from these potentially irritating herbs.
