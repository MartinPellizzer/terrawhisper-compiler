1. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra creams contains compounds like glycyrrhetic acid and glycosides, which have anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds help reduce swelling in the throat, making it easier to swallow. The antispasmodic properties of glycyrrhiza glabra also help relax the muscles in the throat, which can become tense and painful during swallowing. Additionally, glycyrrhiza glabra creams have a soothing and protective effect on the mucous membranes in the throat, providing relief from irritation and discomfort.

Regular use of glycyrrhiza glabra creams may help reduce the frequency and severity of painful swallowing episodes.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of distilled water, 1 tablespoon of glycerin, 1 tablespoon of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of coconut oil, 2 tablespoons of shea butter, and 1 teaspoon of dried Glycyrrhiza glabra root powder.
  2. Combine distilled water and glycerin in a small saucepan. Heat on low until glycerin dissolves.
  3. Add beeswax to the saucepan and heat until melted.
  4. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in coconut oil, shea butter, and Glycyrrhiza glabra root powder.
  5. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool and solidify. Once set, use as a cream for painful swallowing.
