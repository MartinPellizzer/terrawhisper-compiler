11 Herbal Essential Oils for Babies with Jaundice

Babies can get jaundice, a condition where their skin turns yellow due to too much bilirubin in their blood.

This happens when their liver isn't working properly, and it can't remove the bilirubin. As a result, the bilirubin builds up and causes the yellow color. Jaundice is common in newborns, and it usually goes away on its own within a few weeks. However, in some cases, it can lead to serious health problems if left untreated. Babies with jaundice may seem okay, but they can be feeling unwell and uncomfortable. They may have trouble feeding, be irritable, and even have trouble sleeping.

In severe cases, jaundice can cause brain damage or other complications, so it's essential to get it checked out. When a baby has jaundice, their parents and healthcare providers may try various treatments. These can include light therapy, which exposes the baby to a special light that helps break down the bilirubin, and fluids to help the baby pass the bilirubin through their urine. However, some parents are looking for more natural ways to help their baby feel better. That's where herbal essential oils come in. These oils are derived from plants and can be used to help support the baby's liver function and reduce the bilirubin levels. They can also help to soothe and calm the baby, making them feel more comfortable and relaxed.

When used properly and under the guidance of a healthcare provider, these oils may be a helpful addition to the baby's treatment plan.

Ruta graveolens essential oils, also known as rue essential oil, may help with jaundice due to its properties that support liver health and promote the removal of bilirubin from the body.

This oil contains constituents like rutaecarpine and limonene, which have been shown to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. These properties can help reduce liver congestion and improve the flow of bile, allowing bilirubin to be eliminated from the body. If you're considering using Ruta graveolens essential oils for jaundice, it's recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before taking any action. In general, you can add a few drops of the oil to a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil and apply it to the skin in a gentle, circular motion.

You can also inhale the oil by adding a few drops to a diffuser or mixing it with water and inhaling the vapor. It's essential to note that the oil can be quite potent, so start with small amounts and gradually increase as needed. When using Ruta graveolens essential oils for jaundice, you may start to notice improvements in your condition, such as reduced yellowing of the skin and eyes, and increased energy levels. The oil may also help reduce liver inflammation and promote the growth of new liver cells.

However, it's crucial to be patient and consistent with your treatment, as the effects may take some time to manifest.
