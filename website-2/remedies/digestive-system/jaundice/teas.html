7 Herbal Teas For Jaundice

Jaundice is a common condition in newborn babies, where the skin and eyes turn yellow due to a buildup of bilirubin in the blood.

This happens when the liver can't remove bilirubin from the blood quickly enough. If left untreated, high levels of bilirubin can cause serious brain damage and other complications. Jaundice can make babies look and feel sick, and it can be painful for them to eat and breastfeed. They might be fussy, sleepy, and have trouble feeding, which can be stressful for both the baby and the parents.

If a baby's bilirubin levels get too high, they might need to stay in the hospital for treatment, which can be scary and overwhelming for everyone involved. In some cases, hospitals may prescribe herbal teas to help treat jaundice in newborns. These teas work by gently stimulating the liver to remove bilirubin from the blood. They are usually given to the mother to drink, so the baby can benefit from the medicinal properties while still breastfeeding. The teas help to stimulate the liver and improve the flow of bile, which helps to remove bilirubin from the blood more efficiently.

This can help reduce the baby's bilirubin levels and prevent the need for hospital treatment.
