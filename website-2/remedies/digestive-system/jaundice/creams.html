1. Silybum marianum

Silybum marianum creams contains silymarin, a mixture of bioactive compounds including silibinin, silidianin, and silicristin.

These compounds have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, which help to reduce liver damage and promote liver cell regeneration. Silymarin also has a protective effect on liver cells by blocking the action of toxins and free radicals. The antioxidant properties of silymarin help to reduce the levels of bilirubin in the blood, which is a key factor in the development of jaundice.

By reducing liver damage and promoting liver cell regeneration, silymarin in Silybum marianum creams can help to alleviate the symptoms of jaundice.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Silybum marianum flowers and 2 cups of coconut oil in a clean glass jar.
  2. Heat the coconut oil in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of boiling water until it reaches 120°F to 140°F.
  3. Add the dried Silybum marianum flowers to the coconut oil and let it steep for 2 hours.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids.
  5. Let the mixture cool, then whip it with an electric mixer until it becomes a smooth cream. Store it in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.
