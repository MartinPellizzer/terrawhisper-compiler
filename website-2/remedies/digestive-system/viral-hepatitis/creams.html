1. Silybum marianum

By reducing liver damage and promoting liver regeneration, silymarin in Silybum marianum creams can help to alleviate symptoms and improve quality of life for people with viral hepatitis.

  1. Gather 250g of dried Silybum marianum flowers and 1 cup of coconut oil.
  2. Combine the dried flowers and coconut oil in a saucepan. Heat the mixture over low heat for 2-3 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Discard the solids.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of beeswax and 2 tablespoons of shea butter to the oil mixture. Heat the mixture over low heat, stirring until the beeswax and shea butter are fully melted.
