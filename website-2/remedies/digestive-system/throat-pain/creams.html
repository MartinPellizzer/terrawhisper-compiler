1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale creams contains gingerols and shogaols, which are active compounds that help reduce throat pain.

Gingerols and shogaols have anti-inflammatory properties that soothe and calm the mucous membranes in the throat, reducing swelling and discomfort. They also have analgesic and antipyretic properties, which help to numb the pain and lower the temperature, providing quick relief from a sore throat. The warming sensation of ginger when applied topically can also help to increase blood flow to the area, promoting healing and reducing inflammation.

By applying Zingiber officinale creams to the affected area, you can experience fast and effective relief from throat pain.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of honey, 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon of ginger powder, and a few drops of peppermint essential oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water. Stir until the beeswax is fully melted.
  3. Add the shea butter, honey, turmeric powder, and ginger powder to the melted mixture. Stir well until combined.
  4. Remove the mixture from the heat and let it cool slightly. Stir in the peppermint essential oil.
  5. Pour the mixture into a small tin or jar and let it cool completely. Store in the fridge for up to 2 weeks and apply as needed to soothe throat pain.
