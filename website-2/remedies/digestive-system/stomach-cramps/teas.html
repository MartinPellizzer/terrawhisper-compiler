Ginger helps to slow down this process, giving your stomach and intestines time to relax and reducing the pain. Ginger tea also has a soothing effect on the stomach and intestines, helping to calm nausea and vomiting, which often accompany stomach cramps. Additionally, ginger has natural antispasmodic properties, which help to relax the muscles in the stomach and intestines, preventing them from contracting too quickly and causing more pain. By drinking ginger tea, you can help to ease stomach cramps and get relief from the discomfort.
