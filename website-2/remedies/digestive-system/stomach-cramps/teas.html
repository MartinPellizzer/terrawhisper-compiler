1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale teas contains a compound called gingerol, which has anti-inflammatory properties that help soothe stomach cramps.

The volatile oils in ginger, specifically gingerol and shogaol, help to relax muscles in the digestive tract and reduce inflammation. Ginger's ability to inhibit the production of prostaglandins, hormone-like substances that cause pain and inflammation, also helps to alleviate cramping. Additionally, ginger's antispasmodic properties help to calm down the muscles in the stomach and prevent spasms.

By reducing inflammation and relaxing muscles, ginger helps to provide quick relief from stomach cramps.

  1. Get 1 tablespoon of fresh ginger root.
  2. Peel the ginger root and chop it into small pieces.
  3. Boil 1 cup of water in a pot.
  4. Add the ginger pieces to the boiling water and let it steep for 5 minutes.
  5. Strain the tea and drink it while it's warm. Repeat as needed for stomach cramps.

2. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra teas contains compounds like glycyrrhizin, flavonoids, and triterpenoids that help soothe stomach cramps.

Glycyrrhizin, a major active constituent, has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and pain in the digestive tract. Flavonoids, such as kaempferol and quercetin, have antioxidant properties that protect the stomach lining from damage and irritation. Triterpenoids, including 18α-glycyrrhetinic acid, help relax the muscles in the digestive tract, reducing spasms and cramps.

By reducing inflammation and relaxing muscles, Glycyrrhiza glabra teas can provide relief from stomach cramps.

  1. Gather 2 tablespoons of dried Glycyrrhiza glabra root. You can buy it from a health store or online.

  5. Drink the tea warm or at room temperature. You can add honey to taste if needed.

3. Foeniculum vulgare

Is it safe to consume herbal teas for stomach cramps every day?

While herbal teas can help ease stomach cramps, consuming them daily may not be ideal.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in stomach cramps?

What time of day is best to drink herbal tea for stomach cramps?

If you have stomach cramps, try drinking herbal tea in the morning or early afternoon.
