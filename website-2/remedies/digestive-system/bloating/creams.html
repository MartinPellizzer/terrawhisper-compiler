1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale creams contains active constituents like gingerols and shogaols.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties, which help reduce inflammation in the digestive system and alleviate bloating. Gingerols also have a natural ability to relax the muscles in the digestive tract, allowing gas to pass through more easily. Shogaols, on the other hand, have a stimulating effect on digestion, helping to break down food more efficiently and prevent the buildup of gas.

By combining these properties, Zingiber officinale creams can help to quickly and effectively alleviate bloating symptoms.

  1. Gather 2 cups of distilled water, 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of ginger essential oil, and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Once melted, remove from heat and let cool slightly. Stir in the ginger essential oil and vitamin E oil.
  4. Slowly pour the distilled water into the oil mixture while whisking constantly. Continue whisking until the mixture thickens into a creamy texture.
  5. Pour the Zingiber officinale cream into a clean glass jar and let cool to room temperature before use. Store in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
