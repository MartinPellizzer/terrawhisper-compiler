Older Adults and Bloating: 10 Proven Herbal Creams

As people age, their digestive system can slow down, leading to problems like bloating.

Bloating is when the stomach gets full of gas and feels tight. It can be uncomfortable and painful. Older adults who experience bloating may feel embarrassed to eat in public or have trouble sleeping because of discomfort. It can also lead to feelings of anxiety and depression. Some older adults may also have other health conditions like diabetes, high blood pressure, or irritable bowel syndrome (IBS) that can make bloating worse. Bloating can also cause older adults to lose interest in activities they love, like traveling or spending time with family and friends.

Older adults who experience bloating may also have trouble managing their weight, as bloating can make it hard to lose weight. They may also have to take more medication, which can have side effects. Some older adults may feel isolated because of their bloating, as they may avoid social situations or gatherings where food is involved. Herbal creams can help older adults treat bloating. These creams work by soothing the digestive system and reducing inflammation. They can also help to relax the muscles in the stomach and improve digestion. When applied to the skin, these creams can also help to reduce stress and anxiety, which can contribute to bloating.

They can be used in combination with other treatments, like dietary changes or medication, to help manage bloating symptoms.
