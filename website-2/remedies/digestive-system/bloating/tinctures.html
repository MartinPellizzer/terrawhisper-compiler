1. Foeniculum vulgare

Foeniculum vulgare tinctures contains a high concentration of essential oils, including anethole, limonene, and pinene.

These compounds have a direct effect on the digestive system, helping to relax the muscles in the intestines and reduce spasms that can cause bloating. Anethole, in particular, has a strong anti-inflammatory property, which can help to reduce inflammation in the digestive tract and alleviate discomfort. The essential oils in Foeniculum vulgare tinctures also have a carminative effect, helping to release trapped gas and reduce bloating.

By promoting healthy digestion and reducing inflammation, Foeniculum vulgare tinctures can provide relief from bloating and other digestive issues.

  1. Gather 250ml of 80% vodka and 50g of dried Foeniculum vulgare (Fennel seeds).
  2. Combine the vodka and Fennel seeds in a clean glass jar, making sure the Fennel seeds are completely covered.
  3. Seal the jar and store it in a cool dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids.
  5. Label the jar and store the Fennel tincture in a cool dark place. Use 20-30 drops in water when needed to help with bloating.

2. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale tinctures contains compounds like gingerols and shogaols, which have anti-inflammatory properties.

By reducing inflammation and improving digestion, Zingiber officinale tinctures can help to alleviate symptoms of bloating and discomfort.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of fresh Zingiber officinale (ginger) roots, 2 cups of 80% vodka or glycerin, and a clean glass jar with a lid.
  2. Chop the ginger roots into small pieces and pack them into the glass jar.
  3. Pour the 80% vodka or glycerin over the ginger roots, making sure they are completely covered.

  5. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids. Store the tincture in a cool, dark place.
