2. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale essential oils contains active constituents like gingerol and shogaol, responsible for its therapeutic properties.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which help reduce acidity in the stomach. The essential oils' ability to relax the muscles in the digestive tract and improve digestion also contributes to alleviating hyperacidity. The combination of these properties makes Zingiber officinale essential oils a potential remedy for hyperacidity.
