When consumed in moderation, these herbal decoctions can provide relief from morning sickness symptoms, allowing women to lead a more comfortable and active life. Moreover, taking care of morning sickness can also reduce the risk of complications associated with dehydration and weight loss, such as preterm labor and low birth weight.
