10 Herbal Tinctures For Morning Sickness

Morning sickness is a common problem for new moms, especially during the first trimester of pregnancy.

Hormonal changes in the body cause nausea and vomiting, making everyday activities like eating, working, and taking care of the home a challenge. This can be frustrating and exhausting, affecting a new mom's mood, energy levels, and ability to bond with her baby. Simple tasks like getting out of bed, cooking meals, and caring for her baby become overwhelming, and she may feel guilty for not being able to enjoy this special time. She may also experience weight loss, dehydration, and fatigue, which can lead to other health issues. New moms often try various remedies to alleviate morning sickness, but many are ineffective or have unpleasant side effects.

Some may feel forced to take time off work, leave their jobs, or rely on others for help, which can be financially and emotionally straining. This can also impact her relationships with her partner, family, and friends, as she feels like a burden to them. Herbal tinctures are a natural and gentle way to treat morning sickness. These liquid extracts of plants have been used for centuries to soothe the stomach and reduce nausea. They are often made from a combination of plants that work together to calm the digestive system and ease symptoms. When taken correctly, herbal tinctures can provide quick relief from morning sickness, allowing new moms to feel better and enjoy their pregnancy.

They are also a safe alternative to prescription medications, which can have harsh side effects.

1. Zingiber officinale

4. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba tinctures contains flavonoids and terpenoids that may help alleviate morning sickness in pregnant women.
