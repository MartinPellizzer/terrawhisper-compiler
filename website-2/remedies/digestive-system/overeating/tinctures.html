7 Herbal Infusions for Diabetics with Overeating Habits

People with diabetes often struggle with overeating because their bodies may have trouble producing enough insulin, a hormone that helps turn food into energy.

When they eat more than they need, their blood sugar levels can rise too high, causing problems like fatigue, blurred vision, and even organ damage over time. This can be especially challenging for those with type 2 diabetes, as they may have to carefully balance their diet to manage their condition. Overeating can disrupt this delicate balance, leading to blood sugar spikes and making it harder to control their diabetes. As a result, diabetics may feel anxious, stressed, or guilty after overeating, which can create a vicious cycle of poor eating habits. For those who struggle with overeating and diabetes, managing their condition can feel like a constant challenge.

It's not just about what they eat, but also when and how much. They may feel like they're constantly on a tightrope, trying to balance their love of food with the need to keep their blood sugar levels in check. This can lead to feelings of frustration, hopelessness, and even despair. Herbal tinctures may offer some relief for diabetics who struggle with overeating. These liquid extracts contain concentrated plant compounds that can help regulate appetite, improve digestion, and reduce cravings for unhealthy foods. By taking a small dose of an herbal tincture before meals, diabetics may be able to feel fuller faster and eat less overall.

This can be especially helpful for those who have trouble controlling their portions or tend to snack excessively between meals.
