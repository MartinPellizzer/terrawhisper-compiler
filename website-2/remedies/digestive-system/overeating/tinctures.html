1. Gymnema sylvestre

Gymnema sylvestre tinctures contains gymnemic acid, a key bioactive constituent that helps with overeating.

This compound blocks the sugar receptors on the tongue, reducing the taste of sweetness. The tannins and flavonoids in Gymnema sylvestre tinctures have astringent properties that help reduce cravings for sweet and fatty foods. The saponins present in the tincture also aid in weight loss by suppressing appetite and improving metabolism.

By inhibiting sugar absorption and reducing cravings, Gymnema sylvestre tinctures can help individuals manage overeating and achieve a healthier weight.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh or dried Gymnema sylvestre leaves, a clean glass jar, and a measuring cup.
  2. Fill the glass jar with 1 cup of Gymnema sylvestre leaves and add 2 cups of 80% vodka or glycerin.
  3. Seal the jar tightly and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-6 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
  4. After 2-6 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another glass jar.
  5. Store the Gymnema sylvestre tincture in the refrigerator and take 20-30 drops, 2-3 times a day, as needed to manage overeating.

2. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba tinctures contains flavonoids and bilobalide, two active constituents that have been found to improve digestion and reduce symptoms of overeating.

Flavonoids help to relax the smooth muscles in the digestive tract, reducing inflammation and promoting better nutrient absorption. Bilobalide has antioxidant properties, which can help to protect the body from damage caused by free radicals, often associated with overeating. Ginkgo biloba tinctures also has anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to soothe the digestive system and reduce discomfort after eating.

Regular use of ginkgo biloba tinctures may help to regulate digestion and alleviate symptoms of overeating.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Ginkgo biloba leaves, 2 cups of vodka (at least 80 proof), and a clean glass jar.
  2. Combine Ginkgo biloba leaves and vodka in the glass jar. Make sure the leaves are covered by the vodka.
  3. Close the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.
  5. Store the Ginkgo biloba tincture in a cool, dark place. Use 20-30 drops, 2-3 times a day, to help manage overeating.

Is it safe to consume herbal teas for overeating every day?

Herbal teas can be a helpful aid for digestion after overeating, but it's essential to be mindful of your body's sensitivity.

Some teas, like peppermint, can ease stomach discomfort, while others, like ginger, may help reduce nausea.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in overeating?

Herbal teas can help with overeating, but it's different for everyone.
