When we eat sweet foods, our taste buds send a signal to the brain that we're hungry for more sweetness. Gymnemic acid stops this signal, so we don't feel as hungry for sweets. This can be helpful for people who overeat due to a sweet tooth. Additionally, Gymnema sylvestre tea may also help lower blood sugar levels and improve insulin sensitivity, which can be beneficial for people with diabetes or those who are trying to manage their weight.
