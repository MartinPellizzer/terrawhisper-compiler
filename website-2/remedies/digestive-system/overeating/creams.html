1. Curcuma longa

Curcuma longa creams contains curcumin, demethoxycurcumin, and bisdemethoxycurcumin, which are its active constituents.

These compounds have potent anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which help reduce inflammation and oxidative stress in the body. Curcumin's ability to modulate the gut-brain axis and regulate digestion can alleviate symptoms of overeating, such as bloating and discomfort. The antioxidants in Curcuma longa creams may also help protect the liver from damage caused by overeating and excessive sugar consumption.

By reducing inflammation and promoting a healthy gut, Curcuma longa creams can support digestive health and reduce the negative effects of overeating.

  1. Gather 2 cups of heavy cream, 2 tablespoons of turmeric powder, 1 tablespoon of coconut oil, and 1 teaspoon of honey.
  2. In a medium saucepan, warm the heavy cream over low heat. Remove from heat once it starts to simmer.
  3. In a small bowl, mix turmeric powder and coconut oil until well combined.
  4. Slowly add the turmeric mixture to the warm heavy cream, whisking constantly to avoid lumps.
  5. Stir in the honey and let the mixture cool to room temperature. Store in an airtight container in the fridge.

2. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale creams contains bioactive constituents such as gingerols and shogaols, which have anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds help reduce nausea and alleviate digestive discomfort caused by overeating. The warming properties of ginger also stimulate digestion and improve blood circulation, which can help alleviate bloating and discomfort after eating too much. Ginger's natural anti-inflammatory properties may also reduce inflammation in the digestive tract, which can contribute to overeating-related discomfort.

By applying Zingiber officinale creams to the skin, the bioactive compounds can be absorbed and help alleviate digestive issues from the inside out.

  1. Gather ingredients: 2 cups of coconut oil, 1 cup of beeswax, 1/2 cup of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of ginger powder (Zingiber officinale), and 10 drops of peppermint essential oil.
  2. Melt coconut oil, beeswax, and shea butter in a double boiler or a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second increments, stirring between each interval until smooth.
  3. Add ginger powder and stir well to combine. Let it cool for 5 minutes to allow the powder to absorb.
  4. Add peppermint essential oil and stir well. Pour the mixture into a glass jar or tin.
  5. Let the mixture cool and harden completely before applying it to the skin after overeating. Use as needed, up to 3 times a day.

3. Gymnema sylvestre

Gymnema sylvestre creams contains the active constituents gymnemic acids, which are known to inhibit the sugar receptors on the tongue, reducing the desire for sweet tastes.

The gymnemic acids also help slow the absorption of sugar into the bloodstream, making it easier to control overeating. Additionally, the saponins in Gymnema sylvestre have been shown to enhance the production of insulin, helping to regulate blood sugar levels and reduce cravings for sweet foods. The flavonoids in Gymnema sylvestre have antioxidant properties, which can help protect the body from the negative effects of overeating and sugar consumption.

By reducing sugar cravings and regulating blood sugar levels, Gymnema sylvestre creams can help individuals manage overeating and promote a healthier relationship with food.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of Gymnema sylvestre extract, 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Remove melted coconut oil and beeswax from heat. Add Gymnema sylvestre extract and vitamin E oil. Stir until well combined.
  4. Pour mixture into a silicone ice cube tray or a small tin container. Let it cool and solidify at room temperature.
  5. Once solid, remove cream cubes or tin from tray/container. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

4. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba creams contains bilobalide and ginkgolides, two active compounds that can help with overeating.

Bilobalide has anti-inflammatory properties that may reduce swelling in the stomach, making it easier to digest food. Ginkgolides have antioxidant properties that can protect the stomach lining from damage caused by overeating. The flavonoids in Ginkgo biloba creams can also help to reduce stress and anxiety, which are common triggers for overeating.

By reducing inflammation and stress, Ginkgo biloba creams can help to regulate appetite and improve digestion.

  1. Gather 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of beeswax, and 1 teaspoon of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and shea butter in a double boiler or a microwave-safe bowl.
  3. Add the beeswax to the melted mixture and stir until it's fully incorporated.
  4. Add the Ginkgo biloba extract (about 10-15 drops) and vitamin E oil to the mixture. Stir well.
  5. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool and solidify. Once solid, use as a cream for overeating.

5. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra creams contains active constituents like glycyrrhizic acid, flavonoids, and saponins.

These compounds help to reduce inflammation in the digestive system caused by overeating. Glycyrrhizic acid in particular, has been shown to help reduce water retention and alleviate symptoms of bloating. The flavonoids present in the cream have antioxidant properties, which help to protect the body from oxidative stress caused by consuming large amounts of food.

By reducing inflammation and protecting the body from oxidative stress, Glycyrrhiza glabra creams can help alleviate some of the discomfort associated with overeating.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of distilled water, 2 tablespoons of glycerin, 2 tablespoons of beeswax, 1 tablespoon of glycyrrhiza glabra root powder, and 1 teaspoon of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt beeswax in a saucepan over low heat, then add distilled water and glycerin. Stir until beeswax dissolves.
  3. Add glycyrrhiza glabra root powder to the saucepan and stir well. Remove from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Discard the solids.
  5. Pour the mixture into a container and add vitamin E oil. Mix well and let it cool and thicken before use.

6. Camellia sinensis

Camellia sinensis creams contains active constituents like theaflavins, thearubigins, and catechins.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce swelling and ease digestion after overeating. The theaflavins, in particular, have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes, which can contribute to digestive discomfort. The thearubigins have antioxidant properties that help protect the stomach lining from damage caused by overeating.

By reducing inflammation and protecting the stomach lining, Camellia sinensis creams may help alleviate symptoms of overeating, such as nausea, bloating, and stomach pain.

  1. Gather 2 cups of Camellia sinensis leaves, 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of shea butter, 1/4 cup of beeswax, and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Heat the coconut oil, shea butter, and beeswax in a double boiler until melted and well combined.
  3. Add the Camellia sinensis leaves to the melted mixture and let it steep for 2-3 hours or overnight.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl, discarding the Camellia sinensis leaves.
  5. Let the mixture cool and solidify, then store it in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.

  1. Gather 2 cups of distilled water, 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, and 2 tablespoons of Panax ginseng extract in a double boiler.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in the double boiler over low heat, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the distilled water to the melted mixture and stir well.
  4. Add the Panax ginseng extract to the mixture and stir until well combined.
  5. Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool. Once cooled, pour the mixture into a container and let it set before use.

8. Silybum marianum

Silybum marianum creams contains silymarin, a mixture of flavonoids and polyphenols, including silibinin, silidianin, and silicristin.

These compounds have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that help protect the liver from damage caused by overeating. Silymarin also enhances the production of liver enzymes, supporting the liver's natural detoxification process and promoting the elimination of toxins.   2. Heat the coconut oil in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water until it reaches 120°F to 140°F.
  3. Add the dried Silybum marianum flowers to the coconut oil and let it steep for 2 to 3 hours, or until the mixture cools to room temperature.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a clean bowl, discarding the solids.
  5. Transfer the infused oil to a jar and mix in 1 tablespoon of beeswax. Heat the mixture in the double boiler or a microwave-safe bowl in 10-second increments, stirring between each heating, until the beeswax is fully melted and the mixture is smooth.

9. Withania somnifera

Withania somnifera creams contains bioactive constituents like withanolides, alkaloids, and glycosides.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help reduce stress and anxiety, common triggers for overeating. Withanolides, in particular, have been shown to regulate blood sugar levels and improve insulin sensitivity, which can help manage cravings for unhealthy foods. The alkaloids in Withania somnifera creams also have a calming effect on the nervous system, reducing the emotional urge to overeat.

By regulating stress hormones and improving metabolic function, Withania somnifera creams may provide a natural solution for those struggling with overeating.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup coconut oil, 1/2 cup beeswax, 2 tablespoons Withania somnifera powder, 2 tablespoons aloe vera gel.
  2. Melt coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl in the microwave for 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval, until melted.
  3. Add Withania somnifera powder to the melted mixture and stir well to combine.

What time of day is best to drink herbal tea for overeating?
