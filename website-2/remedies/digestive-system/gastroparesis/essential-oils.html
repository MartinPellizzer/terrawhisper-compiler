11 herbal extracts for alcoholics suffering from gastroparesis

Imagine being unable to eat a nutritious meal, feeling weak and tired all the time, and struggling to overcome an addiction on top of it. Gastroparesis can also make medication absorption harder, which can lead to more health problems. Alcoholics with gastroparesis often have to deal with symptoms like bloating, abdominal discomfort, and difficulty swallowing. Their eating habits may become irregular, which can lead to malnutrition and weight loss.

This can be especially difficult for those in recovery, as it can make them feel like they're taking a step backward. By promoting a healthy gut, these oils can support overall recovery from gastroparesis and addiction.
