9 Herbal Tinctures For Gastroparesis

People with multiple sclerosis (MS) often deal with a complication called gastroparesis.

This is when the nerves in the stomach that control digestion get damaged, leading to slow movement of food through the digestive system. As a result, people with MS may experience nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain after eating. They might also feel bloated and uncomfortable, which can interfere with their daily activities and quality of life. Gastroparesis in MS patients can make it difficult to manage symptoms of the disease, as they may have trouble absorbing medications and nutrients. Some people with MS may also experience fatigue, weight loss, and difficulty managing their emotions due to gastroparesis.

This can be frustrating and isolating, making everyday tasks a challenge. Herbal tinctures have been used by some people with MS to help manage gastroparesis symptoms. These tinctures are made by steeping plants in a solvent, such as ethanol or glycerin, to extract their active compounds. Some of these compounds have natural anti-inflammatory and digestive properties, which may help soothe the stomach and improve digestion. By reducing inflammation and promoting digestive health, these tinctures may alleviate nausea, vomiting, and abdominal pain in people with MS.

This can help improve their quality of life and make it easier to manage their MS symptoms.
