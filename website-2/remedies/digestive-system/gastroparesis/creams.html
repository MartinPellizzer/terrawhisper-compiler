11 Herbal Creams For Gastroparesis

Gastroparesis is a condition where the stomach muscles are weakened, causing food to stay in the stomach for too long, leading to nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain.

For people undergoing chemotherapy, gastroparesis is a common side effect due to the harsh effects of the treatment on the digestive system. The chemotherapy can damage the nerves that control the stomach muscles, making it harder for food to move through the digestive system. This can lead to a range of symptoms, including feeling full quickly, bloating, and abdominal pain. It can also cause weight loss, fatigue, and a decrease in appetite, making it harder for patients to recover from the treatment. For patients with gastroparesis, even simple tasks like eating and digesting food become a challenge.

They may need to take medications to help manage symptoms, but these can have their own side effects. Gastroparesis can also make it harder for patients to stick to their chemotherapy treatment, which is crucial for their recovery. Herbal creams are being used by some patients to help manage gastroparesis. These creams are applied topically to the abdomen and are believed to help relax the stomach muscles and improve digestion. They may also help to reduce nausea and vomiting, and ease abdominal pain.

They are often used in combination with other treatments, such as medications and dietary changes, to help manage symptoms and improve quality of life.
