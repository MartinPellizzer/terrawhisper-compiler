10 best herbal teas for upset stomach

1. Zingiber Officinale

Zingiber officinale tea, also known as ginger tea, is good for upset stomach because it helps to calm down the digestive system. When we eat something that doesn't agree with us, our stomach can get upset, causing nausea, vomiting, and stomach pain. Ginger tea works by reducing inflammation in the stomach and intestines, which helps to ease the discomfort. It also helps to move food through the digestive system faster, which can prevent the stomach from getting too full and causing pain. Additionally, ginger has natural anti-inflammatory properties that can help to soothe the stomach lining, reducing irritation and discomfort. As a result, ginger tea can provide quick relief from stomach upset, making it a popular remedy for nausea and digestive issues.

When consumed as a tea, fennel seeds can help to ease symptoms of indigestion, gas, and irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
