What herbal essential oils you should avoid if you have upset stomach?

If you have an upset stomach, it's best to avoid certain herbal essential oils that can irritate or worsen digestive issues.

Another oil to be cautious with is 'Lavandula angustifolia', commonly known as Lavender, although generally considered safe, its potency can sometimes trigger stomach issues in sensitive individuals.

'Cistus ladanum' or Rock Rose oil should also be used with care as it may cause nausea and stomach discomfort due to its strong, sedative properties. Additionally, 'Cassia alba' or Chinese Cinnamon oil can be too intense for sensitive stomachs, potentially leading to stomach pain and nausea.

Lastly, 'Juniperus communis' or Juniper berry oil, should be used with caution as it can stimulate digestion, which may exacerbate stomach issues for some people.
