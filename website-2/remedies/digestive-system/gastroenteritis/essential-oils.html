Slippery elm is a natural demulcent that forms a protective barrier on the stomach and intestinal lining, reducing inflammation and irritation. Licorice root can also soothe stomach inflammation and nausea. Its active compound, glycyrrhizin, has anti-inflammatory properties that calm the digestive system.

These medicinal herbs work by reducing inflammation, relaxing muscles, and soothing the nervous system.
