8 Herbal Creams For Chronic Hepatitis

Liver transplant recipients often face a new set of challenges after the surgery.

One of them is chronic hepatitis, a condition where the liver becomes inflamed, leading to scarring and damage. This happens because the new liver is not immune to the same viruses and bacteria that affected the old one. The immune system in a transplant recipient is suppressed to prevent organ rejection, making it harder for the body to fight off infections. As a result, liver transplant recipients are at a higher risk of developing chronic hepatitis. This condition can cause symptoms like fatigue, loss of appetite, and abdominal pain, affecting daily life and work.

In some cases, it can even lead to liver failure again, requiring another transplant. Living with chronic hepatitis can be stressful and difficult, as it requires ongoing medical treatment and regular check-ups. Liver transplant recipients often need to take antiviral medications, which can have side effects and interact with other medications. In addition, these medications may not work for everyone, and some may not be able to tolerate them. This is where herbal creams come in - they can provide an alternative approach to managing chronic hepatitis, by using natural ingredients that have anti-inflammatory and antiviral properties. These creams can be applied topically to the skin, where they can be absorbed and work directly on the liver, reducing inflammation and promoting healing.

They can be used in conjunction with traditional medications, or as a standalone treatment, under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
