1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale tinctures contains active compounds like gingerols and shogaols, which have anti-inflammatory properties.

These properties help reduce inflammation in the esophagus and throat, making it easier to swallow. The warming and soothing effects of ginger also relax the muscles in the throat, allowing for smoother passage of food. Ginger's ability to thin mucus and ease congestion also helps with difficulty swallowing caused by respiratory issues.

By reducing inflammation and relaxing muscles, Zingiber officinale tinctures can provide relief from difficulty swallowing.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Zingiber officinale root, 2 cups of vodka, and a clean glass jar with a lid.
  2. Chop the Zingiber officinale root into small pieces and put them in the glass jar.
  3. Pour the vodka over the root pieces, making sure they are completely covered.
  4. Close the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
  5. After 2-3 weeks, strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.

2. Glycyrrhiza glabra

By reducing inflammation and promoting relaxation, Glycyrrhiza glabra tinctures can help alleviate difficulty swallowing caused by throat irritation.
