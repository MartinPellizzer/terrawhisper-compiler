Understanding Gastritis: Causes and Natural Relief with Medicinal Herbs

What are the causes of gastritis?

Gastritis is inflammation of the stomach lining, and it's caused by several things.

One major cause is Helicobacter bacteria, which can infect the stomach lining and cause irritation. This bacteria is usually found in the stomachs of many people, but it can cause problems if it gets out of balance. Nonsteroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) like ibuprofen and aspirin can also cause gastritis.

These medicines can irritate the stomach lining and make it inflamed. Stress is another cause, as it can make the stomach produce more acid, leading to irritation. Drinking too much alcohol can also cause gastritis, as it can irritate the stomach lining and make it inflamed.

Finally, eating too much spicy food can cause gastritis, especially if it's spicy and greasy, as it can irritate the stomach lining and make it inflamed.

When it comes to treating gastritis, herbal preparations can be a great option.
