1. Hamamelis virginiana

Hamamelis virginiana creams contains boiactive constituents such as tannins and flavonoids, which have anti-inflammatory and astringent properties.

These properties help to reduce swelling and itching associated with hemorrhoids, providing relief from discomfort. The tannins in Hamamelis virginiana creams also help to constrict blood vessels, which can help to reduce bleeding and promote healing. Additionally, the astringent properties of flavonoids help to dry up excess mucous and reduce irritation, making it easier to manage hemorrhoid symptoms.

By using Hamamelis virginiana creams, individuals may find that their symptoms of hemorrhoids are alleviated, allowing for a faster recovery.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Hamamelis virginiana flowers, 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil, and a double boiler.
  2. Combine the Hamamelis virginiana flowers and coconut oil in the double boiler. Heat the mixture on low heat for 2-3 hours, or until the oil turns green.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Discard the solids.
  4. In a separate bowl, melt the beeswax in the microwave or in a saucepan over low heat. Add the melted beeswax to the Hamamelis virginiana mixture.
  5. Add the vitamin E oil and mix well. Pour the mixture into small containers and let it cool. Store in a cool, dry place.
