It can dry it out even more, making your lips feel worse. Another herb to avoid is the root of the Oregon grape plant (Berberis aquifolium).

Like Goldenseal, Oregon grape root also contains berberine and can have a similar drying effect on dry skin. You should also avoid decoctions made from the root of the Senna plant (Cassia senna). Senna root contains chemicals that can cause dryness and irritation in the skin, which can make dry lips feel even drier. Lastly, decoctions made from the root of the Dandelion plant (Taraxacum officinale) should be used with caution if you have dry lips.

While Dandelion root is often used to treat skin conditions, it can also dry out the skin if used too frequently or in high concentrations.
