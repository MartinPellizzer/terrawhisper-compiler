Salvia officinalis

Salvia officinalis, also known as sage, is a herb that has been used for centuries to help with health issues. Research has shown that this herb has properties that can help prevent and treat periodontal disease, a condition that affects the gums and bones that support the teeth. The antioxidants and flavonoids in sage help reduce inflammation and fight bacteria that cause periodontal disease. Additionally, the anti-inflammatory properties of sage can help reduce swelling and pain in the gums, making it easier to clean and maintain good oral hygiene. The antimicrobial properties of sage also help prevent the growth of bacteria and fungi that can cause periodontal disease. When used as a mouthwash or applied topically to the gums, sage can help prevent the progression of periodontal disease and promote healthy gums and teeth.

The oil is often used in combination with other natural remedies and conventional treatments to help manage the symptoms of periodontal disease and promote healthy gums.
