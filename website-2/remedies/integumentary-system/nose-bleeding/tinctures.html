These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help reduce swelling and promote healing in the nasal passages. The polysaccharides, such as arabinogalactan, also help to stimulate the production of white blood cells, which can help to fight off underlying infections that may be causing the nosebleed.

By addressing the root causes of the bleeding, Echinacea purpurea tinctures can help to provide relief from frequent and persistent nosebleeds.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh or dried Echinacea purpurea flowers and leaves.
  2. Combine the flowers and leaves with 2 cups of vodka in a clean glass jar.
