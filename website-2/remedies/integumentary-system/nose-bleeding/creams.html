1. Echinacea purpurea

Echinacea purpurea creams contains active constituents like alkylamides, glycoproteins, and polysaccharides.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce swelling and calm the blood vessels in the nose. The alkylamides in Echinacea purpurea creams have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory chemicals, which can cause nosebleeds. Additionally, the glycoproteins and polysaccharides in the cream have antimicrobial properties that help prevent infection in the affected area.

By reducing inflammation and preventing infection, Echinacea purpurea creams can help promote healing and stop nosebleeds.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of Echinacea purpurea flowers, 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil, and 2 tablespoons of aloe vera gel.
  2. Dry the Echinacea flowers completely and then grind them into a fine powder.
  3. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  4. Add the Echinacea powder, vitamin E oil, and aloe vera gel to the melted mixture and stir well.
  5. Pour the mixture into small containers and let it cool and solidify before use. Apply a small amount to the nose for nose bleeding relief.
