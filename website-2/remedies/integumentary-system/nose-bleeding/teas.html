Ginkgo biloba teas contains flavonoids, bilobalide, and ginkgolides, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

By improving blood vessel function and reducing inflammation, Ginkgo biloba teas may help to alleviate nosebleeds.
