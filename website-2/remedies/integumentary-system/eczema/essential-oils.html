10 herbal oils for pregnant women with eczema

Pregnant women can suffer from eczema due to hormonal changes and increased sensitivity to their environment.

As their skin becomes more delicate, even the smallest irritants can trigger a flare-up, causing itching, redness, and dryness. This can be a real challenge for pregnant women, who often have to adjust their daily routines to accommodate their eczema. Simple activities like taking a shower or dressing their baby can become painful and time-consuming. Furthermore, eczema can affect a pregnant woman's self-esteem and make her feel self-conscious about her appearance.

To make matters worse, many eczema treatments are not suitable for pregnant women, leaving them with limited options for relief. This can be frustrating and isolating, as they may feel like they're struggling with their eczema in secret. Herbal essential oils have been found to be a safe and gentle alternative for pregnant women to alleviate their eczema symptoms. These oils, derived from plants, contain natural anti-inflammatory and soothing properties that can help calm the skin and reduce itching.

When used in moderation, they can provide quick relief from eczema symptoms, without exposing the mother or the baby to harsh chemicals.

1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis essential oils have been known to help with eczema due to its anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The constituents of Calendula officinalis, such as triterpenoids and flavonoids, help to soothe and calm irritated skin, reducing inflammation and redness associated with eczema. These compounds also have antiseptic properties, which can help to prevent infections that can exacerbate eczema. If you're experiencing eczema, you can try using Calendula officinalis essential oils in a few ways. First, mix a few drops of the oil with a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil and apply it topically to the affected area.

You can also add a few drops to your bath water for a relaxing soak. Be sure to dilute the essential oil properly, as it can be quite potent. When you start using Calendula officinalis essential oils, you may notice a reduction in inflammation and redness within a few days. Your skin may also start to feel softer and more moisturized. Some people may also experience a reduction in itching and discomfort.

However, it's essential to note that everyone's skin is different, and it may take some time to see the full effects of the oil.
