Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis, also known as pot marigold, is a helpful plant for insect bites. Its flowers have anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties that can soothe redness and swelling caused by insect bites. When applied topically, calendula oil or cream can reduce itching and pain, making it easier to tolerate the bite. It can also help prevent infection by killing bacteria that might enter the bite area. Furthermore, calendula's soothing effects can calm the skin and promote healing, reducing the risk of scarring. This plant has been used for centuries in traditional medicine to treat various skin conditions, including insect bites, and its effectiveness has been backed by scientific studies.
