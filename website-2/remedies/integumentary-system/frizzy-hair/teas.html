By controlling frizz, you can improve your overall appearance and feel more comfortable in your own skin.

Additionally, healthy hair can reduce stress and anxiety caused by hair-related problems, leading to a better quality of life.

What herbal teas you should avoid if you have frizzy hair?

If you have frizzy hair, some herbal teas you should avoid are those containing Sage (Salvia officinalis) and Chamomile (Matricaria chamomilla).

Sage tea can be too drying for your hair, as it contains compounds that strip your locks of their natural moisture. This can make your hair even frizzier and more prone to breakage. Chamomile tea, on the other hand, contains apigenin, a compound that can cause your hair to become brittle and more prone to frizz.

Both teas can also cause irritation to the scalp, leading to dryness and itchiness, which can further contribute to frizzy hair.
