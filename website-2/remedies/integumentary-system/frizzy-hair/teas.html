10 best herbal teas for frizzy hair

1. Rosmarinus Officinalis

Rosmarinus officinalis tea, also known as rosemary tea, is good for frizzy hair because it has antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties. These help to protect your hair from damage caused by free radicals, which can make your hair look frizzy and unmanageable. Rosemary tea also helps to improve blood circulation on your scalp, which brings more oxygen and nutrients to your hair follicles. This helps to strengthen your hair and make it less prone to breakage, which can contribute to frizz. The tea's antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties also help to reduce dandruff and itchiness on the scalp, which can make your hair look frizzy. Additionally, rosemary tea has natural moisturizing properties that help to lock in moisture and keep your hair hydrated, which is especially important for people with dry or damaged hair that tends to frizz.

Some people also use lemongrass tea as a final rinse after shampooing, as it can help to seal the cuticle and lock in moisture, leaving hair smoother and less prone to frizz.
