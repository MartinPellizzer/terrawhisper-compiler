What Are The Main Causes Of Itchy Scalp?

An itchy scalp can be caused by several conditions.

Dandruff is a common cause of itchy scalp. It's a skin condition where dead skin cells flake off, causing white patches on the scalp. This happens when yeast on the scalp overgrows and irritates the skin, leading to itching and flaking. Seborrheic dermatitis is another cause of itchy scalp.

It's a skin condition that causes red, itchy, and flaky skin on the scalp. This condition is caused by an overproduction of oil in the scalp, which leads to a buildup of yeast and bacteria that irritate the skin. Psoriasis is a chronic autoimmune condition that can cause itchy scalp. It occurs when the immune system mistakenly attacks healthy skin cells, causing them to grow too quickly and die.

This leads to itchy, scaly patches on the scalp.

Below are the main causes of itchy scalp.

  • Dandruff: Dandruff is a cause of itchy scalp because it leads to dry, flaky skin on the scalp that can irritate the skin and cause itching and discomfort.
  • Seborrheic dermatitis: Seborrheic dermatitis is a cause of an itchy scalp because it causes the skin on the scalp to become inflamed, red, and irritated, leading to intense itching and discomfort.
  • Psoriasis: Psoriasis is a cause of itchy scalp because the skin cells on the scalp grow too quickly, leading to dry, scaly patches that can cause intense itching and discomfort.
  • Fungal infection: A fungal infection, such as ringworm of the scalp, causes an itchy scalp because the fungus irritates the skin, leading to inflammation and an intense urge to scratch.
  • Eczema: Eczema is a cause of itchy scalp because it makes the skin on the scalp become dry, inflamed, and irritated, leading to intense itching and discomfort.
  • Tinea capitis: Tinea capitis is a fungal infection of the scalp that causes an itchy scalp because the fungus creates an allergic reaction that makes the skin on the scalp feel itchy and inflamed.
  • Allergic contact dermatitis: Allergic contact dermatitis is a cause of an itchy scalp because the skin on the scalp reacts to a substance, like a shampoo or hair dye, by becoming inflamed and itchy.
