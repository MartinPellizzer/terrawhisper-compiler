1. Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis tinctures contains valerenic acid, isovaleric acid, and valeranone as its main bioactive constituents.

These compounds have a sedative and relaxing effect on the body, which can help reduce puffiness and swelling under the eyes. The antispasmodic properties of valerian root tincture can also help alleviate tension and stress that may contribute to eye bags. Additionally, valerian root has anti-inflammatory properties that may help reduce redness and swelling.

By promoting a good night's sleep and reducing stress, Valeriana officinalis tinctures can help minimize the appearance of eye bags.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Valeriana officinalis roots and 2 cups of vodka in a clean glass jar.
  2. Chop the Valeriana officinalis roots into small pieces and add them to the jar.
  3. Pour 2 cups of vodka over the chopped roots, making sure they are completely covered.
  4. Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
  5. After 2-3 weeks, strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids and store the tincture in a cool, dark place.
