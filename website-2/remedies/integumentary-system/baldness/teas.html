10 best herbal teas for baldness

1. Serenoa Repens

Serenoa repens tea, also known as saw palmetto tea, is made from the berries of the saw palmetto plant. It's been used for years to help with hair loss and baldness. The main reason for this is that it blocks the production of dihydrotestosterone (DHT), a hormone that causes hair follicles to shrink and eventually stop growing hair. DHT is a byproduct of testosterone, a male hormone, but it's also present in women. When DHT builds up in the body, it can lead to hair loss, especially on the top of the head. Serenoa repens tea contains compounds that stop DHT from entering the hair follicles, which helps to slow down hair loss and even promote new hair growth. It's also thought to improve blood flow to the scalp, which can help to nourish the hair follicles and promote healthy hair growth. Some people also find that it helps to reduce inflammation and itching on the scalp, which can be a problem for people with hair loss.
