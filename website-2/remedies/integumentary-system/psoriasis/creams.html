Psoriasis Remedies for Adults: 7 Herbal Creams to Consider

Psoriasis is a common skin condition that affects many adults, causing red, itchy, and scaly patches on their skin.

It's not just a cosmetic issue, but a chronic condition that can also lead to discomfort, pain, and anxiety. For some adults, psoriasis can be a constant companion, affecting their self-esteem, social life, and even their work or school performance. It can also increase the risk of other health problems, such as diabetes and heart disease. Adults with psoriasis may struggle to find effective treatments that work for them, and some may experience side effects from conventional medications. This can be frustrating and demotivating, leading to a cycle of disappointment and self-doubt. The exact cause of psoriasis is not fully understood, but it's thought to be related to an overactive immune system, genetics, and environmental factors.

Some adults may experience triggers that set off a psoriasis flare, such as stress, certain foods, or changes in the weather. While there's no cure for psoriasis, there are various treatments available to help manage the symptoms. Herbal creams have been used by some adults to help treat psoriasis. These creams are typically made from plant extracts that have anti-inflammatory and soothing properties. They work by reducing inflammation, calming the skin, and promoting healing. Some herbal creams may also help to slow down the growth of skin cells, which can contribute to the development of psoriasis.

While more research is needed to fully understand the effectiveness of herbal creams for psoriasis, some adults have reported positive results from using them.

1. Aloe barbadensis

Aloe barbadensis creams are a popular natural remedy for psoriasis, a chronic skin condition characterized by red, itchy, and scaly patches.

The gel of the aloe vera plant contains anti-inflammatory compounds like aloin and aloe-emodin, as well as vitamins A, C, and E, which help soothe and calm the skin. These properties reduce redness, swelling, and inflammation associated with psoriasis, making aloe vera creams an effective topical treatment.

When using aloe barbadensis creams for psoriasis, it's essential to apply them directly to the affected areas 2-3 times a day, ideally after a warm bath or shower to open up the pores. Be sure to choose a cream that contains at least 90% aloe vera gel, and avoid any products with added fragrances, dyes, or other ingredients that may irritate the skin.

By following this simple routine, you can expect to see an improvement in your psoriasis symptoms within a few weeks, such as reduced redness, itching, and scaling, and potentially even the disappearance of psoriasis patches.

2. Melaleuca alternifolia

Melaleuca alternifolia creams, also known as tea tree oil creams, have been used to help manage psoriasis symptoms.

The main reason is that the cream contains compounds like terpinen-4-ol, which has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties. These properties can help reduce the severity of psoriasis by controlling the growth of bacteria and fungi that can exacerbate the condition. Additionally, the cream's anti-inflammatory properties can help soothe and calm the skin, reducing redness and irritation. If you're considering using Melaleuca alternifolia creams for psoriasis, start by applying a small amount to the affected area and see how your skin reacts.

Look for creams that contain high concentrations of tea tree oil, typically around 3-5%. You can apply the cream 2-3 times a day, gently massaging it into the skin until it's fully absorbed. Be patient and consistent, as it may take some time to notice improvements. When using Melaleuca alternifolia creams for psoriasis, you may experience reduced inflammation and redness, and possibly even a decrease in the number of psoriasis lesions. You may also notice an improvement in the overall appearance of your skin, making it look smoother and more even-toned.

However, keep in mind that everyone's skin is different, and what works for one person may not work for another.
