11 Herbal Tinctures For Psoriasis

Smokers are more likely to get psoriasis because nicotine and other chemicals in tobacco smoke can trigger an immune system response that leads to skin inflammation.

This can cause red, scaly patches to appear on the skin, often on the elbows, knees, and scalp. For smokers with psoriasis, managing their symptoms can be a daily struggle. They may experience itching, bleeding, and pain, which can be embarrassing and affect their self-confidence. Psoriasis can also make everyday activities, like getting dressed or going to work, much harder.

Smokers may find themselves constantly dealing with flare-ups, trying to find ways to soothe their skin and reduce the frequency of these episodes. This can be frustrating and affect their quality of life. Smokers with psoriasis may also be more prone to depression and anxiety, which can make it even harder to manage their condition. However, some herbal tinctures may help. These tinctures are made from plants that have anti-inflammatory and soothing properties, which can calm the skin and reduce the symptoms of psoriasis.

They can be taken orally or applied topically, and some people find them to be a helpful addition to their treatment plan.
