1. Aloe barbadensis

Aloe barbadensis tinctures contains aloin, aloe-emodin, and acemannan, which are powerful compounds that help achieve glowing skin.

Aloin has anti-inflammatory properties that soothe skin irritations and reduce redness. Aloe-emodin is a potent antioxidant that protects skin cells from damage caused by free radicals, promoting a healthy and even skin tone. Acemannan stimulates collagen production, which helps to plump and firm the skin, reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

By incorporating these beneficial compounds, aloe barbadensis tinctures can help to leave skin looking clear, smooth, and radiant.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Aloe barbadensis gel and 1 cup of Vodka (at least 80 proof).
  2. Combine the Aloe gel and Vodka in a clean glass jar. Stir well to mix.
  3. Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
  4. After the 2-3 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.
  5. Store the Aloe tincture in the refrigerator. Use 10-20 drops of the tincture under your skin or add it to your skincare products as needed.

2. Silybum marianum

Silybum marianum tinctures contains silymarin, a mixture of flavonoids, polyphenols, and lignans.

These compounds help protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals and oxidative stress. Silymarin also has anti-inflammatory properties, which can reduce redness and swelling in the skin. Additionally, silymarin has been shown to improve skin elasticity and firmness by increasing collagen production.

By reducing damage and inflammation, and promoting collagen production, silymarin helps to give skin a more even and radiant glow.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Silybum marianum flowers and 2 cups of 80% vodka in a clean glass jar.
  2. Add the flowers to the jar, then pour the vodka over them. Cover the jar and shake well.
  3. Store the jar in a cool, dark place and let it sit for 2-3 weeks. Shake the jar every day.
  4. After the sitting time, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean jar.
  5. Store the tincture in a dark glass bottle with a tight lid and use 5-10 drops as needed for glowing skin.

5. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra tinctures contains glycyrrhizin, a triterpenoid saponin that helps to brighten and even out skin tone.
