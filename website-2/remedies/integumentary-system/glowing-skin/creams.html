1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis creams contains triterpenoids, flavonoids, and carotenoids as its active constituents.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties that help soothe and protect the skin. The triterpenoids, particularly saponins, reduce redness and inflammation, while the flavonoids, such as quercetin and kaempferol, have antioxidant effects that fight free radicals and promote collagen production. The carotenoids, including lutein and zeaxanthin, protect the skin from sun damage and promote cell regeneration.

By addressing inflammation, protecting against sun damage, and promoting collagen production, Calendula officinalis creams can help achieve glowing, healthy-looking skin.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Calendula officinalis flowers, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of shea butter, and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Heat the coconut oil in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Add the beeswax to the coconut oil and stir until melted.
  4. Remove the mixture from the heat and add the Calendula officinalis flowers, shea butter, and vitamin E oil. Let it steep for 2-3 hours or overnight.
  5. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl. Pour the mixture into a jar and let it cool before use.

2. Aloe barbadensis

Aloe barbadensis creams contains aloin, aloe-emodin, and vitamin E, which work together to promote healthy and glowing skin.

Aloin has anti-inflammatory properties that reduce redness and soothe irritated skin, while aloe-emodin has antioxidant properties that protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals. Vitamin E, on the other hand, helps to neutralize skin-damaging free radicals and promote collagen production, which can improve skin elasticity and texture. The combination of these properties in aloe barbadensis creams helps to even out skin tone, reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, and give the skin a radiant and youthful glow.

By using aloe barbadensis creams, you can help to nourish and protect your skin, promoting a healthy and glowing complexion.

  1. Gather 1 cup of aloe vera gel, 1/4 cup of coconut oil, 2 tablespoons of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of shea butter, and 2 teaspoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Once melted, remove from heat and stir in the shea butter and aloe vera gel until smooth.
  4. Add the vitamin E oil and stir well. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool until solid.
  5. Apply the aloe vera cream to your skin and massage gently for glowing skin.

3. Echinacea purpurea

Echinacea purpurea creams contains alkylamides, glycosides, and phenolic acids as its bioactive constituents.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help to soothe and protect the skin. The alkylamides in Echinacea purpurea creams can help to reduce redness and inflammation, resulting in a clearer and more even-toned complexion. The glycosides in this cream have been shown to have a brightening effect on the skin, which can help to give it a more radiant and glowing appearance.

The antioxidant properties of the phenolic acids in Echinacea purpurea creams can help to neutralize free radicals and reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Echinacea purpurea flowers and 1/2 cup of coconut oil in a saucepan.
  2. Heat the coconut oil over low heat, then add the Echinacea flowers. Let it simmer for 2 hours.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Discard the solids.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of beeswax and 2 tablespoons of shea butter to the oil mixture. Heat it over low heat until the wax and butter melt.
  5. Pour the mixture into a glass jar and let it cool. Once set, your Echinacea purpurea cream is ready to use.

4. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra creams contains glycyrrhetic acid and triterpenoids, which are powerful antioxidants that help protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals.

These compounds also have anti-inflammatory properties, which reduce redness and soothe irritated skin, creating a calm and balanced complexion. Glycyrrhiza glabra creams also contain flavonoids, which help to brighten and even out skin tone, giving the skin a natural glow. Additionally, glycyrrhizin, a key component of glycyrrhiza glabra, has been shown to inhibit the activity of enzymes that break down collagen, helping to maintain skin elasticity and firmness.

By reducing inflammation and protecting the skin from damage, glycyrrhiza glabra creams help to promote healthy, glowing skin.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of distilled water, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of glycerin, and 2 teaspoons of dried Glycyrrhiza glabra root powder.
  2. Boil the distilled water in a saucepan and let it cool.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the coconut oil and shea butter until smooth.
  4. Add the cooled water, glycerin, and Glycyrrhiza glabra root powder to the coconut oil mixture. Stir well.
  5. Pour the mixture into a clean container and refrigerate until it hardens. Use as a glowing skin cream.

5. Lavandula angustifolia

Lavandula angustifolia creams contains linalool and linalyl acetate, which are known for their anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

These properties help to reduce redness and irritation in the skin, promoting a smooth and even tone. The cream also contains vitamin E, which protects the skin from damage caused by free radicals and helps to improve skin elasticity. The combination of these ingredients helps to enhance skin hydration, reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

By soothing and nourishing the skin, Lavandula angustifolia creams can help to give it a radiant and healthy glow.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, 1/4 cup of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of lavender essential oil, and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler over low heat.
  3. Add the shea butter to the melted mixture and stir until it's fully incorporated.
  4. Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool for 10 minutes. Stir in the lavender essential oil and vitamin E oil.
  5. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool and solidify. Once solid, your Lavandula angustifolia cream is ready to use.

6. Rosmarinus officinalis

Rosmarinus officinalis creams contains rosmarinic acid, a powerful antioxidant that helps protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals.

This acid also has anti-inflammatory properties, which reduce redness and irritation that can lead to dull skin. The essential oils in Rosmarinus officinalis creams, such as camphor and borneol, help to improve circulation and reduce puffiness, giving the skin a brighter and more even appearance. Additionally, the vitamin B complex in these creams, particularly thiamine and riboflavin, helps to boost collagen production and improve skin elasticity, resulting in a more radiant glow.

By combining these properties, Rosmarinus officinalis creams can help to give the skin a healthy and glowing appearance.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of rosemary essential oil, and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt coconut oil and shea butter in a double boiler or a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval, until smooth.
  3. Add beeswax to the melted mixture and stir until combined. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  4. Add rosemary essential oil and vitamin E oil to the mixture and stir well. Pour into a container and let cool completely.
  5. Once cooled, use a hand mixer or whisk to whip the mixture until light and fluffy. Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.

7. Melissa officinalis

Melissa officinalis creams contains bioactive constituents like linalool, linalyl acetate, and geraniol.

These compounds have antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties that help to soothe and calm the skin. The anti-inflammatory properties of Melissa officinalis creams can reduce redness and irritation, promoting a healthy and even skin tone. The antioxidants in Melissa officinalis creams also help to neutralize free radicals that can cause skin damage and discoloration, resulting in a glowing and radiant complexion.

By reducing inflammation and protecting the skin from damage, Melissa officinalis creams can help to achieve a smooth and even-toned skin.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of Melissa officinalis flowers, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of beeswax, and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Combine Melissa officinalis flowers and coconut oil in a saucepan. Heat over low heat for 10-15 minutes, then let cool.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Discard the solids.
  4. Melt the shea butter and beeswax in a separate saucepan over low heat. Add the vitamin E oil and stir until combined.
  5. Combine the cooled flower mixture and the melted shea butter mixture. Pour into a container and let cool before use.
