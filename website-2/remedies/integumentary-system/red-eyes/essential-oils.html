1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis essential oils have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties that help with red eyes.

The oil contains constituents like triterpenoids, flavonoids, and carotenoids, which calm and soothe the eyes. When you apply Calendula officinalis essential oil to your eyelids, it reduces swelling and redness by blocking the production of pro-inflammatory chemicals. To use Calendula officinalis essential oils for red eyes, mix a few drops of the oil with a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil. Gently apply the mixture to your eyelids with a cotton swab.

You can do this 2-3 times a day as needed. You can also add a few drops to a warm compress and place it over your closed eyes for a few minutes to help reduce puffiness and redness. When you use Calendula officinalis essential oils for red eyes, you can expect to see a reduction in redness and swelling within a few hours. If your red eyes are caused by an underlying condition, using Calendula officinalis essential oils may help to alleviate some symptoms, but you should consult a doctor for proper diagnosis and treatment.
