Calendula officinalis creams contains anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties that can help with red eyes.

When you apply Calendula officinalis cream to your red eyes, the triterpene saponins and sesquiterpene lactones work together to calm the irritated area and promote healing. You may start to see improvement in the redness and swelling within a few hours, and it's not uncommon for the redness to completely subside within a day or two.

However, if your red eyes persist or worsen, it's always best to consult with an eye care professional for proper diagnosis and treatment.
