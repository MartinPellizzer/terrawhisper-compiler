10 Soothing Herbal Creams for Psoriasis Patients with Skin Irritation

People with psoriasis often experience itchy skin, which can be really uncomfortable and affect their daily lives.

The itchy skin can be caused by the body's immune system attacking healthy skin cells, leading to inflammation and flaking skin. This can make it hard for people with psoriasis to sleep, work, or even enjoy simple activities. The itching can be so intense that it leads to scratching, which can cause more damage to the skin and make it harder to treat.

This can create a cycle of discomfort and frustration, making it challenging for people with psoriasis to manage their symptoms and feel confident in their own skin. When it comes to treating itchy skin in psoriasis patients, topical creams can be a helpful option. These creams are applied directly to the skin and contain soothing ingredients that can help calm inflammation and reduce itching. Some creams contain plant-based extracts that have anti-inflammatory and antihistamine properties, which can help to calm the skin and reduce the urge to scratch.

By applying these creams regularly, people with psoriasis may be able to find some relief from itchy skin and improve their overall skin health.

1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis creams are a popular remedy for itchy skin due to their anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

The creams contain triterpene saponins, flavonoids, and carotenoids, which are responsible for their soothing and calming effects on the skin. When applied topically, these constituents help to reduce redness, swelling, and itching associated with skin irritations and allergies. If you're experiencing itchy skin, try applying Calendula officinalis creams directly to the affected area 2-3 times a day. Gently massage the cream into the skin until it's fully absorbed, and then leave it on. You can also add a few drops of coconut or olive oil to the cream for added moisturizing benefits.

Be sure to clean and dry the skin before applying the cream to ensure it's fully absorbed. When you start using Calendula officinalis creams, you may notice a decrease in itching and inflammation within a few hours. The redness may also start to subside, and the skin may appear smoother and more hydrated. In severe cases of eczema or dermatitis, it may take a few days to a week for the symptoms to fully subside. It's essential to be consistent with your application and to monitor your skin's response to the cream.

If your symptoms persist or worsen, consult with a healthcare professional for further guidance.
