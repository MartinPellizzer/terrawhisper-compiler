Causes and Herbal Remedies for Bleeding Spots in Mouth: A Comprehensive Guide

What are the causes of bleeding spots in mouth?

Bleeding spots in the mouth can be caused by several factors.

Oral cancer is one of them, which occurs when abnormal cells in the mouth grow and multiply uncontrollably. This can cause ulcers or sores that bleed easily. Gum disease, also known as periodontal disease, is another cause.

When plaque and bacteria in the mouth cause inflammation of the gums, they can become irritated and bleed. Oral surgery, such as tooth extraction or biopsy, can also lead to bleeding spots in the mouth due to the incision made in the tissue. Some people have a bleeding disorder, a condition that affects the blood's ability to clot, which can cause even minor mouth injuries to bleed excessively.

Oral trauma, such as biting the tongue or cheek, can also cause bleeding spots in the mouth due to the injury causing damage to the tissue.
