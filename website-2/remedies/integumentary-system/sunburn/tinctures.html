To make Aloe vera herbal tincture for sunburn relief, start by gathering fresh Aloe vera gel from a mature plant.

Harvest the gel in the morning, as it's most potent at this time. Cut off a thick leaf from the plant, and slice it open to extract the clear gel inside. Scoop out the gel with a spoon and transfer it to a clean glass jar. Next, add a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil to the jar, covering the Aloe gel completely. The general ratio is 1 part Aloe gel to 2 parts carrier oil.

Stir the mixture well and seal the jar. Allow the mixture to steep in a cool, dark place for 2-6 weeks, shaking the jar every few days. This process will help the active compounds in the Aloe gel infuse into the carrier oil, creating a potent tincture. After the steeping period, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids. Your Aloe vera herbal tincture is now ready to use as a soothing treatment for sunburned skin.

Simply apply a small amount to the affected area, and gently massage it in.

What herbal tinctures you should avoid if you have sunburn?

If you have sunburn, it's best to avoid tinctures made from herbs like 'Tea tree oil' (Melaleuca alternifolia) and 'Eucalyptus globulus'.

Tea tree oil tinctures can be too harsh for sunburned skin and may cause more irritation. Eucalyptus tinctures can also be too cooling, which may make the burn feel worse.

'Ginger' (Zingiber officinale) tinctures might not be the best choice either, as they can cause blood vessels to dilate, leading to increased blood flow to the affected area, making the sunburn look worse. 'Turmeric' (Curcuma longa) tinctures contain curcumin, which has anti-inflammatory properties, but it can also make the skin more sensitive, making sunburn worse. 'Sage' (Salvia officinalis) tinctures can be too drying for sunburned skin and may cause it to become even more irritated.

'Cayenne pepper' (Capsicum annuum) tinctures are not suitable for sunburned skin either, as they can increase blood flow and cause more pain.
