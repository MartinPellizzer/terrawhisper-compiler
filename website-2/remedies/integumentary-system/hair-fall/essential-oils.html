7 Herbal Essential Oils For Hair Fall

Pregnancy can be a challenging time for many women, and one of the less talked about issues is hair fall.

During pregnancy, hormonal changes can cause an increase in the growth phase of hair, but also lead to a sudden stop in the growth phase, resulting in hair fall. This can be distressing for many women, especially those who value their long hair or have a special occasion to look forward to. Hair fall can also cause emotional distress, affect self-esteem, and make everyday tasks like washing and styling hair more difficult. Moreover, some pregnant women may experience hair fall due to nutritional deficiencies, stress, or underlying health conditions. For those who are already experiencing hair fall, the added pressure of pregnancy can exacerbate the issue, making it harder to manage.

When hair fall occurs during pregnancy, it can be especially frustrating because many common treatments or shampoos may not be suitable for pregnant women. This can leave many women feeling helpless and unsure of what to do. However, there are some natural remedies that can help alleviate hair fall during pregnancy. Herbal essential oils have been used for centuries to promote hair growth and reduce hair fall. These oils can help to balance the hormonal changes that occur during pregnancy, reduce stress, and promote a healthy scalp. They can be added to hair care products, used in a scalp massage, or inhaled to promote relaxation and reduce tension.

By incorporating herbal essential oils into their hair care routine, pregnant women can help to reduce hair fall and promote healthy hair growth.

1. Rosmarinus officinalis

Rosmarinus officinalis essential oils contains properties that can help with hair fall.

It has constituents like alpha-pinene, beta-pinene, and camphor, which have anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties. These properties help to soothe an itchy scalp, reduce dandruff, and prevent bacterial or fungal infections that can contribute to hair loss. The anti-inflammatory properties also help to reduce redness and irritation, creating a healthy environment for hair growth. To use Rosmarinus officinalis essential oils for hair fall, mix a few drops with a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil and massage it into your scalp.

Leave it on for at least an hour before washing your hair. You can also add a few drops to your shampoo or conditioner for an extra boost. When you start using Rosmarinus officinalis essential oils, you may notice a reduction in dandruff and itchiness on your scalp. Your scalp may start to feel cooler and more comfortable, and you may see an improvement in hair texture and density. Regular use may also lead to a decrease in hair shedding and breakage, and an increase in hair growth.

However, results may vary depending on individual circumstances and the severity of hair fall.

4. Cymbopogon citratus

Cymbopogon citratus essential oils contains a unique combination of properties and constituents that make it effective in addressing hair fall.

Massage the mixture into your scalp and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before washing your hair with a mild shampoo. You can repeat this process 2-3 times a week for best results. The likely outcomes of using Cymbopogon citratus essential oils include a reduction in hair fall, improved scalp health, and a decrease in dandruff and itchiness.
