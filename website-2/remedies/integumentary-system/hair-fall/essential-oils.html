Hair Fall During Pregnancy? Use 7 Herbal Essential Oils

Pregnancy can be a challenging time for many women, and one of the less talked about issues is hair fall.

During pregnancy, hormonal changes can cause an increase in the growth phase of hair, but also lead to a sudden stop in the growth phase, resulting in hair fall. This can be distressing for many women, especially those who value their long hair or have a special occasion to look forward to. Hair fall can also cause emotional distress, affect self-esteem, and make everyday tasks like washing and styling hair more difficult. Moreover, some pregnant women may experience hair fall due to nutritional deficiencies, stress, or underlying health conditions. For those who are already experiencing hair fall, the added pressure of pregnancy can exacerbate the issue, making it harder to manage.

When hair fall occurs during pregnancy, it can be especially frustrating because many common treatments or shampoos may not be suitable for pregnant women. This can leave many women feeling helpless and unsure of what to do. However, there are some natural remedies that can help alleviate hair fall during pregnancy. Herbal essential oils have been used for centuries to promote hair growth and reduce hair fall. These oils can help to balance the hormonal changes that occur during pregnancy, reduce stress, and promote a healthy scalp. They can be added to hair care products, used in a scalp massage, or inhaled to promote relaxation and reduce tension.

By incorporating herbal essential oils into their hair care routine, pregnant women can help to reduce hair fall and promote healthy hair growth.

1. Rosmarinus officinalis

Rosmarinus officinalis essential oils, also known as rosemary essential oil, is a natural remedy that can help reduce hair fall.

It contains properties like antioxidants, anti-inflammatory compounds, and essential nutrients that stimulate hair growth and improve scalp health. The constituents of rosemary essential oil include camphor, borneol, and bornyl acetate, which have been shown to improve blood circulation to the scalp and reduce dandruff and other scalp conditions that can lead to hair loss. If you're experiencing hair fall, you can try using rosemary essential oil in the following ways: dilute a few drops of the oil in a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil, and massage it into your scalp before shampooing. You can also add a few drops of rosemary essential oil to your shampoo or conditioner for an added boost. Another option is to use a rosemary essential oil-infused hair mask once or twice a week to nourish and stimulate your scalp.

When using rosemary essential oil, start with a small amount and gradually increase as your scalp becomes more tolerant. When you begin using rosemary essential oil for hair fall, you can expect to see improvements in your scalp health and hair growth within a few weeks. You may notice a decrease in dandruff and other scalp conditions, and an increase in hair density and thickness. Your hair may also become stronger and more resilient, with a reduced rate of shedding. However, it's essential to note that rosemary essential oil may not completely stop hair fall, especially if it's caused by underlying medical conditions.

But it can certainly help to reduce the rate of hair loss and promote a healthy scalp.

2. Lavandula angustifolia

Lavandula angustifolia essential oils, also known as English lavender, is a popular natural remedy for hair fall.

The oil contains properties like linalool and linalyl acetate, which are known for their antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties. These properties help to soothe and calm the scalp, reducing stress and anxiety that can contribute to hair loss. The antioxidant properties of the oil also help to protect the hair follicles from damage caused by free radicals. To use Lavandula angustifolia essential oils for hair fall, you can add a few drops of the oil to your shampoo or massage it directly into your scalp.

Start by mixing a few drops of the oil with a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil, and gently massage it into your scalp. Leave it on for 30 minutes to an hour before washing your hair as usual. As you continue to use the oil, you may notice a reduction in hair fall and an improvement in hair growth. The oil may also help to reduce dandruff and itchiness on the scalp, leading to a healthier and more balanced scalp environment.

With regular use, you may experience a decrease in the number of hair strands falling out and an increase in the length and thickness of your hair.
