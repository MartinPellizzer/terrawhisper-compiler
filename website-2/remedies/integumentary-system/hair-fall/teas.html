10 best herbal teas for hair fall

1. Rosmarinus Officinalis

Rosmarinus officinalis tea, also known as rosemary tea, is a natural remedy that can help reduce hair fall. The tea is made from the leaves of the rosemary plant, which are rich in antioxidants and essential oils. These compounds help to improve blood circulation to the scalp, which is essential for healthy hair growth. Rosemary tea also has anti-inflammatory properties that can help to reduce dandruff and other scalp irritations that can cause hair fall. Additionally, the antioxidants in rosemary tea can help to protect the hair follicles from damage caused by free radicals, which can lead to hair loss. Drinking rosemary tea can also help to strengthen hair roots and promote healthy hair growth.
