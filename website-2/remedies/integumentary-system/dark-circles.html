Dark Circles: Causes and Herbal Remedies for a Refreshed Look

What are the causes of dark circles?

Dark circles under the eyes are a common issue, and there are several reasons why they happen.

Genetics can play a role, as some people are naturally more prone to dark circles due to the shape of their eyes or the thickness of their skin. Dehydration is another cause, because when our bodies don't have enough water, the skin around our eyes can become dry and dark.

Lack of sleep is also a major contributor, as our bodies need rest to repair and recharge, and when we don't get enough sleep, our skin can look tired and pale, making dark circles more noticeable. Fatigue, which is related to lack of sleep, can also cause dark circles, as our bodies are working hard to stay awake and alert.

Lastly, allergies can cause dark circles by making our eyes puffy and swollen, which can make the skin look darker.
