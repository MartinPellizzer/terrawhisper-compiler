It's also best to avoid tinctures of Evening Primrose (Oenothera biennis) and Dandelion (Taraxacum officinale) as they can cause stomach upset and worsen the pain.

Always consult with a healthcare professional before using herbal tinctures, especially if you have a toothache or are unsure about their safety.
