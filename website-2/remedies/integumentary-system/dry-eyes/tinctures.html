What herbal tinctures you should avoid if you have dry eyes?

'Capsicum annuum' can make your eyes more sensitive, while 'Gelsemium sempervirens' can reduce tear production and cause dryness. 'Krameria triandra' (Rhatany) may also not be suitable, as it can cause dryness and irritation. 'Sanguinaria canadensis' (Bloodroot) should also be used with caution, as it can cause irritation and potentially exacerbate dry eye problems.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using any herbal remedy, especially if you have pre-existing eye conditions.
