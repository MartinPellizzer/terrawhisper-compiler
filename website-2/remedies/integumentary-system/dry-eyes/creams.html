What herbal creams you should avoid if you have dry eyes?

If you have dry eyes, it's best to avoid herbal creams that contain ingredients like chamomile, witch hazel, and tea tree oil.

These herbs may be helpful for other skin issues, but they're not suitable for dry eyes.
