11 Herbal Creams For Dry Eyes

People with glaucoma often have trouble with dry eyes.

This is because the condition can damage the nerves that help keep the eyes moist. These nerves tell the eyes to produce tears, which is important for keeping them comfortable and clear. Without enough tears, the eyes can get dry, itchy, and even painful. This can affect daily activities like watching TV, reading, or even just being outside on a windy day. Some people with glaucoma may even have trouble sleeping because of dry, irritated eyes.

When eyes are dry, it's not just uncomfortable - it can also make it harder to see. This is because dry eyes can cause blurred vision, and even lead to infections. Glaucoma patients often try to use special drops to help with dry eyes, but these can have side effects. That's why some people are turning to herbal creams to help soothe and moisturize their dry eyes. These creams often contain special plant extracts that help to calm and comfort the eyes, making them feel better and more comfortable.

They can be applied directly to the eyes, and are often gentle and safe to use.

If you're experiencing dry eyes, try applying a small amount of Symphytum officinale cream to the affected area.
