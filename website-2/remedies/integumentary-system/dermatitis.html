Natural Dermatitis Treatment: Causes and Medicinal Herbs

What are the causes of dermatitis?

Dermatitis can be caused by different things.

One main cause is irritant dermatitis, which happens when the skin comes into contact with harsh chemicals or substances that damage it. This can be from soap, detergent, or other cleaning products that dry out the skin. Another cause is allergen dermatitis, where the skin reacts to substances like nickel in jewelry, pollen, or certain foods.

Sometimes, the skin can become sensitive to these substances and react by getting red, itchy, and inflamed. Some people might experience skin irritation from wearing certain types of jewelry, especially if it contains nickel, which is a common allergen. Additionally, exposure to soaps and detergents can strip the skin of its natural oils, leading to dryness and irritation.

Detergents, in particular, can be very harsh on the skin, especially if you have sensitive skin.
