1. Melaleuca alternifolia

Terpinen-4-ol works by penetrating the skin and killing the fungus, helping to reduce the symptoms of athlete's foot. Cineole also helps to reduce inflammation and soothe the skin, making it a popular natural remedy for athlete's foot.

These compounds in Melaleuca alternifolia creams work together to help treat and prevent athlete's foot.

  2. Add 2 tablespoons of beeswax and 1 tablespoon of coconut oil to the bowl.
  3. Melt the beeswax and coconut oil in the microwave for 10-15 seconds or over low heat.
  4. Add 10 drops of Melaleuca alternifolia essential oil to the melted mixture and stir well.
  5. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool and solidify before use.
