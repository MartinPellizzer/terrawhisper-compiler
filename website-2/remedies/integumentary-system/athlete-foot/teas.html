When used as a foot soak, the tea tree oil can help to dry out the affected area, making it harder for the fungus to survive. The tea tree oil also has anti-inflammatory properties that can help to reduce redness, itching, and swelling associated with athlete's foot. As a result, using Melaleuca alternifolia tea as a treatment can provide quick relief and help to clear up the infection faster.
