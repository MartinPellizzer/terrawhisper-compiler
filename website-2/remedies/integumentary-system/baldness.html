Baldness: Causes, Prevention, and Herbal Remedies

What are the causes of baldness?

Baldness is a common issue that affects millions of people worldwide.

There are several main causes of baldness, and understanding them can help. One of the main causes is androgenetic baldness, which is caused by the presence of dihydrotestosterone (DHT) - a hormone that's produced from testosterone. DHT causes the hair follicles to shrink, leading to thinner and eventually no hair growth. Genetic baldness is another cause, where the condition is inherited from parents. If your family members have a history of baldness, you're more likely to experience it too. Aging is another significant cause of baldness.

As we age, the growth cycle of hair slows down, and the hair follicles start to shrink. This leads to thinner hair and eventually baldness. Alopecia is a medical term for hair loss, and it's not just related to baldness. There are several types of alopecia, including alopecia areata, which is an autoimmune disease that causes hair loss on the scalp. Hormonal imbalance is also a cause of baldness. Changes in hormone levels, especially an increase in DHT, can lead to hair loss.

This can be due to various factors, including puberty, menopause, or certain medical conditions.
