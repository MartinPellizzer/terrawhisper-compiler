Calendula officinalis, also known as pot marigold, is a plant with many healing properties. For ingrown toenails, its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic qualities can be very helpful. The antiseptic properties of calendula also help prevent infection, which is common with ingrown toenails. By reducing inflammation and preventing infection, calendula can help the skin heal faster and prevent further irritation.
