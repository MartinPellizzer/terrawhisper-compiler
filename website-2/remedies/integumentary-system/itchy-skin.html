Aloe barbadensis

Aloe barbadensis, also known as aloe vera, is a plant that has been used for centuries to soothe itchy skin. The gel from the plant contains many good things that help to calm and cool the skin. One of these things is a compound called aloin, which has anti-inflammatory properties. This means it can help to reduce swelling and redness, making it easier for the skin to feel better. Aloe vera also contains vitamins A, C, and E, which help to protect the skin from damage and promote healing. When you apply aloe vera to itchy skin, the coolness of the gel can help to calm the skin and reduce the urge to scratch. This can be especially helpful for people who have skin conditions like eczema or psoriasis, where the skin can become very itchy and uncomfortable. The gel can also help to lock in moisture, keeping the skin hydrated and feeling softer.
