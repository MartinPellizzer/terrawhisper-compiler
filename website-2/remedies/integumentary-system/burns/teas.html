What herbal teas you should avoid if you have burns?

If you have burns, it's best to avoid teas that can irritate or worsen the skin.

Chamomile tea is not recommended, as its anti-inflammatory properties can actually slow down the healing process of burns. Ginger tea is also not suitable, as its warming properties can increase blood flow to the affected area, which can cause more pain and discomfort.

Licorice root tea should be avoided as it can cause skin irritation and allergic reactions. Sage tea is not a good choice either, as its antiseptic properties can dry out the skin, making it more prone to cracking and further irritation. Peppermint tea is another herb to steer clear of, as its cooling sensation can cause the skin to feel numb and increase the risk of infection.

Finally, teas made from sassafras, willow bark, and yarrow should also be avoided, as they can cause skin irritation and allergic reactions, and may even delay the healing process.
