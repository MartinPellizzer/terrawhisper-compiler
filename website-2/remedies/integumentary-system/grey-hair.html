Grey Hair Causes, Treatment, and Medicinal Herbal Preparations

What are the causes of grey hair?

So, what makes our hair turn grey?.

Let's break it down. Genetics is a big one - if your parents or grandparents started going grey early, you might too. It's just how our DNA works. Age is another obvious one - as we get older, our hair follicles produce less melanin, the pigment that gives our hair its color. When we're young, melanin production is in full swing, but as we hit our 30s and 40s, it starts to slow down, leading to grey hair.

Hormonal Imbalance is another cause - changes in our hormone levels can affect melanin production, leading to grey hair. This can be triggered by things like thyroid problems or menopause. Family History also plays a role - if your family has a history of going grey early, you might too, simply because you've inherited their genetic makeup. And then there's Stress - high levels of stress can cause our hair follicles to produce less melanin, leading to grey hair. It's like our body is saying, "Hey, we're under a lot of pressure, let's conserve energy and reduce pigment production.".

It's a way our body tries to cope with stress, but it can be frustrating for those of us who are trying to hold on to our youthful locks.

What are the most used herbal preparations for grey hair?

Herbal preparations can be a great way to address grey hair, and there are several options to choose from.

Tinctures, on the other hand, are concentrated extracts of herbs made with a solvent like alcohol or glycerin. They can be added to a shampoo or conditioner, or applied directly to the hair. Tinctures are a good choice for grey hair because they can help to stimulate hair growth and improve the overall health of the scalp.

Decoctions are similar to infusions, but they are made by boiling the herbs in water instead of steeping them. This method is often used for herbs that are too harsh to be infused in oil, and it can be a good choice for grey hair because it can help to stimulate hair growth and improve the circulation of the scalp.
