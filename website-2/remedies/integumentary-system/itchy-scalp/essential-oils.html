What herbal essential oils you should avoid if you have itchy scalp?

If you have an itchy scalp, there are some herbal essential oils you should avoid.

Bergamot oil (Citrus aurantium bergamia) can be irritating to the skin, especially if you're sensitive to citrus. Its high concentration of limonene can cause skin reactions like itching, redness, and even burns. Similarly, Geranium oil (Pelargonium graveolens) can cause skin irritation and allergic reactions in some people, which may worsen an itchy scalp.

Also, Clary Sage oil (Salvia sclarea) contains thujone, a compound that can be toxic in high amounts and may cause skin problems, including itching. Another oil to avoid is Galbanum oil (Ferula galbaniflua), which can be irritating to the skin and may cause allergic reactions. Lastly, Hyssop oil (Hyssopus officinalis) contains thujone and other compounds that may cause skin irritation and itching.

If you have an itchy scalp, it's best to consult with a healthcare professional or an aromatherapist before using any essential oils.
