The Dandruff Solution: Causes, Medicinal Herbs, and Herbal Preparations

Melaleuca alternifolia

Melaleuca alternifolia, also known as tea tree oil, is a natural remedy for dandruff. This oil is extracted from the leaves of a plant native to Australia. It has antifungal and antibacterial properties, which help to kill the yeast that causes dandruff. When applied to the scalp, tea tree oil reduces inflammation and prevents the flaking of skin cells. It also helps to balance the natural pH of the scalp, creating an environment that is not favorable to the growth of yeast. As a result, tea tree oil can help to soothe and calm an itchy scalp, and reduce the occurrence of dandruff flakes. Regular use of tea tree oil can lead to healthier and cleaner-looking hair.
