Understanding Bad Breath: Causes, Medicinal Herbs, and Natural Preparations

Mentha x piperita

Mentha x piperita, also known as peppermint, has natural properties that help fight bad breath. When you eat or drink something, the bacteria in your mouth break it down and release bad-smelling compounds. These compounds can cause bad breath, also known as halitosis. Peppermint contains compounds like menthol and menthone that have antibacterial properties. This means they can help stop the growth of bacteria in your mouth, reducing the production of bad-smelling compounds. Additionally, peppermint's cooling sensation can help stimulate saliva production, which helps wash away bacteria and food particles that can cause bad breath. By reducing the growth of bacteria and washing away bacteria and food particles, peppermint can help freshen your breath and make it feel cleaner in your mouth.

It also has a fresh, minty smell, which can help mask bad breath and leave our mouths feeling clean and fresh.
