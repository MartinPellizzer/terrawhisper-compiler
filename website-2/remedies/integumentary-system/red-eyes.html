Red Eyes: Causes, Herbal Preparations, and Home Remedies

What are the causes of red eyes?

Red eyes can be caused by a few things.

One common cause is allergies. When you're allergic to something like pollen or dust, your body releases histamine, which makes your eyes get itchy and red. This is because your eyes are trying to protect themselves from the allergen. Another cause of red eyes is dry eyes. This happens when you don't produce enough tears or your tears are not of good quality. This can be due to various factors like using contact lenses, being in a dry climate, or having a condition like Sjögren's syndrome. Without enough tears, your eyes get irritated and become red. Sinusitis can also cause red eyes.

This is because the sinuses and eyes are connected. When you have sinusitis, which is an inflammation of the sinuses, it can cause your eyes to become red and itchy. This is because the infection is spreading to the eyes. Exposure to irritants like chemicals, smoke, or dust can also cause red eyes. These substances can irritate your eyes and make them get red and itchy. This is because your eyes are trying to protect themselves from the irritant. Lastly, conjunctivitis, also known as pink eye, can cause red eyes. This is an infection that causes inflammation of the conjunctiva, which is the thin membrane covering the white part of your eye and the inside of your eyelid.

The infection can be viral or bacterial, and it can cause your eyes to become red, itchy, and watery.
