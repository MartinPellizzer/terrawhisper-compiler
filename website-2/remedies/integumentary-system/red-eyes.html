Euphrasia officinalis

Euphrasia officinalis, also known as Eyebright, is a herbal remedy that has been used for centuries to help with red, itchy, and watery eyes. The plant's leaves and flowers contain special compounds that can reduce swelling and calm irritated eyes. When you put Eyebright drops or eye ointment in your eyes, these compounds get to work, soothing the eyes and helping to get rid of redness and puffiness. Eyebright is especially useful for people who suffer from allergies or dry eyes, as it can help to balance the amount of moisture in the eyes and prevent irritation. The plant's anti-inflammatory properties also make it a good choice for people who have experienced eye strain or injury. By reducing inflammation and calming the eyes, Eyebright can help to make your eyes feel more comfortable and relaxed.

Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis, also known as pot marigold, is a plant that has been used for centuries to treat red eyes. The reason it's good for red eyes is that it has anti-inflammatory properties. When we get red eyes, it's usually because of swelling or irritation in the blood vessels around our eyes. Calendula officinalis contains compounds that help reduce this swelling and irritation, which in turn reduces redness. It also has antimicrobial properties, which means it can help prevent infections that might make red eyes worse. When applied to the eyes, calendula can bring down the swelling and calm the irritation, making the redness go away. This is because the compounds in the plant help to reduce the amount of blood flowing to the area, which reduces the redness and swelling.
