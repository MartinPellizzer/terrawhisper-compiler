What herbal teas you should avoid if you have open wounds?

If you have open wounds, it's best to avoid certain herbal teas that may cause problems or make the wound worse.

Sage tea can also make wounds more sensitive, and some studies suggest it may slow healing. Comfrey tea, which is sometimes used to treat wounds, can actually cause problems if ingested in large amounts, as it contains a chemical that can cause liver and kidney damage.
