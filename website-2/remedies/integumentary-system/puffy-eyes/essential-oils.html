What herbal essential oils you should avoid if you have puffy eyes?

If you have puffy eyes, it's best to avoid using essential oils from herbs that can irritate or inflame the skin.

One such oil is Peppermint (Mentha piperita), as its cooling sensation can cause blood vessels to dilate, making the eyes appear even puffier. Another oil to steer clear of is Eucalyptus (Eucalyptus globulus), which can be too intense and irritating for sensitive eye areas.

Similarly, Tea Tree (Melaleuca alternifolia) oil's antiseptic properties can be too harsh and cause more inflammation. Frankincense (Boswellia serrata) oil can also be too drying and irritating, especially if used undiluted. Finally, Birch (Betula alba) oil and Wintergreen (Gaultheria procumbens) oil contain compounds that can be too astringent and cause more puffiness.

These oils can be beneficial for other skin issues, but for puffy eyes, it's best to choose gentler options.
