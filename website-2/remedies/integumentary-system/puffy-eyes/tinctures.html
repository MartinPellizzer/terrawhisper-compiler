9 tinctures to alleviate puffy eyes in women naturally

Women often experience puffy eyes due to various reasons, including hormonal changes, allergies, and lack of sleep.

These factors can cause fluid to build up in the delicate skin around the eyes, leading to puffiness. This can be quite uncomfortable and affect a woman's daily life, making her feel tired, self-conscious, and less confident. Puffy eyes can also be a sign of underlying health issues, such as kidney problems or thyroid disorders. As a result, women may try various remedies to reduce puffiness, from cold compresses to expensive creams. However, many of these solutions only provide temporary relief and may not address the root cause of the issue.

Women may also find that certain lifestyle factors can exacerbate puffy eyes, such as consuming too much salt, not drinking enough water, or not getting enough rest. In addition, the skin around the eyes is very thin and sensitive, making it prone to irritation and inflammation. This can make it challenging to find a solution that works for everyone. Herbal tinctures are a natural and effective way to treat puffy eyes. These liquid extracts are made from plants that have been used for centuries to calm and soothe the skin.

When applied topically, they can help to reduce inflammation and fluid buildup, giving the skin around the eyes a more refreshed and revitalized appearance.

1. Euphrasia officinalis

Euphrasia officinalis tinctures are a natural remedy that can help with puffy eyes.

The plant, also known as Eyebright, contains properties like flavonoids, phenolic acids, and saponins, which have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. These constituents help to reduce swelling and combat free radicals that can cause puffiness. When using Euphrasia officinalis tinctures for puffy eyes, apply a few drops to a clean cloth and gently press it over your closed eyes.

You can also add a few drops to a cotton pad and place it in the fridge for a few minutes before applying it to your eyes. As you apply the tincture, you can expect the puffiness to start reducing, and your eyes may feel cooler and more relaxed. You might also notice that the redness and swelling decrease, giving you a more refreshed and awake appearance.

If you're taking Euphrasia officinalis tinctures orally, it's recommended to start with a small dose and gradually increase as needed, under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
