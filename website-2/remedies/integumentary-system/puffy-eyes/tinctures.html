1. Echinacea purpurea

Echinacea purpurea tinctures contains alkylamides, glycosides, and phenolic acids, which are responsible for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

The alkylamides in Echinacea purpurea tinctures have a soothing effect on the skin, reducing puffiness and swelling caused by excess fluid and inflammation. The glycosides, specifically echinacoside and caftaric acid, have been shown to have a potent anti-inflammatory effect, which helps to reduce puffiness and promote healthy circulation. The phenolic acids, particularly chlorogenic acid, have antioxidant properties that help to protect the delicate skin around the eyes from damage caused by free radicals.

By reducing inflammation and promoting healthy circulation, Echinacea purpurea tinctures can help to alleviate puffy eyes.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Echinacea purpurea flowers, 2 cups of vodka, and a clean glass jar with a lid.
  2. Combine the Echinacea flowers and vodka in the glass jar, making sure the flowers are completely covered.
  3. Seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids.
  5. Store the tincture in a cool, dark place and use 10-20 drops as needed to help reduce puffiness around the eyes.

2. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis tinctures contains flavonoids, triterpenoids, and carotenoids as its bioactive constituents.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties, which help reduce puffiness around the eyes. The flavonoids, specifically quercetin and kaempferol, inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes, while the triterpenoids, such as oleanolic acid, have antioxidant effects that protect the delicate skin around the eyes. The carotenoids, including lutein and zeaxanthin, have a soothing effect on the skin, reducing redness and swelling.

By reducing inflammation and soothing the skin, Calendula officinalis tinctures can help alleviate puffy eyes.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh Calendula officinalis flowers and 2 cups of 80% vodka in a clean glass jar.
  2. Add the Calendula flowers to the jar and top with vodka, leaving 1 inch of space at the top.
  3. Steep the mixture in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids.
  5. Store the Calendula tincture in the fridge and use 5-7 drops under the eyes to reduce puffiness, as needed.
