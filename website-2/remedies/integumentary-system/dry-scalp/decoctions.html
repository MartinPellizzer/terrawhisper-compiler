'Glycyrrhiza glabra' (licorice root) can also be too drying for a dry scalp, as it can strip the scalp of its natural moisture. Similarly, 'Echinacea spp.'. decoctions can be too drying and irritating for a sensitive scalp.

'Equisetum arvense' (horsetail) decoctions can also be too harsh and drying due to their high silica content.
