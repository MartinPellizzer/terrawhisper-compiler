Medicinal herbs like horsetail and nettle can help address this issue. Horsetail is rich in silica, a mineral that can strengthen bones and improve calcium absorption. Nettle, on the other hand, is a rich source of calcium and vitamin D. Another issue that people with grey hair may face is high blood pressure. This is often caused by inflammation and oxidative stress in the body. Herbs like ashwagandha and ginseng can help reduce inflammation and improve blood flow.

Ashwagandha is an adaptogen that can help the body cope with stress, while ginseng can improve cardiovascular health. Some people with grey hair may also experience digestive issues, such as constipation or irritable bowel syndrome (IBS). This can be caused by a lack of fiber and digestive enzymes. Herbs like slippery elm and peppermint can help soothe the digestive system and improve gut health. Slippery elm is a demulcent that can protect the gut lining, while peppermint can relax the muscles and improve digestion. Finally, people with grey hair may be more prone to cognitive decline and memory loss. This can be caused by a lack of antioxidants and brain-boosting nutrients. Herbs like ginkgo biloba and bacopa monnieri can help improve cognitive function and memory.

Ginkgo biloba can improve blood flow to the brain, while bacopa monnieri can enhance memory and cognitive processing.
