Grey Hair in Genetics Prone: 9 Herbal Essential Oil Remedies

People who are genetically prone to going grey often start to see their hair turn silver or white in their 30s or 40s.

This can be a frustrating and emotional experience, especially if they have always taken pride in their youthful appearance. The sudden change can make them feel older than they are, and it can affect their self-confidence and self-esteem. Genetics prone individuals may feel like they're losing their identity and struggle to cope with the emotional impact of premature greying. They might also feel anxious about how this change will affect their social and professional lives.

As a result, they may try to hide their grey hair with dyes or styling products, but these solutions can be temporary and may cause damage to their hair. They might also feel like they're fighting a losing battle, as their grey hair continues to grow and spread. Herbal essential oils can be a natural and chemical-free way for genetics prone individuals to address their premature greying. These oils are derived from plants and have been used for centuries to promote healthy hair growth and color. They work by nourishing the hair follicles and promoting melanin production, which is the pigment responsible for hair color.

By using these oils, genetics prone individuals can help to slow down or even reverse their greying process, giving them more control over their appearance and their emotions.

1. Rosmarinus officinalis

Rosmarinus officinalis essential oils, also known as rosemary essential oil, may help with grey hair due to its stimulating and antioxidant properties.

The oil contains constituents like camphor, bornyl acetate, and alpha-pinene, which may help improve circulation to the scalp and promote healthy hair growth. The antioxidants in rosemary essential oil, such as carnosic acid and rosmarinic acid, may also protect the hair follicles from damage caused by free radicals, potentially slowing down the greying process.

To use rosemary essential oil for grey hair, mix a few drops with a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil and massage it into the scalp. Leave it on for at least an hour before washing your hair as usual. Regular use may lead to improved hair texture, reduced dandruff, and a slower rate of greying.

However, it's essential to note that individual results may vary, and the effectiveness of rosemary essential oil for grey hair can depend on the underlying cause of hair greying.

Sesamum indicum essential oils, also known as sesame oil, is a natural remedy that may help with grey hair.

The antioxidants in sesame oil also help nourish the scalp, promoting a healthy environment for hair growth. Leave it on for at least an hour before shampooing your hair. Repeat this process once or twice a week for optimal results. When you start using sesame oil, you may notice a reduction in grey hair growth, as the antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties help promote healthy hair growth. You may also notice an improvement in hair texture, making your hair look shinier and healthier.

However, it's essential to note that sesame oil may not completely reverse grey hair, but it can help slow down the process.
