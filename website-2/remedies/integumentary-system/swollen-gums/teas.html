2. Eucalyptus globulus

3. Salvia officinalis

Salvia officinalis teas contains compounds like rosmarinic acid and carnosic acid, which have anti-inflammatory properties.

FAQ

Consuming herbal teas for swollen gums daily can be safe if you choose the right ones.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in swollen gums?

Herbal teas can help reduce swollen gums, but it's hard to predict exactly when you'll see results.

What time of day is best to drink herbal tea for swollen gums?

To help with swollen gums, drink herbal tea in the morning or early afternoon.

Drinking too close to bedtime may interfere with a good night's sleep, making it harder for your gums to heal.
