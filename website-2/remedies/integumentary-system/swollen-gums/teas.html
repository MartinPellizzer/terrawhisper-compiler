8 Herbal Teas For Swollen Gums

During pregnancy, many women experience swollen gums, also known as gingivitis.

This is because hormonal changes cause their gums to become more sensitive and prone to inflammation. Swollen gums can be painful and make eating, speaking, and even smiling uncomfortable. They can also increase the risk of gum disease, which can lead to more serious health problems. For pregnant women, swollen gums can be especially challenging because they may need to eat nutritious foods to support their growing baby, but pain and discomfort can make mealtime a struggle.

Additionally, swollen gums can be a sign of an underlying issue that needs to be addressed, so it's essential for pregnant women to talk to their healthcare provider about their symptoms. In some cases, swollen gums can be a precursor to more severe pregnancy complications, such as preterm labor or low birth weight. To help manage swollen gums, pregnant women can try using herbal teas that have anti-inflammatory properties. These teas can help reduce swelling and ease discomfort. They are also a gentle, natural way to promote healthy gums and prevent further inflammation.

When consumed regularly, these teas can help pregnant women maintain good oral health and reduce their risk of more serious complications.

1. Echinacea purpurea

Echinacea purpurea teas contains properties that may help with swollen gums.

FAQ

What time of day is best to drink herbal tea for swollen gums?

To help with swollen gums, drink herbal tea in the morning or early afternoon.
