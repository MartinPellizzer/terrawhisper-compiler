The medicinal herb quercetin, found in plants like oak and maple trees, can help reduce inflammation and relieve sinus pressure. Quercetin is a natural antihistamine that blocks the release of chemical histamine, which causes allergic reactions. Itchy eyes can also be a symptom of conjunctivitis, an eye infection caused by bacteria or viruses. People with itchy eyes may also experience dry eyes, which can be treated with herbs like eyebright and goldenseal.

Eyebright has anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce swelling and redness in the eyes, while goldenseal has antimicrobial properties that can help fight off bacterial infections. People with itchy eyes may also experience skin issues like eczema or dermatitis, which can be treated with herbs like aloe vera and chamomile. Aloe vera has anti-inflammatory properties that can help soothe and calm irritated skin, while chamomile has antiseptic and anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce redness and itching. In addition, people with itchy eyes may also experience respiratory issues like asthma or bronchitis, which can be treated with herbs like ephedra and ginkgo biloba. Ephedra has decongestant properties that can help relieve nasal congestion, while ginkgo biloba has anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce inflammation in the lungs. Herbal remedies like these can provide relief from itchy eyes and other associated health issues.

They work by reducing inflammation, fighting off infections, and soothing irritated skin and eyes.
