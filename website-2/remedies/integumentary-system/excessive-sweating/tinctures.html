1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale tinctures contains a high concentration of gingerols and shogaols, bioactive constituents that have been shown to have a positive effect on excessive sweating.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, which help to reduce the nervous system's stimulation of sweat glands. Gingerols and shogaols also have a warming effect on the body, which can increase blood flow and help to cool the body more efficiently, reducing the need for excessive sweating. Additionally, zingiber officinale tinctures contain compounds that help to regulate body temperature, making it easier for the body to manage heat stress.

By addressing the root causes of excessive sweating, zingiber officinale tinctures can provide relief from this condition.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh Zingiber officinale (ginger) roots and 2 cups of vodka.
  2. Chop the ginger roots into small pieces.
  3. Combine the chopped ginger with 2 cups of vodka in a clean glass jar.
  4. Seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily.
  5. Strain the tincture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into another clean jar, discarding the solids.
