For older adults, bleeding spots in the mouth can be a source of worry, discomfort, and anxiety, affecting their daily lives and self-confidence. Bleeding spots in the mouth can be a symptom of an underlying condition, and it's essential for older adults to consult with their healthcare provider to identify the cause.
