If left untreated, oral thrush can spread to the baby's mouth, leading to similar symptoms. To treat oral thrush, mothers can try using herbal teas. These teas have antifungal properties that help combat the growth of Candida in the mouth. They can be used as a natural remedy to soothe the affected area and promote healing.

By drinking these teas, breastfeeding mothers can help reduce the symptoms of oral thrush and create a healthier environment for themselves and their baby.
