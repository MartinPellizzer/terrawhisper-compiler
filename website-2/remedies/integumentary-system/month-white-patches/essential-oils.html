1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis essential oils contains triterpenoids, flavonoids, and carotenoids as its bioactive constituents.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant properties that can help with white patches on the mouth. The flavonoids in calendula essential oils have been found to inhibit the growth of bacteria and fungi, which can contribute to oral infections. The triterpenoids in calendula have anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce swelling and promote healing in the mouth.

Regular use of calendula essential oils may help reduce the appearance of white patches and promote overall oral health.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Calendula officinalis flowers and 2 cups of carrier oil (like coconut or olive oil). Clean and dry the flowers.
  2. In a clean glass jar, combine the Calendula flowers and carrier oil. Make sure the flowers are fully covered by the oil.
  3. Leave the jar in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day. This is the steeping process.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the oil mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.
  5. Transfer the Calendula essential oil to a small dark glass bottle and store it in a cool, dark place. Use 2-3 drops of the oil on the affected area 2 times a day.

2. Melaleuca alternifolia

By addressing these underlying causes, Melaleuca alternifolia essential oils may help to promote healthy oral mucosa and reduce the appearance of white patches.

  1. Gather 2 cups of Melaleuca alternifolia leaves and 1 cup of carrier oil (coconut or olive oil).
  2. Steep the Melaleuca leaves in the carrier oil for 2-3 weeks in a glass jar with a lid, shaking it daily.
  3. After 2-3 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another container.
  4. Discard the solids and transfer the liquid into small glass bottles with tight-fitting lids.
  5. Label the bottles and store them in a cool, dark place for up to 6 months. Use 5-7 drops of the essential oil for skin application 2-3 times a day.
