Teenagers Unite: 9 Herbal Tinctures to Fight Pimples

Teenagers often struggle with pimples, and it's not just about their skin.

Hormonal changes during puberty can cause glands in the skin to get clogged, leading to red, painful bumps. These bumps can be embarrassing and affect a teenager's self-confidence and mood. They might feel self-conscious about their appearance, avoid social situations, or even develop anxiety. Pimples can also leave scars, which can be a long-term concern. When a teenager has pimples, it can feel like a constant battle to keep their skin clear.

They might try different products, but sometimes these products can irritate their skin even more. In this situation, teenagers might look for more natural solutions to treat their pimples. That's where herbal tinctures come in. These are liquid extracts made from plants that have been used for centuries to soothe and heal the skin. When used correctly, they can help reduce inflammation and fight off bacteria that cause pimples. The active ingredients in these tinctures can also help to unclog pores and promote healthy skin.

By using herbal tinctures, teenagers can take a more holistic approach to treating their pimples and work towards achieving clear, healthy-looking skin.

1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis tinctures have been used for centuries to help with pimples due to their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

The tincture is made from the flowers of the marigold plant, which contains compounds like triterpenoid saponins, flavonoids, and carotenoids. These constituents help to reduce redness and swelling, fight off bacterial and fungal infections, and promote healing of the skin. To use Calendula officinalis tincture for pimples, you can apply it topically by soaking a cotton swab with a few drops of the tincture and dabbing it directly on the affected area.

You can also take it internally by mixing a few drops of the tincture with water or a carrier oil and drinking it. However, it's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before taking it internally, especially if you're already on any medications or have a sensitive stomach. When you apply Calendula officinalis tincture to your pimples, you may notice a reduction in redness and swelling within a few hours. The tincture may also help to dry out the pimple, reducing its size and visibility.

Over time, you may find that the frequency and severity of your pimples decrease.
