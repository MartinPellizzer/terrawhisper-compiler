Also, avoid Stinging Nettle's (Lapsana communis) decoction, as its sap can cause skin irritation, especially in people with sensitive skin. Similarly, avoid Goldenseal's cousin, Goldthread (Coptis chinensis), which contains similar compounds that can cause skin irritation. Another herb to be cautious of is Sassafras (Sassafras albidum), as its root bark contains safrole, which can cause skin irritation and allergic reactions in some people.

Lastly, avoid Wormwood (Artemisia absinthium) decoction, as its bitter compounds can cause skin irritation, especially in people with sensitive skin.
