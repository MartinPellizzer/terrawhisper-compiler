This can lead to low self-esteem and anxiety, making everyday activities like hanging out with friends or taking selfies more difficult. Dark lips can also be a source of stress and frustration for teenagers, as they may feel like they're constantly trying to cover them up or hide them.

This can be especially true for teenagers who participate in extracurricular activities like sports, theater, or music, where their appearance can be noticed by their peers. This can lead to healthier, smoother-looking lips that are less prone to darkening.
