Inflamed Mouth Care: Causes, Medicinal Herbs, and Natural Remedies for Healing

What are the causes of inflamed mouth?

An inflamed mouth can be caused by several issues.

Oral Thrush is a fungal infection that occurs when yeast overgrows in the mouth, often due to a weakened immune system or the use of antibiotics. This can lead to white patches on the tongue and inside the cheeks. Gingivitis is a mild form of gum disease that causes inflammation of the gums due to poor oral hygiene, such as infrequent brushing and flossing.

It can cause redness and swelling of the gums. Periodontitis is a more severe gum disease that can lead to the breakdown of the bone and tissues that support the teeth, also caused by poor oral hygiene. Mouth Ulcers are small, painful sores that can appear on the inside of the cheeks, lips, or tongue, often due to irritation from food, toothpaste, or dental work.

Canker Sores are similar to mouth ulcers but are usually larger and more painful, often caused by stress, hormonal changes, or a weakened immune system.
