1. Melaleuca alternifolia

Melaleuca alternifolia creams contains bioactive compounds like cymene, limonene, and 1,8-cineole, which have antimicrobial properties that help prevent infection in ingrown toenails.

The anti-inflammatory properties of cymene and limonene reduce redness and swelling, making it easier to manage pain and discomfort. 1,8-cineole also has antiseptic properties that help to clean the affected area and promote healing. The tea tree oil in Melaleuca alternifolia creams has a cooling effect, which reduces inflammation and promotes a healthy environment for the nail to grow.

By reducing inflammation and preventing infection, Melaleuca alternifolia creams can help to alleviate the symptoms of an ingrown toenail.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of water, 1 cup of beeswax, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of Melaleuca alternifolia essential oil.
  2. Melt beeswax and coconut oil in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Add shea butter to the melted beeswax and coconut oil mixture. Stir until fully incorporated.
  4. Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool until it reaches 120°F to 130°F. Add Melaleuca alternifolia essential oil and stir well.
  5. Pour the mixture into a clean, sterilized container and let it cool completely before use. Store in a cool, dry place.

2. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis creams contains triterpenoid saponins, flavonoids, and carotenoids, which are active constituents that help to soothe and heal ingrown toenails.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and redness, making it easier to treat the affected area. The flavonoids in Calendula officinalis also have antimicrobial properties, which help to prevent infection and promote a healthy environment for the nail to grow. Additionally, the saponins in Calendula officinalis have antifungal properties, which can help to combat fungal infections that can contribute to ingrown toenails.

By applying Calendula officinalis creams to the affected area, you can help to reduce pain, promote healing, and prevent further complications.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Calendula flowers, 1 cup of coconut oil, and 1/2 cup of shea butter.
  2. Combine the dried Calendula flowers and coconut oil in a saucepan. Heat on low for 2-3 hours, then let cool.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl. Discard the flowers.
  4. Add the shea butter to the bowl and mix until smooth. Transfer the mixture to a blender or a food processor.
  5. Blend the mixture until it thickens and becomes a creamy consistency. Pour into a jar and store in a cool place.
