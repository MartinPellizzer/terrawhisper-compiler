It also contains antioxidants that protect the skin from damage and promote healing. Calendula has antimicrobial properties, which help to fight off infections that can cause rashes. When applied topically, it creates a barrier on the skin's surface that prevents further irritation and allows the skin to heal. The herb also has anti-allergic properties, which help to calm and soothe the skin, making it less itchy and uncomfortable. This makes calendula a popular natural remedy for skin irritations and rashes.

Symphytum officinale

Symphytum officinale, also known as comfrey, is a plant that has been used for centuries to treat skin problems, including rashes.
