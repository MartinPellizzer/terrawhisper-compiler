1. Melaleuca alternifolia

Melaleuca alternifolia tinctures contains compounds like cineole, limonene, and beta-pinene, which are known for their antimicrobial properties.

These properties help to reduce the growth of bacteria and fungi that can cause ear infections. The antiseptic properties of these compounds also help to soothe and calm irritated skin in the ear canal, reducing itching and discomfort. Additionally, the anti-inflammatory properties of limonene and beta-pinene help to reduce swelling and redness in the ear, promoting a healthy environment for the ear canal.

By applying a few drops of Melaleuca alternifolia tinctures to the affected ear, you can help to alleviate itchy ears and promote a healthy ear canal.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh Melaleuca alternifolia leaves and 2 cups of 80% vodka in a clean glass jar.
  2. Add the Melaleuca alternifolia leaves to the jar and cover with vodka. Make sure the leaves are fully covered.
  3. Store the jar in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day to help the tincture mixture.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.
  5. Bottle the tincture and store it in a cool, dark place. Use 5-7 drops of the tincture in a few drops of warm water and apply it to itchy ears 2-3 times a day.
