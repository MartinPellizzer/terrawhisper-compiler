What herbal essential oils you should avoid if you have itchy ears?

If you have itchy ears, it's best to avoid using essential oils that can irritate the sensitive skin inside your ears.

Sage oil (Salvia officinalis) is another oil to be cautious with, as it can be too strong and may cause skin irritation, especially in the delicate areas of the ears.

Similarly, Geranium oil (Pelargonium graveolens) should be used with care, as it can cause skin irritation in some people, especially when applied to sensitive areas like the ears.

Lastly, Birch oil (Betula alba) should also be avoided, as its salicin content can cause skin irritation and itching in some individuals.
