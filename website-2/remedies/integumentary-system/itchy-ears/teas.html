1. Echinacea purpurea

Echinacea purpurea teas contains bioactive constituents like alkylamides, caffeic acid derivatives, and flavonoids.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce swelling and ease discomfort in itchy ears. Caffeic acid derivatives, in particular, have been shown to inhibit the production of prostaglandins, which contribute to inflammation and itching. The flavonoids present in Echinacea purpurea teas also exhibit antioxidant properties, which can help protect the delicate skin inside the ear from damage and promote healing.

By reducing inflammation and promoting healing, Echinacea purpurea teas may provide relief from itchy ears.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh Echinacea purpurea flowers or 2 tablespoons of dried flowers.
  2. Combine the Echinacea purpurea flowers with 1 cup of boiling water in a tea infuser or a heat-resistant cup.
  3. Steep the mixture for 5-7 minutes, then strain the liquid using a tea infuser or a piece of cheesecloth.
  4. Let the tea cool down to a comfortable temperature for drinking.
  5. Drink 1/2 cup of the Echinacea purpurea tea 2-3 times a day for itchy ears.

2. Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus teas contains essential oils, including eucalyptol, a major bioactive constituent that has anti-inflammatory properties.

The decongestant properties of eucalyptol also help to open up the ear canal, allowing air to flow in and out and promoting a healthy environment. Additionally, the antimicrobial properties of eucalyptol help to combat bacterial and fungal infections that may be causing the itchiness.

By using Eucalyptus globulus teas, you can take advantage of these properties to help soothe and calm itchy ears.

  1. Gather 1 cup of boiling water and 10-15 fresh Eucalyptus globulus leaves.
  2. Chop the Eucalyptus leaves finely and add them to a cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the Eucalyptus leaves and let it steep for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a separate cup using a tea strainer or cheesecloth.
  5. Let the tea cool down and use it as an ear drop by pouring a few drops into your ear to relieve itchiness.
