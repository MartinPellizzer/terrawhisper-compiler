This can affect their performance and overall well-being. In severe cases, ringworm can even spread to other parts of the body, such as the scalp, feet, and nails. When athletes have ringworm, they may need to take time off from their sport, which can be frustrating and demotivating.

They may also worry about getting a medal or recognition if they're unable to compete. Herbal tinctures can help athletes treat ringworm. These tinctures are made from plants that have antifungal properties, which help to kill the fungus that causes the infection. Herbal tinctures can be taken orally or applied topically to the affected area, depending on the specific product and the severity of the infection.
