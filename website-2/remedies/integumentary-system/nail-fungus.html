Natural Treatments for Nail Fungus: Causes and Herbal Remedies

What are the causes of nail fungus?

Nail fungus is caused by several factors.

A fungal infection is the main cause, and it's usually triggered by a type of fungus called dermatophytes. These fungi thrive in warm, moist environments, which is why people who swim frequently or wear tight shoes are more likely to get infected.

Onychomycosis is another term for nail fungus, and it's caused by the same fungal infection. Athlete's foot, a common fungal infection of the foot, can also spread to the nails and cause fungal nail infections. Bacterial infections, such as paronychia, can also contribute to nail fungus.

Paronychia is a bacterial infection of the skin around the nail, and it can lead to inflammation and damage to the nail, creating an environment where fungi can grow.
