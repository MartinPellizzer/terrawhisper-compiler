10 best herbal teas for dry skin

Calendula officinalis tea is made from the flowers of the marigold plant. The plant is rich in antioxidants and has anti-inflammatory properties. The anti-inflammatory properties help to reduce redness and irritation, making the skin feel smoother and more comfortable. Calendula tea also has a moisturizing effect on the skin, helping to lock in moisture and keep the skin hydrated. This is especially helpful for people with dry skin conditions such as eczema or dermatitis. Drinking calendula tea can also help to promote healing and reduce the risk of infection in wounds and skin irritations.
