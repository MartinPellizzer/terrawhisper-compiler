8 herbal oils to soothe fever blister on lip for college students

College life can be stressful, and it's no surprise that many students suffer from fever blisters on their lips.

When a fever blister appears, it can be painful and unsightly, making it difficult for students to focus on their studies. This can lead to feelings of isolation and anxiety, making it harder to manage the stress of college life.

As a result, finding a quick and effective way to treat fever blisters is crucial for college students. While over-the-counter medications can provide some relief, they may not address the underlying cause of the outbreak. This is where herbal essential oils come in - they can help reduce the severity and duration of a fever blister.
