Arnica montana also contains anti-inflammatory properties that help to calm down the body's response to injury, which can cause more pain and swelling. When applied to a bruise, the Arnica montana can help to bring more oxygen to the area, which helps to repair damaged tissues. This can help the bruise to heal faster and look better, with less pain and swelling.
