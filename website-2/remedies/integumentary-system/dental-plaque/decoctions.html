Fixing the issue of dental plaque is important because it can lead to serious health problems, such as tooth loss and heart disease. Dental plaque can also cause bad breath, gum recession, and pain while chewing. By using herbal decoctions to help remove dental plaque, you can reduce your risk of developing these serious health problems.
