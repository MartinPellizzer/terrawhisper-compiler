9 Herbal Remedies for Athletes to Soothe Cracked Heels

Athletes often put a lot of pressure on their feet, which can lead to cracked heels.

Running, jumping, and other high-impact activities can cause the skin on the heels to dry out and crack. This can be painful and make it hard to do everyday activities, like walking or even just standing. For athletes, cracked heels can be a real problem, especially if they have to compete or train on hard surfaces. It can also be a distraction, making it hard to focus on their sport. Some athletes may even need to take time off to let their feet heal, which can be frustrating and affect their performance. Cracked heels can also be a sign of other issues, like dry skin or a lack of moisture in the skin.

Athletes who live in dry climates or have to train in cold weather may be more likely to get cracked heels. They may also be more prone to other foot problems, like blisters or ingrown toenails. Taking care of their feet is essential for athletes, and that includes treating cracked heels. Herbal tinctures can be a helpful way for athletes to treat cracked heels. These tinctures are made from plant extracts and can be applied to the skin to help lock in moisture and soothe dry, cracked skin. They can also help to promote healing and reduce pain.

By using herbal tinctures, athletes can help to keep their skin healthy and prevent further cracking.

1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis tinctures are a natural remedy to help soothe and heal cracked heels.

The tincture is made from the flowers of the marigold plant, which contains properties like flavonoids, carotenoids, and triterpenoids. These constituents have anti-inflammatory, antimicrobial, and antioxidant effects, making them perfect to combat cracked heels. The tincture works by promoting tissue repair, reducing inflammation, and preventing infection, which are all essential steps to heal cracked heels. To use Calendula officinalis tinctures for cracked heels, simply apply a few drops of the tincture to the affected area, ideally after a warm bath or shower when the skin is soft. Gently massage the area to help the tincture penetrate deeper into the skin.

You can also add a few drops of the tincture to your foot cream or lotion to enhance its healing properties. For best results, repeat the application twice a day, ideally before bedtime, so the tincture can work while you sleep. After using Calendula officinalis tinctures for cracked heels, you can expect to see improvements in the appearance and texture of your skin. The tincture will help to reduce the size and depth of the cracks, making them less painful and less noticeable. The skin will also become softer and more supple, reducing the risk of further cracking.

With regular use, you can also expect to see a decrease in the redness and inflammation associated with cracked heels.
