9 Herbal Tinctures For Inflamed Gums

Smokers often suffer from inflamed gums, which can be painful and affect their daily lives.

This is because smoking reduces blood flow to the gums, making them more susceptible to infection and inflammation. When the gums become inflamed, they can become tender, swollen, and even bleed easily. This can make eating, drinking, and even speaking uncomfortable. In severe cases, inflamed gums can lead to tooth loss, which can have a significant impact on a person's self-esteem and quality of life. Smokers with inflamed gums may also experience bad breath, a metallic taste in their mouth, and a general feeling of discomfort.

To make matters worse, inflamed gums can be a sign of a more serious health issue, such as gum disease or heart disease. Smokers with inflamed gums often turn to conventional treatments like antibiotics or painkillers, but these can have side effects and may not address the underlying cause of the problem. This is where herbal tinctures come in. Herbal tinctures are liquid extracts made from plants that have anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties. They can help to reduce inflammation, fight infection, and promote healing in the gums. By applying a few drops of the tincture to the affected area, smokers can start to feel relief from their symptoms.

The tinctures work by stimulating blood flow to the gums, reducing pain and swelling, and promoting a healthy balance of bacteria in the mouth.
