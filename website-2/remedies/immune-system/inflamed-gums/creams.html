1. Aloe barbadensis

Aloe barbadensis creams contains a high amount of aloin and aloe-emodin, which are powerful anti-inflammatory compounds.

These components help reduce swelling and soothe irritated tissue, making Aloe barbadensis creams effective for inflamed gums. Aloe-emodin also has antibacterial properties, which can help prevent infection and promote healing in inflamed gum tissue. Aloin and other bioactive compounds in Aloe barbadensis creams also possess antioxidant properties, which can help protect gums from damage caused by free radicals.

By reducing inflammation and preventing infection, Aloe barbadensis creams can help promote healthy gum tissue and alleviate inflamed gums.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of distilled water, 1/4 cup of aloe vera gel, 2 tablespoons of coconut oil, 2 tablespoons of beeswax, 10 drops of tea tree oil, and 10 drops of peppermint oil.
  2. In a small saucepan, heat the distilled water over low heat. Add the aloe vera gel and stir until dissolved.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix the coconut oil and beeswax. Heat the mixture in the microwave for 10-second intervals, stirring between each interval, until melted.
  4. Combine the aloe vera mixture and the coconut oil mixture. Stir in the tea tree oil and peppermint oil.
  5. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool. Once cooled and thickened, your aloe barbadensis cream is ready to use. Store it in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.
