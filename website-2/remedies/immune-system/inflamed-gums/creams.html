11 Herbal Creams to Ease Inflamed Gums for Smokers

Smokers often suffer from inflamed gums, also known as gum recession or gum disease.

This happens because smoking reduces blood flow to the gums, making it harder for them to heal and fight off infection. As a result, smokers may experience pain, bleeding, and bad breath. Inflamed gums can also lead to tooth loss, which affects not only a person's self-esteem but also their ability to eat and speak properly. Smoking can also damage the bone that supports the teeth, making it harder to chew and digest food.

This can lead to digestive problems, weight loss, and a lower quality of life. When inflamed gums go untreated, they can cause more serious health issues, such as heart disease and diabetes. Smokers who experience inflamed gums often struggle to find effective treatments, but one option is using herbal creams. These creams work by reducing inflammation and promoting healing in the gums. They contain natural ingredients that are absorbed quickly by the skin, providing relief from pain and discomfort.

By applying these creams directly to the affected area, smokers can reduce their symptoms and promote healthy gums.

1. Calendula officinalis

Calendula officinalis creams are a natural remedy that can help soothe inflamed gums.

The creams contain triterpenoid saponins and carotenoids, which have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. These compounds help reduce swelling, fight off infection, and promote wound healing. When applied to inflamed gums, Calendula officinalis creams can ease discomfort, pain, and inflammation. To use Calendula officinalis creams for inflamed gums, apply a thin layer to the affected area with a clean finger or cotton swab. Gently massage the cream into the gums for a few seconds.

Repeat the application 2-3 times a day, ideally after brushing and flossing teeth. For best results, use the creams in conjunction with good oral hygiene practices, such as regular dental check-ups and a balanced diet. When you start using Calendula officinalis creams, you may notice a reduction in gum inflammation and swelling within a few days. The pain and discomfort associated with inflamed gums may also decrease. As the cream continues to work, you may see an improvement in the overall health of your gums, which can help prevent further problems. However, it's essential to note that Calendula officinalis creams are not a replacement for medical treatment.

If you have persistent or severe gum inflammation, consult your dentist for proper diagnosis and treatment.
