8 Herbal Teas For Inflamed Gums

As we age, our gums can become inflamed, a condition known as gingivitis.

This happens because our teeth and gums are more sensitive, and we may not be able to clean them as well as we used to. Inflamed gums can cause pain, bleeding, and discomfort when eating or speaking, making everyday activities difficult for older adults. It can also lead to bad breath, which can be embarrassing and affect self-esteem. In some cases, inflamed gums can even be a sign of a more serious health issue, such as diabetes or heart disease. Older adults may also have to deal with other health issues, such as arthritis, which can make it harder to clean their teeth and gums properly.

This can create a cycle of pain and discomfort that can be hard to break. When we have inflamed gums, we need to find ways to soothe and calm the area. Herbal teas have been used for centuries to help with this. These teas contain special properties that can reduce inflammation and promote healing in the gums. They can also help to reduce pain and discomfort, making it easier to eat and speak. Some herbal teas may even have antibacterial properties, which can help to prevent infections and promote healthy gums.

Drinking herbal teas can be a gentle and natural way to treat inflamed gums, and it can be especially helpful for older adults who may not be able to use harsh chemicals or undergo invasive treatments.
