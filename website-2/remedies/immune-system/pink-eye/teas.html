Teachers may also be more prone to getting pink eye due to their busy schedules, lack of sleep, and exposure to germs in the classroom. The symptoms of pink eye can be quite miserable, and if left untreated, it can lead to complications like vision problems or even permanent damage to the eye. By incorporating herbal teas into their treatment plan, teachers may be able to recover faster and get back to their normal routine.
