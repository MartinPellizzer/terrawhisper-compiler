1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale tinctures contains a high concentration of gingerols and shogaols, which are the bioactive constituents responsible for its medicinal properties.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which can help reduce swelling and promote healing in the spleen. The warm and moistening properties of Zingiber officinale tinctures can help break down adhesions and congestion in the spleen, allowing it to return to its normal size and function. Additionally, the tincture's expectorant properties can help clear out mucus and fluid buildup in the spleen, further contributing to its reduction in size.

By promoting healthy spleen function and reducing inflammation, Zingiber officinale tinctures can be a useful natural remedy for an enlarged spleen.

  1. Gather 250ml of vodka, 1 cup of sliced fresh Zingiber officinale (ginger root), and a clean glass jar.
  2. Combine the sliced ginger root and vodka in the glass jar. Make sure the ginger is covered by the vodka.
  3. Seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks. Shake the jar every day to help the extraction.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.
  5. Transfer the tincture to a dark glass bottle and store it in a cool, dark place. Use 20-30 drops of the tincture 2-3 times a day as needed to help with an enlarged spleen.
