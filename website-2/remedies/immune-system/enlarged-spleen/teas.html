People with lymphoma often experience swollen spleens, which can be a real challenge.

The spleen filters the blood and stores red blood cells, but when lymphoma grows, it can put pressure on the spleen, causing it to swell. It's not just the physical discomfort that's a problem, but also the emotional toll - feeling anxious, stressed, and worried about your health. When the spleen is enlarged, it can also affect the way the body fights infections, making lymphoma patients more susceptible to illness.

As the disease progresses, the enlarged spleen can rupture, which is a serious and potentially life-threatening complication. This is why it's essential to manage symptoms and address the spleen's swelling. Unfortunately, conventional treatments, like chemotherapy, may not always be effective in reducing spleen size. In addition to conventional treatments, some people turn to herbal teas to help alleviate their symptoms. These teas are made from plants that have been used for centuries to promote healing and ease discomfort. They work by reducing inflammation, improving circulation, and supporting the body's natural healing processes.

By incorporating herbal teas into their daily routine, some lymphoma patients have found relief from their enlarged spleen, which in turn improves their quality of life.
