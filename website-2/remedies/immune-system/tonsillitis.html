Tonsillitis: Causes, Herbal Remedies, and Homeopathic Preparations

What are the causes of tonsillitis?

Tonsillitis is an inflammation of the tonsils, and it can be caused by two main types of infections: viral and bacterial.

A viral infection, such as the adenovirus or coronavirus, can cause tonsillitis by attacking the tonsils and making them swell. Adenovirus is a common virus that causes tonsillitis, especially in children, while coronavirus is more commonly associated with respiratory infections like the common cold and COVID-19.

On the other hand, a bacterial infection, often caused by Streptococcus pyogenes, can lead to tonsillitis. Streptococcus pyogenes is a type of bacteria that can cause a range of infections, from mild to severe, and can spread quickly from person to person.

When this bacteria infects the tonsils, it can cause severe symptoms like a high fever, sore throat, and difficulty swallowing.
