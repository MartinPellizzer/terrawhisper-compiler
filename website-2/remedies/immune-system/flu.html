What are the causes of flu?

There are several viruses that can cause the flu.

One of the main causes is Influenza A, a highly contagious virus that can spread quickly from person to person. It's known for causing severe illness and is often associated with pandemics. Influenza B is another common cause of the flu, which can cause mild to moderate illness.

It's less severe than Influenza A but still contagious. Influenza C is a mild virus that usually causes a mild illness, but it's not as contagious as Influenza A and B.
