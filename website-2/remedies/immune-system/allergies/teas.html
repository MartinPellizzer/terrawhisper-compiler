10 Herbal Teas For Allergies

Young children can suffer from allergies, which is when their body doesn't like something in the air, like pollen, dust, or pet dander.

This can cause them to feel unwell, with symptoms like a runny nose, itchy eyes, and a sore throat. Allergies can be a real nuisance for kids, making it hard for them to play outside, go to school, or even sleep at night. Sometimes, allergies can even lead to more serious issues like asthma, which is a long-term condition that makes it hard to breathe. When kids have allergies, they might need to use medicine to feel better, but it's not always easy to get them to take their meds.

Their parents might try to avoid things that trigger their allergies, like taking them to parks on days when the pollen count is high. They might also try to keep their home clean and dust-free, but it's not always easy to keep all the allergens out. Some kids might even have to carry an EpiPen with them at all times, which can be scary for both the child and the parent. Herbal teas can be a helpful addition to a child's allergy treatment plan. These teas are made from plants and flowers that have been used for centuries to soothe and calm the body.

When a child drinks a cup of herbal tea, the plant compounds can help to reduce inflammation and calm their immune system, making it easier for them to breathe and feel better.

1. Echinacea purpurea

If you're looking to try Echinacea purpurea teas for allergies, you can start by making a warm tea with dried flowers and leaves. FAQ

Can drinking herbal tea prevent allergies from forming?

Drinking herbal tea may help reduce allergy symptoms, but it's unlikely to prevent allergies from forming.

Some herbal teas, like peppermint and chamomile, have anti-inflammatory properties that can ease congestion and itching.

However, allergies are a complex condition caused by immune system reactions, which herbal teas can't directly address.

Is it safe to consume herbal teas for allergies every day?

Herbal teas for allergies can be a good choice, but it's best to use them in moderation.

Drinking them every day may not be safe for some people. Some herbs can interact with medications or have side effects, especially if you have other health conditions.

It's essential to be mindful of how your body reacts.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in allergies?

Herbal teas can help with allergies, but it might take some time to notice results.

If you're trying to relieve congestion, you might feel better after a few hours. For skin issues like hives or itching, it can take a few days to a week to see improvements.

It's different for everyone.

What time of day is best to drink herbal tea for allergies?

Drinking herbal tea for allergies can be most effective in the morning. This is because it can help clear congestion and boost your immune system, preparing you for the day ahead.
