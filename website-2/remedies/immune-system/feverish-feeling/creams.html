1. Echinacea purpurea

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of Echinacea purpurea flowers, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of shea butter, and 2 teaspoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Dry the Echinacea flowers in a low-temperature oven (150°F) for 2 hours or air-dry them for 24 hours.
  3. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl in a pot of simmering water.
  4. Add the dried Echinacea flowers, shea butter, and vitamin E oil to the melted coconut oil mixture. Stir well to combine.
  5. Pour the mixture into a clean container and let it cool and solidify at room temperature. Store the cream in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.
