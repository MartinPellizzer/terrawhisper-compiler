What Herbs To Avoid If You Have Feverish Feeling?

If you have a feverish feeling, it's best to avoid certain herbs that can make it worse.

One such herb is Goldenseal (Hydrastis canadensis), which contains a compound called berberine. This compound can raise blood pressure and heart rate, making fever worse.

Another herb to avoid is Yarrow (Achillea millefolium), which can stimulate the body and increase sweating, but it can also increase heart rate and blood pressure. Willow bark (Salix alba) contains salicin, a compound similar to aspirin, which can thin the blood and make bleeding worse in people with fever. Also, Sage (Salvia officinalis) can increase heart rate and blood pressure, making fever worse.

Finally, Black cohosh (Actaea racemosa) can interact with certain medications, including blood thinners, which can be a problem for people with fever who may be taking medication.
