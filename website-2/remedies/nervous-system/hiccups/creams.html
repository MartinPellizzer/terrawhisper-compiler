Many elderly people suffer from hiccups, which can be caused by various factors such as eating too quickly, eating too much, or having a medical condition like a lung disease or a neurological disorder.

The elderly may be more prone to hiccups due to age-related changes in their bodies, such as a slower digestion rate, reduced muscle tone, and decreased nerve function. Herbal creams have been found to be effective in helping elderly individuals treat hiccups. These creams typically contain natural ingredients that soothe the nerves and muscles in the chest area, where hiccups are triggered.
