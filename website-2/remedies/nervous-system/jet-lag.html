Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis, also known as valerian root, is a natural herb that can help with jet lag. This is because it regulates the body's internal clock, which can be affected when we travel across different time zones. Valerian root contains chemicals that calm the nervous system and promote relaxation, helping us to sleep better. When we travel, our body's internal clock is confused by the new time zone, making it hard to fall asleep or stay asleep. Valerian root helps to reduce stress and anxiety that can come with jet lag, allowing us to adjust to the new time zone more easily. It also has a sedative effect, making it easier to fall asleep and get a good night's rest. By taking valerian root supplements or drinking valerian tea before bedtime, we can help our body adjust to the new time zone and reduce the symptoms of jet lag.
