8 Herbal Essential Oils for Older Adults to Shake Off Chills

As people age, their bodies go through natural changes that can affect their ability to regulate temperature.

Older adults may experience chills due to a decrease in muscle mass, a reduction in the body's ability to produce heat, or underlying health conditions such as anemia or hypothyroidism. When older adults get chills, it can be a sign of a more serious issue, and it can also be uncomfortable and disrupt their daily lives. Chills can make it difficult to sleep, cause fatigue, and even lead to falls or other accidents. Older adults may also experience anxiety or depression due to the discomfort and uncertainty of not knowing what's causing their chills. Chills can be especially problematic for older adults who may already have difficulty managing their daily needs, such as bathing, dressing, or taking medication.

When they're feeling chilled, it can be hard for them to get out of bed, get dressed, or participate in activities they enjoy. This can lead to feelings of isolation and frustration. In some cases, chills can be a symptom of an underlying infection, such as pneumonia or urinary tract infection, which requires medical attention. Herbal essential oils can be a helpful addition to the treatment of chills in older adults. Certain oils have natural warming properties that can help increase circulation and relax the body, making it easier to manage chills.

These oils can be inhaled, applied topically, or added to a warm bath, providing relief from discomfort and promoting relaxation.

1. Eucalyptus globulus

Eucalyptus globulus essential oils contain compounds like eucalyptol and alpha-pinene that have natural decongestant and anti-inflammatory properties.

These properties can help to ease chills by reducing mucus in the body and relieving congestion in the lungs. When you inhale the oil, the eucalyptol can help to open up airways, making it easier to breathe. To use the oil to help with chills, you can add a few drops to a diffuser or inhale it directly from a cloth or handkerchief.

You can also mix a few drops of the oil with a carrier oil like coconut oil and apply it to your chest or back. When you use Eucalyptus globulus essential oils to help with chills, you may start to feel relief from congestion and a reduction in body temperature. Your breathing may become easier, and you may feel more comfortable.

You may also find that your cough subsides and your energy levels increase.
