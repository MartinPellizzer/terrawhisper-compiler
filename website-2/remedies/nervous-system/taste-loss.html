Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale, also known as ginger, is a plant with many benefits for people who have lost their sense of taste. This is because ginger has anti-inflammatory properties that help to reduce swelling in the mouth and throat. When we lose our sense of taste, it's often because of a condition called oral mucositis, which is swelling and inflammation of the mucous membranes in the mouth. Ginger can help to reduce this swelling, allowing the taste buds on our tongues to function properly again. Additionally, ginger contains compounds that help to clear out the bacteria and viruses that can cause inflammation in the mouth. By reducing inflammation and clearing out bacteria and viruses, ginger can help to restore our sense of taste and get our appetite back on track.
