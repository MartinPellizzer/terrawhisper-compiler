1. Zingiber Officinale

Zingiber officinale tea, also known as ginger tea, is a popular remedy for hangover. The reason for its effectiveness lies in its ability to calm the stomach and alleviate nausea. It also helps to break down and absorb the toxins and waste products caused by alcohol consumption. Furthermore, ginger has natural pain-relieving properties, which can help to ease headaches and body aches commonly associated with hangovers. Additionally, ginger tea has been shown to have antioxidant properties, which can help to neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress in the body caused by excessive drinking. This makes Zingiber officinale tea a valuable natural remedy for people looking to alleviate hangover symptoms.

Drinking Ginkgo biloba tea after a night of heavy drinking may help alleviate some of the unpleasant symptoms associated with hangovers.
