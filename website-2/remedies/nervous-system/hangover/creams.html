7 Herbal Creams to Help Social Drinkers Overcome Hangover

Social drinkers often wake up feeling miserable after a night of partying.

Drinking too much alcohol can dehydrate the body, disrupt the balance of electrolytes, and affect the liver's ability to process toxins. This can lead to headaches, nausea, fatigue, and sensitivity to light and sound. For some people, hangovers can be so severe that they're unable to work, go to school, or even take care of themselves. They might miss important events, struggle with relationships, and feel embarrassed about their condition.

Hangovers can also lead to anxiety and depression in some individuals, making it difficult to enjoy social events without fear of getting hungover. To make matters worse, many hangover remedies available in the market contain harsh chemicals or caffeine, which can lead to more problems. But, some herbal creams have emerged as a potential solution to help alleviate hangover symptoms. These creams typically work by soothing the skin, reducing inflammation, and replenishing the body's natural moisture balance. They often contain compounds that help to relax muscles, ease headaches, and calm the nervous system.

By applying these creams to the skin, social drinkers may find some relief from their hangover symptoms, allowing them to recover faster and feel more comfortable.

1. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba creams have become a popular remedy for hangover relief.

The key to their effectiveness lies in their unique blend of flavonoids, terpenoids, and bilobalide, which work together to combat hangover symptoms. Ginkgo biloba's active constituents, such as quercetin and kaempferol, have potent antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the body, common causes of hangover discomfort.

When applying a Ginkgo biloba cream to the skin, it's essential to massage it gently into the skin, focusing on areas prone to hangover pain, such as the temples and neck. It's recommended to apply the cream 30 minutes before bedtime, allowing it to absorb into the skin while you sleep. As the cream takes effect, you may start to feel a reduction in headache and nausea, as well as improved mood and reduced fatigue.

The likely outcome of using Ginkgo biloba cream is a more comfortable and refreshed feeling the next morning, allowing you to bounce back from a night out more quickly and easily.

2. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale creams, also known as ginger creams, can help alleviate hangover symptoms due to its anti-inflammatory properties and constituents.

The main active compounds in ginger, such as gingerols and shogaols, have been shown to reduce nausea and vomiting, which are common hangover complaints. These compounds also help to ease stomach discomfort and reduce inflammation in the digestive system. When experiencing hangover symptoms, try applying a thin layer of ginger cream to your skin, usually on the wrists or temples. Gently massage the cream into your skin, allowing the active ingredients to be absorbed.

You can also take a warm bath with ginger essential oil to help relax your muscles and calm your digestive system. As the ginger cream is absorbed into your skin, you can expect to feel a decrease in nausea and stomach discomfort. The anti-inflammatory properties may also help to reduce headache and body aches associated with hangovers. Some people may also experience a sense of calm and relaxation as the ginger cream works to soothe the digestive system.

However, the effectiveness of ginger cream for hangovers may vary from person to person, and individual results may differ.

3. Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis creams may help with hangover due to its properties and constituents.

The cream contains valerenic acid, isovalerenic acid, and valepotriates, which have a sedative effect on the nervous system. These compounds help to calm the body and mind, reducing the severity of hangover symptoms such as headaches and nausea. Valeriana officinalis also has anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to reduce swelling and pain in the body. To use Valeriana officinalis cream for hangover, apply a thin layer to the affected area (such as the forehead or neck) and gently massage it in. You can also take a warm bath with Valeriana officinalis added to the water, or use it as a compress on the affected area.

Start with a small amount and gradually increase as needed. It is recommended to use the cream within 24 hours after the hangover symptoms appear, for best results. Taking action with Valeriana officinalis cream may lead to a reduction in hangover symptoms, such as headaches, nausea, and fatigue. You may also experience improved sleep quality and reduced anxiety and stress. Additionally, the anti-inflammatory properties of the cream may help to reduce swelling and pain, making it easier to move and perform daily activities.

It is essential to note that Valeriana officinalis cream is not a cure for hangover, but rather a complementary treatment to help alleviate symptoms.

If you're looking to try Passiflora incarnata creams for hangover relief, here's what you can do: apply a small amount of cream to your skin, ideally to your temples, neck, and shoulders, where the muscles tend to be tense and sore after a night of drinking. When using Passiflora incarnata creams for hangover relief, you can expect to experience a range of benefits. You may also experience a sense of relaxation and reduced anxiety, which can be particularly helpful if you're prone to hangover-related anxiety or stress. Some people may also notice improved sleep quality and reduced muscle tension after using the cream.

However, it's essential to note that individual results may vary, and some people may not experience the same level of relief as others.

Mentha x piperita creams, also known as peppermint creams, may help alleviate hangover symptoms due to their soothing and anti-inflammatory properties.

The main constituents of peppermint, including menthol and menthone, work to ease stomach discomfort and nausea by relaxing the muscles in the digestive tract. When applied topically in cream form, mentha x piperita can also help reduce headaches and migraines by relieving tension and promoting blood flow. To use peppermint creams for hangover relief, apply a small amount to your wrists, temples, or anywhere else you feel tension.

When using peppermint creams for hangover relief, you may notice a decrease in nausea and stomach discomfort within 15-30 minutes. Within 30 minutes to an hour, you may start to feel a sense of calm and relief from your hangover symptoms. Your headache and fever may subside, and you may experience reduced nausea and fatigue.

However, keep in mind that Artemisia absinthium creams are not a cure for hangover, and it's still important to drink responsibly and pace yourself when consuming alcohol.
