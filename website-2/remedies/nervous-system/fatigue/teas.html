Another herb to be cautious with is Coleus (Solenostemon scutellarioides), as it can cause insomnia and restlessness, making it harder to recover from fatigue.

Lastly, teas containing Ephedra sinica's close relative, Echinophora sibthorpiana, should also be avoided.
