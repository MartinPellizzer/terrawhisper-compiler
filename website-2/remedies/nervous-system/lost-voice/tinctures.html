1. Echinacea purpurea

Echinacea purpurea tinctures contains compounds like alkylamides and caffeic acid derivatives.

These constituents have anti-inflammatory properties, which help reduce swelling in the throat and vocal cords. The tannins in Echinacea purpurea also have astringent properties, which can help tighten up the tissues in the throat and promote healing. Additionally, the immunomodulatory effects of Echinacea's polysaccharides can support the body's natural defense mechanisms and help fight off infections that may be causing the lost voice.

By reducing inflammation and supporting the immune system, Echinacea purpurea tinctures may help alleviate symptoms of a lost voice.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Echinacea purpurea flowers and 2 cups of 80% vodka in a clean glass jar.
  2. Combine the Echinacea flowers and vodka in the jar. Make sure the flowers are fully covered by the vodka.
  3. Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily.
  4. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids.
  5. Label the jar and store it in a cool, dark place. Take 20-30 drops of the tincture, 2-3 times a day, as needed to help soothe a lost voice.
