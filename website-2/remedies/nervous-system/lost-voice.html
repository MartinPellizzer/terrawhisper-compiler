Causes and Remedies for the Lost Voice

Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra, also known as licorice root, is a natural remedy that has been used for centuries to soothe a lost voice. The root contains a compound called glycyrrhizin, which has anti-inflammatory properties. When you have a lost voice, your vocal cords are often inflamed and sore, making it hard to speak. Glycyrrhizin in licorice root helps to reduce this inflammation and soothe the vocal cords. It also has a protective effect on the mucous membranes in the throat, preventing further irritation and damage. Drinking licorice root tea or taking licorice root supplements can help to calm the throat and promote healing, allowing your voice to recover faster. Additionally, licorice root has been shown to have antimicrobial properties, which can help to fight off infections that may be causing the lost voice. As a result, using licorice root as a natural remedy can help to alleviate the symptoms of a lost voice and promote recovery.

Drinking ginger tea or eating ginger can help to bring back your voice quickly.
