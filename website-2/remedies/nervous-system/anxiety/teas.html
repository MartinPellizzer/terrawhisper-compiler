10 best herbal teas for anxiety

1. Valeriana Officinalis

Valeriana officinalis tea, also known as valerian root tea, is a natural remedy that has been used for centuries to help with anxiety. The tea is made from the root of the valerian plant, which contains chemicals called valerenic acid and isovaleric acid. These chemicals have a calming effect on the body and help to reduce stress and anxiety. When you drink valerian root tea, the chemicals are absorbed into your body and work to slow down your heart rate and lower your blood pressure. This helps to calm your nervous system and reduce feelings of anxiety. Valerian root tea also helps to improve the quality of your sleep, which is often disrupted by anxiety. By promoting a good night's sleep, the tea helps to reduce anxiety and leave you feeling more relaxed and calm. Additionally, valerian root tea has a sedative effect, which can help to quiet your mind and reduce worries.

One of the main chemicals in passionflower is a compound called flavonoid, which has a calming effect on the nervous system. It helps to slow down the heart rate and lower blood pressure, making it easier to breathe and feel calm. Passionflower tea also contains a chemical called harmine, which is a natural sedative that helps to quiet the mind and reduce anxiety. When consumed as a tea, passionflower helps to reduce symptoms of anxiety, such as rapid heartbeat, trembling, and feelings of panic. By promoting relaxation and reducing anxiety, passionflower tea can be a valuable tool for people who struggle with anxiety and stress.

10. Matricaria Chamomilla

Matricaria chamomilla tea, also known as chamomile tea, is a popular natural remedy for anxiety.
