1. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba tinctures contains flavonoids and terpenoids, which are active constituents that help with numbness in hands.

Flavonoids, such as quercetin and kaempferol, have anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and improve blood flow to the hands. Terpenoids, including ginkgolides and bilobalide, increase nitric oxide production, which widens blood vessels and enhances circulation. This increased circulation helps to deliver oxygen and nutrients to the hands, reducing numbness and discomfort.

By improving blood flow and reducing inflammation, Ginkgo biloba tinctures can help alleviate numbness in the hands.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Ginkgo biloba leaves and 2 cups of 80-proof vodka in a clean glass container.
  2. Combine the Ginkgo biloba leaves and vodka in the container. Stir well to mix.
  3. Steep the mixture in a cool, dark place for 2 weeks, shaking the container daily.
  4. After 2 weeks, strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into another clean container.
  5. Store the Ginkgo biloba tincture in a dark glass bottle with a dropper lid. Take 20 drops, 3 times a day, as needed for numbness in hands.
