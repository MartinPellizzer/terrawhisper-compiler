Devil's claw has been used for centuries to treat pain and inflammation and can help reduce migraine frequency. Herbs like lavender and peppermint can also help alleviate migraine symptoms. Lavender has a calming effect on the nervous system and can promote relaxation, while peppermint can help ease nausea and vomiting.

These herbs can be consumed as tea, taken as supplements, or applied topically to relieve pain and inflammation.
