What herbal decoctions you should avoid if you have eye strain?

If you have eye strain, it's best to avoid herbal decoctions that can cause eye problems or make them worse.

Additionally, 'Silybum marianum' (Milk thistle) may interact with certain medications and worsen eye strain, although it is primarily used for liver health.

'Vaccinium macrocarpon' (Cranberry) should also be avoided, as its high tannin content may cause eye irritation.
