What herbal teas you should avoid if you have eye strain?

If you have eye strain, it's best to avoid some herbal teas that can make your eyes worse.

One of these teas is Sage (Salvia officinalis). Sage can dry out your eyes and make them feel worse, which is the opposite of what you want when you have eye strain. Another tea to avoid is Peppermint (Mentha piperita).

While peppermint can help with headaches, it can also irritate your eyes and make your eye strain worse. Similarly, Eucalyptus (Eucalyptus globulus) tea can cause eye irritation and dryness, which can make your eye strain more uncomfortable. Finally, Yerba Mate (Ilex paraguariensis) tea can also cause eye irritation and dryness, especially if you already have dry eyes.

It's best to avoid these teas and stick to ones that are gentle on your eyes, like Chamomile (Matricaria chamomilla) or Hibiscus (Hibiscus sabdariffa) tea.
