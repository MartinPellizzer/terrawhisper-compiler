Furthermore, skullcap (Scutellaria lateriflora) can cause drowsiness and lightheadedness in some individuals, which may worsen dizziness. It's also essential to be cautious with tinctures made from black cohosh (Actaea racemosa) and goldenseal (Hydrastis canadensis), as they can cause dizziness and nausea in some people.
