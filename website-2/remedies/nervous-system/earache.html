Earache Treatment with Natural Medicinal Herbs and Herbal Remedies

What are the causes of earache?

Earaches can be caused by several issues.

A middle ear infection, also known as otitis media, happens when bacteria or viruses enter the middle ear and cause inflammation. This can be due to a cold or flu, which weaken the immune system and make it easier for germs to get in. Earwax buildup occurs when old earwax doesn't come out of the ear canal properly, blocking the ear and causing pain. This can happen when using Q-tips to clean the ears, which can push the wax further into the ear.

Swimmer's ear, or otitis externa, is an infection of the outer ear and ear canal. It's usually caused by bacterial growth when water gets trapped in the ear, often after swimming or taking a shower. A tooth abscess can refer pain to the ear, as the nerves in the face are connected. This type of pain is usually a sharp, stabbing sensation.

Sinus pressure occurs when the sinuses become infected or inflamed, which can cause pain in the ears due to the close connection between the sinuses and the ears.
