Jet Lag in Shift Workers: 9 Herbal Creams for a Quick Fix

Shift workers, like those who work at night or have rotating schedules, can suffer from jet lag because their bodies are constantly adjusting to new times.

This can make it hard for them to sleep, feel tired during the day, and have trouble concentrating. As a result, their work and personal lives can be affected, leading to decreased productivity, mood swings, and even health problems. Shift workers often have to adjust to new time zones when traveling, which can make it even harder for their bodies to adjust. This can lead to feelings of exhaustion, disorientation, and irritability.

When shift workers experience jet lag, their bodies are not getting the rest they need, which can disrupt their natural sleep-wake cycles. This can cause problems with their appetite, energy levels, and ability to focus. They may also experience headaches, stomach problems, and other physical symptoms. Herbal creams can help shift workers treat jet lag by providing a natural and gentle way to support their bodies. These creams often contain plant extracts that have been shown to promote relaxation, reduce stress, and improve sleep quality.

They can be applied to the skin before bed to help the body relax and prepare for sleep.

1. Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis creams have been used to help alleviate symptoms of jet lag due to their soothing and calming properties.

The key constituent valerenic acid has been found to have a sedative effect on the nervous system, which can help regulate the body's internal clock and reduce symptoms of fatigue and insomnia. Additionally, the herb valerian has been traditionally used to treat restlessness and anxiety, making it a suitable remedy for people experiencing stress and unease due to travel. To use Valeriana officinalis creams for jet lag, apply a small amount to the skin before bedtime, focusing on areas like the wrists, temples, and behind the ears.

Gently massage the cream into the skin until fully absorbed. Some people also find it helpful to take a warm bath or shower with a few drops of valerian essential oil added to the water to promote relaxation. By using Valeriana officinalis creams and following a consistent sleep schedule, you may be able to reduce the severity of jet lag symptoms. You may experience improved sleep quality, reduced fatigue, and a more stable mood.

However, it's essential to note that Valeriana officinalis creams may not completely eliminate jet lag, but they can help alleviate some of its symptoms.

Take a warm bath or shower to help the cream penetrate deeper into your skin. As you sleep, the cream works to regulate your body's internal clock, making it easier to adjust to the new time zone.

In the morning, you may feel more refreshed and alert, with reduced jet lag symptoms.
