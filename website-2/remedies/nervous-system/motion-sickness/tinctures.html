9 Herbal Tinctures For Motion Sickness

Sailors often have to deal with rough seas, which can cause their bodies to feel queasy and disoriented.

This is because their inner ear, which is responsible for balance, sends conflicting signals to their brain. As a result, they may feel like they're going to be sick, and it can be hard for them to focus on their work. Motion sickness can be a real problem for sailors, especially on long voyages. It can make them feel weak, dizzy, and nauseous, and it can even lead to vomiting. This can be not only uncomfortable but also a safety issue, as a sailor who's feeling queasy may not be able to perform their duties properly.

For example, a sailor who's feeling sick may not be able to navigate the ship or handle important equipment. In severe cases, motion sickness can even lead to dehydration and other health problems. Herbal tinctures have been used for centuries to help treat motion sickness in sailors. These tinctures are made from plants that have natural anti-inflammatory and antacid properties, which can help to calm the stomach and reduce nausea. When taken as directed, these tinctures can help to alleviate symptoms of motion sickness, such as dizziness and vomiting. They can also help to reduce stress and anxiety, which can exacerbate motion sickness.

Some sailors find that taking herbal tinctures before a long voyage or during periods of rough seas can help them to feel more comfortable and in control.
