Valerian tea also reduces stress and anxiety, making it easier to wind down and prepare for bed. Drinking valerian tea before bedtime can also help you fall into a deeper sleep, making it harder to wake up during the night. Many people find that valerian tea helps them sleep more consistently and wake up feeling more rested.
