What Are The Main Causes Of Stress?

Stress is a feeling of being overwhelmed or anxious.

The main causes of stress are work, health, and death. Work is a major cause of stress. It can be due to long working hours, heavy workload, lack of job security, and conflicts with colleagues or managers. Many people struggle to balance their work and personal life, leading to feelings of burnout and exhaustion. They may also worry about meeting deadlines, achieving targets, or being promoted. Health is another significant cause of stress.

This can be due to serious illnesses, chronic conditions, or even minor health issues. People may worry about their health, the impact of their condition on their daily life, and the cost of medical treatment. They may also feel anxious about going to the doctor, undergoing tests, or facing a diagnosis. Death, either of oneself or a loved one, can be a major source of stress. This can be due to a terminal illness, a serious accident, or even a natural disaster. People may worry about their own mortality, the well-being of their family members, and the impact of a loss on their daily life.

They may also feel anxious about funeral arrangements, grieving, and adjusting to a new life without the deceased person.

Below are the main causes of stress.

  • Work: Work can be a cause of stress because people often feel overwhelmed by heavy responsibilities, tight deadlines, and lack of control over their workload.
  • Health: When our body is not feeling well, or we're hurt, it can cause stress because our mind is worried about getting better and feeling healthy again.
  • Death: Death can be a cause of stress because it brings uncertainty and sadness to people's lives, making them worry about losing loved ones and dealing with the loss of a family member or friend.
  • Financial: Financial stress occurs when people worry about not having enough money to pay bills, buy necessities, or achieve long-term goals, leading to feelings of anxiety and uncertainty about their financial future.
  • Time: Time can be a cause of stress because it creates pressure to complete tasks and meet deadlines, making us feel rushed and worried about not having enough time to do everything we need to do.
  • Divorce: Divorce is a cause of stress because it brings big changes to a person's life, such as losing a partner, dealing with financial problems, and taking care of children alone, which can be overwhelming and affect their mental and emotional well-being.
  • War: War causes stress because it involves loud noises, scary situations, and the risk of being hurt or losing loved ones, which can be overwhelming and frightening for people involved.

What Herbs To Avoid If You Have Stress?

If you're stressed, it's essential to know which herbs to avoid.

One herb to be cautious with is Ashwagandha, as it can initially increase anxiety and stress levels in some people. Another herb to be mindful of is Yohimbine, which can cause jitters and anxiety due to its stimulating properties.

Ginkgo Biloba can also exacerbate stress in some individuals, especially when taken in large doses, by increasing heart rate and blood pressure. Similarly, Ginseng can be over-stimulating and worsen anxiety in those who are already stressed. Additionally, Ephedra and Guarana are stimulants that can increase anxiety and stress levels by releasing excess adrenaline.

Finally, Yerba Mate can cause anxiety and jitters due to its caffeine-like properties.
