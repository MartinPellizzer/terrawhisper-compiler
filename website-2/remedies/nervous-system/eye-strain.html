Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba is a plant that has been used for years to help with eye strain. This is because it contains special chemicals that help improve blood flow to the eyes. When you look at a screen or do close work for a long time, your eyes get tired. This is because they have to work hard to focus and adjust to the light. Ginkgo biloba can help by making the blood vessels in the eyes stronger and wider, so they can carry more oxygen and nutrients to the eyes. This can help reduce eye strain and make it easier to focus. Ginkgo biloba also has anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce swelling and pain in the eyes. Some people find that taking Ginkgo biloba supplements or drinking tea made from the leaves helps to reduce eye strain and make their eyes feel more comfortable after looking at a screen for a long time.
