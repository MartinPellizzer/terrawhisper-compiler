Difficult speaking solutions: 9 herbal creams for deaf or hard of hearing

Many deaf or hard of hearing people struggle with speaking due to a condition called apraxia of speech.

This is when the brain has trouble sending messages to the muscles in the mouth, tongue, and lips to form words and sounds. It's like having a software glitch in the brain's communication system. As a result, speaking can be a laborious and frustrating experience, making everyday interactions like ordering food or talking to friends and family a daunting task. Deaf or hard of hearing people may feel embarrassed, anxious, or self-conscious about speaking in front of others, which can lead to social isolation and low self-esteem.

They may also miss out on important conversations, like hearing important news or understanding their child's needs, which can affect their relationships and daily life. Herbal creams can be used to help alleviate some of these difficulties. These creams typically contain natural ingredients that are absorbed through the skin and can help stimulate the nerves and muscles involved in speech. Some people find that using these creams before speaking or practicing speech exercises can help reduce muscle tension and improve articulation. However, it's essential to note that these creams are not a substitute for professional speech therapy or medical treatment.

They can be a complementary tool to help make speech exercises more enjoyable and effective.

With regular use, you may find that your voice becomes stronger and more resilient, making it easier to communicate effectively.
