7 Herbal Tinctures For Difficult Speaking

Herbal tinctures have been used to help children with autism who struggle with speaking. These special liquids are made from plants and are taken in small amounts by mouth. By taking these tinctures, some children have reported being able to talk more easily and clearly, and even making new friends.

It's not a magic solution, but it can be a helpful tool for kids who really need it.
