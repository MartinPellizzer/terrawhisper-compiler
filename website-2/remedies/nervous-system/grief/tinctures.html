They might even start to believe that they're not worthy of love or happiness. This can be a really tough time for orphans, and they might not know how to deal with their emotions. But there are some things that can help orphans deal with grief. Herbal tinctures are one of them. These tinctures are made from plants that have been used for a long time to help people feel better when they're sad or anxious. They can help orphans calm down and feel more relaxed, which can make it easier for them to sleep and eat. They can also help orphans feel more positive and hopeful, which can make it easier for them to make friends and do well in school.

By using herbal tinctures, orphans can start to feel better and move forward from their grief.
