1. Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis tinctures contains valerenic acid and isovaleric acid, two key bioactive constituents that have a sedative and calming effect on the nervous system.

These compounds help to reduce anxiety and stress, which can be overwhelming when experiencing grief. The tincture also contains valeranone, a compound that has been shown to have a positive effect on sleep quality, which is often disrupted during times of sorrow. By promoting relaxation and reducing stress, Valeriana officinalis tinctures can help create a sense of calm and tranquility, allowing the body and mind to heal from the emotional pain of grief.

By reducing anxiety and promoting restful sleep, Valeriana officinalis tinctures can help the body recover from the physical effects of stress.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Valeriana officinalis roots and 2 cups of 80% vodka in a clean glass jar.
  2. Add the Valeriana officinalis roots to the jar and pour the vodka over them, making sure the roots are completely covered.
  3. Close the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another glass jar, discarding the solids.
  5. Store the Valeriana officinalis tincture in a dark glass bottle with a dropper lid and use 20-30 drops as needed to help with grief.
