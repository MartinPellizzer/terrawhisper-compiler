What herbal tinctures you should avoid if you have grief?

If you're experiencing grief, it's best to avoid certain herbal tinctures that can exacerbate your emotional state.

For example, you may want to steer clear of St. John's Wort (not to be confused with the previously mentioned Hypericum perforatum) as it can interact with medications and worsen anxiety and depression. Another herb to approach with caution is Yohimbe, which can increase feelings of anxiety and agitation.

Additionally, Khat and Kratom are stimulants that can worsen anxiety and make it harder to sleep, which can be especially challenging during times of grief. Lastly, Ephedra and Ephedrine can increase heart rate and blood pressure, making it difficult to cope with emotional stress.

It's essential to consult with a healthcare professional before using any herbal tinctures, especially if you're experiencing grief or struggling with mental health.
