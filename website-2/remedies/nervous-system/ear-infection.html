Causes and Herbal Preparations for Ear Infection Relief

What are the causes of ear infection?

Ear infections happen when there's a problem in the middle ear, which is the small space behind the eardrum.

The main causes of ear infections are: Eustachian tube dysfunction: This tube is like a small airway that helps to balance the air pressure in the middle ear. When it doesn't work properly, it can't let the air in and out, causing a buildup of pressure and fluid behind the eardrum, which can lead to an infection. Bacterial infection: Sometimes, a bacterial infection can spread from another part of the body, like the throat or sinuses, to the middle ear, causing an infection.

Viral infection: Viruses can also cause an ear infection, especially if they're causing a cold or flu. When the viruses infect the middle ear, it can cause inflammation and fluid buildup. Middle ear fluid: When the middle ear is filled with fluid, it can create a perfect breeding ground for bacteria to grow, leading to an infection.

Cold: When you have a cold, your Eustachian tubes can get congested, making it harder for them to work properly, which can lead to ear infections.
