What herbal tinctures you should avoid if you have sinus headache?

If you have a sinus headache, it's best to avoid herbal tinctures that can irritate or overstimulate your sinuses, making symptoms worse.

One such herb is 'Lavandula angustifolia' (Lavender). While lavender is known for its calming effects, its strong scent can sometimes trigger headaches or worsen sinus congestion. Another herb to be cautious with is 'Foeniculum vulgare' (Fennel).

Fennel can cause an overproduction of mucus, which can exacerbate sinus congestion and headache symptoms. Additionally, 'Artemisia absinthium' (Wormwood) contains compounds that can stimulate the nasal passages and worsen sinus inflammation. Lastly, 'Cinchona officinalis' (Cinchona) contains quinine, which can cause blood vessels to constrict and worsen sinus congestion.

These herbal tinctures should be used with caution and under the guidance of a healthcare professional if you have a sinus headache.
