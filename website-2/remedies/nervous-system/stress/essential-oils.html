11 Herbal Essential Oils For Stress

Students can suffer from stress due to various reasons like heavy academic workload, exams, and pressure to perform well.

They may also experience physical symptoms like headaches, stomachaches, and fatigue. Stress can further affect their relationships with family and friends, making them feel isolated and alone. It can also impact their mental health, leading to depression and low self-esteem. When students are stressed, their minds and bodies are in a state of constant alert, making it difficult to relax and unwind.

The constant stress can also affect their mood, making them irritable, moody, and short-tempered. Herbal essential oils can help students manage stress by promoting relaxation and reducing anxiety. These oils contain natural compounds that can calm the mind and body, helping students to unwind and relax. When inhaled, they can slow down the heart rate, lower blood pressure, and promote a sense of calm. They can also help students to focus and concentrate, improving their mental clarity and reducing stress and anxiety.

By incorporating herbal essential oils into their daily routine, students can find a natural way to manage stress and improve their overall well-being.

1. Lavandula angustifolia

Lavandula angustifolia essential oils contains linalool and linalyl acetate, two compounds that contribute to its calming and relaxing properties.

To use Lavandula angustifolia essential oils for stress relief, you can add a few drops to your bath water, inhale it through steam inhalation, or apply it to your skin (diluted with a carrier oil) on your temples, wrists, or behind your ears.

Start by inhaling the scent for a few minutes, then gradually increase the duration as you become accustomed to its effects. For a bath, add 10-15 drops to warm water and soak for 15-20 minutes. When you take these actions, you may experience a reduction in anxiety and stress levels, improved sleep quality, and a sense of calmness. You might also notice a decrease in muscle tension and an improvement in your mood.

As your body relaxes, you may feel more centered and focused, allowing you to tackle challenges with greater ease and clarity.
