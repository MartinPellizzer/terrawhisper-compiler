1. Lavandula angustifolia

Lavandula angustifolia creams contains linalool and linalyl acetate, which are the main active constituents responsible for its therapeutic properties.

These compounds have a calming effect on the mind and body, helping to reduce anxiety and stress. The sedative properties of linalool and linalyl acetate also promote relaxation, making it easier to fall asleep and improve the quality of sleep. By reducing cortisol levels and promoting relaxation, Lavandula angustifolia creams can help alleviate stress and promote a sense of calm.

Regular use of these creams may also help regulate mood and emotions, leading to a more balanced and peaceful state.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of distilled water, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, 1 tablespoon of dried Lavandula angustifolia flowers, and 1 teaspoon of vitamin E oil.
  2. Combine distilled water and dried Lavandula angustifolia flowers in a saucepan. Heat on low until flowers release their essence.
  3. Strain the mixture into a separate container, discarding flowers. In the saucepan, melt coconut oil and beeswax over low heat, stirring occasionally.
  4. Combine the oil mixture with the Lavandula angustifolia essence. Stir until well combined and pour into a mixing bowl.
  5. Add vitamin E oil and mix until smooth. Pour the mixture into small containers and let cool before use.

2. Melissa officinalis

Melissa officinalis creams contains rosmarinic acid, a powerful antioxidant that helps reduce stress and anxiety.

This compound also has anti-inflammatory properties, which can soothe the nervous system and promote relaxation. Additionally, the creams contain apigenin and luteolin, flavonoids that have a calming effect on the mind and body. These compounds work together to decrease stress hormones, like cortisol, and promote a sense of calmness and well-being.

By reducing stress and anxiety, Melissa officinalis creams can help improve sleep quality, focus, and overall mood.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Melissa officinalis leaves and 1 cup of coconut oil in a clean glass jar.
  2. Heat the coconut oil in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water until it reaches 120°F to 140°F.
  3. Steep the dried Melissa officinalis leaves in the heated coconut oil for 2-3 hours, then strain the mixture through a cheesecloth into another clean glass jar.
  4. Add 1/4 cup of beeswax and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil to the strained coconut oil mixture and heat it gently until the beeswax is melted.
  5. Pour the mixture into small glass containers, let it cool, and store the Melissa officinalis creams in a cool, dark place.

3. Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis creams contains valerenic acid and valeranone, two active constituents that contribute to its stress-relieving properties.

These compounds have a sedative effect on the nervous system, helping to calm the mind and body. Valerenic acid, in particular, has been shown to bind to GABA receptors, increasing the production of GABA, a neurotransmitter that helps regulate sleep and relaxation. This action can help alleviate symptoms of anxiety and stress, promoting a sense of calm and well-being.

By reducing stress and anxiety, Valeriana officinalis creams can help improve sleep quality and enhance overall relaxation.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Valeriana officinalis root and 1/2 cup of carrier oil (coconut or sweet almond oil).
  2. Steep the dried root in the carrier oil in a saucepan over low heat for 2-3 hours. Stir occasionally.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a bowl. Discard the solids.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of beeswax and 1 tablespoon of vitamin E oil to the oil mixture. Heat gently until the beeswax melts.
  5. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool. Once set, your Valeriana officinalis cream is ready for use.

4. Passiflora incarnata

Passiflora incarnata creams contains flavonoids, alkaloids, and glycosides that contribute to its stress-reducing properties.

The flavonoids, specifically flavonoids like kaempferol and quercetin, have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory effects that can help calm the mind and body. The alkaloids, including harmine and harmaline, have a sedative effect that can help reduce anxiety and promote relaxation. The glycosides, particularly passifloric acid, have a calming effect on the nervous system, which can help alleviate stress and promote a sense of well-being.

By combining these compounds, Passiflora incarnata creams may help alleviate stress symptoms by promoting relaxation and reducing anxiety.

  1. Gather ingredients: 2 cups of dried Passiflora incarnata flowers, 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of beeswax, 1 tablespoon of vitamin E oil, and 1 teaspoon of lavender essential oil.
  2. Combine dried flowers and coconut oil in a saucepan. Heat on low, stirring occasionally, for 2-3 hours to make a strong tea.
  3. Strain the tea through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a bowl, pressing on the solids to get all the liquid.
  4. In a separate saucepan, melt the beeswax over low heat. Add the vitamin E oil and lavender essential oil. Stir until combined.
  5. Add the tea liquid to the melted wax mixture and stir until well combined. Pour the mixture into a container and let cool to form a solid cream.

5. Avena sativa

Avena sativa creams contains a combination of bioactive constituents, including avenanthramides, beta-glucans, and ferulic acid.

These compounds work together to help reduce stress by promoting relaxation and calming the mind and body. Avenanthramides have been shown to inhibit the production of stress-related hormones, while beta-glucans have anti-inflammatory properties that can help alleviate anxiety. Ferulic acid also has a calming effect, which can help regulate the body's response to stress.

By incorporating these compounds into a topical cream, Avena sativa creams can provide a natural way to manage stress and promote relaxation.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Avena sativa (oats) and 2 cups of water in a saucepan.
  2. Heat the water over low heat, then add the oats and let it simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a bowl, and discard the oats.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil to the cooled oat mixture.
  5. Mix well and pour the mixture into a heat-proof container, then let it cool and solidify before use as a stress-relieving cream.

6. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale creams contains active compounds like gingerols and shogaols, which are responsible for its stress-relieving properties.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that help reduce anxiety and stress levels by regulating the body's response to stress. The 6-gingerol, a key component in ginger, has been shown to have a calming effect on the nervous system by inhibiting the production of stress hormones like cortisol. Zingiber officinale creams also contain other bioactive compounds like bisabolol, which has a soothing effect on the mind and body.

By reducing inflammation and promoting relaxation, Zingiber officinale creams can help alleviate stress and promote a sense of calmness.

  1. Gather 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/2 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil, and 2 tablespoons of ginger essential oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the vitamin E oil and ginger essential oil until well combined.
  4. Pour the mixture into a silicone candy mold or a small tin container. Let it cool and harden.
  5. Once hardened, remove the Zingiber officinale cream from the mold or container and store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

7. Hypericum perforatum

Hypericum perforatum creams contains the active constituents hyperforin and hypericin, which have a calming effect on the mind and body.

These compounds work by inhibiting the production of stress hormones like cortisol, helping to reduce feelings of anxiety and tension. Hyperforin also has a sedative effect, promoting relaxation and reducing stress-related symptoms like insomnia. Additionally, the creams' anti-inflammatory properties can help alleviate physical symptoms associated with stress, such as headaches and muscle strain.

By reducing stress hormones and promoting relaxation, Hypericum perforatum creams can help alleviate stress and promote a sense of calm.

  1. Gather 1 cup of beeswax, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of shea butter, and 2 tablespoons of Hypericum perforatum oil.
  2. Melt the beeswax, coconut oil, and shea butter in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of boiling water.
  3. Remove the melted mixture from heat and stir in the Hypericum perforatum oil.
  4. Pour the mixture into a jar or container and let it cool until solidified.
  5. Once solid, your Hypericum perforatum cream is ready to use. Apply as needed to help manage stress.
