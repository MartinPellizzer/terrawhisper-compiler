10 best herbal teas for stress

1. Valeriana Officinalis

Valeriana officinalis tea, also known as valerian root tea, is a natural way to help with stress. The tea is made from the root of the valerian plant, which has been used for centuries to promote relaxation and calmness. When you drink valerian root tea, the active ingredients in the plant work to slow down your brain and body, helping you to feel more relaxed and less anxious. This can be especially helpful when you're feeling overwhelmed or struggling to fall asleep due to stress. The tea can also help to regulate your mood, making it easier to manage stress and anxiety. Additionally, valerian root tea has been shown to have a sedative effect, which can help to calm your nervous system and promote a good night's sleep. This can help to reduce stress and anxiety over time, leading to a sense of calm and well-being.

2. Lavandula Angustifolia

Lavandula angustifolia tea, also known as English lavender tea, is a popular herbal remedy for stress relief. When we're stressed, our bodies produce high levels of a hormone called cortisol, which can disrupt our sleep and mood. Lavandula angustifolia tea has been shown to lower cortisol levels, promoting relaxation and reducing feelings of anxiety. Drinking this tea before bed can also help improve the quality of our sleep, making it easier to wake up feeling refreshed and revitalized. Additionally, the tea has anti-inflammatory properties, which can help soothe and calm the mind and body.
