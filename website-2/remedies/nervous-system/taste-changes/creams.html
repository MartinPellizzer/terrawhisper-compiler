This can be caused by chemotherapy, radiation, or the tumor affecting the nerves that control taste. This can make everyday life difficult, as simple pleasures like enjoying a favorite meal become a challenge. Taste changes can also affect a patient's quality of life, making them feel weak, irritable, and anxious.

Some patients may even experience a metallic or bitter taste in their mouth, which can be unpleasant and difficult to manage. To manage these taste changes, cancer patients often try different treatments, such as changing their diet, taking supplements, or using medications. However, these treatments may not always be effective, and patients may be looking for alternative solutions. This is where herbal creams come in. These creams are applied topically to the skin, where they can be absorbed and potentially help to reduce inflammation and soothe the mouth and tongue.
