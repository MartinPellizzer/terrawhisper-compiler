10 Herbal Creams For Peripheral Arterial Occlusive Disease

Peripheral arterial occlusive disease (PAOD) is a common problem in obese people.

This is because excess body fat can lead to inflammation and damage to the blood vessels, making it harder for blood to flow through them. As a result, the legs and feet may not get enough oxygen and nutrients, leading to pain, cramps, and fatigue when walking or standing. This can make everyday activities difficult and affect the quality of life. For instance, simple tasks like cooking or taking care of oneself can become a challenge.

The pain and discomfort can be so severe that it forces people to limit their physical activity, leading to a vicious cycle of inactivity and weight gain. Obese people with PAOD may also be at a higher risk of developing other health problems, such as heart disease and diabetes. Herbal creams can be a helpful addition to the treatment plan for obese individuals with PAOD. These creams typically contain anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties that can help reduce pain and swelling in the affected areas. They can also improve blood flow and promote the growth of new blood vessels, which can help alleviate symptoms and improve overall health.

Additionally, herbal creams are often easy to apply and can be used as needed, making them a convenient option for people who may not be able to follow a strict medication regimen.
