Palpitation: Understanding Its Causes and Natural Medicinal Herbs

What are the causes of palpitation?

Palpitations are irregular heartbeats that can be caused by various factors.

One of the main causes is anxiety, which can trigger palpitations by releasing stress hormones like adrenaline into the body. These hormones speed up the heart rate, making it beat irregularly. High blood pressure, or hypertension, is another cause of palpitations. When blood pressure rises, the heart has to work harder to pump blood, leading to an irregular heartbeat. In some cases, hypertension can also cause the heart to beat too quickly or irregularly.

Hyperthyroidism is a condition where the thyroid gland produces too much thyroid hormone, leading to an increased heart rate and palpitations. This is because the thyroid hormone stimulates the heart to beat faster, causing irregular heartbeats. Dehydration can also cause palpitations by reducing the amount of blood in the body. When the body is not getting enough water, the heart has to work harder to pump the limited blood, leading to an irregular heartbeat. Finally, excessive caffeine consumption is a common cause of palpitations.

Caffeine is a stimulant that speeds up the heart rate and can cause an irregular heartbeat, especially if consumed in large amounts or on an empty stomach.
