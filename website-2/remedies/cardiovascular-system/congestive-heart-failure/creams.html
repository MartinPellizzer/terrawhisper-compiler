1. Panax ginseng

Panax ginseng creams contains bioactive constituents like ginsenosides, which are known to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the body, which are common factors contributing to congestive heart failure. The ginsenosides in Panax ginseng creams also have vasodilatory effects, which can help lower blood pressure and improve blood flow to the heart. Furthermore, the antioxidant properties of ginsenosides can protect the heart from damage caused by free radicals, which can worsen heart failure over time.

By reducing inflammation and improving blood flow, Panax ginseng creams may help alleviate symptoms of congestive heart failure and improve quality of life for those affected.

  1. Gather 1 cup of distilled water, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, 1 tablespoon of vitamin E oil, and 1 teaspoon of Panax ginseng extract.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Add the distilled water and stir until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  4. Remove the mixture from the heat and stir in the vitamin E oil and Panax ginseng extract.
  5. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool and solidify. Once solid, your Panax ginseng cream is ready to use.

2. Digitalis purpurea

Digitalis purpurea creams contains the biologically active constituents digoxin and digitoxin.

These compounds have cardiotonic and inotropic properties, which help increase the strength of the heart's contractions. By improving the heart's pumping ability, Digitalis purpurea creams can reduce the amount of blood that accumulates in the body's tissues, alleviating symptoms of congestive heart failure. The cardiotonic properties of digoxin and digitoxin also help regulate the heart's rhythm and reduce the risk of arrhythmias.

Regular use of Digitalis purpurea creams may help patients with congestive heart failure experience improved heart function and reduced symptoms.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Digitalis purpurea flowers, a saucepan, and cheesecloth.
  2. Combine 2 cups of water and 1 cup of dried flowers in the saucepan. Heat it over low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Strain the mixture through cheesecloth into a bowl. Discard the solids.
  4. Let the liquid cool, then mix it with 1/4 cup of beeswax and 1/4 cup of coconut oil in a double boiler.
  5. Heat the mixture until the beeswax and coconut oil melt, then pour the Digitalis purpurea cream into small containers and let it cool.

  3. Add the turmeric powder, ginger powder (from 3-4 grams of Zingiber officinale), and aloe vera gel to the melted mixture. Stir well.

  5. Pour the mixture into small containers and refrigerate for 30 minutes to set. Use as needed to help alleviate symptoms of congestive heart failure.

6. Ginkgo biloba
