When people have congestive heart failure, their heart is not pumping enough blood, leading to swelling and shortness of breath. Digitalis purpurea tea helps by increasing the heart's contractions and reducing the amount of fluid in the body. This makes it easier for the heart to pump blood, reducing symptoms like swelling in the legs and shortness of breath. Additionally, digitalis purpurea tea can help slow down the heart rate, which can become too fast in people with congestive heart failure.
