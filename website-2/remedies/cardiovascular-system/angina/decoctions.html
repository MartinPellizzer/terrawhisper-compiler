Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) can also be problematic for individuals with angina. While it's often used to reduce stress, its stimulating effects can increase heart rate and worsen angina symptoms.

Furthermore, the herb Ephedra and Ashwagandha can interact with other medications used to treat angina, such as beta-blockers, leading to adverse reactions.
