When these blood vessels relax, they become less stiff and more flexible, allowing blood to flow more easily and reducing the pressure on the blood vessels. This can lead to a decrease in blood pressure, which can reduce the risk of heart disease, stroke, and other complications associated with high blood pressure. The tea may also help reduce anxiety and stress, which are common causes of high blood pressure. Drinking valerian root tea regularly may help regulate blood pressure and improve cardiovascular health.
