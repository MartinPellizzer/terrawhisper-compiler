Hypertension in Older Adults: 8 Herbal Tinctures for a Healthier You

As people age, their blood vessels naturally lose some of their flexibility and elasticity, which can lead to high blood pressure.

This is because the muscle walls of the blood vessels become stiffer, making it harder for blood to flow through. High blood pressure in older adults can cause a range of problems, including heart disease, stroke, and kidney disease. It can also lead to fatigue, shortness of breath, and swelling in the legs and feet. Furthermore, older adults with hypertension are more likely to experience cognitive decline and dementia. Managing hypertension is crucial to maintaining quality of life, as it can help prevent these complications and allow older adults to live independently for longer.

However, many older adults struggle to manage their blood pressure with medication alone, and may experience side effects like dizziness and fatigue. In this context, herbal tinctures have been studied as a potential complementary therapy for hypertension in older adults. These tinctures are made by infusing plant extracts in a solvent like ethanol or glycerin, which helps to preserve the active compounds and make them easily absorbed by the body. Some studies have shown that certain plant-based compounds can help to relax blood vessels, improve circulation, and reduce blood pressure. They may also have anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to reduce the strain on the cardiovascular system.

While more research is needed to fully understand the benefits and risks of herbal tinctures for hypertension, they may offer a natural and non-invasive way for older adults to manage their blood pressure and improve their overall well-being.
