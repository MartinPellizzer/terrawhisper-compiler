The Causes and Herbal Treatments of High Blood Pressure

What are the causes of high blood pressure?

High blood pressure, also known as hypertension, is a condition where the blood pressure in your blood vessels is too high.

This can put extra strain on your heart and blood vessels, and increase the risk of serious health problems. So, what causes high blood pressure?. Well, one major reason is your family history. If your parents or grandparents had high blood pressure, you're more likely to develop it too, because you may have inherited a tendency to have high blood pressure. Another big risk factor is smoking. When you smoke, it damages the lining of your blood vessels, making them narrower and less efficient.

This means your heart has to work harder to pump blood through your body, which can increase blood pressure. People with diabetes are also more likely to develop high blood pressure. This is because diabetes can damage the blood vessels and make them less able to regulate blood pressure. Obesity is another major cause of high blood pressure. When you're overweight, your body has to work harder to pump blood through your body, which can increase blood pressure. Finally, age is a big risk factor for high blood pressure.

As we get older, our blood vessels naturally become narrower and less efficient, making it more likely that our blood pressure will rise.
