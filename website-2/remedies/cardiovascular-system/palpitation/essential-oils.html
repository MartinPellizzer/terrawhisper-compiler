8 Herbal Essential Oils For Palpitation

Anxiety can be a real nightmare for many people.

It can make them feel like they're walking on eggshells, constantly worried about what might happen next. And for some, anxiety can also cause a racing heart, known as palpitations. These uncomfortable feelings can be a huge problem for anxiety sufferers, making it hard for them to go about their daily lives. They might feel like they're on edge all the time, and even small things can trigger a panic attack. This can make it hard to work, spend time with loved ones, or even take care of basic needs like eating and sleeping. It's like their body is constantly on high alert, and they can't seem to calm down. When anxiety causes palpitations, it's often because the body's "fight or flight" response is triggered.

This response is meant to help us react quickly to danger, but for anxiety sufferers, it can be triggered by things that aren't actually life-threatening. This can lead to a buildup of stress hormones like adrenaline, which can make the heart beat faster and feel like it's pounding in the chest. It's like the body is stuck in a perpetual state of emergency, and it doesn't know how to turn off the alarm. But there is some good news. Herbal essential oils have been shown to be a helpful tool in calming the body and reducing anxiety symptoms like palpitations. These oils are derived from plants and can be inhaled or applied to the skin to promote relaxation and reduce stress. They can help slow down the heart rate and calm the nervous system, making it easier to breathe and feel more centered. Some of these oils have a natural sedative effect, while others can help reduce anxiety by promoting feelings of calm and relaxation.

By using these oils in conjunction with other relaxation techniques, anxiety sufferers may be able to find some relief from their palpitations and other symptoms.
