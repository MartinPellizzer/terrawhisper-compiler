9 Herbal Teas For Bladder Infection

Using a catheter can make you more prone to bladder infections.

This is because bacteria can easily enter the bladder through the catheter, causing an infection. These infections can be painful and uncomfortable, and in severe cases, can lead to other health issues like kidney damage or sepsis. For people who use catheters, bladder infections can disrupt their daily lives, causing constant trips to the bathroom, pain, and discomfort. They may also have to take time off work or school, and may need to see their doctor frequently to manage their symptoms. Bladder infections can also affect a person's mental health, leading to feelings of frustration, anxiety, and depression.

They may feel embarrassed or self-conscious about their condition, especially if they need to use a catheter in public. This can make it hard for them to socialize, travel, or engage in activities they enjoy. To manage bladder infections, catheter users may need to take antibiotics, which can have side effects and may not be effective for everyone. This is where herbal teas come in - they can help soothe and calm the bladder, reducing the symptoms of an infection. Herbal teas can also help prevent future infections by creating an environment that is less welcoming to bacteria.

They can be a natural and gentle way to support bladder health, and can be drunk as often as needed to help manage symptoms.
