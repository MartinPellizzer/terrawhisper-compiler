1. Juniperus communis

Juniperus communis tinctures contains compounds like terpinen-4-ol, limonene, and pinene, which are known for their antimicrobial and antiseptic properties.

These properties make Juniperus communis effective in combating bacterial infections, including those that cause bladder infections. The antimicrobial compounds in Juniperus communis help to reduce the growth of bacteria in the urinary tract, which can help to alleviate symptoms of a bladder infection. Additionally, the antiseptic properties of Juniperus communis help to prevent the spread of infection and promote healing in the urinary tract.

By reducing bacteria and promoting healing, Juniperus communis tinctures can help to alleviate the symptoms of a bladder infection.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Juniperus communis berries and 2 cups of 80% vodka.
  2. Combine the berries and vodka in a clean glass jar, making sure the berries are completely covered.
  3. Steep the mixture in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids.
  5. Store the tincture in a cool, dark place and take 15 drops, 3 times a day, as needed for bladder infection relief.

2. Berberis vulgaris

Berberis vulgaris tinctures contains a combination of bioactive constituents that help alleviate bladder infections.

Berberine also has anti-inflammatory properties, which can reduce swelling and discomfort in the bladder and urinary tract.   1. Gather 100g of Berberis vulgaris roots and stems, and 50% vodka or ethanol for making the tincture.
  2. Place the Berberis vulgaris roots and stems in a clean glass jar, and pour 1 cup of vodka or ethanol over them.
