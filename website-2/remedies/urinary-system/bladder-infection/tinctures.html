Many people who use catheters have to deal with these issues, which can be very frustrating and affect their quality of life.

A bladder infection can also cause other problems, like a fever, chills, and a general feeling of being unwell. If left untreated, it can even lead to more serious health issues. To treat a bladder infection, people often take antibiotics. However, these can have side effects and may not work well for everyone. This is where herbal tinctures come in - they are liquid extracts made from plants that have been used for centuries to help fight infections and soothe the bladder.
