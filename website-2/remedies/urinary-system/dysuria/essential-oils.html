The pressure on the bladder can also cause infections, such as urinary tract infections (UTIs), which can worsen the symptoms of dysuria. When left untreated, UTIs can lead to more severe complications, including preterm labor and low birth weight. As a result, pregnant women need to find effective ways to manage dysuria and prevent infections.
