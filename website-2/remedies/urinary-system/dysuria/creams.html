1. Aloe barbadensis

Aloe barbadensis creams contains biologically active constituents like aloin, aloe-emodin, and aloe-juice, which provide anti-inflammatory and antibacterial properties.

These properties help in soothing the burning sensation caused by dysuria, a condition characterized by painful urination. The aloe-juice in the cream has moisturizing properties, which can help in soothing and healing the irritated tissues in the urinary tract.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of aloe vera gel, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and shea butter in a double boiler or a microwave-safe bowl in 10-second increments, stirring between each interval, until smooth.
