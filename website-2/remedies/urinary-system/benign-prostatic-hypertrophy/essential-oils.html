1. Serenoa repens

Serenoa repens essential oils contains active constituents such as saw palmetto and phytosterols, which have been identified as key components that help alleviate symptoms of benign prostatic hypertrophy (BPH).

Phytosterols, particularly beta-sitosterol, have been shown to inhibit the conversion of testosterone to dihydrotestosterone (DHT), a hormone that contributes to prostate growth. Saw palmetto's ability to block DHT receptors helps reduce prostate size and alleviate urinary symptoms associated with BPH. The anti-inflammatory properties of Serenoa repens essential oils may also help reduce swelling and discomfort in the prostate area.

By inhibiting DHT and reducing inflammation, Serenoa repens essential oils may provide relief from BPH symptoms.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Serenoa repens leaves, dried and crushed.
  2. Add 1 cup of carrier oil (like coconut oil) to the crushed leaves in a clean glass jar.
  3. Steep the mixture in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar.
  5. Discard the solids and store the Serenoa repens essential oil in a dark glass bottle with a tight-fitting lid.
