1. Serenoa repens

Serenoa repens tinctures contains several bioactive constituents, including saw palmetto extract, fatty acids, and phytosterols.

These compounds work together to block the conversion of testosterone to dihydrotestosterone, a hormone that contributes to prostate enlargement. Saw palmetto also increases the levels of certain enzymes that break down dihydrotestosterone, further reducing its effects on the prostate. The phytosterols in Serenoa repens tinctures inhibit the binding of dihydrotestosterone to prostate cells, preventing further growth and enlargement.

By blocking the action of dihydrotestosterone, Serenoa repens tinctures may help alleviate symptoms of benign prostatic hypertrophy, such as frequent urination and weak urine flow.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Serenoa repens root and 2 cups of 80-proof vodka in a clean glass container.
  2. Combine the dried Serenoa repens root and vodka in the glass container. Make sure the root is completely covered by the vodka.
  3. Steep the mixture in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the container every day.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another glass container. Discard the solids.
  5. Label the tincture and store it in a cool, dark place. Use 20-30 drops, 2-3 times a day, as needed.
