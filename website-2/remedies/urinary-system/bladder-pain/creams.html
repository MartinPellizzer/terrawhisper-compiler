1. Urtica dioica

Urtica dioica creams contains compounds like histamine, serotonin, and acetylcholine, which have anti-inflammatory properties.

These compounds help reduce swelling and irritation in the bladder, providing relief from pain. Urtica dioica also contains flavonoids, such as quercetin, which have antioxidant properties that can help soothe the bladder and reduce inflammation. The cream's anti-inflammatory properties can also help relax the muscles in the bladder, allowing for easier urination and reducing pain.

Urtica dioica creams can provide immediate relief from bladder pain due to its fast-acting compounds like acetylcholine, which helps relax the bladder muscles.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of Urtica dioica leaves, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil, and 2 tablespoons of shea butter.
  2. Dry the Urtica dioica leaves in a low-temperature oven (150°F) for 2 hours or until crispy.
  3. Grind the dried leaves into a fine powder using a coffee grinder or spice grinder.
  4. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water. Add the ground leaves, vitamin E oil, and shea butter. Stir until well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into a small tin or jar and let it cool and solidify. Store the Urtica dioica cream in the fridge for up to 2 weeks.
