10 Herbal Oils for Older Adults with Urinary Calculus

As people age, their bodies undergo many changes.

One common issue that older adults face is urinary calculus, also known as kidney stones. These small, hard deposits can form in the kidneys and travel down to the bladder, causing severe pain and discomfort. This can happen due to a variety of reasons such as dehydration, diet, and certain medical conditions. Older adults with urinary calculus often experience symptoms like severe pain in the side or back, nausea, vomiting, and difficulty starting or stopping the flow of urine.

These symptoms can be so painful and debilitating that they affect daily life, making it hard for older adults to perform simple tasks, sleep, or even socialize with family and friends. In severe cases, urinary calculus can lead to complications like kidney damage, urinary tract infections, and even sepsis. The good news is that some herbal essential oils have been found to be effective in treating urinary calculus, helping to break down and eliminate these painful stones from the body. These oils work by reducing inflammation, fighting off infections, and improving digestion, making it easier for the body to pass the stones.

By incorporating these oils into their treatment plan, older adults can find relief from the pain and discomfort of urinary calculus, and improve their overall quality of life.
