Fluid Retention: Causes, Herbal Preparations, and Holistic Treatments

What are the causes of fluid retention?

Fluid retention, also known as edema, occurs when your body holds onto excess fluid.

There are several main causes of this condition. One reason is Hormonal Imbalance, particularly an overproduction of hormones like aldosterone, which helps control fluid levels in the body. When aldosterone is produced in excess, it signals your body to retain sodium and water. Salt Intake plays a significant role as well.

Consuming high amounts of salt can lead to Sodium Retention, causing your body to hold onto excess fluid. This is because sodium helps your body retain water, and when you have too much sodium, your body holds onto more water as well. Water Overload is another cause, which can happen when you drink too much water or consume foods with high water content. This puts extra strain on your kidneys, which can't process the excess fluid, leading to fluid retention.

Finally, Kidney Disease can also cause fluid retention, as damaged kidneys struggle to filter excess fluid from your body, leading to a buildup of fluid in your tissues.
