Fluid retention can also make it harder for them to manage their blood sugar levels, as excess fluid can dilute the levels of glucose in their blood. This can be a vicious cycle, making it harder for them to control their diabetes.

To make matters worse, many medications used to treat diabetes can also contribute to fluid retention.
