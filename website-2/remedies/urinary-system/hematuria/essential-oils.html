1. Silybum marianum

Silybum marianum essential oils contains boivolatile constituents like flavonoids, silymarin, and sesquiterpenes.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce swelling and irritation in the urinary tract, which can contribute to hematuria. The sesquiterpenes in Silybum marianum essential oils also have antioxidant properties, which can help protect the kidneys from damage and promote healing. The flavonoids in this essential oil have been shown to have antiseptic and antispasmodic properties, which can help prevent infections and reduce muscle spasms in the urinary tract.

By using Silybum marianum essential oils, individuals may experience relief from hematuria symptoms due to its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, antiseptic, and antispasmodic properties.

  1. Gather Silybum marianum flowers, carrier oil (coconut or olive oil), a clean glass jar, a cheesecloth, and a dark glass bottle.
  2. Dry 1 cup of Silybum marianum flowers in a low-temperature oven (150°F) for 2 hours to remove moisture.
  3. Combine 1/4 cup of dried flowers with 2 cups of carrier oil in the clean glass jar. Stir well.
  4. Steep the mixture in a cool, dark place for 2 weeks. Shake the jar daily to help the oil extract from the flowers.
  5. Strain the mixture through cheesecloth into the dark glass bottle. Discard the solids and store the Silybum marianum essential oil in a cool, dark place.
