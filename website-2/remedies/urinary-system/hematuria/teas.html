Hematuria in runners: 11 herbal teas for support

As runners, many of us know the rush of endorphins and the sense of accomplishment that comes with completing a long run.

However, some of us may experience a different kind of pain - literally. Hematuria, or blood in the urine, can be a painful and frustrating condition, especially for runners who rely on their bodies to perform at their best. This can cause discomfort, pain, and even fear of exercising, which can be a big blow to someone who loves running. It can also lead to anxiety, stress, and a disrupted routine. Runners who experience hematuria may need to take time off from running or modify their training plans, which can be disappointing and frustrating. The pain and discomfort of hematuria can also make everyday activities, like going to the bathroom, a chore.

So, what causes hematuria in runners?. It's often due to inflammation or irritation of the urinary tract, which can be caused by running long distances, especially if you're new to running or have recently increased your mileage. Other factors, like dehydration or urinary tract infections, can also contribute to hematuria. For runners who experience hematuria, finding relief is essential. While medical treatment may be necessary in some cases, herbal teas can be a helpful addition to your recovery routine. These teas work by soothing and calming the urinary tract, reducing inflammation and pain. They can also help to prevent future occurrences of hematuria by promoting overall urinary health.

By incorporating herbal teas into your daily routine, you may find that you're able to run more comfortably and confidently.
