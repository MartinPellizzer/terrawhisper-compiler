One common condition is kidney stones. Kidney stones can cause severe pain and may lead to hematuria. Medicinal herbs like juniper berries and marshmallow root can help. Juniper berries have anti-inflammatory properties that reduce swelling and ease pain. Marshmallow root soothes the lining of the urinary tract, making it easier for kidney stones to pass out. Another condition people with hematuria may experience is urinary tract infections (UTIs). UTIs are often caused by bacteria and can lead to pain, fever, and blood in the urine. Herbal remedies like uva ursi and goldenseal can help fight bacterial infections.

Uva ursi has antibacterial properties that kill the bacteria causing UTIs, while goldenseal reduces inflammation and promotes healing. People with hematuria may also experience inflammation and irritation in the urinary tract, which can be relieved with medicinal herbs like dandelion root and burdock root. Dandelion root promotes the removal of toxins from the body and reduces inflammation, while burdock root has anti-inflammatory properties that soothe the urinary tract. In addition, people with hematuria may experience anemia or iron deficiency due to the blood loss. Herbal remedies like alfalfa and nettle leaf can help. Alfalfa has iron-rich properties that help restore iron levels in the body, while nettle leaf is rich in iron and other essential minerals that promote healthy blood production. Lastly, people with hematuria may experience fatigue and weakness due to the underlying condition. Medicinal herbs like ashwagandha and ginseng can help.

Ashwagandha is an adaptogenic herb that helps the body adapt to stress and promotes energy, while ginseng boosts energy levels and reduces fatigue.
