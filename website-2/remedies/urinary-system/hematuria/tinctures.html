11 herbal remedies for older adults experiencing hematuria

As people age, their bodies go through many changes.

One of these changes is that their kidneys, which filter waste and excess fluids from their blood, may not work as well as they used to. This can lead to a condition called hematuria, where blood appears in their urine. Older adults with hematuria may experience pain and discomfort, especially when they urinate. They may also feel weak and tired, and have a hard time concentrating. In some cases, hematuria can be a sign of a more serious problem, such as kidney stones or bladder cancer. This can be very scary and stressful for older adults, and can affect their daily lives in many ways.

They may need to take time off from work or activities they enjoy, and may have to make changes to their diet and lifestyle. Hematuria in older adults can be caused by a variety of factors, including infections, inflammation, and certain medical conditions. Some older adults may also experience hematuria as a side effect of certain medications. In some cases, the cause of hematuria may not be clear, and further testing and evaluation may be needed to determine the underlying cause. Herbal tinctures are a type of natural remedy that some older adults may find helpful in treating hematuria. These tinctures are made by extracting the active ingredients from plants and mixing them with a liquid, such as water or alcohol. Some herbal tinctures may help to reduce inflammation and promote healing in the kidneys and bladder, which can help to alleviate symptoms of hematuria.

They may also help to support the health of the kidneys and bladder, which can reduce the risk of complications and improve overall quality of life.
