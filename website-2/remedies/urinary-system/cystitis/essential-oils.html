1. Equisetum arvense

Equisetum arvense essential oils contains flavonoids, alkaloids, and phenolic compounds that give it anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties.

These properties help to soothe and calm the urinary tract, reducing inflammation and discomfort associated with cystitis. The alkaloids present in the oil, such as palustrine and fumaric acid, have a direct impact on the bacterial growth that causes cystitis. The antimicrobial properties of Equisetum arvense essential oils help to combat bacterial infections and reduce the risk of complications.

The anti-inflammatory properties of the flavonoids and phenolic compounds in the oil also help to reduce pain and discomfort associated with cystitis.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh Equisetum arvense plants, leaves and stems.
  2. Wash the plants with 2 cups of water to remove dirt and impurities.
  3. Use a steamer or a double boiler to heat 2 cups of water. Bring the water to a boil, then reduce the heat.
  4. Add the washed Equisetum arvense plants to the steamer or double boiler. Steam the plants for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
  5. Collect the essential oil by pouring the steamed plant mixture into a bowl. Let it sit for 2 hours, then carefully pour the oil from the top layer into a dark glass bottle.
