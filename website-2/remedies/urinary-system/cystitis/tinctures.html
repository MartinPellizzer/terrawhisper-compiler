1. Equisetum arvense

Equisetum arvense tinctures contains silicic acid, flavonoids, and equisetin, which are responsible for its therapeutic properties.

These bioactive constituents have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties, making them effective against infections that cause cystitis. Silicic acid helps to reduce inflammation and strengthen the bladder wall, while flavonoids and equisetin have antioxidant properties that help to combat oxidative stress. The antimicrobial properties of Equisetum arvense tinctures also help to fight off bacterial infections that can cause cystitis.

By reducing inflammation, fighting off infections, and combating oxidative stress, Equisetum arvense tinctures can help to alleviate the symptoms of cystitis.

  1. Gather 250g of dried Equisetum arvense, a cup of 80% vodka, and a clean glass jar.
  2. Combine the dried Equisetum arvense with 250ml of vodka in the glass jar.
  3. Seal the jar tightly and shake it well for 10 minutes.
  4. Store the jar in a cool, dark place and let it sit for 2-3 weeks, shaking it every day.
  5. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.
