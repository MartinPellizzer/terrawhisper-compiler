The symptoms of prostatitis can be uncomfortable and embarrassing, and can also lead to anxiety and depression. Many men with prostatitis try various treatments, such as antibiotics or pain relievers, but these may not always provide relief. Herbal essential oils can be a natural and effective way to help alleviate the symptoms of prostatitis.
