Prostatitis is a common problem that affects many adult men.

The exact cause of prostatitis is often unknown, but it can be triggered by infections, stress, or even certain medical conditions.

Men with prostatitis may experience symptoms like burning urination, frequent urination, and pain in the pelvic area. When men are diagnosed with prostatitis, they often turn to conventional treatments like antibiotics or pain relievers.
