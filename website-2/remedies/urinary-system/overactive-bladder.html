Crataegus monogyna

Crataegus monogyna, also known as hawthorn, is a plant with many health benefits. Its leaves and berries are rich in flavonoids, which are powerful antioxidants that help protect the body from damage. For people with overactive bladder, hawthorn can be beneficial because it has a relaxing effect on the muscles in the urinary tract. This can help to reduce the urge to urinate and the frequency of trips to the bathroom. The flavonoids in hawthorn may also help to improve blood flow to the bladder, which can help to reduce inflammation and promote healing. Additionally, hawthorn has been shown to have a mild diuretic effect, which can help to reduce the amount of urine produced in the body. This can make it easier for people with overactive bladder to manage their condition and reduce their symptoms.

Vaccinium macrocarpon, also known as cranberry, is a type of fruit that may help with overactive bladder symptoms. This can reduce the risk of urinary tract infections (UTIs), which are a common cause of overactive bladder. Cranberry may also help to relax the muscles in the bladder, making it easier to hold urine and reducing the frequency of trips to the bathroom. Additionally, some studies suggest that cranberry may help to reduce inflammation in the bladder and improve bladder control. This may be due to the antioxidant properties of the fruit, which can help to protect the bladder from damage and promote healing.
