Turmeric, another herb, contains curcumin, which has strong anti-inflammatory effects that can help with IBS symptoms.

Depression and anxiety are often linked to chronic pain conditions like RA. St. John's Wort, a natural antidepressant, can help alleviate symptoms of depression. However, it should be used under medical supervision due to potential interactions with other medications. Gout, a type of arthritis characterized by joint pain and inflammation, can be managed with celery seed and nettle leaf. These herbs help reduce uric acid levels in the blood, which can contribute to gout attacks. Fibromyalgia, a condition characterized by widespread muscle pain and fatigue, can be alleviated with herbs like devil's claw and passionflower.

These herbs have anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties that can help reduce fibromyalgia symptoms.
