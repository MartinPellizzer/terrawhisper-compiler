You should also limit or avoid 'Dong Quai' (Angelica polymorpha or Angelica sinensis alternative - Pueraria lobata) tincture due to its estrogenic effects. It's also a good idea to avoid 'Kava' (Piper methysticum) tincture, as it can weaken bone density and increase the risk of osteoporosis. Lastly, be cautious with 'Red Clover' (Trifolium pratense) tincture, as it has isoflavones that can mimic estrogen in the body, which might affect bone health.

Please consult with a healthcare professional before using any herbal tinctures, especially if you have concerns about your bone health.
