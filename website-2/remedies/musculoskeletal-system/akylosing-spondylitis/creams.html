By reducing inflammation and oxidative stress, Curcuma longa creams may help alleviate the symptoms of ankylosing spondylitis and promote joint health.

  1. Gather 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, and 2 tablespoons of Curcuma longa powder in a double boiler.

  3. Add the Curcuma longa powder to the melted mixture and stir well.
  4. Remove the mixture from heat and let it cool until it reaches 120°F to 130°F.
  5. Pour the mixture into a clean container and let it cool completely before using as a topical cream for ankylosing spondylitis.
