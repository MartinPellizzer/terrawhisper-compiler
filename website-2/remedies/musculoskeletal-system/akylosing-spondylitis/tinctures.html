As a result, construction workers with ankylosing spondylitis may need to modify their work habits, take regular breaks, and use assistive devices to manage their condition.

However, for many, this is not enough, and they are left searching for alternative treatments to help manage their symptoms. By using herbal tinctures, construction workers may be able to improve their symptoms, reduce their pain levels, and enhance their overall well-being.
