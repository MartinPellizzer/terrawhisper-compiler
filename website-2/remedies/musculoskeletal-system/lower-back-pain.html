Curcuma longa

Curcuma longa, also known as turmeric, is a plant that has been used for centuries to help people with lower back pain. The main reason for this is its active compound, curcumin, which has strong anti-inflammatory properties. When you have lower back pain, the muscles in your back can become inflamed, leading to more pain and discomfort. Curcumin in turmeric helps to reduce this inflammation, allowing the muscles to heal and relax. This can help to ease the pain and make it easier to move around. Additionally, turmeric also has antioxidant properties, which help to protect the cells in your body from damage. This can also help to prevent future episodes of lower back pain.

By reducing inflammation and relaxing muscles, ginger can help to improve mobility and reduce stiffness in the lower back, making it a popular natural remedy for lower back pain.
