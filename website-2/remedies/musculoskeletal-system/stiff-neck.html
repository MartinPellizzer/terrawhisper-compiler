Zingiber officinale, also known as ginger, is a natural remedy that can help with a stiff neck. It contains a compound called gingerol, which has anti-inflammatory properties. The warmth and heat from ginger also help to relax the muscles, making it easier to move your neck and reduce stiffness. By reducing pain and inflammation, and helping to eliminate lactic acid, ginger can help to provide relief from a stiff neck and make it easier to move and perform everyday activities.
