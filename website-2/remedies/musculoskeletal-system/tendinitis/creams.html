1. Arnica montana

Arnica montana creams contains bioactive constituents such as flavonoids, sesquiterpenes, and triterpenoids.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which help to reduce pain and swelling in the affected area. The sesquiterpenes, specifically thymoquinone, have been shown to inhibit the production of pro-inflammatory enzymes, which contribute to the development of tendinitis. The antioxidants in Arnica montana creams also help to protect the affected tissues from oxidative damage, promoting healing and reducing inflammation.

By reducing inflammation and promoting healing, Arnica montana creams can help to alleviate the symptoms of tendinitis, including pain and stiffness.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Arnica montana flowers, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of beeswax, and 2 tablespoons of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a microwave-safe bowl in 10-second increments, stirring between each interval, until smooth.
  3. Add the Arnica montana flowers to the melted oil and beeswax mixture and let it steep for 2-3 hours or overnight.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a bowl, discarding the Arnica flowers.
  5. Add the vitamin E oil and mix well. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool and solidify before use.

2. Calendula officinalis

The anti-inflammatory properties of these compounds also aid in reducing redness and heat in the affected area. By reducing inflammation and promoting healing, calendula officinalis creams can help alleviate the symptoms of tendinitis and support the body's natural recovery process.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Calendula officinalis flowers and 2 cups of coconut oil in a clean glass jar.
  2. Pour 1 cup of olive oil and 1 tablespoon of beeswax into a separate saucepan and heat it over low heat.
  3. Add the Calendula flowers to the saucepan and let it steep for 2 hours, then strain the mixture through a cheesecloth.
  4. Combine the strained mixture with the coconut oil and stir well. Add 1 teaspoon of vitamin E oil and 1 teaspoon of aloe vera gel.
  5. Pour the mixture into a mixing bowl and let it cool. Once cooled, whip the mixture with an electric mixer until it thickens into a creamy consistency.
