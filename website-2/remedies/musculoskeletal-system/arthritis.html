Curcuma longa

Curcuma longa, also known as turmeric, is a plant-based remedy that has been used for centuries to help with arthritis. The active compound in turmeric, called curcumin, has powerful anti-inflammatory properties. When you have arthritis, your joints can become swollen and painful due to inflammation. Curcumin works by blocking the chemicals that cause inflammation in the body, which helps to reduce pain and swelling in the joints. As a result, people with arthritis who take turmeric supplements or eat foods with turmeric may find that their symptoms improve. Turmeric also has antioxidant properties, which help protect the joints from damage caused by free radicals. This can help slow down the progression of arthritis and improve joint health over time. Additionally, turmeric has been shown to reduce stiffness and improve mobility in people with arthritis, making it easier to perform daily activities.
