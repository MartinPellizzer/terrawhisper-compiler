10 Herbal Creams For Frozen Shoulder

As people age, their bodies go through many changes.

One of these changes is a higher risk of developing frozen shoulder, a condition where the shoulder joint becomes stiff and painful, making everyday tasks like dressing and cooking difficult. This can lead to feelings of frustration and isolation, especially if older adults are unable to perform activities they once enjoyed. They may struggle to care for themselves, and even simple movements like raising their arm or turning their head can become excruciatingly painful. This can have a significant impact on their quality of life, making it hard for them to participate in social activities, maintain their independence, and even enjoy basic comforts like a warm shower. Frozen shoulder is also often associated with other health conditions that are common in older adults, such as arthritis, diabetes, and heart disease.

These conditions can increase the risk of frozen shoulder and make it harder to treat. In many cases, older adults may be advised to try non-surgical treatments, such as physical therapy, before considering surgery. However, these treatments can be time-consuming and may not be suitable for everyone. Herbal creams have been studied as a potential treatment for frozen shoulder in older adults. These creams typically contain a mixture of plant extracts that have anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties.

When applied topically to the affected area, they may help reduce inflammation and ease pain, making it easier for older adults to move their shoulder and perform daily activities.

Can drinking herbal tea prevent frozen shoulder from forming?

Drinking herbal tea may help prevent frozen shoulder.

Consuming herbal teas for frozen shoulder every day can be safe, but it's essential to choose teas that are gentle on your body.
