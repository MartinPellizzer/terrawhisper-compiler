1. Valeriana officinalis

Valeriana officinalis tinctures contains valerenic acid and isovaleric acid, which have a sedative effect on the nervous system.

This helps to calm the mind and body, reducing stress and anxiety that can lead to jaw clenching. The tannins and flavonoids in Valeriana officinalis also have anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to soothe and relax the muscles of the jaw. The sedative and anti-inflammatory properties of Valeriana officinalis work together to promote relaxation and reduce muscle tension, making it easier to release the jaw and prevent clenching.

By promoting relaxation and reducing muscle tension, Valeriana officinalis tinctures can help to alleviate jaw clenching and related symptoms.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh or dried Valeriana officinalis root, 2 cups of 80% vodka, and a clean glass jar.
  2. Chop the Valeriana officinalis root into small pieces and place them in the glass jar.
  3. Pour 2 cups of 80% vodka over the root pieces, making sure they are completely covered.
  4. Seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
  5. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass bottle, and discard the solids.
