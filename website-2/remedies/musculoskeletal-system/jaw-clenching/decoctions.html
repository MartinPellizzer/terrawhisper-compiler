7 Herbal Remedies to Ease Jaw Clenching Symptoms

Jaw clenching, also known as bruxism, is a common issue where you unconsciously grind or clench your teeth, often causing pain and discomfort in your jaw and face.

This can be due to stress, anxiety, or even a misaligned bite. Fixing this issue is crucial because it can lead to headaches, earaches, and even tooth damage if left unaddressed. Herbal decoctions can be a helpful remedy for jaw clenching. One such herb is Valeriana officinalis, also known as valerian root. It's a natural relaxant that can help calm your nervous system and reduce stress, which is often a trigger for jaw clenching. Drinking a valerian root decoction before bed can promote relaxation and help you sleep better, which can in turn reduce jaw clenching during sleep. Another herb that can help with jaw clenching is Passiflora incarnata, or passionflower.

It's a natural sedative that can help calm your mind and body, reducing anxiety and stress that can lead to jaw clenching. Passionflower decoctions can be especially helpful for people who experience anxiety-related jaw clenching. By addressing jaw clenching, you can improve your life in several ways. For one, you'll be less likely to experience painful headaches and earaches. You'll also be able to sleep better and wake up feeling more refreshed. Moreover, fixing jaw clenching can help you manage stress and anxiety more effectively, leading to a better overall quality of life. To make a herbal decoction for jaw clenching, you can combine equal parts of dried valerian root and passionflower in a cup of boiling water. Let it steep for 5-10 minutes, then strain and drink before bed.

You can also add honey or lemon to taste.
