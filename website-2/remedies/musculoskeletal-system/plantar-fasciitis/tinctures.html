Runners with Plantar Fasciitis: 11 Herbal Tinctures for Pain Relief

Runners are more likely to suffer from plantar fasciitis due to repetitive impact on their feet, especially if they're new to running or have recently increased their mileage.

This can cause pain in the heel and bottom of the foot, making it hard to run or even walk. The pain can be sharp or dull, and it's often worse in the morning or after a long run. For runners, this can be a real problem - it can stop them from training, and even force them to take time off from running altogether. This can be frustrating and demotivating, especially if they're training for a big event.

When plantar fasciitis flares up, runners may need to rest, ice their feet, and stretch regularly. They may also need to change their running shoes or try orthotics to reduce the impact on their feet. However, for some runners, even these measures may not be enough to get rid of the pain. This is where herbal tinctures come in - they're a natural way to reduce inflammation, ease pain, and promote healing in the affected area.

By taking a small amount of these tinctures regularly, runners may be able to reduce their pain and get back to training sooner.

Glycyrrhiza glabra tinctures have been found to help alleviate plantar fasciitis due to its anti-inflammatory and anti-oxidant properties.

Glycyrrhizin, a key constituent in Glycyrrhiza glabra, has been shown to reduce inflammation and promote healing in the affected area. The tincture also contains flavonoids and phenolic acids, which have been known to exhibit pain-relieving and anti-inflammatory effects. To use Glycyrrhiza glabra tinctures for plantar fasciitis, start by mixing a few drops of the tincture with water and applying it to the affected area. You can also take it internally by mixing a few drops with a glass of water, but consult with a healthcare professional before doing so.

Take the tincture 2-3 times a day, and massage the affected area gently for a few minutes. As you start using the tincture, you may notice a reduction in pain and inflammation in the affected area. This is because the glycyrrhizin and other constituents are working to reduce inflammation and promote healing. You may also notice an improvement in your ability to walk and stand without pain.

However, it's essential to note that it may take a few weeks to see noticeable results, and it's crucial to be consistent with the treatment.
