Another herb to avoid is garlic (Allium sativum), as it can cause skin sensitivity and allergic reactions. Some people may be allergic to chamomile (Matricaria chamomilla), which can cause skin irritation and make foot pain worse. Clove (Syzygium aromaticum) oil can be too strong for foot skin and cause irritation.
