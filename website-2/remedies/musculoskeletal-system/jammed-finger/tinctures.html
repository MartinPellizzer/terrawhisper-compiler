When you drink an herbal tea made from these herbs, the active compounds are absorbed into your bloodstream, where they can help reduce pain and inflammation in your finger. 1. Arnica montana

Arnica montana tinctures contains bioactive constituents like sesquiterpene lactones and flavonoids, which are responsible for its anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties.

The sesquiterpene lactones, specifically thiarubrin and thiamin, help reduce swelling and pain in the affected area. The flavonoids, including quercetin and kaempferol, have antioxidant properties that promote healing and reduce inflammation. When applied topically, the active compounds in Arnica montana tinctures can help reduce pain and swelling in a jammed finger by promoting blood flow and reducing the formation of scar tissue.

This can help speed up the healing process and reduce the risk of long-term damage.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh Arnica montana flowers, 2 cups of 80% vodka, and a clean glass jar.
  2. Combine Arnica flowers and vodka in the glass jar. Make sure the flowers are completely covered.
  3. Seal the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks. Shake the jar every day.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.
  5. Store the tincture in the refrigerator and use 5-7 drops, 3-4 times a day, to relieve pain and inflammation in a jammed finger.
