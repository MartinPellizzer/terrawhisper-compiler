1. Glycyrrhiza glabra

Glycyrrhiza glabra tinctures contains glycyrrhizin, a potent bioactive constituent with anti-inflammatory properties.

Glycyrrhizin has been shown to inhibit the production of prostaglandins, hormone-like substances that cause swelling and pain in the joints. The tannins present in Glycyrrhiza glabra tinctures also have astringent properties, which help to reduce inflammation and alleviate swelling in the knees. Additionally, the flavonoids in Glycyrrhiza glabra tinctures possess antioxidant properties, which help to neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress in the body, further contributing to reduced swelling and inflammation.

By reducing inflammation and oxidative stress, Glycyrrhiza glabra tinctures may be effective in alleviating knee swelling.

  1. Gather 500ml of vodka and 100g of dried Glycyrrhiza glabra root. Clean a glass jar and a glass measuring cup.
  2. Cut the dried Glycyrrhiza glabra root into small pieces and put them into the glass jar.
  3. Pour 500ml of vodka over the dried root pieces in the jar. Make sure the root is completely covered by vodka.
  4. Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks. Shake the jar every day to mix the vodka and root.
  5. After 2-3 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another glass jar. Discard the root pieces and store the tincture in the jar.
