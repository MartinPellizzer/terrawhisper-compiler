Jaw Clenching and Bruxism: Causes, Medicinal Herbs, and Herbal Preparations

What are the causes of jaw clenching?

So, you want to know what causes jaw clenching?.

Well, there are a few main reasons. Bruxism, for example, is a condition where people unconsciously clench or grind their teeth, especially at night. This can be due to a misaligned bite or teeth, which puts extra pressure on the jaw muscles. Teeth grinding, on the other hand, is similar to bruxism, but it's more related to how we chew and bite our food. Some people grind their teeth when they're eating or even when they're stressed or anxious.

This can cause strain on the jaw muscles and lead to clenching. Stress and anxiety are also big contributors to jaw clenching. When we're feeling stressed or anxious, our bodies tend to tense up, and that includes our jaw muscles. This can lead to clenching or grinding our teeth, especially if we're not aware of it. Finally, sleep disorders like sleep apnea or snoring can also cause jaw clenching. When we're not sleeping properly, our bodies can become tense and restless, leading to clenching or grinding our teeth.

This can be especially true if we're sleeping on our backs or have a narrow airway.
