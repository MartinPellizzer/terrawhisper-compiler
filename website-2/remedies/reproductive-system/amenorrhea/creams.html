1. Paeonia lactiflora

Paeonia lactiflora creams contains the active constituents paeoniflorin and paeonol, which are responsible for its therapeutic effects.

Paeoniflorin has a sedative effect on the uterus, helping to regulate menstrual cycles and alleviate menstrual cramps. Paeonol, on the other hand, has anti-inflammatory properties that help to reduce swelling and pain associated with amenorrhea. The creams also contain paeonol acetate, which has a uterotonic effect, helping to stimulate menstrual flow and regulate the menstrual cycle.

By regulating the menstrual cycle and alleviating menstrual cramps, Paeonia lactiflora creams can help to address the symptoms of amenorrhea.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh Paeonia lactiflora flowers, 1 cup of water, and a clean glass jar.
  2. Combine the Paeonia lactiflora flowers and water in a saucepan. Heat over low heat for 10 minutes.
  3. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into the glass jar. Discard the solids.
  4. Add 2 tablespoons of coconut oil and 2 tablespoons of beeswax to the liquid mixture. Heat gently until the beeswax is melted.
  5. Pour the mixture into small containers and let cool. Store in a cool, dry place. Use as needed to treat amenorrhea.
