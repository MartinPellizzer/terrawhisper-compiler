9 Herbal Creams For Premenstrual Syndrome

For many adult women, premenstrual syndrome (PMS) is a common and often frustrating experience.

It's caused by hormonal changes that occur in the days leading up to a woman's period, when levels of estrogen and progesterone drop. This can lead to a range of symptoms, including bloating, mood swings, breast tenderness, and fatigue. For some women, PMS can be mild, but for others, it can be severe and disrupt daily life. Women with PMS may find it hard to concentrate, experience irritability, or feel overwhelmed by everyday tasks. They may also struggle with relationships, work, or even simple things like cooking and cleaning.

As a result, PMS can take a toll on a woman's mental and physical health, affecting her self-esteem, relationships, and overall well-being. Herbal creams have been used for centuries to help alleviate the symptoms of PMS. These creams are topical, meaning they're applied directly to the skin, rather than ingested. They contain plant-based ingredients that work to calm and soothe the body, helping to reduce symptoms like bloating, cramps, and mood swings. By applying these creams to specific areas of the body, women can target the root causes of their symptoms and find relief. The creams work by interacting with the body's natural chemistry, promoting relaxation and reducing inflammation.

This can help women feel more in control of their symptoms, rather than being controlled by them.
