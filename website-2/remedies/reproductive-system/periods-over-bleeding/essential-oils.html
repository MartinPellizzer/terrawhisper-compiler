1. Lavandula angustifolia

Lavandula angustifolia essential oils contains linalool and linalyl acetate, which are the main active constituents responsible for its benefits.

These compounds have a regulating effect on the body's hormonal balance, helping to reduce excessive bleeding during periods. The antispasmodic properties of linalool also help to relax the uterine muscles, reducing cramping and heavy flow. Additionally, the essential oil's anti-inflammatory properties soothe the uterine lining, promoting a healthy environment for menstrual flow.

By regulating hormonal balance and reducing inflammation, Lavandula angustifolia essential oils can help alleviate heavy bleeding and related symptoms.

  1. Gather 2 cups of fresh Lavandula angustifolia flowers and 1 cup of carrier oil (like coconut or olive oil).
  2. Place the flowers in a clean glass jar and pour the carrier oil over them. Store in a cool, dark place for 2 weeks, shaking the jar daily.
  3. After 2 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.
  4. Add 10 drops of vitamin E oil to the liquid and mix well. Vitamin E oil helps to preserve the essential oil.
  5. Store the Lavandula angustifolia essential oil in a dark glass bottle with a tight-fitting lid. Use 5-7 drops in a warm bath or mix with a carrier oil for massage during periods.

2. Angelica archangelica

Angelica archangelica essential oils contains bioactive constituents like angelic acid, isobutyl angelate, and ligustilide.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, which help to soothe and relax the uterine muscles. The antispasmodic properties of angelic acid and ligustilide help to reduce cramping and spasms, while isobutyl angelate has a calming effect on the nervous system. The essential oil's ability to reduce inflammation and relax the muscles helps to reduce heavy bleeding and alleviate symptoms of menstrual cramps.

By promoting relaxation and reducing inflammation, Angelica archangelica essential oils may help to regulate menstrual cycles and alleviate symptoms of heavy bleeding.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh Angelica archangelica roots and 1 cup of carrier oil (like coconut or jojoba oil)
  2. Clean and chop the roots into small pieces, then fill a clean glass jar with 1/2 cup of the chopped roots
  3. Pour 1 cup of carrier oil over the chopped roots, making sure they are fully covered
  4. Seal the jar and let it sit in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day
  5. Strain the oil through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into a clean glass bottle, discard the solids and use the oil as needed

3. Vitex agnus-castus

Vitex agnus-castus essential oils contains bioactive constituents like aucubin, rosmarinic acid, and ursolic acid.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help reduce heavy bleeding during periods. The essential oil's flavonoids, such as vitexin and isovitexin, have been shown to regulate hormonal balances, particularly estrogen and progesterone levels, which can contribute to excessive bleeding. Additionally, the essential oil's coumarins, like scopoletin, can help improve blood clotting and reduce menstrual flow.

By regulating hormonal balances and improving blood clotting, Vitex agnus-castus essential oils may help alleviate symptoms of over bleeding during periods.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Vitex agnus-castus flowers and 2 cups of carrier oil (like jojoba or sweet almond oil).

  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.

Can drinking herbal tea prevent over bleeding during periods from forming?

Drinking herbal tea may help ease heavy periods, but it's not a guarantee it will prevent over bleeding.

Is it safe to consume herbal teas for over bleeding during periods every day?

Herbal teas can help with heavy periods, but it's not safe to drink them every day.

Some teas, like those with ginger or chamomile, can thin the blood and make bleeding worse.

If you're drinking herbal teas, limit them to 1-2 cups per week, and see how your body reacts.

How long does it take for herbal teas to show results in over bleeding during periods?

Herbal teas can help reduce heavy bleeding during periods, but it depends on the individual.

Some people notice a difference within 1-3 menstrual cycles, while others may need to try for 3-6 months.

It's essential to drink the teas regularly and be patient, as they work to balance hormones and ease symptoms naturally.
