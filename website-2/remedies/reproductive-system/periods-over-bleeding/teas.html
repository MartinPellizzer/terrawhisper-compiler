Period Problems? 8 Herbal Teas for Teenagers to Help with Over Bleeding

Some may even have to change their sanitary products frequently, which can be a hassle and a worry. Heavy bleeding can also lead to iron deficiency, which can cause pale skin, shortness of breath, and dizziness. It's not just physical health that suffers, but also mental well-being.

Teenagers may feel anxious, low, and self-conscious about their bodies. For many teenagers, heavy bleeding can be a source of stress and worry. Herbal teas can be a helpful tool in treating heavy bleeding during periods. These teas are made from plants that have been used for centuries to help regulate menstrual cycles and reduce bleeding.
