1. Vitex agnus-castus

Vitex agnus-castus tinctures contains the bioactive constituents aucubin, rosmarinic acid, and vitexin.

Aucubin and rosmarinic acid have anti-inflammatory properties that may help reduce bleeding. Vitexin, on the other hand, is known to affect the hormonal balance, particularly estrogen levels, which can contribute to heavy bleeding during periods. The combination of these compounds may help regulate menstrual cycles and reduce excessive bleeding.

By promoting hormonal balance and reducing inflammation, Vitex agnus-castus tinctures may alleviate symptoms of over-bleeding during periods.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Vitex agnus-castus flowers, 2 cups of vodka, and a clean glass jar with a lid.
  2. Combine the dried flowers and vodka in the glass jar. Stir well to mix.
  3. Close the jar and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks. Shake the jar every day to help the mixture.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar. Discard the solids.
  5. Bottle the tincture and store it in a cool, dark place. Use 5-10 drops of the tincture, 2-3 times a day, as needed to help with heavy bleeding during periods.

2. Cimicifuga racemosa

Cimicifuga racemona tinctures contains a number of bioactive constituents, including sesquiterpenes and alkaloids.

These constituents have anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties, which can help to ease menstrual cramps and reduce heavy bleeding. The alkaloids, particularly actein, are thought to stimulate the uterus and help it contract more efficiently, which can help to reduce bleeding. This combination of properties makes Cimicifuga racemosa tinctures a popular natural remedy for managing heavy menstrual bleeding and period-related discomfort.

  1. Gather 1 cup of Cimicifuga racemosa roots and 2 cups of 80% vodka in a clean glass container.
