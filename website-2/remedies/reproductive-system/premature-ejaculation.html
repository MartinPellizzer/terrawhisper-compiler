What are the causes of premature ejaculation?

Depression is another cause - men who are depressed often have lower self-esteem and a lack of interest in sex, which can make it harder for them to relax and enjoy intimacy. Stress is also a major contributor - when a man is stressed, his body is in a state of fight or flight, making it harder for him to calm down and enjoy sex.
