What are the causes of premature ejaculation?

Depression is another cause - men who are depressed often have lower self-esteem and a lack of interest in sex, which can make it harder for them to relax and enjoy intimacy. Stress is also a major contributor - when a man is stressed, his body is in a state of fight or flight, making it harder for him to calm down and enjoy sex. Relationship issues can also play a role - if a man is feeling insecure or uncertain in his relationship, it can make him anxious and lead to premature ejaculation.

Finally, a lack of sleep can also be a cause - when a man is tired, his body is more easily overwhelmed, making it harder to control his reactions and leading to premature ejaculation.
