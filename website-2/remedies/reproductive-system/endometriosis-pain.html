Curcumin may also help to block the production of certain chemicals that cause pain, such as prostaglandins. Additionally, curcumin has antioxidant properties, which can help to protect the body from damage caused by free radicals. By reducing inflammation and protecting the body, curcumin may help to alleviate symptoms of endometriosis, such as pelvic pain, heavy bleeding, and fatigue.
