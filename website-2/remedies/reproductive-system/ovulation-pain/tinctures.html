1. Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale tinctures contains gingerols and shogaols, which are active compounds that have anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving properties.

Ginger's warming effect can also help to soothe and relax the uterine muscles, providing relief from cramps and spasms.

Regular use of Zingiber officinale tinctures may help to regulate menstrual cycles and reduce the severity of ovulation pain.

  1. Gather 250ml of 80% vodka and 250g of fresh or dried Zingiber officinale roots in a clean glass jar.
  2. Add 1 cup of Zingiber officinale roots to the jar and fill it with vodka, making sure the roots are completely covered.
