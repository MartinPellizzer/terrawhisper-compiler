What herbal essential oils you should avoid if you have endometriosis pain?

If you have endometriosis pain, it's best to avoid certain herbal essential oils.

Also, avoid oils like Thyme (Thymus vulgaris) and Oregano (Origanum vulgare), as they contain compounds that may relax the uterus too much, leading to more severe pain and bleeding. Furthermore, oils like Bergamot (Citrus aurantium bergamia) and Lemon (Citrus limon) may not be suitable for endometriosis due to their potential to thin the blood, which could increase the risk of heavy bleeding.

Always consult a healthcare professional or aromatherapist before using herbal essential oils, especially if you have a history of endometriosis or other health conditions.
