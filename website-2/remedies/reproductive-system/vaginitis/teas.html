1. Echinacea purpurea

Echinacea purpurea teas contains active constituents like alkylamides, caffeic acid derivatives, and flavonoids.

These compounds have anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce swelling and irritation in the vagina, a common symptom of vaginitis. By reducing inflammation and fighting off infections, Echinacea purpurea teas may help alleviate symptoms of vaginitis and promote a healthy vaginal environment.
