Herbs like dandelion root, peppermint, and chamomile can help to soothe the digestive system and reduce symptoms.

Additionally, uterine fibroids can cause fatigue, which can be alleviated with herbs like ashwagandha, ginseng, and licorice root. These herbs work by improving energy levels and reducing stress, which can help to combat fatigue and promote overall well-being. Many women with uterine fibroids also experience anxiety and depression, which can be treated with herbs like passionflower, lavender, and St. John's wort. These herbs work by promoting relaxation, reducing stress, and improving mood. Lastly, some women may experience infertility due to uterine fibroids, which can be helped with herbs like vitex, saw palmetto, and red clover.

These herbs work by regulating hormonal imbalances, improving fertility, and promoting a healthy reproductive system.
