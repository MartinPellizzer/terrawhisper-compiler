1. Vitex agnus-castus

Vitex agnus-castus tinctures contains flavonoids, saponins, and iridoid glycosides as its bioactive constituents.

These compounds have estrogen-regulating properties, which can help balance hormone levels and reduce symptoms associated with fibrocystic breast disease. The saponins in Vitex agnus-castus tinctures have anti-inflammatory properties, which can help reduce breast pain and swelling. The iridoid glycosides have a regulatory effect on the menstrual cycle, which can help alleviate symptoms such as breast tenderness and lumps.

By regulating hormone levels and reducing inflammation, Vitex agnus-castus tinctures can provide relief from fibrocystic breast disease symptoms.

  1. Gather 100g of dried Vitex agnus-castus flowers and 1 cup of 80-proof vodka.
  2. Combine the dried flowers and vodka in a clean glass jar, making sure the flowers are completely covered.
  3. Close the jar tightly and store it in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar every day.
  4. After 2-3 weeks, strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids.
  5. Transfer the tincture to a dark glass bottle and label it. Store it in a cool, dark place and use 10-20 drops, 2-3 times a day as needed.
