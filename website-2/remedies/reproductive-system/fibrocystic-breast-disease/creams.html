1. Silybum marianum

Silybum marianum creams contains the active constituents silymarin and flavonoids, which have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties.

These properties help to reduce inflammation and oxidative stress in the breast tissue, which is often associated with fibrocystic breast disease. Silymarin, in particular, has been shown to have a beneficial effect on the liver, which plays a crucial role in detoxifying the body and reducing the buildup of toxins that can contribute to fibrocystic breast disease. The flavonoids in Silybum marianum creams also have a protective effect on the breast tissue, helping to prevent the formation of cysts and reduce symptoms such as breast pain and tenderness.

By reducing inflammation and oxidative stress, and promoting liver health, Silybum marianum creams may help alleviate symptoms of fibrocystic breast disease.

  1. Gather 1 cup of dried Silybum marianum flowers, 1/2 cup of coconut oil, and 1/4 cup of beeswax.
  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a heat-proof bowl set over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Add the dried Silybum marianum flowers to the melted oil and beeswax mixture. Let it steep for 2-3 hours.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl. Discard the solids.
  5. Let the mixture cool and solidify. Once solid, scoop out the Silybum marianum cream and store it in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

2. Urtica dioica

Urtica dioica creams contains bioactive constituents like histamine, isothiocyanates, and lectins that help alleviate symptoms of fibrocystic breast disease.

  2. Melt the coconut oil and beeswax in a double boiler or a microwave-safe bowl in 10-second increments, stirring between each interval, until smooth.
  3. Add the dried Urtica dioica leaves to the melted mixture and let it steep for 2-3 hours or overnight.
  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth or a fine-mesh sieve into a clean bowl, discarding the solids.
  5. Add the vitamin E oil and mix well. Pour the mixture into a clean container and let it cool before use.

  4. In a small saucepan, melt the beeswax over low heat. Add the infused coconut oil and stir until well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into small containers or tin cans and let it cool and solidify. Use the Melissa officinalis cream as needed to help alleviate symptoms of fibrocystic breast disease.
