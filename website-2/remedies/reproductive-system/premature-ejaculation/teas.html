1. Ginkgo biloba

Ginkgo biloba teas contains flavonoids and terpenoids, which are active constituents that help improve blood flow to the penis.

These compounds also increase nitric oxide production, a molecule that relaxes blood vessels and allows for sustained erections. By reducing PAF levels and increasing nitric oxide, Ginkgo biloba teas help maintain a steady blood flow to the penis, making it easier to achieve and maintain erections.

Regular consumption of Ginkgo biloba teas may also help improve sexual performance and reduce the frequency of premature ejaculation.

  1. Gather 1 cup of fresh or dried Ginkgo biloba leaves. You can buy them at a health store or online.
  2. Use a coffee grinder or a spice grinder to grind the leaves into a fine powder.
  3. Mix 2 teaspoons of the Ginkgo biloba powder with 1 cup of boiling water. Let it steep for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea into a cup using a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth. Discard the solids.
  5. Drink 1 cup of the Ginkgo biloba tea 2-3 times a day to help with premature ejaculation. You can add honey or lemon to taste.
