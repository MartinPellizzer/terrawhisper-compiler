1. Tribulus terrestris

Tribulus terrestris creams contains a combination of active constituents like protodioscin, furostanol saponins, and flavonoids.

These compounds have been shown to improve blood flow to the reproductive system, increase sperm count and motility, and enhance testosterone production. Protodioscin, in particular, is known for its ability to stimulate the release of GnRH, a hormone that regulates sperm production. The flavonoids present in Tribulus terrestris creams have antioxidant properties, which can help protect sperm from damage and improve fertility.

By addressing these factors, Tribulus terrestris creams may help alleviate low sperm count issues.

  1. Gather ingredients: 1 cup of coconut oil, 1/4 cup of shea butter, 2 tablespoons of beeswax, 2 tablespoons of Tribulus terrestris extract, 2 drops of vitamin E oil.
  2. Melt coconut oil and shea butter in a double boiler or a microwave-safe bowl in 10-second increments, stirring between each interval, until smooth.
  3. Add beeswax to the melted mixture and stir until dissolved.
  4. Add Tribulus terrestris extract and vitamin E oil to the mixture. Stir well to combine.
  5. Pour the mixture into small containers and let it cool and solidify. Store in a cool, dry place.
