1. Panax ginseng

Panax ginseng tinctures contains bioactive constituents like ginsenosides, which are responsible for its medicinal properties.

These compounds have been shown to improve blood flow and reduce oxidative stress in the body. Ginsenosides also have anti-inflammatory properties, which can help protect sperm cells from damage and promote healthy sperm production. Additionally, Panax ginseng tinctures contain other compounds like panaxatriol and panaxadiol, which have been found to improve sperm motility and increase sperm count.

By improving blood flow and reducing inflammation, Panax ginseng tinctures may help support healthy sperm production and count.

  1. Gather 100g of dried Panax ginseng root, 70% vodka (1 cup), and a clean glass jar with a lid.
  2. Place the dried ginseng root in the glass jar.
  3. Pour 1 cup of 70% vodka over the ginseng root, making sure the root is fully covered.
  4. Store the jar in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks, shaking the jar daily.
  5. Strain the liquid through a cheesecloth or a coffee filter into another clean glass jar, discarding the solids. Use 5-7 drops of the tincture per day, mixed with water or taken as is.
