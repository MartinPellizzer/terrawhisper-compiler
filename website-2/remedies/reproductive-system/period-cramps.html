Zingiber officinale

Zingiber officinale, also known as ginger, is a natural remedy that helps with period cramps. It has a chemical called gingerol, which makes the muscles relax and reduces pain. When you have period cramps, the uterus contracts to push out the lining of the womb, which can be very painful. Ginger helps to block the chemicals in the body that cause these contractions, so the uterus doesn't contract as much. This makes the cramps less severe and more manageable. Ginger also has anti-inflammatory properties, which help to reduce swelling and pain in the lower abdomen. Some people take ginger in the form of tea, capsules, or even add it to their food to help with period cramps. Many women have found that ginger helps to make their period cramps less intense and more bearable.
