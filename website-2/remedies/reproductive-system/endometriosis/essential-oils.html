Natural solutions for endometriosis in the childbearing age group: 10 herbal essential oils

For women in their childbearing years, endometriosis can be a major issue.

During this time, their bodies are preparing for pregnancy, and hormonal changes are more frequent. Endometriosis can make everyday life unbearable for women in this age group. They may experience severe cramps, heavy bleeding, and bowel or urinary problems. It's not uncommon for them to miss work or school, or even cancel social plans due to their symptoms. Endometriosis can also make it difficult for them to conceive, leading to emotional distress and feelings of isolation.
