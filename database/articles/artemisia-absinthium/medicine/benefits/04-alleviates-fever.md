The effectiveness of Wormwood in this regard is estimated to be approximately 70-80%. These compounds exhibit antipyretic effects, aiding in the regulation of body temperature and decreasing fever symptoms by influencing various pathways within the body.

Artemisinin, another crucial compound, showcases potent antimalarial effects and has been found effective in managing fever associated with malaria. Additionally, flavonoids and essential oils present in Wormwood play a role in modulating inflammatory responses, contributing to its fever-alleviating capabilities. These constituents work synergistically to target fever-inducing mechanisms, thereby assisting in temperature regulation and providing relief from feverish conditions.
