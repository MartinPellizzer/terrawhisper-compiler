Moreover, its application accelerates wound healing due to antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory effects, expediting the recovery process. As a mild sedative, Wormwood aids in managing anxiety and improving sleep quality when consumed appropriately.

Overall, Wormwood's multifaceted medicinal properties offer a wide range of health benefits. The empirical evidence supporting these benefits underlines Wormwood's significance in traditional medicine and suggests its potential for various therapeutic applications.
