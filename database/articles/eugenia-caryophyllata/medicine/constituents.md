Eugenol is the main active ingredient found in cloves and it's a chemical compound that has been extensively studied for its medicinal properties. This organic substance not only works as an antioxidant but also possesses significant antibacterial and anti-inflammatory qualities. It helps neutralize harmful free radicals, which are associated with many diseases like cancer, diabetes, heart disease, and Alzheimer’s disease.

In addition to eugenol, cloves contain a wide range of other compounds that contribute to their medicinal effects. One such compound is caryophyllene, which has analgesic, sedative, and anti-inflammatory properties and aids in relieving pain associated with muscle stiffness. Salicylates are another group of compounds present in cloves. They have been known to possess anti-inflammatory and antipyretic properties and can help reduce fever.

The essential oil extracted from clove buds is also rich in phytochemicals like beta-caryophyllene, trans-eugenol, and eugenyl acetate. These compounds provide potent antimicrobial effects that protect against a variety of bacterial, fungal, viral and parasitic infections. The oil also has analgesic properties, which can be useful for treating toothaches, dental pain, and other minor aches.

For skin conditions like acne or eczema, clove oil can help by killing the bacteria that cause such infections and reducing inflammation. Its antiseptic properties make it an ideal component to include in homemade beauty products like face washes, scrubs, toners, and masks.

Clove's ability to boost immunity is another important medicinal attribute. It contains vitamins A, C, K, and B6 along with minerals such as iron, manganese, and magnesium that are essential for maintaining good health. This natural remedy can help in the treatment of various respiratory problems like common cold and cough by providing relief from congestion, throat pain, and runny nose.

In conclusion, clove contains numerous medicinal constituents including eugenol, caryophyllene, and others with diverse health benefits that contribute to its widespread use in traditional medicine for treating various ailments.
