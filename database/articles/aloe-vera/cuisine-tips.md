When incorporating aloe vera into culinary creations, it is crucial to properly extract the gel and eliminate the bitter latex. Start by thoroughly washing the leaves, then carefully fillet them to access the transparent gel inside. Rinse the gel to remove any residual latex. Use the gel immediately or store it in the refrigerator for future use. Aloe vera pairs well with fruits, adding a hydrating and nutrient-packed element to juices and smoothies. Additionally, its gel can be diced and included in salads, providing a textural contrast and subtle flavor enhancement. However, moderation is key, as excessive consumption may lead to laxative effects due to the natural compounds present in the plant.
