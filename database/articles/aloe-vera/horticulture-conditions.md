Ideal growing conditions for Aloe vera involve a warm environment, with temperatures between 20 to 30°C (68 to 86°F). This succulent prefers sandy or loamy soil, enriched with organic matter, and excellent drainage to prevent waterlogging. Aloe vera is adaptable to different soil types but thrives in slightly acidic to neutral soil. When cultivating in pots, use a mix of cactus or succulent potting soil. Regularly monitor soil moisture levels and allow the soil to dry out between watering to prevent fungal issues. For optimal growth, maintain humidity levels below 50%, replicating the arid conditions of its native habitat.
