Aloe vera boasts a wide geographic distribution, thriving in arid and semi-arid regions. Its habitat includes a range of environments, from sandy soils to rocky terrains. This versatile plant is found in diverse locations worldwide, showcasing its adaptability to different climates and ecosystems. The shallow root system aids in water absorption, allowing Aloe vera to endure in regions with limited water availability. This distribution emphasizes its resilience and ability to flourish in a variety of settings, contributing to its status as a globally recognized and cultivated plant.
