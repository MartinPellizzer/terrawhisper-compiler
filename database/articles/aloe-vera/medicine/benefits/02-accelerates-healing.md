Accelerates healing refers to the capacity of a substance or treatment to expedite the body's natural recovery process from wounds, burns, or other injuries. Aloe vera, a succulent plant, contains various active components that contribute to this healing property. Polysaccharides, particularly acemannan, are major constituents in aloe vera gel. Acemannan stimulates the production of fibroblast cells responsible for collagen formation, thereby enhancing wound healing. Aloe vera gel comprises approximately 80-85% water, 10-20% polysaccharides, and smaller quantities of amino acids, lipids, enzymes, vitamins (such as vitamins A, C, and E), and minerals (including calcium, magnesium, and zinc). The gel's polysaccharides, particularly the presence of acemannan in concentrations ranging from 0.5% to 1.5%, play a crucial role in fostering the skin's healing process.

The healing acceleration properties of aloe vera are beneficial for various health conditions, including minor burns, cuts, abrasions, and sunburns. Studies have shown that topical application of aloe vera gel can expedite wound closure and decrease the healing time for first and second-degree burns. The anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties of aloe vera also contribute to its effectiveness in treating skin conditions like psoriasis and dermatitis, further aiding in healing processes.

For this specific health benefit, the gel extracted from the leaves of the aloe vera plant is primarily used. The clear, viscous gel found in the inner part of aloe vera leaves contains the highest concentration of beneficial compounds responsible for accelerating healing. This gel is directly applied topically to the affected area, promoting wound closure, reducing inflammation, and fostering tissue repair. The leaf's outer skin, while not typically used for healing properties, contains latex with laxative effects, but it's not directly related to the plant's healing acceleration benefits. Therefore, the gel extracted from the inner leaf is the key component used in harnessing aloe vera's healing potential.
