 A mixture of turmeric powder and water blended into a paste is commonly used in Ayurveda for its healing properties. It helps relieve skin problems such as acne, psoriasis, eczema, and other inflammatory conditions. The paste can also be applied to wounds and burns to promote faster healing.

Additionally, it can help protect against heart disease, cancer, and Alzheimer's disease due to its antioxidant properties.

 A natural oil made by infusing turmeric with carrier oils such as coconut or olive oil is used for its skin benefits. It can be applied directly to the skin to soothe inflammation, treat acne and promote radiant complexion. Applying turmeric oil to the scalp can also help prevent hair fall and maintain healthy hair growth.
