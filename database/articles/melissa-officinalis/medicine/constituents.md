Lemon Balm has many medicinal constituents, such as essential oils and polyphenols. The key ingredients in the essential oils are terpenes, flavonoids and citral, which contribute to its unique taste and odour while providing numerous health benefits. Essential oils extracted from Lemon Balm are known for their therapeutic properties, including antiviral, anti-bacterial, antifungal, antioxidant and calming effects that benefit one's overall wellbeing.

 Polyphenols in lemon balm have potent antioxidant properties which help to neutralize free radicals that can cause cellular damage to a host of systems within the body. These compounds, like rosmarinic acid and caffeic acid, are strong disease preventative agents, protecting against inflammation related illnesses such as arthritis and certain cancers.

 Apart from these, Lemon Balm contains bioflavonoids that provide numerous benefits to the body. Bioflavonoids are responsible for giving fruits and vegetables their bright colours. In this case, they contribute to lemon balm's vibrant appearance as well as providing essential health benefits. Among its primary functions, bioflavonoids increase vascular integrity by strengthening capillary walls which reduce blood loss during injury or surgery. They also possess antimicrobial properties that help fight off infections and support the immune system.

 Furthermore, lemon balm is rich in vitamins such as Vitamin A, C, E, K, B1 (Thiamin), B2 (Riboflavin), B3 (Niacin) which plays an important role in various body functions like maintaining a healthy vision, regulating metabolism and boosting the immune system. The minerals found in lemon balm include iron, calcium, magnesium, potassium, manganese, phosphorus and zinc that are essential for bodily processes such as cellular function and muscular activity.

 Lemon Balm's unique combination of medicinal constituents make it a valuable natural remedy that not only has calming effects on the nervous system but also provides a wealth of antioxidants, anti-inflammatory compounds, immune-boosting elements and nutrients to protect your overall health. This versatile herb can be consumed in various ways, including as tea, tinctures or even added to food for a delicious dose of its health benefits.
