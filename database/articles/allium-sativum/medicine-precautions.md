While garlic is generally considered safe for consumption, precautions should be taken, especially for individuals with bleeding disorders or those scheduled for surgery, as garlic may enhance the risk of bleeding. High doses of garlic supplements can cause gastrointestinal discomfort, and allergic reactions are possible in some individuals. It is advisable to consult with a healthcare professional before incorporating garlic into a medicinal regimen, particularly for those taking anticoagulant medications or with known allergies to Allium species. Overall, when used judiciously, garlic can be a valuable addition to a holistic approach to health and wellness.
