Senna has many medicinal constituents, such as Anthraquinone Glycosides which help stimulate the bowel movements, leading to better digestion and reduced constipation. Chrysophanol and Emodin are two more components that have laxative effects, helping to ease bowel movement in the intestines. Rhein is a compound that helps reduce inflammation, so it's good for treating gastrointestinal disorders. Sennoside A, B, and M are special types of sennosides that act as the bowel stimulants. Alkaloids, Flavonoids, Tannins are other components found in Senna which offer a variety of health benefits like antioxidant properties, pain relief, anti-inflammatory effects, and more.

         To summarize, these constituents work together to bring various positive health effects through enhanced digestion, constipation relief, inflammation reduction, and treatment of gastrointestinal disorders. The unique combination of all these compounds contributes to the overall effectiveness and diversity in benefits offered by Senna.
