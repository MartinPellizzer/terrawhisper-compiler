Ju Hua, also known as Chrysanthemum Flower or Flos Chrysanthemi, is believed to possess various therapeutic properties that can help improve both mental and physical well-being. In this context, we will explore some of the most notable benefits of ju hua.  

 Ju Hua contains anti-inflammatory compounds which may help reduce cough, congestion, and sore throat commonly associated with colds. It can also aid in breaking up mucus to provide relief from nasal congestion.

 One of the traditional uses for ju hua is its potential role in promoting mental well-being by acting as a mild sedative, helping to alleviate stress and anxiety. This herb can also be used to ease symptoms associated with insomnia. 

 The analgesic properties present within this flower have been known to provide relief from headaches and migraines due to its ability to reduce inflammation and pain in the blood vessels of the brain.  

 Ju Hua is believed to possess antipyretic effects, which help regulate body temperature by reducing fever. This herb has also been used traditionally for treating malaria, a disease that often causes fevers and chills.
