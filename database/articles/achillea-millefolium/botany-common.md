Achillea millefolium has many common names, but the most common is Yarrow. Other common names are Common yarrow, Milfoil, Thousand-leaf, Soldier's woundwort, Nosebleed plant, Old man's pepper, Staunchweed, Sanguinary, Western yarrow, and Knight's milfoil. A. millefolium also has many variants, such as Red Velvet, Paprika, Cerise Queen, Moonshine, Summer Pastels, Lilac Beauty, Coronation Gold, Saucy Seduction, Apple Blossom, and the Colorado Mix.
