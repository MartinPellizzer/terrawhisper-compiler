The herb boosts immunity through flavonoids and alkaloids, defending against infections and autoimmune disorders. Yarrow also alleviates anxiety and stress, containing sesquiterpene lactones and flavonoids with calming effects. Its support for respiratory health is facilitated by volatile oils and flavonoids, making it beneficial for conditions like bronchitis and asthma. Yarrow improves skin health by combating inflammation and promoting healing, while its circulation-enhancing properties aid in conditions like peripheral vascular diseases.
