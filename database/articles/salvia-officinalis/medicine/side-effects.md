Sage can have some side effects if used improperly, such as weakening one's memory, causing issues such as dizziness and difficulties concentrating. These unwanted results can stem from the misuse or abuse of sage, which might lead to more severe impacts. First on our list is a decline in cognitive function, where an individual may find it harder to recall memories and information they once knew. This issue is closely linked to other consequences, like dizziness, as the mind’s ability to process and maintain balance can be affected by sage's adverse effects. 

 Another potential outcome of using sage improperly is a deterioration in an individual's concentration levels. Concentration is essential for completing tasks and making decisions throughout our daily lives. If this aspect is impaired, it can result in problems both at home and work. Additionally, some people may experience vomiting due to the use of sage. This can be a sign that the body is trying to rid itself of potentially harmful compounds within the herb or to counteract any negative effects it's having on the individual. 

 Sage has also been known to cause hallucinations, particularly in large quantities or when used by those with underlying mental health issues. This can be a dangerous side effect as it may lead to confusion and disorientation, which could potentially put an individual at risk. Furthermore, sage use might result in insomnia, interrupting sleep patterns and overall well-being. This can have a cascading effect on other areas of one's life, such as work performance or relationships. 

 When misused, sage has the potential to trigger anxiety attacks in those who are predisposed to experiencing anxiety disorders. This can be especially problematic for those trying to manage their mental health. Sage can also impact an individual's vision negatively, potentially causing headaches, blurred vision, or even temporary blindness. 

 Lastly, if sage is applied topically, it may cause skin irritation or an allergic reaction. These adverse effects highlight the importance of proper use and understanding of this herb and the significance of seeking medical advice before incorporating it into one's lifestyle.
