
Dong Quai is widely used in modern medicine for various health purposes. Primarily recognized for its potential in women's health, it's utilized to regulate menstrual cycles, alleviate menopausal symptoms, and address conditions like dysmenorrhea. Research suggests its compounds exhibit estrogenic effects, aiding in hormonal balance. Studies indicate its effectiveness in treating gynecological disorders, with doses ranging from 1 to 6 grams of Dong Quai powder daily, demonstrating positive outcomes in managing menopausal symptoms and menstrual irregularities.

This plant contains various active compounds, including ferulic acid, ligustilide, and polysaccharides, contributing to its medicinal properties. Ligustilide, the primary bioactive component, exhibits anti-inflammatory and vasodilatory effects, while ferulic acid displays antioxidant properties, potentially reducing oxidative stress. Polysaccharides found in Dong Quai possess immune-enhancing characteristics, aiding in overall health and potentially supporting the immune system.

Dong Quai is available in diverse medicinal preparations, such as teas, capsules, tinctures, and creams. Dong Quai tea, made from dried roots, is used for menstrual regulation, while tinctures, often in alcohol solutions, offer concentrated doses for hormonal balance. Capsules containing Dong Quai powder provide a convenient dosage form for reproductive health. Creams and oils, applied topically, aim to alleviate pain and reduce inflammation, benefiting skin and muscular issues. Additionally, Dong Quai extracts, pills, syrups, and poultices are tailored for various health concerns, offering versatility in consumption and application methods.

While Dong Quai is generally considered safe, precautions are essential. Excessive doses may lead to side effects such as nausea, diarrhea, or skin sensitivity. Pregnant women and individuals with bleeding disorders should avoid its use. Interactions with blood-thinning medications are also noted. It's crucial to consult a healthcare professional before using Dong Quai, especially in combination with other medications or supplements, to prevent adverse reactions and ensure safe usage.
