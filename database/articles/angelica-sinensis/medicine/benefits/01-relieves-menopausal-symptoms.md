Dong Quai's ability to alleviate menopausal symptoms refers to its potential in mitigating the various discomforts experienced during menopause, including hot flashes, night sweats, mood swings, and vaginal dryness. This benefit is attributed to specific constituents present in Dong Quai root, primarily its phytoestrogens and ferulic acid content. Phytoestrogens, such as ligustilide and butylidenephthalide, act as weak estrogen agonists, potentially helping to balance hormonal fluctuations during menopause. These compounds mimic estrogen's actions in the body, aiding in reducing the severity and frequency of menopausal symptoms. Dong Quai root contains approximately 1-2% ligustilide and 0.1-0.3% butylidenephthalide, contributing to its efficacy in alleviating menopausal discomforts.

This particular health benefit of Dong Quai aids in addressing menopause-related symptoms such as hot flashes, night sweats, mood swings, and vaginal dryness. By assisting in hormonal regulation, Dong Quai can potentially alleviate these discomforts and improve the overall quality of life for women experiencing menopausal symptoms. The phytoestrogenic activity of Dong Quai's constituents offers a natural alternative for managing these symptoms without the use of synthetic hormone replacement therapies (HRT), which may pose certain risks and side effects.

The part of Dong Quai predominantly utilized to relieve menopausal symptoms is the root. Extracts derived from Dong Quai root, commonly prepared in various forms such as capsules, tinctures, teas, and pills, concentrate the phytoestrogenic compounds, facilitating their administration and absorption. These preparations ensure an adequate intake of ligustilide and butylidenephthalide, enabling individuals to harness the plant's menopause-relieving properties effectively.
