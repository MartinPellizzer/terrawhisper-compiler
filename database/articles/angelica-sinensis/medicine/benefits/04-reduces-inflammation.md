
Dong Quai's ability to aid respiratory health refers to its capacity to mitigate inflammation, a complex biological response to harmful stimuli such as pathogens, irritants, or tissue injury. Inflammation is characterized by redness, swelling, pain, and heat in the affected area. Dong Quai contains various constituents contributing to its anti-inflammatory properties, notably ferulic acid, ligustilide, and polysaccharides. Ferulic acid, a phenolic compound present in Dong Quai, exhibits potent anti-inflammatory effects by inhibiting pro-inflammatory markers. Studies suggest Dong Quai contains ligustilide, which has demonstrated anti-inflammatory actions by suppressing inflammatory cytokines. Additionally, polysaccharides found in Dong Quai possess immunomodulatory properties that can reduce inflammation by modulating immune responses. The quantities of these constituents vary across Dong Quai preparations, with ferulic acid reported at approximately 0.15-0.20% of the dried root weight, while ligustilide concentrations can range from 0.03% to 1.2%.

This anti-inflammatory benefit of Dong Quai contributes to alleviating various health conditions associated with inflammation. It aids in reducing symptoms of respiratory issues like bronchitis, asthma, and coughs by soothing inflammation in the respiratory tract. Furthermore, Dong Quai's anti-inflammatory properties have been explored in managing arthritis, rheumatism, and other inflammatory joint conditions. Its potential to alleviate inflammatory responses extends to skin conditions like eczema and dermatitis, offering relief from skin inflammation and discomfort.

Various parts of the Dong Quai plant are utilized to harness its anti-inflammatory properties. The root is primarily employed in different preparations like teas, capsules, tinctures, and poultices to address inflammation-related health concerns. These preparations extract the bioactive compounds, including ferulic acid, ligustilide, and polysaccharides, concentrating their anti-inflammatory potential for therapeutic use. Additionally, Dong Quai oil and creams derived from the root are applied topically to target localized inflammation, providing relief from muscle or joint discomfort.
