
Dong Quai's ability to aid respiratory health refers to its potential in strengthening the immune system, fortifying the body's defense mechanisms against pathogens and infections. This plant contains bioactive compounds such as ferulic acid, polysaccharides, and phthalides, particularly ligustilide, which contribute significantly to its immunomodulatory effects. Research indicates that Dong Quai's polysaccharides exhibit immune-enhancing properties by stimulating the production of immune cells, including macrophages and lymphocytes. Studies highlight the presence of ferulic acid in Dong Quai, showcasing its antioxidant properties, which play a crucial role in supporting the immune system by neutralizing free radicals and reducing oxidative stress. The quantities of these constituents vary across different parts of the plant, with the root containing higher concentrations of active compounds like ligustilide and ferulic acid.

The strengthening of the immune system through Dong Quai can assist in managing various health conditions linked to weakened immunity. This includes reducing the susceptibility to respiratory infections like the common cold, flu, and bronchitis. Additionally, Dong Quai's immune-enhancing properties may potentially contribute to minimizing the severity and duration of these respiratory ailments. Studies suggest that the immune-strengthening effects of Dong Quai could also be beneficial in managing allergies and asthma, where immune system dysregulation often plays a role in symptom exacerbation.

Primarily, the root of the Dong Quai plant is used to bolster the immune system due to its high concentration of active compounds. Both dried Dong Quai root and its extracts are commonly utilized in herbal preparations such as teas, capsules, and tinctures aimed at enhancing immune function. The root's versatility allows for various applications, including decoctions or infusions in teas and the extraction of bioactive compounds for the formulation of supplements that promote immune health. These preparations harness the immune-boosting potential of Dong Quai's root constituents to support overall immune system strength and resilience against infections and respiratory conditions.
