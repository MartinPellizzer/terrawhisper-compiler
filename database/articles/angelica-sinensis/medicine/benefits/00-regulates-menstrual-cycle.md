
Dong Quai's ability to regulate the menstrual cycle refers to its capacity to stabilize and normalize the menstrual process in women. This involves alleviating irregular periods, reducing menstrual cramps, and balancing hormonal fluctuations. The plant's constituents primarily responsible for this benefit are its phthalides, including ligustilide, which plays a pivotal role in its pharmacological effects. Dong Quai contains approximately 0.1% to 0.3% ligustilide by weight in its roots, contributing significantly to its medicinal properties concerning menstrual regulation.

This specific health benefit aids various menstrual-related conditions experienced by women, including irregular menstruation, dysmenorrhea (painful periods), and premenstrual syndrome (PMS). The compounds found in Dong Quai assist in restoring hormonal balance, regulating estrogen levels, and easing uterine muscle contractions. Clinical evidence suggests that the use of Dong Quai can notably reduce the severity and frequency of menstrual cramps, providing relief for women struggling with menstrual irregularities.

The root of the Dong Quai plant is predominantly utilized to harness its benefits in regulating the menstrual cycle. The root contains a concentration of active compounds, including ligustilide, ferulic acid, and polysaccharides, which are extracted and processed to create various preparations such as teas, tinctures, capsules, and extracts. These preparations are specifically formulated to target menstrual health issues and are administered in controlled doses to aid in regulating the menstrual cycle and alleviating associated symptoms.
