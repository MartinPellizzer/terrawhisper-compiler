Dong Quai's ability to enhance fertility refers to its capacity to improve reproductive health and increase the chances of conception. This effect is attributed to specific constituents found in Dong Quai, notably ferulic acid and ligustilide. Ferulic acid, a phenolic compound abundant in Dong Quai, demonstrates antioxidant properties and aids in regulating estrogen levels. Ligustilide, another active compound, contributes to vasodilation and improved blood circulation in the pelvic area. Studies have shown Dong Quai contains approximately 0.3% to 0.9% ferulic acid and 1% to 3% ligustilide, showcasing its potential impact on fertility by modulating hormone levels and enhancing blood flow to reproductive organs.

Enhancing fertility with Dong Quai can assist individuals facing various reproductive challenges. Its properties can benefit women dealing with irregular menstrual cycles, amenorrhea, or conditions related to estrogen imbalances. Men experiencing issues related to sperm quality or erectile dysfunction may also find some benefits from Dong Quai's ability to improve blood circulation and potentially enhance reproductive function.

For enhancing fertility, different parts of the Dong Quai plant are utilized. The root of Angelica sinensis is primarily employed for this purpose. It contains the highest concentration of active compounds, including ferulic acid and ligustilide, responsible for promoting fertility. Roots are processed into various forms such as teas, tinctures, capsules, and extracts to harness their fertility-enhancing properties effectively. Additionally, Dong Quai root is often combined with other herbs or supplements in formulations designed specifically to support reproductive health and boost fertility.
