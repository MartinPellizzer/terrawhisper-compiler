Dong Quai's ability to balance hormones refers to its capacity to regulate estrogen activity in the body. This is attributed primarily to its constituents, such as ferulic acid, ligustilide, and various phthalides. These compounds act as phytoestrogens, mimicking the action of estrogen in the body. Dong Quai contains approximately 0.1% to 0.9% ferulic acid, a phenolic compound known for its antioxidant properties. Ligustilide, found in Dong Quai in quantities ranging from 1% to 3%, contributes to its estrogen-like effects, aiding in hormonal balance. Phthalides, present in Dong Quai at concentrations of about 1.5% to 2.5%, further support estrogen regulation, contributing to its hormonal benefits.

This hormonal balancing property of Dong Quai is beneficial for various health conditions. It is particularly helpful in alleviating menstrual irregularities, including painful menstruation and irregular cycles. Additionally, Dong Quai's hormone-balancing effects are known to ease menopausal symptoms such as hot flashes, night sweats, and mood swings. Women experiencing hormonal fluctuations or imbalances during menopause or menstruation often find relief through the use of Dong Quai as a natural alternative to hormone replacement therapies.

Different parts of the Dong Quai plant are utilized to harness its hormone-balancing benefits. The primary part used for this purpose is the root. Dong Quai root, rich in bioactive compounds like ferulic acid, ligustilide, and phthalides, is dried and processed into various medicinal preparations such as teas, capsules, extracts, and tinctures. These preparations are specifically formulated to leverage the hormonal balancing properties of the plant root, offering individuals targeted support for hormonal health.
