
Visnaga's ability to relieve skin conditions refers to its capacity to alleviate various dermatological issues, including eczema, psoriasis, and allergic skin reactions. This plant contains active compounds such as khellin, visnagin, and essential oils, primarily found in the fruits and seeds, contributing to its therapeutic effects on the skin. Khellin, a major constituent in Visnaga, exhibits anti-inflammatory properties, while visnagin contributes antioxidant effects. In a 100-gram sample of Visnaga seeds, khellin content can range from 0.1 to 0.5 grams, while visnagin levels may range from 0.05 to 0.3 grams, varying based on the plant's growth conditions and maturity.

The relief of skin conditions through Visnaga's constituents assists in addressing various dermatological issues. Khellin's anti-inflammatory properties are beneficial in reducing skin inflammation associated with conditions like eczema and psoriasis, providing relief from itching and redness. Additionally, the antioxidant effects of visnagin aid in protecting the skin from oxidative stress caused by free radicals, potentially mitigating allergic reactions and contributing to overall skin health.

For the relief of skin conditions, Visnaga primarily utilizes its fruits and seeds. These parts contain the highest concentration of khellin and visnagin, making them crucial for preparing extracts, oils, or topical applications aimed at alleviating skin issues. Extracts derived from Visnaga seeds are often incorporated into ointments, creams, or lotions due to their potential to soothe and heal various skin ailments effectively. Moreover, these preparations might be used in traditional medicine or modern skincare formulations catering to individuals dealing with skin conditions seeking natural remedies.
