
Visnaga's ability to reduce blood pressure refers to its capacity to lower elevated blood pressure levels within the human body. This plant contains active compounds like khellin and visnagin, which contribute significantly to its antihypertensive properties. Khellin, found in the fruits of Visnaga, acts as a vasodilator by dilating blood vessels, enhancing blood flow, and subsequently reducing blood pressure. Studies have indicated that khellin content in Visnaga ranges from approximately 1.5% to 3% by weight, making it a crucial component responsible for its blood pressure-lowering effects. Visnagin, another compound present in Visnaga, contributes to this benefit by relaxing smooth muscles in blood vessels, further aiding in blood pressure reduction. Its quantities typically vary between 0.1% to 0.3% by weight in the plant.

The ability of Visnaga to reduce blood pressure is particularly beneficial for individuals suffering from hypertension or high blood pressure. Elevated blood pressure can lead to various cardiovascular issues such as heart disease, stroke, and kidney problems. By incorporating Visnaga into dietary or medicinal practices, individuals with hypertension can potentially experience a decrease in their blood pressure levels. This natural remedy offers an alternative or supplementary approach to conventional medications in managing hypertension, promoting cardiovascular health, and reducing the risk of associated complications.

The fruits and seeds of Visnaga are the primary sources of khellin and visnagin, containing the highest concentrations of these active compounds. These parts are often used to create extracts, tinctures, or supplements specifically aimed at addressing hypertension. The fruits typically contain higher percentages of khellin, making them a key component in producing medications or herbal formulations designed to alleviate high blood pressure.
