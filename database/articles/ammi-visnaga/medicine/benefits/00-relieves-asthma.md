
Visnaga's ability to relieve asthma refers to its capacity to alleviate symptoms associated with asthma, primarily by acting as a bronchodilator and aiding in smoother breathing. This plant contains active compounds such as khellin and visnagin, which are primarily responsible for its anti-asthmatic properties. Khellin, found in concentrations ranging from 0.5% to 1.5% in the fruits and seeds of Visnaga, helps in dilating the bronchioles by inhibiting calcium influx, leading to relaxation of the smooth muscles in the airways. Visnagin, another key component present in Visnaga, contributes to its bronchodilatory effects by similar mechanisms, albeit in smaller concentrations compared to khellin.

The relief of asthma symptoms through Visnaga benefits individuals suffering from various respiratory conditions, including bronchial asthma and bronchitis. Asthma, a chronic respiratory disorder characterized by airway inflammation and constriction, often results in symptoms like wheezing, shortness of breath, and coughing. Visnaga's bronchodilator properties help widen the air passages, facilitating easier airflow and reducing the severity of these symptoms. It aids in managing acute asthma attacks and serves as an adjunct therapy alongside conventional treatments to improve respiratory function.

For its anti-asthmatic benefits, different parts of the Visnaga plant are utilized. The seeds, containing khellin in notable concentrations, are primarily used in herbal preparations targeting asthma relief. Additionally, the fruits of Visnaga also contain these active compounds and are utilized in traditional medicine for their bronchodilator effects. Through various extraction methods, these parts are processed to extract khellin and visnagin, which are then incorporated into herbal formulations aimed at managing asthma symptoms effectively.
