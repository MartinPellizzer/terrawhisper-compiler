Neem has many medicinal constituents, such as limonoids, nimbidin, quercetin, kaempferol, gallic acid, saponins, glycosides, anthraquinones, amino acids, vitamin E. These compounds offer various health benefits to humans. Limonoids help fight against cancer and reduce inflammation. Nimbidin, an organic substance, is a strong antioxidant that prevents aging signs and protects cells from damage. Quercetin, a flavonoid, improves heart health by reducing bad cholesterol levels. Kaempferol is another natural element which is responsible for the immune system’s proper functioning. Gallic acid assists in detoxifying the liver while helping it function efficiently. Saponins have anti-inflammatory properties that relieve pain and also help in controlling blood sugar levels. Glycosides, anthraquinones, and amino acids are all essential for normal bodily functions. Lastly, Vitamin E is a well-known antioxidant with numerous benefits including healthy skin, hair, and eyes. Combining the effect of these compounds in Neem make it an excellent herb for improving overall health and well-being. Remember, incorporating Neem into your daily routine can lead to a better quality of life.
