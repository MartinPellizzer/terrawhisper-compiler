Sweet flag is a versatile plant that can be grown in your garden or landscape with relative ease. To cultivate it, start by selecting a sunny to partially shaded location with well-drained soil. You can propagate sweet flag through rhizome division or obtain potted plants for transplanting. When planting, ensure that the rhizomes are buried about 5-10 centimeters deep and spaced 30-45 centimeters apart to allow for adequate growth.
