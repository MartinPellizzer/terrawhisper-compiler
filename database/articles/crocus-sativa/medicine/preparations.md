To make this, one must soak around 8-10 threads of saffron in a cup of boiling water for about ten minutes to steep properly. Once the liquid cools down, it can be enjoyed warm or chilled as per preference. Saffron tea has several health benefits such as improved digestion, relief from cold symptoms and increased mental clarity, thus enhancing overall well-being.

 A saffron tincture is a concentrated liquid form of the spice containing about 45-60% alcohol. To make it, dried threads or powdered saffron are mixed with a base solution consisting of ethanol and water in equal parts. The mixture is then stored for several days under low light conditions to allow complete infusion. This preparation is used as a concentrated form of saffron's health benefits when needed.

The encapsulated form helps with maintaining the consistency and potency of saffron's healing components until used.
