What to know before using Lippia citriodora (Lemon Verbena)?

Lippia citriodora, also known as lemon verbena, is a shrub that is native to South America and is known for its citrusy scent.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, and can grow up to 10 feet tall. The plant's leaves and flowers are used in herbal medicine for their calming and anti-inflammatory properties.

Lemon verbena is often used to treat digestive issues, anxiety, and insomnia. Additionally, it has antioxidant and antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable addition to skincare products.

The plant's woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves are covered in tiny hairs, and it produces small, purple flowers.
