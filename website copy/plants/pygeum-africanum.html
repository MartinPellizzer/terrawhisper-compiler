What to know before using Pygeum africanum (Pygeum)?

Pygeum africanum, also known as African plum tree, is a plant native to southern Africa that has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating symptoms of an enlarged prostate, or benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH), due to its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. The tree thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers partial shade. Pygeum africanum is an evergreen tree that can grow up to 40 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a dense, rounded crown.

Its bark is dark gray and deeply fissured, while its leaves are oblong, glossy, and arranged in pairs along the branches.
