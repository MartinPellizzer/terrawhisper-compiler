10 Best Active Constituents of Zingiber officinale (Ginger)

Zingiber officinale, commonly known as ginger, has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its active constituents.

The most notable of these compounds are gingerols and shogaols, which have been found to have various medicinal properties. Gingerols, in particular, have been shown to have anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and analgesic effects.

Shogaols, on the other hand, have been found to be effective in alleviating nausea and vomiting, making ginger a popular remedy for digestive issues. Additionally, ginger extract has been shown to have antimicrobial properties, which can help to protect against infections.

Overall, the active constituents of Zingiber officinale make it a valuable resource in the field of medicinal plants.
