10 Best Health Benefits of Centella asiatica (Gotu Kola)

Centella asiatica, also known as gotu kola, is a herb that has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its medicinal benefits.

It is known to improve cognitive function, reduce anxiety and stress, and help heal wounds and skin conditions. The herb contains active compounds that stimulate the production of collagen, a protein that promotes skin elasticity and healing. Additionally, Centella asiatica has been shown to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, making it a potential treatment for various health conditions, including Alzheimer's disease, high blood pressure, and cancer.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the medicinal benefits and potential side effects of this herb.

1. Improves Cognitive Function

Gotu Kola improves cognitive function, meaning it aids in enhancing memory, attention, and overall brain health.

The main active constituents of Centella asiatica that contribute to this benefit are triterpene saponins, specifically asiaticoside, madecassoside, and brahmoside. The primary parts of the Centella asiatica plant that give this benefit are the leaves, which contain high concentrations of the beneficial compounds. The main medicinal preparations of Centella asiatica that provide this benefit include dried leaves for tea, powdered extract in capsules, and tinctures.

This benefit of Centella asiatica helps relieve health conditions such as Alzheimer's disease, dementia, and other cognitive impairments, as well as improving memory and focus in healthy individuals.

Centella asiatica improves cognitive function thanks to the active constituents listed below.

  • Asiaticoside
  • Madecassoside
  • Madecassic Acid
  • Asiatic Acid
  • Centelloside
  • Centellose
  • Chlorogenic Acid
  • Caffeic Acid
  • Rutin
  • Quercetin
