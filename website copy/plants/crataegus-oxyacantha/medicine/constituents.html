10 Best Active Constituents of Crataegus oxyacantha (Hawthorn)

Additionally, Hawthorn contains oligomeric procyanidins, which have been found to have a positive inotropic effect on the heart, increasing its contractility and pumping efficiency.

The plant also contains phenolic acids, such as chlorogenic and neochlorogenic acids, which have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties. Finally, Hawthorn contains triterpene acids, such asursolic and oleanolic acids, which have been shown to have anti-cancer and anti-diabetic effects.

Overall, the active constituents of Crataegus oxyacantha make it a valuable medicinal plant with a wide range of therapeutic applications.
