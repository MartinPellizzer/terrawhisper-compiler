Medicinally, it has been used in herbal medicine as a diuretic and to treat kidney stones. It is a low-growing grass that forms dense mats, making it a popular choice for ground cover in horticulture. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, but can tolerate partial shade.

Botanically, Cynosurus cristatus is characterized by its narrow, grey-green leaves and spike-like inflorescences, which are densely packed with small flowers.
