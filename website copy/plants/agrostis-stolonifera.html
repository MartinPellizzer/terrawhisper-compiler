What to know before using Agrostis stolonifera (Spreading Bent)?

Agrostis stolonifera, also known as creeping bentgrass, is a valuable plant with a variety of uses.

Medicinally, it has been shown to have anti-inflammatory and wound-healing properties, making it useful in the treatment of skin irritations and other minor wounds. In horticultural settings, creeping bentgrass is often used as a turfgrass in golf courses and lawns due to its ability to form a dense, low-growing carpet. Botanically, Agrostis stolonifera is a perennial grass that thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It has narrow, bright green leaves and produces inconspicuous flowers in the form of small, greenish spikes.
