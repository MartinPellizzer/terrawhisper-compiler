What to know before using Leonurus cardiaca (Motherwort)?

Leonurus cardiaca, also known as motherwort, is a herb with a long history of use in traditional medicine.

It is known for its calming and anxiety-reducing properties, and is often used to treat heart conditions such as irregular heartbeat and high blood pressure. In horticultural terms, Leonurus cardiaca prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, and it is a hardy perennial that can grow up to 4 feet tall.

From a botanical perspective, Leonurus cardiaca has distinctive square stems and serrated, heart-shaped leaves, and produces small pink or purple flowers in mid to late summer.
