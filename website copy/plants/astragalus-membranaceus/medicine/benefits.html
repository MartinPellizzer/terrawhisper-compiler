10 Best Health Benefits of Astragalus membranaceus (Astragalus)

Astragalus membranaceus, also known as Huang Qi, is a plant that has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.

Modern research has begun to uncover the various medicinal benefits of this plant, particularly its immune-boosting properties. Astragalus membranaceus has been shown to stimulate the immune system, helping to protect the body against illness and infection.

Additionally, it has anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help to reduce inflammation and protect cells from damage. Some studies have also suggested that Astragalus membranaceus may have potential benefits for heart health, including reducing blood pressure and cholesterol levels. Furthermore, it has been shown to have antiviral properties, and may be helpful in preventing or treating the common cold or flu.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the potential medicinal benefits of Astragalus membranaceus and its active compounds.

1. Boosts Immune System

Astragalus boosts the immune system, meaning it helps strengthen the body’s natural defenses against diseases and infections.

The main active constituents of Astragalus membranaceus that contribute to this benefit are saponins, flavonoids, and polysaccharides. The primary parts of the Astragalus membranaceus plant that provide this benefit are the roots, which contain high concentrations of the beneficial compounds. The main medicinal preparations of Astragalus membranaceus that give this benefit include dried root extracts, tinctures, capsules, and teas.

This benefit of Astragalus membranaceus helps relieve various health conditions such as the common cold, seasonal allergies, and fatigue, and it may also improve the body’s response to chemotherapy and radiation treatments.
