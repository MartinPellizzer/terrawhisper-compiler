What to know before using Epilobium montanum (Broadleaf Willowherb)?

Epilobium montanum, also known as mountain willowherb, is a plant with a variety of medicinal properties.

It contains tannins, which have astringent and anti-inflammatory effects, and has been used in the treatment of wounds, skin irritations, and digestive disorders. It grows well in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil. The plant is a perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer.

The seed capsules of Epilobium montanum are distinctive, containing tiny seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
