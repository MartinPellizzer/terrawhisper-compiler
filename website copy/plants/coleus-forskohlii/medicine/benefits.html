10 Best Health Benefits of Coleus forskohlii (Coleus)

Coleus forskohlii, a member of the mint family, has been used in traditional medicine for centuries and has been found to have several medicinal benefits.

One of its most well-known uses is for improving cardiovascular health, as it helps relax blood vessels and lower blood pressure. Additionally, Coleus forskohlii has been shown to increase testosterone levels and improve bone health, making it a potential supplement for those with osteoporosis.

It also has anti-inflammatory properties, which can help alleviate symptoms of asthma and other respiratory conditions. Furthermore, early research suggests that Coleus forskohlii may have potential as a weight loss aid, as it appears to stimulate the breakdown of fat in the body.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the extent of its medicinal benefits and potential side effects.

1. Improves cardiovascular health

Coleus improves cardiovascular health, meaning it supports the heart and blood vessels, promoting healthy blood flow and pressure.

The main active constituents of Coleus forskohlii that contribute to this benefit are labdane diterpene, namely forskolin, and rosmarinic acid. The primary parts of the Coleus forskohlii plant that provide this benefit are the roots, where the highest concentration of forskolin is found. The main medicinal preparations of Coleus forskohlii that give this benefit include standardized extracts, capsules, and tinctures.

This benefit of Coleus forskohlii helps relieve various health conditions, such as hypertension, angina, and congestive heart failure, by promoting healthy blood flow, reducing blood pressure, and improving overall cardiovascular function.

10. Aids in weight loss

Coleus aids in weight loss, meaning it can help individuals achieve a healthy weight by reducing body fat and increasing lean body mass.

The main active constituent of Coleus forskohlii responsible for this benefit is forskolin, a labdane diterpene that activates the enzyme adenylate cyclase, which increases levels of cyclic AMP (cAMP) in cells, leading to lipolysis or the breakdown of fat in fat cells. The primary parts of the Coleus forskohlii plant that contain this beneficial compound are the roots, which are typically harvested and dried for medicinal use. The most common medicinal preparations of Coleus forskohlii for weight loss are standardized extracts, which are available in capsule or tablet form and contain a consistent amount of forskolin per dose.

This benefit of Coleus forskohlii is particularly helpful for individuals who are overweight or obese, as it can improve body composition and overall health by reducing body fat and increasing lean muscle mass.
