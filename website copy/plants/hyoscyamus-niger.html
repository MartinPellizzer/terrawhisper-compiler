What to know before using Hyoscyamus niger (Henbane)?

Hyoscyamus niger, also known as Henbane, is a plant with a long history of medicinal use dating back to ancient Greece.

Its medicinal properties include being a powerful anticholinergic, making it useful in treating conditions such as Parkinson's disease and motion sickness. However, it is highly toxic and requires careful handling.

Henbane thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, and can grow up to 1-2 meters tall. Its botanical characteristics include blue-violet flowers with purple veins, oval-shaped leaves, and a distinctive unpleasant odor.

Despite its potential benefits, Hyoscyamus niger should only be used under the supervision of a medical professional due to its high toxicity.
