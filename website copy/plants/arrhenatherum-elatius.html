Tall oatgrass thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, and can grow in a variety of soil types, including sandy, loamy, and clay soils. It is a robust plant that can grow up to 1.5 meters tall, with long, narrow leaves and tall, branched spikes that produce small, brown flowers.
