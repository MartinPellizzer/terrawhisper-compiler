10 Best Health Benefits of Zanthoxylum americanum (Prickly Ash)

Zanthoxylum americanum, also known as Eastern prickly ash or toothache tree, has a long history of use in traditional medicine by Native American tribes.

The bark and berries of this plant are commonly used to make teas, tinctures, and powders for medicinal purposes. One of the most well-known benefits of Zanthoxylum americanum is its ability to alleviate toothache pain, hence its name "toothache tree." This is due to the presence of compounds called alkaloids, which have natural analgesic and anti-inflammatory properties.

Additionally, the plant has been used to treat a variety of other ailments, including digestive issues, rheumatism, and fever. Modern research has begun to explore the potential medicinal benefits of Zanthoxylum americanum, including its potential as an antimicrobial agent and its ability to stimulate circulation.

However, more studies are needed to fully understand the extent of its medicinal benefits and potential side effects.
