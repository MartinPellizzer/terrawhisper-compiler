10 Best Active Constituents of Zanthoxylum americanum (Prickly Ash)

Zanthoxylum americanum, also known as American eastern prickly ash or simply prickly ash, is a plant that has been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.

The active constituents of this plant include alkaloids, essential oils, and lignans, which are believed to be responsible for its medicinal properties. One of the key alkaloids found in Z. americanum is called synephrine, which has been shown to have stimulant and decongestant effects.

The essential oils in the plant contain compounds such as limonene and alpha-pinene, which have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. Additionally, lignans such as schisandrin and schisantherin have been found to have antioxidant and anti-cancer effects. Overall, while more research is needed to fully understand the medicinal properties of Z.

americanum, its active constituents suggest potential benefits for a variety of health conditions, including respiratory issues, inflammation, and cancer.
