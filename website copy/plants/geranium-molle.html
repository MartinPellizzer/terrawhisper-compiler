What to know before using Geranium molle (Wild Geranium)?

Geranium molle, also known as Mosquito Geranium, is an annual plant that thrives in a variety of horticultural conditions, preferring well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly its ability to act as a natural insect repellent, making it a popular choice for gardeners looking for a more organic approach to pest control. Botanically, Geranium molle is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and pinkish-purple flowers, which bloom from spring to autumn.

Despite its name, it is not closely related to the common geranium, but is instead a member of the Geraniaceae family.
