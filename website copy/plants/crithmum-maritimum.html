What to know before using Crithmum maritimum (Sea Fennel)?

Crithmum maritimum, also known as sea fennel, is a succulent plant that thrives in rocky coastal areas of Europe.

It has a long history of use in traditional medicine for treating various ailments, including digestive issues and skin conditions. The plant's fleshy leaves and stems are rich in antioxidants and have anti-inflammatory properties, making it a popular ingredient in modern natural health products. Crithmum maritimum prefers well-drained soil and full sun, and it can tolerate salt spray and windy conditions.

Its botanical characteristics include blue-green leaves that are small and fleshy, and white or yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.
