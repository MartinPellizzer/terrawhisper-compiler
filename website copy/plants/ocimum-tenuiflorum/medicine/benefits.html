10 Best Health Benefits of Ocimum tenuiflorum (Holy Basil)

Ocimum tenuiflorum, also known as holy basil, is a plant that has been used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for centuries.

Modern research has begun to uncover the medicinal benefits of this powerful plant. One of the most well-known benefits of holy basil is its ability to reduce stress and anxiety.

Studies have shown that holy basil can help to lower cortisol levels, the hormone associated with stress, and improve feelings of well-being. Additionally, holy basil has been found to have anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties, making it a potential treatment for conditions such as arthritis and other inflammatory disorders. Furthermore, holy basil has been shown to have antimicrobial and antiviral properties, which can help to boost the immune system and protect against infection.

Lastly, holy basil has been found to have adaptogenic properties, which means it can help the body to adapt to stress and promote overall health and well-being.

1. Reduces stress levels

Holy Basil, also known as Ocimum tenuiflorum, reduces stress levels by acting as an adaptogen, which helps the body resist the physical and chemical effects of stress.

The main active constituents that contribute to this benefit are oleanolic acid, ursolic acid, rosmarinic acid, eugenol, and cirsilineol. The primary parts of the Ocimum tenuiflorum plant that provide this stress-relieving benefit are the leaves and the seeds, which contain high levels of antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds. The most common medicinal preparations of Holy Basil used to reduce stress include dried leaves for tea, powdered leaves in capsules, and tinctures or essential oils extracted from the leaves.

By reducing stress levels, Holy Basil can help alleviate symptoms of related health conditions such as anxiety, depression, insomnia, and adrenal fatigue.
