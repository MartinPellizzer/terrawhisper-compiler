10 Best Health Benefits of Chamomilla recutita (German Chamomile)

Chamomilla recutita, also known as German chamomile, has been used for centuries in herbal medicine for its various health benefits.

It is most commonly known for its calming properties and is often used to treat anxiety, insomnia, and restlessness. German chamomile is also valued for its anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving effects, making it a popular natural remedy for conditions such as muscle spasms, menstrual cramps, and digestive issues like irritable bowel syndrome.

Additionally, it has been shown to have antimicrobial and antioxidant properties, boosting the immune system and promoting overall health. The flowers of German chamomile are commonly prepared as a tea, but it can also be found in capsule, extract, and essential oil forms.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new herbal remedies, especially for those who are pregnant, breastfeeding, or have allergies to ragweed or other plants in the daisy family.

1. Reduces inflammation

German Chamomile reduces inflammation, meaning it can help alleviate redness, swelling, and pain in various parts of the body.

The main active constituents of Chamomilla recutita that contribute to this benefit are several volatile oils, including bisabolol oxides A and B, matricin, and chamazulene, as well as flavonoids and coumarins. The primary parts of the Chamomilla recutita plant that possess this benefit are the flowers, which contain the highest concentration of active compounds. The main medicinal preparations of Chamomilla recutita that provide this benefit include dried flower extracts, essential oils, and tea infusions.

This benefit of Chamomilla recutita helps relieve various health conditions, such as inflammatory skin conditions, digestive disorders, and respiratory ailments, by reducing inflammation and promoting healing.
