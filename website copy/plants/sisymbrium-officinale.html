What to know before using Sisymbrium officinale (Hedge Mustard)?

Sisymbrium officinale, also known as London rocket, is a plant with a variety of medicinal properties.

It has been used traditionally to treat respiratory conditions, such as bronchitis and asthma, and it also has diuretic and expectorant properties. This plant is biennial and prefers well-drained soil and full sun. It can grow in a variety of environments, making it a hardy and adaptable option for gardens.

The plant itself has narrow, lobed leaves and small, yellow flowers, and it can grow up to three feet tall.

What are the medicinal uses of Sisymbrium officinale?
