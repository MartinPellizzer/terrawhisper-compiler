10 Best Health Benefits of Barosma betulina (Buchu)

Barosma betulina, also known as Buchu, is a plant native to South Africa that has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for its medicinal properties.

The leaves of Barosma betulina contain compounds with diuretic, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties, making it a valuable herb for treating a variety of ailments. One of the most well-known uses of Buchu is for urinary tract infections (UTIs), as it is believed to help flush bacteria out of the urinary tract.

Additionally, Buchu has been used to treat kidney and bladder ailments, as well as digestive problems such as indigestion and nausea. Recent studies have also suggested that Buchu may have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, which could make it useful in the treatment of conditions such as arthritis and asthma.

However, it is important to note that more research is needed to fully understand the medicinal benefits of Barosma betulina and to determine safe and effective dosages.
