10 Best Health Benefits of Ammi visnaga (Visnaga)

Ammi visnaga, also known as Bishop's weed, is a medicinal plant that has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

One of its most well-known benefits is its ability to dilate blood vessels and improve blood flow, making it a potential treatment for cardiovascular conditions such as angina and high blood pressure. Additionally, Ammi visnaga has been shown to have anti-inflammatory and bronchodilatory effects, making it a possible remedy for asthma and other respiratory ailments.

The plant contains several active compounds, including khellin and visnagin, which have been found to have antispasmodic and smooth muscle relaxant properties, making it useful for treating digestive issues such as colic and cramps. Furthermore, some studies suggest that Ammi visnaga may have anti-cancer properties, although more research is needed in this area.

Overall, Ammi visnaga is a versatile medicinal plant with a wide range of potential health benefits.
