10 Best Health Benefits of Ginkgo biloba (Ginkgo)

Ginkgo biloba, also known as the maidenhair tree, is a popular herbal supplement known for its medicinal properties.

One of the most well-known benefits of Ginkgo biloba is its ability to improve cognitive function, including memory, attention, and overall mental performance. This is due to its antioxidant properties, which help protect the brain from damage caused by free radicals.

Additionally, Ginkgo biloba has been shown to be effective in reducing symptoms of anxiety and depression. It may also help to alleviate symptoms of tinnitus, or ringing in the ears, and improve the balance and coordination of individuals with certain neurological disorders.

Furthermore, some studies suggest that Ginkgo biloba may help to reduce the risk of dementia and Alzheimer's disease, although more research is needed in this area.

1. Improves Cognitive Function

Ginkgo improves cognitive function, meaning it aids in enhancing memory, attention, and overall cognitive performance.

The main active constituents of Ginkgo biloba that contribute to this benefit are flavonoids and terpenoids, which have strong antioxidant properties that protect nerve cells from damage. The primary parts of the Ginkgo biloba plant that give this benefit are the leaves, which contain high concentrations of flavonoids and terpenoids. The main medicinal preparations of Ginkgo biloba that provide this benefit include standardized extracts, which are commonly available in the form of capsules, tablets, and liquid extracts.

This benefit of Ginkgo biloba helps relieve various health conditions, such as age-related memory loss, Alzheimer's disease, and dementia, by improving blood flow to the brain and protecting nerve cells from damage.

Ginkgo biloba improves cognitive function thanks to the active constituents listed below.

  • Flavonoids
  • Terpene trilactones
  • Tannins
  • Proanthocyanidins
  • Myricetin
  • Kaempferol
  • Quercetin
  • Isorhamnetin
  • Alaninic acid
  • Ginkgolic acid

3. Eases Anxiety

Ginkgo biloba eases anxiety, meaning it can help reduce feelings of worry, nervousness, or tension related to stress or anxiety disorders.

The main active constituents of Ginkgo biloba that contribute to its anxiolytic effect are flavonoids, terpenoids, and ginkgolides, particularly ginkgolide B. The primary parts of the Ginkgo biloba plant that provide this benefit are the leaves, which are rich in these active compounds. The main medicinal preparations of Ginkgo biloba that help relieve anxiety include standardized extracts, which are typically standardized to contain 24-32% flavonoids and 6-12% terpene lactones.

This benefit of Ginkgo biloba helps alleviate symptoms of anxiety associated with various health conditions, such as generalized anxiety disorder, panic disorder, and social anxiety disorder.
