10 Best Health Benefits of Schisandra chinensis (Schisandra)

Schisandra chinensis, also known as the five-flavor-fruit, is a traditional Chinese medicinal plant that has been used for centuries for its various health benefits.

One of the most well-known medicinal benefits of Schisandra chinensis is its ability to support liver function. The plant contains compounds that can help protect the liver from damage and promote the regeneration of liver cells.

Additionally, Schisandra chinensis has been shown to have anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties, which can help reduce oxidative stress and inflammation in the body. It has also been used to treat a variety of conditions such as fatigue, coughs, and insomnia.

Furthermore, Schisandra chinensis has been shown to improve mental performance and physical endurance, making it a popular supplement among athletes and those looking to improve their overall health and well-being.

1. Improves liver function

Schisandra improves liver function, meaning it can help protect the liver from damage and promote regeneration of liver cells.

The main active constituents of Schisandra chinensis that contribute to this benefit are lignans, particularly schisandrin, deoxyschisandrin, and gomisin A. The primary parts of the Schisandra chinensis plant that provide this benefit are the fruit and seeds. The main medicinal preparations of Schisandra chinensis that give this benefit include dried fruit, powder, extract, and tincture.

This benefit of Schisandra chinensis helps relieve various health conditions such as hepatitis, liver disease, and liver damage caused by chemicals or toxins.
