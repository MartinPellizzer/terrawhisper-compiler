10 Best Active Constituents of Schisandra chinensis (Schisandra)

Schisandra chinensis, also known as the five-flavor-fruit, is a traditional medicinal plant that contains various active constituents with potential health benefits.

The two major groups of active compounds in Schisandra are lignans and schisandrins. The lignans, particularly schisandrin, gomisin A, and wirearin, are believed to be responsible for the adaptogenic and antioxidant properties of the plant.

These compounds have been shown to protect the liver, enhance cognitive function, and improve physical performance. Schisandrins also exhibit anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and anticancer activities.

Additionally, the fruit of Schisandra chinensis contains various other bioactive compounds, such as flavonoids, phenols, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.
