10 Best Active Constituents of Chrysanthemum morifolium (Ju Hua)

Chrysanthemum morifolium, a flowering plant commonly used in traditional medicine, contains several active constituents that contribute to its medicinal properties.

The most notable of these constituents are flavonoids, which have been shown to possess antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antiviral effects. One specific flavonoid, chlorogenic acid, is particularly abundant in Chrysanthemum morifolium and has been linked to numerous health benefits, including improved cardiovascular health and reduced blood sugar levels. Additionally, the plant contains volatile oils such as camphor, borneol, and chrysanthemol, which have been found to have calming and anti-inflammatory effects.

Finally, the plant also contains triterpenes, which have been shown to have anti-tumor and immune-modulating effects, making Chrysanthemum morifolium a valuable medicinal herb with a wide range of therapeutic applications.
