10 Best Health Benefits of Chrysanthemum morifolium (Ju Hua)

Chrysanthemum morifolium, also known as Chrysanthemum tea, has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries due to its numerous medicinal benefits.

This herbal remedy is known for its anti-inflammatory properties, making it an effective treatment for conditions such as arthritis and skin irritations. It also has antioxidant properties that help boost the immune system and protect the body from damage caused by free radicals. Furthermore, Chrysanthemum morifolium has been found to improve cardiovascular health by regulating blood pressure and reducing cholesterol levels.

Additionally, it aids in digestion and can help alleviate symptoms of cold and flu, such as fever and sore throat.

1. Reduces inflammation

Ju Hua, or Chrysanthemum morifolium, reduces inflammation by inhibiting the production of pro-inflammatory cytokines and enzymes, thus providing relief in various inflammatory conditions.

The main active constituents responsible for this benefit are chrysanthemum acids, flavonoids, and volatile oils. The flower heads of the plant contain the highest concentration of these active compounds. The most common medicinal preparations include dried flowers for tea, tinctures, and powdered extracts.

This benefit of Chrysanthemum morifolium is particularly helpful in relieving inflammation associated with conditions such as arthritis, dermatitis, and respiratory infections.

Chrysanthemum morifolium reduces inflammation thanks to the active constituents listed below.

  • Chlorogenic Acid
  • Luteolin
  • Apigenin
  • Acacetin
  • Tannins
  • Flavonoids
  • Chrysosplenol D
  • Chrysosplenol C
