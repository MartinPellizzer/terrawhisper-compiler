Eucommia ulmoides, also known as the Duke Tree or Chinese Rubber Tree, is a plant with a range of medicinal properties and specific horticultural needs.

Eucommia ulmoides thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, and can grow up to 20 meters tall. It is a deciduous tree with simple, oval leaves that are arranged alternately on the stem, and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in the spring.

The bark of the tree is particularly valued for its medicinal properties, as it contains compounds such as geniposide and chlorogenic acid, which have been shown to have a range of health benefits.
