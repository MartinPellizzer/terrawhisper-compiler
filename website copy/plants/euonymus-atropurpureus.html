Botanically, Euonymus atropurpureus is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 15 feet tall and wide. It has ovate to lanceolate-shaped leaves that are dark green in color and turn a brilliant red-purple in the fall.

In the late summer, it produces small, greenish-white flowers that give way to pinkish-red, capsule-like fruit that splits open to reveal bright orange seeds.
