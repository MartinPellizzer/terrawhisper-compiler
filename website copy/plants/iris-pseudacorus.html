What to know before using Iris pseudacorus (Flag Iris)?

Iris pseudacorus, also known as yellow flag iris, is a plant with beautiful flowers that has been used traditionally for medicinal purposes.

This plant contains irisin, a compound with anti-inflammatory and anti-viral properties, making it valuable in traditional medicine. Iris pseudacorus thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers wet or moist soil, making it an ideal choice for garden ponds or wetland areas. As for its botanical characteristics, Iris pseudacorus has sword-shaped, dark green leaves and produces striking yellow flowers with purple-brown veining in the late spring and early summer.

Despite its beauty and medicinal value, it's important to note that Iris pseudacorus can be invasive in some areas, so it's crucial to monitor its growth and prevent it from spreading uncontrollably.

What are the medicinal uses of Iris pseudacorus?

Iris pseudacorus is used to treat a variety of ailments due to its medicinal properties.
