It has a variety of medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects, making it useful for treating skin irritations and promoting wound healing. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. Epilobium ciliatum is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its pink to purple flowers, which bloom from summer to early fall, add a delicate touch to any garden.
