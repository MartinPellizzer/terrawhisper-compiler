What to know before using Rehmannia glutinosa (Rehmannia)?

Rehmannia glutinosa, also known as Chinese foxglove or Chinese goldthread, is a valuable herb in traditional Chinese medicine.

It contains various medicinal compounds, such as catalpol and rehmannioside, which are believed to have anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and immunomodulatory properties. This perennial plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, and it requires moderate water to grow.

Rehmannia glutinosa has a sprawling habit, with purple or yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, and its fleshy roots are harvested for medicinal use.
