What to know before using Berberis vulgaris (Barberry)?

Berberis vulgaris, also known as European barberry, is a shrub that has been used for medicinal purposes for centuries.

This plant contains berberine, a compound with various benefits such as lowering blood sugar levels, reducing inflammation, and fighting bacteria. Berberis vulgaris thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. It is characterized by its spiny leaves, yellow-orange woody stems, and drooping clusters of red berries.

The plant's bark and root are often used in herbal medicine, and it has been used to treat digestive and urinary tract problems, as well as skin conditions.
