Capparis spinosa thrives in well-drained, alkaline soil and prefers full sun exposure. It is a hardy plant that can tolerate drought and saline conditions, making it suitable for cultivation in Mediterranean climates.

The plant's botanical characteristics include narrow, evergreen leaves, beautiful white flowers, and rounded, fleshy fruit that contains small, edible seeds.
