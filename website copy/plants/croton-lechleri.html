What to know before using Croton lechleri (Dragon'S Blood)?

Croton lechleri, also known as dragon's blood, is a tree native to the Amazon rainforest that has been used for centuries by indigenous people for its medicinal properties.

Its sap is known to have anti-inflammatory, anti-viral, and anti-cancer properties, making it a valuable resource in the development of pharmaceuticals. In terms of horticultural conditions, Croton lechleri prefers a warm and humid climate, with temperatures between 20-34°C and annual rainfall above 2000mm.

It can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a wide canopy. The tree has a distinctive red sap, which is where its common name "dragon's blood" comes from.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and entire, with a leathery texture and a glossy appearance.
