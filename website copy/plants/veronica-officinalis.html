What to know before using Veronica officinalis (Speedwell)?

Veronica officinalis, also known as speedwell, is a perennial plant with a variety of medicinal properties.

It has been used in traditional medicine to treat respiratory and digestive ailments, and recent studies suggest that it may have anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial properties. Speedwell is a hardy plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade. It typically grows to be around 1-2 feet tall and wide, and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, blue-violet flowers that bloom in the summer.

The plant is also known for its square, ridged stems and its fibrous roots.

What are the medicinal uses of Veronica officinalis?

Veronica officinalis has several medicinal uses, such as treating respiratory infections, digestive disorders, and skin conditions.
