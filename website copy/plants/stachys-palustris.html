What to know before using Stachys palustris (Hedge-Nettle)?

Stachys palustris, also known as Betony or Water Betony, is a herbaceous perennial plant that has been used for its medicinal properties for centuries.

Medicinally, Stachys palustris is known for its astringent, diaphoretic, and diuretic properties, and has been used to treat a variety of ailments including digestive issues, fevers, and skin conditions. In terms of horticultural conditions, Stachys palustris prefers full sun to partial shade and moist to wet soil, making it a great option for bog gardens or areas with consistently wet soil.

Botanically, Stachys palustris can grow up to 5 feet tall and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves and tall spikes of pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer.
