10 Best Active Constituents of Lobelia inflata (Lobelia)

Lobelia inflata, also known as Indian tobacco, contains several active constituents with medicinal properties.

The primary active compound is lobeline, which has been shown to have effects on the cardiovascular and respiratory systems. Lobeline acts as a respiratory stimulant, increasing the rate and depth of breathing, and may be used to treat asthma and bronchitis.

Additionally, lobeline has been found to have potential as a smoking cessation aid, as it can help to reduce cravings and ease withdrawal symptoms. The plant also contains other alkaloids, such as lobelanidine, lobelanine, and norlobelanidine, which may contribute to its medicinal properties.

However, it is important to note that Lobelia inflata can be toxic in high doses, and should only be used under the guidance of a qualified healthcare practitioner.
