What to know before using Corydalis yanhusuo (Corydalis)?

Corydalis yanhusuo, a perennial herb native to China, has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries due to its medicinal properties.

It contains alkaloids, such as tetrahydropalmatine, that have been found to have analgesic, anti-inflammatory, and sedative effects. This plant thrives in well-drained, humus-rich soil and prefers partial shade. It grows between 15-30 cm tall, with tuberous roots and bi-pinnate, lobed leaves.

The flowers are typically pink or red, and appear in the late spring and early summer.
