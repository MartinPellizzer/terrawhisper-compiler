What to know before using Arctostaphylos uva-ursi (Uva-Ursi)?

Arctostaphylos uva-ursi, also known as bearberry, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to North America, Europe, and Asia.

Its medicinal properties include being a potent diuretic, aiding in the treatment of urinary tract infections due to its antiseptic and antibacterial properties. This plant thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Arctostaphylos uva-ursi is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers, red or orange-red persistent fruits, and thick, leathery, dark green leaves that are covered with a fine white wax, giving it a dusty appearance.
