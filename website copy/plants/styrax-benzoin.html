What to know before using Styrax benzoin (Benzoin Gum)?

Styrax benzoin, also known as benzoin tree, is a tropical plant native to Sumatra and Java in Indonesia.

It is well-known for its medicinal properties, which include expectorant, antiseptic, and anti-inflammatory effects, making it a popular remedy for respiratory ailments, sore throats, and skin infections. Styrax benzoin thrives in humid, subtropical climates and prefers well-drained, fertile soil. It is an evergreen tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring large, glossy, and elliptical leaves, as well as fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the summer.

The tree's resin, known as benzoin, is extracted from the bark and used in perfumes, incense, and traditional medicine.
