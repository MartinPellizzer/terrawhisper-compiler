Panax ginseng, also known as Korean ginseng, has been used for centuries in traditional medicine for its therapeutic properties.

Its medicinal preparations typically include tisanes, tinctures, and capsules. A tisane, or ginseng tea, is made by steeping dried ginseng root in hot water, releasing compounds believed to boost energy, reduce stress, and improve cognitive function.

Tinctures, on the other hand, are concentrated alcoholic extracts of ginseng root, often taken for their adaptogenic properties, which help the body adapt to physical, chemical, and environmental stress. Capsules containing powdered ginseng root are also popular, used to support immune function, increase stamina, and promote overall well-being.

It is important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen, as ginseng may interact with certain medications and have potential side effects.
