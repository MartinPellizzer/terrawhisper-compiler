What to know before using Abies alba (Christmastree)?

Abies alba, also known as the silver fir, is a species of tree with botanical characteristics including needle-like leaves that are shiny green on top and blue-white below, and cylindrical cones that are purple when young and brown when mature.

This tree thrives in horticultural conditions such as cool, moist, and well-drained soil, and partial to full sun exposure. In terms of medicinal properties, Abies alba has been used in traditional medicine for its essential oil, which has antiseptic, anti-inflammatory, and expectorant properties, and has been used to treat respiratory conditions and skin diseases.

The bark and twigs of Abies alba have also been used to make teas and infusions to alleviate digestive issues and reduce fever.
