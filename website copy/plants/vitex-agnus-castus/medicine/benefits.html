10 Best Health Benefits of Vitex agnus-castus (Chaste Tree)

Vitex agnus-castus, also known as chasteberry, has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to treat a variety of health issues, particularly related to women's reproductive health.

Modern research suggests that Vitex agnus-castus may help regulate menstrual cycles and reduce symptoms of premenstrual syndrome (PMS) such as irritability, mood swings, and breast tenderness. Additionally, some studies have shown that chasteberry may be helpful in alleviating symptoms of menopause, including hot flashes and night sweats. Furthermore, Vitex agnus-castus has been found to have anti-androgenic effects, which may be beneficial in treating acne and hirsutism.

However, it is important to note that more research is needed to fully understand the medicinal benefits and potential risks of Vitex agnus-castus, and it should only be used under the guidance of a healthcare provider.

1. Alleviates menstrual pain

Chaste Tree, or Vitex agnus-castus, alleviates menstrual pain, meaning it can help reduce the discomfort and cramping that some women experience during their menstrual period.

The main active constituents of Vitex agnus-castus that provide this benefit are thought to be flavonoids, essential oils, and diterpenes. The primary parts of the Vitex agnus-castus plant that are used for this benefit are the berries, which are often dried and prepared as a tincture or tea. The main medicinal preparations of Vitex agnus-castus that are used to alleviate menstrual pain include extracts of the berries or a tea made from the dried berries.

This benefit of Vitex agnus-castus is particularly helpful for women who experience menstrual pain associated with conditions such as dysmenorrhea or endometriosis.
