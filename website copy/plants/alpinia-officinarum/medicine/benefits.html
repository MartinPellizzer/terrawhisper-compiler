10 Best Health Benefits of Alpinia officinarum (Galangal)

Alpinia officinarum, also known as lesser galangal, is a plant that has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its various medicinal benefits.

One of the most well-known benefits of Alpinia officinarum is its anti-inflammatory properties, which can help to reduce pain and swelling in the body. Additionally, this plant has been shown to have antibacterial and antiviral properties, making it useful for treating infections and promoting overall immune function.

Alpinia officinarum may also help to improve digestion and reduce symptoms of gastrointestinal disorders such as nausea, vomiting, and diarrhea. Furthermore, some studies suggest that this plant may have potential neuroprotective effects, making it a promising area of research for the treatment of neurological disorders such as Alzheimer's disease.

Overall, Alpinia officinarum is a valuable medicinal plant with a wide range of potential health benefits.

1. Reduces Inflammation

Galangal reduces inflammation, meaning it may help decrease redness, swelling, pain, and warmth in affected areas of the body.

The main active constituents of Alpinia officinarum that contribute to its anti-inflammatory properties include several bioactive compounds such as phenylpropanoids, flavonoids, and essential oils. The primary parts of the Alpinia officinarum plant that have been found to have anti-inflammatory effects are the rhizomes or underground stems of the plant. The main medicinal preparations of Alpinia officinarum that have been shown to reduce inflammation include extracts, powders, and tinctures made from the rhizomes of the plant.

The main health conditions that this benefit of Alpinia officinarum may help relieve include various inflammatory conditions such as rheumatoid arthritis, osteoarthritis, and other types of musculoskeletal pain.
