What to know before using Tylophora asmatica (Asmatica)?

Tylophora asmatica, also known as Tylophora indica, is a medicinal plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It possesses anti-asthmatic, anti-inflammatory, and expectorant properties, making it a valuable resource in traditional medicine for treating respiratory ailments such as asthma, bronchitis, and cough. This evergreen shrub thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, reaching heights of up to 6 feet with glossy, ovate leaves that feature serrated margins.

The plant's flowers are small and white, grouped in clusters, and produce seed pods that contain numerous small, brown seeds.
