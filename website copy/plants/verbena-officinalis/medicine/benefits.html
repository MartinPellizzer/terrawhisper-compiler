10 Best Health Benefits of Verbena officinalis (Vervain)

Verbena officinalis, also known as vervain, has been used in traditional medicine for centuries due to its various medicinal benefits.

It is known to have anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, and analgesic properties, making it useful in treating a variety of ailments such as arthritis, menstrual cramps, and headaches. Vervain has also been used to soothe digestive issues, including indigestion and constipation, and can be used as a diuretic to help remove toxins from the body. Additionally, vervain has been used to treat anxiety and insomnia, as it is believed to have a calming effect on the nervous system.

Its medicinal properties are often extracted through tea, tinctures, or capsules, and it is always recommended to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new herbal remedy.

1. Alleviates anxiety symptoms

The main active constituents of Verbena officinalis that contribute to this benefit are iridoid glycosides, phenolic acid, and flavonoids. The primary parts of the Verbena officinalis plant that have anxiety-relieving properties are the aerial parts, including the leaves and flowers.
