What to know before using Montia perfoliata (Miner'S Lettuce)?

Montia perfoliata, also known as Minnesota Scurvygrass, is a low-growing herb native to North America.

It has a variety of medicinal properties, including being high in vitamin C and historically used by Native Americans to treat scurvy. This plant prefers moist, well-drained soil and can be found in open woods, meadows, and along streams. Montia perfoliata has round, succulent leaves that form a basal rosette and small, white flowers with five petals.

It is a hardy plant that can tolerate a wide range of temperatures and light conditions, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
