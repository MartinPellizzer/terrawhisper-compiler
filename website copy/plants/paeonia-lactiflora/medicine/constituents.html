10 Best Active Constituents of Paeonia lactiflora (White Peony)

Paeonia lactiflora, also known as the Chinese peony, is a popular herb used in traditional Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties.

The plant contains various active constituents, including paeoniflorin, which is believed to be the primary bioactive compound. This compound has been shown to have anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and immunomodulatory effects, making it useful for treating conditions such as arthritis, muscle spasms, and menstrual disorders.

Additionally, Paeonia lactiflora contains antioxidants that help protect against oxidative stress and damage to cells. Other active constituents found in the plant include pentacyclic triterpene glycosides, tannins, and essential oils, which contribute to its medicinal properties.

Overall, Paeonia lactiflora is a valuable herb in traditional Chinese medicine, and its active constituents have been extensively studied for their potential therapeutic benefits.
