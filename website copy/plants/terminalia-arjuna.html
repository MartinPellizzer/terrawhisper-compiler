What to know before using Terminalia arjuna (Arjun)?

Terminalia arjuna, also known as the Arjuna tree, is a revered plant in Ayurvedic medicine, originating from the Indian subcontinent.

Its bark is renowned for its cardiovascular benefits, including improving heart function, reducing blood pressure, and alleviating cardiac debility. This tree thrives in a variety of horticultural conditions, preferring well-drained soil and a sunny to partially shaded environment. Terminalia arjuna is characterized by its tall, straight stem; compound, deciduous leaves; and distinctive, orange-red bark that exfoliates in papery layers.

Its further medicinal potential is currently being explored, with studies investigating its antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and anticancer properties.
