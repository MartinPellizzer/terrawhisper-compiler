In terms of horticultural conditions, Potentilla anserina prefers full sun and well-drained soil, and can be propagated through division or seed.

It is a low-growing plant, typically reaching a height of 10-30 cm, and has hairy, lobed leaves and yellow, five-petaled flowers that bloom in the summer.
