10 Best Active Constituents of Atropa belladonna (Deadly Nightshade)

Atropa belladonna, also known as deadly nightshade, contains a number of active constituents that have medicinal properties, the most notable being tropane alkaloids such as scopolamine, hyoscyamine, and atropine.

These alkaloids have anticholinergic properties, meaning they block the action of the neurotransmitter acetylcholine in the nervous system. This can be useful in medical treatments for conditions such as Parkinson's disease, gastrointestinal disorders, and certain types of eye conditions. However, it's important to note that these alkaloids can also have harmful effects in large amounts, including hallucinations, seizures, and even death.

Therefore, the use of Atropa belladonna and its constituents must be carefully controlled and monitored by medical professionals.
