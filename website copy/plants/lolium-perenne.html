What to know before using Lolium perenne (Perennial Ryegrass)?

Lolium perenne, also known as perennial ryegrass, has a number of medicinal properties: it has been shown to have anti-inflammatory effects, and it may also be useful in treating respiratory ailments.

This plant is a cool-season grass that grows best in temperate, moist climates and well-drained soil with a pH between 5.5 and 7.5. In terms of its botanical characteristics, Lolium perenne is a perennial that grows in compact tufts and can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

It has narrow, flat leaves and produces spikes of small, purple flowers.
