What to know before using Betula pendula (Silver Birch)?

Betula pendula, also known as silver birch, is a deciduous tree native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Its medicinal properties are extensive, including the treatment of skin diseases, inflammation, and fevers, due to the presence of betulinic acid and other compounds in its bark and leaves. Betula pendula thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, and can grow up to 30 meters tall.

Its botanical characteristics include bark that is white and papery, ovate leaves that are dark green and doubly serrated, and drooping branches that give the tree its name.
