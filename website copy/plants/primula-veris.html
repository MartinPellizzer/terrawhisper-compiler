What to know before using Primula veris (Cowslip)?

Primula veris, also known as cowslop primrose, is a medicinally valuable plant that thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Its yellow flowers, which bloom in the early spring, have been used for centuries to treat respiratory ailments, such as bronchitis and asthma, due to their anti-inflammatory and expectorant properties. The plant, which is native to Europe and Asia, typically grows to be between 10-30 cm tall and has a rosette of leaves at its base.

In addition to its medicinal uses, Primula veris is also a popular ornamental plant in gardens, valued for its attractive flowers and ability to attract pollinators.
