Withania somnifera, also known as Ashwagandha, is a popular herb in Ayurvedic medicine with a variety of medicinal preparations.

One common preparation is a tisane, or herbal tea, made by steeping the roots or leaves in hot water. This soothing beverage is believed to reduce anxiety and promote sleep.

Ashwagandha tinctures are another popular form, created by soaking the plant's roots in alcohol or glycerin to extract the active compounds. These tinctures can be added to water or other beverages and are often used for their adaptogenic properties, helping the body manage stress and boost overall well-being. Additionally, Ashwagandha is available in capsule or powder form, making it a versatile herb for various medicinal applications.

However, it is essential to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new supplement regimen, as Ashwagandha may interact with certain medications or medical conditions.
