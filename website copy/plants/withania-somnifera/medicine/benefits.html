10 Best Health Benefits of Withania somnifera (Ashwagandha)

Withania somnifera, also known as Ashwagandha, is a popular herb in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.

It has been shown to have numerous medicinal benefits, including reducing stress and anxiety. The active compounds in Ashwagandha, such as withanolides, have been found to possess anti-cancer properties and can help to regulate the immune system. Additionally, it has been shown to improve memory and cognitive function, as well as reduce inflammation and oxidative stress in the body.

Furthermore, Ashwagandha has been found to be effective in reducing blood sugar levels and cholesterol, making it a potential adjunct therapy for individuals with diabetes and heart disease.

1. Reduces cortisol levels

Ashwagandha reduces cortisol levels, meaning it can help regulate the body's stress and anxiety responses by decreasing the production of cortisol, a stress hormone.

The main active constituents of Withania somnifera that contribute to this benefit are withanolides, withaferin A, and several sitoindosides. The primary parts of the Ashwagandha plant that provide this benefit are the roots and leaves. The main medicinal preparations of Withania somnifera that give this benefit include powdered root, liquid extract, and capsules.

This benefit of Ashwagandha helps relieve health conditions such as chronic stress, anxiety disorders, and adrenal fatigue.

Withania somnifera reduces cortisol levels thanks to the active constituents listed below.

  • Alkaloids
  • Withanolides
  • Withaferins
  • Sitoindosides
  • Acylsterylglucosides
  • Sapogenins
  • Flavonoids
  • Tannins
  • Steroidal lactones
  • Rohitukine
