What to know before using Sargassum fusiforme (Hai Zao)?

Sargassum fusiforme, also known as Japanese seaweed or hijiki, is a type of brown algae that has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.

This seaweed is rich in dietary fiber, minerals, and trace elements, making it a popular ingredient in many health supplements. It is believed to have various medicinal properties, such as anti-cancer, anti-inflammatory, and anti-oxidative effects.

Sargassum fusiforme prefers cool, nutrient-rich waters and is typically found in the northern Pacific Ocean. It has a unique botanical structure, with branching, cylindrical fronds that can grow up to 30 cm long.

The seaweed's thallus is dark brown to black in color and has a distinctive, slightly sweet and salty taste.
