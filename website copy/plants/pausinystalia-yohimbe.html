What to know before using Pausinystalia yohimbe (Yohimbe)?

Pausinystalia yohimbe, also known as Yohimbe, is a tree native to western and central Africa that has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.

It is known for its medicinal properties, particularly in treating erectile dysfunction, as it contains a powerful alkaloid called yohimbine that can help improve blood flow and nerve signaling. This tree thrives in tropical rainforests, requiring a warm and humid climate with regular rainfall throughout the year. Pausinystalia yohimbe can grow up to 30 meters tall, featuring simple, ovate-shaped leaves and pendulous, bell-shaped flowers.

The bark of the tree is harvested for medicinal purposes, and it is important to note that it should be used with caution, as improper use can lead to unpleasant side effects.
