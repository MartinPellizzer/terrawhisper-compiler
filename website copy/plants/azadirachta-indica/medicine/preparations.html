Azadirachta indica, commonly known as neem, is a tree valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in various preparations such as tisanes and tinctures.

A tisane, or herbal tea, can be made by steeping the leaves or bark of the neem tree in hot water. This soothing drink is believed to have numerous health benefits, including boosting the immune system, improving digestion, and reducing fever.

Neem tinctures, on the other hand, are made by soaking the leaves or bark in alcohol for several weeks to extract the medicinal compounds. These tinctures can be used to treat a variety of ailments, such as skin conditions, dental issues, and inflammation.

It is important to note that while neem has a long history of traditional use, its medicinal benefits should be approached with caution, and it is always best to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new treatment.
