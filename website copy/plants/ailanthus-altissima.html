This species can thrive in various horticultural conditions, from full sun to partial shade, and in a wide range of soil types, making it a popular choice for urban landscapes.

A. altissima is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 80 feet tall, featuring compound, odd-pinnate leaves with 10-41 leaflets and small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in large panicles.
