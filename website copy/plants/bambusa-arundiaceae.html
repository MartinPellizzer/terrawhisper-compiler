Horticulturally, Bambusa arundinacea thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for landscapers and gardeners seeking a hardy and attractive plant.

The plant's botanical features include tall, slender canes that can grow up to 30 feet tall and 6 inches in diameter, with distinctive bulging nodes that give it the appearance of a swollen belly.
