10 Best Health Benefits of Apium graveolens (Celery)

Apium graveolens, also known as celery, has been long used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.

It contains a rich array of nutrients, including vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, which contribute to its medicinal properties. Its high water content and diuretic properties make it beneficial for promoting kidney function and helping the body eliminate waste.

Additionally, celery is known for its anti-inflammatory effects, which can help alleviate symptoms of conditions like arthritis and gout. Furthermore, celery seeds have been used to treat hypertension, as they are believed to help reduce blood pressure.

However, it is important to consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new treatment regimen.

1. Lowers Inflammation

Celery lowers inflammation, meaning it can help reduce redness, swelling, and pain caused by inflammation in the body.

The main active constituents of celery that contribute to this benefit are a variety of antioxidant compounds, including flavonoids and polyphenols, as well as the compound apigenin. The primary parts of the celery plant that have been found to have anti-inflammatory effects are the seeds, stalks, and leaves. The main medicinal preparations of celery that can help lower inflammation include celery seed extract, celery seed oil, and celery juice.

This benefit of celery can help relieve a variety of health conditions, including arthritis, gout, and other inflammatory diseases.

5. Reduces Blood Pressure

Celery reduces blood pressure, meaning it can help lower elevated levels of blood pressure, reducing the risk of developing complications such as heart disease and stroke.

The main active constituents of celery that contribute to this benefit are phthalides, which are known to relax tension in artery walls and improve blood flow, and coumarins, which have been shown to lower blood pressure. The primary parts of the celery plant that provide this benefit are the stems and leaves, which contain the highest concentration of phthalides and coumarins. The main medicinal preparations of celery that provide this benefit include celery seed extract, celery juice, and celery oil, all of which have been shown to have a positive impact on blood pressure.

The health conditions that this benefit of celery helps relieve include hypertension, or chronic high blood pressure, and prehypertension, or slightly elevated blood pressure that may develop into hypertension if left untreated.
