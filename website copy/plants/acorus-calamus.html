What to know before using Acorus calamus (Sweet Flag)?

Acorus calamus, also known as sweet flag, is a perennial herb with numerous medicinal properties.

It has been used in traditional medicine to treat digestive disorders, fever, and even mental ailments. This plant thrives in wetlands and can be easily cultivated in moist soil with partial shade. Acorus calamus is characterized by its long, narrow, fragrant leaves and yellow-green flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant's rhizome, or underground stem, is the part commonly used in medicine, known for its aromatic properties and bitter taste.
