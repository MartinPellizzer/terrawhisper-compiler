10 Best Health Benefits of Polygonum multiflorum (Fo Ti)

Polygonum multiflorum, also known as He Shou Wu or Fo-Ti, is a plant that has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries due to its numerous medicinal benefits.

One of the most well-known benefits of Polygonum multiflorum is its ability to slow the aging process and promote longevity. This is thought to be due to its high antioxidant content, which helps to protect cells from damage caused by free radicals.

Additionally, Polygonum multiflorum has been shown to have a positive effect on the cardiovascular system, helping to lower cholesterol levels and improve circulation. It has also been used to treat a variety of other health conditions, including constipation, tinnitus, and dizziness. Furthermore, recent studies have suggested that Polygonum multiflorum may have anti-cancer properties, making it a promising natural remedy for cancer prevention and treatment.

However, more research is needed to fully understand the potential medicinal benefits of this powerful plant.

1. Improves blood circulation

Fo Ti improves blood circulation, meaning it helps to promote the flow of oxygen-rich blood throughout the body, which can help to improve overall cardiovascular health.

The main active constituents of Polygonum multiflorum that give this benefit are stilbenes, such as resveratrol, and tannins. The primary parts of the Polygonum multiflorum plant that give this benefit are the roots, which contain the highest concentration of these active compounds. The main medicinal preparations of Polygonum multiflorum that give this benefit include decoctions, powdered extracts, and capsules.
