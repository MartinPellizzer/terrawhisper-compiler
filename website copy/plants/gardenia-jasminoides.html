Gardenia jasminoides, also known as the cape jasmine or cape jessamine, is a beautiful evergreen shrub hailing from China and Japan.

This plant holds significant medicinal properties, particularly in Traditional Chinese Medicine, where it is used to alleviate digestive issues and relieve anxiety. Gardenia jasminoides thrives best in partial shade with consistently moist soil, making it an excellent choice for shady gardens or as a container plant.
