Citrus limon, also known as the lemon, is a popular fruit with a variety of medicinal uses.

Its peel and juice are often used to make tisanes, or herbal teas, which can help soothe sore throats and aid digestion. The zest of the lemon peel can also be used to make a tincture, an alcohol-based extract, which can be used to treat conditions such as anxiety, depression, and insomnia. Additionally, the essential oil of Citrus limon has antimicrobial and anti-inflammatory properties, making it a popular ingredient in natural remedies for skin conditions and infections.

However, it is important to note that the use of lemon in medicinal preparations should be done with caution, as it can interact with certain medications and may cause skin irritation in some individuals.
