10 Best Active Constituents of Scutellaria lateriflora (Skullcap)

Scutellaria lateriflora, also known as skullcap, is a plant that contains several active constituents with potential medicinal benefits.

The most well-known of these constituents are flavonoids, particularly baicalein and scutellarin, which have been shown to possess anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, and antiviral properties. Additionally, skullcap contains terpenoids, such as ursolic and oleanolic acids, which have been studied for their potential anti-cancer effects. Other compounds found in skullcap, such as volatile oils and tannins, may also contribute to its medicinal properties.

However, it is important to note that more research is needed to fully understand the effects and safety of using skullcap for medicinal purposes.
