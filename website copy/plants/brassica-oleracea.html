In terms of horticultural conditions, Brassica oleracea prefers cool, moist climates and well-drained soil, and is typically grown as a cool-season annual.

Botanically, Brassica oleracea is a leafy green biennial plant that can grow up to 3 feet tall, with long, thick stems and large, lobed leaves that range in color from light to dark green.
