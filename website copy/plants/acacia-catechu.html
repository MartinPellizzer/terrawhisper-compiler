In terms of horticultural conditions, Acacia catechu prefers well-drained soil and full sun, and can grow in a variety of soil types as long as they are well-drained.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 25 meters tall, with a spreading canopy and a straight, cylindrical trunk. The bark is grey and fissured, while the leaves are bipinnate and green.

The tree produces long, cylindrical, yellow flowers that are followed by long, brown seed pods.
