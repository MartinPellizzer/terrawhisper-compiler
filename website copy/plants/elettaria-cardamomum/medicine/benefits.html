10 Best Health Benefits of Elettaria cardamomum (Cardamom)

Elettaria cardamomum, commonly known as cardamom, has a long history of use in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.

One of the key medicinal properties of cardamom is its ability to aid digestion, as it can help alleviate symptoms such as bloating, gas, and heartburn. Additionally, cardamom has been shown to have anti-inflammatory effects, which can help reduce inflammation in the body and alleviate pain.

Some studies have also suggested that cardamom may help lower blood pressure, making it a potential natural remedy for hypertension. Furthermore, cardamom has been found to have antimicrobial and antiviral properties, which can help boost the immune system and protect against infections.

Overall, cardamom is a versatile medicinal spice with a range of health benefits, making it a valuable addition to any wellness routine.

1. Alleviates digestive issues

Cardamom alleviates digestive issues, meaning it can help to reduce symptoms such as bloating, gas, heartburn, and stomach discomfort.

The main active constituents of Elettaria cardamomum that contribute to this benefit are volatile oils like terpinene, limonene, and menthol, as well as various chemical compounds including alpha-terpineol, linalool, and 1,8-cineole. The primary parts of the Elettaria cardamomum plant that provide this benefit are the seeds, which contain the highest concentration of active compounds. Medicinal preparations of Elettaria cardamomum that are effective in alleviating digestive issues include powdered spice, essential oil, and tea made from the crushed seeds.

The main health conditions that this benefit of Elettaria cardamomum helps relieve include dyspepsia, irritable bowel syndrome, and other functional gastrointestinal disorders.

  • Sabinene
  • Myrcene
  • Heptenone
  • Geraniol
  • Nerol
  • Bioflavonoids
