What to know before using Lamium album (White Deadnettle)?

Lamium album, also known as white nettle, is a herbaceous perennial plant with a number of medicinal properties.

It contains tannins, flavonoids, and iridoid glycosides, which have anti-inflammatory, antiviral, and astringent effects. This plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil. Lamium album has distinctive white flowers with purple spots, and grows low to the ground, often forming dense mats.

It is easy to cultivate and can be used as a ground cover in gardens.
