10 Best Active Constituents of Piper methysticum (Kava)

Piper methysticum, also known as kava, is a plant that has been used for medicinal purposes for centuries in the Pacific Islands.

The active constituents of Piper methysticum are called kavalactones, which are a class of compounds that have been shown to have various medicinal properties. These kavalactones are believed to have anxiety-reducing, analgesic, and muscle-relaxant effects, making it a popular natural remedy for anxiety, insomnia, and stress-related disorders. Additionally, kavalactones have been studied for their potential anti-cancer, anti-inflammatory, and neuroprotective properties.

However, it is important to note that the long-term use of kava can have potential liver toxicity, and it should be used under the guidance of a healthcare professional.
