What to know before using Myristica fragrans (Nutmeg And Mace)?

Myristica fragrans, commonly known as nutmeg, is a versatile and valuable plant with a range of medicinal properties.

It has been used in traditional medicine for centuries to treat various ailments, including digestive issues, pain, and inflammation. The tree thrives in tropical conditions, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

It can grow up to 20 meters tall and has dark green, glossy leaves and small, yellow flowers. The fruit of the Myristica fragrans tree is encased in a fleshy aril, which, when dried, becomes mace, another valuable spice.

The nutmeg itself is the seed of the fruit, which, when ground, is used as a popular spice in cooking and baking.
