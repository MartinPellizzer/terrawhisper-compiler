What to know before using Avenella flexuosa (Wavy Hairgrass)?

Avenella flexuosa, also known as feather bentgrass, is a perennial grass native to the cooler regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It thrives in nutrient-poor, acidic soils and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded lawns. The plant's botanical characteristics include slender, flexible stems that grow in loose clumps, and tiny, wheat-like flowers that bloom in the summer. Medicinally, Avenella flexuosa has been used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and diuretic properties.

It has been used to treat a variety of ailments, including arthritis, urinary tract infections, and skin conditions.
