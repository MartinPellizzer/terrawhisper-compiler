What to know before using Piper angustifolia (Matico)?

Piper angustifolia, also known as Cuban coralillo or thin-leaved pepper, is a perennial plant native to Central and South America.

It has a variety of medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and antispasmodic effects, making it useful in treating a range of ailments from arthritis to menstrual cramps. Piper angustifolia thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, and can grow up to 3 feet tall. Its botanical characteristics include narrow, lance-shaped leaves, small, white flowers, and a distinctive, spicy aroma.

The plant's stems and leaves are covered in tiny hairs that contain compounds responsible for its medicinal properties.

What are the medicinal uses of Piper angustifolia?
