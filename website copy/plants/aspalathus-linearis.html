What to know before using Aspalathus linearis (Rooibos)?

Aspalathus linearis, also known as rooibos or red bush tea, is a plant native to South Africa that has gained popularity for its medicinal properties.

This plant is known for its ability to alleviate several health issues, including allergies, headaches, and insomnia, due to its high levels of antioxidants, vitamins, and minerals. Aspalathus linearis thrives in well-drained sandy soil and requires a warm, sunny climate with moderate rainfall to flourish. The plant is a small shrub that grows to about 1.5 meters tall, with needle-like leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

Its leaves are used to make a caffeine-free herbal tea that has a sweet, nutty flavor and is enjoyed by people all over the world.
