What to know before using Impatiens glandulifera (Policeman'S-Helmet)?

Impatiens glandulifera, also known as Himalayan balsam, is a plant species with notable medicinal properties.

It contains compounds with anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects, making it useful in traditional medicine for treating minor wounds and skin irritations. In terms of horticultural conditions, Impatiens glandulifera prefers partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

It is a fast-growing annual that can reach heights of up to 10 feet, producing showy pink or purple flowers. However, it is important to note that this species is considered invasive in many areas due to its aggressive growth habit and ability to outcompete native plants.

The botanical characteristics of Impatiens glandulifera include alternating, green leaves with serrated edges, and seed pods that explosively disperse seeds when ripe.
