10 Best Health Benefits of Hamamelis virginiana (Witch Hazel)

Hamamelis virginiana, also known as witch hazel, has been used for centuries for its medicinal benefits.

The astringent properties of witch hazel make it an effective treatment for skin irritations, reducing inflammation, and soothing minor cuts and bruises. It is rich in tannins, which have been shown to help relieve symptoms of hemorrhoids and varicose veins.

Witch hazel is also known for its antioxidant properties, which can help protect the skin from damage caused by free radicals. Furthermore, it has been found to be beneficial in treating acne, oily skin, and inflammatory skin conditions like eczema and psoriasis.

These medicinal benefits are attributed to the plant's high concentration of polyphenols and other bioactive compounds.

1. Alleviates Skin Inflammation

Witch Hazel alleviates skin inflammation, meaning it can help reduce redness, swelling, and irritation in the skin.

The main active constituents of Hamamelis virginiana that provide this benefit are tannins, volatile oils, and plant sterols. The primary parts of the Hamamelis virginiana plant that give this benefit are the bark and leaves. The main medicinal preparations of Hamamelis virginiana that give this benefit are astringents, creams, gels, ointments, and distilled liquid extracts.

This benefit of alleviating skin inflammation helps relieve various health conditions such as eczema, psoriasis, razor burn, insect bites, and acne.
