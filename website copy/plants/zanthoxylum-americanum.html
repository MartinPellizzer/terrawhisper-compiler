What to know before using Zanthoxylum americanum (Prickly Ash)?

Zanthoxylum americanum, also known as northern prickly-ash or American zanthoxylum, is a deciduous shrub native to North America.

It has distinctive oval-shaped leaves and showy white flowers that bloom in the spring. The bark and fruits of Zanthoxylum americanum have been used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory, analgesic, and digestive properties. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is drought-tolerant and can grow up to 20 feet tall, making it a striking addition to any garden or landscape.
