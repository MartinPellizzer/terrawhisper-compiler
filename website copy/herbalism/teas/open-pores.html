    What is open pores and how can it affect your life?

    Open pores refer to the enlarged appearance of hair follicles on the skin, creating an uneven texture.

    This common skin condition impacts people's lives by causing self-consciousness and reduced self-esteem. The visibility of open pores can lead individuals to feel uncomfortable in social situations and while looking in the mirror, often resulting in a negative self-image.

    In some cases, open pores also increase the likelihood of skin issues such as acne and blackheads, further impacting an individual's quality of life.
