    • Flowers: Contain flavonoids and terpenoids, which possess anti-inflammatory and antifungal properties, helpful for vaginitis symptoms.

    4. Using a clean squeeze bottle, insert the cooled chamomile tea into the vagina.
    5. Repeat this process 2-3 times a day until symptoms subside.
