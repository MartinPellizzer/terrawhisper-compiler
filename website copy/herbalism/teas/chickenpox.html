    4. Strain the chamomile tea into a cup, discarding the spent flowers.
    5. Drink the cooled tea (warm, not hot) three times a day, especially before bedtime.

    What is chickenpox and how can it affect your life?

    Chickenpox is a highly contagious viral infection that primarily affects children, causing an itchy rash with small, fluid-filled blisters.

    Once exposed to the virus, an individual develops immunity and typically doesn't contract it again. However, the disease carries potential complications, such as bacterial infections, pneumonia, or inflammation of the brain, which can lead to hospitalization. Moreover, chickenpox during pregnancy poses risks to the unborn baby.

    Although vaccination significantly reduced its prevalence in recent decades, unvaccinated individuals remain at risk of contracting this widely infectious disease.
