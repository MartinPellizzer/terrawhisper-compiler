The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic spearmint tincture for bad breath.

  1. Chop 1 cup of fresh spearmint leaves and place them in a clean glass jar.
  2. Pour 2 cups of vodka or rum over the spearmint leaves, ensuring they are fully submerged.

  4. Strain the mixture through a cheesecloth into a clean glass bottle, squeezing out any excess liquid.
  5. Store the spearmint tincture in a cool, dark place and use 1-2 dropperfuls daily for bad breath relief.
