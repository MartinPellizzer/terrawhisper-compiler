Right below you will find a list of the most important parts of peppermint that help with bloating.

The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint tincture for bloating.

  1. Measure 1 cup of fresh peppermint leaves and place them in a clean jar.
  2. Pour 2 cups of vodka or rum over the peppermint leaves, ensuring they are fully submerged.
  3. Seal the jar tightly and let it steep in a cool, dark place for 2-3 weeks.
  4. Shake the jar gently every other day to help the ingredients combine.
  5. Strain the mixture using a cheesecloth or fine mesh strainer, discarding the leftover plant material. Store the final tincture in a dark glass bottle and refrigerate for up to 6 months.
