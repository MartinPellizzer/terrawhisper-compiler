What is flatulence and how can it affect your life?

Flatulence, the natural release of intestinal gases from the rectum, affects people's lives by producing unpleasant odors and potentially causing physical discomfort such as bloating and cramping.

While a normal part of digestion, in some cases, excessive flatulence can indicate certain health issues, like lactose intolerance or irritable bowel syndrome, which require medical attention.

Overall, flatulence remains a common human experience that can impact personal comfort and social interactions, making self-care and potential medical intervention crucial.
