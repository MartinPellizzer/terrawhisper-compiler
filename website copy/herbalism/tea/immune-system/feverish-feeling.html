The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic willow tea for feverish feeling.

  1. Gather 2-3 fresh willow branches, each no thicker than a pencil, from a mature tree.
  2. Remove and discard the bark, then chop the inner stem into 1-inch pieces.
  3. Place 1 tablespoon of willow pieces in a tea infuser and steep in boiling water for 10 minutes.
  4. Strain the tea, discarding the willow pieces, and let it cool slightly.
  5. Drink 1 cup of willow tea up to three times daily to alleviate feverish feelings.
