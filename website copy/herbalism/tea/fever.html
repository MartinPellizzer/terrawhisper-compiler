What is fever and how can it affect your life?

Fever is an elevated body temperature, typically above 38°C (100.4°F), that results from the body's immune response to infections, illnesses, or inflammation.

This natural defense mechanism helps to combat harmful pathogens by creating an unfavorable environment for their survival. However, fever can significantly impact people's lives, causing discomfort, fatigue, weakness, and impaired concentration.

Severe or prolonged fevers might require medical intervention to prevent complications, emphasizing the importance of monitoring and addressing elevated temperatures promptly.

What other natural remedies help with fever?

Fever, a common symptom of many medical conditions, signals the body's attempt to fight infection.

Effective natural remedies for fever include hydration, rest, and tepid baths. Staying hydrated replenishes fluids lost through sweating and helps maintain the body's optimal temperature. Consuming water, clear broths, and electrolyte-rich drinks like coconut water can meet hydration needs.

Rest is crucial as it allows the body to dedicate energy towards fighting infection. Tepid baths, maintaining a water temperature between 99 and 102 degrees Fahrenheit, can help reduce fever. Cooler baths risk increasing shaking chills and raising body temperature.

Adequate rest, hydration, and tepid baths form a practical approach for managing fever without using herbal teas or medication.
