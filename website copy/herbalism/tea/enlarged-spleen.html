The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic milk thistle tea for enlarged spleen.

What is enlarged spleen and how can it affect your life?

An enlarged spleen, medically known as splenomegaly, indicates an abnormal increase in the size of this organ.

The spleen plays a vital role in filtering the blood and fighting infections, but when it expands, it can lead to several complications. A severely enlarged spleen can cause discomfort or pain in the upper left abdomen, and it can also result in a decreased number of healthy red blood cells, platelets, and white blood cells. This decrease can further lead to anemia, fatigue, increased susceptibility to infections, and bleeding tendencies.

Early detection and treatment of the underlying cause are essential to prevent these potential impacts on people's lives.

Furthermore, incorporating foods high in antioxidants, like berries and leafy greens, helps combat oxidative stress and inflammation.

Additionally, managing stress through techniques like meditation, deep breathing exercises, or yoga supports the immune system and overall well-being, which in turn, can contribute to a reduction in spleen size.
