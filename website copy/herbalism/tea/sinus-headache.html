The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic elderberry tea for sinus headache.

What is sinus headache and how can it affect your life?

A sinus headache is a condition characterized by throbbing pain in the forehead, cheeks, or bridge of the nose, caused by inflammation in the sinuses.

This impacts people's lives by causing discomfort, interfering with daily activities, and sometimes leading to absenteeism from work or school.

The constant pressure and pain can also result in difficulty sleeping, mood changes, and reduced concentration, further affecting overall quality of life.

What other natural remedies help with sinus headache?

Among the most commonly effective natural remedies for sinus headaches are nasal irrigation, hydration, and warm compresses.

Nasal irrigation, using a saline solution, helps to clear the nasal passages of mucus and allergens, reducing inflammation and relieving sinus pressure. Staying hydrated by drinking plenty of fluids, such as water and clear broths, thins mucus and soothes a sore throat. Warm compresses applied to the face, particularly the forehead, cheeks, and nose, can alleviate sinus pain by increasing blood flow and reducing inflammation in the sinuses.

These natural remedies offer a straightforward, accessible, and reliable method for managing sinus headaches without the need for medication.
