What is white patches in month and how can it affect your life?

The presence of white patches in the mouth can cause discomfort, pain, and difficulty swallowing or speaking. In severe cases, it can lead to nutritional deficiencies if eating becomes too problematic.

Overall, white patches in the mouth can significantly impact a person's quality of life, causing distress and impairing daily activities.
