What is viral hepatitis and how can it affect your life?

Viral hepatitis is a disease caused by several types of viruses, including Hepatitis A, B, C, D, and E, that inflame the liver, impairing its function and negatively affecting people's lives.

In severe cases, viral hepatitis leads to chronic liver damage, liver cancer, and even death. Furthermore, people with viral hepatitis face social stigma, discrimination, and mental health issues due to the lack of understanding and awareness about the disease.

As a result, the disease impacts not only individuals' physical health but also their mental and social well-being, leading to decreased quality of life.

Milk thistle, a herbal supplement, is sometimes used as it contains silymarin which has been shown to have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties that can help protect the liver. However, its effectiveness is still a subject of ongoing research.

Additionally, a balanced diet that includes lean proteins, whole grains, fruits, and vegetables provides essential nutrients that support liver function and overall health.

Regular exercise is also crucial in managing viral hepatitis as it helps the body eliminate toxins, reduces inflammation, and improves overall health.
