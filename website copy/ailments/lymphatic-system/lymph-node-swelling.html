What is lymph node swelling and and how it affects your life?

Lymph node swelling is a condition characterized by an enlargement of the lymph nodes, which are small glands that are part of the immune system.

They play a critical role in fighting infections and filtering out waste and foreign substances from the body. When lymph nodes swell, it can indicate an infection, inflammation, or in rare cases, cancer.

Therefore, recognizing and addressing lymph node swelling is vital for maintaining good health and promptly treating potential health issues, ultimately contributing to one's overall well-being and longevity.
