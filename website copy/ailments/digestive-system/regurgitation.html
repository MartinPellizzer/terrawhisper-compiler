These teas, when consumed regularly, offer a natural, caffeine-free solution to regurgitation.

What is regurgitation and and how it affects your life?

Regurgitation is the process of bringing up food from the stomach to the mouth, a natural action seen in many animals including humans.

This mechanism plays a critical role in our lives as it enables us to eat more food than our stomach can hold at once.

By regurgitating, animals can move food back to their mouths for additional chewing or re-swallowing in smaller portions, promoting efficient digestion and better nutrient absorption.
