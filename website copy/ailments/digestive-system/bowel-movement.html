What to know about bowel movement before using medicinal herbs

Specific herbal teas, such as senna, peppermint, and ginger teas, promote bowel movement.

Senna contains natural laxatives that stimulate the intestines, while peppermint and ginger reduce inflammation and spasms in the gastrointestinal tract, aiding in the passing of stools.

Incorporating these herbal teas into a daily routine supports regularity and overall digestive health.

What is bowel movement and and how it affects your life?

A bowel movement is the expulsion of waste materials from the digestive system through the anus.

This process signifies the body's ability to break down food, absorb essential nutrients, and eliminate toxins, ensuring the maintenance of a healthy and functional gastrointestinal system.

Regular bowel movements play a crucial role in overall well-being by preventing the buildup of harmful substances in the body, supporting a strong immune system, and maintaining energy levels for daily activities.
