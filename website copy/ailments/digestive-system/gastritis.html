What to know about gastritis before using medicinal herbs

Gastritis, an inflammation of the stomach lining, finds relief in several herbal teas.

Chamomile tea, made from flowers of the Matricaria chamomilla plant, boasts anti-inflammatory properties that can soothe the digestive tract. Licorice root tea, derived from the Glycyrrhiza glabra plant, shields the stomach lining with its mucilage content and curbs acid production.

Peppermint tea, crafted from Mentha piperita leaves, calms gastritis symptoms like bloating and nausea due to its menthol content, promoting bile flow to facilitate digestion.

What is gastritis and and how it affects your life?

Gastritis is a medical condition characterized by inflammation of the lining of the stomach.

It is important to understand and address gastritis because it can lead to severe discomfort, stomach ulcers, and, in extreme cases, stomach cancer.

By maintaining a healthy diet, avoiding alcohol and tobacco, and addressing any underlying issues such as bacterial infections, individuals can prevent or manage gastritis, thereby enhancing their quality of life and overall well-being.
