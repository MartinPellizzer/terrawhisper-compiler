What to know about heartburn before using medicinal herbs

Certain herbal teas bring soothing relief to those suffering from heartburn.

Consider chamomile tea, known for its anti-inflammatory properties that can help reduce irritation in the esophagus. Ginger tea is another excellent choice, as it neutralizes stomach acids and helps alleviate digestive distress. These herbal teas bring proven heartburn relief, offering safe and natural alternatives to over-the-counter medications.

What is heartburn and and how it affects your life?

Heartburn is a burning sensation or discomfort in the chest that occurs when stomach acid flows back up into the esophagus.

This occurrence is also known as acid reflux. Heartburn is important for your life because frequent or severe heartburn can lead to more serious health problems, such as esophagitis, a narrowing of the esophagus, and even Barrett's esophagus, which is a precancerous condition.

By managing heartburn through lifestyle changes and, when necessary, medication, you can reduce the risk of developing these complications and maintain your overall well-being.
