What to know about gerd before using medicinal herbs

GERD, or gastroesophageal reflux disease, affects many people worldwide.

When it comes to herbal teas, chamomile, ginger, and licorice teas are widely recognized for their soothing properties. Chamomile tea, with its anti-inflammatory properties, helps alleviate irritation in the esophagus.

Ginger tea, known for its calming and digestive qualities, can help reduce inflammation and acid production. Lastly, licorice tea, rich in flavonoids, protects the gastric mucosa and supports overall gastrointestinal health.

These three teas deliver proven relief for GERD symptoms.

What is gerd and and how it affects your life?

Gastroesophageal reflux disease (GERD) is a chronic condition characterized by the regular flow of stomach acid into the esophagus, causing symptoms such as heartburn, chest pain, and difficulty swallowing.

Its importance lies in the fact that, if left untreated, GERD can lead to serious complications like esophagitis, strictures, or even Barrett's esophagus, which is a precancerous condition.

Thus, recognizing and managing GERD is essential to maintain long-term digestive health and overall well-being.
