What is jellyfish sting and and how it affects your life?

A jellyfish sting occurs when a person comes into contact with the tentacles of a jellyfish, resulting in the discharge of venom.

This venom can cause a range of symptoms, from mild irritation to severe pain and even death in some cases.

Understanding jellyfish stings and how to treat them is vital because it enables individuals to take appropriate action in the event of a sting, reducing the risk of serious harm or death.
