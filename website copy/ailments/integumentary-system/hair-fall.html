What to know about hair fall before using medicinal herbs

Here are the best herbal teas for hair fall: green tea, rosemary tea, and nettle tea.

Green tea is rich in antioxidants that promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Rosemary tea improves blood circulation in the scalp, which encourages hair growth. Nettle tea is a powerhouse of vitamins and minerals that nourish the hair follicles, resulting in less hair fall.

Regularly drinking these teas can help reduce hair fall and promote thicker, fuller hair.

What is hair fall and and how it affects your life?

Hair fall, also known as alopecia, is the excessive shedding of hair from the scalp.

This natural process involves the continuous growth and shedding of hair, with the average person losing up to 100 strands per day. However, when hair fall exceeds this rate, it becomes a concern.

Excessive hair fall can result from various factors, such as hormonal imbalances, stress, poor nutrition, or genetics. Addressing hair fall is important as it can impact one's appearance and self-esteem, leading to psychological distress.

Additionally, extreme hair fall might indicate underlying health issues demanding attention.
