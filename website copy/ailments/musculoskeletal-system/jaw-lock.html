What is jaw lock and and how it affects your life?

Jaw lock, also known as locked jaw, is a condition where the jaw muscles spasm and prevent the jaw from opening or closing fully.

This condition can cause significant discomfort and interfere with basic activities such as eating, speaking, and even breathing. Jaw lock can result from various factors, such as teeth grinding, jaw clenching, or injuries to the jaw.

Due to the crucial role of the jaw in daily functions, jaw lock can negatively impact one's quality of life, making it essential to seek medical attention to diagnose and treat the underlying cause promptly.
