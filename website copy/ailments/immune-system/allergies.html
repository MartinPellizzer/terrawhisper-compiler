What to know about allergy before using medicinal herbs

Certain herbal teas, like nettle, chamomile, and goldenseal, bring natural allergy relief.

Nettle neutralizes histamine, a substance that triggers allergy symptoms. Chamomile's anti-inflammatory properties soothe irritated nasal passages and eyes. Goldenseal, renowned for its antibacterial and immune-boosting qualities, alleviates sinus congestion.

These herbal teas provide a safe, caffeine-free, and chemical-free alternative for those battling allergies.

What is allergy and and how it affects your life?

An allergy is a specific reaction of the body's immune system to a normally harmless substance, known as an allergen.

This reaction occurs when the body mistakenly identifies the allergen as a threat and releases chemicals to combat it. Allergies are significant because they can lead to various uncomfortable and potentially dangerous symptoms, such as itching, swelling, breathing difficulties, and anaphylaxis.

By understanding and managing allergies, individuals can maintain their health, enhance their overall quality of life, and reduce the risk of severe complications.
