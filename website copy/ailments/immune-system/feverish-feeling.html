What is feverish feeling and and how it affects your life?

A feverish feeling is an elevation in body temperature, often accompanied by symptoms such as sweating, chills, muscle aches, and fatigue.

This response arises from the body's immune system as it combats infections or inflammation. Its significance in our lives is immense, as it serves as a crucial defense mechanism, helping to eliminate harmful pathogens and expedite recovery.

Consequently, recognizing and addressing feverish sensations is vital for maintaining overall health and well-being.
