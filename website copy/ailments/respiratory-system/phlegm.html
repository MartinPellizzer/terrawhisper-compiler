What to know about phlegm before using medicinal herbs

Several herbal teas effectively manage phlegm, offering natural relief.

Consider licorice root tea, known for its soothing demulcent properties that help loosen and expel mucus. Thyme tea, with its potent antiseptic and expectorant abilities, reduces phlegm congestion.

Lastly, ginger tea, a powerful anti-inflammatory, alleviates respiratory issues by breaking down and eliminating phlegm, making breathing easier.

What is phlegm and and how it affects your life?

Phlegm is a type of mucus that the respiratory system produces to trap foreign particles such as dust, bacteria, and viruses, preventing them from entering the lungs.

Its importance lies in its crucial role in maintaining lung health and overall well-being.

By trapping and eliminating harmful substances, phlegm helps protect the body from respiratory infections and diseases, ensuring the smooth functioning of the respiratory system and promoting a healthy lifestyle.
