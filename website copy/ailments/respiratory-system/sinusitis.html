What is sinusitis and and how it affects your life?

Sinusitis is an inflammation or infection of the sinuses, which are air-filled cavities located in the skull near the nose.

This condition results in symptoms such as stuffy nose, facial pain, headache, and thick yellow or green mucus. Sinusitis is important for your life because it can negatively affect your quality of life by causing discomfort and pain, and it can also lead to complications such as the spread of infection to other parts of the body.

Furthermore, it can result in lost productivity and increased healthcare costs, making it a significant public health issue.
