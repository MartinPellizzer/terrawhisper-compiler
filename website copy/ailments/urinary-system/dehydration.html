What is dehydration and and how it affects your life?

Dehydration is the condition that occurs when an individual's body loses more fluids than it takes in, leading to an imbalance in bodily functions.

This situation can result from various factors, such as intense physical activity, exposure to high temperatures, or illnesses that cause vomiting or diarrhea. Dehydration is crucial to understand and avoid because it can severely impair numerous body processes, from regulating temperature and maintaining healthy blood pressure to supporting cognitive function and aiding in digestion.

Neglecting the significance of hydration can ultimately jeopardize one's overall health and well-being.
