What is bladder infection and and how it affects your life?

A bladder infection, also known as a urinary tract infection (UTI), is a bacterial infection that affects the bladder.

This infection can cause a host of uncomfortable symptoms such as a frequent and urgent need to urinate, burning sensation while urinating, and lower abdominal pain. If left untreated, a bladder infection can lead to more serious complications, including kidney infections and sepsis. Therefore, it is crucial to seek medical attention promptly if you suspect a bladder infection.

Regular check-ups and proper hygiene practices can help prevent bladder infections, ensuring your overall well-being and quality of life.
