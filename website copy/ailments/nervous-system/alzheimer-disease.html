What to know about alzheimer’s disease before using medicinal herbs

When it comes to herbal teas that benefit individuals with Alzheimer's disease, two options stand out.

The first, ginkgo biloba tea, is derived from the leaves of the ginkgo biloba tree and has been shown to enhance cognitive function and improve memory. The second, lemon balm tea, is made from the lemon balm plant and has demonstrated the ability to reduce agitation and improve mood in individuals with Alzheimer's disease.

Both teas offer promising support for those affected by this disease.

What is alzheimer’s disease and and how it affects your life?

Alzheimer's disease is a degenerative brain disorder and the most common cause of dementia, characterized by a decline in cognitive abilities, behavioral changes, and a loss of independence.

It is important for your life because it affects memory, thinking, and behavior, which can impact your relationships, productivity, and overall well-being.

By understanding Alzheimer's disease, you can take steps to reduce your risk, seek early detection and treatment, and provide support for those affected, thereby enhancing the quality of life for yourself and your loved ones.
