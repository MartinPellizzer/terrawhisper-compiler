What to know about fatigue before using medicinal herbs

Herbal teas offer a natural remedy for fatigue, providing a soothing and healthy alternative to caffeinated drinks.

Passionflower tea, known for its calming properties, helps reduce anxiety and promotes better sleep, both of which contribute to increased energy levels. Ginseng tea is another excellent choice, as it boosts mental performance and physical stamina.

Lastly, lemon balm tea aids in digestion, reduces stress, and improves mood, all of which combat fatigue effectively.

What is fatigue and and how it affects your life?

Fatigue is a state of extreme physical and/or mental exhaustion that significantly reduces one's energy levels, focus, and productivity.

Its importance in life is undeniable as it directly affects our ability to perform daily tasks, make decisions, and maintain healthy relationships.

Chronic fatigue can lead to severe health issues, including depression, heart disease, and weakened immune system, thereby negatively impacting the overall quality of life.
