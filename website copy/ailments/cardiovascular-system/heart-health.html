What to know about heart health before using medicinal herbs

Herbal teas, such as hawthorn and hibiscus, promote heart health due to their rich antioxidant properties.

Hawthorn tea, derived from the hawthorn tree's leaves and berries, is an ideal choice for heart health as it contains flavonoids that strengthen blood vessels and improve blood flow. Hibiscus tea, extracted from the vibrant hibiscus flower, is another excellent option due to its high antioxidant content that helps lower blood pressure and cholesterol levels, contributing to a healthier cardiovascular system.

Regularly consuming these herbal teas fortifies the heart, enhancing overall well-being.

What is heart health and and how it affects your life?

Heart health refers to the condition of one's cardiovascular system, encompassing the heart and blood vessels.

A healthy heart efficiently pumps blood, providing oxygen and nutrients to all body parts. Maintaining heart health is crucial as it directly impacts overall well-being and longevity.

A healthy heart lowers the risk of cardiovascular diseases, such as heart attacks and strokes, and enhances energy levels, promoting a higher quality of life.
