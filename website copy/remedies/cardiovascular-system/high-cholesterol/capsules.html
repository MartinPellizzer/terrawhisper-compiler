Obesity, particularly around the abdomen, is a significant risk factor for high cholesterol. Regular physical activity helps lower LDL (bad) cholesterol and boost HDL (good) cholesterol levels.

By addressing these causes, herbal capsules can effectively support healthy cholesterol levels.

What other herbal preparations helps with high cholesterol?

Other herbal preparations to use with capsules for high cholesterol are tinctures, teas, and topical oils.

Tinctures are concentrated liquid extracts that can be taken sublingually or added to water. Teas can be consumed daily for their cholesterol-lowering properties. Topical oils, when massaged into the skin, can help improve circulation and lower cholesterol levels.

It's important to consult with a healthcare provider to determine the best herbal preparation and dosage for individual needs.
