The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic chamomile tea for abdominal pain.

  1. Boil 1 cup of water in a kettle or pot, reaching a temperature of 212°F (100°C) for optimal extraction of chamomile compounds.

  3. Pour the boiling water over the chamomile flowers, allowing them to steep for 5-10 minutes to ensure adequate extraction of therapeutic constituents.
  4. Strain the chamomile tea to remove the spent flowers, capturing only the liquid in a cup or mug.

What other herbal preparations helps with abdominal pain?

Always follow dosage recommendations and consult a healthcare provider for appropriate use.
