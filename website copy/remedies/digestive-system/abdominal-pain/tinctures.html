The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic peppermint tincture for abdominal pain.

  1. Chop 1 cup of fresh peppermint leaves, then steep in 2 cups of boiling water for 30 minutes.
  2. Strain the mixture, squeezing out the leaves to release excess liquid.
  3. Combine the peppermint liquid with 1 cup of vodka or grain alcohol in a glass jar.
  4. Seal the jar and store in a cool, dark place for 2 weeks, shaking daily.
  5. Strain the mixture and store the resulting tincture in a dark glass bottle.
