The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic elderberry tea for flu.

  1. Gather 1 tablespoon of dried elderberries and place them in a cup.
  2. Boil 8 ounces of water and pour it over the elderberries.

  5. Drink the tea while still warm, up to 3 times a day.
