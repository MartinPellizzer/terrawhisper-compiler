The following recipe gives a procedure to make a basic marshmallow tincture for dry throat.

  1. Chop 1 cup of dried marshmallow root into small pieces, then combine with 2 cups of 80-proof vodka in a clean jar, sealing it tightly.
  2. Let the mixture steep for 2-3 weeks in a cool, dark place, shaking it gently every few days.
  3. After the steeping period, strain the mixture using cheesecloth into a dark glass bottle, squeezing out any excess liquid.
  4. Discard the marshmallow root solids and store the tincture in a cool, dark place, where it can last for up to a year.
