Other herbal preparations to use with capsules for asthma are tinctures, teas, and essential oils.

Tinctures are alcohol-based extractions that can be taken sublingually or added to drinks. Teas are made by steeping herbs in hot water and consumed as a beverage.

Essential oils can be diffused into the air or added to baths for inhalation. Each preparation method offers unique benefits and can be used in combination with herbal capsules to provide comprehensive asthma support.

Always consult with a healthcare provider before starting any new herbal regimen.
