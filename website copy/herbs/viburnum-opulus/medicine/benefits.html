10 Best Health Benefits of Viburnum opulus (Cramp Bark)

Viburnum opulus, also known as cramp bark or snowball tree, has a long history of medicinal use due to its various health benefits.

It is particularly renowned for its antispasmodic properties, making it an effective remedy for muscle cramps, menstrual cramps, and reducing blood pressure. The bark of Viburnum opulus contains several beneficial compounds, including salicin, which has been found to alleviate pain and reduce inflammation. Moreover, it has been used to treat gastrointestinal issues, such as diarrhea and constipation, due to its astringent qualities.

Additionally, this plant has been used in traditional medicine to alleviate symptoms of respiratory conditions, such as bronchitis and whooping cough, by soothing irritated throat tissues and promoting expectoration.
